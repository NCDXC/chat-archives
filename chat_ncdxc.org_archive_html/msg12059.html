Anna worked EP2A last night for an ATNO, but we had a big laugh when we looked at her log. Anna doesn’t operate very much under her home callsign (W6NN), being more of a contester (under K6MMM) and a holiday DXpeditioner, where she has many DX call signs. She does get on to work the occasional “rare one”, but apparently thats ALL she does from home. Take a look at the attached screenshot of her log for the past two years or so.
