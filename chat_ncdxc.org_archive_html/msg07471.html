On Fri,9/5/2014 9:59 PM, Garry Shapiro via Chat wrote:
I have an EU2000i and have not used the Generac competition,
I do as well, and I also have its much bigger brother (EU6500, I think) that does 240V and runs my well motors. Both are quiet and have an econo-mode.
I've reconfigured the wiring in my house to put all the essential 120V loads on one side of the 120-0-120 breaker panel, and have made up an adapter to feed that side from the 2000i. I can run it in economode and still run the refrigerator (but not the well motors, which are 240V). As others have said, quiet, reliable, decent waveform, miserly on petrol. Both Hondas what a common mode choke on their output for radio operations if they're close to antennas.
