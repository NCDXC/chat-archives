Paul,

 

I thought the ad-hock 6-meter dinner at Black Bear Diner was great fun. Tim N6GP did a great job pulling it together in the eleventh hour. I was pleased that the 6-meter ops spontaneously stood up to address the room and made really good comments and observations about the recent 6 meter activities. I think if we pull a 6-meter event into the Convention it could grow and maybe even be an attraction to pull in a few more VHF DXers. I asked Tim if he would be willing to co-chair a 6-meter event with me next year and he agreed. I think having a representative from NCDXC and SCDXC would help generate interest in the event. The wide spread interest and activity on 50 MHz has never been better.

 

Submitted to the committee for consideration.

 

Matt K6EME
