It makes zero sense.  There are three (desirable) DXpeditions being active in the usual windows.  ZERO sense to go head to head.


Elvira moves off the band, when it gets crowded (even when conditions are awesome).  While commendable, ZERO sense to start there.


Yuris, KNOWS better, has proven that too (both great ops).  Zero sense.


The third one, in SoPac... we know his reasons.


Now you have stations on top of the DX, too close to the (weak) signals for anyone to copy and when asked, they don't move or don't care.  No winners there.  There has been name calling and worse as the result (well, more than the usual).  Including DQRM.


Yuris was imminently workable on 15, EXCEPT he's on the standard window (instant chaos).  So he moved to 20 to make an even larger disaster (bigger audience).  He IS paying attention to propagation, working EU before the band close, then moving west.  Kudos, almost rare these days for that to happen.


If someone has a back channel to Yuris or Elvira, please ask them to use the standard DX FTx windows (i.e. 14.09), not the main.  There is simply too much competition to hear any team now active on the usual places, all at once.  They WILL be found and everyone has a better chance instead of competing with the usual traffic loads; organized chaos is a step or two better than utter.


Only the super stations win trying to get over this one.  I ain't one of them.


Yes, Yuris will be there a while; that's NOT the point.  Following acceptable practices and not causing a mess, is.


Let's hope this isn't the new trend.
