I was very honored to have worked VU3WIJ, Shaji, in South India on 20 Meters few days ago on the first attempt with 100 watts.  Today, I twisted a knob on front of my radio labelled "RF Power".  I wondered if I can work VU3WIJ again with reduced power.  Adjusting the power to 5 watts or less, I called him, again and again and on the fourth attempted, he responded back to me.  I managed to have an exchange of signal reports while I was transmitting QRP < 5 watts.  Shaji send a 51 signal report and I jumped with joy.  I sent him 55 report, great audio.  Oh, by the way, this was long path.

 

This QRP Q is the longest distance that I have worked anyone with 5 watts.

 

I submitted my online QSL request just few minutes ago.  Once I receive the QSL card, I am going to frame it.

 

I am still flying with the Q I just had.

 

DX if fun but QRP DX, that is sweetness.
