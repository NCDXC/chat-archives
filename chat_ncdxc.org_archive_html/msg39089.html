My main DX goal besides Top of HR is to work and confirm everything on CW and SSB.
I have been trying to repeat an 80 M SSB contact with Dominica, VP2D now J7.  The VP2D was in March 5, 1977.  I finally worked another with effort on March 5th, 2025, 48 years later.  I worked J7A when he was on in the ARRL DX test and only on the band for half an hour.  I gave my contact a 75%.
ClubLog OQRS said it was good once they put up the log.  LoTW showed it but did not match to my log because it somehow was logged as a crossband QSO.  Huh?  I modified the record, re-uploaded and got the match.
Sometimes it takes patience.  80 M is not an easy band, even for the 'easy', closeby entities.
