Two of the "boots were completely demolished by UV. When I rotated the boom, so elements were vertical, both actually fell out. Both SS clamps were holding rubber under them just fine. 
The other boots looked almost like new. I had not taped over any of the boots.  People, in areas of heavy icing, safety wire the two SS clamps to avoid this problem. I had always considered this overkill ---for sunny CA.  Not now. I have the wire/tools from my aviation days--takes 1 minute---so why not.
Lots of glass showing on the top side. UV resistant deck paint is on the schedule. Very inconsistent weathering element to element. SteppIR supplied cables are in good shape.  Yaesu G-2800 cable not so good.
