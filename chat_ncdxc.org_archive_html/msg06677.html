Hello all,  been QRT for the last couple of weeks handling some major "To-Do" items.  Sounds like I haven't missed much on the bands and elsewhere.  

As far as LOTW and other websites being down, I have heard that we can expect virtually every website that requires a login id and password - especially those using SSL - to be down for maintenance on and off over the next several weeks getting their 'heart bleeds' patched up.  This nasty little worm has created a lot of headaches and work (and billable hours) for those in the IT security field of late.  

The Amazons of the world will likely only see their systems slow while service is being conducted.  Smaller systems, like ARRL, will have to pull the plug completely for several hours at a time to do the necessary work. Glad to know that ARRL and others are taking this seriously, but it is still a pain for LOTW users. 

In the meantime, be patient (like we have a choice?), practice safe 'surfing' and treat all account passwords like a baby's diaper ... change early and often.

Ugh.
