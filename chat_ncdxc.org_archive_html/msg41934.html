On Saturday, February 7th, 2026 at 9:37 AM, Al N6TA via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
Ryan,
Being a casual DXer means that you will grow your worked entities list more slowly than if you employ some tools. To assist in knowing if there is a needed DX entity on the air, you can use a number of SW tools that will alert based on what you have not yet worked. Sitting on one 'standard' frequency and waiting for the DX to appear could delay your awareness of DX being elsewhere on the band working your neighbors. Use of a bandscope to look away from the normal frequency may show activity or simply tuning to other frequencies will show you what is or is not there. It does take some time but it does provide some treats from time to time.
