The small group of organizers and volunteers did a great job.

I still really like the Marriott hotel.  

Despite the one broken elevator issue, every single person I spoke to on the Marriott staff, from the front desk people to the daily room cleaners, were extremely friendly and helpful.

This hotel offers free gated parking for hotel guests, free EV charging for hotel guests (a new and most welcome feature in my case), two free bottles of water in the room delivered every day, clean modern showers with tons of water pressure, and free access to the service elevator to compensate for one of the two main ones which was not working this year (only).  And of course, the convention area itself is large and spacious, with good lighting and a good sound system.

Perhaps more extensive promotion of IDXC, including perhaps subsidized or free rooms for invited international guests and speakers, could help the convention to grow again.  The domestic and JA ham population of DXers and Contesters may be aging, but in Asia and Europe, that doesn't seem to be the case, at least not yet.  Barring another pandemic, this convention still has the potential to grow.

73,
Bob, N6TV
