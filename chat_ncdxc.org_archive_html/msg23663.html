Does anyone know someone who does tower work in our area? My 55’ crank-up has been up for 13 years, with absolutely no problems, but I’m thinking it could use some maintenance. There is one antenna (a 2m/70cm whip) at the top that is misbehaving, and I suspect it’s time to replace the lifting/lowering cable, lubricate and inspect the tower, etc. 

I was able to raise/lower/tilt the tower myself when I installed it, but I’m 13 years older now (who isn’t?) and need to hire someone to do this work. It’s a US Tower TX-455, manual raise/lower, with the tilt-over winch installed.

Assuming that the tower lowers and tilts properly, there isn’t even a need to climb; everything is at ground level (within reach of a ladder) when tilted down. The rotator pointing angle is still spot-on.
