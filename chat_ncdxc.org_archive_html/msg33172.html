Hi Vijay;

For solid state amps in particular, I’d recommend you consider only two brands: Elecraft and SPE. 

The Elecraft amps are very reliable, and with Elecraft being right around the corner (so to speak) in Watsonville, service and support are readily available if you ever do need them. If you have an Elecraft K3 or K4, just stop here and go buy either their 500W or 1500W amp. The integration with the radio makes it well worth it. But even if you don’t, they integrate easily to other brands of radio so they’re still a good option. 

I have a SPE 1.3K-FA, and it is also an excellent choice. There are a number of other DX club members with SPE amps as well, and they’re popular with DXpeditioners (as are the Elecraft, of course). I have the internal tuner and it works well, but be aware that it can only handle a 3:1 SWR or less. It’s really intended for fine tuning the SWR of resonant antennas like my hex beam and fan dipole, not for something like an end-fed or random length wire antenna. 

The bottom line is that I think you’d be happy with either an Elecraft or SPE amp. 

73,
  Frank N6OI
