Thanks for stopping by yesterday, great to see you and Dick..

Found this in an old ad on the Internet.
"Tristao CZ-471FS 72-Foot Tower. Tristao was bought out by U.S. Tower which uses the Tristao design 
for their towers. It is comparable to a U.S. TOWER: TX-472. "

The US Tower specs document is available on the UST website:
http://www.ustower.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/TX-472.pdf

Compare the Tee base to the specs shown in this document..

UST charges $600 for a wet stamp copy by an AZ PE.
The PE will inspect the embedded bolts and the cage, he will not look at the tower. My tower wasn't here when he did the inspection.
The county's final inspection will be done by Skype Video Call, it took a total of 5 minutes. He asked me to pan the tower top to bottom and any electrical service (there was none). 

That was it for the county.
