One comment here, having done RTTY on two DXpeditions.  It really hard to “copy” RRTY signals.  It’s usually only possible to pull out a callsign at the very edges of the pileup. What you probably heard is the pileup so huge the guy couldn’t work anyone.

The signal structure of RTTY is a pair of tones, 170Hz apart.  Most decoders won’t work when there is energy in between the two tones.  This situation often happens in a pileup.  Lots of signals in the band, and splitting everyone’s tone pair, so no one can get thru.
