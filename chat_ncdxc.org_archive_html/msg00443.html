Paul and friends,

I replaced the rotor in my 55' crank-up tower earlier this year after the Hy-Gain T2X developed location indication problems.  The Hy-Gain motors seem to work OK but that resistor gets very noisy and has afflicted every one of my T2X and Ham-M series rotors.

I asked and received many responses from club members on what they would recommend for a couple of yagi antennas (F12 C-19, 20,15,10 and a two element for 40 meters) which are around 15 sq.ft. or so.

Most recommended the Yaesu rotors and since I didn't want to change it but once, I purchased the 2800 and the shock absorbing base.  The base makes it easy to mount and raises the mast clamp to the same height as the old T2X.  Also, this rotor's mast clamp assembly comes completely off of the motor box making it VERY easy to slip it through the tower zig-zag bracing.  It includes quite a bit of rotor cable with the connectors attached.  This model has the coast/slow down feature.  I also purchased the Idiom Press rotor control interface instead of the overpriced Yaesu box.

So far, I have been very happy with this rotor...

Bill
WB6JJJ
