I believe Al is right, and will also find out soon. What I do know, however, is that DXCC can withdraw credit even after it's been accepted for an award if later evidence reveals something improper. A couple years ago, there was a question about XU7GNY's operation - his license had "expired" due to some change in Cambodian regulations, or something like that. ClubLog stopped showing matches for his QSOs because of it. I already had DXCC credit for my QSOs with him, so I checked with DXCC and Sharon said the matter was temporarily on hold to see if things could be straightened out with the Cambodians - and that if they weren't I'd lose the credits. Fortunately, the Cambodians came through.
