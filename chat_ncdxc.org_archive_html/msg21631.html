The only memorable DX this year that really stands out for me is to work E44CC on 80m CW. It was an ATNO for me so that was super epic, I think it is my only unique that I have worked on 80m and no other band. That means a lot to me.

I did work Uruguay on 6m SSB during early April TEP and that was super epic since it was a new band entity for 6m which was pretty sweet and it was not FT8.

Sure I worked a dozen new ones on 6m FT8 but they don’t compare to that one CW contact on 80m. The Joe Taylor modes are cool at the bottom of the cycle to keep the ham industry going and us entertained but I am thinking we will see some F2 E/W on 6m during the peak of SC25. That means some serious SSB and CW contacts the like of which I hear in the legends. Working DXCC on CW and phone in a month on 6m. Never experienced it only heard about it. Like some urban legend.
