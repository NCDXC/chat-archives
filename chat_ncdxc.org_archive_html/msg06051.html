RSGB, JARL and others were interested in accepting LoTW QSO records roughly a decade ago. When ARRL decided -- early on -- to adopt the "central clearing house" type of system rather than using verifiabe QSL records as originally defined, they made it much more difficult for anyone to actually use the information for awards purposes.
I don't know exactly how they are handling WAZ, but so far, utilizing LoTW for awards requires substantial expenditures for programming. Generally, that has hampered progress. For many non-DXCC awards, the revenue is simply not there.
I don't know that anyone raised this particular issue early on in the development of LoTW.
