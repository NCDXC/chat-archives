To make progress on this issue you need to understand why they are refusing
access.

My guesses:
1)  Cost - they simply have no budget to go and babysit a bunch of hams who
want to go to an island they rarely visit - their charge is to leave the
birds alone - not to develop a new park - hence they have no budget -
letting no one go to the island meets the goal of leaving the birds alone;
2)  Fear - they don't want to get sued - for example, KP1 is full of UXBs
and KH3 is slowly falling into the ocean, has a trashed runway, and a couple
of bombs worth of Pu cleaned up by a bunch of airmen pushing bulldozers,
and, to top it off, there is a bunch of nerve gas lying around - a formula
for disaster;
3)  Politics - the East Coast KP1 guys appear to think that they own the
island and will create bloody hell for anyone who thinks they can end run
around them.  They did this last time as I recall and managed to get people
with a valid permit arrested.

Unless and until you can resolve these issues, they will prevent any honest
bureaucrat from saying YES.  Can they be overcome - yes - but it will cost
you - you'll need agreements, lawyers and insurance policies.  You'll have
to pay the cost the government thinks it will incur.  And you'll need to
find an advocate within the government - like a Rep or a Sen - to push (and
that usually isn't free these days).  And you'll need to get the hams to all
agree to play nice and not say or do anything negative - VERY HARD.
Probably best to get the ARRL involved to coordinate (oh yeah, they can't
even run a server)...

I'd like to go!
:-)


Just my 2 cents.
