Thank you for the updates from the pilot.

Hauns, it's simpler.

Don't call if you don't see/hear the station, a basic DX Code tenet.  When you do, double click on that call and the software will take care of the rest.

He asks for a caller tone > 1 KHz, NOT be in F/H mode so there is little left for the FT8 op to choose (tap the screen before the time out timer ends and have patience, send your best signal possible).

It's not much more complicated than that.  The rest is simply his choices and propagation; things beyond your control.  [CME impacts are expected, things will change.]

The pileups have been... well excessive, ALL the time.  It's challenging for everyone.

He has a 3 week operating period (with a stay of 3 months until the ship returns) and soon there will be a time that the big guns will be sated (well mostly hi hi) which is when the lil pistols remaining can step in for success.  It's a classical situation.

For a solo operation, he's done very well overall though some pileup management on his part could be improved.
