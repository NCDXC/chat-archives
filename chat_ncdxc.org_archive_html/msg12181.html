libya shipping suspended - Google Search

From Canada Post
The designated postal operator of Libya has informed member countries of the Universal Postal Union that, owing to current events in the country, The Libyan Post has suspended the processing of incoming and outgoing mail and parcels.
Mail and parcels that have already been deposited with Canada Post for delivery in Libya will be held temporarily. If there is no change in the situation in the coming days, these items will be returned to sender. Once postal service has resumed, customers will be able to resend their mail items at no additional charge.

From USPS.com
Due to the current situation in Libya, and the closure of Libya's international airport and office of exchange, these nations have temporarily suspended their transit mail services to the country:

Greece
Malta
Turkey

Until further notice, the above countries will not accept transit dispatches forwarded from third countries destined to Libya. (posted 8/15/14)
