According to clublog, I am now about 80% or so digital (83% so far this year) and 10% CW, 10% SSB or thereabouts on average. One interesting thing is the number of QSOs has increased dramatically since FT8! Prior to FT8, I was very active on JT65 and JT9 and before that on RTTY and PSK31 so my digital counts were always high, but never like this! It was nice to work SSB and CW this weekend for field day, I must say. Just for fun! Although the FD mode on WSJT-X is super far as exchanges too, not much back and forth. I could barely keep up.

Read some interesting statistics in the recent QST I believe:

85% QSOs on 6m are now FT8, only just over 4% is CW

60% QSO's on HF are now with FT8 at the expense of the other modes

Not saying for or against but some interesting statistics since FT8 came into play in 2017. What I find interesting too given FT8 is very helpful in working DX weak signal when no other modes work, is the number of USA to USA contacts I see on FT8 at any given time now.
