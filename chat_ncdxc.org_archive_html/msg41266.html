I just realized I failed to adequately describe step 2. 

After going into Dark Style, to change the colors to make it easier to read, do the following. 

1) With WSJT-X Running, click File, Settings and then Colors.
2) To Change the background color of any item, right click on it. You can change the Foreground color or the Background color. 
3) I recommend to select item CQ in message. I recommend changing the text from White to Black for that item. 

Click Okay, and you will have your new colors. 

You can experiment to get more colors to your liking.

I am using WSJT-X v3.0.0 Improved PLUS edition. 

It works amazingly well, but if you are like me, your eyes get weary as the many call signs go flying by. 

Here is a little trick to improve your view of WSJT-X. 

From the menus on WSJT-X along the top, select View, then select Dark Style. 

You will quickly see a black background with text in white. This gives you more contrast and is easier on the eyes. But Wait!  There is more. 

Click Files, Settings.  You can get better contrast for CQ in message by 
Selecting a background such as bright green and a text color of black. 

These two changes greatly improved my viewing of WSJT-X. 

Give it a try, I have attached a screenshot to show you what I mean.
