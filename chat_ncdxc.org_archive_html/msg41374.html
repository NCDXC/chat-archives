I have received very sad news today; Dave Gomberg, NE5EE is now a silent key. I received the news via the SFARC Slack workspace.

 

Dave was a former member of NCDXC and was very active on HF sideband a few years back. I recall driving him to some meetings back in the day when we met at Holders, pre-Covid era. I also recall going over to his house, adjacent to Sutro tower in San Francisco to help with an antenna install about a decade ago now. I got to see his dream DX location on top of SF, next to the TV tower. I could always hear him in early morning EU pileups with his unique callsign pegging my meter here in Pacifica. More recently, he was only active on 2m VHF and with SFARC.

 

RIP Dave, you will be missed!
