On 6/13/2014 3:17 AM, Russell Bentson via Chat wrote:
Guys: The VHF contest starts at 11:00 AM Saturday and ends at 8:00 PM Sunday.
And stay off the calling frequencies. I NEVER work anyone on the 6M calling frequency, and I delete from my log anyone I hear trying to run on 50.125. The calling frequency is, by definition, a CALLING frequency, not a WORKING frequency. Running on the calling frequencies is for LIDS, right up there with "please copy." The right way to use a calling frequency is to direct callers to another frequency -- for example, "listening 160."
6M has not been great, but I've picked up 4 new grids so far, bringing my total to about 315. And use CW -- at least half of my new grids have always been CW, often double hop, there for a few minutes, then gone.
