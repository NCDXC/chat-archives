For me, it wasn't a particular DX; I heard a couple of potential ATNOs but couldn't break through the pileups.

I haven't used SSB much for several years, so my goals were:
  • See how well my newish transceiver worked in that mode, both RX and TX, and learn to efficiently use the built-it TX voice memories
  • Focus on new Phone entities, then multipliers
  • Learn to use N1MM+ for logging
I'm quite happy with my outcome on all counts!
