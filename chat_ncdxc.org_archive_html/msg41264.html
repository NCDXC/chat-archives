I, too, am awaiting cards - EP5TJS (2 bands) 1st request 12/30/24 & 2nd request 1/25/25; YN9H 1st request 1/13/25 & 2nd request 10/25/25 (yup, impatient); and tonight requested 7X5CY 

I call bullcrap on the statement Pedro made to N6TA:
"I don't think I explained myself well.
I don't receive the Clublog payment; it goes to EP5TJS. If you send $10, he manages that money and then sends me only what's needed to send the QSL cards. Even though you see my name on it, I don't receive it. You can use any name you want on PayPal, and he thought my name would be more reliable. This is difficult to explain, but in Iran, he can't have a PayPal account, so the money goes to someone else who personally delivers it to him during his trips to Iran. I can't explain this to you because it's confidential; if you want, EP5TJS can explain it to you.
I'm just the QSL Drop Manager for now. He's investing the money to improve the station, but it's difficult to get things there, and a friend from outside the country is helping him with the money collected through PayPal. He sends me a list every so often of the stations I need to send QSL cards to and the necessary funds. I think some are also being sent from Germany, where your friend is from, because the postal service is cheaper there. As you can see, it's a bit complicated. I just want to help so you have the possibility of confirming EP and other countries, which wouldn't be possible without this method. I can't afford a large amount of money for shipping costs, so I have to wait for him to send me the money, but I can send it to you, and if you have any other friends, I'll pay for it myself."  And to Paul Booth:  "Yes, but if I receive the money directly, he doesn't know what I receive or don't receive. With PayPal, he can monitor everything."

I print out every PayPal transaction receipt for QSLs (yup, OCD German at work here).  EACH PayPal receipt for those OQRS requests going to Pedro clearly states, "You paid XXX EUR to ea5gl@hotmail.com".   So the money is going to Pedro, not the DX.  I am seriously considering emailing EP5TJS, YN9H & 7X5CY to let them know what their QSL manager is telling those of us who are awaiting cards from them.  Maybe that will stri Pedro to fulfill his job as QSL manager.
