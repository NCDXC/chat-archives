Hi Paul and NCDXC friends, 
 
In the Winter months, I find 40 meters open about 20-22 hrs of the days. Of course, signals are much stronger in the gray lines and during full darkness. 
30 meters is also open 20-22 hrs per day.  Having a Yagi on these bands enhances that greatly. 
 
Of course, FT modes enhance what little propagation there is and our signals are decoded where SSB or CW might not be. 
 
Operating in FT modes has really enhanced my knowledge of propagation, particularly those Summertime middle of the day openings to the Middle East that I never knew existed. 
 
Best 73, 
 
Paul N6PSE
