Fantastic discussion!

I have quite a few I regret buying, mostly because I have them sitting in a box (usually unopened).  Of the ones I've actually used, I have an EFHW up in the air now that's allegedly able to do 160m.  It's up on a hill and terminates on a very tall tree, near some utility lines.  The furthest east I've gotten is Colorado and 20 of my 35 Qs on 160 have been in California.  I don't know if it's the antenna, or the way it had to be deployed, but it's pretty useless for that band.  Works well enough 60 to 6, but my ZS6BKW is the real winner for 80-6.

I've bought quite a few portables over the years.  Right now I'm playing around with an outbacker clone from Raddioddity.  It works pretty well, but has some serious shortcomings that are 99% attribuatable to operator error, I'm sure.  EFHW tends to be my goto for still for POTA/SOTA.  Trying some other configs with it next weekend at a summit in Avila Beach, so we'll see.
