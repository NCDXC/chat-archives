I sure agree that these guys are doing a FB job thus far.  Good signals most of the time and the CW ops are first rate.

I have all my needs for Ducie already confirmed so I am working them with 10 W to see if I can get in the log.  So far, 40 thru 10 on CW (but 25 W on 10).  40-17 SSB.
