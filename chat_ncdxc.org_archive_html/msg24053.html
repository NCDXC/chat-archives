Note unusual frequency. There are very few callers.

S9OK is also on 20m and 80m CW (very good ops), and 17m and 40m FT8.

There is a real-time log, with frequencies, at https://clublog.org/livestream/s9ok

The logging is nearly instantaneous. You can see your callsign scroll by within a minute of your QSO, do a log search, and if you wanted to, you could order a QSL via OQSL about a minute after that.
