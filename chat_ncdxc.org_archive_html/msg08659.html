Thank you John.
 
Martti was a most willing and engaging subject. He assumed the role of interviewee just as he would tackle a DXpedition or a massive pileup, with gusto and vigor!
 
I have been hoping to interview all of the DX greats of yesteryear and today. Unfortunately, not all of Martti’s peers are as approachable and as willing.
