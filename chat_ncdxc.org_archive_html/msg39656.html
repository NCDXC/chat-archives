I feel very fortunate too to have worked him on 10, 15, 20, 30, 40 & 80m FT8, 20 and 40m CW.  The 10m opening was short, same with 15m.  I was lucky but luck does play a role in DX hunting too.  I was never able to work him on 17m FT8 and I tried for hours.  I guess I just missed the right time as I heard a couple of w.c. folks but he worked mostly Asia and Europe while I tried and other times I was just not home (darn, I do have a life sometimes).  I did hear him briefly on 15m CW but too much dqrm and police to make an honest try.  I would have loved to have the 17m FT8 as you can never get too much DX, it's a virus with no cure.

 

My understanding on the brief time he worked 15m SSB, he did it by numbers.  I heard MUCH USA calling (up 5) and I don't think he worked a single one of them.  According to statistics he worked 49 that one time 2 others a previous time.
