Good afternoon:

Craig kindly informed me of the upcoming expedition to St. Peter & Paul Rocks. NCDXCers might wish to click on the film clip below and read some readers' comments:

It looks like we have some new DX for the weekend: The PT0S team departed Natal, Brazil setting sail for St. Peter and St. Paul Rocks yesterday around 2000Z.  The trip is expected to take approximately 80 hours, putting them on the rocks late Friday or early Saturday (GMT).
 
73,
- Craig, AE6RR


I replied as follows:

Craig, good morning:

On one of my occasions when I was conducting research in Brazil I  was invited to participate in a similar expedition but in retrospect wisely declined. You cannot imagine more of a trial & tribulation place.
It is just what it is named: the rocks. Hammocks are strong over jutting rocks; there's little flat space to work and there are millions of large & ferocious crabs that crawl over you if you dare rest in your hammock!


Whether I work them or not (incidentally I have " the rocks" confirmed)  I am going to donate just to leave me feeling that I am easing their hardship.

regards.....harry
