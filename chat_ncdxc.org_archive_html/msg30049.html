In case you missed it, after the IARU contest this weekend (1200Z Sat. to 1200Z Sun.), be sure to upload your IARU Contest logs to the ARRL server ASAP, as explained below, especially if you worked any of the WRTC Teams.  We have several locals from Northern California at the event (but spotting only your "friends" is strongly discouraged, as the goal is for the ops to remain anonymous until the competition is over).

List of ops:

https://www.wrtc2022.it/en/competitors-24.asp

The special callsigns they will use are not known at this time, but may be similar I4xx.  They will be announced Friday on the WRTC website and perhaps on CQ-Conetst as well.

The WRTC judges only have about 18 hours after the contest ends on Sunday to check the WRTC logs and produce results.  You can help!

Logs submitted for the IARU Contest will be used by the WRTC2022 Judges for log adjudication.  Submissions received within the first 10 to 12 hours are particularly important.

Your cooperation in submitting your IARU logs to the ARRL immediately following the end of the contest is greatly appreciated by the organizers and competitors.
