When I have these QSOs in LotW that have been sitting there for one or more months, sometimes 5-6 months and DXKeeper indicates they are LotW members, I go to the direct method.

 

Using direct DXKeeper provides some QSL information but I always back that up with a QRZ’d check for current information.  Keep in mind when the QSO was recorded it could be several months ago and the guy might have a new mailing address.

 

Good luck.
