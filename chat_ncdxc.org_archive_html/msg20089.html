To All NCDXC Members:

I want to personally invite you to a special Webinar:  "The VP8PJ DXpedition to South Orkney Islands"

This free webinar, sponsored by the Northern California DX Foundation, is open to all contributors and major clubs who’ve supported NCDXF over the years.  NCDXC is prominent among those clubs.

The VP8PJ webinar will be held online via ZOOM, on Saturday, May 23rd, 2020 at 12:00 Noon PDT = 1900 Zulu.  Team Leaders Gene, K5GS; Dave, K3EL; and Les, W2LK will be the presenters.
 
You can register in advance via this link with your Name, Email Address, and Callsign:
 
https://us02web.zoom.us/webinar/register/WN_OeZnXr7RQc6_vWNak2EUbw
 
After registering, you will receive a confirmation email containing information about joining the webinar.
 
We look forward to hearing about this great adventure from the guys who actually made it happen !

And thanks again to NCDXC for your continuous support.
 
73, 
John Miller, K6MM
President, NCDXF
