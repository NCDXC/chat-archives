On 5/2/19 at 3:54 PM, chat@ncdxc.org (AL_N6TA via Chat) wrote:
Guess I need to get a 2 M rig. Last one was in 1983 and battery died in 1984. 
Anyone have a no-brainer easy handheld or little desktop to recommend?
It's hard to give advice. If you are near enough to the repeater to hit it with a HT, the cheap Chinese handhelds have terrible UIs but will do the job for something on the order of $30. If you also dream of doing EME, a transverter may be the way to go at a lot more $$$. 

I would say that the big differences between radios are:

  * Are they FM only?
  * Can they do weak signal work?
  * Can a human use their UIs?
  * Do they have mis-features that just get in the way?
One of the problems with VHF/UHV radios is that most of the companies that make them also serve the government/business markets and include features designed to lock people into their radios. They try this sh** with the ham market as well.
