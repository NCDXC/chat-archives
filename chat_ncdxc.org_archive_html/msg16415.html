I just managed to pull off a FT8 contact with 9L2JM, but it wasn't easy. His call does not fit in the FT8 specs, so the messages are mostly 13 character free text. Here is how I think I pulled it off:
He is running Fox and Hound so call him from at least 1000 Hz above the frequency listed on the web page.
When he comes back to you, you will not receive a signal report. Instead you will receive something like, "AE6JV 9LY2JM ". (If you have a 4 character call you will get, "xxxx 9LY2JM -".
When you receive this message select the TX 3 message and change your transmit frequency to below 1000 Hz.
Eventually you should receive a RR73 and a prompt to log the QSO. If you don't receive the RR73, I would manually log the QSO and see if it appears on Clublog.
