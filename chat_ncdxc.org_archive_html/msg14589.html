Hi Bill,

As previously noted by Wayne N7NG, the issue has to do with incompatible anti-spam handling protocol between Mailmail and your email domain/server.  This is why the issue only affects certain people and not everyone.  Perhaps you could ask the list administrator to white list your email address and see if this helps.  If you have another email domain you may try switching to the other domain.  I belong to another club in Colorado and we recently had an issue with a particular user.  A couple sharp guys solved the problem however I deleted the email thread that contained the solution.  Please keep me in the loop on your issue.  As a last resort the list administrator can open a help ticket with the Mailman host provider and Scott at QTH.com will be able to assist.  Good luck.
