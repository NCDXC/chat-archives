Hi Frank,

When someone calls on a frequency on top of the DX you are trying to work, it’s probably because the caller did not or could not receive the DX

Here is a suggestion: Tx on the same frequency:
<QRM callsign> QRLQSY
(If the caller is calling you)

Or
<QRM callsign> QRMQSY
If he is calling CQ or someone else.

I only saw this used once but it worked.

 

Thanks,
Gabriel AJ6KX
