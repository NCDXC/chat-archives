I am out in Pescadero helping provide radio support to the Large Animal Evacuation Group. They have evacuated everything from horses to goats to flocks of turkeys. They are extremely well organized. Hams help provide comms between the Large Animal Evac folks and the EOC, and hams go out with the animal trailers to provide communications where cell service is spotty. 

The fires are a few miles away but clearly
visible. Every hour, additional communities are placed under evacuation watch or orders. And yet, the local folks seem reluctant to evacuate. 

Any amateurs interested in providing radio support for these efforts should call Angelo at (631) 880-8416. The Large Animal Evac people will be working late tonight and tomorrow until they are no longer able to do so. They are doing amazing work, and they really appreciate help from hams. 

73 and stay safe,
Andy K6ZP
