If it is any consolation, W1AW/7 was at our noise level too today and yesterday for me but so far I have been able to work W1AW/7 in Oregon on 12/15/17/20/30/40/80 and 160M the last two days but it has really been challenging for me on 10/12/15/17M!  I am not sure who the operator was at W1AW/7 on 12/15/17M (it may have been you) but he or she has my thanks and appreciation. It was a lot harder than I thought it would be from California for both of us to make the contacts on 12/15/17M !  J

 

Thanks for the effort you and the other W1AW/7 operators are putting into this for us from Oregon!!!

 

FWIW, I have yet to hear W1AW/7 well enough above the noise level on 10M on any mode to make a QSO on 10M  but I intend to keep listening whenever I can find them or when  they are spotted.
