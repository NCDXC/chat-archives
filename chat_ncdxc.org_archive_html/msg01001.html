What is your most memorable DX Contact?   What contact sticks in your mind as most memorable to you?

My most memorable DX contact was in the 1980's with a member of our diplomatic corps in Africa.

He was running DX-style until a friend called. While chatting, he bemoaned the fact that he had badly damaged his prized Maherajah slalom ski. [For those of you who aren't into water skiing, the Maherajah skis are top of the line.]

I eventually broke the pileup and mentioned that Maherajah was still in business and could easily repair his ski. Less than a week later the ski showed up at my front door. I related my story to Bob Maher and he 'extended' the warranty for 20 years.

A few weeks later the ski was back in Africa. I still talk on the radio with my new friend.

Steve, K6UM
