Nice work, Paul!

I can't think of a best one, they are all good!

ATNOs for 2021, so far:

ST2NH:  20m FT8
Z81D: 17m FT8
4J3DJ: 30m FT8
AP2HA: 20m FT8
YI3WHR: 20m FT8
S21RC: 20m FT8
SV2RSG/A: 20m CW (OK, maybe this is the best one.)

These brought me up to 316 total, 312 current.

Too many band fills to list, so I won't bother with those.

I achieved 12m DXCC this year, after conditions finally improved a bit and the higher bands opened up.
I hit 250 confirmed on Digital, and am only 2 away from 300 on 20m.

I'm still looking for the ZC4 !

Hoping for a great year for DX in 2022!
