Wow!  What a fun morning at XZ1J!   This morning at 4AM (2100 UTC) we enjoyed a great opening to NA on 20 CW and SSB!   Then shortly after, I enjoyed a great opening on 15 meters SSB all across NA. I was very surprised to have so many callers from the mid-west, I had to ask W6/W7 to stand by so that I could work them.

 

When they faded away it was full speed ahead for the West Coast.  It was really fun to work K6YP, KY6R, W6OPO, N6SJ, AA6XV, K9YC, N6ML, AA6G, K6XN, K8JRK and many others!

 

When 15 meters faded away I went to 12 meters and did it all over again!   When 12 faded away, I went to 10 meters and continued working the West Coast until that band died. 

 

There were a lot of problems with tuners and guys calling out of turn. I'm proud to say that the NCDXC guys that I hear are some of the finest operators on the bands.

 

Tonight, at our sunset, I'll be on 10/12 meters Long Path which favors the East Coast. I'll start doing RTTY in the next day or two but will be limited to 100'watts.

 

Paul N6PSE
