But, heck… in my opinion it seems to correlate well with observed performance during the two DXps. My only disagreement would be with the relevance of AWGN as a predictor of decoding sensitivity in real world conditions… random tuning during the even cycle, pulse noise preventing reception of tones, etc. With noisy conditions, my decodes fell off far more than expected. There was also intentional jamming consisting of a 2.7 KHz wide spectrum of evenly spaced tones that totally wiped out decoding. That’s easy enough to generate and darkly effective. 
I had guessed that SF was roughly equivalent to 2 stream FH, and I think the throughput performance of SF at CY9C speaks for itself.
