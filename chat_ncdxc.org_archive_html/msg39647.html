I share Paul’s misgivings about the predominantly MSHV expedition. That said, I was pleasantly surprised by Yuris’ facility in the CW pileups. I’ve worked him before in years past, and he’s grown. 
I was late to the party, having done a side trip to the Amazon on the way back from HD8G, and never got him on 30 or 20. I’d expectantly wait for the PSK spots to walk over to the West Coast, only to see him QRT while in the Midwest. NCDXF needs to send him some sourdough and Dungeness to remind him of our side of the country. 
 I was really stoked to get the 80m slot with my little HF-9V during what was probably a unique prop opportunity. He was always loud on 40, but the higher band SP openings between 6:45 and 7:30 AM were remarkably brief, focused over a small region, and fleeting. Earlier this week, 17 was SP… today LP. 
I never was able to hear him during his brief foray on 15 SSB.
