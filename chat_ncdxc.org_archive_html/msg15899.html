ARRL Director Elections are Happening Now

It's important to understand that all five ARRL Directors who are currently trying to get re-elected supported not only the unjustified censure of N6AA, but also the ARRL "Code of Conduct", and the disastrous revisions to the Amateur Radio Parity Act (ARPA).  The quiet disqualification of Director candidates K3RF and K4AC in a prior election is also worth reviewing.

Here in California, we don't get a vote this time, but many of our friends in the following divisions have just received an ARRL ballot, and it's important to make them aware of what is at stake:  ARRL Transparency vs. ARRL Confidentiality (e.g more of the same).

Here's a list of the ARRL Divisions having elections right now:

Central:  IL, IN, WI
Incumbent:  Kermit Carlson, W9XA 
Challenger:  Valerie Hotzfeld NV9L

Hudson:  ENY, NLI, NNJ
Incumbent:  Mike Lisenco, N2YBB 
Challenger: Ria Jairam N2RJ

New England:  CT, EMA, ME, NH, RI, VT, WMA
Incumbent:  Tom Frenaye, K1KI 
Challenger:  Fred Hopengarten, K1VR

Northwestern:  AK, EWA, ID, MT, OR, WWA
Incumbent:  Open (Jim Pace, K7CEX is not running for re-election)
Challengers:  Michael Ritz W7VO (recommended)
Bonnie Altus, AB7ZQ (Incumbent vice director)
Horace Hamby, N7DRW

Roanoke:  NC, SC, VA, WVA
Incumbent:  Dr. James Boehner, N2ZZ 
Challenger:  George "Bud" Hippisley W2RU

So what's going on at the ARRL Board?  Please see this excellent letter written by Bruce Perens, K6BP, which I have personally endorsed, as have others:

http://perens.com/static/ARRL/TransparencyOctober2018.html

Who and what was behind the Censure of N6AA?  All of the records and correspondence behind this has recently been made public, and it's now clear that outright expulsion of N6AA from the board was the original intent, and all they needed was a convenient justification that the ARRL could make public, which turned out to be the presentation by N6AA at the ARRL Forum at Visalia in 2016, where Dick simply summarized the new ARRL Code of Conduct for those in attendance.

Please see:

https://www.kkn.net/n6aa/arrlcensure/

Kermit Carlson, W9XA, a Director up for re-election, says he'd vote for the censure again, and you can read his side of the story here, and make up your own mind has to who has more credibility in this matter:

http://w9xa.us/Norton.html

Feel free to forward this to your friends in the divisions listed above.  I hope they will all vote for the challengers, not the incumbents.  This may be the only way to reverse all of these problems we've recently seen at the ARRL:  the ill-conceived Code of Conduct, the unjustified censure of N6AA, the disqualification of Director Candidates, the revised ARPA disaster, the selection and resignation of the ARRL CEO, the dismantling of the National Traffic System (NTS), and more.

It's time for a change.

73,
Bob, N6TV
