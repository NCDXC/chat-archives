Hello NCDXC friends:
 
A few hours ago, we crossed from Wonjon, N. Korea back into China. We are now in Yanji and will fly to Beijing tomorrow. This past 12 days in the DPRK has been quite an adventure. I will have many stories to tell.
 
Things I missed most:
 
1) Cold clean water to drink.
2) Working toilets.
3) Hot showers.
4) Reliable electricity.
5) Ice.
6) Internet/Email.
7) Soap and water for washing hands.
 
Things I won't miss:
 
1)Kim Chee.
2) Squid.
3) Rice
4) Dog meat.
5) Constantly being watched (Minders)
6) Being locked in Hotels.
7) Dust.
8) Bed Bugs.
9) Unpaved roads.
10) Broken down busses.
 
 
Anyway, it was a lot of fun. Tomorrow, Dave AH6HY and I fly from Yanji to Beijing and then I will fly home the following day.
 
All the best from China.
 
Paul N6PSE
