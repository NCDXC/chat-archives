The subject line says it all. I want QSLs for Z81X, and I'm happy to donate to the costs of the trip, but I damn sure don't want to spend money on postage, and Radio Arcala (via Club Log) wants $4 for a QSL. LOTW and eQSL are nearly free. Why do they refuse to use these services?
One of the things I believe that we as a club should be doing is using our influence to urge DXers and contesters to use LOTW. It may not mean much for old timers on the honor roll, but it does for those starting out, for those chasing band slots, and other awards.
