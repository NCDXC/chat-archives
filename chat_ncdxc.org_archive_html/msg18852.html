I'm likely preachin' to da choir here, but don't forget to check your location forecast at the K6TU propagation forecast page:

https://www.k6tu.net/?q=node/add/dx-prediction-vp8-signy

Just enter your grid square and station info.  It does require a free account, but it's a VERY valuable resource so one should anyway (many other useful tools available).

The forecast (for here) shows an excellent chance for 40M through 15M; (the 'money' band being 20M, with openings for 17 hours a day) hinting that 12, 10 and 80M are possible as well.  It does not create a forecast for 160M but looking at the 80M forecast, it has a slight potential, though late in the season here.  The 80/160M band windows will be VERY narrow, based on common periods of darkness, but ya gotta have hope!  The expected openings also mean no all nighters needed (unless you want to).

Today's news from the island indicates they've made safe landfall and are setting up camp; hoping to start on Sunday.  For the time being, the weather is cooperating.

See you on the bands,
Rick NK7I
