I am not a contester any longer but do like to participate a bit as a DXer.  For me, it is fun to compete for ‘rare ones’ in different kinds of pileups.  I also like to see if I can lower the power needed to make a contact with a given entity.  My goal is everything with 10 W but if 10 W won’t make it, I increase to what makes the QSO happen.  This weekend, I was able to lower the power needed to make a QSO on several entities which keeps the skill set oiled up.
