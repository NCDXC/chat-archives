I, too, have paper logged for decades--and will continue for the large benefit of endless margin notes, solar data, new ideas, people met,etc. written on the facing page..When  I got back on the air 18 months ago I decided to focus on the WARC bands--which led me to LOTW as I was looking at hundreds of needed confirms (DXCC and WAZ)and costs and time seemed excessive. That in turn led me to DXLAB also--on Ross Forbes' recommendation and help---the guy who "got it" that I was not interested in being a computer expert. I was able to load the last 15 months very fast and very easily, am set to hook to LOTW for confirms as soon as they settle down and it is modular--probably the best feature. P.S. I have all my qsl-paper/card confirmed contacts for the decades past on DXBASE---all loaded by my stepdaughter.THAT took time but she needed summer work, I needed to see what I had (many pleasant surprises). Last, both DXBASE and DXLAB will also soon be uploaded to
 CLUBLOG. I do not have either program tied to the radio...but I hope to add some spotting sites, but one step at a time. DXBASE will be used only to record paper confirms, DXLAB for all future contacts, CLUB LOG for focus....JIM..
