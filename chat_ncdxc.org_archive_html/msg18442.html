On Jan 1, 2020, at 5:36 AM, Keith Heimbold via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

I think FT8 is a pretty interesting mode but I think a Dxpedition should have the right to decide whether or not to use it. I personally enjoy the fun of the pileup and find spending hours tackling a major DXpedition using CW (mostly these days)


As a veteran of leading DXpeditions, I can tell you the DXpedition team ALWAYS, under all circumstances has the right to decide how they will conduct the DXpedition.

That makes for the diversity of DXpeditions, and why different groups exists and why different groups can visit the same place and product just as many contacts.

Would you refuse to work a DXpedition that didn’t offer FT8?  Of course not.  No one ever boycotts a DXpedition.  You may choose not to donate to the DXpedition - the limit of that is the DXpedition doesn’t make the trip - that is the scenario with the current 3Y0I group.
