I’m running WSJT-X v2.6.1, and when I am in Fox/Hound Mode as a Hound, the only interval available to me is Odd.  I do not find any method of changing the Hound interval to Even within WSJT-X.   The WSJT-X Even interval selection box is greyed out in F/H mode and there does not appear to be any setting that would permit this change.

 

If 3Y0J is using JTDX and is running their Fox transmitting in the Odd interval, there does not appear to be a way for a station using WSJT-X in Hound mode to make a QSO with them – and the greater majority of callers will be running WSJT-X.

 

I’m hoping we can get some clarification of this issue, so we all know what we should be doing going forward, particularly in light of the comments recently from the 3Y0J team regarding callers not using F/H mode.

 

Thanks!

 

- 73 and good DX de Mike, K6MKF, NCDXC Life Member

Just posted on their Facebook page: 

 

3Y0J Bouvet Island 2023 Update:

The 3Y0J Team is approaching the 7,000 QSO mark and continues to operate through the ongoing storm. They had a good night sleep. Last night 30m FT8 received great signals from JA and NA West coast. Unfortunately, some callers were not using Fox/Hound. Their QSOs were not logged. As stated previously, please remember that on FT8 mode 3Y0J will always be Fox/Hound. ￼The weather in Bouvet was sunny skies and winds around 40 knots. We will continue to operate weather permitting. The 3Y0J Team wishes to thank the Amateur Radio community for their support.
