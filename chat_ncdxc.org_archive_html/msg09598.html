Tuesday evening. NO joy.

E30FB was posted sparsely during the afternoon on 7030 but with no copy here and a handful of eastern callers. It was then posted at about 0240Z on 7026; I of course was in the kitchen. It is now 0315 and I have yet to be able to copy a callsign from E3 on a noisy band (S5+) and have made no calls. Meanwhile the pile is larger, growing still larger and very loud, as in >-90 dBm. How often it is that you hear the DX but not the pile, or vice versa! This  time it is vice versa. The tuners and cops are well represented.

OTOH, N6RK with his high MonstIR out in the open near Sacto reports them as "579"--what a difference a MonstIR makes. It seems that is what it takes this evening. Rick seems to own this one. Maybe the LARGE boys can make it.

There are complaints that the op "never signs" but I cannot tell the E3 from the wrong-VFOers. My good old 80/40 wire is not cutting it out here today.

I am looking forward to three upcoming Oceana IOTA groups I need. E30 is not happening yet for me on the low bands.

Garry
