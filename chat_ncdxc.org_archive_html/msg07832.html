This message is intended only for our NCDXC friends. This is not a press release.

Good evening, tonight we returned to Tehran, where the Intrepid-DX Group will conclude our 2014 friendship tour tomorrow.

We enjoyed a nice dinner with Mohsen-EP3SMH and Mohammed-EP3MIR. We enjoyed talking about all aspects of amateur radio.

We also learned that our request for a DXpedition license was nearing final approvals. We also learned that another group was recently issued a license and will announce their plans soon. We talked to them on the phone and we wished them well. The DX Community will be delighted to see a flurry of activity from the Islamic Republic of Iran.

More details to follow,

73 from Tehran.

Paul N6PSE
