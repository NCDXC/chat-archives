Hello fellow NCDXC friends.

I've had a very enjoyable time here in Beijing.  My meetings with our Chinese emissary to finalize our strategy and presentation have gone well.  I also enjoyed a nice meeting with the founders of the Love the DPRK Children. These  fine gentlemen operate Orphanages and food production bakeries in the DPRK.

Last night, I was taken to dinner by Chinese hams and we discussed their own efforts to carry out a DXpedition from the DPRK.

We will have further discussions about aligning and combining our efforts as a means to increase our chances of success. We are delighted to enjoy the friendship of the amateur radio community in China.

In just a few hours, we are flying to Pyongyang where we will present our proposal for a large DXpedition, complete with humanitarian components to representatives of the various Ministries.

Later next week, I will return to Beijing where I will be joined by Dave AH6HY.  We will then tour about the DPRK visiting various sites and evaluating potential DXpedition venues.

Wish us luck!

Paul N6PSE
