Tough question for me, Paul.  No ATNOs, nothing that enabled an award.  Just plain QSOs to fill band slots, all of which were satisfying and some thrilling!

Slots I managed on  80 and 40 slots that have eluded me for decades include:

75 M SSB: A35, ZK3 and H40

40 M SSB: C21, FO/M, T30, ZK3, and VK9C (probably the most satisfying due to conditions and distance)

 

Worked on 80 CW that were nice to get include 6W, 9G, CY9, VK9C

 

Most important QSL received was VP8 South Sandwich from 1992!  Followed by a YT on 10 M CW that would have been tough to work again.

 

QSLs for 2020?  Going to try again to confirm HZ and 9N on 10 M.  Toughies!

 

GL to all as we look for the new ones starting early January!  ZC4 and/or E4, anyone??

73.
