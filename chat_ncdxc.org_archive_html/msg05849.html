As I had done a month ago with the ARRL DX CW contest, I concentrated on 10M with the goal of adding to my band-country count on that band. In about 12 hours, I made 475 QSOs in 83 countries. That gave me one new band-country (Romania) and one new SSB country (Tunisia). Back in the CW contest, I worked 98 countries on 10M, 7 of which were new for 10M.
In addition to picking up new countries, serious contesting can make you a better operator, and you can also learn a lot about propagation. And for some of us, it's fun.
