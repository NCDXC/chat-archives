On Mon, Oct 4, 2021 at 12:59 PM Andrew Calman <acalmanmd@gmail.com> wrote:
Paul,

I made the mistake of thinking that S9OK was working fox/hound yesterday because they had two streams going. But the FT8 plan posted on the DXpedition website makes it clear that they will not be doing any FT8, but will be running two or more streams from approximately the middle of the audio passband (about 1200 Hz). They are listening from 200 to 3000 Hz.

If you make the mistake of setting your software to fox/hound mode, it seems to work anyway, at least it did for me. And the QSO's are posted almost instantaneously to their Clublog livestream and log search. Apparently they can run up to 8 radios simultaneously. Seems like it's going to be an excellent DXpedition!
