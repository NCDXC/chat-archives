Paul:

I have been using a Gap Voyager on 80 and 160 for many years.

It's very good on 80 -- I have made 125 confirmed countries on 80 using that antenna without too much effort.

On 160, the antenna seems to work better on receive than on transmit.  I have only 17 confirmed countries on 160, but in all fairness,
I haven't spent a whole lot of time on that band.

The Voyager uses a capacitive "top hat" on 160 to get the antenna to load on that band.  160 meter guru NI6T says that makes
for less than optimal transmit performance compared with other designs.

If you would like to discuss this in further depth, feel free to call me at my telephone number that is in the roster.

73,  Ed  AJ6V
