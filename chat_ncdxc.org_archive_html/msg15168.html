I should clarify about the surf causing lack of access.

It’s possible that we won’t be able to get onto the island for up to three days when we arrive.  That’s unlikely though.  The boat owner is confident he will get all of our equipment on the island.

There is a high chance we will get on the air as planned.

What is possible - we will be stuck on Baker for up to three days without support from the boat.  We’ve planned for that.  We will have seven days of water on the island, three days of gasoline, and three days of MREs. And of course, we have a bottomless stock of QSOs with us at all times.
