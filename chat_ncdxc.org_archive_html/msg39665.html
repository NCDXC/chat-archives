FT8_DXpedition_Mode

https://search.app/XDEvGE56K2WAvfmT8


It is FT8 but optimized for in demand calls, such as DXps, to allow as many QSOs as possible in a given time period. 
The DX transmits on 1-5 streams simultaneously below 1000 Hz above the “base” frequency. In this case that’s 28078. He’s the Fox. You’re the Hound. You and the rest of the World call ABOVE 1000. Any calls below 1000 are ignored by the software. You keep on calling til you get ‘im. 

I’ve attached the classic monograph by the Nobel prize winner in physics and one of the prime developers of FT8, Joe Taylor K1JT- a real ham hero.
It has everything you need.
