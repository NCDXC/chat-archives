I hope you have or are having a wonderful Thanksgiving.

 

I am the West Coast pilot for 3Y0I, and today they completed cold water rescue training in Cape Town. For more info, please visit:

 

https://bouvetoya.org/

 

and (especially):

 

https://www.rebeldxgroup.com/

 

My job is to take reception reports from you on the West Coast an forward these onto the Lead Pilot. The purpose of this is to inform the team when the West Coast has a path and on what bands. Other contacts (log issues, how they should run the DXpedition, busted calls, etc) should be directed to the right person listed on the Rebel DX web site.

 

I think its amazing that another team is going to make the effort to activate Bouvet in 2018 – especially after 3Y0Z. I only need 2 more to work them all – and Bouvet is one of the two – so you can imagine how excited I am to get this (possible) second chance this year. I also know that Bouvet is not an easy location to get to.

 

I will send messages to this group as soon as I get any news – although I have been promised that the latest news from here on out – plus a ship tracker will be available for you to browse on their Rebel web site.

 

73,

 

Rich
