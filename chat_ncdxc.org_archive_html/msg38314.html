[Multi-posted; apologies if you receive multiple copies; this is important]

ARRL has become increasingly closed, run by a shadow board that makes
smoke-filled room decisions, excluding directors with whom the central
cabal disagrees. They recently disqualified several candidates who want to
promote more transparency, using trumped-up charges that they now want to
enshrine in the Bylaws, along with other draconian policies of questionable
ethical and legal qualification. Attached to this appeal is an analysis by
former ARRL luminary K1VR. I urge you to read it.

The new Bylaws will be subject to vote at a Board meeting in TWO WEEKS.
Please contact your ARRL Director to plead for defeat of this ridiculous,
totally anti-democratic Bylaws revision. Its sole apparent purposes are to
prevent competition for Board seats and to keep the membership in the dark.
As just one example, try this: If these amendments pass, it will be
forbidden for a member in one Division to TALK to a member in another
Division about a pending election, on pain of disqualification of any
candidate discussed. (Of course, only challengers disfavored by the current
Board will be DQ'd, enforcement being solely at the discretion of the
incumbents.)

Some subscribers to the mailing lists I am addressing disagree with me.
Apparently, they think the good ol' boys and girls of the ARRL Shadow Board
know better than the members of the League how it should be run and they
don't want competition in the upcoming Board elections or even input on
League business. Some of them are my friends, so I regret having to join
their opposition. But I feel strongly about this and I urge you to take the
side of transparency and help to elect Directors in coming election cycles
who will bring the League back to the members. If my friends who disagree
with me wish to comment, they surely will.

73,

/Rick N6XI
