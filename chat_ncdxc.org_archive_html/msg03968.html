On 11/4/2013 8:36 AM, Chris Tate - N6WM wrote:

Incidentally I worked them on 80m ssb at 1540!!! Well into our daylight.  Fb.


That's quite normal.  Propagation in that direction is often good for an hour or so around sunrise on 160M, for about an hour after sunrise on 80M, and for two hours on 40M.

Yes, they are quite close to JA.  One very good thing though -- the JA CW allocation is 1810 - 1825 kHz, so a DX operation can keep that part of the pileup under control by where they listen.
