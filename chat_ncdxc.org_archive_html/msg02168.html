When I am in front of my HF rig, the Club repeater is on.  While trying to work the DX, I turn the radio down to reduce distractions in the shack.

There has been many times, while in other parts of my home doing various things, I listen to the club repeater for those DX spots.  The Club Repeater has helped me increase my country count.  Thanks guys.
