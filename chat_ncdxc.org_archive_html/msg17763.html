Remember the nicely done VP8STi to South Sandwich in 2016?  I had a lot of fun making QSOs but never got thru on 15 CW.  SSB, yes, but not CW.  What to do?  Go back to the old logs and find a QSO with VP8SSI from 1992 and try to QSL the 15 CW QSO I made back then.  One of the ops, WA4JQS was contacted and Tony was able to supply a QSL which I received today.  What a guy!
