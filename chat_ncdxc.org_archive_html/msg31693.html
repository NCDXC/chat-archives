If the connectors all appear to be OK, the next thing to consider is the balun at the antenna. RF on the shield of the coax will impact SWR, and especially if the total length of coax happens to be close to a 1/4 or 1/2 wave on the frequency you’re trying to use. The proper balun will prevent RF on the coax. So if you don’t have a balun, get one. If you do, check to make sure it’s the right balun, and that it’s working properly.
