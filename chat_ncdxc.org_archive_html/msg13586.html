On Sun, May 7, 2017 at 1:38 PM, David Fannin <dfannin@sushisoft.com> wrote:
Forwarding an Announcement:

There is a QSO Today meeting on Tuesday, May 9th at 7:30pm to meet with Eric Guth, 4Z1UG, the host of the Podcast). Eric is in town on his way to Dayton.  The information on the meetup is below 

If you don't know who Eric is, or what the QSO Today podcast is, then here is a summary. QSO Today is an outstanding podcast on Ham Radio and its members.  you can find it here: http://www.qsotoday.com/.   Eric is the host for this most interesting show, and does fantastic job of keeping it interesting, technical and fun.  The show is based on his interviews of hams that have contributed to the hobby, including folks like Roy Lewallen, W7EL,  Randy Hall, K6AGE, Bob Wilson, N6TV (from NCCC), Bob Wolbert, K6XX (also from NCCC), Rich Holoch KY6R, and many others. 

According to his website, Eric grew up in SoCal during the 60s, and shares a lot of his experiences during the 60s and 70s growing up as a ham.  He moved to Israel a couple of years ago, where he lives and produces the podcast. 

In any case, all are welcome to attend, there's no cost, and  you just show up..  You'll have to pay for your own pizza and drinks. 

Thanks

Dave KK6DF
