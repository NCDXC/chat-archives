My best by far was working Somalia, 6O7O, for my first ATNO (#314 Mixed) of 2019.  It took me about seven hours of trying mostly on 40m CW with my elevated Butternut HF6V and 900W but finally got him on 40m. It was also super satisfying that it was a CW contact since I am a mediocre at best CW op.  I have tried for years to work Somalia and failed even during the big Italian DXpedition in the summer 2012 (from San Diego). 

Now if I can work the illusive Palestine next month or in February (that I have been chasing for years), then I will get another fairly common DX off my needed list.
