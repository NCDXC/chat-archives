The 70 foot free standing commercial tower of WY6N (SK) in Fairfield is available free to qualified person(s) for take-down and removal.  Bonnie his wife contacted Wendell Peterson, K6POU asking if there was someone who would like to take on the project. 
 
The tower is a 70’ self supporting (AN Wireless? or similar design), over 36” at the base with heavy duty 20 foot sections; it is located next to the house on a standard residential lot.  There are HF beams on the tower and power lines near-by.  The tower was permitted and professionally installed, so the permit information for re-installation should be available from the City of Fairfield/Solano County.
 
If you are interested and qualified (experience and insurance) please call Wendell, K6POU.  Telephone: 925-934-2962 E-mail: k6pou@juno.com
