I’ll be giving this presentation one last time during the Pacificon Antenna Forum:

 

“DXCC Honor Roll in One Solar Cycle: A Little Pistol Adventure”

 

The content has been reviewed by Dean, N6BV for accuracy. It is based on DX-ing and DXCC with plenty of stories relating small lot antennas, terrain assessment – using HFTA, and EZNec antenna modeling. It also includes antenna construction techniques and a few operating hints and tips for “Little Guns”.

 

I will also be dedicating several slides to the Heard Island 2015 Project – and has some interesting possibilities for West Coast DX-ers.

 

If you are a “big gun” – you will at least be amused – and hopefully laugh a bit. Anyway – it would be nice to meet you there – and I should be there at 7 AM – if you are up for a cup of Java. By being there at 7 or 8 AM – you prove that you are a low band morning grey line DX-er!

 

73,
