Good Morning Will,

Expect lots of responses with a lot of different recommendations.

I think one of the best bargains out there is the Mercury 3S or the Mercury Lux.   Take a close look at those two amplifiers.

I’ve got three amplifiers and I love them all - Elecraft KPA 1500, Mercury 3S, and the SPE 1.5 K Mark II.  

I don’t think you can go wrong with any of the above.  

One of the keys is to make sure your entire antenna system is working properly and well maintained. 

Lots of people have had failures due to reasons other than the amplifier, only to blame them on the amplifier. I am one of those people.  It’s a hard lesson to learn.

Good Luck!

Bob
