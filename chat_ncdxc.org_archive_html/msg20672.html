I have a Force 12 EF140S linear loaded 40m dipole removed from a Force 12 C-4 tribander. All parts are present and marked,  and I have the matching balun, as well as new fiberglass insulators for the feedpoint and "trunk" sections. As partially broken down, it's about 10 feet long and weighs less than 10 pounds. All tubing joints were cleaned, Noalox'ed, and re-riveted in 2017: hardware was replaced with SS. IIRC, it's about 37 feet when assembled. 2:1 bandwidth is purported to be 130 KHz.

Andy K6ZP has first dibs on the C-3, but the EF140 is free for the pickup at my QTH near College of San Mateo.
