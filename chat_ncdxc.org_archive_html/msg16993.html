I want to thank John-K6MM, Rich-KE1B and all of the fantastic organizers and donors who organized a superb 70th International DX Convention. 

This was a great event, with many wonderful prizes. Thank you HRO, Elecraft, Icom and many others for the many great prizes. 

Kristen-K6WX did a great job as MC and it was nice to wrap up earlier than normal. 

I was disappointed to learn that next years Convention will be moving back to the Wyndham Hotel (formerly the Holiday Inn) I think that is a step backwards and I fear it will detrimental to attendance and vendor participation. 

I think the SCDXC ought to conduct a poll of their plans and see what kind of reception they receive. 

Thanks again 2019 IDXC Team!

Paul N6PSE
