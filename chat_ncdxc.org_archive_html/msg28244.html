Those of you unable to get a contact, I condole with you.  I've been there many many times (and is one reason I moved away from noise to a place I could build a 'proper' station).  I really do know your pain.

However, I got confirmed on 17M FT8.  I made one FT8 QSO on two other bands as my form of insurance for digital mode (which I've received now) since they didn't upload the logs.  I did not dupe on any band mode, once I logged the entry.  Once they uploaded the CW logs, I was DONE in that mode (NOT my usual style).

[As a kid, when gramma laid out a fresh from the oven batch of cookies, saying I could have ONE, she KNEW that I would take a couple more when she wasn't looking hi hi.  Some things will never change and I doubt anyone will be on Bouvet again in my lifetime.  😉]

I uploaded my 3Y0J adif log and paid the US$15 to get a card for what they confirmed.  I also filed a NIL request for what I am SURE are solid FT8 contacts.  My take is IF enough such FT8 NIL requests are made, they might reconsider their stance.  That may be our ONLY hope, including ATNO for many.

I remain hopeful another upload will occur for more happy operators,

For example:
My 15M FT8 which was ON the correct time slot, IN F/H mode, yet is not appearing in their log (and I've waited for another upload, hoping they used more than one computer and haven't completed upload yet).

I submit (to this list) this segment from ALL.TXT  for comment (I don't save screen shots, I put the bold on the text for simpler viewing, my frequency shift in calling is the 'fake it' mode, tones below 1500 Hz):

It doesn't GET any cleaner, you can see my TX tone change, proving it WAS in F/H mode AND the correct time slot and yet this and another (30M) are not in the log (yet).  And they won't accept submissions for FT8?

Is there another laptop log to upload?  No clue, the lack of information over the entire voyage, is astounding (and I'm NOT putting that on the pilots, they had nothing to work with).  We are COMMUNICATORS, what's the issue?

I claim FOUL!  Landing the gear and really sucky working conditions were clearly not their only issues.

It was a trying place to get to and many things were terrible, we accept that but I'm sorely disappointed in so many other aspects of what should have been a glorious time for all.  While I'm also happy to have the ATNO.

And that's not counting the largest lidfest, DQRM and sheer idiots being seen in many many years.  That topic is for another time.
