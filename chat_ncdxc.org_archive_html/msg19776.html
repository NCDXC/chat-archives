This is unfortunate, as this is the reason I joined the NCDXC chat email group. It was highly recommended to me to have high quality information as to where and more importantly the how (F/H, Split, ext).

I'm new to this group but not new to operating on JT/FT modes, and have been operating them since the beginning of them being used on HF. I have noticed lately that there are a lot of stations that call on their QSO partner's QRG a lot, or calling a station on the wrong time slot. The biggest problem is getting stations to not call on the same QRG as the station they are calling. Unless they specifically say so either on a self spot or sked. I've noted it a lot when I'm calling a directional CQ and another US station calls to me on my QRG overtop of a much weaker station in the area I'm CQing to. I don't mind also working the US station but I tend to blacklist the ones that cause me to bust a QSO with a DX station.

My $0.02 (adjusted for inflation).
Paul, I hope we do hear spots from you again in the future, because you have some really good ears there. I have appreciated the spots the last few days that I've belonged to this group.
