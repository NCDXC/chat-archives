To me what really makes working a DX Expedition so much fun is what George, AA7JV and Tomi, HA7RY do. Every day they post a message and new pictures on their website during the expedition detailing what they have done the previous day and what is going on at their end. It gives me the feeling that I am right there along with them. Like when they had to rebuild and repair their antennas and generators, what they ate, what the weather was, how the band conditions were, etc. This is icing on the cake for me. They are also remarkably good operators and very skilled at low cost expeditions and I like that they specialize on the low bands. They sometimes rent a boat and therefore have no crew. Sometimes I think they are daredevils and do risky things which adds excitement to their adventures. Some expeditions do not let us in on what is going on at all. Like all of a sudden they disappear and we have no idea why.
73, Bob, W6CYX
