From his FB page this hour:

 

"It's been four days since Marek started his traffic. Another more cramped air-conditioned location was found, modifying the initial antenna installation plan with the difficulty of optimizing the choice of locations in view of the structures present... Today Marek has completed the installation of the second Hexbeam SP6CYN, so for the moment there are two Hexbeams, one vertical from 40 to 10m, one vertical 30m, and the beam 5 elements 6m, leaving the 160/80m verticals to be installed shortly... #ft4gl https://ft4gl.blogspot.com/"
