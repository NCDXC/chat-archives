Hey Don;

Yup, I too am now waiting to see if the QSO actually gets into the log. But I'm pretty confident that the op got my call right. 

I was out of town on business last week, so this weekend is the first chance I've had to try to catch them. I've been calling a lot too, but the pileups are -- as expected -- pretty brutal on the weekend.

I have a band scope on the IC-7600 so I was able to see that he was making his way up the band. But since I was running barefoot, I basically used the same tactic you did... went to the first clear spot I could find above the fray. I only called a couple times before he came back to me.

It's nice to have the first one in the log. Once the storm blows through and the wind dies down, I'll put the Tribander back up and then maybe I'll have a shot at working them on a different mode sometime during week.
