I have Yeasu-FT 710 + WSJT-X working well for some time on a "very old PC(circa 2012) ".  I got myself a new "mini-pc" - It's an AMD Ryzen 5 based  mini-pc. The PC works well, drives multiple monitors , audio / net/usb all features  seem to work just fine. 

Windows 11 PC

PC -> FT 710 link is a USB cable . 

After setting up WJST-X direct (no hamlib/omnirig etc) to FT-710 . 

1. Sync Time

2. Audio  set to "USB Audio"  Check the settings on the PC (same codec)  - PC Sound Mixer Level and the Playback Level set to 100 and 0db (same as old pc) . Enhanced Mode - off. 

3. TX mode on FT-710 is USB 

WSJT-X works fine, waterfall & decode is fine. But the TX is not going out. 

FT-710 has "monitor" mode to listen to the audio. This was / still is a clean tone with the old PC.  With the new PC - its very low and "scratchy/intermittent" at a particular level. Increasing or decreasing the "PWR" on WSJT-X will make even the scratchy output go away. 

Regular Audio out on the new PC seems fine ( via HDMI to speaker). 

Anyone know the  function of "USB MOD GAIN" / USB OUT LEVEL setting on Yeasu FT-710 or FTDX-10  ?   How to tune it assuming it's related to the problem I am seeing. 

Is there anything else I can try before calling it "bad hardware"? Thanks for your thoughts.
