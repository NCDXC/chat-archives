If you need a shack to work VP8PJ-South Orkney my shack is available to visitors when I am not at work. I have 1500 watts for CW/SSB and use less for FT8. 

South Orkney is one of those rare/remote entities that is only activated every 10-15 years so you will not want to miss it. 

I think propagation will favor 40 meters early in the morning and at our Sunset and a few hours after. 
30 meters should be good before our sunset.
20 meters should be good late in the afternoon before their sunset. 

Above 20 meters will be gravy!

Good luck everyone!
