I needed Conway on 160 & 80m. I worked both with 100w.

The 75m contact was difficult as I discovered my antenna wasn't working properly. Fortunately, the tuner would load it. Really pleased that the operator stuck with my weak signal.

160m was much like NH8S. There was a long QSB cycle (15 minutes). At the bottom I could not hear 3D2C. I'd start calling before the peak and gave my signal report as the signal started to fade. The NR function on the K3 really made the difference. Did you have separate receive antennas on 160m?

Steve, K6UM
