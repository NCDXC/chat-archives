Some may or may not need them, but I dud for sure - based on all of the different noise sources near my property. Here are two Blog entries that detail my story - and how effective Jim, K9YC's chokes were:

http://ky6r.wordpress.com/2013/11/09/common-mode-chokes-work/

Here is how I eliminated the lions share of the noise by phasing two RX antennas through an MFJ - 1025 (a suggestion by Wayne, N7NG):

http://ky6r.wordpress.com/2013/11/07/dropped-160m-noise-from-s9-to-s2/

In total, I have dropped my noise something like 22 dBM - while retaining enough signal to work very far stations on 160M - i.e. Namibia, China, Amsterdam, etc.

You will also see how I use the P3 to monitor an tweak things to lower the noise.
