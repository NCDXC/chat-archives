Past night two tents and most of the yagis were destroyed

by high winds. Both of their main tents collapsed partially and the

team worked very hard through the night to save them.

 

The Braveheart recorded winds of 70 mph (112 Kph). They are

running only on 40 meters due antenna damage.

 

Once they can assess damage they can determine whether they

can proceed to South Georgia or not.

 

Right now they are doing last QSO as VP8STI due they have asked

the Braveheart to extract them when it´s possible.

 

They regret that they have fallen short of their plans, however

the team and crew safety is most important.
