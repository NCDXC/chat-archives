Stu,

 

SmartMeter - No problem.

 

I have had a SmartMeter on my home for around two years or so.  I have sniffed around the thing looking at the spectrum and could not find any RF energy.

 

You being a networks guy probably know about its “mesh” network.  When it is polled its response must be at an exceptionally low level.  After all it may only need to talk to my neighbor’s meter which in my subdivision is only about 100 feet away.

 

The meter works fine.  I like being able to see direct reading of instantaneous KW and other data as it cycles through its display.

 

You should not have any reservations about having a SmartMeter installed.

 

73,
