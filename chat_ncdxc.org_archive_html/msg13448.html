DXK is kept current with LotW by the Sync LotW QSOs and Sync LotW QSLs on the DXK QSL/LotW tab.

 

I consider my local DXK log as the “master log”.  I have DXK configured to automatically upload to Club Log as each QSO is logged into DXK.  But, for LotW, I have to explicitly click a button or two in DXK to upload QSOs.  It would be nice for that to happen automatically like it does for Club Log.  But AA6YQ explicitly does not provide that feature, saying it would overload LotW in some situations, e.g., a high rate contest operation.  LotW is designed for batch uploading, although a single QSO batch is fine as long as the batch rate isn’t too high.

 

With Club Log’s announcement yesterday of their LotW integration, I was hoping (in vain, it turns out) that might provide the automatic upload to LotW.  It doesn’t have that feature for the same reason that LotW is a batch upload design.  Seems to me that providing a feature (in either DXK or Club Log) that automatically uploaded all new QSO data on a schedule, say once a day, or even once an hour, would satisfy the LotW batch requirement and still give us an automatic upload feature.

 

Ed W0YK

You guys are ahead of me because I haven’t actually tried it yet, but it “seems” like a very nice upgrade.

 

DXKeeper allows automatic uploads to ClubLog every time a QSO is logged in DXKeeper, if you configure it to do so.  But, there is, by design, no way to automatically upload to LotW.  (Dave says that would be too much of a load on LotW.)

 

However, with this new ClubLog-LotW feature, ClubLog can automatically upload to LotW, so it should no longer be necessary to manually upload from DXKeeper to LotW.  If I understand it correctly, this is a nice benefit.
