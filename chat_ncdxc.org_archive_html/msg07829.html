Effective until further notice, some website services are or will be unavailable as maintenance work proceeds. Users of Ham Radio Deluxe versions prior to version 6.0 will be unable to upload or download records to LoTW. This outage will also affect SwissLog users and any other logging applications that interact with the ARRL p1k servers. The Online DXCC service, http://p1k.arrl.org/onlinedxcc, will also be offline until further notice. All other ARRL website functions remain operational.
