Discussion Question for the Brain Trusts and Experienced Elmers:
(your responses REALLY will be implemented as best as possible within the next two years, and all responses appreciated) If you wish to respond privately, that is fine. See email address at end.
Let your imagination run wild, and suggest the best station you can imagine...you aren't paying for it!
Picture this: you've got a plot of land - an acre or so or maybe 5 - level - about 30 feet above sea level. No HOA, no covenents, no restrictions (well, some: not to impede airplane traffic!). In W7 land, facing Asia. Low local noise level. Medium budget (not like the 300 meter towers of some stations in OH land!). What would you select for a DXer/contest station? FYI: CW + MARS frequencies. SRI - not an SSB op hr. 73.
Incentive for answers: maybe I'll be part of your contest club after this is implemented@! 

Desired:
 160M to 6M, low SWR
 2M local repeater capability
 high front-to-rear gain
 contesting plus MARS EmComm capabilities
 low maintenance tower over time

Consider and suggest:
 tower(s)  (guyed or not, height, manufacturer, )
 possibly a radial(s) into salt water...
 antennas (stacked, slopers, inverted Vs, beverages. ...)
 rotator (max wind load, control at shack, stop loss control, ...)
 control equipment
 builder (KL7WV or ?)
 software (DXlab, so2r, ...)
These will be part of a K3-P3-KPA500 (so2r) station. These components are non-negotiable (already present).
Discussions appreciated. Maybe some lunches at local W6 restaurants for detailed sharing at my expense?
