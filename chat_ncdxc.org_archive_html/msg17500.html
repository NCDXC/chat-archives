The 40 foot tower has a 2 element SteppIR.  The
height comparisons were done on 20 through 10 meters,
taking into account the reduced gain of the 2
element.  I don't have any direct A/B comparisons
on 30 and 40.  But 40 feet is simply too low
for 30 and 40 meters anyway.  If anything, the
advantage of the big tower would be even more
lopsided on those bands.
