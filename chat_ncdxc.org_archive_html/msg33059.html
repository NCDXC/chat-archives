Rick, I want to know the answers to your questions as well.  What are they going to hide and what do the board members have to personally gain?

I was annoyed when they decided to increase the dues AND eliminate paper magazines.  I personally do not find hardly any value in QST the way they used to have real technical articles but I also subscribe to QEX.  The price of QEX has become unreasonable considering that there are literally half the number of articles per issue and there are only four issues per year. I am an old timer who prefers reading printed technical articles rather than on a screen so I objected to the elimination of send just four paper issues per year to me.

I do not think that the ARRL has transparently presented its complete budget report to us, the members, with an explanation of why it is getting too costly for them to operate without booth a dues increase and also elimination of mailed magazines. Yet these decisions were forced upon us. And regarding the secrecy policy, I think they are trying to hide other pending decisions as well.  I am seriously worried that they intend to "sell" our microwave bands including 10GHz to commercial interests.
