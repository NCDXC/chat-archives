(https://www.optibeam.net/index.php?article_id=1&clang=1). It assembles like a precision machine. I especially like the idea of a SQUARE boom (with appropriate square U-bolts), so there is never a problem making the elements all lie in the same plane. 



The early MonstIR's (with 70 foot elements) had a problem with the
elements rocking back and forth and enlarging the holes.  They issued
a retrofit kit consisting of a drill bit and a special interference
fit bolts.  I used the bit to slightly enlarge the existing holes and
then forced the bolts through the boom and the element mounting
brackets.  I had not put up my MonstIR when I got the kit.  I
installed it as per instructions 15 years ago, and the elements haven't
budged in all that time.
