Please don’t take this personal or in any bad way, but why is this so objectionable? 

I don’t complain about other DXers who get to Honor Roll the easy way by erecting 120 foot towers with stacked 7 element 20M Yagis. 

Why not by the same token add the categories of Superstation, LuckyNoHOA and SuckerWithBedspring?

What matters is where the transmitter and receiver are located. It’s in the DXCC rules, and none are broken. Now, if I were to have a remote station in Germany and then claim the QSO’s for my NA DXCC - that would be cheating. 

How long is the cable to your CW key from the radio? More than 18 inches should be in the “remote” category :-)

Prosit!
