What entity has been most elusive for you?   Which one has been active but you just have not been able to get it in your logs, or you have done so with great devotion and patience?
 
 
For me, a number of them come to mind. SU was very elusive until Vlad-SU9VB went there and I was able to make multiple band contacts with him on CW. Mt. Athos was very elusive for a long time.
