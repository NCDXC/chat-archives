Just to clarify one point.

Years ago you could be the Trustee for several different club stations -- or hold several different Trustee call signs for one club.  

Three years ago, that all changed.  To prevent mass harvesting of call signs to benefit just one club or organization, FCC Part 97 now specifically states:

"Effective February 14, 2011, no club station license grants will be issued to a licensee who is shown as the license trustee on an existing club station license grant."

http://www.arrl.org/part-97-text

Translation:  You can only be Trustee for one club call now. 

Trustees with multiple calls signs, like Ted (K6XN) have correctly been grandfathered.  The 2011 ruling only applies to new Club Call applications.

From what I've seen thus far, Ted has chosen to use his trustee position to benefit others positively in many different aspects of this hobby.

73,
John, K6MM
