Gentlemen,

If I may, as a concerned radio enthusiast of these topics... 

It's reassuring to learn that Paul's (or maybe other players too) comments are considered in the inner sanctum.  It's however alarming to see the tone of the reaction signed by an obviously moody Director of the NCDXC and maybe more so the seeming lack of creativity and openness to refining and evolving a process to be the best we can be.  Can this be the sentiment of this group?  Does this director represent the leadership here?

Exhibit A:  

"BTW, I am not aware of a single club member besides you who has contracted the President, the VP, Secretary or the Board and voiced his/her disappointment about not having the DXer of the year award. You are the only one."


This is not shining a light on this director alone but to those who sit by quietly allowing the flogging of one who wishes to question the norm and just maybe incite creativity and potency into our ranks; in my opinion the opposite of oppression, which surely is the "old way," 

Where is the system to handle the hard questions?  Where is the intelligent approach to maintaining world class status?  What do we do with real talent and energy that may seem misguided?  Assuming we are wanting to evolve a vision of our own.

Reaction?  :-)

Just my 2 cents.
