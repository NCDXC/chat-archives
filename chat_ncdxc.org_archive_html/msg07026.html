I have bought several refurbished Dell OptiPlex machines (740, 755, etc.)
from Action Computer, they are in the same center as HRO Sunnyvale.  The
machines have the hard drive reformatted and the OS installed and have
serial ports, they even have a parallel printer port if you need one.  They
are small (about the size of a big dictionary) and are handy in the ham
shack as they don't take up much room.  They have Win XP Pro installed.  I
recently bought another one, expanded the memory to 4 Gigs and installed Win
7 Pro on it with no problem.
