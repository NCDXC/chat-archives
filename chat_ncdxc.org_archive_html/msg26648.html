"Delete" already has a specific meaning within the DXCC program. How about "inactivate"?
It would still be an entity, just not needed to qualify for DXCC, Honor Roll or Top of Honor Roll. If it became active again, e.g. the P5 government officially allows licensed operations, then it is back in the count.
Could apply to entities where radio is banned, landing permits are not being granted, etc.
