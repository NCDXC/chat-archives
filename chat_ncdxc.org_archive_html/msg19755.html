The end of an era. 

If you have a tower and want a TiltPlate or KarrLock you must move quickly (there are some items in their last production).  So far, a new 'owner' of the line hasn't appeared.

It is a little pricey but over time, far cheaper than a crane or hoist when you need to do tower/antenna work.

So far mine has survived events of 45 MPH without issue (the DB36 is a BIG antenna), yet tilts as designed when lowering the tower.
