FWIW, I agree 100% with Garry’s last post here, and also with Paul, Rich and others.

 

For those of you who still worry..here is a happy thought for you young farts under 71….  many  (almost all?)  of we old farts who are already on HR#1 will probably not make it through another solar cycle and we will become SKs and dropped from the Honor Roll listings thereby clearing the way in any event for young farts to automatically ascend to the top spots.

 

Some of we old farts remember the way it used to be in the old days (long ago when we were young farts back in the 60’s) when honor roll  number 1 meant being *the one operator in the world* who actually had confirmations from the most deleted plus current countries.  The rest of we DXers usually had to wait for the number 1 operator to become a silent key to even have a shot at the number 1 position. The rest of us just kept working as much DX as we could as often as we could over the years to increase our country totals.

 

Life is good for the Dxer……..especially for you young farts who are not yet even in your  70’s or operated yet over 50 years to achieve HR#1. I say enjoy being alive and having the fun of working DX while you can…..worry less and enjoy more…….  J

 

FWIW, staying alive long enough to achieve your goals IMHO is the most important thing over the years…. And sometimes that is harder to achieve than simply working the DX!  J

 

73, Ted, K6XN, cranky, deteriorating old fart…. But still having fun without worrying about it.
