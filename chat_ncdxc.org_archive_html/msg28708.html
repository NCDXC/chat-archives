Depending on which WSJT build you’re using, when you switch to FH the frequency might revert to 14074. That’s been fixed in the latest build. FWIW the latest version is better at decoding, and has as “hound” button that is faster than going to settings. 

Although you can store as many y frequencies as you like into WSJT, it’s becomes cumbersome after a while. As noted above, just tuning to the published frequency is easiest. Since DX will not hear you in FH mode if you’re under 1000, be sure you set the frequency accurately. In MSHV it makes no difference where you call.
