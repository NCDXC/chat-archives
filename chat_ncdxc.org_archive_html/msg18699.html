Right after I sent the last note, two things happened:  The noise floor FELL 3 dB (128 dBm) and they were once again good print (right as they went JA only) PLUS their signal strength peaked (~9:20-9:40 Pac time). Clean error free copy.

But with a long series of CQ when they went back to all comers, it was apparent the a fear has been realized; they have serious local noise. Numerous calling, still CQing.  They couldn't see the callers.

So, it seems possible, but it won't be simple.  Again, FT8 is likely the best chance, it was designed for these conditions (but I prefer CW when possible).

They're still there but the copy is now marginal and other non-ham band users are speaking up, their IMD makes copy worse.

I'll continue to listen, who know what that band will provide (S_L_O_W QSB?).  It's more unique than others.
