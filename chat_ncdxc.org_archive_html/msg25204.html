DX Summit (perhaps the premier cluster site in the world) has adopted a policy of NOT posting spots and messages from Ukraine and the invading countries of Russia, Belarus, Donetsk, etc. Kaliningrad Oblast is the westernmost part of Russia.

Perhaps we, as hams, should avoid encouraging warring nations by similarly not posting spots for these regions. Whether an individual chooses to make contact with amateurs in those regions is a personal choice (IMO), but spots are an encouragement to make such contact.

I personally will not make contact with amateurs from countries that choose to initiate war and invade democratically elected sovereign nations. I know that it’s not the amateurs who are causing this, but every little bit of pressure helps to force change from within.
