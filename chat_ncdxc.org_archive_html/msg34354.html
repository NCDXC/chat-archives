FT4GL - Glorieuses 2024

We are pleased to announce to the international community, the agreement of the
French Southern and Antarctic Lands, the future amateur radio activity which will
take place from May 24 to June 19, 2024 on the island of Grande Glorieuse with the callsign FT4GL.
This operation carried out for more than a year of effort has finally been rewarded,
it will be atypical and difficult, because the Glorieuses are ranked 7th in the DXCC
Most Wanted List, and the amateur radio traffic carried out exclusively by a single radio amateur, Marek FH4VVK in parallel of his professional activity.

We will inform you regularly about the progress of preparations.
