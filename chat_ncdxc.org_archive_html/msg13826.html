Once again, it’s time for the super, giant, big-time Ham Radio Auction from the San Lorenzo Valley ARC!

That’s right, the SLVARC is getting ready for its annual auction to raise funds for club activities. Lots of you have gotten some bargains at this event, and lots of you have donated great surplus stuff that was just cluttering up your shack. (And we thank you for those donations!)


 This year’s festivities will be held on Friday, October 13th. This gives us time to find, organize, and promote auction inventory. As always, I will prepare a catalog and make it publicly available. The intent is to give as much advance notice of items to be auctioned, including pictures, so that we can entice those folks with real green to come and part with some of it. I already have some great stuff donated, and there will be much more to come.

To that end, I ask all of you to:

(1) Go through your garage, shed, attic, and closets and see if there is anything USEFUL, preferably RADIO OR TECHNOLOGY RELATED that you would like to donate to the club for auction. Non-technology items are also welcome, within reason. (Read: Here’s that opportunity you have been waiting for, to “re-gift” those Christmas presents you really didn’t like. No velvet Elvis portraits, please.) 

All items must be outright gifts; no fee-splitting or consignment arrangements accepted. Kickbacks for the auctioneer can be discussed off-line. ;^)

(2) Email me a photo along with a brief description of each item. If you are concerned about donating something, but want to make sure that the club really gets the proper value for it, send me your best estimate of:

     (a) What the item is worth (check eBay or the like for recent sales of similar items if you really have no idea), and

     (b) What is the minimum amount that you want the club to get for the item (i.e., the price below which you wouldn't have donated it).

I can place a "reserve" price on such items, and the item will not be sold for less than some minimum, reasonable value.

All items will be sold for at least the minimum amount you specify, or they will be returned to the donor. If you don't care about a minimum bid, it will be auctioned for whatever we can get. Certain items that have broader appeal *outside* the club may be auctioned on eBay.

If you don't have easy access to a digital camera, send me a link to a "stock" photo and description of the item. (At worst, drop off the item at my QTH and I’ll take a picture of it.)

The "Quality Review Board" (yours truly) will decide whether donated items are better classified as "eWaste", and therefore not suitable for auction.

Please get your photos and descriptions to me as soon as possible, but NOT LATER THAN WEDNESDAY OCTOBER 4th for inclusion in the auction catalog. Earlier is better, as I will be updating the catalog regularly between now and the auction date.

Any questions, please contact me directly at

mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com.


 Please do NOT send emails to the reflector.

I repeat, please DO NOT SEND AUCTION ITEM EMAILS TO THE REFLECTOR. DO NOT HIT “REPLY" TO THIS MESSAGE!!!!

Should I say that again?
