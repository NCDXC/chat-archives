Paul-

 

I think I first heard Seymour back in the mid-1970’s, when I had my Cubex quad mounted on its first Douglas fir in the mountains here.  Seymour would be chatting with VU’s on 20M SSB short path in the mornings when I worked swing shift at Farinon Electric.  He seemed to have armchair copy on these guys when I could barely hear them.  And of course we all remember his phonetics ending with “Pa-Paah”, just the way Grace Kelly pronounced it when she spoke to her father in the movie High Society!

 

RIP Seymour.

 

73,

 

Steve

4X6TT is reporting on Facebook that Seymour-W6CCP has gone SK as confirmed by his daughter Paula.

 

I recall fondly many a morning listening to Seymour on 20 meters SSB calling for DX. He had an amazing signal and literally created his own propagation. 

 

He was very fond of working the Long Path into Europe and Africa. It seems like I just heard him a few days ago on 20 meters SSB working a bunch of ZS stations. 

 

RIP old man, 

 

Paul N6PSE
