I have a 106' tower with a full size 3 element Yagi on 40 meters,
but I never got good enough copy to call 1A0C tonight.   This
same beam was great in AA running all night long.  There were
a couple of W6 spots, FWIW. I listened from before my SS until 45 minutes after their SR, and there was no discernible SS or SR peak. They were always 5 or 10 dB too weak to copy through the static crashes. 
On occasion QSB peaks, I could copy their call or CQ or TU or 5NN, etc,
but nothing actionable.

Congratulations to Paul for working them last night.
