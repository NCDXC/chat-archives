On 5/8/2014 3:29 PM, Rob via Chat wrote:
Yahoo email service has recently instituted its new DMARC policy in an effort to help fight spam. As side effect of this new policy is that it may also block emails that are sent out from email discussion lists just like the NCDXC chat reflector and in essence, you will not see the chat posts. If interested, there is a more detailed explanation at this link -> http://www.spamresource.com/2014/04/up-in-arms-about-yahoos-dmarc-policy.html To help alleviate this new restriction we have enabled an option in the chat email system that will replace the sender's email address with the ncdxc.org address. For example:
"First Last" <user@yahoo.com> - will now be "First Last via NCDXC Chat" <listname@ncdxc.org>
The impact of this change to all users will be transparent since you will still receive email responses back to your original email address. This notice is being sent to answer any potential questions regarding the change to the email address header in chat posts.
