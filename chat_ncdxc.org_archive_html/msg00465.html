Easy - I just did this

65' Tower - Rohn 55 - DB36 on top (Big antenna!)
K7NV Prop Pitch System "Small" (B-24 ?) Prop Pitch rotor and Green Heron
Prop Pitch Controller (modified RT-21 or -20) available from K7NV.  Fully
integrates with Writelog and other computer programs.  Point and Shoot.

The current Hy-Gain stuff uses unreliable pots to determine azimuth
measurement.  Older Hy-Gain products worked better.  If you want one, get an
old one and have it rebuilt by CATS in Ohio.   Anyway I think the T2X is
rated around 20SF - more than that and you are likely to tear it up fairly
quickly.  The locking mechanism isn't all that strong.

Next choice probably an M2 Orion 2800 with Green Heron box.  Very strong
locking mechanism.  Brackets are prone to slipping in high winds.

Green Heron boxes are good not only because they integrate with your logging
program, they also have a knob where you can select a bearing and then
forget about the rotor and it will get there eventually.  Nice when you are
busy with other things.
