Generous Volunteers,

 

Keith and I were at the tower today to inspect the lowering cable and winch.   The lowering cable is in good shape, but we decided that the winch needed replacing.   Keith and I removed the old rusty winch, unwound and inspected the cable, installed the new winch and wound most of the cable on the new winch drum.   There’s still some winding to do, but I got tired ( I’m an old guy. )

 

If any of you have a couple of sawhorses you could bring, they would be most helpful with disassembling the yagi – thanks!

 

We finished up about 2PM today and I was sweating, so I recommend that you:

 

  • Bring work gloves if you have them.
  • Wear sunglasses, sunscreen, hats, and whatever sun protection you normally use for outdoors activities.  The only shade is in the garage.
  • Bring water or beverages to keep yourself hydrated.
  • Bring a folding chair so you have a place to sit when taking a break.

 

My wife Sue will be bringing us sandwiches and sodas around noon,  and I will let her know how many when I have an accurate team headcount tomorrow.
