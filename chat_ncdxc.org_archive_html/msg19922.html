There a many reasons that turn off you mentioned could happen.  A far more study of conditions in the radio and attached to the radio has to be made to have any hope of identifying the source.

 

The self-regulating power supplies in these radios have a lot of capacity to deal with input mains voltage changes.  The same goes for external 12VDC supplies especially the switching type.  They can run from 90/100 to 250VAC without user intervention.
