On 11/1/2013 1:09 PM, Rich Holoch wrote:
1) How long did it take you

3-4 years.  After six seasons, I have 133  worked.

2) What antenna(s) and power output were you running

86 ft Tee vertical, lots of radials on the ground, Beverages, legal limit. 

3) Urban or Rural environment

Rural -- Santa Cruz Mountains

4) What is your favorite band (if you have one)

While I enjoy the challenge of 160M, it's not nearly as much fun here as it is around the Atlantic basin, where there are many more active hams and many more countries within relatively easy range. For that reason, 160M is not my favorite band. 

I enjoy all the HF bands, more CW than SSB or RTTY, and also 6M.

73, Jim K9YC
