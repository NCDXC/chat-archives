Right at the moment she is running with an offset of 456 Hz two-stream, and at the same time 5X3K is also nearby at 1000 Hz (so says HamSpots - I can't see/hear him). I don't know how to tell the difference between someone running F/H vs MSHV, so I took a chance and transmitted at 750 Hz: above her Xmit offset, but below 5X3K - figuring that anything above 1000 Hz would just be a cacophony.

And it worked! Since a true Fox theoretically wouldn't hear anyone below 1000, I'm assuming that means she's MSHV (?). So if you're trying to reach her (and not 5X3K) right now, that might be a tactic to try.
