There are some that hold that 1000D in some type of reverence. I remember Seymour W6CCP in San Diego county used one with his giant antenna farm. I remember he talked very fondly about that radio.

Also my friend Patrice FK8HA in New Caledonia is a pretty big fan of the FT1000D. He asked me to source parts to repair his and at one time asked me to look into shipping one to him that was for sale in the US. He specially did not have any interest in the MP model. 

I guess a lot of radios have their Koolaid fan club. I was always fond of the FT920 especially on 6m (it has supposedly favorable sensitivity on that band) and of course have owned quite a few K3 radios until I switched over to Apache Labs (I don’t take that radio anywhere, it stays put in my shack)).
