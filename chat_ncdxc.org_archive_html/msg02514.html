This started off with only a couple but grew as I typed. 

Great ears, of course. 

Consistent, reliable flow of Q's, provide hope of contact. 

Random pattern of the frequency used within the TX window (up 5-10 somewhere). (Otherwise the hoard simply follows along, self defeating.)

Patience by the barrel.
 
Ability to politely control the hoards and exercised OFTEN. 

Listens for the lil pistols; tries to be balanced (in zones worked too).

Being fully aware of band conditions, but watching for places that aren't booming in (east coast or Europe walls) yet are still open. 

Team attributes:
Informative: if the local environment sucks (wind, rain, physically challenging) then let the world know so (some) will ease off a bit.  It also allows some empathy for the team.  You're there for us, make us part of the experience. 

Uploading logs frequently to active web sites to minimize 'insurance dupes', lowering the pressure; allowing more new ATNO Q's.  (good in theory at least).
