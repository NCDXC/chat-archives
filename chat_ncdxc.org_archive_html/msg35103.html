There's been a lot of speculation here, and few facts. Blame has been assigned -- with very few facts.

The announcement on www.arrl.org does not give any details, and is largely unhelpful to us or them:

" we are in the process of responding to a serious incident involving access to our network and headquarters-based systems. Several services, such as Logbook of The World® and the ARRL Learning Center, are affected. Please know that restoring access is our highest priority, and we are expeditiously working with outside industry experts to address the issue. We appreciate your patience."

"Incident" is a bad word in a cybersecurity context.

Hopefully the problem is not one of cybersecurity, but a garden variety network or power failure.  Time will tell.   The timing is unfortunate with Hamvention going on.  I expect there is a skeleton crew at ARRL HQ -- if anybody at all.

Jeff n1kdo
