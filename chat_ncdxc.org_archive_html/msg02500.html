Bill

Your mast has suffered some damage in a very high stress area, I suggest that you move the mast plate down far enough so that the damaged area is completely above the mast plate.  Also you may have some corrosion in the lower left corner of the mast plate as viewed in the picture, another high stress area that if it fails will probably make junk of the antenna.  Replace the plate if there is corrosion and paint it with appropriate primer followed by a good paint.

73,

de Jim W6EB
