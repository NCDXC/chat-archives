I feel that the 5U5R DXpedition was superb!  The operators were highly skilled and they used excellent strategy to work the bands. They appear to have been on the right bands at the right time for a lot of DXers. I heard them very well for five of their nights on 80/160. 

They made 75,327 contacts and almost 2200 of those were on 160 which is a very difficult feat and demonstrated the level of energy and strategy that they put into their operation.

It was really fun to work the "DX Friends" from Niger. 

Great job!
