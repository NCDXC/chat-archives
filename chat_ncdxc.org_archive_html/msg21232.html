Well, last December I made a 30 M QSO to EU that seemed way to easy.  My OM-partner was really strong and very Q5.  I ended up making a solid QSO with 10 W into my SteppIr dipole at 60'.  Card received and am happy.
Meanwhile, I got a note from the OM:
Al,
OK...my pleasure to you got OM land on 30 meters...I using OM4000 linear its 4kw out and 2el HB9CV beam on 30m...I want making WAS on 30m CW..Now i have confirmed all US states on 30m...OK..I will be now hunting Orknezs island..Hope to got him all bands..HI..
All the best and good health to you and family..

Maybe this explains why he had a great signal!

Later in December, I managed a 80 CW QSO with the same guy and got the card this last week.  I hope the conditions from last year repeat again this year.  Not so good yet, but still hopeful.

73,
