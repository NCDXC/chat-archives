On 8/15/2014 7:30 AM, Bob Lanning via Chat wrote:
So when considering an amplifier and its tube compliment – what is the truth?
K6XX gave a great presentation on the "clean signals" aspects of this to NCCC this past winter. It's on my website. k9yc.com/publish/htm
Two things that Bob observed are that tube amps are cleaner than solid state for the same power/budget, and that an amp is cleaner at less than its rated output level. By "cleaner" he means less IMD, which means less clicks and splatter.
I was discussing this at the picnic with a couple of guys who had designed power amps on their job, and they went farther, to say that some devices have a "sweet spot" where it will be cleaner.
