True.  That's why it is advisable to power-cycle the K3 with the power button after you've got it setup the way you want it.  Think of it as "save configuration".  Especially if you are likely to have a power failure.
 
Or, use a UPS.

Bob W6OPO noted:   
 

But with a “power cycle” as in power failure common on DXpeditions, the K3 cannot do any housekeeping before shutdown.  It is immediate lights-out.  That is why Elecraft suggest to only turn the radio off with its power button, not the external power supply.  If the power supply is used as the master ON/OFF switch then the last settings are not stored.
