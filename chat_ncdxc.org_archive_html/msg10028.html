John, Kevin and Rich,

 

               I’d be remiss if I didn’t write to tell you guys what a great job you did for this year’s IDXC.  I’ve been going to Fresno and later Visalia for some 40 years and I think this was the best of all of them.  I’m sure there were some glitches but from an observer’s viewpoint the convention went off without a hitch.  There seemed to be a “mood” this year that made everyone friendly and glad to see their old friends—which I think is one of the main aspects of the convention.  I hope you guys found the time to enjoy the convention—and I also hope that two years from now you’ll agree to do it again.

 

               Congratulations on a superb job!

 

               73 Kip W6SZN
