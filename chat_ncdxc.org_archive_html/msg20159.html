Check out FISTS – they have slow-speed activities:  https://www.fistsna.org/operating.html

 

Google up ‘slow speed nets’ – they are a bunch.

 

7125 is an old favorite frequency of mine.  In fact, I still have my 7125 crystal from my novice days that I used to work my first DX – Scotland!
