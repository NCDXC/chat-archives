I tried the Heil noise canceling headset once.  I tried 2 different sets and
they both picked up RF into the phones (one was in both ears, the other only
in one).  I even tried ferrite cores on the cord with no good results.  As I
don't like to listen to my self squawking SSB, I returned them to HRO.  The
large Heil phones that completely cover the ear work best for me.

I suggest searching amazon on "noise cancelling headphones" and consider
those listed from Audio Technica, Bose, Sony, and Sennheiser that clearly
say "active noise cancelling." I just did that, and the Audio-Technica
ATH-ANC7B, ATH-ANC29,  and ATH-ANC9, Sony MDR10RNC, Sennheiser PXC450 and
PXC250 II, and the various Bose models.

This is one of those purchases that you would really like to try on (and try
out for RFI), so it might be worthwhile chasing down a brick and mortar
dealer.
