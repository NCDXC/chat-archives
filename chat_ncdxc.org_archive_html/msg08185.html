If yesterday is an example, you have a shot at them on 20 in the late afternoon.  They were on RTTY but for some reason were not great copy here (even at S3 above noise).  I'm going to try different software for decoding to see if I can do better, HRD just isn't decoding well lately, any mode.

Their Q:minute rate wasn't all that high, I suspect they're getting slammed making copy really tough (in ANY mode).  They continue to work the (ever expanding) upper edge of the pile up, which is um, different and tends to prove my theory about their situation.  An after action (or current) report from them would be interesting.

This morning I was at the console early again trying for Tromelin on more bands.  They were workable on 40 (already confirmed in LOTW) but much weaker than yesterday and they continued to stay on the higher bands (working EU).  I suspect conditions simply weren't as good as yesterday, but it is heartening that they're trying gray line again.  That is the best shot for lil pistols (one spot even had a EU station whining about NA again, amusing).  

Hurry hurry, be done before the weekend when the bands will be more crowded (I don't think that this group will EVER thin the crowds enough for cap gun stations to work them).

Has anyone West Coast worked them on 160 or 80?   

On 17M this morning, they were *just* above my noise floor, on phone; WAY too low to even try.

I saw spots for the VU4 as well, no joy here; the hunt continues...
