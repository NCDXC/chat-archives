Hello NCDXC friends, 

 

The CQ WW SSB DX Contest just ended. Did you work any good DX?

 

I focused on 160 and 80 meters. 

 

I was able to work V26B, PZ5K, 6Y1LZ, 6Y4T, PJ4K. VY2ZM, ZF9CW, KH7M and KL7RA on 160 meters. Conditions seemed fair. Many of the big Contest stations could be heard much better than they could hear. 

 

My best contact was early this morning working T2AR on 80 meters!

 

I'll try to get 3C1L on 80 or 160 tonight if I can. 

 

Did you get any good DX?

 

Paul N6PSE
