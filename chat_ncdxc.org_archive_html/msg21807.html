This morning I stumbled upon a bunch of South Korean stations at 3.547 MHz FT8. They were working VU2DED and RD4F, whom I could not hear - but the Koreans were more than happy to work me!

Stations heard include HL4CJG, HL2ZN, HL4GHT, DS5TOS, and HL2DBP.

Have other people seen this frequency used for FT8?

73 and good DX,
