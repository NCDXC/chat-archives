Hello NCDXC friends, 

I am eager to improve my 160 meter capabilities and I am seeking a local Top Band Elmer. 

I am currently using an Alpha-Delta 80/160 dipole strung at 60 ft from a tower side arm. This dipole tends to be a bit of a "cloud warmer" however it did work FT5ZM on top band so it isn't all bad. My goal is to improve my station's performance capability on Top Band. 

Before they folded, I ordered a 65' aluminum sectional vertical antenna from Force-12. It has a "turbo tuner" that goes midway up on the antenna. Unfortunately, I got only the antenna from them and no documentation of any kind. This antenna might be salvageable with the proper base etc. 

I may also want to explore shunt feeding my tower in some manner. It is a 72' US Tower HD-72.  I have a less than ideal space for ground radials. In fact, my tower more or less sits in a bowl as there is a steep incline in my back yard. 

I also have some 60' Spiderbeam poles so a stand alone Inverted L would not be difficult. 

For receive antennas I have a DX Engineering RF-Pro-1B active Magnetic Loop antenna that I am about to install. 

If you are interested in visiting and advising me on 160 antenna solutions, please email me directly at pauln6pse@gmail.com

Thanks!

Paul N6PSE
