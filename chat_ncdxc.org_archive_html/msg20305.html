I know it certainly can be ignorance.  I know a guy who just never got the picture.  He would be listening for DX (SSB) and not hear any but he heard the pile up.  He would get in the pile up and call and call and call.  Yet he never heard the DX station yet he would call in the blind cluttering up the pile up.

 

In a personal setting I made it clear to him what he was doing and to knock it off. He felt the DX station would eventually call him.  But all he could hear was the pile up.  If the DX station actually did hear him and replied this guy would never know.  Ignorance.

 

Clearly this guy is monitoring Chat for the FT8 spots.  Address him via Chat and in the text put his call sign tell him what he is doing and the impact he is making.  Include what he should be doing.  Via Chat with his call.  Maybe the embarrassment factor will work.
