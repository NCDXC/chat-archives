On the ARRL web site, Rule 3g concerning
field card checking states:
"The applicant shall provide a SASE addressed to ARRL HQ, or, sufficient postage, to the DXCC Card Checker for mailing applications to ARRL HQ." 

I brought cards to Pacificon and the checker
requested a SASE.  I had not brought one so
I instead offered money for postage.  Which
was declined.  I was told the rules clearly
state a SASE is required.  Hopefully, my application
got mailed to HQ in spite of this dispute.

Am I missing something here?  The paper work for
the whole day could have been put in one large
envelope and mailed to HQ.  As opposed to individual
envelopes for each applicant.

This whole encounter was awkward.
