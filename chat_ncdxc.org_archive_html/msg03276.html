I don't know for a fact, but I sense that they are just running 100 watts to a simple Tri-band Yagi and verticals for the WARC bands.  Their signals have been minuscule
Compared to the JA/YB signals calling them.

Zorro and Champ are excellent operators and I've enjoyed the challenges of working them. I noticed that they are getting very little sleep as they are operating almost around the clock. Zorro is also in Gov't meetings for future humanitarian work.

Zorro has exchanged messages with me and given me an idea of the tremendous effort required to re-activate amateur radio again in Myanmar.  He is a true Ambassador for this great hobby.
