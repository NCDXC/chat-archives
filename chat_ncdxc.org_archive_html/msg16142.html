90 YRS. HORDER HOUSE, LOS ALTOS ESTATE SALE, Fri-Sun,  
Nov 9 - 11
 
HAM RADIO OPERATORS - THIS GARAGE IS PACKED FROM FLOOR TO CEILING WITH HAM RADIO EQUIPMENT - A MUST SEE!!!!!  
 
There is something for everyone!!! One of the most interesting estates I've done - owner was highly intelligent and dabbled in many areas of science, electronics, ham radio operator, prolific reader, collection of
minerals and fossils and skilled woodworker and outdoor photographer and camper.
 
Garage - this is a 2 car garage filled from ceiling to floor all walls and center of the room items filled on workbenches - vintage vacuum tubes and electronics from 1940's to current, microphones, telescopes, testing equipment, vintage radio collection, tons of old Ham Radio equipment, supplies and equipment related to vacuum tubes, old power wood working tools and hand tools, WE ARE NOT EXAGGERATING THE QUANTITY OF OLD EQUIPMENT IN THIS GARAGE!  
    

1000+ books - all subjects shelved ceiling to floor in the entire front room & dining room, hundreds of Life & Look magazines 1930-60's (well preserved), Sci-Fi magazines, lots of old paper goods/ephemera, stamp collection, vintage cigar boxes, original artwork and photographs, old vintage board games, etc.
 
Hundreds of vintage LP albums, camera equipment, multiple large telescopes, etc.
 
American Indian headdress, steer skulls, old blankets, Chimayo pieces, Saltillo serape, 1906 "Gibson" mandolin, etc.
 
WWII U.S. & German Military items - old military trunks, clothing, vintage camping equipment, etc.
 
 
Vintage men & women's clothing -  Vintage Men's clothing - Tewa, Wavers, Native American neckties, Daks, Oshman, Pendleton, Robert Hall, Norweigan ski sweaters, wool hunting pants, vintage flannel shirts, LL Bean, Eddie Bauer, Saville Row, Woolrich, London Fog, hunting and fishing vests.   Vintage Women's clothing - Norweigan & Irish knit sweaters - Kolmer, Charlotte, Samantha O of California, Alfred Dunner, Koret. 
 
Sewing room - packed - office supplies, vintage typewriter!  
 
Vintage Linens - Mid-Century pillows, ethnic wall hangings and linens. 
   
Vintage camping equipment, old metal coolers & jugs, etc.
 
Garden - garden filled with old "Rusty Gold" - wheel barrels, vintage garden tools, large flower pots, saucers, plants, cactus, collection of old garden gnomes, 3 vintage French "Moto Becane" bikes, too much to mention in garden.
      
Kitchen is packed - lots of cookware and Mid-Century items, metal pole lamp. Glassware and bric-a-brac throughout the house, "Borden's" metal cannisters, also costume jewelry.
 
Wheel chairs and walkers (like new). 
 
We have lots of boxes for our book people - you will need them!
 
Coffee & donuts served. Plenty of parking. See you at the sale, Mark
