You're fortunate.  My hard earned 80 meter Q is now missing (was there).  The 30M Q is still not there and I was confident it was a good one.

I won't panic, but something is obviously not blending the data properly (on site), or it's lost in transmission...  The core issue is likely local conditions merging the various data sets.

As long as I have one, I'm good.  If all 5 of those currently in my log appear, it's icing.  It's also possible that in processing so many Q's at one gulp, the server is a bit behind the curve.

BTW, the ONLY reason for filling band modes on a rare one, is just that, it's rare.  There may come a time when I'm actually wanting to get wallpaper or similar (I don't now) and odds are that by the time it comes available again, a large portion of this crowd won't be around.  Not morbid, but statistical;  our median age is above 50 and life happens.

I don't believe that my meager station is keeping anyone from obtaining a Q and by the end of the weekend, K1N will be (relatively) starving for Q's.  I can fill band modes or I could keep making Q's on the same band for insurance.  I'd rather fill band modes.  I want to KNOW I have one validated.  Insurance Q's beyond proof in the online log does however cross the line in my book, I won't do that.
