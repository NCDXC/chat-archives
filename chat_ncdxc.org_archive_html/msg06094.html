Proposal for the board:

It's great that ladder is published in every DXer. More people should participate.

There's one very depressing way how numbers are reported though. I don't think we should report deleted entities in it but only active ones.That's common practice in DXCC HR and would better encourage new DXers to participate.

Just looking at the list and guys with over 370 entities... that's for today's DXer impossible. I have been QRV from U.S.A. for 10 years now and have only two deleted entities (PJs). Counting it by current entities would put everyone to the same line. Clublog where these are taken has a checkbox to do the ladder that way.
