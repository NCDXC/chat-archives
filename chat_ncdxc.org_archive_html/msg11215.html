Listened for hours this evening for them on 160 – about at the end of the window for them as its getting close to dawn in South Sandwich. 

Has anyone heard them or better yet worked them on 160m?


Nothing at all yet.


VERY tough path for us from W6 to them.


Yes.
Not a peep but the waterfall on the Flex-6700 showed there was ‘something’ there – could have been ET or the ghosts of the ether I guess!
Same here on my K3/P3, but as Garry noted, some guys did have propagation long enough to make a QSO. 160M prop can be very much like the "spotlight" effect of the sun visible through an opening in clouds that drift over the landscape, but on a much larger scale. For example, the times I've worked EU and AF, east coast stations don't have prop to them. VP8STI to NA is very typical of this aspect of 160M prop. 160M CW contests are a great time to study this effect.
Another thought -- what do you have for RX antenna(s)? Most stations who have worked them or called them have serious RX antennas.
