Al,

Load up all your wire, the car, the kids swing set, the porch cover and tune it all for 1.8825.  When I got up this morning (too late) at 4:30 they were VERY copyable (using their KPA1500 amp but a short antenna) then they took a 15 minute break, which was enough delay for the sun to rise here.  Looking at the spots, they got on about half past midnight (half hour after I went to bed, of course) and had a great time, even worked some in CA with QRP.  I kept trying then they just ... went away (near dawn there time).

So some sleepless nights are ahead.  Today I spent a good portion of the afternoon and evening on 20 CW, trying and trying.  At times they were S9 on the K3.... but the JA pipeline won out most of the day.  Then the band shifted and it was the mid-west and some TX (of course).  As I write this, it's EU only and if they do what happened last night, it'll be that way until the band closes here.

Some DQRM happened, I won't feed the troll.

Nothing above 20 meters, not a whisper of them here.

I know they're having IT issues, but I'd love to see them upload so I can start breathing again.

FT8 got me there on two bands so far (not even ESP for my ears on one of those), I just want to see it confirmed.

I have a good feeling about making it on the lower bands... but we'll see.

Hang in there Al, it's the bottom of the ninth, er, cycle and things are pretty sucky (try 6M, it's been fun lately).  If I can do it from N ID on the R7, you certainly have hope.

I'll go watch the rest of the sunset, it's pretty awesome (again) tonight.

Rick nhc

PS Still waiting on a contractor to make the tower base... at this point being done by Sept may not happen.  I want that DB36 in the AIR darnit!

I made a 17 CW QSO at about 0120Z today and had to struggle more than I expected to get good copy. The QSO was so marginal, I spent another 20 minutes to make another that was tough but 90%.

I then went to 15 M where there were NA spots for 21.285 and 21.023.  Nothing to copy at 285 but I could copy about 20% of the time on 023.  Midwest and SW were calling and I gave up due to chances were minimal that I could QSO.

 

I hear QRN but the S-meter is only at 0-1.  It may be that it is enough to bury the desired signal, I guess.
