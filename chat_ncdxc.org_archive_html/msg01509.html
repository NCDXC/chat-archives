I was hoping that tonight I could get a 40 CW contact. I've had great fun and great success working TX5K on 160-10 meters, however 40 meters CW has been quite elusive.  I usually hear them at 10DB over S9, however they are ""EU only"" from our sunset to midnight when I cannot stay awake any longer.
