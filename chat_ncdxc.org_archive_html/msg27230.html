I pulled this off https://www.dx-world.net/ft8ww-crozet-island-2022-23/

NEWS UPDATE – Thierry was made aware of the issue of his TXing period on FT8. From now on he will transmit 1st (00 and 30).

He will still be multi-stream.

You can now work him normal mode or F/H.

Hopefully this change will reduce the confusion with his operating mode.

-Use normal mode, not Fox/Hound
-Must transmit on the odd's (15 and 45)
-Transmit above 1000 Hz (at a minimum stay well above his transmit frequencies or you will interfere with people trying to complete F/H QSO's)
-Don't send 73 after receiving RR73. When you get RR73 from him, the QSO is complete and you are in the log. The extra 73 just adds more QRM. Easy to forget as you are celebrating the QSO! :-)
