Al:

Try this:

1.  Go to:  http://www.dxsummit.fi/

2.  Click on the SEARCH link at the top of the page.

3.  Enter ZL7AAA in the Search String.

4.. Choose PHONE under Mode.

5.  Choose 10M under Band.

6.  Change NUMBER OF SPOTS to 1000

7.  Click SEARCH

Now scroll down to March 22 around 0230 Z and you'll see he was indeed on 28380 working several West Coast stations at that time.

I'd say you're correct at 0233 Z.  You can use this data to contact the QSL manager for a further chat.
