Very , very sad news. When I was a new ham in search of a club in 2012, I found Ross at the NCDXC table at Pacificon. I’m quite sure he sponsored me on my application for NCDXC membership. He was also responsible for convincing me to take up FT8 when conditions were so poor (2017) and I was hearing nothing on SSB/voice with my wire hanging out the condo window. He was so kind, and also so influential to my path as a ham, and in this club.

 

He will be greatly missed.

 

73,

Sheryl, WW6G

I am sad to report that our ham colleague and good friend Ross Forbes, K6GFJ, passed away yesterday after a prolonged illness.  Our thoughts and prayers are with his wife Mary, who was at his side as he succumbed to complications from esophageal cancer.  

 

Those who knew Ross are aware of the many contributions he made to our hobby over the years.

 

RIP my friend.  We miss you already.
