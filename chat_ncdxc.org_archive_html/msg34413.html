Well – my idea is to modify “eQSL” so that it compatible with the goals of LOTW, or modify LOTW so that you can download a bunch of “QSL CARDS” with pretty pictures for free.  The rigor would still be there but it might be a little more fun.  Carting pieces of paper around the world is expensive these days.  As to QSL charges – I think everyone should be required or at least heavily incentivized to drop to LOTW after 12 months unless “other” arrangements are made.  So if you are some poor camel farmer in Godknowswhereistan you just wait and it shows up.  If you are big time US Dxer, you pay because you want it yesterday and you realize that you are funding the dxpedition this way. These guys typically work 50000 QSOs in a dxpedition and if they net $1/Q that is an extra $50000 to help fund the mission.  I’m willing to do that.

While eQSL has added some contacts in my log (missed or incomplete log entries, none resulting for DXCC credit gains), I have stopped using it because it's too much bother for no return of consequence (except payment for 'awards' in the ARRL style).

There are a LOT of bluff contact attempts (to be polite); folks attempting to confirm slots when I wasn't even on the air or that band or mode.  Plus the load of SWL confirmation requests means I have to take even more time; for no return (yes, some places may need SWL to get a license which seems... questionable).

The time and cost:value ratio makes it not worthy to me.

Clublog would be simpler to convert for award issuance but they don't want the burden (and I get that too; all donation, volunteer and time limited).

Rick nk7i
