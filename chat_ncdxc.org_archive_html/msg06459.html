If you haven’t already donated to the upcoming Tromelin FT4TA DX-pedition, please consider doing so:

 

http://www.tromelin2014.com/en/

 

You will see that Stu, K6TU is listed as providing propagation tools and advice, and that I am listed as the Pilot for all NA West of the Mississippi. Don, N1DG is listed as the East Coast and SA Pilot, and the FT4TA Team has been very good to make sure that the West Coast is given the best possible chance to make QSO’s. The NCDXF grant is very prominently listed on their web site. All very good news.

 

I need it for an ATNO – so I am personally very interested in helping all of us get our ATNO’s and band fills. I remember TO4E and how they listened to the pilot at the time – and called for West Coast specifically on the morning grey line Long Path on (especially) 40 and 30M, and even 20M – and how that worked like a charm.
