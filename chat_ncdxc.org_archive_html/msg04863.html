Mover the cursor on the vertical plot down to 5 degrees elevation, then look at the horizontal pattern. As the HFTA statistical data for most paths will show you (and especially the long ones), that's where most of action is.  That can, to some extent, be improved by a choice of mounting height. And before you do that, set NEC to compute in 1 degree increments. What you will see is that the 13.8 dBi at 25 degrees will turn into 4-5 dBi at 5 degrees.
