On Tue, Mar 17, 2026 at 9:39 AM Gene K5GS via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
Charles M0OXO uses the Bespoke OQRS application to confirm OSOs.

The only way to ask him to research missing QSOs is from the Log Search result screen for your callsign.
 Click on Report missing QSO.

When you input your details it places your request in his work queue, which he works down in sequence.
Sending him emails is an annoyance which, at the moment, he doesn't need.

Charles, and Tim M0URX, worked with us to design this application and process.
It eliminates the QSL manager wading through hundreds of emails with all sorts of unstructured explanations.

Please follow his process, it will make his life easier.

Cheers,
GS K5GS
