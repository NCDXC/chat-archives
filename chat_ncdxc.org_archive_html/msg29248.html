Hello,

              It’s time for my usual pilgrimage to Visalia. This year I’ll be coming into northern California a week before and spending time in Santa Rosa. I haven’t been able to promote a ride from Sonoma County to the convention. Still open to something like that. If that doesn’t work out, my plan so far is to ride the Sonoma Airport Express bus to SFO on Thursday morning. Hopefully, I’ll arrive at 10 a.m. to pick up my rental car I have reserved. Not being an elite rental car customer, I budget an hour to get out of the parking deck. >From the airport, it will probably be US 101 south to CA 152. I have never seen San Luis Reservoir full. Return will be via the same route Sunday.

              I’m posting this for a couple of reasons. I wouldn’t mind promoting a ride with somebody so I don’t have to rent a car. I would need to be picked up at the airport or some public transportation point. Guidance for bus or rail service heading south would help. That way we could talk and tell all the DX lies we know on the round trip while saving a couple of bucks. Barring that, if I follow through with the rental car, I wouldn’t mind picking up a passenger or two. It’s a long trip and a rider or two would sure make the trip more enjoyable. No financial obligation. At that point I’ll have to rent the car anyway.

              On the trip down, I will have time to veer off freeway to pick somebody up a their house as long as it’s kind of on the way. Since my flight back to Atlanta leaves at 11:30 p.m., I’ll have plenty of time to continue the taxi service on the return.

              Again, the schedule is down on Thursday, the 20th. Back up on Sunday the 23rd. Let me know if there is any interest in this plan. Thanks for the bandwidth.
