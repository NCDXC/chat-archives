Privacy concerns yes - but also latency. I contacted the developer and he was pretty firm on not having local processing. 

I think this becomes a trade off as if he distributes his model to every ones PC then 1) he loses control of it, and 2) the model learning is local

In this case a centralized model learns off all users data. As I said I do share the privacy concerns, not so much on the audio I know I'm sending....it's what I don't know that worries me. 

Sending received audio for processing, which can be listened to via a web SDR anyway is no big deal.

Yeah sure, Basically I have SmartSDR on windows and I am able to route the audio from the flex from the radio through some software called RM Audio. It uses AI to reduce the non voice noise, and I was able to basically get HF Voice sounding like FM. There is a cost however, processing of the audio stream is not local so there is some noticeable delay but its pretty amazing to see how it works. 

I just uploaded my example video to youtube so you can hear the difference.
