There are TONS of on-line custom rubber stamp companies that will make a custom stamp from any artwork you send them. I have made a few text-only stamps for things like endorsing checks, etc.

You won’t be able to do full color, but a monochrome logo should be fine. Do a Google search for “custom rubber stamps” and you will be overwhelmed.
