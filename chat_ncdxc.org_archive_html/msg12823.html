I have a very short window of time to work DX on 80.  It is after 7 AM PST when a loud local QRN turns off.  & AM is before sunrise these days but it comes fast and he opportunity is gone by maybe 720AM or so.  I also have a window from maybe 1830 PST and 1900 when the noise generator is back on.

Yesterday, I turned on the rig at 7 AM and listened in the bottom of 80 CW.  There was LZ2WO calling CQ.  I called and got noticed.  After about 5 calls, he put together my call and gave me a 339.  I gave a 449.

This is the first LZ on 80 M for me and worked barefoot!  The dipole slopes from 60’ to 10’.  Very satisfying!

 

Last and only other barefoot EU on 80 was an OH in 2005.
