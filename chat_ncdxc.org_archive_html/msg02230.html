On 5/10/2013 10:01 AM, paul@n6pse.com wrote:
Where does the NCDXC Membership Live        

There's also the self-fulfilling prophecy -- if the meeting location and other club resources are limited to one area, hams outside that area are far less likely to join or be active members if they do join. 

It helps to realize that meeting locations only 20-30 miles from each other can make enough difference in travel times to make it much easier for more distant members while still being feasible for those who find the South Bay location convenient. 

And we should be encouraging car pools -- we regularly cor pool from the Santa Cruz area to NCCC meetings, not only in the South Bay, but also to Livermore, Petaluma, Vallejo, and last summer's joint meeting around Fairfield (where Paul made a fine presentation on one of his trips). Car pools encourage more members to attend, are excellent opportunities for "fellowship," conversation that help us get to know each other better, and they really make the miles fly by.
