First Merry Christmas and Happy Holidays.  I know that FT8WW is on everyone's mind (and congrats to those who worked him), but I have another DX hunt story to tell.

 

I've been chasing VK9WX (cause it's there).  I'm sure there are those of you out there that need him.  He's on around 3am every morning on 7163 DX Net run by KE5EE.  So I couldn't set an alarm, but I told my wife that if she happens to wake up that hour, to wake me up.  Well, I was snoring so loud she woke up and in turn woke me up and I got on the list to work him with just seconds to spare.  VK9WX likes to work on the 7163 net and sometimes the 14207 net later though it's way past his bedtime for that one.  He was 5x8 to 5x9, gave me a 5x9 and should be an easy work for most of you on the west coast if you need.  Now on to FT8WW but this holiday-family stuff will be in the way yesterday and today.
