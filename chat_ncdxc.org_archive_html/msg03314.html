Jim,

This rule -- DXCC Rule 9 -- was part of the DXCC rule re-write adopted in
1998. As part of the so-called DXCC -2000 committee, I could explain it to
you, but it would take at least one or maybe two beers. In my opinion, there
is no rationale that applies in 2013. The remote control issue is one of two
of the most important issues currently being considered by the DXAC.
