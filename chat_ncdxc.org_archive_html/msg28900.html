After 40+ years with my current call, I will be transitioning to a new callsign soon, and I'm trying to figure out how to handle some of the issues, such as:

 

1) outstanding QSLs from old callsign that have not come in yet.  What happens when they show up in LOTW for a callsign I no longer hold?

You will get a new LOTW certificate for the new callsign and keep the old one. LOTW tracks them separately. To avoid getting confused, I kept my logs for the two callsigns in separate files for a while. I guess most logging programs track your own callsign for each QSO, DXLab suite does. I merged the two logs when I felt confident I knew what I was doing. Back up individual logs before merging.

 

2) How does LOTW and ClubLog work--do I just start over?  

No, you can combine the credits. You register the new callsigns.
Clublog is easy, you can combine the reports from the old and the new callsign.
LOTW will track the credits for each award separately. You can combine the credits for each award (this can’t be undone); Google pointed me to the right LOTW instructions page: https://lotw.arrl.org/lotw-help/dxcc-setup/?lang=en#linking .
For tracking the verified DXCC credits from LOTW in DXLab, you have to update the DXCC award number. Again, Google can point to the right DXLab page detailing this: https://www.dxlabsuite.com/dxlabwiki/DetermineDXCCAccountNumber

 

3)  How do I handle QRZ.COM pages for old and new call?

You will have a separate page for each callsign; you can set your new callsign as the manager of the old one. QRZ will verify your new callsign automatically within a few days: https://www.qrz.com/page/addcall.html

 

4)  Are there any QSL card printers left out there?  Reccomendations?

I’m designing my own and printing them at http://gotprint.com , one of the few printers that makes the 3.5”x5.5” in full color on both sides. A few years ago it was the best choice.



Any other issues I should be aware of?

All other awards schemes I ever used allow combining QSOs from different callsigns.

 

Thanks for any advice or pointers to information sources.
