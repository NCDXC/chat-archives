Yes…I have both units.  One I take to Field Day, CQP county expeditions, and other Ham outings.

The other runs my house when the power is out.

The Generac isn’t an inverter type, and hence doesn’t have nearly the load regulation that the Honda does.  It’s ambient noise is a lot higher too.

With the Honda set to eco mode, it slows down a lot, making it very quite and gas conservative.
