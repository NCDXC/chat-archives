Garry,

they decoupled the LOTW and paper/filed checker applications. There are no more "hybrid" applications. First you do your LOTW, then you go to the DXCC site of the business and fill out their new online submission form for the paper cards. Then you print out the online form and take it with the cards to your card checker. This saves the ARRL from having to transcribe all the paper forms. 

Use this link for the paper cards:

https://p1k.arrl.org/onlinedxcc/

73,

Andreas, N6NU
