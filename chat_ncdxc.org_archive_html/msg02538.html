Hello Dear Northern California DX Club Friends:

I've just returned from a five day visit to the DPRK.  We enjoyed a very cordial 
meeting with representatives of the DPRK Government.

Our proposals were presented and are receiving serious consideration. There is 
some interest in a radio sport type of event with International competition. 

I am very encouraged by the tone of our meeting that there is serious
Consideration for our proposals.  In fact, the Deputy Minister of Tourism told 
me that if the decision was solely his, then permission would be granted.  

We must wait for all levels of both Ministries to review our proposals.

Tomorrow, David Flack-AH6HY and I will return to the DPRK.  We will visit 
various potential venues including the Rason Free Enterprise Zone.

We will also visit the Orphanages & Bakeries of the "Love North Korea Children 
Mission of the DPRK.

Best wishes from Beijing.

Paul N6PSE
www.Intrepid-DX.com/humanitarian.php
