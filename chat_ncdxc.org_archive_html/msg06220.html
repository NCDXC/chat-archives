Questions for the group:

I don't want to trivialize recent events in the Ukraine, and I almost didn't want to put this out there.  But curiosity's gotten the better of me here. What happens when one distinct DXCC entity - like the Russian Federation - swallows another entity, such as the the former Ukrainian region of Crimea (or the Ukraine proper, Belarus and Moldova, which according to some experts may be the next courses on Vladimir's dinner menu)?  

1) Do you lose credit for these entities if the credits have already been awarded? 

2) If a confirmed QSO took place prior to the "annexation," but credit for the QSO is still pending award - or worse, has yet to be submitted for credit - can you still apply for credit using the pre-annexation QSL? 

3) Does anybody know how the ARRL dealt with Hitler's "annexation" of the Sudatenland and other independent states during the 1930's?

I fully expect some heat for even putting this out on the listserve, and apologize in advance for being this crass about a horrific situation.

Any input from the group (hot or helpful) is greatly appreciated.
