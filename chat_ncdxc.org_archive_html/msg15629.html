I am sorry to hear of the loss of a ham and club member.  I did not get to know Reg but do recall the K6SSJ callsign from the mid 70’s.  Maybe it was Reg, maybe a prior call holder.

For a few years from 1971 onward, 15 M was open most days for good DX.  There was a mid-morning ‘net’ on 21.355 MHz going by ‘Afrikaners Group’.  It was usually conducted by W2PPG, Cal, with Herbie, WA6BJS helping out on the West Coast.  This group helped little pistols like me to work the AF that checked in.  K6SSJ, Sugar Sugar Japan, was a regular there ‘competing’ with me to get a shot at the DX.  He was usually able to get a QSO and I was iffy most of the time with a HB 3-el Yagi about 8’ off the family home’s roof.  Maybe some others out there remember this but it was pretty fun for a novice DXer.  SSJ was not a novice but fun to hear him in there working the DX.

 

Al

N6TA.
