As with the other comments here, I think it depends on what you're needs are.  But FWIW, I really like my AIM-4170C.  You have to have a PC connected to it, but it is very easy to use and gives you a lot of great information.  It's available direct from the manufacturer, Array Solutions: http://www.arraysolutions.com/Products/AIM4170.htm

If you really need to have a wireless connection between it and the PC, Bluetooth is an option.  I've seen reports of people using the GridConnect FireFly Bluetooth dongle to be able to remote the unit from the PC.
