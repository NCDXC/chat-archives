What Iain said - the number one reason that the Microhams at W7RN don't diddle is that the FSK output has gotten unplugged. Also make sure the Microham is actually set to FSK and not CW or something. Good luck - jeff wk6i

On Sun, Dec 28, 2014 at 4:18 PM, iain macdonnell - N6ML <ar@dseven.org> wrote:
A steady tone indicates that the K3 is getting PTT but not FSK (it's
not shifting). I'm not familiar with the MicroHam stuff, but presume
it presents a virtual COM port for FSK, and has a connection to the
K3's ACC port?? You'd want to have MMTTY pointed to that virtual COM
port, and on the "Misc" tab of MMTTY Setup, make sure that "Tx Port"
is "COM-TxD(FSK)".
