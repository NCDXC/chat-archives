This afternoon, I was parked in my shack watching for any/all 3Y0J activity. I was hoping to help some of the Club Members get an ATNO. 

I had made careful notes of propagation/decodes by band and time of day so I knew that a 17 meter opening would soon be upon us. I saw W5s and a few W7's (Arizona) calling 3Y0J. 

Then I saw Steve WB6RSE down in Los Angeles call him and soon worked 3Y0J. 
Just as Steve was working 3Y0J, I was on the phone with a friend who was texting through the Iridium Satellite network to one of the Ops who confirmed that he was QRV on 17 meters FT8. 

As soon as Steve WB6RSE worked him, I started getting decodes on 3Y0J. I looked at all of the callers and I saw no other N6/W6/K6 callers so I decided to make a contact. 

I called two times and 3Y0J came right back to me with a signal report. I gave him a signal report but never got the RR73 and all transmissions from 3Y0J suddenly came to a stop. 

My friend on the phone told me that the Op said that the generator died and that I was in the log. This was all happening in real time at 2141 UTC. I asked my friend if they were going to add fuel to the generator and he said they are out of fuel. 

So I logged the contact, hoping it will be confirmed later. 

Here some hours later, I still see many calling 3Y0J but I have seen no signs that 3Y0J is active on any band. 

Paul N6PSE
