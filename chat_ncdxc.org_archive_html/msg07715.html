$5.00 is the norm for OQRS and has been so for almost five years. Remember, you are paying for the convenience of not having to mail your card to the Dxpedition.

DXpeditions now pay taxes on OQRS income, so your $5.00 is really about $3.50 after taxes. The cost to print and Mail your card is about 90 cents. PayPal will take about 4% of the $5.00 for their fee. So your $5.00 OQRS payment gives the Dxpedition about $2.25 about what a cup of coffee costs these days.
