My own take on funding DXpeditions:
I contribute to funding DXpeditions through organizations like the NCDXF, INDEXA, and local clubs. I get several advantages from this route:
* The organizations do due diligence one the DXpedition. I don't have the expertise, and having an organization do the due diligence saves both me and the DXpedition time. 

* Donations to many of these organizations are tax deductible.
If I should be so fortunate as actually make some QSOs, I will order a priority card with perhaps an extra donation as a reward for them running an efficient enough operation to reach me.
