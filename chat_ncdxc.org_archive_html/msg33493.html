The Feb 15 NCDXC meeting will be at the Wagon Wheel BBQ, 861 Leong Drive, Mt. View. Dinner from 6:00 -7:00 then meeting and program. K6MM will talk about the remote control of AA7JV RIB on Penryn Atoll. Probably the best way to get to the Wagon Wheel is to take 85 to the Moffet Bvld exit immediately ahead of Hiway 101.
Turn right on Moffet Bvld. (toward 101) and then in a few hundred feet turn right onto Leong Drive. In a few hundred more feet, Leong Drive tees to the left and right. Turn left and follow Leong Drive along to the Wagon Wheel BBQ which will appear on your right in about 1/2 mile.It is co-located with several other businesses in a small strip mall. A Zoom link will be sent the week of the meeting for those of you that are not able to be at the Wagon Wheel.
