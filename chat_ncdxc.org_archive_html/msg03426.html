On 10/2/2013 7:10 PM, Jim Brown wrote:
On 10/2/2013 5:22 PM, Paul Ewing wrote:
Jim, should I just thank Pooyan for his offer, but tell him no thanks, my club members want a turn being sought after so they would like to defer the sked until another time?

The worst thing we could do is set him up for failure. The CW and SSB portions of the contesting bands are going to be really full at the planned times, few of us on county expeditions will have internet (we're mostly in the boonies), so we won't know we're QRMing him. And many of us go out with well-equipped stations -- decent antennas and 600W. .

The issue here is not "we're a DX club, tough" as some of our less sensitive members have posted.  It's a really bad time to try to work weak signals on 20, 15, and 10.  If you want to go ahead with it, I would first check about a RTTY contest this weekend, and if there is not a major one, I would suggest RTTY.  WA7BNM has the definitive list of contests. Google to find it.
