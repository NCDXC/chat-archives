I was waiting to hear FT5ZM on 160M, drinking coffee and saw a spot on 20M – somewhere in OC or Asia. Just for laughs, I decided to tune the that 20M SSB frequency. WOW – I couldn’t believe it – he had a strong signal and I worked him.

 

The 160M QSO was so amazing that I totally forgot about this one. I don’t ever remember hearing a path open at 4:30 AM on 20M before – but that is because I have always assumed it’s just closed then.
