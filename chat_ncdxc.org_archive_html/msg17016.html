On April 16, 2019 at 10:59 PM "Bob Wilson, N6TV" <n6tv@arrl.net> wrote:

My 2019 Visalia International DX Convention photos are now online on Google Photos at:

https://bit.ly/Visalia2019

For the best viewing experience, click on the first photo (or any photo), then p ress the right arrow key on your keyboard to step through the photos quickly.  Click the circled "i" for caption information (callsigns).

I could use a little help identifying some folks noted with a "?" in the captions.  Please email me directly with updates/corrections.

You may use the "three-dot" menu, top right, to download the entire album or view a slideshow.

Photos are displayed in chronological order taken, from Friday morning through Sunday morning DX Breakfast.  Unfortunately, no "search" feature is available on the Google Photos site.   If you need help finding a particular photo of a particular person, feel free to write to me directly.

Permission is hereby granted for publication of these links and any of these photos in the NCCC Jug, NCDXC DXer, SCDXC Bulletin, SDDXC Bulletin, The NCDXF Newsletter, The FARS Relay, The Daily DX, MLDXCC Nugget, REDXA Sunspots, the ARRL Contest Update, the National Contest Journal, and club web sites.

Suggested photo credit: "N6TV photo."

For photo use in other publications, please contact me directly.

Please publish only the short URL (" bit.ly" link) listed above, not the longer URL that this link redirects to.  Please post the link to your local club reflectors and newsletters (I've already copied NCCC, NCDXC and MLDXCC above).

To save a high resolution copy of any photo, first select the photo, then at the top right, click the three dot menu and select "Download photo."  Even higher resolution originals are available upon request.

73,
Bob, N6TV
