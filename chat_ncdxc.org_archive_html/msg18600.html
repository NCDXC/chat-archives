Beholden to their donors?  It's been 5+ straight hours of EU EU EU now (and their signal strength is now half of what it was a few hours ago).  I understand that they want to work EU and take advantage of the meager propagation until EU sunrise....  I get that, but it lacks consideration for the rest of the world.
I'm seeing a LOT of spotting (and other) commentary that isn't complementary, even some from EU.  They're not ATNO for me, so I'm less invested.  They're wanted by many on Top Band also, so far nothing.  Perhaps that will change because not all hams are retired folks who can afford to stay up all night (hoping anyway), so the crowds will thin.
On the other hand, I added 15 and 20M band slots (CW) from them today (one call and logged, likely I'm all they heard).  Plus another 7 band mode slots for the HU1DL team.  NEITHER team has considered (or worked as far as I can tell yet) anyone in AF or beyond a few in AS (except JA of course, always as plentiful as deer in the woods around here, hi).  That propagation should be there; it'll be interesting to see the stats later.
Running CW at > 30 WPM is not making them points either.  Many that can't copy (even their own call) that fast, simply repeat their call making the pileup mess worse.  With the usual robocallers, near disastrous.  With LONG pauses between Q's (on 80M), their rate is pretty low.
It's the team choices, just like the team last week that wouldn't go digital; their total numbers were WAY down considering the amount of time they were active AND had commercial power, accommodations etc.  Those signals were below wimpy, unusable across the US as a whole for the most part (all EU and AF show).
The choices each team makes are remembered though, so if they want money to go out again; it can bite them. 

It could be worse, it could be a place that's rare... ;-)
