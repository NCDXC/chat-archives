No, changing the RF gain will have no effect on the signal reports; the software looks at the noise floor vs signal strength; which would vary in proportion to what the sounds card is given; both drop equally so no change.
The software is amazing for what it does, but a loud signal will put the sound card into AGC, which eliminates the weaker signal. I would prefer  that the software was fed raw audio and do it's own AGC.
I'll try with the radio AGC off, but I don't think that will change anything, since it's the sound card apparently that limits weak signals.
So far the only 'cure' is to limit the bandwidth to the desired signal (plus a couple hundred Hz for multi-stream).
