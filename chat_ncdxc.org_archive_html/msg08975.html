If you don't care where in the USA the transmitter is that is fine as well.;.

Actually that's not true. As it is now, you have no choice but to keep it. As far as ARRL is concerned, your DXCC account is tied to your call. When I go on LOTW, they try to credit my Chicago QSOs to DXCC. If I have a California QSO for a band-country-mode slot, they ignore it and only show me the Chicago Qs. So the only way I can stick to my personal ethics on this is to continually compare my California log with those old LOTW credits and accept them, one at a time, when I've worked the new ones.
