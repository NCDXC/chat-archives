I have one on a US Towers ALM-30 crank up.

It's a folded dipole on 40 and slightly folded on 30.

It really does perform like a 2 element yagi on 20-6m.

Because mine is only up 36', it's a DX antenna on 30 - 6M and a cloud burner on 40M, but during grey line high angles can be worked, so DX on 40M is sometimes possible.

I use phased verticals on 40M when I get serious about DXing

It's not that hard to put together, just take your time and read and read the instructions. Measure twice, cut once.

A heat gun really is mandatory.

I love mine and it plus the crank up with go with me when I retire and move in 5 years.
