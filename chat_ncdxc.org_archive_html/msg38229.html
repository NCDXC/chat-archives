In the last few months I’ve encountered two issues. There’s a price to paid for decoding sensitivity in the form of false decodes… they’re not frequent enough to be worrisome. 
In addition, my version will not populate the messages windows if I’ve clicked on a DX call within a compound statement. When that happens, the solution is simply to manually enter the DX call and click populate. 
Other than that, it’s been great.
