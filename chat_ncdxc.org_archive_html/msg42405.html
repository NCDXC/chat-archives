Hello NCDXC Chat Group Members,

We are writing to announce a major upgrade to the club’s digital infrastructure. On [3/25/2026], we will be migrating our email systems and reflectors from our legacy Mailman system to Google Workspace for Nonprofits.

Why are we doing this?

Our current system has served us well, but this move allows us to:

  • Improve Reliability: Eliminate the "bounce" issues and delivery delays common with older shared mail servers.

  • Modernize Archives: Provide a searchable, user-friendly web interface for our club history.

  • Prepare for the Future: Build a foundation for automated dues renewals and better club management.

The Migration Window (Service Outage)

On [3/25/2026], starting at 1pm Pacific , we will perform the technical "cutover."We expect the actual move to take 4–5 hours, but it may take 24 hours for everything to be working as expected

CRITICAL IMPACTS DURING THIS WINDOW:

  • Groups Offline: Both members@ncdxc.org and chat@ncdxc.org will be unavailable.

  • Email Risk: Any emails sent to an @ncdxc.org address during this window may be delayed or permanently lost.

  • Please refrain from sending any club-related emails during this time

What happens next?

Once the move is successful, you will receive an automated "Welcome" email from the new Google Groups system. This will confirm your subscription is active and provide instructions on how to manage your settings (Digest, No-Mail, etc.).

We appreciate your patience as we move the club to a more modern and reliable platform. Please note that we are not experts in this type of migration so please be patient.
