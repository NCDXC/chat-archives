My neighbor Pat Miller, W6DRX, has been an NCCC member for about seven years, but has been inactive for most of that time due to health issues. Pat and Sandy are moving out of their house (which they will rent out) to live on a sailboat that they just bought. He's selling all his radio gear, including an early serial number K3. He built it from a kit, and I opened it up to verify was in it.
