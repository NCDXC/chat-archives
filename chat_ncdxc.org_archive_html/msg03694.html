I've discovered two resources that may be of general interest to all NCDXC members.

Dollar Stores
In the South Bay there are two great dollar stores where you can find many low-cost items such as plastic storage cases, tools, desk supplies, zippered travel cases, etc.

K&M is a smaller store located at 6132 Bollinger Rd in San Jose.
http://www.yelp.com/biz/k-and-m-one-dollar-store-san-jose

Daiso is much larger and located at 19750 Stevens Creek Blvd, Cupertino.
http://www.yelp.com/biz/daiso-cupertino#query:Japanese%20Dollar%20Store

Both stores are within 2 miles of each other.  You and your significant other will have fun shopping for hours.  : >)

Battery Cases & Supplies
All-Battery.com sells a lot of interesting battery-related products.  One of my favorites is their Tenergy plastic battery cases for AA and AAA batteries.  High quality and low cost -- excellent value.  Great for keeping batteries safe and secure for trips or general storage..  They also sell a variety of Ni-MH rechargeables at good prices.

Website: http://www.all-battery.com/

AA cases: http://www.all-battery.com/10AATenergyPlasticCases-90441-1.aspx

AAA cases: http://www.all-battery.com/10PCSTenergyPlasticBatteryCaseforAAASize-90553.aspx

Ni-MH Batteries: http://www.all-battery.com/ni-mhbatteries.aspx

Have fun!
