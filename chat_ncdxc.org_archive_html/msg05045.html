I used my phased hatted 40M verticals - Christman feed and 44 radials each.

They oriented about 20 degrees and 200 degrees end fire (to hit Tromelin and Heard) and the broadside direction is probably 290 - ish but with a wide enough lobe to cover Amsterdam.

They also work very well on 30M since they are 24' and hatted. Tuned for 40M, but still beat my 63' top hatted and loaded 160M TX antenna by a margin.

Getting that first band let's me relax and wait until tomorrow to get serious again - although hoping late afternoon has good propagation on 20 and 17.

I'm not an "adrenalin junky", so I don't mind waiting a day or so before doing band fill battle - hi hi.

These guys sure know their antennas and prop and are super ops.
