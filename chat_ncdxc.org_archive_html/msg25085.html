In F/H it doesn't matter. The DX will add you to the hound queue either way and the back and forth count will be the same. Also note the DX does not care about your 73 in F/H. Once the Fox sends RR73, the QSO is logged.
I favor the grid square format as a Hound because some ops will sort the caller queue based on distance.
I usually sort by dB as a Fox because the loudest usually are worked the fastest (fewest back and forth), but that is the DX ops choice.
