I am intrigued by this topic and a non physics major… has to ask a friend (Stanford RF Engineer) and this is his reply for a solution to the BC RFI (RK):

Use a high pass filter of multiple steps, (only 1% caps and inductors), Tune for about 1.6 MHz. Forget the bandpass filter. Then null the output before the receiver to null the BC station, or what's left of it. Or....build series resonant filters at 1160 KHz and shunt them between the verticals end and ground.....a really good ground.

Or do all of the above. 
 

I remain interested.
