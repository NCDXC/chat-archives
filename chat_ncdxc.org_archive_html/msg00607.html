My favorite was Conway Reef – but that’s just because I was on the team!  It’s getting my vote!

 

Going on that DXpedition gave me real insight into what it takes to organize and execute one.   And have been there, I know what I don’t know when it comes to judging a particular DXpedition.   Each DXpedition is unique.  I’m just happy they keep happening.

 

I thought this year’s DXpeditions were all quite good.   I think I was able to work most of them.   They are special on-air events for me, and I look forward to each one.

 

My major dislike has been the noticeable increase in bad behavior on the part of some operators, particularly on the DX TX frequency.
