Very interesting article about gravity waves in the ionosphere which result in raising and lowering the electron density when they travel through causing a periodic fading effect. They used data gleaned from the RBN, WSPR, and PSK-Reporter to reproduce ionospheric data gathered by much more expensive, sophisticated, and dedicated equipment. 

Rare to see a serious, peer-reviewed article basing its findings on ham radio technology.

https://agupubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/epdf/10.1029/2022GL097879
