Free to anyone that could use this set:

 

1959 2-volume set of the Scott’s Postage Stamp Catalogue.  Over 2200 pages of stamp images from everywhere, including places you and I probably never worked!!

This is a good book for stamp collectors but also has some interesting listing of colonies and former colonies of parent states like France, Italy, etc.  Did you know that Italy once had a post office in Crete and Tripoli, Libya?

Or, did you know that former KZ5, Canal Zone, had its own stamps as did Hawaii?  Not be, and I doubt many of you care.  But, someone might so let me know if you want these two books.
