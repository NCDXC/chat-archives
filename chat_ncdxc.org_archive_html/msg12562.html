At the last minute, an incredibly generous donor has offered an AMAZING set of original, brand-name Greenlee knockout punches. Most are NEVER USED, in the original boxes and wrapping. For those of you who build stuff (radios, amplifiers, etc.), these are indispensable tools for making precision holes of various sizes and shapes in sheet metal. If you look at eBay (and I did), you’ll see that used ones go for $30-100 or more EACH, depending on size. Serious bidders only; we’ll put the set out on eBay if we don’t get a fair price, but I’d rather see it go to someone local who will use and enjoy them.
