Try around 7050 and 14050.  The SKCC  (Straight Key Century Club)   users hang around these freqs.  Many slow speed CW operators using straight keys.  A great bunch, they will gladly QRS (if they are not already slow enough, hihi)   The groups websiite if you are interested is  https://www.skccgroup.com/   I have been having fun with my old J38 around these freqs.  I make a lot of sending mistakes  hihi
