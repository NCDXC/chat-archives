I made the QSO in 1974 – but never received the QSL back then. I wasn’t a DX-er (I was a “CW Traffic Man”), and was inactive from 1977 – 2001 when I got into DX-ing. When I got back in – I typed my old ARRL logbooks into a logging program and realized – hey – there’s an oldie but goodie. I did QSL 100% back then, and if I worked DX – it was just because some DX station came back to my CQ.
 
I write a blog called “The DXCC Sleuth”, and the full story is here – you’ll get a kick out of my very modest station:
 
http://dxccsleuth.wordpress.com/

Yes, I sure am lucky.
