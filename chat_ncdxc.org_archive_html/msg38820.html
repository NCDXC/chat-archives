Welcome aboard, Mike! We all started somewhere, and as long as you get a thrill in your heart when you faintly hear a new one, you’re one of us. 
Format should be something like this:
Subject: call-entity-band-mode
Then, in the email body, include the frequency and important details, such as listening up 5, calling by the numbers, unusual propagation such as skew or long path: anything that will allow fellow members to rapidly point the beam and find the DX and begin calling without doing something embarrassing. 
There’s no “right” way to do things, just keep it succinct and give us enough information to find the DX quickly. Nothing wrong with including the time or SNR, but I usually assume the spot is contemporaneous and don’t want to be reminded that you have a better antenna or live in a DX paradise like N6TA. 
We’re all here to not only help, but to continue to learn ourselves.
