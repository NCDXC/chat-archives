At 08:11 3/27/2013, KE1B wrote:
Anna and I have thought about doing Monaco as a Holiday DXpedition, specifically targeting the West Coast. As others have noted, the problem is the terrain. With being nestled below a huge cliff to the west, we would probably need power and/or gain antennas
I looked carefully at this and there is a large pier extending into the Med (43d 44m 10sN 7d 25m 53sE). If you can get permission, you could operate from the pier using a vertical over salt water with a killer signal, no tower or beam needed. You could even add a director or use a phased vertical array. There is a article on how to do this in one of the antenna handbooks by Dean Straw. He might even be willing to consult with you....
