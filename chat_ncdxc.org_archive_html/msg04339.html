This is not an official press release:

Hello NCDXC friends,

Things are going well at the XZ1J Dxpedition. We are enjoying nice openings to North America at our sunrise and sunset. Sometimes we are having difficulty hearing you due to the high noise and many EU and South America stations also calling.

Yesterday, some of us opted for some sightseeing instead of sleeping. We saw the sacred white elephants, the Golden Palace and the Presidential palace. We toured the ancient city and toured the market place.

There are many American flags flying over Nay Pyi Taw in celebration of President Obama's recent visit to Myanmar. They seem to like Americans very much in Myanmar.

Even at the police hut next to our hotel, when we walk across the road to our SSB shack, the policemen come out, lay their machine gun down and give us a high five!

Soon, we will be dancing the Macarena together!

We are trying to get our RTTY working, so that we can start this weekend. We are planning SSB and RTTY only during the CW contest.

We will begin tearing down on Nov 25th. We will go QRT on the morning of the 26th. Most of us will travel 30-40 hours and get home just in time for Thanksgiving Dinner,

Today, the Intrepid-DX Group made a donation of $500 to the Dxpedition. We are hoping for more Foundation and Club support.

73 for now,

Paul N6PSE
XZ1J Team member
