For those that were concerned, the evacuation order for our home has been lifted. The fire is still not completely out, but the fire chief believes that the area south of Bear Creek Road (where we live) is not longer threatened. We have returned home after spending two days with friends in Saratoga.

No damage, no power outage. There is a light dusting of ash on the ground (to be expected). And if anyone wants to smoke some meat, you’re welcome to hang it on a clothesline up here. ;^) (It’s pretty smoky outside; we’re hoping for some rain and/or wind to sweep it out.)

Thanks for everyone’s concern. I am hoping for the best for those folks on the other side of Bear Creek Road who are still in danger. We are ALWAYS fire-aware up here in the hills.
