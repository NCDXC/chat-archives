Hi Paul,

I worked them (E31A) yesterday morning on 40m CW on the long path.  I’m in Colorado.  I also worked them on 30m FT8 a couple hours later, also on the long path.  On 40m their signal peaked at my sunrise.  They were working all continents and mostly JAs.  I had trouble finding their QRG unless they picked up a NA caller.  That’s the most difficult part of working them when they are working multiple continents at the same time.  I have not chased them on the higher bands however many locals here worked them on 17m and 20m in the evenings.  The openings on the higher bands may be much shorter in CA.  GL on your chase.
