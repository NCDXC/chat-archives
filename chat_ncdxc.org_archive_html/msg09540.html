Subject: Update from E30FB

Hello Col, Bernie and Mike (NCDXC) from Asmara.

Yesterday, the team made improvements to the antennas including a WARC vertical antenna as well as an 80/160 meter vertical on top of the six floor of our hotel.

Tonight we will continue to provide ATNO's on 15/20 meters and we will begin operations on 30/80 and 160 tonight.

We are glad that we have been able to work many US West Coast stations around 0200 UTC on the high bands.

We are uploading logs daily via our BGAN satellite terminal.

This is a team effort and we are having a lot of fun bringing you these contacts.

We hope to see you in our logs!

The E30FB Team
