I have been corresponding with Paul F6EXV and Flo PB8DX about their magnificent 3A operation. I inquired about their operating location 
as previously I had always worked Monaco via the long path. 

They have shared with me that they were operating on a jetty at the waterfront and they were surrounded by salt water in all directions. 

They were using a home made fan dipole 15 meters high and an Inverted L for 80 meters with a tuner box at the base. 

Sounds like a very simple operation that greatly benefited from being surrounded by salt water. 

That's it,
