NCDXC friends, what was the best part of IDXC 2023?


For me, it was seeing old friends that I had not seen since before Covid. It was great getting together and just feeling what life was like before Covid shut things down. That felt really good. 

I marvelled at the various DXpedition stories, the outstanding Forum presentations and the fun and good cheer. 

What was the best part for you?
