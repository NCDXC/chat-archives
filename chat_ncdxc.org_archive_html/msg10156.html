Keith,

 

The DXCC criteria was born over 80 years ago. It might be considered remarkable that it hasn’t changed more than it has over that period. One could ask why entities weren’t deleted/removed as the criteria has morphed. The answer is that DXers have invested a lot in working these places over the years, and removing them raises a howl you wouldn’t believe. Can you imagine removing 50 entities all at once? (But, that’s another story…)

 

The answer to your question is in the history. Go back and read DeSoto’s original 1935 article for an explanation of the beginnings – it’s on the Web.
