A better club?

 

First thought that came to me was more member interaction.  Then I saw Mike’s K6MKF message along the same line with a bit more focus.

 

I appreciate the need to move a meeting along to get to the program and raffle and out before Carrow’s staff throws us out.  But we have been doing it at the expense of the “introduction” portion.  We are scooting through that part of the meeting with name and call then moving on to the next member.

 

I think this portion of the meeting needs to be expanded in time.  Let’s expand that portion of the meeting to not only report any new DX since the last time we met but also anything special.  It could be “Hay guys, I’m having trouble hearing… or X isn’t working anymore”.  Let the issue get aired.  Maybe we can’t spend more than a moment on it but the subject is on the table and can be discussed later in the meeting or after it.

 

Members of course need be active not passive.  They need to take notes and seek out the member to exchange ideas.

 

Bottomline – more time for members to mention something new/changed or problem with their station and any operating accomplishments (DX).  More exchange between members.

 

I think we have swung too far into the “push” mode of information transfer.  There is a balance in there somewhere.

 

73,

 

Bob – W6OPO
