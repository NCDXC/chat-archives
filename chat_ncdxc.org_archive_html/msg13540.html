Please join me in congratulating Paul, N6PSE, Bob, KK6EK and Ken, LA7GIA.

 

At the IDXC Banquet Saturday evening:

 

Paul Ewing, N6PSE, received the 2017 DXer of the Year award from the Northern California DX Club in recognition of his efforts organizing and executing the South Sandwich, VP8STI, and South Georgia, VP8STI, DXpeditions.

 

Bob Schmieder, KK6EK, received the 2017 DXer of the Year award from the Northern California DX Club in recognition of his efforts organizing and executing the Heard Island, VK0EK, DXpedition.

 

Ken Opskar, LA7GIA, received the 2017 W6OL CW Operator award from the Northern California DX Club in recognition of his outstanding DX CW operation.

 

Well done, Paul, Bob and Ken!!   You guys made a lot of great DX happen for us all!!

 

- 73 and good DX de Mike, K6MKF, Past President - NCDXC
