My most memorable contact was with KH6IJ during the 1952 ARRL CW DX contest on 160 meters.
 
I had been licensed a little bit more than a year. I was running a Hallicrafters HT-9 at 100 watts input. My antenna was an end fed 60 foot piece of wire about 25 feet high. It was much more KH6IJ's skill to make the contact than mine.
