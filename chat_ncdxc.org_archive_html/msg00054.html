I was very fortunate. I needed confirmed QSOs on 160, 80, 40, 30,12 and 6M and I also needed confirmation on any of  the bands for RTTY and additional mode confirmations for CW or SSB for various bands depending on the band.

 

Bottom line, I was able to make 14 confirmed contacts with 3D2C on nine (9) bands, including 160M through 10M and I was also able to make contacts on CW, SSB and RTTY as hoped for.

 

I used various power levels. I was able to work them on most bands with 100W to 400W although I increased my power on 160M to 500W +/-.

 

>From my perspective this operation was outstanding (although I too was frustrated with the malfunctioning CW keyer on 12M (RFI?) that occurred one day. It only happened to my knowledge on 12M and on one evening)

 

I say THANK YOU and CONGRATULATIONS to the 3D2C team!!!  J  73, Ted, K6XN
