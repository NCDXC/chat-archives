I too am curious about this site and have been since it first appeared.

The instructions page is the only (fleeting) reference to the underlying technology with the terms HF Ionospheric Interferometry and reference to NASA PolSAR.  If you Google these terms, you don’t find a heck of a lot of stuff that relates to propagation measurement.

I have tried contacting the guy who operates the site several times asking for some pointers to relevant research papers but have never had any response.

The absence of transparency regarding how this works raises some skepticism in my mind – but hey, like horoscopes, if reading it seems to be useful, go for it!
