My perception? Toughest DXped to crack since, well, Glorioso. That said, it is better run than was Glorioso, but I am pretty much getting nowhere with the pileups, which are too damned wide to figure out most of the time.

The first day--30th Oct zulu, I was out of town finishing a short vacation. No participation. Ditto for

The second day--31st Oct zulu. Got home Friday afternoon and spent some time in the maelstrom  before hosting a neighborhood Halloween party. No joy.

The third day--1st Nov zulu. Thought I worked them on 30m at 0143Z and logged it. Clublog says I was dreaming. No joy. 30 would be a new slot.

The fourth day--2nd Nov zulu. Blew off SS--can't do long sits any more.Logged what I thought was an imperfect Q on 20m RTTY at 02Z. New mode for me, so I went back into the pile later to try for a solid Q. Clublog says I made TWO RTTY Q's; I have no idea when the second one occurred but the one I did here is in the log. Yippee---something new! Went to a dinner at friends.

The fifth day--3rd Nov zulu. Sleepless still, I got on in the morning. Logged 15m CW Q at 1619Z/ Don't need. Current Clublog download last time earlier than that, so don't know.

The sixth day---4th Nov zulu. Decided to try to fix both Beverages in hopes of a chance on 80m. Big dead oak (SODS) had fallen on both antennas, crushing them. Chronic back pain had me postponing the repair--hard to walk without pain dealing with tons of branches and blackberries. Got the NE/SW whole (I think) before being overtaken by darkness. Have no idea if it is functional--I have not heard them yet on 40 or 80, much less 160. Tomorrow I tackle what is left of the other Beverage. Back pain is no help. Spent hours on 30m slugging it out with the   30 kHz-wide pile. No joy. Went to 20m SSB--don't need but  wanted to work something, dammit! Re-learned why I hate huge SSB piles. No joy. Quit to try to clear all the political begging from my inbox and cook dinner. Will fill out ballot tonight--and to Hell with radio.

So---so far---one new mode in the log, one other Q probably in the log. Pretty modest for six days. Not my finest hour, but glad to have RTTY--only 12 left on digi to have them all, two for HR.

I hate DXpeds that so grossly favor Yurp--as most do. 57% Yurp sucks, big  time.

Garry, NI6T
