I just did a routine upgrade of Firefox to ver. 29. An unheralded "surprise" was that Mozilla did away with the Status toolbar, which has, if selected under "toolbars" had forever appeared at the bottom of the window and displayed things like WX apps and --of interest to me---the N0HR Foxfire add-on, which displayed SFI, A and K and offered additional display histories.

The Mozilla folks evidently decided to ditch the Status bar in favor of a new bar in that location---which has not yet been fully vetted by Mozilla and is currently only available in an unsupported release. Worse, they inform that "not all" add-ons that worked with the old Status bar will work with the new and, indeed, that is the case. My WX add-on, for example, seems to run OK, but Propfire is lost in the great void.

Anyone in the Club who has encountered and defeated this problem? I like Foxfire because one sees current prop at a glance.
