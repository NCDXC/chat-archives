Hello NCDXC Friends, this information was posted on the Visalia International DX Convention Facebook page by Bill Kendrick. 

I’m just sharing this news for those not on Facebook.

Paul N6PSE 
Beirut, Lebanon 

We are all ready to release the hotel reservation date! The announcement will go out tomorrow night via email to all past attendees and club members. You will have plenty of advanced notice so you can plan to be on the webpage at 10 AM when the reservations open. You can access the hotel reservations on www.dxconvention.org There will be a hypertext link to the hotels which will lead you to our hotel webpage from there you click on the link to the Visalia website where the hotels are listed with reservation links. For those who want to jump the gun neither our webpage hotel link or the Visalia hotel webpage will be available until the date and time specified. Furthermore, if you try to make reservations for the convention directly to the hotels we have on contract they will not allow it. We want everyone to receive the announcement and have the same opportunity to reserve a room.

If you are on the convention webpage before 10 AM remember to refresh the webpage after 10 AM or you will miss the start!
