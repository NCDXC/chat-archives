Members –

Several years back I had expressed interest in HF mobile operation and a ham buddy of mine gave me a Kenwood TS-50 w/ matching AT-50 tuner. This was done with the understanding that when I no longer needed it, that I’d GIVE it away to a deserving young ham. As my needs have changed, the time has come to honor his wish.

The radio and tuner are in good operational condition. It comes complete with microphone, radio to tuner data cable, radio mounting bracket and DC power cable and documentation. All that is required to get on the air is a suitable 12V DC power supply, HF antenna and appropriate licensing.

Now, what do I require? I’d like a written paragraph or two describing their need / desire / commitment to the hobby. Have them emailed it to me at – nc6r4dx@gmail.com. Please feel free to encourage a deserving young ham.

Once selected, this needs to be an in person pickup.
