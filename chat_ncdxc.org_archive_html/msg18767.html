Next NCDXC Regular Meeting February 20. Doug Hendricks KI6DS from qrpguys is the guest speaker. The following 2 months; March and April see below.


NCDXC is inviting DXers and all those interested in hunting DX to two special Saturday afternoon meetings on March 14 & April 11 at 2:30 PM


Many Members and fellow HAMs work long days; these two Saturday meetings are an experiment to see if a more convenient day and time is needed for our meetings.


The Club’s regular March and April meetings will be held Saturday March 14 and April 11 at 2:30 PM at Holders Country Inn at 998 S.DeAnza Blvd., San Jose, 95129.


Meeting order for these Saturday Meetings will be:

2:30PM – 4:00PM - Club meeting & Program

4:00PM – 6:00PM - Social hour & dinner if desired


Speakers:

March 14 speaker: Paul, N6PSE, on what the DX hears, needs from us and how to work them;

April 11 speaker: John, K6MM, on how to use tracking/logging programs such as DX Lab or Club Log to record that DX
