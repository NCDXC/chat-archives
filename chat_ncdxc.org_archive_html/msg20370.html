Before FT8, I worked DX.  I still work DX without FT8 and find it quite satisfying.  I get easy  rag chews with Asia and Oceana when I look at the right time.  EU comes less frequently but on 40, 30, 20 and 17 recently.  AF is available on 40 M in our mornings regularly.

Sure, the contacts can be difficult at times but I welcome the effort when it pays off.  I still try what I can at 10 W before turning up the knob.

June QSOs: VU ( six on 20 CW), S5, F, FK, HS, UA, UA9, 4O, 9A, SM, YL.  Not particularly rare, but the VU confirmed a long sought slot on 20 CW.  Also got a pedestrian mobile in VK twice suing a vertical and backpack ex-military radio.

 

Pressing on with home improvement project in the meantime.  90% done painting the inside of the house, replaced a pocket door, new carpet, new vinyl plank floors, staining and finishing 6 other interior doors.  Got new granite countertops in kitchen and tile backsplash.  New kitchen LED lighting in.  Redwood deck on the list, all 900 ft-sq of it, will get refinished.  When that gets done, I will upgrade the 160 M antenna so I can make QSOs there and not feel totally lucky.

 

I hope all are well and having a good summer regardless of the lack of great condx or in-person meetings.

73,
Al
