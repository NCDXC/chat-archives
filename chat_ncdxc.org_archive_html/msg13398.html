Hi All,

I have an HP 4396A instrument available.  This is a combined VNA/ Spectrum analyzer/ Impedance analyzer unit.  It’s long since been discontinued by H-P/Keysight. The unit served by test and design needs for many years.  Sadly, it recently failed.  It’s beast of a unit, and I’ve replaced it with smaller equivalents, albeit not a nice as this HP unit.

The failure is in the CRT display - either the tube has gone, or the high voltage section.  It had current calibration and was in excellent working order till the display failed.  SIMM CONN Labs in SJ makes an LCD replacement kit for the CRT display.

I’d like to get this unit a new home to someone that is willing to repair it.  I’m asking $100.00 for the unit.

If you want to know more about this model of instrument, google ‘4396A'.  You will find many listings eBay, and even get the original manuals for it, as scanned PDFs.
