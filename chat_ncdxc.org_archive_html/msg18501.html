In the spirit of sharing findings, I recently replaced my old set of Bose QC-15 noise cancelling headphones with a much less expensive set called the Soundcore Life Q20  ($60 on Amazon).  Years back I discovered that digging out whisper-faint CW sigs was aided by cancelling out shack ambient noise from cooling fans and such.

I use them wired with my rig, but they support Bluetooth which works nicely when you want to stay up watching a show and not disturb your spouse. The Amazon FireTV stick sends out BT.

Anyway they are the around-the-ear, not the on-the-ear type and are quite comfortable for long butt-in-chair sessions. And the noise cancelling scheme, after this long, is way better I found than the old QC-15's. I'm sure the new Bose's are even better but at $$$, so these were a better value.
