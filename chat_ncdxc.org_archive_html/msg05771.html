Clearly propagation can be ³advantaged² by operating from different
locations.  If I operated 6m from the East Coast, I have many more DXCC
entities in ³normal² range than if I operate 6m from my QTH in Woodside.

operate split location to achieve 6m DXCC (and yes, I recognize this is
likely not in my present version of the future).

DXCC is seen by many as a competitive endeavor - hence the desire to get
on the honor roll.  To those to who this is competitive, equitability in
the process is appropriate.
