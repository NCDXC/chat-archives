On 8/15/2014 9:34 AM, Kevin Rowett via Chat wrote:

I’ve worked more DX waiting for the AMP to warm up, than with the AMP.
Same here -- sort of. I have both solid state and hollow state amps. The Elecraft KPA500 is instant on, gives me at least 600W (500W on 6M), and saves me money on the PG&E bill. I'm able to work most DX with it, but if the DX is much closer to the east coast, I'll fire up the Titan to be ready with legal limit if needed.
I use the Titans for contesting -- bigger signal, but also cleaner. Sure wish more guys would pay attention to the "cleaner" part.
