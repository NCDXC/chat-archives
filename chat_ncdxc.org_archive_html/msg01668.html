P5 on CW.

The last time there was a P5 CW signal was years ago. My email connection was dialup and I had two screens going, with email in back of me. I was in the shack and oblivious that W6OSP was frantically calling me on the phone---and getting a busy signal. Dialup. The news was on the cluster but I was not looking. Propwise, P5 is a slam dunk, no harder than JA and 20 was open. A total setup, which I did not put together. Last one for CW and should have been a gimme.

I share K6XN's recollection of 7O1YGF except I never bothered sending for the card, because they had not submitted documentation to ARRL that ARRL accepted. They were supposed to be "7Oscar1 Yemeni-German Friendship" but became known as "7O1 You Got F[rustrated]". Frustrated was not the actual word.

Fast forward to last year or so. I was invited to speak at REDXA by K6SRZ and I drove up to his place. He greeted me with "I got a new country today." Not having seen any new operations posted, I asked him what was new. He said "Yemen". I was a bit disoriented until he said YGF had been approved. "Then I have them all", I said. I sent for the card the next day.
