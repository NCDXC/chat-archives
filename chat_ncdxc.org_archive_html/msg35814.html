That is a rather rude and insulting statement.  Whoever phrased it this way, could do a lot better.  Not everyone seeks the same DX goals (that would be boring).

There is life beyond the ATNO and there are ample examples of those stations working well beyond that gateway.  Just watch them scroll by, any band, any day.  The number of uniques in the stats, will bear that out.

As a slot chaser, I’ve already had SEVERAL days of not calling Marek at all, because of the lack (for whatever reason) of flexibility to expand the repertoire of band modes.  Or that Marek quits, just as the west coast is opening (20 phone, more than a couple times).   Not targeting the west coast near mutual gray line, has proven his inexperience as well (PRIME time for the low bands, helpful in the higher bands).

Marek does not get that the west coast is not the east coast but needs treatment as almost another entity.

Yes, he is new at this and his time constrained by work and local demands.  But after several repeated stops when it comes to the west coast windows, one could wonder.

As a solo noob op, he has done well enough for a first timer as the DX, while showing room for many improvements over time should he continue.  I hope he CAN continue.  I’m not disappointed in him, just the lack of variety; some timing and some prop related; not unexpected.  10M to the west coast, difficult on any day while EU was a cakewalk.

Yes, he’s working the more productive bands, the money bands makes sense if sheer numbers are the goal.  But there is no reason to insult those wanting more in their DX logbooks than one contact.

I’m in the log, but don’t expect more slots for this entity.  I’ve moved onto the next target while continuing to watch.
