Thanks to voiceover artist - Josh Pierce, KM4UOL the audiobook version of “Ham Radio DX: A Complete Guide” is now available. There have been many requests for this to come out in the audiobook format and I finally decided to hire someone to handle this for me. At the time of purchase, there is a PDF available to download containing all the photos and illustrations from the book. The book came out to 15.5 Hours! Josh has an amazing voice and he did an absolutely amazing job. Not just saying that because it is my book, but I really mean it. If you are interested in the audiobook version, you can get a copy at: https://w6aer.com/audiobook

 

In fact, you can get it free with a trial of Audible using this above link. For the paperback version, please visit and support DX Engineering at: https://www.dxengineering.com/parts/vdx-dx-guide

 

For the Kindle edition (also Kindle Unlimited), please visit: https://w6aer.com/kindle

 

Have a favor to ask of the membership. If you own the book, and you have not done so already, can I kindly ask for a review or at least a rating? You can use the above links. For DX engineering, look for write a review (or just a rating) towards the bottom of the page. On Amazon it is about half way down, right below the review bars on the left. Much appreciated!

 

Lastly, I am gathering opinions on my YouTube channel and have a few 1 question surveys going on. These are for upcoming videos. Curious about your thoughts on the topics in question. If interested, please visit: https://www.youtube.com/@MaximumRF/community

 

Thank you, much appreciated!
