This is the latest incarnation of Tom Callas, the artist formerly known as KH0/KC0W. I was saddened to hear he had dropped out of ham radio, turned his wire antennas into fencing, and was no longer a late night presence on FT8. Despite that, I did receive all outstanding QSLs from him long after he was reported QRT, including the 80m verification I had been coveting.  
 According to QRZ he is now the trustee of the “Saipan CW Club” and operates exclusively mobile while cruising his idyllic Pacific home. No… actually while cruising!  He has a great signal, and a great fist. It’s nice to hear him again!
