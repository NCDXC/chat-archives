NCDXC friends, 

I have been monitoring 145.800 FM as it is one of the International Space Station downlink frequencies. 

In the past several days, I am hearing fishermen using this frequency. Never any call signs. They discuss depths in fathoms etc. Lots of profanity. Maybe if a few more of us listen, we can determine if they are out of Pillar Point or within the SF Bay?
