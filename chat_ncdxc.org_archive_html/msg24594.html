On Saturday, December 4, 2021, 06:13:40 PM PST, Roger Miller via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:


All,
Recall a few months ago I posted that Randy, WW6RG, is an airline pilot who flies into Wake Island KH9 on a regular schedule, and is accepting skeds as KH9/WW6RG.
He operates with a IC-705 QRP radio and a magnetic loop antenna.

I submitted the QSL to ARRL for DXCC credit.
I've been informed by ARRL that the QSO is not acceptable for DXCC credit until Randy submits proof of a "landing permit".

I'm not sure exactly how or when he will submit that proof, but he has been informed of the issue, so I'll leave it up to him to settle it.

Until then, please be aware of this if you have worked him and have submitted a card, or are planning to.

Thanks, best DX,
Roger
