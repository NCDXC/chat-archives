I'm always amazed with the guys who operate their stations with skill & finesse. I've compared them to skilled surgeons who get in and out. 
There is an active group of us on the W6TI repeater that share DX spots and encourage and applaud each other's successes. 

Many of us have been chasing VK9DX on six meters. The openings are minutes long. Yesterday was the first time I decoded VK9DX, but I had to wait for him
to work the local DXer who worked him a few days ago as witnessed by many of us. I see this not as an insurance QSO but an inconsiderate operator, who by the way
was chewed out on the Club repeater a few days ago by another member for doing the very same thing. Some of us are tired of inconsiderate behavior and my frustration yesterday was vented on the Club chat reflector which I regret. 

73 to all, 

Paul N6PSE
