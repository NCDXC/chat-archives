finally came way up a few minutes ago.  I think
I was the first west coast station of the day.

working the east slowly, often taken several
times to complete the QSO.  Its like on 160

don't hear the station come back to you.

this may be an opportunity.  OTOH, I am not
optimistic for 12 meters based on this.
