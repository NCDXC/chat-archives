Here on the Central Coast, things got a bit crazy. 

I installed my weather station in 2012. The highest wind gust it had ever recorded was 41mph in 2022. Today it saw several 54mph gusts. The whole house was shaking. I had lowered my tower completely the day before. I watched the DB-36 dance in the wind all afternoon. Only 1.43 inches of rain so far.

The only damage was we lost a fence between our neighbors and us.
