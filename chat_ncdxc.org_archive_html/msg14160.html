Hello fellow club members,

 

    It is with great sadness that I report the passing of Don Amyx, N6DA, this

evening (11/21/2017).  He suffered from cancer that was diagnosed as stage 4

lung cancer 18 months ago.  The doctors initially thought he had from a few weeks

to a few months to live when he was diagnosed in April of 2016.  But he responded

well to the chemo and he managed to stay alive for 18 months.  My wife and I

visited him in the hospital last Saturday just after he had finished a round of kidney

dialysis.  We knew he was sinking but had no idea it would come so fast.  I met Don

when we were 13 years old, 57 years ago.  He was WA6TQK and I was WA6SII and

we met through ham radio.  Sorry to ramble on ... this news is just a couple of hours

old.  Thanks for listening.

 

73,

Jim W6SC
