I also use v29 of firefox (on Mac) and had long ago gotten rid of
Propfire - while I loved it, it seemed to have trouble updating to get
new numbers.

Today I installed it again and it does indeed install and work -
albeit in the "new" add-on bar, which in my browser is up in the upper
right corner.

You may have tried this - but if not, it might be worth uninstalling
the propfire add-on, and re-installing it from the add-on store.
