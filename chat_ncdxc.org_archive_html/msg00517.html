When you make a LoTW upload, you should watch the results on the screen. On the left side of the screen below the menu, it will say tell you that your submission is queued for [        ] processing. You should watch for that message. It means that your log has been received and placed in the queue. If you do see it,  you should NOT make any duplicate submissions. That slows the system.

 

An exact duplicate won’t take nearly as much time as processing the whole log, but it does take time and should be avoided. If you make ANY change in the log, even one byte, and then resubmit it, the whole log will be re-processed.

 

Some people regularly submit cumulative logs. That is a major problem for LoTW. You should use the date window if you can’t separate parts of your logs. The date window allows you ask LotW to process only the parts of a log between two dates that you enter. Watch for it as you are signing your logs. Trimming your log to only what you want to submit will speed LoTW processing for everyone.
