Rich,

This is great news, but the likelihood of little (and medium) pistols making successful QSO's is much higher on FT8 than SSB. Also QRM (and DQRM) tend to be worse on SSB.

If Marek could just pick one or two bands with best propagation on FT8, and ignore all stations already in the log, it would go very far toward maximizing the number of ATNOs.

He also might want to call "CQ ATNO" for those who have not gotten the word.

Just my two cents. Like many hams around the world, I'd really appreciate the opportunity to get that ATNO!
