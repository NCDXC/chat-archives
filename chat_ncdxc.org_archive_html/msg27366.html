Also coming soon: Bouvet 3Y0J.

The operating team will be leaving for the Falklands in about ten days, then 1-2 weeks sail to the island, and a few days of setup, weather permitting. Expect SOME (brief) operation by individuals under their own callsigns, e.g. 3Y0GIA, 3Y0THA, 3Y/LB5GI, etc. These count just as much as 3Y0J for approved awards, and they will be quite the rare set of callsigns!

The Bouvet team has pre-coordinated their operating frequencies with Thierry on Crozet, so for whatever overlap there may be between them, the operating AND pile-up (receive) frequencies will not clash.

We wish “Fair Winds and Following Seas” to the Bouvet team. (For those who don’t know or realize it, they will actually be going on a SAILBOAT, in the South Ocean. The Marama is quite a vessel!)
