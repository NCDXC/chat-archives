In my humble opinion, the Array Solutions Shared Apex Array (SAL) 30 is probably the best directional/switchable receiving antenna. Too bad that Array Solutions has closed and the SAL-30 is no longer available. It was an improvement on the K9AY loops. The only downside to the SAL-30 is that it requires a fair amount of space. 

Otherwise, great receiving antenna. I wish I had room for one.
