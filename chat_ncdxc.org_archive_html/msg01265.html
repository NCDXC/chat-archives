On 2/13/2013 6:42 AM, paul@n6pse.com wrote:
Craig makes some pretty good points about the Kenwood TS-590 vs the Elecraft K3.

Again, I was comparing low cost radios like the TS-590 to the KX3 with it's 100W power amp, NOT the K3.  If I was doing the packing for a trip, I'd give the KX3 with it's power amp (when it's a product) a serious look.

The genius of the KX3 design is that while it CAN be a backpacking rig, and while it IS the size of the removable head of the TS480, when you add the 100W amp it's the QUALITY of the K3 but at the price of a 480 or 590. Those who fail to realize this are missing one of the nicest bargains in ham radio.  No, it doesn't have all the bells and whistles of a K3, but if you're on a DX trip, it's got everything you need!
