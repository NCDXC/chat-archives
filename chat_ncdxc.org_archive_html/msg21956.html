It was 1/1/2013 at 0625 UTC that I worked Monk Apollo SV2ASP/A on 40 meter SSB for my first contact with Mt Athos. 

 

It was really a thrill to work him as I had travelled to Mount Athos only a few months earlier and I saw how modest his 40 meter station was. He had a trapped two element yagi about eight feet off the ground. His Yagi was in bad shape and not very horizontal.  We spent some time adjusting his antennas for him and talking in his shack. 

 

 Monk Apollo remembered my visit and said "Hello Mr Paul-Nice to see you again"

 

I had seen him spotted on DX Summit and I saw that he was working the East Coast, then the midwest and finally the West Coast. I remember Rich-KY6R also worked him right about the same time. 

 

That was a very memorable contact. 

 

Happy New Year from N6PSE!

 

Paul N6PSE
