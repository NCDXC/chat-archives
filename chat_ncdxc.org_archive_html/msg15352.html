Since I'm a Comcast customer and was affected by this (yay for LTE by Verizon), I did a little digging, and got more info.


The outage was actually with CenturyLink (owner of L3) and Zayo (another provider). Both of these companies provide backbone connectivity to Comcast. It was 2 unrelated CenturyLink fiber outages in NC, plus the single Zayo fiber severed near Chicago, that together caused the outage.


It's not a common occurrence for 2 providers to experience 3 unrelated issues all at once, lightning must have struck... LOL


I feel sorry for the on-call techs, it was near the end of the work day for the folks back east.
