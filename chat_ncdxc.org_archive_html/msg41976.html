One problem I've observed in F/H is when the hound falls back to the fox frequency after being contacted, and for whatever reason the QSO doesn't complete, the Hound then continues to transmit on top of the Fox frequency which could interfere with other QSOs  in the works as the Fox has moved on, rather than start over again above 1000Hz.
