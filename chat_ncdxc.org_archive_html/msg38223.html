I’m not sure I qualify as a brain, but…
I’ve been using WSJT improved 2.7.1 which is in the process of continual refinement and does not have a drop dead date. The guy who programs the “improved” versions is now an official part of the dev team. 
To the best of my knowledge, you do not need to uninstall previous versions- just install “on top” of the old one and your settings are preserved. 
I’ve been delighted with my version of 2.7.1! Decodes down to -27, a gorgeous spectrum display, and buttons to switch bands. It’s intuitive and takes about one QSO to adapt to the changes. 
It’s available at SourceForge.
