This is the wrong time of day for ZS on 20 meters.
They normally come in on long path during
our morning.  I once observed a station operating
mobile in W6 work another mobile in ZS on 20
meters during our morning using SSB mode.
With all the gratuitous dB's on FT8, ZS is truly
a chip shot via that mode.

During the CQWWSSB contest this last weekend,
there was a ZS station on 40 meters that was
an easy QSO both days.

Rick N6RK
