On Mon, Mar 17, 2014 at 7:54 PM, Paul@n6pse.com <paul@n6pse.com> wrote:
The Italian DXpedition is now QRT from Zambia as 9J2T,

How did you do with them?

I had a blast with this DXpedition, their signals in California were strong and the operators were quite good!   These guys sure know where the West Coast is. They made 71,728 contacts!

Their pileups were well managed and they stayed with the propagation. I worked them from 10-40 meters. I actually heard them on 80 meters a few times!

On top of this, the operators seemed to be having a good time. Zambia was not rare but they sure gave out the band fills to anyone who wanted them, there near real time logging system is quite good!

A really good show by the Italians,

Paul N6PSE
