Initially licensed in 1962 and continuously active, I have held every class of amateur license the FCC has gone through. In the early years there were extended periods when I did nothing but CW. I don't believe was ever very proficient and as such got away from it. I "played" the FT modes off and on for a year or so. The technical magic soon wore off. I am now back to my preferred mode of J3E where I can talk and share the experience with a live person. I enjoy working DX and understand the political and monetary issues faced to put on a successful expedition to the "rare one." Not making the "honor role" will not end my life and I prefer to wait for a voice out of the ether from a live body rather than a box.
