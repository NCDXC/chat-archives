Hi Donna,

I would recommend a US Tower TX-489 (90 foot) crank up or motorized tilt over tower. i would install a "Tilt Plate" for easy antenna maintenance.

If a fixed tower is desired, I'd go with a AN Wireless.

I would go with K7NV prop pitch rotor or M2 Orion 2800. Antenna SteppIR DB-42 covers 6-80 meters. I would hang an Alpha Delta 80/160 inverted V dipole from the tower. I would use Davis Bury Flex Coax.

You could have a Comet GP6 at the top of the mast for VHF/UHF needs.  
Install beverage antennas as needed for receiving on low bands.

I would recommend Kurt-K7NV and Glenn-K6NA of "Tower Busters" to build this dream station.

Kurt and Glenn just installed a Steppir DB36 for me on my 72' tower. They really know their stuff.

Good Luck!
