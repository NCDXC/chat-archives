On 2025-09-30 10:42 pm, Bob Wilson, N6TV via Chat wrote:

All ARRL Members within the Pacific Division with a current email address on file at the ARRL should have received an Electronic ballot by now.  The subject line begins with "ARRL Division Election" and it is sent by "ARRLElection@mg.electionservicescorp.com".
 
If you don't see it, check your Spam folder or "ARRL": folder if you auto-file emails with "ARRL" in the subject line.
 
Members without an email address on file with the ARRL should receive a paper ballot by mail.
 
This is an important election to select the next ARRL Pacific Division Director.  I recommend voting now rather than later, before the email gets pushed to the bottom of your inbox.
 
If you are a paid-up ARRL Member in the Pacific Division who never received a ballot. I recommend contacting ARRL HQ as soon as possible.
 
https://www.arrl.org/contact-arrl
