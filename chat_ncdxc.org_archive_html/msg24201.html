Bob,

I used a Heathkit DX60 TX and a 75A-2 RX with an Amphenol coax relay to switch between TX and RX.  As I recall, the relay had external open frame contacts that I used to short out the RX antenna jack during transmit.  There was enough leakage to provide a TX sidetone.  Never tried fully muting the RX.
