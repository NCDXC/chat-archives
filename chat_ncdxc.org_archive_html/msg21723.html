I have worked Chris 12 times and actually met him at the A4 club station in Muscat.  Oman faces the Indian ocean and has a great LP look.  Heading 180 from that is hills to mountains that separate Oman from HZ lands.  Not sure if those interfere with SP propagation or not.

Oman is generally sparsely populated but the urban areas look like a lot of middle eastern countries: haywire overhead wires, crowds, warrens of buildings, etc.  A high noise level would not surprise me at all.  I do not know where he is located in relation to the older parts of Muscat.
