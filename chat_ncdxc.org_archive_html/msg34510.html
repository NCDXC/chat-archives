They appear to be using MSHV (an EU favorite) not f/h mode... BUT after getting a report twice, recognizing the intensity of the pileup; I shifted to F/H mode on my third answer, which put me in a more clear tone space and I was then heard, closing the exchange.

A small trick and I might have gotten through without it; but one tool in the box available to us.

They are also using a tone ~500, which means that some successfully made contact with a tone BELOW the DX team...  (Don't go below 300 Hz, the roll off of the filters can be dramatic below 300 Hz., they won't hear you.)
