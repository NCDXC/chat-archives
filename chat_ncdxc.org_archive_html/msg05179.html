Here's an interesting fact taken from N0UN's blog.  I live only 200 miles away from Amsterdam's antipode.

Here's a lil' tidbit I bet you didn't know:  "Colorado is one of only two continental US states (along with Montana) which is antipodal to land.  Amsterdam Island is antipodal to a spot 15 miles SE of Lamar, Colorado and Saint-Paul Island is antipodal to a spot 7 miles SW of Cheyenne Wells, Colorado".  (Northern Montana is antipodal to Kerguelen Island).

Here’s a link to a “tunneling” (or antipode) map. Kinda neat gadget. Remember, you’ll only find land on the antipode from near Lamar, Colorado (Amsterdam), Cheyenne Wells, Colorado (St. Paul) and Northern Montana (Kergeulen). 73, N0UN
