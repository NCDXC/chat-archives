I have an inverted V with the apex at 55' for 80m.

I only listened one day and they were ESP until about 20 minutes before sunrise. Their signal quickly rose to about 15 dB above my noise floor and stayed there for about 10 minutes. They came back to my first call.

I heard them the night before, but the antenna was in its 160m configuration, which the KAT500 can't load on 80m.

I'm using a K3 + KPA500.
