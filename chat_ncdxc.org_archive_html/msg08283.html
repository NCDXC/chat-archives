Garry,

I have a similar noise issue here.  I am fortunate in that my noise source is from mostly in one direction from a power line.
I am using a phasing control box from DX Engineering, their NCC-1 with a separate small noise antenna and am able to null
the noise down to the band noise most of the time on the low bands where I need the help.    If your noise is from one direction it may worth trying a null approach.
I still haven’t been able to work  FT4TA though, hihi.
