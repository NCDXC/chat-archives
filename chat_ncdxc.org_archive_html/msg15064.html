I ran into the same problem.  A little help from a friend and some research resolved it.

 

Yes Windows 10 1803 makes a change blocking the use of the microphone.  It is a privacy issue – safety.  (Thanks for the clue about notes in the DXLabs user group.)

 

Go to Settings via the Window icon on the left end of the task bar. 

 

In the “Windows Settings” screen, select Privacy. 

 

Select Microphone and turn the switch to ON for “Allow apps to access your microphone”.

 

Now applications can use the microphone.

 

Adjust levels –

 

Right click the speaker icon next to the time and date at the right hand end of the task bar.  Select Sound.

 

Select the tab of interest and in Properties adjust the LEVEL.  I found the bar graphs don’t show any signal in Recording unless the level is way too high.  Adjust the level watching the vertical receive bar in WSJT.  Play with the Playback device and the Pwr bar on WSJT.
