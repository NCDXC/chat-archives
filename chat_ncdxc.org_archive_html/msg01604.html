This was the first DXpedition after installing the KPA500.

What a difference! I worked them in 13 of the 19 bandslots and 8 out of 9 bands. Except for 160m, it didn't take more than 3 calls to work them on any band. 160 and 40m were new for me.

160m was a zoo. Several guys that I used to respect, not only wouldn't quit calling when 9M4SLL came back to someone else; but, would then QSY to that frequency and call on top of them. This really slowed down the rate.

My only complaint (confirmed by the ClubLog statistics) was the low amount of time allocated to NA on 160-40m. I worked them on 160 and 40m well past sunrise. They often didn't start LF operations until 30 minutes before west coast sunrise.

Steve, K6UM
