I am happy to say that I finally have a room in the house that I can make into my "permanent" shack.

I feel the AC power upgrade is now under control, so want to focus on antenna connections. I'm picturing a surface-mounted box on the exterior wall near the peak of the roof. It would terminate the external cables, and bring them safely inside where they can be run down inside that same wall to the operating position. I'd like to have capacity for at least 5 coax lines plus 3 rotator/SteppIr control cables.

I'm still in my 6th year as a ham, and my current station was built kind of ad-hoc as my experience and budget grew. I'd like to do things right from the beginning this time.

I know at least a few members have done major shack upgrades lately. I appreciate any and all feedback. Feel free to reply directly to repoma6103@aol.com, as I don't want to clutter the chat list.
