For me, 6O3T and E44OM were good operations.  I liked it that 603T made an extra effort to work the furthest stations. That is a feature of Elvira and I was glad to see her influence on this small team.  E44OM was very active 24X7 and when propagation carried them our way they were not difficult to work. 

FW5K was a highlight because no one ever does 160M when they go to FW and they made an extra effort on 160. Thank you K6VHF!
FP5KE was super easy to work and was active on all the bands and modes with a huge team. 

For me, the highlight of 2025 was being a part of the HD8G Galapagos team. I had always wanted to go to Galapagos and it was a great pleasure to join the Araucaria DX Group and spend an enjoyable week with them. We had some technical challenges but we overcame them and had a great time. 

Let's hope that 2026 brings us an equal amount of great DX to chase. 

Paul N6PSE
