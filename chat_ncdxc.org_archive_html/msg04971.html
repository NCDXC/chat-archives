It took me ~ 20 years,with intervening years plagued by overseas duty tours,several wars,and a bunch of changes/deletions/re-emergence of once worked&credited countries that were deleted & came back as independents etc.....for example, once while k6md.... in Germany the Caribbean Island became independent, and as 'old' counters,were deleted....so, I was suddenly 5 down from my count and had to re-work them,get qsls all over again,re-submit,etc...so,it took a while........but,I am now in the lucky position to have to only keep up with 'new ones'.........and yes, it was a looooong time hunting.......and the first 300 were sans repeaters,nets call-outs,etc.....I did work ~ 20  or so on DX nets,since I only had dipoles & verticals mostly....no real beam until ~ 1989.....made HR ~ 1991, HR#1 in 2013......k6md....
