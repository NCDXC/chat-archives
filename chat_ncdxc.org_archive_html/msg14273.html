I wanted to echo my colleague Paul’s comments.  With an addition.  I wanted to personally thank the NCDXC for its sponsorship of the upcoming VP6D expedition to Ducie Island(and of course other dxpeditions as well!) .  The NCDXC is so very generous when teams are trying to pull it together to get to these rare entities and we truly appreciate it.

 

A bit of background.  Having started in Ham Radio and DXing well into my 40’s, VP6DX 2008 was one of,  if not THE first Dxpedition I ever worked for credit.  So as you can imagine, it’s a great honor to be on the team that will present the entity to the next cycle of DXers a decade later, and hopefully some different band/mode slots for those who were there for that one as well.  Rest assured, we will be listening for you!

 

Thanks to the NCDXC for your pro-active support for all things DX.  I am grateful to be part of this organization.

 

P.S.  – Sorry for a bit of reflector silence from N6WM.  I am currently serving actively as VP/CC of the NCCC, as well as VP6D team member, and as an alpha tester of new Flex Radio technology, some fresh new toys of which are sitting in my shack now undergoing testing. (the Power Genius XL is quite a piece of equipment) I miss seeing you all and will try and get to a meeting soon!

 

73 ex gd dx, and cu in the Bouvet pile ups!

 

Chris
