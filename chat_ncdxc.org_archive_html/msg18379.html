On Mon, Dec 23, 2019 at 6:40 PM Rick Bates, NK7I via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

I have worked him twice so far (80 and 30M).  I've been occupied with things for the holidays so it hasn't been a serious effort.

It is not fox/hound but allows multi-stream (with the power out reduction).  Last night on 30M, he was running three streams (each -14), I didn't hang around to see his CQ (usually given as one stream).  I would expect that CQ to be in the -5 range (slam dunk for the west coast).

So if you hear him at all, he will likely hear you.  I used 200 watts (legal limit) into the DB36 (3 elements) at 20 meters for a -5 report.

One small niggling point is that he should sign /R (for Rotuma, but often means remote) because as he is logged, it comes up as Fiji, so each time the log must be changed (QRZ and Clublog, but eqsl and hrdnet seem to accept it).

Antoine also doesn't normally use LOTW (very expensive Internet on SoPac islands).  I've not checked on on how he will QSL.

I have him on 2-3 bands from his home station, a PayPal charge (a few bucks) was enough to cover his mailing expense for a card.
