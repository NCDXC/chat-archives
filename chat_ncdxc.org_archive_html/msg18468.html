On Jan 3, 2020 9:43 PM, Paul N6PSE via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
It is being reported on the NCCC reflector that Low Band Legend John Devoldere ON4UN is SK. 

John was a pillar in the DX/Contesting world and very well known for his many publications around low band antennas. 

John always contacted me when we announced a DXpedition and he always quizzed me about our low band plans. He always shared his views and made positive suggestions. 

When we were going to the new country of South Sudan in 2011 he told me to forget about our 160 meter sloper plans hanging from the water tower of the hotel and urged us to use an Inverted L which we did. He said the sloper would have interaction with the structure supporting the water tower. We assumed he was right and changed our plans. 

He was a very bright and positive person who had a profound effect on me. I wish this news were not true. 

Rest in Peace John ON4UN.
