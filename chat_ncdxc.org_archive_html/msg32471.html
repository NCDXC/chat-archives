More info on XU7GNY.   Ham radio was not allowed in Cambodia until June 2023.  XU7GNY was operating in Feb/Mar.  In addition, the license that was provided to the DXCC desk was expired.  Therefore, his approval was revoked. If XU7GNY was able to renew his license, then the current operation will be OK.  We will have to wait and see if and when he sends documentation to the DXCC desk. 73 John K9EL
