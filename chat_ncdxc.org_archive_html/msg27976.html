According to Adrian (one of the team members on Bouvet), he has “verified” that they are running WSJT-X in F/H mode. Everyone has noticed that they are transmitting ODD, and everyone I have checked with says that it is impossible to run WSJT-X on ODD as a Fox. But that’s what they SAY they are doing.

I worked them last night (at least I think I did), using JTDX in Hound mode, transmitting EVEN, i.e., the reverse of normal WSJT-X. Bernie McClenny published a note about using JTDX, or using WSJT in Normal (not Hound) mode and then moving yourself down to the low frequency to complete the exchange.

No one really knows what is going on until we see the logs.
