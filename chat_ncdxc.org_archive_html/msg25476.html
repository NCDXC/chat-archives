N7RCA NORTHERN NEVADA ANNUAL HAMFEST

 

Minden NV Our 19th year ARRL Sanctioned Event

DATE: JUNE 4, 2022, 7:00 AM TO 12:00 PM

RAIN OR SHINE EVENT

Electronics, ham radio, audio, Hi-Fi, boat anchors, radio, computers

 

HOTEL UPDATE: Due to the events happening in Minden there is a hotel

room shortage.

Towns close by: Carson City, S Lake Tahoe, Topaz, appx 20 to 30 min.

 

This is a free event for hobbyists with an interest in all

electronics, Buy, Sell, Trade

Free overnight RV, tent, parking etc. on site.

Sellers can set up the night before, (please let N7RCA know)

 

Electronics including, Ham Radio, Boat Anchors, Broadcast transmitters,

radios, audio gear, electronics, tubes, modern gear HF, VHF, UHF Test equip,

audio and more!

Bring tables, chairs, coat, hats, food, and drinks (no refreshments on site)
