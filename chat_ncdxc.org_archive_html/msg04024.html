Two things:

 

1)            DXA has been shut down to technical difficulties (satellite uplink). They will focus on fixing satellite uplink issues so they can send their Clublog updates instead. Please QRX.

2)            The e-Pilot idea has been a smash hit. Several DX-ers have praised the idea – because it has eliminated the confusion of sending emails to pilots and then aggregating that email. Instead – with only one pilot – all comments and requests are moderated, collected and sent twice a day to the team! There is zero confusion – which was my biggest problem in previous DX-peditions acting as a pilot. All of the requests posted on http://banaba2013.wordpress.com have now been sent to the team.
