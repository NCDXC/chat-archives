Hi All,

For anyone who needs the UN HQ entity (4U1UN), there is a multi-single effort planned for the ARRL SSB DX Contest this coming weekend.  The ops will be K2QI, KO8SCA, N2RJ and OH2BH.  Unfortunately, the station does not have antennas for either 80m or 160m, so they will be operating only from 40m to 10m.  Because 4U1UN is DX in this contest, they will be looking to work only USA and Canadian stations!  :-)
