Here is your NCDXC Question of the week (QoTW)
 
 
What is your most memorable DX Contact?   What contact sticks in your mind as most memorable to you?
 
 
 
One of my most memorable contacts occurred only a few weeks ago during the last hours of 2012. I saw SV2ASP/A posted on DX Summit on 40 meters SSB. Having met him some weeks earlier and having seen his antennas, I had doubt that I would ever work this entity, however I decided to go into my shack and fire up all of my equipment and take a listen. Much to my surprise, I heard Monk Apollo working W6's and W7's. I fired up my amplifier and it only took three calls to him. He said "Hello N6PSE nice to meet you again. You are 5-6 5-6 QSL?   I was speechless!
 
Other quite memorable contacts for me are my CW/SSB and RTTY contacts with T6AF/WA2EWE Jim McLaughlin (SK) from his QTH in Kabul Afghanistan. He was from the West Coast and he was so eager to work all of us out here. He was really patient and he operated very well. I had met him only two weeks before his death and I still think of him often.
 
 
Paul N6PSE
