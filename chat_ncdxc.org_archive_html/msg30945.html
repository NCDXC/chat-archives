Tonight, I am decoding ZD9W fairly well on 30m FT8. 
He is running three streams. 
The Stream at 537 Hz has been giving a signal report to W3ICM for an hour. 
The Stream at 596 Hz has been giving a signal report to W7CQQ for an hour. 
Only the third stream at 656 is working new stations. 

My EU friends tell me that they like MSHV because they can put it into "robot mode" and go to sleep. 

I believe this is what I am seeing right now with ZD9W.  I also saw this same type of operating from FT8WW where you just knew that if an Operator was there, awake and alert, he would intervene, but the constant calling of one station persisted for quite some time. This happened frequently. 

I believe that MSHV is a buggy derivative and has been manipulated beyond what the creators of FT4/FT8 intended.
