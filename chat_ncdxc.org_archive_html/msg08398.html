As many of you already know (but some may not) Dr. Laura is an excellent operator and QSLer and she will promptly return a traditional QSL card if you send her yours with an SASE and postage. She didn’t ask me for an SASE but I traditionally send one for those stations whom I need a QSL from who do not appear to use the ARRL LOTW or QSL via LOTW.

 

I don’t know if Dr. Laura is now using LOTW but for me the traditional paper QSL with an SASE worked great.
