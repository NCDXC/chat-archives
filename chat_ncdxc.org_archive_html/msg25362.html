I am so very sad to learn of the passing of my dear friend Yasuo "Zorro" Miyazawa JH1AJT earlier this morning in Japan. 

Zorro had been battling reoccurring cancer for many years. 

Zorro was a Humanitarian, a business leader and a fine and generous human being.  I was fortunate to join him for radio adventures to Myanmar and Eritrea. He was working on some future projects for some very rare entities. He had such passion for radio and while on DXpeditions he loved working ten meters. 

I hope that Zorro is now free of the pain as he had been suffering. I hope he is at peace.

Champ E21EIC has written a nice tribute and posted it on DX-World.net This is a big loss for the amateur radio community. 

Paul N6PSE
