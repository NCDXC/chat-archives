This FTDX10 is truly like new.  I made my first QSO on 1/19/23 and first QSO on the replacement K4D rig was 3/19/23.  I then went on vacation to Qatar (A7) for almost 3 weeks.

I bought this rig to get on FT8 fast so I could work Bouvet Island and that is what happened.  I had never used the mode and wanted a way to get active and this rig was easy to get going and to use.  I did hook up a connection to my amplifier and it drives the amp fine so most of my contacts were with low output from the FTDX10. 

The rig has no flaws of any kind and there are no smokers or pets in our home.  My shack is a dedicated room in a new house.  Images via this link:  https://photos.app.goo.gl/8EzqzKxzgNnERXqB7

I made one SSB QSO and one CW QSO before getting FT8 going.  I am selling because I wanted to try a rig from Elecraft after three high end Yaesu rigs including this one.  All of which were excellent, but I now use the K4D and am still learning it.

The rig comes with its DC power supply cord, microphone, bag of piece parts and a USB cable all in the factory box and plastic bags.  The manual, map and decal are in its plastic bag, unused.  I used the online manual, so the printed manual is perfect, just like the rig.    If you want a copy of my invoice, images of my log showing when I first and last used it, I will supply so you know that this really is a new rig with extremely ‘low mileage’.  Finally, if you want a more images, I will take it back out of the box and get you what you want to see.  If you are going to get one of these and want to save a few bucks, this is the one to consider.

Offered at $1,199 and I will pay half the domestic shipping.  Double boxed, of course!
