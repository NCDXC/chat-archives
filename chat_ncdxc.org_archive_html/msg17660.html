Gang,

 

This is a question for the NCDXC brain trust.

 

For years I have heard Lou EA3JE on 80, 40 and 20 meters. He always says

he is running a Yaesu FTDX1000D and stacked Yagis on 20 meters. He sometimes 

mentions 4 el on 40 meters. I have never heard him discuss his 80 meter antenna situation.

He always gives his location as Barcelona Spain. His QRZ address is also in Barcelona.

Looking at his QTH in Google Earth does NOT reveal a huge antenna farm but

he could be operating remotely even from the USA. I always wondered how he can

hear so well and have an honest 15 dB over S9 signal at K6YP. He has given me

15 dB over S9 reports when I am calling him with 50W (exciter power).

 

I called him today and he was easily 20 dB over S9 even though conditions were

terrible. He was the ONLY signal from EU at the time I worked him at 2209Z. 

Absolutely no QSB. Amazingly I could hear his signal coming around the world

a second time. I looked at me received audio with an O'scope and sure enough his

echo was delayed about 0.13 second or one transit around the globe. He gave me

a 10 dB over S9 report today. I was running 100W into a 3 element SteppIR up 43 ft.

 

Hearing Lou's second trip echo probably negates the possibility of a remote station

unless he has more than one with a 0.13 sec latency. He heard me unbelievably well

and he heard other guys all across the US well too. He was as he usually does calling 

for North America an working stuff coast to coast.

 

Either he has a huge antenna system (side by side towers with 5 over 5 over 5 over 5 

element beams on each tower) with 10 dBi gain for each beam for a total  gain close to

19 dB to have the performance he seems to have. He would also need a quiet location and

a damn big amplifier. One would think he would have shown this setup to his buddies

and it would be reported on the internet. There really is not a lot of creditable info on EA3JE

on the web. I have been hearing Lou for a least 30 years. My guess is he is in his 60's to 80's

from listening to him on the air. Not a spring chicken.

 

I am writing this because I am puzzled. Do any of you have more info on EA3JE?

 

73,

 

john k6yp
