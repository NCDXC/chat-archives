What I've done over the years with my towers has been to put an extra bearing between the rotator and the top bearing of the tower. This way the two bearings  work in tandem and take the flex and pressure off the rotator itself. Yes extra money, but money well spent. It's cheaper to replace a mast than a rotator any day.
