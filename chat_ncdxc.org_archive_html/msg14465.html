Michael,

You might want to consider an outdoor autotuner at the feed point of your antenna.
The 43' vertical will have a high vswr on several bands. If the tuner is in the shack
you will have a (long?) run of coax with a high vswr at the load end. This will significantly
increase the loss of your feed line. With 100W you need all the power possible going to
the antenna and not being dissipated in the coax. If LDG makes an outdoor unit I would
consider it. MFJ makes several outdoor autotuners. You will want toi protect the tuner
from strong rain by putting a roof over it. BTW I would take LDG over MFJ.

73 and DX,

john k6yp
