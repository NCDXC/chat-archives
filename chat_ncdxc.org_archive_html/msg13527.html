I just wanted to take a moment to convey my appreciation to John K6MM, Kevin K6TD and Rich KE1B for an outstanding experience at Visalia this year.  I had to reluctantly come home a bit early, but grateful to have spent Thurs, Fri and a good chunk of Saturday at the event. 

 

It was well executed, professional and the content of the material presented was of highest quality.

 

Assessment : You guys knocked it out of the park.  This was the most fun I have ever had at IDXC and the opportunity for the eyeball qso’s from friends from all over the world will be well cheerfully remembered.

 

I also wanted to Thank Don K6GHA and  Tom KW6S and all others involved for a stellar job coordinating NCCC Contest academy 2017. I was honored to be able to contribute as a presenter for the first time in many years.  The quality of all the presenters was top notch, and IMO added huge value to the paying IDXC participants looking to step up their contest game.   Folks who signed up for this got their money’s worth for sure.

 

Oh and also to Saraj KU6F and Alan K6SRZ for enabling the NCCC hospitality suite, always one of the best parts of IDXC.

 

Great Job to all involved in this years IDXC and it was great to see all that were able to make it down to Visalia!

 

Thanks and 73

 

Chris

N6WM
