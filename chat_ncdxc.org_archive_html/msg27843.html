Because you need a digital mode QSO for 3Y0?

Not many parts of the world are either a) awake or b) have prop at this time of evening  (0200-0400) so go for it and don't feel too guilty.

In the A case, real DX'ers get out of bed so zero sympathy if someone finds working the expedition inconvenient to their home schedule :-).

There is a huge difference between picking up a few band/mode slots and being a true "DX Hog".

My analysis of PDXG past expedition logs shows a power law distribution with a *very* small percentage of call signs that work us more than a few times. I think it's a concern that is being way overblown leaders. Personally I am looking for twofers. I need digital and I need 17m. If I can get that early I will. Otherwise since I already have 30 CW I will wait and hopefully they will still be on "later".

The people on this list are some of the biggest donors to major expeditions in the world. I'll leave it at that.

Go get 'em.
