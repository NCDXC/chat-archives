Rich,

 

Not me. 

 

I had a goal to reach of 300 confirmed mixed and I got it.  I have over 100 and close to 300 confirmed across 6 bands.  But I was only interested in reaching and passing 300 mixed.  The DXCC desk has my credits but I have no desire for certificates after the initial mixed, CW and Phone awards were received.

 

Similarly for WAS.

 

I love the chase!  Also of course a good rag-chew QSO too.

 

Also note where I live.  I really like where I live and it is kind of where I have to live.  The downside is CCR, HOA and the City of San Jose limiting what I can do on my 4000sf lot.  So a 135ft dipole and a 2-element per 10-15-20 meter band yagi has so far not caused any grief in the neighborhood.  But I am pushing the edges of the envelope here.

 

On with the chase…

 

In the meantime FT8 or a spattering of other digital modes occupy the void between ATNOs.

 

What I love most? Tinkering with and learning how these radios work.  Doesn’t take long for the screws holding the covers on to come off.

 

73,

Bob – W6OPO
