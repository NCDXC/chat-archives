Good Morning,

  This is a second call for a member volunteer to take up the position of Awards Chairperson. As you may know the NCDXC presents annual awards in the categories of Member of the Year, DXer of The Year and the W6OL CW Award. We need an impartial individual, a member in good standing, to head up the selection committee. You will pick your own team members, take candidates for each award and select the winners. The deadline for selection is in February to allow for time to order the award plaques in time to present at this year's IDXC Banquet on April 13th. So, time is of the essence. We need you NOW! To volunteer or ask questions, reply to this email. Thank you.
