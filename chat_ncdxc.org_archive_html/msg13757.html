Looking through my log, I have 112 QSOs with Greece (not including contests). The majority are in the 0230-0600Z range (late afternoon, local time), on all bands from 40-10 meters. (This roughly corresponds with local sunset.) There are another bunch in the 1600-1900Z period (late morning to noonish), all on the high bands, of course.
