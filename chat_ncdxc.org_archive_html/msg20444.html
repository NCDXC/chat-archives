I enjoy chasing DX on FT8. Many of the CW stalwarts (W6OAT, W0YK) are also very active on FT8. 

FT8 is a very visual mode. The more you pay attention and focus on what is being worked and the more you learn how the application works (either WSJT-X or JTDX) you will have more success and more fun. Skill and finesse work much better than brute force on FT8. 

FT8 has shown me that there is propagation when you would not otherwise expect it. 

It is all about having fun with radio,
