In a message dated 3/22/2013 1:40:39 P.M. Pacific Daylight Time, jim@w6eb.com writes:

It is with great sadness that I have to report that my very good friend Smitty W6CS passed away this morning.  He was an active DX chaser, contester, and participated in many other ham radio activities for decades.  He also was very active in humanitarian efforts in Africa and the Soviet Union/East Europe area dealing with the aftermath of Chernobyl.

Smitty was the president of the first Foothill College board starting the very successful school that has had more than two million students pass through.

I will post information on when a memorial service will be held when it becomes available.

Good DX and KB Smitty
