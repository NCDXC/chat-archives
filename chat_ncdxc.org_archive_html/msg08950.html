> All that's fine. But what is NOT fine is that there's no distance limit on the TX/RX locations from which QSOs can be counted for DXCC. QSOs from a rent-a-shack or motel in Maine, or even a previous home QTH in Maine, can be combined with those from QSOs from the Bay area for DXCC credit. And that stinks -- it cheapens the DXCC program, making it a longevity contest rather than one of operating or technical skill.
