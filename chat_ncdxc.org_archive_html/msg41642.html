Verticals do NOT need lots of radials ... if the radials are elevated. Then merely 4 radials are adequate. (maybe 2 in a pinch). Also, ina top hat consisting of 4 "umbrella wires" (maybe 2 in a pinch). Finally, use your tree as a gin pole to lift up 60 feet of 3 inch irrigation pipe and excite that as the vertical. Elevated radials can be equipped with loading coils in case your don't have room for full sized ones. Or use the N6BT single spiral radial.
