In case you didn't see the following. I read that Chip is going longer with us. Following is from Janet.

 

The light and love of my life is gone. I’m saddened to report Chip, K7JA, has joined the ranks of SK, and I truly hope they are having a great time swapping tales together. He enjoyed 60 wonderful years as a ham and 45 happy years with me. Between DXing, contesting, ragchewing, moonbounce, satellite, writing, designing, building antennas, and Field Day, there was never a dull moment. His love of ham radio shined everywhere he went with everyone he met. I hope you will share any stories or snippets you might have and raise a toast of your favorite beverage in his memory. He will be missed, and hopefully remembered fondly. Until we meet again, I know we’ll feel your spirit at Field Day.

 

Tom NW6P
