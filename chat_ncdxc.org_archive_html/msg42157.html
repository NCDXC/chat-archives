Last night I was beginning to despair about ever working 3Y0K.  I couldn't hear them on any band in  CW, and rarely even printed them on FT8.

Last night I tried again, and around their local sunrise, they were so loud on 40m CW,  I thought it must be a pirate.

I worked them 30 minutes after their sunrise, on a noisy vertical close to HV lines and leaky Comcast cable.  They got my call wrong, I sent it twice more.  They sent the report, I sent my 599.   They sent NF6S 599?  OH, SHIT.  I sent NF6S 599 599.  I didn't hear a TU, but other stations were calling.  I held my breath.

The next day--IN THE LOG.

Do not give up, my fellow little pistols.

73,

Eric NF6S
