On Thu, Mar 29, 2018 at 3:17 AM, Paul N6PSE via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
> What has been your best DX so far on FT8 mode?

I was new to FT8, flipping thru the bands, not much action 30m, but I
heard a CQ:

FR/F4DNU 30M 10.13777 FT8 DATA 20180124 023145 N

He answered me, completed the QSO, no one else tail-ended, and he did
not CQ again.

The "N" means "NO LOTW QSL", but I have worked Reunion 4 times on CW,
and still no LOTW QSL, so perhaps this is typical.

The other side of the world with only 50W. This made me feel good
about trying a new mode.

-John AC6SL
