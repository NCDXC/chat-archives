I am not a contester (but did earn a KB-1000 CW once) but I get on when there is a DX contest to see if I can find something I need.  This last weekend was odd for me.

 

First, I got nothing new on 7 MHz on up despite a ton of DX action on 40 and 20 M.  For every spotted entity, I had confirmed them all.  Never had that happen!

80 M found me straining to hear the ‘millions’ of spotted entities I could NOT hear.  I finally worked 6V7 A at 0725Z for a new slot.  Heard earlier but weak and lots of others calling, including EU.

I did get two of the few remaining zones I needed for 8BWAZ: Zone 2 on 80 and 40.  That was nice!

I also finally made my very first 160 M QSOs after 49 years!  Got 8 entities, the easy ones.  Duh.

 

So, I hope others had some time on the radio and fun with DXing or contesting.

73,

Al
