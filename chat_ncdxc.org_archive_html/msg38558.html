I tend to agree with Rick and Steve or something in between.
I would love to see this SV/A count for my last ATNO.  If it does not count because of no permission and that the authorities there say no permission will ever be given, then it becomes one of those that have no purpose on the current list.  If generations of DXers will never have a chance, why is it there?  All it tells DXers that they have no chance to reach the Top and that is a disincentive. 
To me, obtaining HR or higher is about trying hard to work DX by spending the time, knowing when to listen, honing skills, learning new modes and being patient.  It means that you might climb the ladder if you work hard.  To hit an artificial ceiling is a real downer.
Almost every current DXer has missed working a lot of entities that went to the Deleted List.  I do not regret missing out on some of those and some I never had a chance due to when they got deleted and when I got active.  If SV/A gets deleted and I do not have a valid contact, OK, just another one that I missed.  Why do that '80%' care so damn much?  They can brag about their totals which includes the deleted they worked.  They will have one more than me at this point.  So what?
ARRL seems not to want to delete unless it no longer meets the rules under it was added.  The rules have changed over the years and it would be a project to figure out what was in effect when Athos was added.  Maybe someone can educate us on this particular one.  Maybe someone can remind us why that square meter given to the USA in England dedicated to JFK cannot count while they are at it.
The rules have been moving targets that ought to be fixed.  I have  some besides SV/A that ought to be move to Deleted or as Steve suggests, Suspended.  Is that worse than the current situation?  Not as far as I am concerned.
ARRL should take the hit and get right going forward.  The bitter DXers won't last forever to bitch about it.
