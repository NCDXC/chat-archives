Having been through this before…  here are the sources I have narrowed down.
  1. Allen Steel & Supply – Redwood City
  2. Online Metals – onlinemetals.com
Allen carries a very wide range of steel, aluminum etc. including schedule 60 (thick wall) black steel pipe.  They will cut to order and sell by the foot.

Onlinemetals carries a lot of stuff that is hard to find, sell by the foot and while you have to pay shipping, they are in Seattle and UPS ground is inexpensive.

When I put up my self supporting 55' top loaded vertical for 160m, I needed some pretty stiff steel to handle the base load (packed into 1/2 yard of concrete).  I fiddled around all over the place trying to find suitable steel locally – not a prayer.  Onlinemetals had it to me at a reasonable cost in a week.

Sending this as a reference for folks to stash away.
