Correct me if I am wrong, but I was under the impression
that TV sets have excessive lag time making them unusable
as monitors.  Either that or TV sets vary as to lag time,
so there is no way to know ahead of time whether they
will work.  I know the sound bar I have has a time
delay setting to time align the audio with the video.
Obviously, this would not have been necessary if TV
sets did not exhibit lag.
