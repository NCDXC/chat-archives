Keith I have been using SteppIR products for years.  Sorry you had some bad experiences. (not in my high wind big station but in my low level home stations).

If it was a driven element for a multi element model,  make sure you pull the transformer out of the EHU(and keep it in case you decide to go to 2 el later..)   I currently have a steppir Dipole on my little pistol home station and it works quite well.  

Mine was an old driven element for a beam and a 30 minute balun extraction was fairly easy and converted it to its native Dipole form.

FWIW..
