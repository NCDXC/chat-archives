You may find that Google translate (http://translate.google.com) may be useful.  One choice that must be made is if to use 'Romanji' (English characters) or katakana or hirigana.  For that, you'd have to ask which is preferred in Japan and then what Google translate can provide.  Generally speaking (pun), the Japanese are very understanding in this and are pleased that someone is at least making the attempts in their language.
