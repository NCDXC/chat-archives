I’ll be honest—we haven’t gotten a HUGE influx of items for this year’s auction, but there is still a lot of time to go.

And as a way to stimulate your desire to dig those goodies out of your garage, here’s the first edition of the catalog, with about a dozen interesting items (some more interesting than others).

Check it out at:

https://www.dropbox.com/s/xaiw6s09kytenfu/2017%20SLVARC%20Auction.pdf?dl=0

As always, there are a few gems hidden in there, and it’s a fun read just for the item descriptions. I will accept email bids on any item (a la eBay), even if you don’t plan to be at the physical auction. All of the auction details are in the catalog when you download it. (And let me know if there are any problems with the link.)

mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com
