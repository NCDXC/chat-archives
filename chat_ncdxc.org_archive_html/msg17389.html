On Jul 10, 2019, at 11:03 AM, Paul N6PSE via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

Tony, 

London is one of my favorite cities. I like to stay in the Docklands section which is East of central London. 

Hotels are crazy expensive in London, but there is a Travel Lodge and a Holiday Inn Express in the Docklands area. 
Also easy walking distance to several London "tube" stops. There is also a DoubleTree and Crowne Plaza if you want something nicer. 

The Docklands area has been newly renovated and has many nice hotels, shops and restaurants. Chelsea Harbor is also nice if you want to stay in West London. 

Be sure to see the Imperial War Museum while you are in London and the Churchill War Rooms just around the corner from #10 Downing Street
and Westminster. 

If you want to see the most sites, buy a pass on the London "Hop on Hop Off" bus routes. They take you to all of the major places in London. 
Also be aware that there are some "no go" areas of London that make Oakland/Chicago look appealing. You can easily see those areas on Google. 

Have fun!

Paul N6PSE
