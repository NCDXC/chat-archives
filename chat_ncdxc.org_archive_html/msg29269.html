I've logged him on a couple bands (12/17 logged) now and callers should be aware of some things making it tricky...

He IS using F/H mode, he did not hear my report (apparently) until I shifted to F/H
He does NOT consistently decode after calling you (if the signal is +11, why the 3 repeats?) *
He ONLY repeats three times (this is appropriate, move on)
He MAY log without RR73, but you have no way of knowing until Clublog appearances (he may or Mayotte? ;-P)  *
Once logged, he may never see your call on that band, for 24 hours (hours of wasted watts) *

and worst of all; while his DT has been pretty good (+/- 0.2) his transmitter has a key up lag (often) of up to 2.5 seconds, making the packet undecipherable.  It is NOT a DT issue, you do not have to Time Fudge him (another wasted time window to learn that one).

You must be patient (KEY point!).  Be happy he hasn't run more than two streams, so the 'lil guys' can still hear him (time window of a few hours, our morning).

* It appears he is having some software 'feature issues' causing these points.  Or his station configuration (shared gear?) isn't quite 'right'.

Again, have patience.

I called both times, to FH/F4IFF, which ONLY appears in his packets if calling CQ OR he goes multi-stream.
