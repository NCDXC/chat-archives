In Germany, a posted speed sign is "The absolute maximum speed you are permitted to travel. Period."  

In the U.S., it is "Pretty much the posted speed limit, give or take."  

In Italy, a posted speed sign is "The absolute MINIMUM speed of travel, unless you want to be violently run off the road. Punto e basta, feccia!"
