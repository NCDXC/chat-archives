When I am operating from our Saratoga QTH I normally have one rig monitoring the W6TI 147.36 MHz repeater 24 x 7.  When I am engaged in other projects away from the computers I am often alerted to band openings or DX by W6TI DX announcements that I would not otherwise have been alerted to. In addition to the DX announcements I find the DX related discussions among DXers on the W6TI repeater of interest.

 

When I am actively chasing specific DX I monitor several DX reflectors via the internet and I usually have my Heil headset on. I usually pay more attention to and focus on what I am actually hearing on the band but I am in effect multi-tasking with the internet DX reflectors and the W6TI repeater in the back ground.

 

On occasion when I have found the DX and the signal is difficult to copy I turn down the volume on the 2M rig so I can better concentrate on the DX…but after I work it I bring the volume back up for W6TI. This has been my standard practice since 1977.  J

 

73, Ted, K6XN
