Hi Keith,

If you decide at some point to try the trombone elements again, below items may help mitigate risk of Cu tape binding if that was root cause of your initial challenges.


The SteppIR assy. manual shows instructions to use included conical drill bit to chamfer the ends of the trombone tubes where you attach the "U" sections so that ends of copper tape (shown above) don't bind as they make the "U turn":


There are also inserts that get glued together and act as internal sleeves for the copper tape inside the trombones.

SteppIR will have a vendor booth at IDXC this weekend and may be able to provide some pointers and possibly some feedback on your original challenges.
