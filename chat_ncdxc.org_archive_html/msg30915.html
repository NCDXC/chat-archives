Dave,

In F/H mode, when you get a response from the DX, it tells your program to change to the STREAM TONE on which the report was sent (stream 1-6), for 2 cycles; then if not acknowledged, your app will change the tone again to the same neighborhood (not on a stream tone) to get out of the way but still let the DX hear you.

Their repeat response (if they don't hear your answer) MAY be on a different stream tone (so you'd move again).

Clear as bay water?  😉
