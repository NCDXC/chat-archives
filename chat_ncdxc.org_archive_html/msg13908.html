Did anyone experience strong and gusty winds last night?

 

I live close to 85 and 17 intersection next to Los Gatos/Campbell.  I woke up a little after 2AM with the noise of very strong winds hitting the house then the power went out.  The power outage was 3-hours long.  We have underground utilities but the feed to our area of course is with overhead lines.

 

The wind gusts had to be hitting 50mph.  I went out and lowered the MA-40 tower.  The Force-12 was bending a lot and of course there was the noise.  Lowering wasn’t easy with the high winds.  It isn’t a pull-down so it kept binding up.  With no power I couldn’t turn it to tips into the wind either.  The rotator took a beating with the torque as the beam swung up against the mechanical stops.

 

No damage just tree branches in the street and a neighbor’s garbage can got dumped on to the street.  Of course the timing was right with garbage pickup this morning.

 

It was a sleepless night over here in this part of the valley.

 

73,

Bob – W6OPO
