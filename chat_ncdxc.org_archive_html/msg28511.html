Paul,
If you already have your transciever audio linked into your computer (i.e. for FT8) you can use an app such as soundwire to stream audio from that input to your android phone. 

https://georgielabs.net/

If you have an iphone this may work

https://www.iosappweekly.com/stream-any-audio-computer-iphone-ipad-airfoil/
There are a number of possible solutions for this that can take advantage of the WIFI availability at your house. You just need one that has the ability to select the transciever input on your windows machine for the audio device.
The bluetooth device is also possible but may be unrealiable as you add more distance between you and the transceiver. 

-Ryan
