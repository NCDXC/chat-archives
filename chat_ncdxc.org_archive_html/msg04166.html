Hello NCDXC fellas,

Last night, just before sunset, we completed all of the antenna and station set up.
Our CW and SSB shacks are located on top of a ridge approximately 1500'. 

The shacks are separated by about 1000'. Our CW operations are working well and band conditions are very good. Last night, we discovered that our SSB site was plagued by S9 noise levels on all bands. We worked throughout the night despite the noise. This morning right after sunrise, we tore down the SSB site at moved it away from the adjacent power lines and 24x7 construction site.

We are now QRV on 10 meters SSB and the noise levels are much lower.  

The people of Myanmar continue to be very warm and gracious hosts for the XZ1J DXpedition.

73 for now.

Paul N6PSE
