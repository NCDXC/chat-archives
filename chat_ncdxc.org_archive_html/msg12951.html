Well, I am not sure what others may think so I am asking if I was a lid or just a hungry DXer!

 

I had just tuned in on 7012 kHz looking for TL8TT and heard him Q3 working split.  This was 0119Z and I had little hope of making a QSO thru the wall.  Before I had a chance to fully assess, 4S7AB comes on almost on top of the TL8, off maybe 100Hz, and calls CQ.  I never worked 4S on this band so I rotated my dipole to a little W of N and called him.  He came back and I made a fast QSO.  My barefoot signal surely would QRM the TL8 but for those that were probably hearing him poorly.  Whole incident was maybe 20-30 seconds.

 

Bad form or just a DXer?

 

It would be really bad if it was a prank I fell for and QRMed the DX too.

 

Sri if I bugged anyone.
