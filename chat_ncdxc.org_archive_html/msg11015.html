I was deeply saddened by the news today that Bob, W6RJ, is now SK.

Just two days ago I was looking a his photo in an online draft copy of the
2016 FOC Directory and hoping Bob would come to Visalia again in April.

Many fond memories from back in the 'good old days' when Bob was K6AHV and a
Police Officer in Pittsburg. Jim Neiger and I made periodic trips up to
have eyeball QSOs and 807's with Bob (and his beautiful police dog/law
enforcement partner).

When I QRT'd from HF for 30 years, I will always remember during a landline
QSO Bob inviting me to come up for dinner one Friday night. When I arrived,
it turned out to be the CW SS Weekend (back when SS was 48 hours each
weekend). Bob talked me into getting on the air before dinner to 'make a
few SS QSOs'. Ahhhhh, I ended up staying the rest of the weekend working SS
with wonderful meals provided by Bob's XYL.

The last 50+ years have zipped by way too fast ;-(

R.I.P., my friend.

73 & KB,

Rick - K 6 V V A * The Locust
