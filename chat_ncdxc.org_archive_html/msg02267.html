  Several Comments:
1] The formal Club meetings almost have to be in the area that contains the most members. That maximizes meeting attendance and has proved necessary to produce enough members that will volunteer to actually run the club.
Right now that area is the South Bay.  It could be other areas in the future.
2] In those areas where members are to far away to attend every monthly meetings ---they should consider having a somewhat informal Sub-Chapter local DX meeting--- every month.
This could work for Sacramento, Napa and Hayward --for sure. These local meetings can actually be more rewarding-- in that there can be some true personal interaction on DX/Antennas/Equipment subjects---just because of the groups size [ie 6 to 8 attendees]. With some effort I am sure the local Sub-Chapter could grow.   And I am sure the Club would support activities
It should be noted that this important Ham to Ham interaction is a missing element at the existing Club Meetings.      Large meetings become tutorial by their nature--so there is minimal real specific interchange, at meetings, between attending members. Not sure how to solve this problem.
 3] If outlying areas want to host a monthly meeting, I  am sure this can be done--- it would require some effort on the part of that Sub-Chapter.    Some real event, or special program would be necessary to get the attendance above the local population level  [some specific events have been suggested].     Sub-chapters would have to take the lead on this.
4]  Dispersed meeting locations have been tried in the past--with dismal results. It led to declining overall membership, decreased meeting attendance and a loss of people willing assume Club leadership.
The NCDXC came very, very close to disappearing.
5]  It should be noted that during the Clubs doldrum days some "informal small DX meetings" were held--very  similar to what I suggest above [Sub-Chapters]. Some 6 to 10 of us met monthly [and sometimes weekly] next door to HRO --- and later at the Fishmarket/Palo Alto. The interchange of ideas, and Dx information, was really great and it was only possible because there was time for people to talk.   I miss that today ---and I may well start doing it again to supplement the NCDXC meetings.
6]    Right now I think the club has a good thing going---I would not mess with it very much.
 I would suggest that the Club promote Sub-chapters------ and hope the "locals" step up to do it.
