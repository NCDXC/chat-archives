Guys,

Last nite was a pleasant surprise. Had worked ZD9 on 40-10M except 30. Literally on the way to bed decided to take one more listen on 30M. Was staring at a completely empty band at 10.142 and was literally reaching to turn off the power when “CQ ZD9W” decoded and at -14.  I received a -07.  One call and in the log. Rob followed immediately and worked him also in one call. 

This was an a great reminder to me that even an apparently dead band is often quite open. Also interesting to note that the band where I have the least antenna (low vertical GP) and lowest power (200W) was by far the easiest QSO. Hmm maybe some luck too!

Chuck NA6XX
