Paul,
Have a look at the new common mode cookbook from K9YC.:http://audiosystemsgroup.com/RFI-Ham.pdf  This is revised from the earlier work and especially important for the low bands.  The paper you reference talks about choking impedance of 1K (better than nothing!), but Jim recommends at least 5K and many of the recommended windings are 7K and better.  Example: My 160 inverted L is very close to my 80 dipole.  I use two chokes in series at the feedpoint of each, 12K or better impedance on both bands.  
Best 73,
Glen W6GJB
