The Force-12 has thinner but full length elements since there are no traps or other matching hardware.  Just thin pipes.

 

These elements flop around a lot when trying to get them to point into the wind especially with gusts.  You do what you can which includes lowering the tower.

 

Here next to Los Gatos the weather bureau clocked 86MPH about 4 years ago.  Blew 5 cement roof tiles of the roof.  $10,000 later I had a new roof.  Force-12 survived just fine but howled.
