I took a look at WAE, and it looks like a pretty neat award!
It's administered by DARC:
https://www.darc.de/en/der-club/referate/committee-dx/diplome/wae-award/
They accept LoTW, eQSL, ClubLog and paper QSLs, so there are many ways to confirm
Yes, there is a 5 Band award. It's called WAE Trophy, and requires contacts on any 5 bands, but for ALL the countries on the WAE list. Very tough!
Thanks for letting us know about this! I've already started tracking my QSOs. I'll shoot for WAE Top award, or at least WAE1.
