Moreover, Taskin uploads to LotW so you can see precisely if your QSO(s) are confirmed.

 

All of this discussion is interesting, but irrelevant for a contest log.  Your submitted contest log should include all the stations you worked whether or not you suspect they are pirates, operating illegally, etc.  The contest organizer will determine whether you get credit.  You will never be penalized unless the other station turns in a log without your QSO in it.  Other than that the worst that will happen is that you don’t receive credit for a QSO that the contest committee determines is a pirate.  WFWL still applies.  The important action for you to take is to simply log, as best you can, every contest QSO you make. 

 

Ed W0YK
