On 5/11/19 at 7:47 AM, chat@ncdxc.org (Roberto Sadkowski via Chat) wrote: 


Does anybody have an idea what happened?
There are several possibilities. If his clock is off, then the best way to deal with it currently is to adjust the clock on your computer. There are some Windows utilities that will tweak the clock settings, and I suspect there may be some for Linux and MacOS, but I have not heard of their existence.
He could be running a varient of wsjt-x which allows a station to RR73 a call and send an exchange at the same time. Frequently DX stations using this varient will call on the odd (15, 45) windows. Use normal wsjt-x to respond to them, but expect to have to manually log the call because wsjt-x doesn't recognize the RRR73+exchange except when Fox/Hound mode.
