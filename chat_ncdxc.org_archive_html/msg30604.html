Tonight I was trying to work Chatham Is., ZL7IO, on 80m FT8.  I was only printing him very occasionally, perhaps 4 or 5 sequences in a row--followed by 20 minutes or more of nothing.  I only called him while I was printing him, and shortly thereafter.  

About seven minutes after my last TX, the amp fans suddenly start up, and I look up to see NF6S ZL7IO -07, the TX button is red, and with my jaw hanging open, two sequences later he sends RR73 and the logging prompt pops up.  

At first I thought this could be some cruel joke by a pirate, but I am indeed in the log.

The only explanation I can think of is I must have been "in the pipe" at some point and failed to complete.  He did the same thing with at least one other station, I think right before he QSY'd.

A VERY nice and generous gesture!  A QSO to remember.


73, Eric NF6S
