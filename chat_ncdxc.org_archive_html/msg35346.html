I have grown to really like MSHV. 

We first saw it during FT8WW over a year ago.  We quickly realized its benefits as we could call in clear spaces below or above the DX station. We got more spectrum real estate and that was a plus. 

We also saw how the station could operate 24X7 with few breaks.  But then the cracks appeared. We soon saw where the station would go wonky and give a single station a signal report over 20 minutes before stopping or moving on.  We saw this a lot, usually in the overnight hours at the DX location. Then there is the "feature" where not everyone gets an RR73 after a signal report. 

We thought the Operator was an "Iron Man" operating 24X7 for days on end. We did not know about the "robot mode" within MSHV. I've later learned that some EU groups choose MSHV for the very reason that they can sleep while the station makes QSOs. You can't do that with Fox and Hound. An Operator has to manually load the que. 

Just now, we are seeing the FT4GL "robot" calling CQ over and over again without working anyone. Good propagation to us is being wasted.  I would much rather work a competent operator than an automated/unattended station. 

Whereas I once liked MSHV, I now greatly dislike it. 

Paul N6PSE
