Tom McShane NW6P in Cupertino asked me to share this information with you, about a local Tower Guy that he recommends:

Paul N6PSE


I was having some intermittency on my 10 m beam. Previously Bill W9KKN found out what the problem was. The coax was loose and filled with water at one end. Although Bill said he could fix it he told me I should get new low loss Heliax go from the tower to the shack. 

I hired tower climber Mark Brenner N9LS. Last Friday he fixed the problem with the 10 m beam and installed new Heliax. He did an outstanding standing job. 

This was the third time I have hired Mark and I am very pleased with all the work he has done at NW6P. If you have any antenna or tower issues I completely recommend Mark. I believe he lives near Livermore and is available if you contact this email: mbrenner3@gmail.com

Tom
