Turkmenistan is not likely to be re-activated until there is a change in Leadership. The current leader is obsessed with controlling all media and communications in/out of the Country. Very astute DXpeditioners have tried every angle to gain permission. 

Syria is very complicated. Currently, the Syrian Army controls all movement within the country with roadside checkpoints every few miles manned by 18 year old kids with no authority. Everything is searched and all travel permits are closely inspected. 

I was just in Syria and the Syrians told me that they are weary of the Russian presence and very much want them to go home and for life to return to normal.  The runways at Damascus airport were just bombed again and there are no flights currently. The only way in or out of Syria is by road from Lebanon or Iraq.
