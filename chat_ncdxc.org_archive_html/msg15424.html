The team has decided to break down early. They will start tearing it down late on July 4th, with plans to set sail on the 6th. 

They have the stats from Clublog, and will target modes are regions that appear “light” on Qs. At this point, requests for specific bands/modes/regions/times will probably go unanswered, in an attempt to maximize ATNOs, and fill gaps per Clublog.

Quote from Don Greenbaum (team captain):  “After a week of 100 deg plus days we are all exhausted.” 

I believe that.

If they’re not in your log, put your hearing aids on and grab them in the next two days.

Good luck!
