Hello from the sweltering East Coast...


I’ve enjoyed the Baker contact chats and while I haven’t been able to benefit immediately because of my location, they have helped with the planning that eventually resulted in QSOs.


Generally sigs have been slightly above “esp” level here with 20M being the most consistent.  I did manage a 20 CW QSO @ 0335 on 1 July.


Today around 0630 I was finally able to consistently copy Baker on 40M FT8 @ ~ -0.13.  Many calling but very few acknowledgements.  Initiated the calling sequence around 3500 and was totally blown away when I ended up with an RR73 after just a minute or so!


Thanks again & 73


Bob W3RW
