I had never had a first hand experience with the EU Zoo until this morning.

FT5ZM was loud on 30m RTTY and was hearing NA/JA/EU at the same time so was working by the numbers.

Are all the IKxxxx stations brain dead? No one from Italy was following the numbers and the cops kept telling IKxxxx what an idiot he was.

The IKxxxx guys would call so often that FT5ZM had a function key for "IKxxxx please send me my signal report". 

My hats off to you DXpeditioners who have to put up with this for hours on end.

Good thing there is no Club Med on FT5Z or the ops would be at the bar.
