My view is a little different that Paul’s.  I have heard them on all bands but 12 and 10 M.  I just worked them on 3524 kHz but needed 200 W to get a QSO.  I have started all my QSOs with 10 W and stepped up the power until I got a QSO.  What I have worked:

20 CW, 10 W 2333Z

17 CW, 25 W 0012Z

40 CW 10 W 1452Z

15 CW 25 W 0107Z  tough copy, QSB

30 CW 25 W 0439Z

17 SSB 10 W 0020Z  not in log but a solid QSO!  Twice, I was called K6TA and they may not have made the correction…

20 SSB 10 W 0049Z

80 CW 200 W 0414Z

 

None of these matter if I cannot find them on 40 SSB!

 

GL to all
