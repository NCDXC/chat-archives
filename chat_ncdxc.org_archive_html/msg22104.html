I have listened on 40 CW and SSB daily for the last couple of months.  The last few days have been worse in my opinion.  The big SSB signals from EU from OZ8BV, IW8RVG were down a lot.  On CW, only OK2RZ was strong and others like E74ZZ, LZ2QI were way down.  LA1MFA was also weaker than usual.  This is from about 1445 to 1530 utc.

I also note that 30 M sigs were down from EU as well.  S57V is a regular but was weak to my dipole.  I did hear a strong OE but he was not a regular.

 

Last night at EU SR was really great!  I will be there tonight from about 0500-0600 utc.  I heard and copied DF2BO, YT1AA and F5USG.  Only Tom did not copy me and the other two called me when Tom did not reply to my call.  France will hopefully confirm for a new band.  As I switched from my dipole to my RX loop, the loop made copy possible where on the dipole, I would not even know the DX was there.  I highly recommend a loop if you want to improve your chances on 80 M.

 

GL to all!

Al
