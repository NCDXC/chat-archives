On 6/13/2020 6:14 PM, Robert Harmon via Chat wrote:
You guys are encouraging to a non digi DXer !   I love CW and it looks like good times are ahead ! 



This time of year (near summer solstice) we have over 1.5 hours of
mutual daylight with the middle east.  Before FT_ modes, it
was routine to work this DX easily on 20m SSB, even
during sunspot minima.  This should totally be a piece of cake
on the FT_ modes.  Good times are ahead for the next month or
so anyway.  IMHO, this situation doesn't indicate anything in
particular about sunspots.

Next weekend, this situation will give the East Coast their
best propagation into Asia for the All Asian contest, right
at the solstice.  Like we are 11 hours behind the Middle East,
the East Coast is 11 hours ahead of Japan.  Maybe that is
the reason for the choice of the date?
