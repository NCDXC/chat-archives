Good Morning,

 

The Board of Directors of the Northern California DX Club met on Sunday, June 30th using WebEx. Minutes of the meeting will be placed on our web site within the next week or so with complete details of the meeting. Two of our decisions will help all DX’ers. One is our decision to make a donation of $8000.00 to the Northern California DX Foundation. This donation will be presented to the NCDXF at our annual picnic on July 20th. The second decision was to make a donation of $500.00 to the Wake island (K9W) DXpedition scheduled for late September. One of our NCDXC members (John Miller, K6MM) will be an operator on the K9W DXpedition.  The money has already been sent to the K9W DXpedition and we wish everyone participating on the trip a successful operation.

 

73,

Ross
