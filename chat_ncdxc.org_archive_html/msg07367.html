It works the same for me, Bob. But there IS a semi-automatic process within DXKeeper that downloads your current status from the ARRL DX desk, matches new approvals with QSOs, and lets you approve the matches. I discovered this reading the help file. The time to do this is after you have received you paper report from ARRL after a submittal.
Because I started over after moving here 8 years ago, and also because I pursue band-countries, I've submitted a lot of QSOs each year, so the semi-automatic process is easier. For OTs like you who probably submit only a few, the manual process may be better.
