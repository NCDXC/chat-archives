This cat has been out of the bag for a long time!  Too late to put it back!

When I started DXing in 1977 I think the rule was 150 miles.  Now it is anywhere within your country.  People started getting very mobile in the late 70's and early 80's, maybe even before that.  The rules changed.  Too late to change it back!  I've moved several times since then, all less than 20 miles apart.  I wouldn't loose one country, if the rules changed back to 150 miles.  I don't think many people would continue in the DXCC program if you had to start over every time you moved.
