Ralph,

Thank you indeed for the email!  I was about to drop you a note this week figuring you would be back and decompressed from the trip.  Yes, the conditions were better than predicted – primarily because the solar activity took a rise and was an effective SSN of about 85 for your whole visit there versus the 62 that NOAA had predicted and on which the predictions were based!

You set the bar for many to follow!  An excellently run operation and I was one of the 160m QSO’s! :-)
