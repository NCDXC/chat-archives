I am sad to report that our ham colleague and good friend Ross Forbes, K6GFJ, passed away yesterday after a prolonged illness.  Our thoughts and prayers are with his wife Mary, who was at his side as he succumbed to complications from esophageal cancer.

RIP my friend.  We miss you already.
