During the 60’s and 70’s, some of you might remember Dick Vaughn, N5AA. He was first in charge of the FCC Monitoring Station in Kingsville, TX. He then went on to become the West Coast Regional Director in SF.

I first met Dick when he was W5ARJ, working in the FCC Field Office in Houston and an avid DXer. He became part of the early contest team at WA5LES and K5RC.

Dick was a fanatic about following the FCC rules. I would venture to say that the Kingsville station sent out more notices of violation than all the other stations combined. When he was the Director, he worked behind the scenes to enforce the rules in all of the services.

As with many symptoms of our Country losing its accountability, without Dick’s personal commitment, we have had decades of mediocrity within the Commission.

If there is a renewed effort, I hope Dick sees it from his vantage point at the SK monitoring station in the sky.
