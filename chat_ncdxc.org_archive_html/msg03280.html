I had no problems with the place either. It's not the best Mexican but it's not the worst either (try some taco places).

I think it was excellent to get some rotation and serve membership on wider area than just in deep south bay in edge/corner (not even close to "center") of our membership concentration (just see http://w6rk.com/ncdxczips.jpg).

Attendance seemed to be about same as earlier meetings (~30) in Holder's.

Cabinet as a meeting room worked well after the music was shut down. Nicer to have everyone in same table than segregated in different small groups.

Parking was little surprise... I had no problems finding a spot when I came in at 7pm but it was much busier than I expected for that time of the day. There was some big martial art/karate/judo event going on next door.

Traffic was exceptionally bad -- I only came 2-3 miles away from work and thought I'd be clever (after checking sigalert.com) and avoid traffic by taking El Camino/Hillsdale... bad choice.... it was probably even worse than 92/101 and took me 40 minutes (normally maybe 10min drive).

People are mostly judging their experience because of bad traffic. But what can we do... it happens and this was exceptional case. It's never going to be full speed unless we move meetings to Saturdays at noon like Jim suggested. This type of bad traffic is very common occurrence going to deep south to Holder's as well.... it's very common to sit in 101/280/85 for 60-90 minutes from Penisula as well. So now you know how we long distance travelers feel about Holder's :-P
