Paul; I spent some time each day they were there. I finally got one QSO with them on 17CW 03-16-2013 at 23:25UTC. I just checked their on-line log and found it was a good QSO. I'm thankful for that one, considering my 100W and "invisible" antennas. The pile-ups were huge and some of the behavior I was hearing was unruly, ranging from the folks calling on their transmit frequency, being followed almost continuously by the Up Up cops. I also heard several instances of deliberate QRM, apparently by some disgruntled folks who were unable to make a contact. However their operators were very good and seemed to be working a variety of calls, unlike the experience of the Clipperton group with the hours and hours of working Eu while our window came and went. Bert, K6CSL
