Yes, right, Al.  The concept was two fold - give the fox a clear channel to be heard, and to give a QSO partner, a clear channel too.

F/H mode started out because we (KH1/KH7Z team) explains to Joe, the horror and difficulty working RTTY on a Dxpedition.  You really can only work the edges of the pileup,  
I recall Joe’s first react to ops splitting other guys frequency  - that’s just plain rude.  JOe got an education about the frenzy associated with DX’ers ad not being able to always here the other guy.
