Hello NCDXC fellas,

The XZ1J team is here in Yangon, we are having a team meeting tonight.
Tomorrow we will fly to the Capitol of Nay Pyi  Taw where we will set up for
the DXpedition.

The entire team is very excited!

Yangon is a very interesting place, a mix of old and new. There is an
incredible amount of construction and renovation underway here. Everyone has
been quite friendly and they seem very fond of Americans here. Today we
visited various Pagodas and Temples, we saw the Ministry of Communications
and Posts and the US Embassy.

Traffic is very congested here as you can imagine, interestingly,
motorcycles are forbidden. There are cars, busses and pedicabs everywhere!

I'm really excited about this activation. We will be on all bands and modes.
We really need to make an effort to work the US east of the Rockies, however
I'm looking forward to working all of you!

73 for now,

Paul N6PSE
