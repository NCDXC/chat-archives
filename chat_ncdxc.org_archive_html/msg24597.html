The special callsign NEPM is being used today from the Battleship Iowa.  They are transmitting on 14781.5 kHz and listening for amateur radio callers on 20m SSB.  They announce their specific listening frequency regularly, and presently are listening on 14336.0 kHz.  Be certain your radio is set to split so you don't transmit out of the band.
