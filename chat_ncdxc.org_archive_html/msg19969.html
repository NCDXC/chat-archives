Hi John,

Clean Solar's website identifies that for microinverters, they use Enphase Energy and for single inverter with optimizers, they use Solar Edge.

I have no personal experience with solar/battery, but I have spent some time researching the subject.

As far as I could determine, most of ham-identified complaints with RFI from inverters have involved Solar Edge inverters.

But I have read that Solar Edge has cooperated with hams to attempt to resolve RFI on the HF bands.

I understand your concern with this particular issue, and hope that your neighbor's system doesn't cause interference to your HF activities.

Please keep us informed.

Stay well.

73,
