Dear Bob,

The Wake Island Commemorative DXpedition team, lead by Lou Dietrich, N2TU, would like to thank members of the Northern California DX Club for their very generous donation.  As all of you well know, DXpeditions have become very expensive, and this money will help defray the costs of this trip.  We certainly appreciate NCDXC's assistance, and please be assured that we will spend your money wisely.

The Northern California DX Club logo is featured as a donor on our website, www.wake2013.org

We are excited to be going on this trip and look forward to working as many members of the Northern California DX Club on as many bands as possible from Wake.

Please extend our heartfelt thanks to each member of your club for this donation.  We could not do this without the support of hams all over the world, and we appreciate your  support.

Sincerely,

Margarett
