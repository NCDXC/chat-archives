Well, Honor Roll is one thing.  CW, Phone, RTTY or Mixed?  Then, there is the Challenge on all HF bands.  If you nail all that, then you can use your logger, e.g., DX Keeper can extend the ARRL Challenge to all various modes including JT65, etc on every band, crazy, but never ending.  There are enough variations to keep us chasing DX ‘til “the end”.  Then, there are Grid Squares … then band-modes on Grid Squares … then … well, it never ends.
