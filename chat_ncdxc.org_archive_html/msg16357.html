I worked 3A about 5 years ago on phone with
a French DXpedition.  I need CW.  The only
op from 3A on CW appears to be 3A2MD.  But
she seems to have taken up FT8 lately in
place of CW.  FT8 doesn't help me get on
CW HR, and I don't care about "digital" HR.
I need 4 more for CW HR.

Regarding emails for QSL questions:  I worked
Turkmenistan a couple of times in the 1980's.
The calls started with U rather than EZ in
those days.  One QSO was in a contest with
the op operating portable.  The home call is
no longer licensed, but I was able to cross
reference it to his current R call (not in
Turkmenistan) and even get an email address.
I emailed him asking about getting a QSL and
he said no problem.  I sent him my QSL and
a few weeks later a printed card arrived showing
the original home call / portable prefix. He
still had some after 30 years. The DXCC desk
was good with it.  Sometimes ya get lucky.

Rick N6RK
