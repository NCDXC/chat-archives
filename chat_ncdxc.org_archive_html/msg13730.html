A dues reminder...

All regular 2016 - 2017 NCDXC memberships expired on July 1, 2017.  If you renew by October 31, 2011, you will remain a member in good standing and be included in the 2017 - 2018 roster.

Is your membership current?  You can find out by going to www.ncdxc.org and selecting Members Only then entering your personal password.   Need your member's password?  Send an email to needpassword@ncdxc.org asking for it. 

Scroll down to “NCDXC Real-Time Roster”.  It is sorted by callsign suffix and is always current.

To pay the dues, go to http://www.ncdxc.org/paypal/renew.html

 
Please remember those of you who are Life or Honorary members do not pay annual dues.

The board has lined up more good programs for the club meetings.  Your dues support the club’s activities including the annual picnic and donations to DXpeditions.  Please renew now.
