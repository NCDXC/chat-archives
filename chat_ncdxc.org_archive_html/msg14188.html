In our Basic HF Operating Skills class, Sheryl WW6G and I have a slide that mentions we Elmers and other Club members can arrange skeds for those new to HF who are struggling to make that first QSO.

 

This is a great way to break the ice.  I remember in the summer of 1961 I listened for what seemed like weeks before I got up the nerve to call someone on 40M CW!  A sked would have been a big help.
