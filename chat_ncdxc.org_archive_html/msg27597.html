Some of us were watching ET3AA today on 20m and trying to discern if he was F/H or MSHV. We saw stations worked that remained above 1000Hz and were not moved down to ET3AA's transmit range (301-469Hz) which would indicate that he was not F/H. 

I sent a message to Nodir EY8MM who is one of the current ET3AA Operators and he confirms that they are using Fox and Hound. 

They will be there until Saturday.
