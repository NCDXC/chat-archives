It’s not the USPS, but Mongolian rules, the entire country has been closed (doors locked).  And the infrastructure is not what one here is used to seeing.   It’s limited, fragile and COVID limits (now slowly being lifted) compound that. 

 

I’ve gotten lotw confirmations, which implies a functioning power grid and communications network (a city) for those stations.   Hams in more rural confines (on batteries, the power grids often barely support lights) may need years to confirm.   Plus it’s not a wealthy populace by any means (except in smiles and gratitude). 

 

Patience is a powerful asset, with a modicum of persistence. 😉
