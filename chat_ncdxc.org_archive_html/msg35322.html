Yesterday, we had a lot of fun chasing DX and sharing our excitement, frustrations and celebrations on the Club's W6TI repeater. 

If you are within range, you ought to consider joining us on W6TI, There is a lot of camaraderie, some ribbing/teasing but some good fun to be had. 

NCDXC operates a 2M repeater using our Club Callsign: W6TI

Frequency: 147.360+ (Input 147.960)

PL Tone: 110.9
