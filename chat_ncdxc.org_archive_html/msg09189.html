According to K1N’s Clublog link, I’ve now worked K1N on 4 band slots, including a 10 M CW QSO this morning that I was convinced was a very skilled and hard working pirate.  
He was operating at the same time K1N was operational on 10 SSB, and their website was pretty clear that no 10 M CW was planned.  
I had worked them on 12 Meters on 1/3, but it wasn’t showing in the log as of this morning - so I was intent on making at least one additional Q as “insurance.”  As the day progressed, I
snagged them on 10 CW, 15 Phone and then 17 CW in pretty rapid succession.  I’ve been lucky, as three of my Qs took place just as K1N was getting back on after going QRX and the
pileup hadn’t had a chance to build to a boiling point.  Guess I can relax now.
