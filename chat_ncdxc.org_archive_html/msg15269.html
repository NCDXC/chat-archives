Must have a lobe from those verticals pointing to Oregon.

Above S9 on 160m and 40m. One call and in the log.

+06 (sent me a +13) on 30m FT8 and S9+20 on cw.

Best signal is 20m cw from 4-10pm. Solid S9+++, but no luck breaking pileup. ESP on SSB, but worked them. FT8 was -16.

17m has been a few S units lower than 20m, but much smaller pileups.

15m was weird. Easy on FT8 with them at -04, but no trace of them on cw or SSB.

Don't hold much hope for 12/10m.

I then went to 15 M where there were NA spots for 21.285 and 21.023.  Nothing to copy at 285 but I could copy about 20% of the time on 023.  Midwest and SW were calling and I gave up due to chances were minimal that I could QSO.
