The 2014 Intrepid friendship tour continues in Iran. We are now in Estefan, where the 4AM call to prayer has just concluded.

We received word that our proposals to the Iran government were well received. We are very excited about the future possibilities. 

Tomorrow, we will take a long bus ride back to Tehran where our journey will conclude on Friday night after we have dinner with some EP hams.

Almost all of our experiences here have been quite positive. We will certainly have some stories to share. The Iranian people are quite friendly and welcoming. They are very surprised to meet Americans for the first time. The population here is young and open minded. They are eager for good education and a better future.

These kinds of visits always remind me how good our life is in America and the many freedoms that we enjoy.

73 for now,

Paul N6PSE
