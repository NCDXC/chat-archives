I?ve been asked how the fall of Syria?s Assad regime might affect our
efforts to do a DXpedition from Syria. I visited Syria in August 2023
during a time of relative calm.

I?m very interested in these latest developments and am following closely.

At this point, I would say that an unstable Syria is not likely to be
activated anytime soon.

I?ll be watching to see how the rebel forces treat the Syrian police and
military that remain in Syria. If they are rounded up and tortured or
killed, then Syria is headed back into the dark ages. On the other hand if
the police and military put down their weapons and there is peace and
joyous celebration then there is room for hope for a new peaceful Syria.
The next few days are very important.

Last year, after my visit, I made a comprehensive proposal for a
humanitarian DXpedition. I was cautiously optimistic that this might
happen. Let?s see what transpires in the next 30-90 days, if peace and
stability return, anything is possible.

Paul N6PSE
