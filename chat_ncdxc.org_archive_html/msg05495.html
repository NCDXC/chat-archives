Steven:

Have you ever driven on the Autostrada in Italy?  If you ever have a chance, you should, just to be able to say you survived it.  If you think the IK stations are nuts on the air, you will be blown away once you deal with them on the ground at 75-100 MPH.  

Been there, done that, and it was a complete shock - even for someone who is Italian-American by blood.  Before heading to Italy the first time, I read stories about older American drivers who were hauled out of their cars and beaten to a pulp for driving too slowly in the passing lane.  Thought it was nothing more than twisted legend until I hit Rome.

- 73 /// K6TTD

Good thing there is no Club Med on FT5Z or the ops would be at the bar.
