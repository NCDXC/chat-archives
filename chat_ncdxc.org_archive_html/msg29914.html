And the QRZ page was updated an hour-ish ago, leaving the departure as 26 Jun (WX may change that).  Soonest, is bestest for you to attempt (always, der).

To the other question, they've been (randomly) on 160M CW the last few nights.  The latest was about 3-4 AM PacTime this morning.  You HAVE TO watch the self-spotting although some have had direct access to the team in the background.

This morning they were REALLY REALLY weak (90' Inverted L) and the QRN made for lousy copy here in N Idaho (barely made out it was them).  It's simply the wrong time of year (and cycle); doable but very challenging.  A Canadian station (PSKreporter) had them at +08 dB (CW), but not everyone has access to a 160M 4 square. 

It's one time that FT8 would be a better choice, it can better ignore the noise and use the tiny signal.  I have both 160M slots from an earlier team, so I know it can be done.  I know that George and Gregg are CW ops at heart.
