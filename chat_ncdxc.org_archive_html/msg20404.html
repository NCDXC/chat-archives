Here is a good reason why you don't want to transmit in the same waterfall frequency in the opposite time synch of the DX that you are trying to work. 

A71AE has been active for over an hour and in the last twenty minutes, he has flip flopped from being ODD to being Even and Back to ODD again. 

Each time he has flipped from ODD to EVEN, there are a few guys transmitting on top of him because they do not see that he has flipped. (you can't see his changes unless you take a pause)

IF you transmit in a clear frequency (on the waterfall) you avoid transmitting on top of the DX if he changes his status from Even to ODD etc.
