Who or what is S20MUJ?

S20MUJ, is not a pirate, nor a real station, nor a station jamming
7O73T but rather a software glitch that occasionally decodes 7O73T
incorrectly, says K9EL, John. The reason it happens is that "7O73T
is not a standard callsign and confuses the FT8 software", says
John.
