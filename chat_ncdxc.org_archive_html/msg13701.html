Well heck, I was able to make a two night reservation at the Visalia Marriott promptly at 10AM. It was so easy!

Three hours later, I just got a call from Brittany at the Visalia visitors and Convention Bureau. 

She said there was a glitch in their software and that the bookings for the Marriott were supposed to be for three or more nights. Two night bookings like mine should have been rejected. 

So they cancelled my reservation and asked me to go on line and make a new one. 

I see that I can still book two nights at the Marriott (they haven't fixed that glitch I guess) but I am now booked two nights at the Hampton Inn instead. 

I hope others have better success!
