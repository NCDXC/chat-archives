As part of the settlement with U.S. Towers I am not allowed to say anything but when my tower came down and cut the second story of the house in half and drove one element of the shorty forty thru my mattress it was a calm day with no wind at all. During all the attorney meetings and engineering studies it was well documented that elements into the wind not only lessens the load on the tower, it lowers the twisting torque on the tower base.... Wind or not, it also helps a great deal to have a well constructed tower fabricated by certified welders 😉
