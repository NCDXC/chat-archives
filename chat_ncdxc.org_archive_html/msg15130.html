I learned this morning that Joe Hypnarowski W6VNR (better known as ZF2AH) passed away yesterday in a hospital in La Jolla. Joe was a very active contester from the Caymans, often hosting the HQ station for the IARU HF Championships. Anna operated that contest with him a few years back, on the Caymans.

Joe was also a regular at Visalia, and a personal friend. I’m sorry to have lost him.
