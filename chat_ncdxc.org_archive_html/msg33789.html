Hello NCDXC friends,


We are pleased to report that our team and our equipment has arrived safely at Pigeon Island, Temotu Province. Unfortunately, one of our team members became Ill and was not able to travel the final leg to Temotu. 

It took two different aircraft and flights to carry out team and equipment. We are now busy setting up our stations and antennas. We are running on generator power.

We want to remind everyone that while on FT8, we will be using the Fox and Hound mode.

We greatly appreciate all of the support that we have received and we hope that you enjoy chasing us across the bands. 

We expect to be QRV by the end of the day tomorrow.

Thank you, 

The H40WA Team
