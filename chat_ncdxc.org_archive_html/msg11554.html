I continue to occasional see spots for the ET7L team. This is a small group of Italian hams who have a work assignment in Ethiopia, and they are active as their work schedule permits. W6GJB and I have worked them on 17, 20, and 30 (all CW). They are usually not very loud -- just above vapor for me on 17 and 20, weak but solid copy on 30 -- but the guy I worked Saturday was a very good op and he heard well. My guess is that they're running barefoot or with limited antennas.
The best way I've found to monitor their activity is to log into dxwatch.com and search on their callsign. This was a new country for both Glen and me, so I suspect that others need ET as well. 

73, Jim K9YC
