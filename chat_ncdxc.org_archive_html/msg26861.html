3Y0J Team to Present at IDXC 2023!
Dear Fellow DXer:

We are delighted to announce the Keynote Speakers for IDXC 2023:

Kenneth Opskar LA7GIA and Adrian Ciuperca KO8SCA of the 3Y0J Bouvet 2023 Team will be our Keynote speakers for the convention. This will be among their first opportunities to tell their story from beginning to the end. Hear about their arduous round trip to Bouvet first hand.

We hope that you will join us in Visalia for this exciting three-day event. Our team is working hard and has lined up some amazing prizes! Perhaps you will take home a nice IC-7610!

You can now register for the Convention with many exciting options. In addition, Hotel Room Reservations are open and rooms are available!
