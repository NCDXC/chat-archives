Paul,

I appreciate your spots and glad you're back at it.

Here's some advice for others:

I usually look for an open slot in a band and park there for the duration of my stay in the shack/den/home office. Sometimes I'm forced to move due to a heavy QRMer (and there are hundreds of them here in CM87 - Silicon Valley) who, like me, are now working from home with the radio on in the background. I usually have Hold TX Freq checked, but sometimes after several unsuccessful attempts to contact a wanted station (DX, new grid, state or country) I will move closer to their calling frequency. To avoid calling on their Freq by accident, I have XIT on all the time with more than 50Hz distancing.

73

Bob
