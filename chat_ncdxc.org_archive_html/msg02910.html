In May, PGE was asked to shut down the grid to protect some firefighters up on skyline.  So, out with the generator and voila!  Unbelievable low noise on all bands!  I was stunned at how quite it can be and have never in my 40 years of hamming heard such conditions.  But I was frustrated that it would last only a few hours.  I did get a new 12 M band that would have been impossible without a much stronger received signal.

 

I have some work to do with Comcast and a neighbor that I know has a solar system.  If I can sort things a bit with little more effort, that is my next step.  Then, I am going to see where I get with PGE efforts since I have some serious heat on them right now.  Then, maybe mobile is a good next step.

 

No, I have no mobile or VHF at all.  Maybe that is where I need to invest next.  Anyway, I appreciate the offer and may see if there is a time when we could plan something.  You could borrow my 33 V SteppIR supply if you want an A/B test.
