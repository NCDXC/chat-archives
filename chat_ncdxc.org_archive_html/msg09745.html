…to my request for volunteers to help at the upcoming Visalia DX Convention. I got a grand total of two responses. (Thank you both.)


We REALLY REALLY REALLY need some volunteers to help out with operations before and during the event. Specifically we need:

(A) Around 5-6 people to stuff registration packets. This will happen on Saturday, April 11th, between 9 am and 3 pm. Lunch is included. Location is The Monkey House, (aka K6MMM QTH), 21885 Bear Creek Way, Los Gatos. A beautiful drive up to the Santa Cruz Mountains on what is always a lovely spring day. You can even put a winning lottery ticket in your own envelope!

(B) Around 10-15 people to staff the Registration Desk in Visalia. Registration will be open from around 10 am until 4:30 pm on Friday, and then from 7:30 am until 11 am on Saturday. If you can offer a couple of hours, please help us out here. The “rush hour” usually starts at the opening bell each day, and to a lesser extent late Friday afternoon. The Registration Staff are the first people that convention attendees see, and we want everyone to have a GREAT START to their weekend, so only folks with happy, smiling faces need apply.

(C) Around 10-15 people (perhaps more) to staff the Raffle Ticket Sales Booth. All you need to do here is take people’s money (always lots of fun!) and give them almost-worthless pieces of cardboard in return. People with smartphones or iPads who are willing to load (and learn) a credit-card swipe application are especially helpful.

(D) Two Room Monitors to manage the operation and smooth transition of the DX/Technical Presentation speakers on Saturday afternoon, from 1 pm until 5 pm. As we are providing five (5) presentations in each track, timing is critical. The Room Monitor just needs to make sure that speakers start on time, and STOP ON TIME (much harder). You can choose which track (DX or Technical) you wish to monitor, and you are guaranteed the BEST SEAT IN THE ROOM. 


If you are willing to do any (or more than one) of these jobs, please contact me directly:

mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com



I look forward to seeing you in Visalia! (And preferably, BEHIND one of the desks!)

Rich KE1B
Co-Chairman, 2015 International DX Convention
