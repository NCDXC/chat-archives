As the chair for the Willamette Valley DX Club (near Portland, OR) I have been in contact with Stephano, PT2IC. He and two others will be traveling via  Brazilian Navy vessel that will take 4 to 5 days each way to the island. The Navy will replace personnel and resupply the island. During this 3 to 4 day part of the journey, the hams will be able to set up two sites, SSB/CW and FT8, separated by 300 to 400 Meters. They plan to use Starlink and seven remote operators (and two guests) to run the FT8 station.
The dates are not set as it is the Navy making it available… they are not a scheduled service… Hopefully, this November.
They plan for 40 - 10 Meters with 80 and 160 Meters if conditions permit.
By the way, Trinidad’s & Martim Vaz is ranked #11 on the West Coast ClubLog list.
See their web site:  http://www.pr0t.com.br/home/

So, keep an ear out listening for them, then please announce on the NCDXC email reflector.

Happy hunting,
