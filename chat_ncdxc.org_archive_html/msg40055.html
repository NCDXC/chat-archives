Paul,
How about for one of our monthly meetings instead of having a formal presentation we have an open forum where everyone shares how they go about operating.

Examples:
  • QSLing
  • Logging
  • Tracking awards
  • using spotting systems: email, slack, internet, etc...
  • Station layout
    • How the desk is arranged
    • Getting coax in and out of the house
    • Getting coax to the radios. around the desk, through the desk, over the desk.
      • Example, my operating desk only has one corner touching the wall. I can get to all 4 sides without moving stuff.
    • Anchoring antennas in trees
Just the other day Gary N6PM shared that he uses QDURE for outgoing QSL bureau cards. This is the solution I have been looking for. Reducing the time it takes to get card outs (not having to print labels and stick them to cards). Also having one off cards for POTA activations. Having done a lot of POTA on the coast in 2023/2024 I am now getting a few requests from JA land.

Thanks,
Clay N5YJZ
