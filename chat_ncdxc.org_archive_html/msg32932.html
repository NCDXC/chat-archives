Hi All,

 

Question for those in the bay area, especially in the south bay area.

 

Has anyone experienced this 80 Meter noise? (Picture and audio clip attached - careful, might play loud)

It (they) shows up as a pair noise signals which wander in frequency +/- a few KHz and have been present throughout the 24hr day for months without break (that I have seen) at approximately 3.588 and 3.608 MHz.

 

They replicate in pairs going up in frequency through other bands, but not on strict harmonics that I can tell.

Besides 80 Meters, prominent pairs are found at around 4.380 and 4.410 MHz at S5.5, and at 5.173 and 5.210 MHz at S3 and S5.

In addition, pair occur below 80 Meters at about 2.785 MHz (S5.5) and 2.808 MHz (S5.5) [pair1], and [pair2] 3.206 MHz (S4.5), and 3.236 (S5).

I have found distinct pairs up the spectrum through 12 Meters, most of these are at S Zero.

 

I have a guess about the sources(s) but I'd love to hear what you all think.

 

-Jeff AJ6LG
