Yesterday afternoon I went to my mailbox and low and behold, there was a mailing tube in it from CQ magazine!

 

It was my WAZ certificate!  I worked my last zone about a year ago (C91B in Mozambique, zone 37).  It took a few months to get the QSL card and then some time to get a local CQ awards card checker appointed.  Rusty stepped forward and filled that spot.  He checked my cards in May.  Then the financial problems with CQ magazine surfaced and I began to lose hope that I would ever get the certificate.

 

I did it all with a wire antenna (G5RV), so this award is special for me.

 

Yes Virginia, there is a Santa Claus!

 

CU @ the Holiday Luncheon today!
