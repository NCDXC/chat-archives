Microwave Update 2012 – Less than 1 week to go!
 
Microwave Update 2012 registration for Full Conference tickets will end on
Sunday 10/14.
These tickets include access to all activities, including lunch on Friday,
Saturday’s Banquet, and over $1000 in prizes at the banquet.
If you are coming from out of town, The Biltmore MAY still have some rooms
available.  Mention the Microwave Update Discount.
More information is available on our website.
 
Starting Monday 10/15, we will offer Limited Access Tickets for $50 for those
who have missed the Full Conference cutoff date.
Limited Access Tickets can be purchased on the web starting Monday 10/15, or at
the door.
These tickets include access to presentations, auctions, swaps, raffles, labs,
and events.
They DO NOT include Lunch, Banquet, and Banquet prizes.
 
We are still accepting donations towards the auctions, hospitality room, and
general support!
 
For more information or ordering tickets please visit:
www.microwaveupdate.org
 
or contact us at:
mud2012@pacbell.net
