Hi NCDXC fellas;
Pooyan has agreed to another sked, this Saturday at 0230. This will be done again in conjunction with the Mile High DX Association in the Denver area.
As in the past, when the sked is getting started, I will advise the band and mode on chat and the club repeater.
We still have many members of the NCDXC seeking their first EP contact, please remember the rules:
1) If you have any EP confirmed on any band or mode, please QRT for this Sked. It is for guys seeking a all time new country. No band fills.
2) Please don't jump the gun on the sked. Whichever side of the Rockies, us or Denver that has propagation at the start time will go first.
3) If on CW, send only your call. You don't need to send Pooyan's call. If you do get through, please give only RST and TU. We want to get as many members a contact with Pooyan as possible before he is all packed up and moved to Australia. 

Thanks and good luck!
Paul N6PSE
