No complaints since I've worked VU4 on many slots in the past, but it seems like they their signals are not as good as the recent VK9C or VK9X there recently.  They are all in the same general area.  But I'm not hearing them very much nor are they me.  The other evening on 10m CW, their signals were faint and they could not copy me.  I did hear them work a couple of west coasters but that was it.  This morning I would have expected to hear them on 40m SSB but not a whisper (though there was a ragchew on top of them).  FT8 is very light if at all too.  Is it the antennas?  These guys use Spiderbeams on the high frequencies, not sure about 40m.  Like I said, not complaining just curious.

 

BTW, I emailed the pilot about them having a very short window on 10m to USA vs. a 12+ hour window to JA and he said he would relay that information to focus more on USA.
