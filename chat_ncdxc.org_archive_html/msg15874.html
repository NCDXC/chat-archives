Since NCCC is not eligible to compete in CQP Club Competition, you might want to consider assigning your points to another club.  Assuming you are (were) a member in good standing of another club before the contest started on October 6th @ 1600 UTC, you have a few options.

Single-Operator
a. To assign 100% of your points to another club, just indicate so in the CLUB: field in your Cabrillo header, such as: 

CLUB: REDWOOD EMPIRE DX ASSOCIATION
or
CLUB: MOTHER LODE DX/CONTEST CLUB

b.  If you want to split your points between two clubs, use the SOAPBOX: field in your Cabrillo header, such as:

SOAPBOX: Please assign X% of my points to Club A  and Y% of my points to Club B

Multi-Operator
The Cub Competition rules for Multi-Operator stations describe how to proportionally award points for multiple operators.  
See section 7.0 here: http://www.cqp.org/Clubcomp.html

If you’ve already submitted your log and want to update the Cabrillo header, just make the changes and resubmit your log.  The last log received before the deadline (23:59 UTC Monday, October 22, 2018)  is always counted as your official entry.
