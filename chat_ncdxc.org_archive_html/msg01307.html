I have two humble request from the DX spotters on this list. 

Please put SPOT in the subject line.  I have filters running that key of that.  

Please put the ZULU time in your spot since it takes up to 15 minutes to get the email (the ARRL is slow). By then the DX is often gone. 

Paul, N6PSE,  does that in his spots and it works fine. 

Again, this is a friendly request that helps to keep the signal to noise ratio down and makes the spots more useful - at least to me. 

People with filters can either delete the spots automatically or even forward them to their SMS if they are interested. Everybody wins.
