I have seen and like very much the Monthly DX matrix on DXCOFFEE.com.  But there is more…

 

 

Today I discovered it on another site.

 

http://www.hamradioweb.org/forums/index.php

1 – click on the callsign and it takes you to the DXpedition’s website.

2 – click on the link below the matrix to download a “.ics” file
