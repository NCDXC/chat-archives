The ARRL has rolled out the process to apply for the new Trident Award. 

To qualify for the award, you must have 100 entities confirmed on phone, CW and digital modes. There are endorsement plates for those confirming 200, 300 and honor roll status. 

You can read about it here:   https://www.arrl.org/dxcc-trident-award
