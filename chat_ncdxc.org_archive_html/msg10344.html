Spoke with the boss lady (Peggy I think)about catering.  She says; Okay but they must use the city provided equipment for cooking.  No gas stoves or similar equipment, so it means the barbecues in the park for cooking. Parking places cannot be roped off for the catering vehicles, but we can always have people park in the desired places and vacate them for the caterer. Caterer needs to have a business license, about $76.00.  But you can have a food truck and they can have a spot roped off for them, they can cook in their truck and they don't need a business license.  Only requirement is no money exchanged at the point of sale and a sign must be posted on the truck stating that food is only for a specific group of people and no general public sales. So I guess that it can work but food has to be prepared off site or cooked in the park BBQ.
