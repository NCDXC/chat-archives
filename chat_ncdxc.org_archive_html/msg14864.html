On 4/7/2018 7:53 PM, The Intrepid-DX Group via Chat wrote:
Steve, they are using two element vertical dipole arrays (VDA) at the waters edge and turning them towards the continent they are intending to work. 

"towards" implies always short path.  Do they ever beam LP?  I am
concerned that the LP may be better, but because they are beaming SP,
we have to also beam SP, and get the inferior path.  Is the
idea of a pilot station that they check both paths with the
pilot station before starting up?  Do they just go by propagation
charts? (All the ones I've seen don't address SP/LP)  I always
check the paths using my instant reversing SteppIR Yagi.  Wouldn't
they be better of using a omni directional antenna for near antipodal
areas?  Also, there is the skew path issue.
