I don't have any insight on the current problem being
discussed.  FWIW, my Alpha 9500 has a scheme
where at power up, it determines what the power
mains voltage is and chooses one of five possible
transformer taps for the filament transformer.
If the power mains voltage changes too much
while it is on, I believe it just triggers a
power error or something and shuts down.
Better than leaving it up to the operator to
set a variac or whatever.
