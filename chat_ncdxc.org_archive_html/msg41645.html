Well, my first antenna at my parent's house was just a long wire.  That was terrible.  Finally Dad let me put up a Hustler 5BTV antenna on the garage roof with elevated radials.  That was actually a pretty good antenna.   My next antenna was a Cushcraft R7000, I believe an updated version of the R7.  I still have it along with the 80m addition as a backup.  Not so good an antenna.  That was the worst antenna but still slightly better than nothing.  Then I got a used Mosley TA33jr.  What a difference that made.  So much less noise.  But limited to 500w.  Now I use the TA53, slopers for 80 and 160.  A surprisingly good antenna I still use is the Comet H222 rotatable dipole for 40-10m.

 

Oh yes I forgot, I had a portable yagi from Mosley, forget the model number, that I lent to Al, N6TA, to use in the middle of his move.  His reports tell me it's aweful, and has a very narrow match window.  It is a compromised antenna all around.

 

73, Paul, W6IBU

What is your antenna regret?
