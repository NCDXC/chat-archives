I, for one, am sorry to see HRO Oakland close.  I live in Berkeley and would commute down via 880 and stop off on the way to pick up this or that, or just browse.

And even if I didn't stop, seeing the antenna from the highway was always a welcome sight, like a lighthouse in a storm.

I purchased my first "modern" radio there in the late 1990s when I got back on the air after a 25-year hiatus.  It was a valuable resource.

Brick and mortar stores have their pluses and minuses, but I'm sorry to see this neighborhood one go.
