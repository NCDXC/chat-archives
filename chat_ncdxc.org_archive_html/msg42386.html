This is not to disparage Charles in any way, I think he is a straight arrow.  But once again* I feel like I've been scammed by an unscrupulous DXpedition leader.

Although I don't need another QSL from Bouvet, and in fact didn't engage in "slot hunting" to any extent, I nevertheless sent my $15 plus a tip via OQRS.  I got back from Charles a confirming email with the footnote **Please not (sic) that cards & LOTW will be sent as per the 3Y0K QSL Policy**

Of course, there is no published policy that I can find.

*  I'm not a rich guy but ham radio has been a big part of my life for 67 years and since I don't play golf, I figured that being generous to various DXpeditions was a way to pay back.  I've donated to NCDXF (and encouraged one of my clubs to donate $100s), INDEXA, and many individual expeditions.  So much so that I got on somebody's list and used to get emailed requests for donations. But, from VK0EK forward I've often been disappointed with failed promises.

Maybe I'm painting with too broad a brush but it seems to me that lately expeditions with EU leaders favor EU contacts, over and above propagation effects.  So, no more early donations from me.  Monetary support, or lack of, is the only way
 I have to express my feelings over how these things run.
