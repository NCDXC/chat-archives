I have the full Elecraft K-Line and have used FT5ZM to try to see how effective using the P3 is in slipping my call in a "hole" in the pileup / split.
 
I have found that it isn't nearly as effective as my old method - which is to simply tune and hear the last station worked and jump in there - ESPECIALLY when the DX station makes an "excursion".
 
HOWEVER, the P3 does tell me where the MONSTER DX-ers (you guys with the full power and massive antennas) are - and to stay away from trying to compete there. Some of you guys have the power to "duke it out". I don't.
 
I have found that the ops I have worked at FT5ZM are jumping around rather than going up and down by small increments. I think every time I worked them - I tracked the "swing" out and away from the "pack".
 
How about you? What's your experience?
