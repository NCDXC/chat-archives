I was camping at Lake Almanor last weekend which is just on the other side of Mt. Lassen.  I always take a portable radio and antenna to play with.  This time I chose a Yaesu FT-450D….which is an easy to use and inexpensive radio, and an Outbacker 500 which is essentially a heavily loaded vertical and no radials.  I’m always amazed at the what I can hear there vs. the home QTH and how quiet the band is.  I only worked 20m but Europe was coming in like locals.  There was essentially no background noise (only after I turned off the electronic rat repellor in the truck engine).  Elevation is about 6500ft.  I was able to work Japan to Europe on this vertical and 100w.  However, many could not hear me despite me hearing them.  Maybe I’ll bring a portable yagi next time.  It was like the sunspots were back but now back to reality…….I am looking for a good portable CW paddle however if anyone has a recommendation.  The one I took was lousy.

 

73s, Paul, W6IBU
