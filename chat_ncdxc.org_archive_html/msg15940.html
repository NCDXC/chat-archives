Hi Gang,

A few years ago I met Violetta, KM4ATT, on the air.  Then she was only 13 years old and already an Extra Class licensee.  

https://www.qrz.com/db/KM4ATT 

Violetta has become an experienced and enthusiastic DXer and contester and has even been on a DXpedition, operating as PJ2T from Curaçao. 

Now 14, she and her 14 and 15 year old friends are hoping to go to the W3LR super-station to operate in the CQ WW WPX SSB contest under multi-2, March 30-31, 2019. 

They have a GoFundMe page for supporting this dream of theirs at:

https://www.gofundme.com/team-exuberance

Violetta and her young friends are literally the future of DXing and Contesting!   

If you feel so inclined, I’m sure they will greatly appreciate any support you can provide!
