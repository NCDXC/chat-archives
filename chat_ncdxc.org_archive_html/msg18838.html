Hi Mike,

The Arlan Radiosport RS60CF has become the defacto standard used on more and more DXpeditions. I own a set and I can wear them for many hours without fatigue or the "Heil Head Crush".

You are welcome to borrow a set from me and try them out for yourself.  They do a really good job at blocking the outside noise from entering your head.
