Martti seems to favor Yaesu.  All the pictures I have seen of his stations have had Yaesu radios, new ones each time they are released. 

 

He may have hauled around the FT-1000D (heavy with built in linear power supply) in the early years I don’t know.  But the next generation – three MP models – the Mark-V (not Field) is the better radio in my opinion.  It is a full 200 watts, has Class-A available, CW memories, and has DSP tools for both TX and RX.  More creature features than the FT-1000D.  I like the FT1kD a lot but the Mark-V with its outboard switching power supply (30VDC) is the better performer, more interference fighting tools and creature features like independent receiver Mute buttons and a Fast toggle button.  The FT1kD has a Fast button but it is momentary – maddening.

 

Pretty subjective question isn’t it?
