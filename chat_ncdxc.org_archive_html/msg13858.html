At NCDXC club’s August 17 meeting we learned the Three Amigos will once again co-chair the IDXC 2019 in Visalia.  That is wonderful news!

 

I want the Three Amigos, John Miller K6MM, Kevin Rowett K6TD and Rich Seifert KE1B to know how much that means to me and I’m sure the rest of the committee and club.  Your leadership inspired us committee members to hang in there when the times got tough and we got it done.  Each of our goals were clearly defined fitting together contributing to the end result - providing a fun and learning experience for the attendees.  And we liked doing it.

 

Thanks again for taking on the leadership for the 2019 IDXC in Visalia!
