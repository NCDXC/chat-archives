I've got some good news and some BETTER news!

First, I have updated the catalog for the upcoming San Lorenzo Valley ARC auction with some GREAT new items (and some even funnier descriptions, so check it out just for that). Auction information is available at:

http://www.slvarc.org (there is a link to the catalog at the end of the information post)

and the catalog is available at:

https://www.dropbox.com/s/zhgtz91nitp2j3b/2013%20SLVARC%20Auction.pdf


Now, here's the best part. Just before the auction on Friday, October 11th, the San Lorenzo Valley ARC will host a Tri-tip Barbecue dinner for all auction attendees. No charge to attend, eat, and bid on these great auction items. Folks are encouraged to bring along a pot-luck side dish (salad, veggie, dessert), but it's not mandatory. The club will provide meat, bread, and soft drinks. We're also assured of the barbecue not getting out of control, because the meeting is held at the Zayante Fire Station, and our grillmaster is a former Fire Chief!

Dinner/schmooze at 6:30 pm, club meeting/auction starts at 7:30. Come for one or both, but MOST IMPORTANT:

You MUST send me an email indicating your intent to attend the dinner NOT LATER THAN MONDAY OCTOBER 7. Please send an email to:

mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com

and indicate how many people will be attending the BBQ dinner. (No need to email if you are just coming for the auction.) We need this to know how much food to prepare. Please DO NOT REPLY TO THE REFLECTOR!!

I repeat: E-mail me directly at the link above, and DO NOT REPLY TO THE REFLECTOR.

And even if you can't attend the auction, you can bid by email without coming to the meeting. Just send me a note indicating which items you want, and your *maximum* bid (a la eBay). You will win the item at your maximum price or lower, if no one else bids it up beyond your cap. Send pre-bids to the email link, above.

Rich, KE1B
