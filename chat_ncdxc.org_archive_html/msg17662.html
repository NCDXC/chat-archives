Once again, it’s time for the humongous Ham Radio Auction from the San Lorenzo Valley ARC!

That’s right, the SLVARC is getting ready for its annual auction to raise funds for club activities. Lots of you have gotten some bargains at this event in previous years, and lots of you have donated great surplus stuff that was just cluttering up your shack. (And we thank you for those donations!) Sometimes you’ve even donated the gear you just bought the previous year! (Yes, this HAS happened!)


This year’s festivities will be held on Friday, October 11th, 2019. This gives us time to find, organize, and promote auction inventory. As always, I will prepare a catalog and make it publicly available. The intent is to give as much advance notice of items to be auctioned, including pictures, so that we can entice those folks with real green to come and part with some of it. I already have some great stuff donated, and there will be much more to come. You will be able to bid on items in person at the auction (http://www.slvarc.org for meeting details, location, time, etc.), OR you can bid online, a la eBay, by sending your best offer to me, the auctioneer. Of course, you’ll need the catalog before you bid, unless you’re really anxious (or crazy).

To that end, I ask all of you to:

(1) Go through your garage, shed, attic, and closets and see if there is anything USEFUL, preferably RADIO OR TECHNOLOGY RELATED that you would like to donate to the club for auction.. Non-technology items are also welcome, within reason. (Read: Here’s that opportunity you have been waiting for, to “re-gift” those Christmas presents you really didn’t like. But try to focus on the GOOD STUFF. You know what I’m talkin’ about.) 

All items must be outright gifts; no fee-splitting or consignment arrangements accepted. 

(2) Email me a photo along with a brief description of each item. If you are concerned about donating something, but want to make sure that the club really gets the proper value for it, send me your best estimate of:

     (a) What the item is worth (check eBay or the like for recent sales of similar items if you really have no idea), and 

     (b) What is the minimum amount that you want the club to get for the item (i.e., the price below which you wouldn't have donated it).

I can place a "reserve" price on such items, and the item will not be sold for less than some minimum, reasonable value.

All items will be sold for at least the minimum amount you specify, or they will be returned to the donor. If you don't care about a minimum bid, it will be auctioned for whatever we can get. Certain items that have broader appeal *outside* the club may be auctioned on eBay, to get maximum value.

The "Quality Review Board" (yours truly) will decide whether donated items are better classified as "eWaste", and therefore not suitable for auction.

Please get your photos and descriptions to me as soon as possible, but NOT LATER THAN WEDNESDAY OCTOBER 2nd for inclusion in the auction catalog. Earlier is better, as I will be updating the catalog regularly between now and the auction date.

Any questions, please contact me directly at

mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com.
