 Dave:  For you and anyone else reading the mail:

 The great circle (short) path from 3A to
 NA is blocked by the mountain that Monaco
 is backed up against.  Look at a photo of
 it.

 At the winter solstice, there is a near
 perfect gray line coincident with the
 short and long paths.  The long path is now
 getting less optimum day by day, but at this
 time, we should still have good propagation
 LP.  We know this because neighbors such
 as Germany have strong signals on the LP
 at our sunrise.  The LP is actually the
 optimum path for Germany at our sunrise even
 though there is not blockage of the short
 path

 I checked 20 meters at the same time as when 40
 was booming it, and no EU stations.  So that
 doesn't appear to be an option this time of
 year.

 Rick N6RK
