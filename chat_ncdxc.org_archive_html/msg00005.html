24 September, 2012



Congratulations to the team which helped to reintroduce Amateur Radio to
Kosovo, and welcome back to the airwaves to the returning Hams.



As of this time Z6ØK, and/or any other Z6 station, will not count for any
entity for DXCC awards purposes as they do not qualify under the DXCC
rules. Any future change in Kosovo's DXCC status will be announced by
ARRL.
