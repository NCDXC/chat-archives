I have RG8X, but its a bit too "unwieldily" to wind enough turns on my FT-240-31 cores. I wish I had RG-174 (these chokes are for RX only - so don't need the "heft" of a TX choke). 

I have two types of cable that I am considering using:

Cat 5 - Jim said taking a pair of that cable is about 100 ohms, and at 160M and for RX - it should work fine

Audio cable - I have lots of shielded audio cable that I could use - its about the same thickness as RG-174 and would be perfect to easily wind on the core.

Any thoughts? I guess both would be easy to wind on the core - but if you have tried either or both I'd love to hear your experience.
