On 9/26/2013 12:40 PM, Wayne Mills wrote:

The remote control issue is one of two
of the most important issues currently being considered by the DXAC.
Thanks Wayne. Another matter with considerable abuse potential I'd like to see them address is the rule that says that contacts made from any QTH within a DX entity count for a given DX award. It makes no sense to me that a US ham can count contacts made from QTHs more than 2,800 miles apart and count them for the same DX award.
Yes, I understand that people move around for a variety of very good reasons. But when we do, we must start over with lots of things about our lives. DX awards should be one of them. A nice side benefit of this is that we might see a lot fewer guys with credits for entities that only get on the air every 20 years.
