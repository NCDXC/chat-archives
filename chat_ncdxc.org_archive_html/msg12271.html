Each ham can set his rules for what is a 'gud' QSO.  I will not count a QSO
in my log if I did not copy the call myself and that means waiting, waiting,
suffering QSB and QRM.  I may know who he is by seeing spots but I need to
hear it myself.  How many guys work a 5A based on a spot when it was HA?
I prefer the DX knows who he worked by his own skills and not crutches to
'fill in the blanks'.  My QSO with 5A1AL a couple weeks ago was as sweet as
it gets: ATNO, tough on both ends but solid in the end without help.
