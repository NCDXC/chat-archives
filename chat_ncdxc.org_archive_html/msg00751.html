To All NCDXC Members:

As you know, last year NCDXC created a new annual DXpedition of the Year award.  The first award was given to the ST0R (Southern Sudan) team at the IDXC meeting on April 21, 2012.

Now it's time to select a new awardee for 2012.  We have reviewed all of the DXpeditions for 2012 based on a number of criteria, including how successful they were in working NCDXC members, and have put together a list of 17 candidates.  

You can access the survey from the Members Only page at www.ncdxc.org.  

If you need your members only area password, please send an email to  needpassword@ncdxc.org.

Please vote for only one DXpedition.  Voting closes on Thursday, February 28, 2013.  

The 2012 NCDXC DXpedition of the Year will be announced at the IDXC meeting in Visalia on April 20, 2013.

Thank you.  And Happy Holidays!

73,
The NCDXC Board
