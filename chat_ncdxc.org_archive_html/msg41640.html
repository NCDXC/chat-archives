Good question Paul.

I think that I'm still so new at ham radio (2022) that I don't think I can really answer but I can tell you that I'm still using my first antenna I made right after I got my license.  It's a home brewed EFHW that I only use on 40 m now since I have  a hexbeam that is sort of on the roof I use on 20-6 m.
The EFHW is truly a compromise but it's the only way I can get 40 m so, it'll have to do,

However, going back to regrets, I may classify two very good antennas I have in my garage that I regret buying only because I bought them as a wishful thinking ham with stars in my eyes and I really cannot use them because I don't have the space for a tower.  One is a Mosley CL-33 and the other is a KLM KT-34(A), both very good antennas but I cannot put up a tower, so they sit in the garage. I regret not being able to use them.

Cheers,
Rich WN6A
