An Introduction to the DXLab Suite
I recently had the opportunity to provide a presentation on the DXLab Suite to a number of local Amateur Radio Clubs. To make the presentation more interesting, we included several demos of DXLab in operation in our station and its use of DXLab to make contacts "on the air". I thought that some of the folks who follow this group might find the presentation and the associated demos interesting so we have posted them on our blog. You can view the presentation and demos here 

https://stationproject.wordpress.com/2014/12/11/an-introduction-to-the-dxlab-suite/.
