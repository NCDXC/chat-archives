Glad to know this is still on:
Following on from the previous news below, we have a short update from Felipe, XQ7IR. He expects to receive landing authorization no later than January 7. Meanwhile, he has now booked a charter flight for January 9 (may possibly need changed to January 10). The charter will depart Santiago de Chile for Juan Fernandez Island (CE0/Z), and Felipe then expects to stay there for one or two days as the boat is being loaded with cargo (including his gear) ready to sail to San Ambrosio Island. Once underway, videos of the voyage will be shared with the DX community using a Starlink connection.
