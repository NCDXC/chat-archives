Got them from far North Idaho (almost Canada, eh?) on FT8, already confirmed in Clublog for ATNO #280.  All I have is the R-7 vertical antenna now but with some power, it was enough.

Once he said 73, I responded with only my call and a signal report, he responded in kind, then '73' sent, done.

The signals here will improve next year, I just bought a 55' tower, SteppIR DB42 and prop pitch rotator to go with the new Elecraft KPA1500, arriving next month.  Soon, the inland NW WILL be heard!  :-)

He was loud on 20 SSB, but what I have, just wasn't enough.
