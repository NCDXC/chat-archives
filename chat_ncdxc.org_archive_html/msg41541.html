I note the following in the recent update:
6m Operators — We Need Your Help!
Please report 6m openings into the Desecheo area directly to Team Pilot Steve N2AJ via his QRZ email address. With two stations operating, the team will attempt to QSY to 6m whenever openings occur.

KP5 is very rare. Taking one of two stations off HF to serve a 6 m opening to somewhere seems to be a significant deviation from what I would consider should be the main focus of continuing ATNOs to a lot of Deserving. I suppose if I were a serious 6 M operator, I would be thrilled at getting more action.
What do you think? Is this DXPedition long enough that is a good idea?
