A50BOC (Bhutan) was on 160M this morning and will be until they leave on Friday.  He (Adrian) worked a few west coast stations, but I did not hear a whisper.

He was also on 160M FT8 but I missed them by a few minutes.

He may coordinate contacts on the ON6KST chat group, he tried this morning but the band was already closing (not as open as a couple days ago).

I'd suggest starting around 1200 UTC ( Oh bloody early 4 AM in the morning!).

CW was 1.824.500 up
FT8 was 1.836 (NOT f/h)

They (the rest of the team, I think Zorro is with them) were also active on 80 CW and 40 SSB this morning but no joy here.  Clearly it is a case for those with really good RX antennas (two of the stations used the Hi-Z 8 arrays, which got me looking at space to put one.

Bhutan would be an ATNO for me on any band.

Merry Christmas!
