On Mar 9, 2015, at 2:44 PM, Steve WB6RSE via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

> For those of you lamenting HOA antenna restrictions, it’s time to ask your US Congressional Representative to support HR1301. It will give the FCC requested authority by Congress to enforce PRB-1 in CC&R restricted areas the same as it now does within municipalities.
> 
> General information is here along with a link to identify your Congressional representatives.
> 
> http://www.arrl.org/hr-1301
> 
> 73 - Steve WB6RSE

Be careful what you ask for. This bill gives the “guvmint” the authority to override private contracts. In general, I believe this is a bad idea. You may like the result when it overrides an HOA provision against antenna construction, but you might not like the result in many of your other private matters.
