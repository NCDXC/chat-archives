Hi All,

The DX beacons (https://www.ncdxf.org/pages/beacons.html) have new equipment install at most locations.   The next phase of this project is to update the HF antenna.

AA7A and N6NAC have designed a new antenna - dual band discone - DBD for short that covers 20m thru 10m.  It’s a substantial improvement in terms of RF radiation and physical “stamina”.  Thru the effort of K6GSJ, we’ve had one installed at W6WX (very close to Mt. Um) for over a year.  We’re now fabricating metal parts to build a couple more for test deployment.

We could use a technical publications writer to help with the assembly manual.  AA7A has started a draft, describing the basic steps.  We need: illustrations, great technical working, and a fresh perspective.

If you can help, please contact me!

Thanks.


73, K6TD
