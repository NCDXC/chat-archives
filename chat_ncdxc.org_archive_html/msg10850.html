Subject: Radio Club of America (RCA) Banquet in Cupertino, CA

On Saturday, Nov. 21 the oldest wireless club in the world will celebrate its annual awards banquet for the first time in California.

This year is even more special because the keynote speaker is Dave Leeson, W6NL.

N6TV (official RCA banquet photographer), N5KO and Julie are attending.

N9JA will be inducted into the Fellows class of 2015.

The RCA is a club of wireless oriented professionals. Many of them are hams – some are not.

RCA supports lots of Amateur Radio youth initiatives – and we also give many scholarship awards every year

The banquet is a tremendous networking opportunity. The technical symposium is very good.

You do not need to be a member of RCA to attend.

It would be GREAT to see hams from the Bay Area attend.

Please go to the link below for more info – and to sign up.

http://radioclubofamerica.org/annual-banquet/

Hope to see you in W6 next weekend.
