Quick survey – will publish the results if enough folks are interested.

You may know that I provide propagation/antenna analysis assistance to Dxpeditions to help with their site selection, antenna choice (although on 160, this is almost exclusively a top loaded vertical or a Titanex), and operating planning.

I use a variety of software tools to do this including some commercial (i.e. Not free) packages that perform detailed ray tracing.  This is about as good as you can get – short of turning the radio on! :-)

Many of the DXPeditions know that California is a significant source of funding between the NCDXC, NCDXF and individuals.  So they are more motivated to figure out how to provide ATNO to W6 operators.

So on 160, I’d like to know the following to help me with planning on 160m:
  • What RX antenna do you use (typically) on 160m
  • What is your typical background noise around sunset/sunrise in a CW filter either in dBm or S units on that antenna?
  • Is the S meter calibrated ? (usually yes for Elecraft, FlexRadio – often No for everything else)
  • What is the filter bandwidth?
Thanks!
Stu K6TU
