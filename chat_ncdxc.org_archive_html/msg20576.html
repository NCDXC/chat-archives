I have noticed a definite pattern on AP2HA, AP2TN, SU1SK, SU3YM, HZ1SK and HZ1ZM, 

 

They are most active on their Thursday night and their Fridays. As Friday is a Holy day in the Islamic world, that is when they seem most active. 

 

I have been seeing these stations on during our early Friday morning and well into mid-day and sometimes on our Friday night. 

 

Pakistan is often active around 7:30-8AM our time as they were this morning. 

 

Good luck working these stations!
