Here is a study of copying CW by ear at low S/N ratios
giving a median threshold of -0.6 dB in 100 Hz BW.
This validates the figure of "-17dB in a 2500 Hz BW"
given in the paper that was posted to this list last
week.

See:

http://www.g1ogy.com/www.n1bug.net/tech/w2rs/The%20Human%20Ear.pdf
