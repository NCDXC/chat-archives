Last I heard, eQSL has always been acceptable for CQ awards. Am I
wrong?  Also, if you're going for any CQ awards and have a lot to
submit, it would be worth asking if LOTW is acceptable.  I recently
submitted for the CQ Field award, and the K0KG, CQ awards manager,
agreed that a screen print of an LOTW confirmation that clearly showed
the location within a Field was acceptable.  As a result, I was able to
submit for 175 zones with a combination of paper cards already in hand,
printouts of eQSL and LOTW, and a half dozen new cards that I had to
solicit. Saved me a LOT of time and money.
