On Mon, May 6, 2024 at 8:27 AM KE1B via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

Got him on 30m CW around 0350z last night. Big pileup, but terrible operator. Couldn’t pull out a callsign. It’s so frustrating when you can see lots of people calling, and he just gives up and calls CQ again. [Sigh]

But then worked him again on 160m CW, around 0420. Barely copyable, going up and down in QSB, sometimes coming up to S4 or so. Fortunately, NO ONE ELSE seemed to be calling him, and he was persistent with me. First he thought I was KE2D, then KE1D, and finally KE1B, but probably took 20-30 tries over 3 minutes or so. But he stayed with me. I don’t get out very well on 160 (using a loading coil on my 80m inverted vee), but with no competition, managed to make it work.

Suprised there was no one else there; he WAS picked up by at least one skimmer, which is where I found him.

Rich KE1B
