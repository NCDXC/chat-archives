I’m almost ashamed to admit that I had (and still have) my dream rig as a senior in high school. A neighbor Elmer and dentist sold me his 8 year-old S-1 series S-line for a sum compatible with my part time job. He was really giving it to me, although it was all the money I’d ever saved at the time. 
The audio quality on that radio is still unparalleled! Today, I’m happy with my Kenwood TS-990S, which has all of the old skool bells and whistles, as well as lots of knobs. When it becomes too expensive to maintain, it’ll probably be a Flex, but only if the virtual main tuning knob feels right! It’s all about desktop presence. 

73, Bruce K3NQ
