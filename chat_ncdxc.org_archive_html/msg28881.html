FYI:

Elecraft has NOT yet increased the price of the K4 (or other products) but it is planned sometime this month.  They ‘got busy with trade shows, etc.’  and did not get around to it.

So, the current website price for the K4D is $5,499.99 which is what you pay now if you buy now.  It is the price you will pay if you order one now and wait to be invited to pay, which will be out a few weeks.

Also, if you want to, you can pay with a credit care for no charge and get the points/rebate from the card company.  No discount for a cash deal. 

Finally, shipping out of state will save the tax which is 9.25%.  I was going to pick up at the factory but that makes it a CA sale.  And, no factory tour, lobby pick up only.  I choose shipping to OR.

 

So, that is that until they raise the price later this month (unless they change their mind again.
