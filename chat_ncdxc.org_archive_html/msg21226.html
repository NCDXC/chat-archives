Like many of us, I enjoy operating on FT8 and FT4. 

Frequently, I see the following scenario played out. 

A DXer calls the DX and works him in the same frequency on the waterfall. (Not Split) The DXer then proceeds to call CQ CQ or Call other DX without moving away from the first DX station on the waterfall. This causes great QRM to the DX and anyone trying to work him. 

I see this happen a lot, yet this entire scenario is easily avoided by working Split. In WSJT-X all you have to do is check the Box for TX-Hold Freq. This makes you transmit only where you have placed your red cursor. It will not automatically move.  I have great success transmitting below 500 Hz or about 2500 Hz. 

I rarely work DX on his frequency, but if I do, I carefully move my red cursor away after making the contact. 

This avoids causing needless QRM or causing me to be a LID. 

Please let's all try to be considerate of our fellow DXers.
