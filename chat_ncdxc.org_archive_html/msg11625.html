I listened on 20m LP today but only heard a faint sound of CW under the noise.  I think the high K index is not helping matters.

On DX-World I saw a picture of his WARC and lowband vertical.  Having more band options should help.

Ken has to shut off at 2200z due to local restrictions.  Yesterday I heard him on 20m SSB running a JA pileup without ID'ing.  Right at 2200z he finally ID'ed but announced he was shutting down.  I think Ken knew he was under a tight time constraint and wanted to work as many stations as he could until 2200z.
