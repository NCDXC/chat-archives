Greetings Paul and the Group,

 

I enjoyed the IDXC very much.  I arrived Friday in time to enjoy the Contest Dinner, then of course the Saturday Night Banquet event as well. 

 

I for one don’t mind a different venue regardless of the location.  It’s not where we meet, but with whom we meet with that is important.

 

I heard a lot of grumbling about the good old days of 800+ attendees.  Memory Lane is nice, but we have to live in reality. 

 

Not on the schedule, but fun to observe was my walk around the parking lot looking at the mobile installs and antenna set ups on the cars and trucks.

 

Perhaps a small episode on mobile installations in cars and trucks would be interesting.

 

73

 

Jerry Olive KD6WKY
