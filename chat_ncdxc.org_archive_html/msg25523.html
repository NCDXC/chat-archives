Reminds me of a fun QSO a few years ago when the 15 M band was ‘working’.  It was July 6th, 2014.  July, not wintertime.  I got on the rig at 1419Z and worked at UN6 barefoot.  Then a UA0, then a JY4 but needed power.  Then a UA3 and a F5.  I decided to call CQ and worked a VE3, N2, DL, N5, K8, and a VE7.  I chatted with them all.  Then, at 1704, I was called by A65BR/M.  Joel was mobile on the beach, and we chatted for 20 minutes.  Quite unexpected.

I continued answering calls from a CT1, OE3, JY89, 4Z5, 4X6, 4J0, EW8, XE1 and finished with LA5.

If I can get my tower delivered, installed and build an antenna, I would like to enjoy that type of operating again one day!

Good DX to all!
