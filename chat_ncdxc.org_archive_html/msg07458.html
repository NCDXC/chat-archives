First, a big Thank You to those who submitted logo entries for IDXC 2015.  Here 
is the complete list:

http://ncdxc.org/pages/logos2015.html

L1-L3 were all submitted by Roberto Garcia, EA2RY

L4-L7 were all submitted by Jonathan Woo, W6GX

L8 was submitted by Mike Flowers, K6MKF

L9 was submitted by Joanna Dilley, K6YL

For IDXC 2015, we plan to offer at least two convention shirt color choices for 
attendees.  Factoring in other complex issues such is "stitch-abiility" on 
fabric, colors, fonts, etc. --- we decided to form a 3-person Logo Committee to 
make the final  decision, instead of putting it to a club-wide vote.

Therefore we are please to announce that Logo Entry L5 from Jonathan Woo, W6GX, 
has been selected as the IDXC 2015 logo for the convention next year.  

Jonathan will receive a free convention shirt for his efforts, and his logo will 
help promote IDXC 2015 in many ways, including the IDXC website:

http://dxconvention.com/index.html

Congratulations, Jonathan!

And thanks again to Roberto, Mike, and Joanna for their entries......much 
appreciated.

IDXC 2015 Logo Committee
