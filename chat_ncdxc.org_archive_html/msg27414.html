Hi Bob;

As others have said, set your yagi up for vertical polarization.

And yes, you will find members of the NCDXC on the repeater, especially in the mornings, and most especially if there are currently any interesting DX stations on the HF bands.  The club repeater is often used to spot DX stations so that others can work them, so you'll hear people announcing such spots from time to time.

Of course, the repeater is open to all, and we encourage people to use it for general use as well as DX spotting.
