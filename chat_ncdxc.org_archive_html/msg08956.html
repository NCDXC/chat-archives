On Wed,1/21/2015 4:47 PM, Kevin Rowett via Chat wrote:
Do you think it’s important for the ARRL to recognize that differently than those who earn DXCC either via remote, or moving locations?
I think the "any location in a DX entity" rule makes a mockery of DXCC in an entity the size of Austrailia, Russia, China, and the US. It seems to me that locations separated by no more than 500 miles could reasonably be considered equivalent for DXCC purposes. There are distance limits on VUCC and WAS, and there should be for DXCC.
Granted that horse has been out of the barn far too long, but ARRL should issue DXCC for QTHs within some reasonable distance limit.
