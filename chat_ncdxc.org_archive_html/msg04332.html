On 11/21/2013 3:09 PM, Paul@n6pse.com wrote:
I too support the concept of proxy voting for club members. Why should remote members be marginalized because they cannot attend the meetings?
I strongly agree. Disallowing voting by members not at a meeting might be reasonable for a club with a small geographic footprint, but not for a club with members as widely scattered as ours. It disenfranchises many members who live at a distance from the meeting, or who have difficulty traveling, and clearly reinforces the concept of a closed little group in the south end of Silicon Valley.
