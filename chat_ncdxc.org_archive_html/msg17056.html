I was really glad to read in this morning's Daily DX that the NCDXF is supporting the So Orkney DXpedition Team. The Perseverance DX Group has a proven track record of delivering results. We all had tons of fun with the recent VP6D trip. 

 

South Orkney is a very expensive destination with a very expensive Braveheart Charter. Orkney is one of those very remote, difficult to access places that very few ship captains are willing to venture to. 

 

I hope that the NCDXC will re-consider their position on supporting the So. Orkney DXpedition team. The So Orkney team has NCDXC members on it and they could really use our help. 

 

 

Good day!
