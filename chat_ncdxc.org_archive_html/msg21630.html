2020 is almost over, what was your best DX of 2020?

Looking over my log, I was crazy busy on FT8 and filled a lot of band slots. I don't think I had any new countries this year but the highlights are:

Worked VP8PJ So Orkney in February from 15m to 160m on SSB/CW, RTTY and FT8. 

I also worked at E44CC Palestine in Feb for a few band slots. 

TI9A  Cocos Island was also great fun in early February. 

Working SU3YM, SU1SK, HZ1ZM on FT8 during the Summer months were also memorable. 

What has been your best DX thus far?
