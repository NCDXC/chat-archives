Here is some info about TY5AD from W3UR's Daily DX. 

TY5AD is a new Russian DX Team remotely controlled station hosted 
by a local near Cotonou, Benin. The station is capable of operating 
on 80 through 10 meters running 100 watts into a vertical. QSOs 
will be uploaded regularly on Club Log and those needing 
confirmations can check your contacts and "make a QSL-request". 
After requesting confirmation on Club Log's OQRS QSOs will be 
uploaded 2-3 days later on LoTW. https://www.qrz.com/db/TY5AD
