On Aug 21, 2023, at 6:49 AM, Andy Calman via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

﻿QRP is an enjoyable challenge and well suited to FT8. I’ve been having fun with it for the past few months. It started completely by accident when I forgot to turn on the amp! I’m up to 93 entities now, using 2 to 5 watts into a shortened rotating dipole at 30’. Signal reports are often surprising - last week a Belize station gave me a +01 with 5 watts. 

I find that almost any station that’s -04 or better can be worked QRP. You just need to wait for good propagation, pick an empty audio freq (often above 2500 Hz) and be patient. There are even unofficial QRP DXCC and WAS awards. 

Try it, you’ll like it!
