boor
bo͝or
noun
  1. an unrefined, ill-mannered person.

I’m sure that like me, from time to time you find some of the conversations on the reflector to be socially unacceptable or bordering on abusive to some one or some thing.

Unfortunately our modern conflict adverse society tends to stop our natural response to call foul when we see it – frequently leaving the target of the abuse to feel isolated and unsupported.

While First Amendment rights are often touted to support the legitimacy of the boor’s view, the boor still breaks the golden rule of “Don’t be a dick” and proves they lack social skills – let alone graces.

My personal solution to the boor both on this reflector and others is an email rule that simply deletes all emails from boorish people.  Simple, expedient and for those so adverse, conflict free.

Sadly, this still leaves the abused to appear isolated and unsupported – so I obey the rule of criticize in private and send the abused a personal email of support.

I hope you will find this suggestion helpful and that it will provide a solution for you too.

In the meantime, I will still be tempted to respond publicly…
And from time to time will!

Respectfully,
