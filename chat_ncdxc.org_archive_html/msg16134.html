In a message dated 11/05/18 14:28:18 Pacific Standard Time, chat@ncdxc.org writes:

My setup here in San Jose is similar to Mike’s. I have the NA4RR hex on a 33’ Rohn mast with a Yaesu 450 rotor mounted at the bottom.

It works well given that I’m also in a noisy urban environment.  Until a few months ago, it was up only 20 feet, and I still managed to work a lot of DX with it.

It’s no match for much larger multi-element yagis, but it’s an excellent option for a lightweight, low profile multiband antenna.

73,
  Frank N6OI
