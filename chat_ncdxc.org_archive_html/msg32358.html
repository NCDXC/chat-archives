You all are probably way ahead of me here, but what do you use for spotting sources?  I guess DXLab (which I don't use....yet as it seems to have a learning curve more than I have time for now) does that?  I've always used DXSummit.  But recently I have hamalert installed on my phone.  It has helped me tremendously.  I often get spots sent to my phone which don't show up on DXSummit and has gained me a number of band/mode slots recently as they are early enough and if I act fast enough, get there before the crowd.  But I understand there are other sources too.  The most recent example is KG4NE which showed up on Hamalert, but no where else until I spotted him.  Got him as NO ONE was calling him.  Clearly not all spotting sources are equal.  Comments?
