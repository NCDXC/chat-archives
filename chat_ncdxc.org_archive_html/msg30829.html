Good morning fellas, 

I was not on 40m yesterday, so I can't speak to what Rick saw, however I know that 40m often has propagation to somewhere from here to almost mid-day in our Winter months. 

I chased ZD9W most of yesterday and finally worked him on 20m in our late afternoon. During that time, I saw propagation fluctuate wildly from reasonably strong signals to completely gone and then back again later. This happened over and over again and it appeared that propagation was fluctuating wildly.

ZD9W's operating on normal FT8 frequencies was very poor and really impacted his rate and made the pileups extremely challenging. I see this morning that he is operating in the same fashion at 24.915.
