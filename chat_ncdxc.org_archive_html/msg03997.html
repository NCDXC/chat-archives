Hi All,

This is a solicitation request but it's for worthy cause.  Please read on.

Many of you are successful in your careers and your DX'ing accomplishments.  Looking back in time you might remember a time (perhaps when you were young) when someone did the totally unexpected, perhaps an act of kindness that touched your heart and inspired you to become the person you are.  Now there's an opportunity for you to be that person and make a huge difference in someone's life.

Pooyan EP3PK is about to embark on a new journey.  He's leaving Iran and moving to Australia.  The date has been set, Jan. 21st.  He told me he's both excited and nervous.  Not too many people get an opportunity to leave Iran.  Pooyan is only 33 years old and I expect him to do great things and carry on the 'ham spirit'.  To help him succeed in the VK I'm donating $250 towards a surprise present to be given to him after he lands in the VK.  Pooyan has never asked nor mentioned the word 'donation' to me.  He uses LoTW and he enjoys ham radio as a hobby.  He doesn't receive compensation from his QSL manager.  The $250 monetary gift is for a very worthy cause and it will be a big surprise for him.  I hope you'd agree with me.  No amount of donation is too small.  I will collect the money and keep a list of donors to be given to Pooyan.  If you wish to remain anonymous please let me know.  My goal is to raise $1,000 for this worthy cause.  You don't have to be a sked participant to make a donation.  My Paypal account is the same as my email address.  Please provide your callsign under the comments field.  On Paypal please mention the transfer as a gift to a friend.  This way 100% of the money goes to Pooyan.

Thank you for your time and I hope to hear from you soon.  73.

Attached are some photos Pooyan sent to me.  Hopefully the photos will show up via the list.

ps I hope Pooyan will sign up as a Chat member in the future (but not before Jan. 21st, hehe).
 
Best Regards,
