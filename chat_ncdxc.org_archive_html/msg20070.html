I’m starting to see Australian 2 x 4 call signs.  Just worked VK4FDAD.  I’ve seen a few others.  Not a special even station, looks like a regular call sign.
 
Jerry Olive KD6WKY


This is an Australian “Foundation” class license, intended as an entryway for new hams. It allows operation on HF/VHF, all modes, but with a 10W power limit. It’s a simple 25-question written exam concerning mostly *regulations* (not technical/electronics), and no code.
