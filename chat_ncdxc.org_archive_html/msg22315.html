propagation.  They had a chart showing dozens
of Cycle 25 predictions that covered from very
low to very high (as in this case).  There is
no good basis to determine which one will turn
out to be correct.  The situation seems to be
similar to stock market forecasts, which are
notoriously inaccurate.
