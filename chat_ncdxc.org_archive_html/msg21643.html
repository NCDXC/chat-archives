Best so far? Probably the A7 on 80 M. which was also the most difficult.  I had fun working EU in the first QTR with new ones EA, HA, I, OM, S5, 9A.  Also got 9J on 80.
Fun was ZD8 on 17 M for a new band with 10 W and 4X on 40 SSB.
Naturally, I liked chasing the TI9 and VP8-O.  Good operations and fun from this end.

Been thin for a non-digital op but I am doing my best to work everything I hear with 10 W or the minimum power that will get me in a log.
I am ready for a couple of other new goodies before the end of the year!  Always QRV for SV/A...
