You and I noticed the same pattern. I am using only a very modest 32 foot 80M vertical antenna on 80M and I feel very lucky to have made a solid contact this morning. FT5ZM is still strong here on the vertical with no jammers etc on his frequency right now. For anyone on the west coast who hasn’t worked them yet on 80M this is a great time to get on 80M!
