My best 2019 QSO had nothing to do with DX or distance, but rather with memory.

 

I came across a digital copy of the Fall 1953 Radio Amateur Call Book and wondered if I could find the name of my high school Electronics teacher.   Sure enough, there he was:  Frank Wiebel, W3JVZ.  On a whim, I checked each callsign in my hometown with QRZ.COM to see if any of these folks might still be around.

 

I was batting .000 until I came across W3SCC, Fran Little.  An email later we had a schedule for a 20M SSB QSO.  We chatted for about 20 minutes, and he remembered most of the hams on my list from 1953.  He and Frank had been good ham buddies.

 

It was a great QSO!

 

- 73 and good DX de Mike, K6MKF, NCDXC Secretary
