The QSB really got me.  I woke up at 1100z to use the restroom.  I also wanted to see if they are on 80m or 160m which has now become a routine ever since TI9 came on the air.  Sure enough I heard them on 160m but just too poorly to call.  I was about to shut the rig off but decided to check the emails.  A few minutes later their signal came up nicely on my RX loop.  One call and I was in the log.  In Colorado they say if you don't like the weather just wait a few minutes.  On 160m if you don't like the signal just wait a few minutes, hi.
