From the Rebels' past operations, I think it probably means they'll try operating on a non-standard FT8 frequency using MSHV with multiple slots, and when that produces too few responses, they'll switch to the standard FT8 frequency and use MSHV with multiple slots. Don't worry about the fact that there's no such thing as MSHV Super Fox mode. The Rebel kids are pretty loose with their terminology.
