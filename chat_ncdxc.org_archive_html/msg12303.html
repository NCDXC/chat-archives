AFAIK, only the MFJ "Intellituner" series is
capable of matching up to 1600 ohms.  This
will have a pretty good chance of tuning
half wave wires.  This is actually better
than the LNR product.

I have a lot of experience actually designing
auto tuners.  The design of the MFJ
is far and away superior to anything else.
It's designed the way I would have designed it.
The MFJ series seems
to be an optimum design, but of course
MFJ has a well deserved reputation for
manufacturing problems, so it is something
of a gamble.

For the ridiculous price of $159.00, I
can get an MFJ159 that interfaces with
my IC-706.  I am thinking of mounting
a 102 inch CB whip on the roof of my
van and tuning it with an MFJ159.
I don't trust a screwdriver antenna up
there mechanically.  I can park the van
and clip a longer wire to the whip
and throw it up in a tree, etc.  I'm
also considering the MFJ929.

Rick N6RK
