Hi Vijay.  My comment is that, if possible, get a chance to hear an amp before you buy one.  Bruce noted that tube amps can be quieter than SS designs.  I agree, but it is not always true.
I bought a Palstar Commander that was very robust and a good amp (no longer available).  Two Eimac tubes for lots of plate dissipation.  But, WOW, was it loud in the shack!  I could not believe that a cooling fan could be that loud.  A 'squirrel cage blower' which forces air up the tube socket is best but those blowers can make some serious noise.  Try to listen first if you can.  Bob, W6OPO, let me hear his SPE 1.3K and it was way quieter than my tube amp.  My KPA1500 is quiet enough for me until the stage four cooling kicks in and it is pretty noisy.  There is an app that an change how the KPA1500 does its cooling and I am going to try it when I get around to it.
