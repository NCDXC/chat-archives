In 2018, we took a Rhine river cruise from Amsterdam to Basel, and
then took a train to Friedrichshafen. Be aware that there are 2
different train stations in Basel.

If you want to go for a ride in a Zeppelin, you need to reserve months
in advance, because it only carries 12 passengers per flight, and is
usually fully booked.
https://zeppelin-nt.de/en/Zeppelin-flights/scenic-flights-Friedrichshafen.html
The Zeppelin Hangar is adjacent to the convention center.

Separately, the Zeppelin Museum is down by the lake, has many
interesting exhibits, including a partial reconstruction of a
Hindenburg class ship. The restaurant at the museum has outside
seating on a balcony, with a view of the lake.
