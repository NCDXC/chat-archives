As Bob,  W6OPO  mentioned, I did know Cas but the members and call signs that Ross mentioned knew him very well.  I did keep the very last issue of the West Coast Bulletin in my file.  What many did not know, Cas actually paid the postage out of his pocket for many hams around not only the Bay Area, but also around the country.  What a swell guy.  I am also a bit surprised that a "CAS AWARD" is given by some club that is not even on the West Coast and that our club had not ever considered (to my knowledge) anything to honor him. Many have tried to copy his very unusual style of writing about Ham Radio, but it does not have the touch that Cas managed to squeeze in his comments and reports.  I consider myself lucky to have know Cas for a very short time. I do have a copy of DX IS! and would be happy to loan it to anyone that wishes to borrow it.
 
73 ........................... Dick, W6KM
