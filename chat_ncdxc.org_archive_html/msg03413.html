Subject: [NCDXC Chat] A chance to work EP3PK on a Sked.

This information is for the NCDXC fellas. It is not for publication anywhere by anyone.

As you may remember, earlier this year we tried some skeds with EP3SMH.  Unfortunately, band conditions and his 100 watt station just weren't conducive to our making many contacts. I’ve also been in touch with Pooyan-EP3PK. Pooyan is agreeable to a sked if it is done in a coordinated fashion.

He does not want dozens of emails requesting a particular date and time.  I am coordinating some dates and times with Pooyan for early morning skeds on 15 or 20 meters (his request) and evening skeds on 15 or 20 meters (around 0300). These will be grey-line contacts.

Pooyan has received an immigration visa to move to Australia.  He won't be moving until Jan. however he will tear down his station in the next 2-3 months.  He has offered to provide scheduled QSOs to anyone who needs EP.

Pooyan is a very nice fellow and he would do what it takes to help others.  He's doing it as a hobby and there's no financial incentive for him (he has an Italian QSL manager and he uses LoTW).  We will adjust the bands and timing as conditions change.   The first sked will take place this weekend. Watch NCDXC chat for further updates!

Rules for participating in these Skeds:

1) Only call EP3PK if you can hear him. Do not call him if you cannot hear him.

2) Do not post him on a DX Cluster during the Sked period. He is doing this for us as he wants to help the West Coast.

3) Only call him if you need EP3PK for a new country. Do not call him for a band fill. That is not the purpose of this sked. Let the guys that need their first EP contact have a chance.

4) Do not ask him to QSY to another band or mode.

5) If on CW, you do not need to send his call. We do not want the skimmers to pick up his call.

Watch your NCDXC Chat for updates on the next dates and times for a sked with EP3PK.


Thanks,

Paul N6PSE
