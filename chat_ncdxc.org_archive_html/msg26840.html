Yes, Starlink is tough to deal with.  I have learned the way to open a ticket for service whether it is technical or account related and I have had both.  Go to the FAQs, find one that is semi related to your concern and go to the bottom where you can vote thumbs up or down.  Double click on a thumbs down and you might get the support form to come up allowing a real question.  They respond quickly to these.  I also learned that doing so on a home computer is different than using the Android App, which seems to be offering some additional functionality.

YMMV
