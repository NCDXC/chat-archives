I thought the 9M4SLL team did a pretty good job. I made 12 contacts with them 10-40 meters. I didn't get them on 80/160 but that is due to my inadequate antenna on those bands. I worked them on CW/SSB and RTTY. I thought all of the operators were very competent and eager to work everyone.
 
I felt like the team made an effort to work all regions and despite the poor solar conditions they did a great job!
