15 minutes ago XX9THX was an S8 on my S meter and as I listen now he is down in the noise at S0 and I can barely hear him. Either propagation changed or he probably turned his antenna. By the time I called him very few stations were calling him from the US and I was able to work him with one call. After I worked him he called CQ a number of times with no takers.
