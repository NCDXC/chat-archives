As DXers, you should be aware that the DXAC is considering changed to the 5BDXCC award.  Currently, you must confirm 100 entities on these bands: 80, 40, 20, 15 and 10 M.  The proposal is to allow ANY five bands to contribute to the 5BDXCC award.

 

Interested DXers are encouraged to provide comments to your local DXAC contact.  For many of our members, that is Ken, K6TA.  K6TA@volcano.net  Here is the full list of contacts: http://www.arrl.org/files/file/DXCC/DXAC%20List%20April%202019.pdf

 

Here is the source of this info:

“As of yesterday, the DXAC has been asked to review whether 5BDXCC should be granted for any 5 bands instead of the original 80-40-20-15-10. Comments to your DXAC representative are encouraged.

 

73

 

John K2VV

 

Chairman ARRL DX Advisory Committee”

 

I sent Ken my comments and you should do, if you like or dislike the potential change.
