I received an interesting QSL card in the mail yesterday from KH6JF/MM.   I had an FT8 “QSO” with an autonomous maritime robot called a Wave Glider, deployed by the Jupiter Foundation ARC.

 

 

But was it a QSO, or just a contact, as there was no control op at the other end – just a robot running FT8 and responding to CQ calls?  Either way it was kind of cool to work it.

 

Anyway, more info here:  http://www.jrfarc.org/hf-voyager/

 

And some cool whale song recordings here:  http://jupiterfoundation.org/
