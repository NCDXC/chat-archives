Greetings Paul and the Group,

 

I enjoyed the IDXC very much.  I arrived Friday in time to enjoy the Contest Dinner, then of course the Saturday Night Banquet event as well. 

 

I for one don’t mind a different venue regardless of the location.  It’s not where we meet, but with whom we meet with that is important.
