Elvira IV3FSG has now uploaded her Uganda Dxpedition logs (5X3R) through 8/25 to QRZ.com, Clublog, and HRDLog. Previously she had uploaded SSB QSO's only, but now it appears that all modes are uploaded.

You can check your QSO's at the bottom of her QRZ page, or here: http://www.hrdlog.net/Viewlogbook.aspx?user=5X3R

QSL can be ordered via OQRS on Clublog, or direct to IK2DUW.

She's planning to remain in Uganda through September, doing radio in her spare time (she works for an NGO).

73 and good DX,
Andy K6ZP
