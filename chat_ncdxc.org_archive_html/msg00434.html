On Nov 19, 2012, at 2:50 PM, K6XN wrote:

> Good signal now in Saratoga and working a lot of West Coast stations.

 

What a madhouse! The V84SMD station was on 21290, listening 5 up. PT0S was on 21295, listening 5-30 up. So lots of folks calling the V84 on top of PT0S, and of course the frequency-cops are telling them that they are all lids, but of course the interferers can't hear the cops because they are listening to V84, 5 down. The V84 realized what was going on, and switched to listening 5 down, i.e., 21285, but not everyone got the message.

BTW, there is never a good reason to request spreads of 25 kHz (and sometimes they are working station waaaay beyond that). On 12 and 17m it can take out the entire band. Even on 15m, it's not necessary.

Worked them about 12 kHz up, on 21307.
