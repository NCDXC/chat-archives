Just gotta thank you for all the posts and hints on this site.  Having so much fun chasing the Dx this year.  Learning a ton and having fun watching everyone work this latest entity.  I get up and sit behind the radio hunting down some good stations.  At night I check to see if there's anything going on as well.  Weird how just seeing that redline RR73 or your own call on live stream brings a bit of joy to the day.  Interesting hobby and one that people in my "hood" don't quite get.  I will admit that I am an eyesore with 3 towers but I'm sure most of us see the beauty in a good tower and antenna system.  I suspect I'm not alone when I drive around and keep looking in the air for antennas.

Anyways, have a good day and thanks to any service members or family of those who serve or have served in our military!  

I'll be watching as we chase FT4GL today.  I see them on 17FT8 and 15 SSB.   Good luck and good hunting.
