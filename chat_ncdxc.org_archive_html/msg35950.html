Friends,

I wanted to share a photo of my old friend Ross since he and I both worked at KFJC back in 1967 or 1968.  I met his dad Jack and went to his parent's house many times growing up and he encouraged me to join the NCDXC. I was glad to get this itch to pick up the phone and call Ross just prior to his death and have a long conversation. I don't question what drew me to make that call out of the blue, but I'm glad I did.  Ross said he had four strokes recently and then I find out that both he and my recently deceased wife, Alice, both died suddenly of esophageal cancer and both in the month of June.

RIP OM,
William, N6BM
