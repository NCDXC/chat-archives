Hello All,

The opening to VK started here just before 0100Z, and continued until I took a break at 0215Z.  Japan started coming in here around 0130Z.  During that period of over two hours, I made 58 QSO in VK and JA, including three QSO as far west as zone 29.  My antenna direction during this period varied between 240 to 255 degrees.

73, Bill
N6WS
