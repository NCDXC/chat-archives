Northern California DX Club Joins Cass Award Sponsors

 

Club Log and DXLab are happy to welcome the Northern California DX Club (NCDXC) as a sponsor of the Cass Award. Named in honor of legendary DXing proponent and NCDXC member Cass WA6AUD, this award has encouraged single-operator DXpeditions to maximize the number of unique DXers worked with a Plaque for each year’s single-operator DXpedition that works the most unique callsigns within a two-week interval. Winners of the Cass Award include Jeff Martin K5WE, José Luis Murano LU1FM, Nigel Cawthorne G3TXF, Peri Monioudis, HB9IQB, and Rob Chipperfield M0VFC.

“The Northern California DX Club strongly encourages DXPeditions to work as many DXers as possible”, said Ross Forbes K6GFJ, NCDXC President. “This award expands the number of DXers who recognize that DX Is!”.

Beginning in 2018, Club Log, DXLab, and the NCDXC will offer a second “unlimited” Cass Award: a Plaque for the multi-operator DXpedition that works the most unique callsigns each year.

For more information about the Cass Award, see http://www.cassaward.com .
