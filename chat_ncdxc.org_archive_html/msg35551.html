Some sobering facts:

Density distribution of FT4GL QSOs:


Lots of EU, Lots of JA, Fair amount of NA, but mostly East Coast and Midwest. Scant coverage out here im West Coast.

For those waiting for SSB, he has made ONLY 96 SSB QSOs with NA, mostly East Coast. Over 5000 FT8 QSOs, though. Note that he is sharing living (and shack) quarters with a couple of other people, so he cannot run SSB while ANY of them are asleep.

Last night he was running all night on 30m and 20m FT8. I put my own station in “robot mode”, and discovered that I worked him on 20m during my dinner. (I think I saw Bob K6XX doing the same.) Two and a half hours of calling at 1 kW. Clearly, this was due to my superior operating skills. (Not! Personal comment: This is not ham radio. Robot software talking to robot software.)

Tonight he will run 40m and 20m FT8 in his sleep.
