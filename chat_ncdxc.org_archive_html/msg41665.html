In my opinion, JK antennas are the best you can buy. Full stop. We have purchased them for K6LRG, and new antennas at N6RO are also JK antennas, including a jk 801 AT 160ft.
The Monobanders are robust, well build and well engineered and handle hilltop wind loads. the multibanders, like their Navassa series are also tanks, work wonderful and also enjoy the precision engineering and robustness of their Monoband cousins.
If I needed to replace a Steppir at a home station, it would likely be with something like a JK Navassa 5.
These antennas have thousands of qsos and hundreds of entities worked in the few years they have been in the air.
