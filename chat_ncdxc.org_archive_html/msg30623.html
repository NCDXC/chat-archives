Dom by chosen group name and actions, is a Rebel.  That means that he decides his rules, which may not be the generally accepted rules.  Example:  Using F/H on the standard (already busy) freq window, adding more mess and confusion.  To what end?

He only sends RR73 when there is more than one station in the stream; which is not often.  But he claims (and has for years now) that it's sent every time (well documented as not true).  In calling him at times, I would sit and watch; RR73 is rare; only when 2 in one stream.  (I just got it on 40 FT8, on the usual space).  He typically prefers one caller per stream and with that an hour can pass before seeing ANY RR73.

He also has the software lock out those he considers worked (logged); so trying again after getting a report; is a waste of watts.  Wait a day.  Try tomorrow.  He's not a hit and run visitor, it's often weeks of opportunity (as the sun allows); you have another shot (and one reason I prefer to get in and done early, just in case).

He prefers taking (over) the usual frequencies lately, instead of finding a quieter place.  He seems to rather enjoy the controversy that causes (so please don't share this; he can be openly vindictive, even deleting logged entries).

I HAVE gotten the LOTW confirmation, AFTER making a small (and I mean small) donation for cards.  He claims to have 400k cards printed and they'll be mailed on his return home; IN A COUPLE MORE YEARS.  That's a HUGE postage bill.  Wait and see, if your memory of such things still works (I keep a file for all outstanding cards requested).

Your options are: play it his way for the ATNO or slots (me, I'm a slot hound); or not; he doesn't care (and stated that MANY times). 

If you do not donate, you wait at least 6 months for LOTW (he says).  I have not tested that, payment usually gets the confirmation in a day or two.  I wait until the end of the DXpedition, pay once.

I've actually heard him chat on the air about plans and activities, which leaves me more observations, that I'll keep to myself.  He is, a rebel at many levels; that much is certain.
