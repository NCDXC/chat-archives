Try adjusting your SHIFT control to match their tones.  This gives a slight boost in audio for their tones.


Then narrow your bandwidth as much as you need to copy.  This will skew (raise) what signal report you send; that does not matter (it is the EXCHANGE that counts, not the value or no one would be 59/59 all the time).


They hear pretty well (maybe too well some times) but you have to hear the rather meager signals (for the most part) they produce.
