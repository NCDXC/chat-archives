Rick,

hard to tell. I had them at -04 on 30M FT8 and they gave me a -01, which is pretty good. In Fox/Hound mode they are replying to 4 to 5 stations in parallel, so less power per individual “stream"?

They were running Fox/Hound mode on the regular FT8 frequency, competing with the other stations that don’t know there is a Fox/Hound expedition in progress. I think that’s a bad thing to do. It causes lots of collisions and confusion when plenty of innocent callers are picking frequencies below 1000Hz and interfering with the exchanges in progress. Lot’s of retries and missed exchanges. 

73, Andreas, N6NU
