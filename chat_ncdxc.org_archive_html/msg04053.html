OK – I have two chokes – one at each antenna. They are the Balun Designs 1116du (I had two laying around under the house), and now I was going to put these home brewed one’s on each line going into my K9AY control box – and the other into the MAIN of the MFJ-1025.

 

It might be overkill, but my noise level dropped by what seems like (if my calculations are correct) – 20.9 dB

 

K3 S9 to S2, but verified using the P3 where you can see the difference here:

 

http://ky6r.wordpress.com/

 

and where the P3 shows a difference of -105 down to -130 – so “close enough” to my 20log10(Sv/Nv) calculation.

 

But I have never calculated this sort of thing – and do not have any lab gear – so I might be over stating the improvement. I do know it is night and day – and using the K9AY as a steerable “noise antenna” against the N6RK loops (rotatable) that I am getting the upper end of what my expectations were.
