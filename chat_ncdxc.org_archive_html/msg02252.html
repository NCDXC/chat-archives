Hi Jim,

>  How many of us living on the SF peninsula will be willing to spend several hours in rush hour traffic to attend a meeting in Livermore? 

Oh I don't know Jim, I happen to live in the eastern hills in rural Livermore (Altamont Hills halfway to Mountain House) and you know, I do drive not only the 2+ hours in rush hour traffic to make it in time for the monthly meeting at Holder's, but I also drive 2.5 hours to San Jose in rush hour traffic to make the BOD meeting.  Why?  Because it's important for me to be there to vote on and perform my Secretarial responsibilities.  I could make the drive over to the Peninsula or better yet, a meeting at Cattlemens in Livermore ;-) .  Maybe using WebEx is the answer, but I don't know if Holder's has an internet connection available.  To me, the drive time from the Peninsula to San Jose wouldn't be that big of a deal but it's something that we have to deal with traffic when you live in the Bay Area; a drive Russ and many other guys do regularly (from the Peninsula to S.J.).  It is only once a month.

We did bring up this matter of moving the meeting location around the Bay Area up at the last BOD meeting and to join other clubs, but we have been fortunate to lock in our monthly Thursday night at Holder's.  We are concerned that the slot we fill at Holder's can easily be taken over by another organization and when it was time to return to Holder's, we may not be able to get that meeting room again so we tabled the discussion for the Board.  It's good to be able to get some positive feedback on this issue now as it will help the new Board to make that decision in everyone's best interest.

73 de Bill, N6BM
