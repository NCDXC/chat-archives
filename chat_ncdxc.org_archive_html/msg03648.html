Hi Bob,

Yes, first I will give an update on the Intrepid-DX Groups efforts to activate two very rare entities.

In my talk about my visit to North Korea, I will discuss the political, geographical and terrain challenges in activating P5.

I'll cover what it was like to visit Pyongyang, the Capitol and to visit various government offices. I was then able to travel South to Kaesong where I saw the "Voice of Korea" transmitter towers. I then visited the DMZ and had actual conversations with DPRK soldiers who were there to defend against "American aggression".

I then travelled to the Northern part of the DPRK and was one of the first Westerners to walk into the DPRK from China.  I visited with DPRK families in their homes and I visited factories and schools.  

I'll tell you about the various museums and the anti-American propaganda and current sentiment. I will tell you about my plans to return to North Korea with a radio delegation in 2014.

Also, I will be bringing various ham radio books to give away.

See you guys tonight,
