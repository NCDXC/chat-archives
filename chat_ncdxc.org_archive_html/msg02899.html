I am also chasing noise.  As Bob had, I see 15 over 9 on 40 M and up to S9 up to 10 M, directional but cannot pinpoint.  Nothing portable but an AM radio from Heathkit.
PGE came out one Saturday AM and said he would help.  A ham, to boot!  He was parked under the ATT and Comcast lines but not the PGE lines.  I parked next to him, turned on the AM radio and said 'Listen to this'!  Loud nasty buzz.  He did and said it's from a solar system, inverter.  Offered to find it and then got transferred to Livermore.  He suggested it was resonating with the utility lines and could be anywhere.
 
A few days ago, the 15 over 9 on 40 M was gone and I went down to the hot spot under the utility lines.  Same noise.  Suggests two independent sources and both do not sound like PGE crackle.
 
Six visits from Comcast to determine if their line amps are faulting and none of them know anything but how to drop a cable to a living room.  "You gotta talk to the network guys' but they cannot but me in touch.
 
An on and on...
 
At least I do not have Jim's 33 VDC PS switching noise!
 
Al
N6TA
