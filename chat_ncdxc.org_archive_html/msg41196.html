I tried N1MM as a general logger, but I found it insufficient for the task.  N3FJP's ACLog is good and has options for modes that N1MM did not support (at least at the time) such as DSTAR.  At the time, I was doing a bit of HF DSTAR with the weekly DSTAR net.

I've used Log4OM on Linux before, but wasn't as thrilled with it and their contest support was non-existent, but that was V1 IIRC.  Scott and the crew at N3FJP/Affirmatech have the right mindset, buy once & get updates for life.  The way it should be.  I've bought their suite of contesting programs as well.  Scott seems pretty open to suggestions and has been helpful in the Groups.IO N3FJP Users group.  He's also pushed out some free software for Meshtastic.  The only downside is, there is no desire to create linux based versions, so hopefully they work with Wine/Bottles.
