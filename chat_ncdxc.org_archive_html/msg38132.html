I’m saddened to learn from my Tifaritti Gang friends that Tony EA5RM has passed away at age 56.

Tony had become my dear friend and “my brother from another mother”

Tony and I first partnered in 2011 to activate the new country of South Sudan as ST0R. That was the start of an enduring friendship and partnership. 

Over the years, I had the pleasure to travel with Tony and his team to other exotic places. I also had the pleasure to award him the Intrepid-DX Group’s Humanitarian Award as well to nominate him for the ARRL Humanitarian Award and see that award presented by ARRL President K5UR at Dayton. Most recently I had the pleasure of nominating Tony for the CQ DX Hall of Fame. Tony was very proud of that honor.

Tony had big plans for the future but was also taking time with his new wife as he had recently married. 

Tony was a giant in the amateur radio and DXing realm and he will be missed deeply by those that knew him. 

Paul N6PSE
