Some of you have emailed me and asked about the presentation at the next NCDXC meeting.
 
In my presentation, I will tell you about my “Balkan Road Trip and visit to Mount Athos” in November, 2012:
K3LP and I visited YT1AD at his super-station in rural Serbia. We operated from there. I will show you his massive station, many towers and Russian amplifiers.
We visited Kosovo and operated from there. We met with Kosovar amateurs and enjoyed lunch together. I will explain the complex political situation in Kosovo.
We joined with Greek amateurs and visited Mount Athos. I will explain how access to Mount Athos is made. We met with Monk Apollo and toured some Monasteries. I’ll show you photos of Monk Apollo’s antennas and explain how challenging it is for him to work the West Coast. We visited the crypts where the deceased Monks are kept.
We operated from SV2BFN in Greece.
We drove to Macedonia and operated from Z32A.
We drove through Albania and operated from there.
We visited Dragan-4O4A in Montenegro and operated from his QTH.
We returned to Belgrade Serbia. I will show you how Belgrade still has bombed out buildings from the NATO bombing in 2002.
 
Paul N6PSE
