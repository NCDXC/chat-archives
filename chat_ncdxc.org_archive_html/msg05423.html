I just read in the minutes of the Jan BoD meeting that plans to solve
the problems of the W6TI repeater system receive coverage have been
dropped. The excuse was that it doesn't get much use. Of course it
doesn't -- many of us who want to use it can't access it because it's
DEAF. My 2M FM base station runs 160W through 1/2-in hard line to a 5dBi
antenna at 70 ft, the system is nearly always 30dB or better quieting,
but more than half the time I can't even bring the system up, and when I
can, I'm noisy into the system.

If there was ever any doubt that this is the South Silcon Valley DX
Tired Old Mens DX Club, this action is yet another confirmation of it.

73, Jim K9YC
