Michigan weather extremes taught us tapes and such either melt or get brittle---have always used conductive grease on all fittings--center connector and outer ring threads ...a lineman gave me a 1 pint squeeze bottle years ago that I will likely never empty---they use it on all electrical fittings.  I used it on all tower joints, yagi joints---anyplace a very reliable non-corrosive bond, metal to metal, was needed---then over-wrap with the nonstick coax seal (overkill) and then over wrap that with cheap electrical tape ( more overkill) because the coax seal deteriorates in sunlight.  Also makes taking towers down easier. Not brain trust---just don't like re-climbing to find bad electrical joints...
