This year PACIFICON is hosting the ARRL National Convention. The meeting is taking place at the Santa Clara Marriott Convention Center from October 12-14, 2012 ( this Friday-Sunday).  

http://www.pacificon.org/

This should be a great meeting.  NCDXC will have an Exhibit Table at the Convention and we're looking for help from all NCDXC members. 

Some folks have signed up already.....but more Volunteers are still needed.

Please check the sign-up page on the NCDXC Website, and if at all possible, volunteer for one or two hours at the table.   Ideally, we would like to have two people for each time slot.

http://ncdxc.org/pacificon/pacificon.html

This is your oportunity to talk about the club, recruit new members, and network with fellow DXers who are interested in our exciting aspect of the hobby.  

This is also a FUN way to be of service to NCDXC! 

Thanks in advance for your help.

73,
