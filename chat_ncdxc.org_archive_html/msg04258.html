Q: How do I deal with a lousy DX location? A: MOVE!

OR:

Learn HFTA and exploit the paths that will work by engineering your antenna farm to make lemonade where there are lemons. Hilltop QTH’s in the SF Bay Area are a bit expensive . . .  although some have used a remote super contest station to great advantage – in the same geographic (propagational) area as their own QTH.


Q: How do I deal with work interfering with DXing? A: RETIRE!

OR:

Get a job where you can work from home whenever you want. I think I worked full time at home about half of the 11 ½ years it took me to earn HR.

Simple answers to pressing questions.

Garry
