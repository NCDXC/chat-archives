Thank you all for the nice comments.  All of the entries are fantastic.  I especially like Mike's creative use of the 'route 66' logo in his design.  That's very original and creative.  I think we are all winners.  I want to thank the logo committee for providing this opportunity.

The winning design (#L5) was my first design.  I was a first time IDXC attendee this year.  I was awestruck by the hospitality and warmness expressed by all of the people I met at the convention.  And I wanted to express the positive experiences I had to the logo design.  I hope the 2015 IDXC will be one of the best conventions ever.

Thanks again for providing this fun and creative contest.

First, a big Thank You to those who submitted logo entries for IDXC 2015.
Here is the complete list:

http://ncdxc.org/pages/logos2015.html

L1-L3 were all submitted by Roberto Garcia, EA2RY

L4-L7 were all submitted by Jonathan Woo, W6GX

L8 was submitted by Mike Flowers, K6MKF

L9 was submitted by Joanna Dilley, K6YL

For IDXC 2015, we plan to offer at least two convention shirt color choices
for attendees.  Factoring in other complex issues such is "stitch-abiility"
on fabric, colors, fonts, etc. --- we decided to form a 3-person Logo
Committee to make the final  decision, instead of putting it to a club-wide
vote.

Therefore we are please to announce that Logo Entry L5 from Jonathan Woo,
W6GX, has been selected as the IDXC 2015 logo for the convention next year.


Jonathan will receive a free convention shirt for his efforts, and his logo
will help promote IDXC 2015 in many ways, including the IDXC website:

http://dxconvention.com/index.html

Congratulations, Jonathan!

And thanks again to Roberto, Mike, and Joanna for their entries......much
appreciated.

IDXC 2015 Logo Committee
