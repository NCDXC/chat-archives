Worst DX tactic ever!  

One might have the DX at 20 over, but you have to get through to the list taker and the band conditions simply may not allow that (like now).  The QSO rate is also appallingly slow.  Z81D is doing about 1 Q/min now in bursts. 

That style should die a quick death.  Thankfully for the most part, it has. 

Plus in this day and age, if the DX is remotely rare, split is SO much more effective.  Everyone can do it now and it leaves the DX more in the clear [except for UP cops ;-)]

-:-:-

17 has been good for some ATNOs for the last week or so but my all time fave band is still 15.
