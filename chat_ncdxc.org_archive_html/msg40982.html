Just posted on DX-World from the 6O3T team:

We’ve received quite a few messages lately from operators thinking we’re working only in FT8, but that’s not true at all! We love CW and SSB just as much.

Unfortunately, at the moment, we’re dealing with heavy local QRM (above S9!), which makes it almost impossible to copy stations clearly. We’re testing several solutions, like filters, grounding, antenna adjustments, but so far no real improvement. We’ll keep trying and hope to be back soon on CW and SSB, as soon as the conditions allow!

Thanks everyone for your patience and support.
