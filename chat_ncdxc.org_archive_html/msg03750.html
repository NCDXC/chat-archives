Bob Henderson, 5B4AGN, is the designer of the very nice auto-switched bandpass filter kits that several club members built, and that I reviewed for NCJ a year or so ago. Those sets cover the contesting bands, but not the WARC bands, and are primarily intended for SO2R and Multi-Op contesting.
In response to many requests, Bob has published designs and test results for single-band filters optimized for DXpeditions. The design goal was at least 26 dB attenuation on next adjacent band, and most of the filters exceed that by 10 dB. Bob is preparing to take orders for kits to build these single band filters, and also the single enclosures to house them.
Interested parties should contact Bob or sign up for the TXBPF Yahoo email group. Bob's kits are very well designed and put together, his manuals are very well written, and he provides excellent support via email. 

73, Jim K9YC
