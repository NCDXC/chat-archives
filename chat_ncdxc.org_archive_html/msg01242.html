On 2/11/2013 9:32 AM, Bob Lanning wrote:
Kenwood will be there with the TS-990S.  I want lay my hands on that one.

I fail to understand the fascination with radios from JA when truly world class radios like the Ten Tec Orion II and Elecraft K3 and KX3 are made right here in the USA.  If you think Elecraft is out of your price league, take a look at the KX3 with the not yet finished 100W amp. $900 for the KX3, I'm guessing under $500 for the amp, and a few hundred bucks for the 100W tuner if you want it. The plain vanilla KX3 is only slightly larger than the remote heads for most mobile rigs, but has specs that beat the K3, puts out 10W, and comes with the second RX as standard equipment!  When I visited W6GJB last week I saw a really nice desktop stand for the KX3 distributed by the guy who does the Nifty manuals. 

And, BTW, the Elecraft and Orion II rigs do much better with lots of strong signals on the band and put out cleaner signals, so you're being a good neighbor.  Last I looked, you've got to be pushing $10K to match that performance with a JA rig.

73, Jim K9YC
