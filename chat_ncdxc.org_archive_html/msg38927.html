I was able to get 40 phone a couple mornings ago (WITH unfriendly interaction heard from a net that refused to move when asked; VK9XU was there first AND they heard it; let’s not go there, each group thinks the other is stupid).  That opening lasted until around 9 AM Pac time.  I heard Steve SRD wend his way through that mess.

80 CW was last week (I’d have to look at the log); an hour plus into our daylight.  I saw other callers, but       the         op       took             long              pauses   between     callers, so be aware of that.  He appeared to hear pretty well (1/3 of the distance around this rock).

For 40 phone, the SteppIR was still up (tower down and tilted, rotor issues, being repaired) and on 80M the ground plane was used; solid copy both times AND in the log.

I still have the option of using the 160 L on some bands, with tuner, while I wait for the rotor to go back into action.
