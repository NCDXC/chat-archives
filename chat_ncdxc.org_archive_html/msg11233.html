I seriously doubt that it's "dead" to them if they're spending time there. They're a "most-wanted" entity! So yes, they're calling CQ. 


Should we be calling blindly on 1827.5?
NO! NEVER call DX when you can't hear well enough to copy your call and report, and to hear his instructions. You become QRM to the guys who CAN hear the DX. DX stations will often give a partial call, and everyone else MUST stop calling until he's worked that station. How can you stop calling if you can't hear the DX?
