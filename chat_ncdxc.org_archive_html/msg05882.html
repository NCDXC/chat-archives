No caveats or warnings on the website, only a courteous reply with no apology or discount.

You are still within the 60 day period to dispute the charge with your credit card company. File a dispute (usually can do this online), reason is item never received. They will reverse the charge and put the burden on the merchant to show why it should be reinstated.

And never use a debit card.
