TWO FORCE 12 BEAMS OFFERED, ALMOST FREE

I have two used HF multiband antennas by Force 12 that need a new home.
  • "The DXer". (Strike Force Nomad N201715) Classic N6BT triband staggered array. 3 el on 20, 3 on 15 with a 24 ft. boom. Two feed lines. The 15m driver uses Tom's 3-element driver cell on both 15 and 17m and--with the 17m reflector-- provides "two and a half" elements performance-wise on 17m. 9 elements total on the boom. Put up on a treetop in 1994, taken down two years ago. About 56 lbs and 8.5 sf wind load.
  • Strike Force "N1012". Classic N6BT duo-band staggered array. 4 el on 10, functionally 3 el on 12. using a 3 el driver cell and the 10m directors with 10m and 12m reflectors. 7 elements overall on a 14.5' boom, one feed line. 48 lbs and 3.4sf wind load. Put up in 1998 and taken down two years ago. 
Both were taken down from their redwood treetop after rodents chewed the feed lines--out of reach, of course. I intended to refurbish and reinstall them but changed course and instead put up a SteppIR to simplify installation complexity and expense, improve accessibility if the rodents returned--and provide 6m.

The antennas are completely disassembled and the pieces marked and grouped by element. Manuals are included as are used bead chokes. Some or all gray PVC used as insulators may benefit from replacement---PVC gets brittle with age.

I offer the antennas FREE, but there is a catch. When I planned to refurb them I purchased lots of new replacement hardware from Force 12, including a strongback for the DXer, many muffler clamps, bolts, boom/mast plate, a couple of element tips, antioxidant goop, rivets, etc. I would like $250 for the hardware, whose replacement would be necessary in any case. Both antennas are ready for pickup.

These antennas propelled me to #1 HR, although not overnight, from a less-than-ideal QTH.

If interested, email me or call (408) 781-4687.
