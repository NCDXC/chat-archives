What type of support do you have? Why not use a rotatable dipole like the Cushcraft D4? It's 35 feet from on to end and it also does 20, 15 and 10M. 

I just installed one up 55ft in my redwood tree and it rotates 120Deg since it's on the side of the tree and not the top. Good enough to cover 350deg through 110deg headings to Europe, AF and the Caribbean. The bidirectional nature and the beam width take care of all other headings.

I am thinking of using just the 40/30 StepIR trombone as a rotatable dipole down the road.
