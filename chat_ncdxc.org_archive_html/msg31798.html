Until recently, it was difficult to get to Socotra. In 2012, we flew direct from Sana'a the Capital of Yemen, however that is not an option during the war.  

The UAE has established a significant foothold in Socotra and their airline, Air Arabia has twice weekly flights to Socotra from Abu Dhabi. So it is now quite easy and safe to travel to Socotra from the UAE bypassing worn torn Yemen altogether.
