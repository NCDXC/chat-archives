Hi Mac!

 

It’s great to see you on the reflector.  Please don’t be so quiet.  Let everyone know what your thoughts are. As a longtime member of the NCDXC, we’d love to hear your thoughts.

 

By the way, regarding The DX’er, I went up to the California Historical Radio Society a few weeks ago and borrowed everything Jim Maxwell, W6CF, had on file.  The good news is that most of the missing issues prior to 2000 have been found. Years like 1989 and 1960 where everything was missing, have been completed. All of the copies have been made and are safe on my PC, along with extra backups for the NCDXC archives.  As soon as I get everything over to John Miller, K6MM, he will transfer them to the web and fill in the holes in our DX’er section of tNCDXC.ORG. Be looking for them over the next couple of weeks.  There are lots of good memories to be found.
