While there will always be a “People’s Choice Award” – which is what we have today, it is possible to use Clublog or LOTW to come up with a metric where a computer could spit out a computerized log based winner as well - maybe by class too. My favorite would be some kind of ranking of rarity * # atno’s handed out (is this uniques?) with some time based factor as well.

 

Just crazy thinking. Then people like UA4WHX wouldn’t be left out of these yearly awards. In fact – any DX-pedition who submits logs would be eligible. Even people spending a year on a remote base – like the ZS8 people could be in on the fun – and it would encourage giving out more Q’s.

 

It’s almost like Field Day scoring or contest scoring. I’m sure my thinking has a few flaws in it . . .
