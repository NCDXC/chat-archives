   And in a similar vein: what is the DXCC policy with, err..., "unsupervised" FT8 stations? There was forum chatter during some DXpeditions where their FT8 transmissions would get stuck in a loop (e.g. waiting for an RR73) for l-o-n-g periods of time, suggesting that they were running in some kind of unmanned robo-mode. Some folks argued that could/should/might disqualify the QSOs from DXCC consideration since fully automated operation is not allowed. 

   AFAIK that never happened - no DXpedition QSOs got retroactively disqualified - but is possible? Some FT8 behavior can be pretty suspicious during off hours...
