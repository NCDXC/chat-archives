Hi Dave,

They were quite strong yesterday at 2317 UTC on 12 Meters 24.921 MHz FT8. They were +23 dB here and my 90 Watts got a +4 dB.

10 Meter SSB was at 1827 UTC on 28.440 MHz.

30 Meters CW was at 0652 UTC on 10.110 MHz.
