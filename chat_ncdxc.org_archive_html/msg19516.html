Last year when I was running Windows 7, an upgrade
messed up the virtual serial ports.  There were
various articles on the Flex site that had different
suggestions.  One curious one said NOT to uninstall
and reinstall them if they got messed up.  Uninstall/
reinstall drivers is usually the first thing you try
after re-booting.

Long story short, the VSP vendor said doing that messes
up the Win 7 "driver store".  Once that happens, they
had no fix other than reinstall Windows.  Uninstalling
Flex doesn't fix it nor does using a newer Flex software.
And, they don't support Windows 7 any more.

I was just about to upgrade to windows 10 at the time,
and that fixed the driver store problem or whatever it
was.

I'm still using 2.6.1 by the way and no problem.
It should not be necessary to pay $200 just to keep
the radio working after an OS upgrade.

Rick N6RK
