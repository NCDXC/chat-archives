Hi Kevin,

I understand the reasons, but there has to be a better way to manage it (put a choke on that shotgun approach).  Moving to a stronger station seems the more obvious answer; sending any number /should/ thin things down too.  If you can clear out some of the big guns, the lil guys have a better chance.  I've not faced that wall from the other side, but there has to be a better way.

Keeping the filters tight helps, which is what I do (just wide enough for the DX, no more), but not all decoders are as forgiving.  I was fortunate that when he came back from dinner, I was able to track his pattern before the pileup re-grew.  It paid off.

K1N is likely the worst case examples of a pileup.  I can't remember any that were larger, ruder or more generally obnoxious.  Of the choices available, there have been times I walked away instead of padding the desk to save my head.  ;o)

Rick nhc
