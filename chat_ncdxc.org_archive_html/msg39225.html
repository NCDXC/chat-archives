hi Paul - I do. I started out getting the FlightAware "package" but switched it over to the FlightRadar24 software because I like the display better. I've never bothered to figure out how to feed both. I have a simple/cheap mag mount antenna stuck to a gutter outside my garage and it does the job well enough.

It didn't cost that much and it's worth it to me to have the "Business" membership on FlightRadar24 up here in the foothills. I help moderate a local public safety online group doing stuff that WatchDuty has been doing more recently. When a veg fire is called out following the planes gives you the best idea of where it's happening, which is good to know in case you have to bug out. 

Let me know how I can help - 73 jeff wk6i
