DXLab's DXKeeper logging component provides powerful logging capabilities to help minimize time spent on "paperwork" - obtaining confirmations, generating outgoing QSL cards and labels, locating QSL routes, tracking confirmation requests, choosing confirmations to submit for award credit, and updating logged QSOs to reflect award credit granted. It also supports DXLab's realtime award tracking, which enables active DX stations with whom a QSO would advance your DXCC, IOTA, Leaderboard, Marathon, VUCC, WAS, WAZ, or WPX award progress to be instantly identified and highlighted. By enabling you to identify a needed station's operating pattern and identify band openings between your QTH and that of a needed station, DXLab helps you find and work the DX you need.
