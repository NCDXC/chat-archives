My 2017 Visalia International DX Convention photos are now online on Google Photos at:

https://goo.gl/photos/2PbjhY4zi8KYXzuD7 (slide show available, but captions are hidden)

They are also displayed in a slightly different "Album archive" format, here:

https://bit.ly/Visalia2017 (captions easily visible in the lower left corner, but no slide show)

At either site, use the "three-dot" menu, top right, to download the entire album.  Press the right arrow key on your keyboard to step through photos quickly.

Photos are displayed in chronological order taken, from Friday Contest Academy through Sunday morning DX Breakfast.  Unfortunately, the "search for my callsign" feature is not available on these Google photo sites, despite almost every photo having a caption. :-(

Permission is hereby granted for publication of these links and any of these photos in the NCCC Jug, NCDXC DXer, SCDXC Bulletin, SDDXC Bulletin, The NCDXF Newsletter, The FARS Relay, The Daily DX, MLDXCC Nugget, REDXA Sunspots, the ARRL Contest Update, and club web sites.

Suggested photo credit: "N6TV photo."

For photo use in other publications, please contact me directly.

Please publish only the short URL links listed above, not the long URLs these short URLs redirect to.  Posting these links to your local club reflectors would be very much appreciated (except for NCCC, NCDXC and MLDXCC, which I've already copied above).

To save a local high resolution copy of any photo, first select the photo, then at the top right, click the three dot menu and select "Download photo."

If you need help finding a particular photo of a particular person, feel free to write to me directly.

73,
Bob, N6TV
