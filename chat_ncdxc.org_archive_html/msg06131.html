Silently blacklisting call signs is a bad idea. Anybody who had his/her call sign plagiarized will be able to tell you how hard it is to clear your name. 

What’s even worse is that the blacklist is a hidden list. How does one know if one is on the list? How does one better oneself?

What keeps a person from completely sabotaging a DX station once he or she finds out about the black listing? They are shut out and have nothing to lose. They will retaliate and make sure nobody else makes it into the log either. If these people are actually bullies, do you think they have any problem going all the way?

Good luck going down that path. 

73, Andreas, N6NU
