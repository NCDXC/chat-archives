I can’t remember a summer with such poor conditions as this one has.  Looking forward to Fall and Winter – and some DXpeditions!

 

To kill time I have been running down QSLs but I’m running out of them.  But have to tell you about this one a few days ago.  I worked ZL2IFB on 80M CW around 10PM on 9/2 last week.  A new country for 80M.  The significance of this QSO is that after looking at his QRZ’d page and seeing a live Club Log log, on a lark I had DXLabs do a QSL match check (Sync LotW QSLs) and what!?  I had a confirmation – in only 10 minutes!  Amazing.
