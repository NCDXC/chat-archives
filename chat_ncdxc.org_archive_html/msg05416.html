I have the K-Line and love it. Even the KPA-500, (I finally put any "hunger for more power" to rest).

Anyway, since I had such great success using the MFJ-1025 to eliminate as much noise as possible for my RX antennas on 160, I figured I'd ask to see if anyone is using a headphone preamp / filter of any sort between the headphone and K3. 

I'm guessing that the K3 already does everything anyone would want, but thought I would ask anyway. I don't have a second RX, and maybe that's the better route to go? I used to have diversity RX on my Orion II years back - and do miss the sub RX and second RX - sometimes . . .
