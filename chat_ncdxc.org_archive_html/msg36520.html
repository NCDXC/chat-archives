The stars lined up and I had 3 hours to play radio today.  I had a consistent
run of running and S&P from 12:55p Pacific to 1:58p Pacific.  I was spotted
twice which was a first on SSB for me, and had small 2-3 call pileups.  I love
learning how to deal with that.  All the callers were very nice.  My noise floor
was very high today and about 5-7 callers I just could not pull out of the
noise.  I'm looking forward to more SSB and CW contests where I can use my ears
to pull out the call sign.
