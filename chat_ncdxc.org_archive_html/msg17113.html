Hi Doug:

I am saddened to tell you that Kat, the love of my life, passed away peacefully at 11pm on 1st May 2019.

Kat was born in Hawaii on 16th November 1963.  She and I were married in Rarotonga in August 1992 and have had a truly amazing life together for the last 27 years, culminating in these last 6 wonderful years in the Cook Islands

You know how much Kat loved the Cook Islands and how passionate she was about her work with the tamariki and teachers, and you know how devastated she was when she was told by the MoE and Immigration that she had to leave.

Our return to Canada was brief.  We arrived on a Thursday, she was hospitalized on Friday after only one night in our new home, transferred to Hospice on Sunday, and passed away peacefully in her sleep at 11 o'clock on Wednesday, May 1st. 

Her final weeks and days were filled with an outpouring of love and compassion, and the care and support she received in the hospital and hospice was absolutely wonderful.  There are no words to describe their kindness, caring and compassion! 

A parting picture and song -- Kei Kali Nei Au - It was sung at our wedding and, in Hawaiian, it expresses the love we have had between us over the last 30 or so years.
Song:  https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hN_u0x-4q0Q

Words Ke Kali Nei Au

Andy - E51AND / AB7FS
