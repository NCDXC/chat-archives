At 12:27 1/8/2014, Paul Ewing wrote:
I believe it is just a matter of time that SWODXA will start enforcing their trademarks and forcing DX-World, NCDXC and others to use a different term for their DXpedition award.
The US PTO says: A trademark is generally a word, phrase, symbol, or design, or a combination thereof, that identifies and distinguishes the source of the goods of one party from those of others.
Since many clubs use the phrase "DXpedition" and "DX dinner" to describe totally different things that are NOT the same as the SWODXA things by those names, it seems they fail to meet the basic definition which seems to imply that the trademark owner is the EXCLUSIVE user of the mark.
