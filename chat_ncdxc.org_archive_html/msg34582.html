Im personally a fan of Chrome Remote Desktop for a few reasons such as
- no client or software is required to access your pc
- you can access it from anywhere without having to setup port forwards etc
- you can give someone one time access to help you out if you are stuck

https://remotedesktop.google.com/

Im also pretty invested in the google ecosystem so you may find better options.
