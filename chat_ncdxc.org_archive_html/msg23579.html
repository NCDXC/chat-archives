FT8 has been a nice outlet for me during Covid. I have been working from home since Covid started and I spend many hours a day on conference calls or remotely monitoring networks and systems. I generally leave FT8 on and when a new band slot or a new digital mode entity flashes on my screen, I pounce and make the contact. I would not be able to do that on SSB or CW as that activity would interfere with my ability to work and be on the numerous calls that I have each day. 

FT8 is a new tool in the tool box and has enabled me to have fun with radios while working. FT8 has also considerably boosted my knowledge about HF propagation as I see DX on my screen at times when I would not otherwise expect that particular band to be open. FT8 has been particularly enjoyable during the low point in the sunspot cycle. I can definitely see that we are on the upswing of the new cycle. 

I look forward to things getting back to normal and chasing DX on SSB and CW as well. I also look forward to advances in the FT modes. Could FT2 be in the works?

73, 
Paul N6PSE

On Sun, Jun 27, 2021 at 2:14 PM Roberto - K6KM via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

As a new Ham I found no activity on the bands at home. Later I figured it was too much RFI from neighbors.

Somebody showed me FT8 and I started moving up WAS. Then suddenly DXCC started climbing. Was it as much fun as SSB or CW. Nope. But I guess if it hadn’t been for FT8 I would have sold the rig and moved on.

Then came contesting, learning CW and loving Summits on the Air. Using Remote quiet stations, operating from MM stations and portable.

QTH still stinks RFI. Hardly do FT8 except when there is an ATNO of filling a band slot.

My point is: Most Hams these days suffer from elevated noise floor and FT8 is the only way to play the game.

Read some interesting statistics in the recent QST I believe:

85% QSOs on 6m are now FT8, only just over 4% is CW

60% QSO's on HF are now with FT8 at the expense of the other modes

Not saying for or against but some interesting statistics since FT8 came into play in 2017. What I find interesting too given FT8 is very helpful in working DX weak signal when no other modes work, is the number of USA to USA contacts I see on FT8 at any given time now.
