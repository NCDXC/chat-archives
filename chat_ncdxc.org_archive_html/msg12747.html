I got a QSL yesterday: 7P8EB, for a 10 M SSB QSL from 1990.  No telling why I was not QSLing at the time.  Never confirmed 7P on 10 phone so figure I should look into it.

QRZ showed to QSL via KC4LLA.  That page had 469 lookups.  The KC4LLA page was at 322 lookups.  I sent a card in September 2015 and got a reply 14 months later.  I am thankful the op still had cards and honored the QSL request.

 

As an aside, LoTW continues to provide surprises.  Got a confirmation from JH1BZJ from 1972!   He has a heck of an antenna: 7 el delta loop over a long rotary dipole over a 2 el quad.

 

Fun to get both!
