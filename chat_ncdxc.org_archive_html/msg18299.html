Hi Greg,
Yes, I know about the station and have been following the developments over the last few years.  They are finally getting on after years of inactivity due to UN security issues that just could not be easily overcome.
My interest is keen in that I have several band slots unconfirmed due to sloppy operators in the past that simply did not keep logs or lost them or whatever.
This morning, a station came on calling 'CQ Test' signing the 4U1UN call and doing this over and over and not working anyone.  A while later, they were spotted and lots calling but I cold just not copy anything from the 4U1.  Then, an hour or two later, here they are again but a solid S8 on the S-meter.  I decided to WFWL and got a QSO but still wonder if it was the UN station or a 'Slim'.
Will see!

Thanks,
