Hi Paul,

- 40 CW
- 17, 20 FT8
On 17 this morning, about 06:00 (local), he was up to +22 while running one stream.

Tried 10 this morning but lost propagation.

One thing about this operation was - his clock seemed to be off much of the time.
Today on 10m, I had to use Time Fudge in order to decode him.

I could hear his tones, but no decodes, Fudge'd the clock and he came right in.
To test my theory, I reset my computer clock with Dimension 4 and I lost the decode.
Time Fudge'd again and started decoding him. Did this several times.

Earlier in the operation a friend on the east coast was calling the ZS8.
My friend's clock was at way off.
I called him on the phone, he said the ZS8's clock was +1.8 and by using Time Fudge to sync with the ZS8, he worked him.

Same thing this morning on 15 with a friend in Colorado. We were on the phone, he changed his clock - Bingo  worked the ZS8.
Propagation plays interesting tricks on FT8 signals.

Cheers,
GS K5GS
