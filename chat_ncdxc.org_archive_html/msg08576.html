Baldur’s Mt Athos activation was counted for DXCC, despite Monk Apollo’s letter to the ARRL that he would go QRT forever.
if they counted it. 
 
You can read Monk Apollo’s letter to the ARRL here: https://groups.google.com/forum/#!msg/rec.radio.amateur.misc/s9Rd7ca0bsk/M36LT9ErmKQJ
 
Given Baldur’s 2012 experience at the hands of the Greek’s at Kos Island, I tend to think he is probably not eager
to activate any more Greek Islands. Baldur is a very interesting man to study and interview. I would enjoy meeting him and knowing him better.
 
Back to Mt Athos, I visited Mt. Athos in November 2012 with some EU friends that had tried for years to activate Athos. We approached the Greek Government there at Karyes, the capitol of Athos. We met with the Police & Fire Chief’s who after some discussion enthusiastically supported our plans for a Dxpedition and offered to allow us to set up and operate from the Athos Fire Department which is on top of the hill at Karyes.
 
Unfortunately, the lera Epistasia (Church Council) controls the visitor’s bureau and the ferry boat. They will not issue visit permits to allow a Dxpedition, nor would they allow us to bring radios, antennas, generators or fuel. Over the years, the lera Epistasia has been convinced that any non-Monk ham transmissions will QRM the daily prayers of the Monks. This is despite the large cellular tower and public safety towers above the village of Karyes.
 
I’m afraid that unless another Monk gains HF privledges, Monk Apollo will have the only signal from Mt. Athos for the foreseeable future.
 
This situation is very bothersome to my Greek ham friends. They feel powerless to resolve it as it currently stands.
 
Paul N6PSE
