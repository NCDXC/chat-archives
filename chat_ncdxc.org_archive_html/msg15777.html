Well, folks, it may be a very “lightweight” auction this year, because I haven’t received many donations (like, TWO).

Please consider cleaning out some of that old gear, or new stuff you know you will never get around to using, and donating to a great local club. And it doesn’t have to be radio-related; we’ve auctioned off some nice bottles of wine and similar items in the past. 


This year’s festivities will be held on Friday, October 12th, 2018. You will be able to bid on items in person at the auction (http://www.slvarc.org for meeting details, location, time, etc.), OR you can bid online, a la eBay, by sending your top bid to me, the auctioneer. Of course, you’ll need the catalog before you bid, unless you’re really anxious (or crazy).

Please get your photos and descriptions to me as soon as possible, but NOT LATER THAN WEDNESDAY OCTOBER 4th for inclusion in the auction catalog. Earlier is better, as I will be updating the catalog regularly between now and the auction date.

Any questions, please contact me directly at

mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com.
