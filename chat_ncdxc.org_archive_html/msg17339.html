Last night (and a few times during the past week), I have been having decent runs of Eastern Europeans on 20 meter *CW* (the “original” digital mode). The band looks dead, but I call CQ, get spotted by the skimmers, and get a mini-pileup of Russians, Ukrainians, Bulgarians, etc. for as long as I like, starting around 9:30-10:00 pm local time (early morning in EU). Nice to see the band open for something other than FT8. I can even chat for a bit with old friends.

Also worked S9A (Sao Tome & Principe) last night on 80 meters FT8. He was running standard mode on the standard frequency, calling CQ and getting no response. One call did it all, for a new band slot.
