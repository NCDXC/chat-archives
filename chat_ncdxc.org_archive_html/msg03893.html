This “may” be the information you are looking for in LOTW.  As far as DXLab, I have no idea.  You might want to ask this on the Chat reflector. In any event try these steps for your LOTW information:

 

Go to www.arrl.org

Click on LOTW

In the next window, near the top you will see some revolving windows.  In window number 1 click on “Log into your LOTW account”.

The next screen “should be” the log in scree for LOTW. Enter your LOTW ID and LOTW password.

This should now take you into your LOTW account information. Click on the AWARDS tab

On the left side of the page, click on “Select DXCC Award account”

The next page should have a list of bands and your DXCC totals on each band. This will include your total overall for mixed, phone and CW

 

At the bottom of this list/matrix click on “View Award Credit Matrix”  This should take you to a list of all DXCC entities and should you which ones are confirmed on CW, SSB and on which bands.

 

CAUTION!! I just tried it and after I clicked on View Award Credit Matrix, the information never came back.  So, the DXCC server might be choking with all of the data from the CQ WW DX Contest.
