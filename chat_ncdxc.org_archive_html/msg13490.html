Thank you to all who provided input on my mast slippage issue. 

Based on your responses, I have learned that Kurt-K7NV has a special/custom Orion to mast clamp that I was not aware of. I have his M2 Orion modified clamps which are just modified M2 clamps. His custom rotator to mast clamp looks like it is able to achieve and maintain a better grip on the mast. 

I will look into that instead of the Slip Knott.
