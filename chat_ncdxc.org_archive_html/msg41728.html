I'm seeing some email messages from JA "big gun" station owners that are quite frustrated. 

KP5 can do nothing to fix their current solar/battery storage system but what they can do is conserve their battery storage and operate when conditions are most favorable for JA/Asia. 

Every successful DXpedition has a Strategist who pays close attention to the daily stats and makes recommendations for bands/modes based on those status. If I were that person, I would be recommending several ATNO or Asia days to reduce the amount of anxiety. 

It is only fair.
