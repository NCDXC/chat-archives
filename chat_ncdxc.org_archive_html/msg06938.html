On 6/21/2014 12:01 PM, SPWoo via Chat wrote:
When I first put ferrite beads on the problem went away, only to return two days later. If ferrite beads don't work
Ferrite beads are NOT part of a solution for HF, because their resonance is in the 150 MHz region. At HF, a single turn through a ferrite core yields a small value of L with a bit of R. Next to useless. For effective choking at HF, multiple turns must be wound through a suitable ferrite core to move the resonance to the range of the TX, which also multiplies the impedance at resonance. 

Study k9yc.com/RFI-Ham.pdf  This is the solution that Steve used.
