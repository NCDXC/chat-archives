Power has been out here in the Cambrian Park neighborhood of San Jose 3 times today. The 3rd time was at 3:30, and it’s still out. But the generator is running and all is good. 

My weather station recorded gusts up to 40 mph today, which is the highest I’ve seen here. And I suspect that’s actually a little on the low side because the sensor array isn’t quite as high as it should be — it’s barely above the roof. 

As for antennas, the hex beam is still standing… and I’m keeping my fingers crossed because the winds haven’t let up yet. 

Hope everyone stays safe.
