http://spawx.nwra.com/spawx/ssne24.html

This is the trailing effective SunSpot Number chart generated by NorthWest Research Associates.  The SSNe is a semi-real time SSN value that can be plugged into K6TU.NET or VOACAP to generate a “now-cast” indicating what prediction you are likely to see.

The number is generated by a back-solved solution to match the observed MUF values (fof2) to those predicted by the model.  Said another way, what would the SSN value of had to of been for the model to output MUF values that matched what was observed...

The thick black line is the 24 hour smooth value while the thin line is the trailing 6 hours.

Note that on March 18, the 6 hour SSNe dropped to almost ZERO!  A stark demonstration of just how severe the impact of that storm was on the HF bands!

Ouch!
