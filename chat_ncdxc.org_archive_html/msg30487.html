Hello Intrepid-DX Group board. 

 

Here are a few items we need to consider:

 

1) Our annual Youth "Dream Rig" Essay Contest:  It's time to ramp up this year's essay contest. I suggest that we make announcements in October and accept incoming essays through the month of November. From Dec 1-14, we will review the essays and on Dec 15th, we will announce a winner. This is the same as how we did it last year. 

 

2) Your vote is needed-H40WA needs to dip into our reserves: The H40WA team has run into an unplanned, unanticipated problem.  Our Solomon Island shipping agent says that the Government is requiring him to pay a bond in the amount of approximately $11,000 which would be refunded to us when our equipment is exported from the islands. We did not plan for this. We have some income and considerable expenses which leaves us short about $5,000 to provide for this refundable fee. 

 

The Intrepid-DX Group normally has about $10,000 in reserves. This money originates from the 2016 VP8 DXpeditions and was kept as "seed" money for future DXpeditions. We now need to use some of this "seed" money to provide for this customs bond. This money will be refunded and replaced into our account after the DXpedition is completed and our equipment is returned. 

 

Please vote yes or no to approve of accessing our funds for this purpose. Please let me know if you have any questions, 

 

Thank you, 

 

Paul N6PSE
