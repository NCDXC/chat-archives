I’m experimenting with something that seems to be working out – and is quite exciting . . .

 

160M, 40M and 30M DX vertical array – for you low band “mavens” . . . . using a combo of MA160V’s and Array Solutions Stackmatch II and Christman phasing lines . . . 40M is the “gain” band – but the others get some “sympathetic and beneficial” parasitic effect phasing . . . 30M way more than 160M . . .

 

18 dB F/B on 40M with 4 dB gain, and 9 dB F/B on 30M – with (I just don’t know yet  - maybe 1.5 dB gain) on 30M, and just something to barely get me on 160M on 160M (I am sure you can appreciate this . . . . ). Loss on 160M for sure, but enough “cowbell” to get me to DXCC and 9BDXCC because of 160M. . .

 

80M – enough to get me checked into the “Mission Trail Net” – which is my goal on 75 / 80M.

 

Results will be reported on my blog . . . over this summer . . .
