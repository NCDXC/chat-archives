Shift will happen if your tone is not between 1500-2000 Hz.  This range is always used, compensated by shifting the TX freq so the third harmonics of audio are chopped by the transmitter band pass filters.  It’s cleaner. 

Use ‘Fake It’, that’s when this kicks in, now you know why.  Using a low pitch tone and not compensating by shift increases the likelihood that you’re transmitting in two audio spaces.
