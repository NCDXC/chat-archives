I'll respond to the (very good) question of the week in another email, but
more important I think, is the following question:

Should the IDXC return to the Holiday Inn next year? Apparently, no decision
has been made at this point, but the rumor is that because of a substantial
fee that the convention has to pay to the city of Visalia, the SCDXC is
strongly leaning toward moving back. The consensus seems to be that the fee is
and reasonable. Sure, that money would be nice to have in a club's coffers...

IMO, the convention center at the Marriott is FAR, FAR superior to the HI.
That place is stuffed, in more ways than one. I really wasn't aware that it
existed until the last year. How the HI could even be reconsidered is amazing
to me.

Is a fee fair? I imagine it is a pretty expensive facility, and Visalia isn't
really the convention center of California. Jackson Hole really needs such a
facility, and is debating the concept right now.. These facilities are
expensive, but in my view worth it. We have been running in to more events
opting for larger facilities. It seems clear that the convention can afford
it. It certainly made a better event, and the NCDXC is to be commended,
applauded for taking the risk. I believe it paid off.

If the IDXC does go back to the HI, there may be plenty of room at the HI
because I know of a number of long time DXers who simply won't attend. I could
be among them. As an OT with 35 conventions under my belt, I may need more
motivation (or less disincentive) to go again.

So again, MY question of the week is: Should the 2014 IDXC return to the
Holiday Inn?
