Thanks guys!  This is something that only hams truly appreciate.  It required patience and a really good seat cushion.  Living alone much of the time didn't hurt.  Working RSA (ZS) surprised me, so did VP8, so I'm hopeful for the 7P8 team if they can resolve their noise issues for TB.  Bouvet too?

Being in a relatively quiet locale, helps a LOT (the P3 is typically showing -117 dBm noise floor on 160M).  But even here I will need better RX antennas (a pair of RBOG is planned in an X pattern).  There is NO way I could have done this in CA although I do get a lot of polar shielding.  :-(

It was sunny for a day and I was bored (still REALLY COLD outside with a breeze, -12 wind chill), so I created a BOS antenna of spare bits laying about, FAR from being engineered and not attempting to be perfect.  RG6 to a 4:1 common mode choke, 100' of wire unterminated (bidirectional) and the BOS was made!  (Beverage On Snow, about 15-25" off dirt).  While it doesn't play well on TB, it does remarkably well on 40/30M (-134 dBm; the point is lower noise floor AND nulling out points).  After the thaw, I'll make something real, it was a simplistic experiment.

101 countries down, 239 to go!  :-D
