Rob,

Sometimes these are decoding errors by the software. If the callsign appears only once, has a grid square that doesn't match the entity, and lacks the /MM suffix, that's the likely cause.

If the callsign comes up a second time, it's most likely real.

Hey, even Dr. Joe Taylor is allowed to make a mistake once in awhile...
