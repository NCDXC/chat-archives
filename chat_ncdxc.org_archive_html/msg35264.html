Yippee! And woot-woot! I finally got him! It was nice to see so many NCDXC calls in the queue. 
I called at about 230 Hz and got through in 11 cycles. 

In my opinion, he’s clearly MSHV. Having one’s transmit tone “moved down” reflects your own selection of hound mode, and not the DX‘s selection of WSJT vs MSHV. The DX’s selection has no influence on the callers’ transmit tone, only whether he can decode you. With WSJT he will not see you calling unless you’re above 1000; with MSHV he’ll see everyone within his bandpass. 

Here’s hoping for more slots!
