On 3/25/2013 12:24 PM, Paul@n6pse.com wrote:
what do you think is the future of the Bureau system? I don't use it as I prefer LoTW. Will LoTW eventually replace the Bureau system?
I don't think it's an either/or question. Some hams prefer the Bureau system over mailing cards direct. I am definitely in that camp. And, of course, I strongly prefer LOTW and eQSL over paper cards.
I'm a pessimist about LOTW replacing the Bureau system or paper cards, for three reasons. First, some hams (and QSL managers) game the paper card system by demanding much more for postage than it actually costs. Second, too many hams resent the small fee that ARRL charges for using LOTW credits, as well as the hoops they must jump through to get on board. Third, too many awards require paper cards, probably due to the fact that ARRL isn't very adult (or technically sophisticated) about opening access to LOTW for that purpose, and up to now, they've been terribly insular about accepting input from anywhere but HQ. 

73, Jim K9YC.
