I recently had the privilege of being involved with the Dave Kalter Memorial Youth DX Adventure project. 

They recently held a Youth essay contest and they donated a complete HF station to the author of the best essay. 

I had the privilege to read and evaluate the essays and I donated a new HF radio to the cause. 

Here is a short video that details last years Youth DX Adventure. This video will warm your heart and show you that the future of this great hobby is alive with these young people. 

Here is the video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ew3L-LNjPfQ&feature=youtu.be
