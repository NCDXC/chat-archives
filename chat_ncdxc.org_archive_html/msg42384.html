I don’t think it’s oeprator error. Glenn W0GJ is there, and he is quite familiar with SuperFox operation.

On another note: CY0S was quite strong here yesterday on 40 CW, but the op could not get any rate. I suspect he’s new to this, and that’s OK. His protocol seemed to be to listen, and keep listening, until he got a complete call. That would take over a minute, sometimes even more, and usually ended up at the far edges of the pileup (where things thin out). He never came back with a partial, which is probably a good strategy for a newbie, since many folks just keep banging out their call, even if the partial isn’t even close to theirs. I hope he gets better (which he surely will). The 15m op was just cranking ‘em out at speed.
