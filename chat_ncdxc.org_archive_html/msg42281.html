I've been using Holy Cluster to find spots for 3Y0K. The conventional concept is that the user enters his own call and his own grid square, then he gets a great circle map centered on his own QTH. I have found that it works better to enter the 3Y0K grid square: JD15QN. The first time I wanted to enter this, there just happened to be a current spot from some EU station who posted a spot containing his grid and 3Y0K's grid. Very interesting that the continental US covers about 45 degrees. Similar to what we see looking at Madagascar. So listening for "NA" is kind of a misnomer. They need to listen for West Coast NA. Try it; Mikey likes it.
