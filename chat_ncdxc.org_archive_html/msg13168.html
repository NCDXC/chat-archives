I was quite happy with the signal strength of all of them, on a dipole here.  I was able to work TU7C on RTTY (confirmed in Clublog) for a new band mode.  I was not able to get anyone else through the pileups.  I missed out on Nepal openings but it appears it will be a busy weekend.

I am NOT impressed with the general behavior of too many 'hams', from tuning up on frequency (WAY slow in tuning), the usual up cops, comments unbecoming.  Clearly some Elmering is needed; or a Gibbs slap to the head... with a piece of lumber... with nails in it.  I even watched one SoCal Club station, 800 Hz above the DX but so wide it intruded ON the DX (robo-calling too, near non-stop).  Not just key clicks, it was a horrible looking signal, raspy and broad.  (If someone cares, I wrote down the call.)

And if the DX answers with a SPECIFIC call; how is it that 20-30 respond?  Communication requires at least two listeners!

No, this is nothing new; it's just a continuation of appalling behavior; venting over because I know this group groks all of this.
