When he was on 14.074, I was called with a -16 report (he was +5).  He never heard my reply on my same tone OR his tone emulating f/h mode (logged anyway, their software can be flaky).  The software is set to 2 responses, then move on (2 strikes, yerout!)

He moved to .092 and heard me at -13 (he spotted on PSK); never answered (locked out from being logged?  Wait and see time).  Their spot said dipole antenna on 20M.  (REALLY?  Was it updated info or default?)

Overall, a disappointing effort but it proves ONE thing (besides good ops, being EU centric):  TAKE GOOD ANTENNAS not wire VDA, vertical and dipoles.  THEN you can have pileup management by peaking/nulling what you (don't) want to hear...  Going all that way, the expense and time to a location KNOWN to have severe limitations and then using lesser antennas... why?

Not providing any info on band plan, antennas, rigs on the web site... why?

The only beam in their posted pics, 6M.  Not even a simple tribander.

They also favor EU in use of prop and when a band opens to EU; BOOM, they're on it.  I'm not whining, have 2 shiny new slots, but I do pay some attention to how the team plays...

The Italian team in Chad, also known great ops; not acting a lot better but they do hear NA at least, at times.

Loads of hope and promise, will that be dashed?  Details matter.
