The San Lorenzo Valley ARC auction will be held this Friday, October 11th. If you are considering making a pre-bid on any items in the catalog, this is the time to do it. There’s still lots of great stuff to be had at bargain prices.


Here’s the link to the auction catalog:

https://www.dropbox.com/s/ydfesveonxx7gnq/2019%20slvarc%20auction.pdf?dl=0

Let me know if there are any problems with the link or download.

If you want to bid on an item without coming to the meeting, contact me directly at mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com to arrange a pre-specified, sealed (private) maximum bid. Your bid will win the item if the real-time bids do not exceed your pre-bid (a la eBay). You will get the item at a lower price than your pre-bid if the real-time bids do not go that high. The auctioneer will bid on your behalf, up to your specified maximum.

Bid early and often! Feel free to pass this email to anyone who might be interested.
