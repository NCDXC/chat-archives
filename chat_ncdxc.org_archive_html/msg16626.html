Title: T31EU 3.567 MHz FT8 F/H

I didn’t expect to see T31EU on FT8 just now but saw a spot from W6QU on SpotCollector.  I was setting up my radio for 80m CW for the morning to try and get an 80m contact that I need for DXCC.  Strong water fall signal, got in there quickly in the F/H set up mode.

Now I can sleep a bit better not checking my smartphone during the night for an 80m activation.  HI HI
