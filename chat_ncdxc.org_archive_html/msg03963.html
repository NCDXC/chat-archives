Yes, no problem Rich. I got up at 09Z, and there he was. I couldn’t hear him well enough to hear exactly where he was listening besides “UP” Eventually I understood that he was listening up exactly 5. Once I got that, it was a piece of cake. I only had Wake on 20M, but now I am full.

 

After ten-fifteen minutes, he started listening down for JAs, and the was the end of the US for a while. Then he went back to NA for a few minutes and shortly thereafter QSY’d to 80M. I couldn’t figure that out, but when I got up around 1330Z, he was back with a weaker signal flying through the Qs. (Not sure who he was working then, but I hope it was NA.) Glad to hear he settled on switching back and forth between JA and NA.
