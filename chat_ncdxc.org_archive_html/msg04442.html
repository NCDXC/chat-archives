Bob and the NCDXC fellas,

 I'm glad to hear that you had fun with this Dxpedition. The XZ1J team really had a great time with this activation. Myanmar is a very unique and special place. The team got along so well and everyone made a huge effort.

Despite what the impression the team pictures would give, this was really a big effort by our very small team.  Our JA organizers only made a few contacts and three of them went home after the first three days. That left Franz, Peter and myself doing SSB/RTTY and David, Champ and Rafael doing CW.   Both David and Franz had really not done RTTY before and they got a real kick out of doing it while the CW contest roared on all around us.

As with most DXpeditions, we found ourselves calling CQ quite a bit in the last days. I really think that we worked everyone who was workable.

We did RTTY to the very end and we could sense that band conditions were really declining.

We are very satisfied that we were able to make over 53,000 contacts in ten days, with a small but focused team.

All in all, the team had a great adventure!

Paul N6PSE
Seoul, South Korea.
