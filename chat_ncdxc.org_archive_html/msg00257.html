Steve and all,

You are exactly correct that the 1998 rules were intended to simplify the
various processes, including those involved in creating new countrie or
removing old ones. Clear, "bright line" criteria was our goal during
committee discussions. However:

Are you all aware that the current rules are NOT the same that we presented
in 1998? Are you aware of the rules change that was made by the Board in the
summer of 2004?

Also, are you all aware of the addition of a new DXCC rule -- related to new
countries -- in 2006? Are you aware of why and how it was added? Are you
interested in why there is no current interest in making a similar change to
accommodate Kosovo? There just might be more to the story that you know.

I have not been moved enough to publish the "whole story," but...

73, Wayne, N7NG/6
Redding, CA
