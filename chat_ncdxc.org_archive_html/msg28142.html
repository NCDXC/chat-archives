For data, how about a point to point wifi, with a gimbal mount on the boat mast (keep it on track with swells); into the Starlink.  A fixed point at the site to the boat, even if it's a mile out.

Giving the site (and boat) instant upload (Clublog, livestreaming too); rapid contact with the boat and pilots; video chat with home and so much more.  At the least, SHOW in live video why it has been so challenging; to keep the frustration down both here and there.

All without waiting for the sat phone to come into range; near immediate.  And a backup of the log on the boat, for added insurance.

We're geeks, this part shouldn't be hard.  ;-P  The biggest challenge would be the gimbal mount, keep it level and true to the point.
