There are still many independent Radio Shack dealers.  In fact some grabbed the telephone numbers and in a few cases the building leases when RadioShack closed their (Cell Phone) stores.

Sadly almost all of the electronic components are gone, but then they are filled in with Philmore parts from LKG in Chicago in many of those independent RadioShack dealers.
I know of a few that are run by Hams - still.

Still the only drive to source for many...
