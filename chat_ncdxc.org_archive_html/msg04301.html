I would like to participate in the upcoming election. Like Rev. Jim, I cannot make the meeting tomorrow night.

I serve on the Board of my community's mutual water company, and also as vice president. I have done so off and on --mostly on-- since the 1970's; I bought my home in 1971. Our Board meetings  are on Thursdays, and I regularly miss the NCDXC meeting when there is a conflict. This month's Board meeting was last week, but due to our having to address numerous minor problems identified by the Dept. of Health Services, which regulates water code issues, we are meeting again on Thursday evening. There is no conflict, per se---mycommunity comes first--but I would like to be able to consider the DX Club's issues and cast my vote.

FWIW, in my first incarnation as a member of this club, I served as secretary, VP, director and (four years as) DXer editor. My absences from meetings do not reflect a lack of concern for the club and I would appreciate a more considered process. But if the bylaws re succession are being observed, I have no problem with that.

That said, I favor a process which includes more members, not fewer.

Garry, NI6T
