If you have the Powerwerx crimper for Powerpole connectors, you can buy a set of dies for it that ar designed for the SB50 pins. I use apowerpoles everywhere and SB50s not only in the shack but also in my vehicle 12V system, so that's what I bought... and it works great. 

The crimper is $40 and works on 15, 30 and 45A apowerpoles. I'm not sure what I paid for the SB50 dies but I'm sure it was less than $50. 

Here's a link to their crimp tool: https://powerwerx.com/tricrimp-powerpole-connector-crimping-tool?gclid=CJXk_M7-h88CFZJbfgodkLoD4g
