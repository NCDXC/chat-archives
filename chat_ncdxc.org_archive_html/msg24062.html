Paul,

I made the mistake of thinking that S9OK was working fox/hound yesterday because they had two streams going. But the FT8 plan posted on the DXpedition website makes it clear that they will not be doing any FT8, but will be running two or more streams from approximately the middle of the audio passband (about 1200 Hz).
