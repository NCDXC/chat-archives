First, thank you to all who have already paid your dues...your prompt response makes it far easier at this end. For those of you considering it :
1) you CANNOT pay your dues directly from the invoice---  its purpose is to notify you dues are due by June 30 .
2) How to pay your dues appears in the message section at the bottom of your invoice--  to wit: go to the NCDXC website, click the "RENEW" tab at the top of the page, enter your callsign in the box in the appropriate dues category (t.e.  Regular, Associate, Family), and click ADD TO CART which will take you to the Paypal link to pay.
3) It appears you cannot leave comments during Paypal checkout---we are working to fix this. Any changes may be sent directly to me at    treasurer@NCDXC.org        73 JIM
