Hi Robert;

If you have any interest at all in DMR radio, I would suggest the BTECH 6X2.  It is a very nice HT for 144/440 MHz FM, but it also handles DMR quite well.  It isn't as cheap as some of the Chinese radios but it is - in my opinion - excellent quality, and much easier to use.  After doing it a couple times, you can easily program new analog or DMR channels into the radio on the fly from the keyboard.  And of course, the software to fully program the radio is free.  

The radio is actually a rebranded Anytone radio with custom firmware.  The custom firmware is what enables the easy keyboard programming, as well as a few other unique features.
