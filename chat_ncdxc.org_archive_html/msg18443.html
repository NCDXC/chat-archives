I keep hearing that FT8 is necessary for the HOA station, etc.
No, that is what remote stations are for.  I would much rather
be the human operator at a remote station than have a
computer be the operator at my own station.

Or the "remote" station could be your mobile setup in your
vehicle parked in a killer location.  I used to work DX
contests mobile (while driving) using 100W to a screwdriver.
Even easier if you arrange to park in an unobstructed quiet location.
I worked Kure Island on 80 meters operating mobile from my
driveway.

KH6DX/W6 earned DXCC working only from his truck ... on 160 meters,
from California.
