Amigos:
Just spoke with an SCDXC co-chair regarding room reservations at the Marriott. Some of you were surprised when, after confirming a two-night reservation, the Marriott called later in the day to inform you that a minimum 3-night stay was required….forcing you to either cancel your reservation or add another night’s stay. SCDXC says this was an unexpected glitch, and are working on a solution. They promise to update us all via the website soon: http://www.dxconvention.org/ 

For now, just hold on to your Marriott reservation, if you still have one.
