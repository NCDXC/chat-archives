I have a complete Cubex 2-element cubical quad (minus wire conductors) in my garage that I don’t expect to put up again.  Two cast aluminum spiders, 8’ boom with boom-to-mast coupler, 8 white fiberglass poles (never drilled, I always tied my elements on with lacing twine), and DE matching transformer.  Worked great on 10-12-15-17-20 meters.  You need to supply 16 SS hose clamps to mount the poles to the spiders, and the wire elements.  I always used 14 gauge stranded Copperweld.  It survived all the winds here on the Skyline ridge.

 

Both are free to a good home.
