Hi Wayne,

I found GOOGLE to be my friend.  I was missing Europa/Juan De Nova. In 1992,
I worked FR5ZU/E but got busy with other things and failed to send him a
card.  I know, I know ....  While I was recovering from my transplant
operation, I used GOOGLE to try and find where the logs might be.  About two
or three pages in to my search, I saw a possible solution.  My lucky stars
were shining when I discovered a VE2 was listed as a QSL manager for
FR5ZU/E.  Was he alive, did he still have the logs, or what?

Off went the QSL request, along with a short note in French asking if he
still had the logs. Two weeks later, "Voila" FR5ZU/E's QSL card was in my
hands.

So, you never know where the logs and cards might be hiding.
