Once again, now that DXpeditions are in the field, I offer what I use for tracking wanted band mode slots. 

The call is on the left (vertical), the upper left cell is the bearing, a check means worked, a crossed check is confirmed.  A circle is previously worked, unneeded.  

The circled number on the right shows how many worked AND confirmed once they are QRT.
