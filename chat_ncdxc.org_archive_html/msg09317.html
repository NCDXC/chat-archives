A few months ago, I posted a message to these lists asking for info about the whereabouts of Hugh Clark, K6HFA. I was seeking a QSL for an IOTA group--NA-236-- he had activated, along with others, in the late 2000's--long before the IOTA bug bit me. Multiple attempts to reach him at his Fresno address brought no replies, and inquiries to ham neighbors in Fresno like W6DPD and K2VCO only seemed to confirm that he was no longer in Fresno. I even tried some of the locator web sites. He appeared to have dropped out of sight, and perhaps had gone SK, but then I found first one and then a second posting to the DXWorld site by K6HFA himself, so I tried again. In the email, I explained that all I wanted was an IOTA confirmation.

Persistence paid off---the card came today from the Spokane area. This email is primarily to inform Chuck and Vic that Hugh is still QRV and his old email address is still viable. Thanks to all who helped along the way.

Garry, NI6T
