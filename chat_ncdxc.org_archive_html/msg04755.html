Rich, KE1B asked a great question regarding my call for volunteers for the Heard Island "logistics" work in the Richmond Warehouse. He wanted to get an idea of what the tasks will be. Here is a preliminary list:

1) Prepare shipping containers for packing. Some are large military style clamshell containers, and some are Pelican cases that will contain radio equipment
2) Repair any damaged mast systems - these are the military masts - aluminum in 5' sections. Since I repaired these for TX5K - they are most likely still good. But typically, teams throw all of the ropes and guys in one big bunch - so some untangling might be in order. That's just an example of one of many tasks like this. Part of it is assessing the existing equipment so we will have a list of the state of existing equipment vs. what will need to be purchased
3) Test all equipment with at least some antennas in the field. We might even have a weekend camp with enough room to test 4 squares, yagi's, etc with the equipment. 
4) Test DXA and satellite communication and logging
5) Load up trucks just before departure

Plans are progressing, and I will add your email to our logistics crew list if you are interested. I will also invite anyone via this chat list once the dates are set. 

This is a very early request - just to get people thinking ahead. Please also follow along with the planning progress on the Heard Island web site:

http://www.heardisland.org/
