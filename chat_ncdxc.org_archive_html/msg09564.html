Hi Wayne,

My plans are to be in your area early June, just FYI.
I hope I didn't sound like the West Coast was whining, I'm sure we're not alone in need for specific conditions and it varies from one DXpedition to the next.
But with the EU wall, the SA ops trying to get in, the East Coast wall, plus the lidfest and DQRM crowd, we're not the right place this time. Poor conditions only increase the problems.
It just seems that if a DXpedition were to consider two points, that things might be simpler.
1) 20 meters is open all day and night to somewhere. This is practically worldwide. Stay on 20, 24/7. 2) By maximizing all opportunities to the WORST location from the DX (ok most challenging); everybody else wins too. Not on all bands, but make more serious efforts to work those areas, generally by restrictions (EU EU).
I generally don't begrudge EU EU or AS AS windows (unless that's all they do for the entire opening). That is maximizing the opportunity. And exactly why I appreciated that consideration yesterday (W6 W6 W6) and hope it continues (which may be by Paul's efforts). 

73,
Rick
