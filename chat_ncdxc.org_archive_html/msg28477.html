Great!  Sounds like a classic springtime Central Asian opening.  I used to listen to the JA’s on 20M in the late afternoon in February or March, then hear the UA0’s start booming in later.  Then around sundown, if there was going to be an opening, I’d hear UA9’s start showing up from West Siberia.  Then the UL7’s, hopefully followed by the UJ8’s and UI8’s.  Rarely it would continue into the Middle East with 4X4’s or HZ’s.  Later in the evening Central Asia would fade and the UA6’s would appear along with LZ’s and YO’s from deep EU, followed a bit later by a full EU opening to DL/F/G etc.  You could just feel the skip swinging across the whole world!
