BTW, his signal strength at my QTH peaks at 345 degrees AZ.

My antenna is a Force 12 C4.

For the Force 12, the best direction on 17 meters is to the REAR of the antenna.
This phenomenon is very pronounced with this VP8PJ.

This is because the single 40 meter element acts as a director on 17 meters,
according to N6BT, who designed this antenna.

This suggests that for those of you with conventional beams, he will
probably peak around 165 degrees.
