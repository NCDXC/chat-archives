On Mon, May 13, 2024 at 6:56 AM Al N6TA <al.n6ta@gmail.com> wrote:
Treat 7059 the very same way you do for 7074.  No difference at all.
MSHV is the software that the DX station is using and it allows for multi stream work but does not require you to be a Hound as in Fox for the DX and Hound for you.  F/H forces you to be above 1000 Hz and you must watch your TXing since it times out after a few unsuccessful calls.  When working someone that uses MSHV, your software is the same and you do not get timed out other than the usual watchdog timer that you set how you want.
Feel free to ask more!
Al  N6TA
