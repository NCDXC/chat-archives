Bill,

According to Hugh Cassidy, WA6AUD in the West Coast DX Bulletin, The term
"Slim" was used by "an especially imaginative fellow who fired up as 8X8AA
in the spring of 1968 giving his name as Slim and his QTH as Cray Island
"off the coast of "Newfoundland."

Slim has often been used to refer to a less-than-legitimate operator.
