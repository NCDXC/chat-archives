Ted and NCDXC fellas,
 
I posted this topic today as kind of a “shot across the bow” at this fella. We all know him, he subscribes to chat. He is relatively young and I see no signs of early onset dementia. He is an otherwise nice decent guy. I’m baffled by his conduct. After calling out of turn this morning, he then made two deliberate transmissions on top of S79VR. It just seems that the rules do not apply to him.
 
I have received several email messages asking me to name the errant club member and I’m just not ready to do that. I don’t want this issue to be controversial or divisive within the club. I just want the guy to pay attention and stop it.
 
I recognize that we all make mistakes. I have to admit that since I’ve had my K3 I have transmitted more times thinking that split was enabled when it wasn’t. Unfortunately, I will probably make this mistake in the future as will many of us. But if I do, I will feel bad, and correct my operating. And I will own up to it.

What we are talking about here isn’t a simple mistake but a person who makes his own rules and I think what we are doing by letting him know that its not okay is a productive way to start addressing the problem locally.
 
Respectfully,
 
Paul N6PSE
