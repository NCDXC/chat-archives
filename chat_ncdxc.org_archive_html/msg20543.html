One of the better advantages of FT8 is that it can give you the real band conditions quickly, without resorting to a lot of math or predictions based on a standard.

Just look at the calls printed, you KNOW where the band is OPEN, with signal levels.  No transmitting involved; real time knowledge.

I prefer other modes but FT modes are the flavor of the year.
