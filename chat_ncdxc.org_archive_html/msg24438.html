Through a fortuitous bout of insomnia, I had the fortune to work HD8R on 80 CW at 4 AM PST this morning. I needed the slot, and listened 'til bedtime last night in hopes of hearing them, but no joy. Based on DX Summit records, it appears they tried a few sessions of both FT8 and SSB on 80m between 9:30 PST and 10:30 PST last night, and finally stayed on 80m SSB ending with the last spot at 2:34 AM PST this morning. They transitioned to CW at 3AM PST, and remained there until 4:47 PST this morning. Local sunrise was 4:37 AM PST.

I turned on the rig at 4 AM, and they were booming in at 3504 (up 1) with only a handful of takers. My Butternut is 10:1 at that frequency, so with an ERP of about 20 w, they responded on my second call for a new band-slot (insert happy face).

If they follow the same band-plan tomorrow morning, it would appear to be another opportunity for us "little guns". IIRC, they leave on Sunday.

 

GL es 73 de K3NQ
