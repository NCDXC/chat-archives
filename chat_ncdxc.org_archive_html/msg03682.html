Andreas,

I put 'quotes' around some words to allow for different interpretations.
Some folks disapprove of both scenarios.  I don't have a problem with either
since it is simply a personal choice.  How you got together is less
important than getting the call signs and reports exchanged without
assistance.  But, there are (or were) some DXers that pride themselves on
never having used a spot to make a QSO: Only what they found tuning around
made the grade in their definition.  I used to be like that until about 4
years ago and now embrace SpotCollector to show me what is on and what I
'need'.  (As an aside, the very first spot I made was for a 9Q5 on 17 M.
One minute later, an irate WA7 in Idaho called on the land line to chew me
out for 'wreaking' his chance for a QSO.  I was speechless....Have since
lost the land line!).

73,
Al
