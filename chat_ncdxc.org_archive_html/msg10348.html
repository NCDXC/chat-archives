Years ago when RTTY was real and not the current solid state mode referred to as RTTY many of us used dual diversity as much of the converted commercial gear was set up for dual diversity... I used a dipole and a vertical on 40 meters and found it worked very well when band conditions were changing... Not so much help otherwise but it never hurt... I have tried it with my 6700 and find the results about the same... Other than dual ants. having two receivers is great for listening to DX and spotting the wrking freq. 
much better than two VFO's my old IC781 is still great for this.
