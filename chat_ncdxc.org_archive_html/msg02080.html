On 4/29/2013 8:04 AM, Mike Flowers wrote:

I think the club meetings would benefit from more interaction and discussion among the members and guests attending as a regular part of the meeting agenda.  When I first joined the club, I really enjoyed that part of the meeting when everyone was given a chance to introduce themselves and talk a bit about their DXing in the previous month – who they worked, station upgrades, antennas, etc.   This aspect of the meetings seems to have faded into the background with longer programs taking most of the meeting time.   I think the programs are great, but I do miss the round-robin discussion of each person’s DX activities.

 

For me, the community of DXers in the meeting discussing DX is one of the central reasons to be in the club.   Of course, we can always chat with our fellow members before and after meetings, but having the members and guests share their experiences with everyone adds, for me, an extra and enjoyable dimension.   

 

- 73 de Mike, K6MKF, W6NAG, NCDXC, Conway Reef 2012, K3-P3 Addict, Maui
