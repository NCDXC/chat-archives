I heard Thomas last night starting at 0703Z on 7146 MHz SSB — fairly weak signal, about a 4x4 signal here.  He operated for about 35 minutes, working mostly JA, VK, and ZL.   I was hoping he’d give a shout out for NA-only but no go.  He did work a couple of stronger US East Coast stations.  Tried calling him several times, but couldn’t get through the JA wall.  Having operated from Wake Island, I’ve experienced that JA wall….pretty impressive.  Hopefully Thomas will return to Wake again sometime soon.
