There was a DXpedition/Contest operation from Andorra last year. http://www.dxfriends.com/c37n/index.php?i=eng 
 
As Ted points out, Monaco is very expensive and very low on the most wanted list.  Laura-3A2MD is quite active and is often beaming to NA via the Long Path.  I heard a different 3A station on the bands yesterday, I think it was 20 CW.
 
Monaco is not a very interesting place for a large DXpedition because of the high costs and relatively low need for this entity. There are so many more than have little or no activity and are much higher on the global most wanted lists.

Here is your NCDXC Question of the Week:
 
What entity has been most elusive for you?   Which one has been active but you just have not been able to get it in your logs, or you have done so with great devotion and patience?
 
 
For me, a number of them come to mind. SU was very elusive until Vlad-SU9VB went there and I was able to make multiple band contacts with him on CW. Mt. Athos was very elusive for a long time. I must have spent ten years trying to work Monk Apollo and had concluded it may never happen.
