I've always thought that, "All's fair in love and DX." I certainly have used spots, and even entered a DX contest to work the last few DXCCs when I got to 100.
But, if you tell me you've worked DXCC QRP, I'll have more respect. Add in: with portable wire antennas, or SSB, or at the bottom of the sun spot cycle, and you'll really get my attention. Do them all and I'll definitely want to know how.
I would also be impressed with a DXCC worked on slow scan TV or some other little-used mode. And the club recently congratulated someone who worked all CQ zones on 6M. 

As Paul says, it's about having fun.
I recently managed to have fun using my new BigIR antenna to work a few ATNOs, mostly on FT8. We'll see what happens during WPX CW.
