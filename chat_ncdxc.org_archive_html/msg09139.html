Just worked K1N, operating 10 CW.  The op was sharp, working with a very high throughput.  But the K1N website said they would be operating 10M "SSB only,” and this CW op was on at the same time as K1N was working 10 Meter SSB.  Not sure if this one is legit or not.  If it is a pirate, he was an op with serious contest / DXp skills who is working his backside off. 
Seems like excessive effort for a pirate.  Any way to connect with the team on Navassa and see if they decided to work 10 CW - or will we just have to wait to see if any 10 CW activity is posted to the log?

- Peter G
