Making IDXC 2025 better:

1.  Focused effort on recruiting-enticing more Exhibitors.  Target both regular vendors and “never before” vendors.  
Minimum Goal = 35-40.  The almost-empty Exhibit Hall was the biggest complaint from IDXC 2024.

2.  The Friday Night Contest Dinner.  This very popular dinner was sorely missed by IDXC 2024 attendees — many comments about it not being on the schedule.  For many years this dinner was hosted by MLDXCC.  Need new sponsor now: perhaps NCCC, SCCC, or combination of both clubs.  Usual attendance: 100-125.  

3.  Major Major Prizes. Target should be 12-15 for Saturday night and also Sunday morning.  Discourage gift certificates.

4.  Schedule and promote at least one “Young DXer” presentation as a part of the overall program  —  either in-person or via Zoom.
There are several DXpeditions underway that include a “young” operator, typically early 20s.  Maybe include one on the DX Forum panel.

5.  IDXC is NOT the place for a flea market:  Most important reasons:  (1) Competing with Exhibitors time.  In the past, vendors were upset that attendees were spending valuable time in the parking lot instead of being in the Exhibit Hall talking with them; and (2) messes with the program, as attendees are coming and going in the seminar rooms, interrupting presentations — instead of adhering to the published hourly format.

Thanks for the question, Paul.
