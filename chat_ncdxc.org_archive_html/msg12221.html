With just a few weeks use, I’ve decided to sell my SDRPlay. It is fine as a Panadaptor with my K3 for chasing DX and seeing calling in the split (which is what most people want this to be), but it isn’t good for my needs, which is to watch the noise level drop when I tune my 160M RX antennas using the MFJ-1025 and the soon DXE NCC-2.

 

I am simply faster finding splits using my ears and not a Panadapter (I know that heresy to some).

 

For the noise level monitoring (I want to “see” the SNR of my 3 loop phased Top Band RX system) and also watch the noise go away when I rotate all three loops.

 

For this I will need to get a P3 – hopefully with the SVGA adapter.

 

I also have a hupRf buffer amplifier for the K3 IF output and input to the SDRPlay - and would sell it for $25 – I did not even bother trying it with the SDRPlay.
