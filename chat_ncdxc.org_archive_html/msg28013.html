On Fri, Feb 10, 2023 at 4:00 PM Paul N6PSE via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
Guys in the SF Bay Area, you have a great tool available to you to help you increase your ability
to work the rarest of DX. 

What is that tool?

It is the NCDXC repeater: 147.360 +offset and PL of 110.9

Every day, there is a group of us on the repeater, spotting DX for each other, alerting each other if they are in the clear or not or if they are calling in the wrong time synch. We often compare notes
on whether the DX is stronger on the long or short path. 

This past week, we have seen several club members who have called 3Y0J for hours in the wrong time sync. If they were on the repeater, we could alert them and let them know. 

Another great tool is the messenger feature of JTAlert. If someone is using JTAlert, you can text them a message and we often let guys know if they are in need of some help. 

So please consider joining us on W6TI and JTAlert.
