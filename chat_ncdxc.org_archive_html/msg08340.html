I have used both the Astron 30A switcher and Powerwerx 30A.  Both have no RF noise and just run for ever.

 

My favorite is the SS-30DV Powerwerx available off the shelf at HRO.  It has both binding posts and Powerpole connectors.

 

No point of continuing to use the heavy linear transformer based supplies.  These switchers a tough, small, lightweight and stay cool and quiet (acoustic and RF wise).

73,

 

Bob – W6OPO
