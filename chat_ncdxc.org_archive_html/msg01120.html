Dave and Jake, I too am in favor of a Club Station. Many Clubs have stations for their members to use. Locally, N6IJ in Monterey is a fine example. Many of these such as N6IJ (MBARA)are now accessible via remote and offer access to their members. Club stations are very common at Universities such as Stanford and San Jose State Univ. Many amateur radio clubs throughout the USA offer club stations for their members.
 
I have joined the remote network at http://beta.remotehams.com/    I am in the process of setting up an HF remote at my house to join this network. Club members that join remotehams.com will have access to it. If it works well, I will be looking to move it to a better location (not on a hillside) 
 
I hope to have it up and going within a few weeks. Once it is dialed in, I will share more information about it.
