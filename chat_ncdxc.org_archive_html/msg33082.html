Hi guys,

Like many of you, I’ve just submitted my score for the 2023 DX Marathon. Last year was a good DX year for me as I managed to work 277 of the 340 entities on the ARRL’s DXCC List. But I missed working the other 63. That got me to thinking about how long does it take to make it to the Honor Roll and to the Top of the Honor Roll. Obviously I have too much time on my hands because I went back looking through my logs to determine my most recent contacts with the entities I missed working in 2023. What I found is that I could have made the Honor Roll if I had started in April, 2008 (16 years ago) when I worked YK9G in Syria, and I could have made it to the Top of the Honor Roll by starting with a March, 2002 QSO (22 years ago) with XR0X on San Felix Island.

For anyone interested, I’ve listed below the 63 entities I failed to work last year along with the date and callsign of my most recent QSO with each of those entities. I must admit some of these entities surprised me because I had no idea it had been so long since I’d managed to work them.

73,

Rusty, W6OAT
