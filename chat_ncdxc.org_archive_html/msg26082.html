From the Daily DX, rooms at the Hope Hotel are available for registration for the Hamvention weekend of May 18th-20th, 2023. 

K3LR, Tim, says "Attention Contesters and DXers!"  Hamvention 2023
hotel reservations at the Hope Hotel will open next Monday, June 6, at
9 AM EDT.  Tim says, "Do not call them before that time," adding "You
can get room reservations by phone only."  If you try to book online
you'll get a message that no rooms are available during Hamvention
week.  The entire hotel, every room, is reserved for us hams.  The
phone number - reminder, for next Monday at the earliest - is 937-879-
2696.  Ask for a reservation in the Contest University 2023 room
block.  There is no minimum number of nights to commit to and the rate
is $145 per night plus tax.  The hot buffet breakfast each day is
included.  The rate is the same for a single queen-size bed, two queen
beds, or a single king bed with a pullout. 
http://contestsupersuite.com and https://www.hopehotel.com/
