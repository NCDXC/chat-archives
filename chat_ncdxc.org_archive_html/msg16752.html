Thanks for your email. We have an update on the budget situation:

The NIST time and frequency broadcasts are not slated to be discontinued. 
NIST radio stations have full funding for 2019 and are expected to be fully
funded in the future.

planned to observe the 100th anniversary of WWV:

WWV 100th Anniversary Special Event Operation in the Planning Stages
