Jim-
 
Here’s a few-
 
Socialize with a bunch of friendly people who share your enthusiasm for the Magic of DX
 
Enjoy great monthly presentations on DX technologies and techniques
 
Learn about the history of DXing from some of the operators who made it happen.
 
Rub elbows with a number of current well-traveled DXers.
 
Help support DXpeditions around the world
 
Receive spots and other valuable DX information via Chat well before anyone else
 
Draw on a friendly crowd who can help solve your station and/or antenna problems
 
Help put on a great show with the IDXC every other year at Visalia
