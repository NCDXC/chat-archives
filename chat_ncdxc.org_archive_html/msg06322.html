From cross-checking the NCDXC Roster with the IDXC Pre-Registration List, it appears that at least  - 64 - NCDXC members will be attending IDXC this year (see the Pre-Registration list below).  That's a great turnout!

As you know, the Northern California DX Club will have an Exhibit Table next week at the IDXC meeting.   It will be strategically located in the hallway near the Registration Desk, so there should be good visibility and traffic.  There will be a few handouts on display, but the main purpose of the Exhibit Table is to give NCDXC a "social gathering" place at the convention, where we can meet-and-greet attendees, generally promote our Club's activities, and perhaps even recruit a few new members.

If you are among those attending IDXC this year, we need your help.  Please review the schedule below and sign-up for an hour (or more) by typing your Call Sign next to a time slot and sending this email back to me, so I can prepare and distribute a final schedule.  I'd like to have your reply no later than 5:00 PM this Tuesday evening, April 1 ---- no fooling !

This is another great opportunity for you to support the Club.

Many thanks in advance.

73,
