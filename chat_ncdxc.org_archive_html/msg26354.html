Tower Removal Project - Volunteers Requested

 

My friend Keith, NZ5F, has been given a 60’ tilt-over Rohn 25 tower and the yagi on top.   The tower is in the southern part of Campbell, CA.

 

Keith is traveling out from Texas to disassemble the tower and yagi, then take it all back to Texas. 

 

Friday afternoon,  7/29/2022,  Keith and I will be replacing the winch and the cable attached to the tilt-over arm.  

 

On Saturday, 7/30/2022,  Keith will tilt over the tower, remove the yagi and disassemble the tower for removal. 

 

We are asking for volunteers to assist Keith as members of our ground support team.   The ground support team will help with the management of the guy wires during tower tilting and disassembly; keeping their eyes on the process to ensure everyone stays safe during the project; and the many other ‘on-the-ground’ tasks that will be needed to safely complete the removal of this tower and antenna.

 

No climbing will be required of the ground crew volunteers.  

 

It would be helpful to Keith if he had the use of a tower jack for Rohn 25 tower sections.    If you have a tower jack you could lend to the effort, that would be very helpful!

 

Also, a bolt cutter may be needed to remove some cheap bicycle locks securing the guy wire turnbuckles. 

 

If you can help us on Saturday, 7/30/2022, please contact me directly at mike.flowers@gmail.com.  

 

I will send you our preliminary project plan, pictures of the tower, and the work-site location. 

 

Thanks!

 

- 73 and good DX de Mike, K6MKF, NCDXC Life Member
