There seems to be conflicting information as to whether the start date of this operation was Jan 24 or will be Feb 24.

I will warn anyone trying for this:

1.  These operations tend to involve a portable
100 watt station with a whip antenna set up on public land near the water.  At least they have the benefit of salt water.

2.  As such, they are probably day time only operations.  That's just as well because the times I have been able to work them were around 1600Z just before their sunset.  That corresponds to darkness to their east, which enables long path on 20 meters.

3.  I have 4 elements on a 34 foot boom at 106 feet.  This was an extremely difficult QSO to make, in terms of hearing them.  I don't know how well they heard me, but other EU stations noted that I was creating a lot of QRM over there.

I still need 3A on CW; this operation is obviously no help with that.  But I also need them on 40 meters, so maybe I'll get lucky.  But I have a bad feeling that on 40 meters they will only try for NA using FT8, and SSB will be only for easy countries.  At the correct times, 40 meter long path to EU is very easy between well equipped stations.

Good luck to everyone (and it will take luck).

73
Rick N6RK
