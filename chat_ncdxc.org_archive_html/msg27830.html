From the Marama, they have internet access and can upload, post to Facebook, etc. Unfortunately, the logs are on the island, and their satellite phones don’t have digital capability (i.e., file transfer). They need someone to take the logs (on a flash drive, presumably) from the island to the boat for upload. That means getting into a survival suit and being towed in the South Ocean to a buoy where a Zodiac can pick them up. Not something you want to do often (or at all, in my case).
