I have all 9 bands.

SSB on 20,15,10 was the easiest. While working on projects on my workbench, I heard the ops give a frequency they were listening on. One quick call and in the log.

CW took no more than 4 calls on 160,80,40,12. The ops all have nice patterns, so it is just a matter to figure out which way they are tuning. The 160,80,40 Qs were after 0930z.

RTTY has been the challenge. I spent 5 hours chasing them on 30m, then had them QRX for 5, which turned out to be 55 minutes. This was at 3am. When they started back up I was the first one in the log. 17m was just luck. I was Gchatting with a friend and we were both sending our calls when not typing. Took 10 minutes for us to both get in the log.

Steve, K6UM (ORegon)
