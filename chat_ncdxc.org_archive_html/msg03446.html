Team

A group of four DXers including me will be activating NC6DX again for CQP
from Nevada County running four KW stations on multiple bands from a
wilderness area using solar power, batteries and generators. We have taken
CQP seriously for over 15 years (almost as seriously as we take chasing DX
:-) If anyone can let us know approximately what frequencies and times EP3PK
will be operating this weekend we will make it a point that all of our
operators stay clear of EP3PK's frequencies. 

GL all who need EP and thank you Paul and EP3PK for your efforts!!!

73, Ted, K6XN, cranky old fart :-)

PS All of the operators at NC6DX will be tired, cranky old farts with most
of us in our 70s but all of us want you guys and ladies who need it to work
EP for a new one!  :-)

Let me just say one more thing.  This has nothing to do with Contesting Vs
Dxing.  This is about scheduling a rare contact needed by many club members,
and stacking the odds against it by setting it up during the 1 day out of
the year where the bands will literally be stacked  by operators in CW and
SSB.  I applaud paul setting this up and making arrangements for this
potential contact.   But the day could be better, not because there is a
contest every weekend.. that's manageable, but because this is CQP weekend
with the primary bands 15/20/40 stacked, end to end with QRO stations here
in California many close by in the bay area, and its very, very hard to work
the middle east in heavy QRM..
