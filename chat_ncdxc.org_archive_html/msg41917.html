For many years, we have been spotting DX on NCDXC chat (this email reflector) 

As you may have noticed, these postings have dwindled recently. The reason for that is the implementation of NCDXC Slack channels. Slack is a platform that allows real time spotting, alerts and the ability to chat. 

Most of us like this much better than NCDXC chat and we have largely migrated our DX spotting to Slack. 

If you would like to join the NCDXC Slack channels, please contact Lucas W6AER. 

Lucas has also prepared a YouTube video about how to use Slack. 

You can view here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=neCIgRDR7pU



Thank you,
