Paul; I hope you will reconsider your withdrawal from chat. I for one, highly respect your knowledge and opinion on DXpeditions and DX matters in this day and age. I happen to agree with your questions and feel that the response you got from your question to Col about criteria for awards we contribute to was very rude and inappropriate. It smarts of a fifedom attitude and an apparent threat to his ego that you would question his process or thought. The donations given by NCDXC and NCDXF are a trust. We should have at least some input to what we help finance. When I donate to a DXpedition I try to have some knowledge of who the people are and their planning process, with a hopeful view of whether or not I might have chance to work the DXpedition. I prefer knowing that the team has clear intentions of benefiting Amateur Radio in general and especially those with small, limited stations in Zone 3. I will certainly miss seeing your contributions to the discussion on Chat, even though I am following your blog. 73's, Bert, K6CSL
