The EnPhase inverters are German made and so have to pass the tougher EMI specs that are imposed in Europe. I don’t know if that’s why they are as quiet as they are, but I suspect it doesn’t hurt.

ah…while the FCC Part 15 class B isn’t as limiting as the CE mark EMI limits, the truth is, most vendors test to CE mark limits, as products are generally sold in multiple markets and building a product for the US market that is different than the EU market, for this class of product, usually doesn’t make sense.

And, for the ham bands  - 160m thru 10m, the limit is 30 microvolt/m, the same as the CE Make CISPR 32.
