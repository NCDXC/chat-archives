Dear NCDXC friends, 

Next month, one of the most important DXpeditions in a lifetime will take place. It is the 3Y0Z DXpedition to Bouvet. Much like Heard Island or South Sandwich, this is one you surely don't want to miss!

Its time to get your station ready for this exciting activation. 
A check of Stu-K6TU's propagation page shows that 15-40 meters will be the most reliable bands for contacts to the W6 area. 

I urge NCDXC members to share their stations with other hams that don't have a robust station able to work 3Y0Z. My station in San Jose is available to anyone who wants to work 3Y0Z. 

You can see more of Stu's Propagation Information at: http://www.bouvetdx.org/propagation/

Good luck to everyone seeking a contact with this exciting activation!

Paul N6PSE
