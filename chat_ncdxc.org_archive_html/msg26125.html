We received the boxes and supplies and got them back to our QTH. 

Thank you to all the members of NCDXC who invested in so much work!  We will not be delaying any of the boxes.  We are filling the nearly full boxes with cards we have sorted here, labels, envelopes and supplies that the sorters have indicated they need and turning them right around to the post office.  Sorters should have plenty of work in hand by the end of next week.

We are confident that our sorters will all get second and third shipments from these boxes by mid July.
