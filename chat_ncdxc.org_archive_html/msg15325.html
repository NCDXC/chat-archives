Paul,
I installed 1.9.1 several weeks ago.  

The problem is not upgrading during the DXpedition, it is not explicitly stating what the latest version is in the instructions.  A lot of people thought they had the latest when they didn't.

The other important point not covered except in the lengthy operating guide is the importance of letting the software control split.  This is a nicety for everyday operation, but becomes very important for expedition mode.  Everybody not running split is stuck in the pileup below about 2600.  If you set up properly and tx above 3000 you'll get worked right away.  Eventually more people will figure this out and it won't be such an advantage.  

The one disconnect I see in the instructions on the KH1 page is that they tell you to wait for a cq and then double-click on that.  That could be a long wait!  I have never seen them cq.  That's because they are busy picking off callers.  Instead, double-click on any call from them that includes their callsign not in brackets <>.  

GL
Glen W6GJB
