There has been a report in the Daily DX News regarding the NCDXC changing its name to the California DX Club in order to absorb members of the now defunct SCDXC club. I can tell you that at this point no such decision has been discussed or decided on by the Board of Directors of the NCDXC.  We will be discussing this issue at tonight's Board Meeting. In casual discussions we were talking about how we could welcome members that were below the boundary line into NCDXC. At this time, other than becoming an Associate Member there is no option. To accept full members under the current geographic Bylaw restrictions NCDXC would need to amend its Bylaws. That would first require approval by the Board followed by a 20% Member approval. There can be no unilateral decision by the Board on this issue. The same would be true if a name change were to be considered. The Board would need to approve it and then it would need to be approved by at least 20% of the Members for ratification. We have made many friends in the SCDXC through our collaboration with the IDXC over the past years. We are discussing how to bring those members into the NCDXC but no decisions have been made and won't be made without NCDXC Member support.
