After two and a half days of arduous work in extreme heat and humidity, we are now QRV. Our team has had to deal with heat exhaustion and very difficult conditions. In some areas we have had to hack clearings in the jungle to allow for antennas. 

We will be operation on all bands 6-160 meters including 60 meters. We will use Fox/Hound on FT8.

We are running on generators and will have infrequent outages to refill fuel. 

Please contact or Pilots, Lucas W6AER or Tony EA5RM as needed. We will make no log corrections while we are QRV.

Thank you,

H40WA Team
