MSHV works with callers who are running as Hounds; it works with callers who are NOT running Hound. However, WSJT-X in Hound mode will always send the Grid exchange first. Take it out of Hound and send the Report exchange instead (TX2).

I use this mode almost always when calling any station in FT. It reduces the QSO time by one exchange. Instead of (1) I send grid, (2) He sends report, (3) I send report, (4) He sends RR73, (5) I send “73", you get (1) I send report, (2) He sends report, (3) I send RR73, (4) He sends “73”. 15 seconds shaved off.
