He was likely using 3 streams, not f/h mode; MSHV allows that.

I logged him without f/h mode; MSHV doesn’t care where the tone is, just the data.

IF he IS using MSHV, he’s done better with the TX audio than almost anyone else; there was not a large imbalance in strength between the streams (the usual give-away for MSHV ops).

It’s a quirk of fox mode; when one reply/report is sent per stream, the call is truncated (no /kh9).
 
When 2 sets of data are sent per stream, that stream will show the full call.
