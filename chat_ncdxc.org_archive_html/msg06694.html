And a little research gave me the answer to my question.  It seems that any custom extensions that have links or buttons will appear in the Add-On Toolbar, which is located at the bottom of the browser window.  PropFire is showing up in that toolbar.

Here's the documentation on the Add-On Toolbar: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/add-on-bar-quick-access-to-add-ons
