> I apologize for the length of this email, but let me try to explain what happened.....before and at the Club meeting last night.
> 
> On November 11, Ross (K6GFJ) announced that he was resigning his position as President immediately due to health reasons.  At the same time, Bill (N6BM) announced that he was leaving the Bay Area in early December and would resign his position as Secretary as of last night -- November 21.  These two events resulted in a face-to-face Board of Director's meeting at VP Jim's House (K8JRK) on Sunday, November 17.  Attending were:
> 
> VP, Jim, K8JRK
> Treasurer, Bob, W6OPO
> Secretary, Bill, N6BM
> Past President and Director, Russ, K6KLY
> Director, Mike, K6MKF
> Director, Phil, W6PK
> 
> Absent were two Directors, you and me:  Paul (N6PSE) and John (K6MM)
> 
> At the meeting the Board reviewed two Sections of the ByLaws:  5.2 (which applies to Directors) and 5.3 (which applies to Officers).
> 
> 5.2 Director - "A vacancy among any of the four elected non-officer directors shall be filled by a majority vote of the remaining directors..."  To deal with the fact that N6BM is leaving his position as Secretary, Mike (K6MKF) agreed to step into the Secretary position -- creating an open Director position.  George Williams (N6NKT), who ran for office last June, was contacted and agreed to fill the Director position held by Mike (K6MKF).  George was voted in -- subject to approval by the Membership at the next meeting -- as allowed by Section 5.2 of the ByLaws.
> 
> To address the Officer issue, that of the role of President, Section 5.3 applies:
> 
> 5.3 Officer: "A vacancy in any office shall be filled by the board of directors and ratified by the membership at the next regular meeting."
> 
> Read this carefully.  The vacancy (President) shall be filled by the Board of Directors (not an election).  What happened next is this:
> 
> As existing VP, Jim (K8JRK) was automatically considered by the Board as the logical choice to replace Ross as President.  At the meeting Sunday, Jim was reluctant to accept the role without understanding who would take his place as Vice President.  On Monday, Jim called me and we had a discussion about these changes.  I agreed to take his place as VP for the remaining term this year, and Jim agreed to move into the President's role. This plan creates an open Director position (as I moved into the VP role).
> 
> The next step was to present this plan to the Board via email.  As allowed and required by Section 5.3 -- this plan was unanimously approved by the following 6 Board members:
> 
> Treasurer, Bob, W6OPO
> Secretary, Bill, N6BM
> Past President and Director, Russ, K6KLY
> Director, Mike, K6MKF
> Director, Phil, W6PK
> Pending Director George, N6NKT, also voted in favor of this plan.
> 
> Note that Section 5.3 next requires this plan to be "ratified by the membership at the next regular meeting".  That meeting was held last night.  Everything I just wrote above was  presented in detail to the almost 50 members attending -- and fully ratified by them.
> 
> Until next June, Jim will serve as Transition President, I will serve as Transition VP, and Mike will serve as Transition Secretary.  George (N6NKT) is now a confirmed Director.  We also had two volunteers to fill the Open Director position:  Rich (KE1B) and Tony (K6BV).  Filling that position remains our next step.
> 
> This morning, I updated the NCDXC website with these changes:  http://www.ncdxc.org/pages/officers.html
> 
> In summary, to use your own words, the Board is not "Afraid Of" anything.  The Board simply followed Sections 5.2 and 5.3 of the ByLaws to immediately and responsibly address the needs of the Club.  As a result of the discussions that took place on the reflector this week, Craig (AE6RR) -- who attended the meeting last night -- has agreed to form a committee to review and recommend adjustments to the ByLaws that can more adequately address these types of transition changes in the future.
> 
> It is unfortunate that you could not physically be here to participate in these discussions personally, but I hope you will support the final outcome.  You are a passionate and respected DXpedition Leader and have expressed an interest in becoming President of NCDXC.  Hopefully, next June will give you that opportunity.
> 
> 73,
