I caught myself doing this. I think I see how it happens.  That the window to enter the log pops up while your last 73 is going out.  So if I click on another call to answer, it'll abort that 73 and switch over to the new QSO.  For me when I'm at home it's easy enough to just wait for the radio to stop transmitting since there are more visual cues. Where it went bad for me was operating remote, I was clicking away prior to the last transmit finishing.  My software still showed the transmit status, but I just didn't notice.
