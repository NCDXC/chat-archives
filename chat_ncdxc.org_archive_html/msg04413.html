Hi All,

We called off the sked for tonight.  I know a lot of you have been waiting for this.  Pooyan is very disappointed as well.

The good news is that Pooyan has Tue. off from work and he would like to try a sked at 1400z.  That would be 7am for CO and 6am for CA.  For the CA folks the MUF might not rise above 14mhz until closer to 1500z.  But we will wait for your band to open.

I'm open to suggestions on possible times that will work.  A few weeks ago we had great propagation at 0245z.  Perhaps the conditions we saw tonight is just an anomaly.  Some of you have suggested trying bands other than 20m at different times.  Pooyan and I have experimented on other bands in the past however I have never heard him on bands other than 20m.  If there's some other band openings (LP?) to EP that I'm not aware of please write to me.
