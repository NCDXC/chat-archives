Well, I woke up at 1345Z, 5:45AM, this morning and noted that VK9CZ was spotted by one of the team there.  3516.4 kHz.  I went there and heard him about 449 with QSB in and out of my noise.  He was working JAs at a good rate and I could not break thru.  He then tried EU and I do not think he made any QSOs.  Then he asked for NA and I got a QSO at 1359Z.  I heard nobody else calling and he then went away.  My computer was off and by the time I got it up, it was too late to spot real time.  Sri.
I sent the op an email telling him to try again before 1400 for west coast.  I also told him his 40 M CW signal on 13 Nov. was S8 at 1415Z and suggested that is a good time for 40 SSB.  Yeah, I need 40 M SSB, too.

So, we will see...
