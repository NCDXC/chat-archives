I had nearly the exact same experience as Keith.  There were a few times when I could hear them but couldn't break through the pileup with my 100W station and modest antennas.  And there were other times when I would see reports of them being workable on one band or another, but couldn't even hear them.

And, also like Keith, I still have a day job, so that certainly limited the number of opportunities I had to work them.  But all in all, it was fun to give it a go.

Maybe by the time the next truly rare DXpedition comes along, I can improve my antennas and get the Heath HL-2200 that's been sitting unused for decades up and running.  We'll see.
