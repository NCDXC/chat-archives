EP5TJS was on 12m FT8 this morning, coming in with a decent signal on my vertical here in SoCal. I tried to get him, but never got a response. Right after I started calling him, I checked his QRZ page and the HamLog widget there showed I'd just worked him - while he was actually working someone else! I then did a search on his HamLog log, and found three more contacts allegedly with me from 2023-2024. I knew I hadn't worked EP5TSJ back then, so I checked my log and found that I had made three QSOs on those dates and times, but they were with 4S7CQ, J28RC, and 7X2ET. And what do those three stations along with EP5TSJ have in common? EA5GL is the QSL manager. Cosmic coincidence, or a very smelly rat? I know that the 4S7CQ contact was bogus - some Sri Lankan hams verified that no one there has that callsign, and the DXCC desk confirmed that my card is just a pretty piece of paper. EA5GL's excuse - he doesn't verify operations, he just receives the logs and sends out cards.

So, caveat emptor. Please think twice before sending money to EA5GL.
