I agree that the quite excellent TX5K team delivered a LOT of frustration tonight.  I was waiting on 80 while they adamantly held to EU ONLY for 3+ hours at a 1 Q/min rate.  Yes, I tracked that while waiting.  An abysmal rate. 

While I'm sure Europe was thrilled, it seemed like time wasted for the team.   I get that this team was going the extra mile, unlike many in Africa that don't do the same for the west coast (on the rare chance I can even hear them on my N/S dipole i.e. xt2tt, just too weak to try even on the K3).  It was just frustrating. 

At 0640Z TX5K finally opened it up to all and now I have them on the 8th band (80-10 CW and almost all on fone).  14 Q's in the log (and one dupe on 15 CW because the first one was dodgy).  45 minutes later, they're gone again from lack of calls (east coasters are likely abed).   This must be how the Europeans feel about Africa.  ;-)

The TX5K team is outstanding and I'm pleased to get Clipperton after missing the last couple DXpeditions.  I'm trying to sweep 80-10 in all modes, RTTY is the challenge (only have 17M).

I wish I could be on 160 but the 100M dipole is too reactive and low in height.  Maybe next time.
