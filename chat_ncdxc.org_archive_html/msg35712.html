While I empathize with the ARRL situation, they have had no IT leadership for well over a year. The last manager resigned and has not been replaced. No surprise that recovery is taking so long. I managed a lot of business critical IT over my career and would rightly have been fired for a month long outage (heck 24 hours would have done it). Accountability seems to be a rare commodity these days. The lack of assurances regarding LoTW and DXCC data in the most recent press release is disconcerting. I'm no ARRL hater, but not going to be an apologist either for poor leadership and inability to execute.
