On 4/17/2014 4:07 PM, Paul Ewing wrote:
I would like to see other hams call for a “give back” of less than legit club call signs and more concern and policing of hams by hams.
I was surprised to learn that there is, indeed, a shortage of 1x2 and 2x1 calls. That was certainly not the case the last time I looked, about three years ago when I filed the application for KU6W.
There are very good reasons for contest clubs to hold a csllsign. Indeed, CQWPX is built around unique prefixes, and a rare prefix is worth a lot of points. It's sort of like being DX without having to spend $20K and 12 weeks to go to an island. :)
Perhaps you are not aware of the US callsigns held by hams from other countries, especially JA, where I suspect they are a status symbol. I receive a lot of JA cards showing US calls, many of them 1x2s.
