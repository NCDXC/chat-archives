Amazing to see two posts asking for help without ever
explaining WHY anyone should care about this.  The
only reason I can think of is that he has some legacy
tapes to transfer to some supportable media.  In that
case, there should be any number of Sony models that
can play back his tapes, maybe even non-Sony models.
I suspect some of these machines are still around and
can be had very cheaply.

About 10 years ago, I bought a very high end, very clean,
late model Sony VHS VCR for a song so I had a way to
play back some legacy VHS tapes.

Then there is this service:

https://greentreeav.com/eiajreels

which works if your format is "EIAJ".
