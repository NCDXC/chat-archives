rfd 3 :  Rural Free Delivery route no. 3

Ur XPDC sigs : ??

QRA/R: old way of sending RST, used by marine radio/pt to pt (like RCA)  

QRM/QRN: no noise no interference

Xmtr: 47 tube for xtal oscillator, 24 tube for buffer, 246 tube for final running 400 v, (probably 50 to 100 watts)

 

Weird that no time or freq is indicated.

 

Hope that helps.
