QST,QST, QST: A reminder that nominations are open in each of three categories for our annual awards. We want your candidate(s) for: DXer of the Year, Member of the Year and the W6OL CW Award. Please note that for DXer of the Year, candidates need not have participated in a DXpedition, but exemplify the best in DXing skills courtesy and dedication. Member of the Year should have also demonstrated the finest qualities that represent NCDXC. The (Dr. William Howard Myers)W6OL Award is commemorative and honors those who demonstrate consistent excellence in CW Skills on a DXpedition. Entrants must be a member in good standing except for the W6OL category. See any Board of Directors member with your selections or reply to this email. Deadline for entries is February 28th. http://www.ncdxc.org/pages/idxc.html
