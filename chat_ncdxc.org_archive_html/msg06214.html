Aaron,

I don't think there is a single answer. As with most choices, it depends upon what you use it for, how it works, its physical size and weight, how much it costs and perhaps other factors of interest only to you,

When my MFJ took its final dive, I had the same choice to make. I chose a RigExpert AA-54, which I purchased at Dayton from WX0B. It covers through 6m, has an LCD display of text and graphics, , does sweeps, provides very nice graphs when hooked to a computer, has decent battery life with two AA alkalines or NiCads, is not as physically awkward as the MFJ and is fairly straightforward to use. It is not a VNA. RigEXpert offers other models that go into UHF.

I like it--that is the final parameter.

Garry
