I worked them at 1331Z.

My antenna is an inverted V with apex at 50' and ends at 6'.

Using a K3 and the KPA500.

I've identified two cw operators. Both are swishers. Both start tuning up the band; at the top (+15 kHz) one jumps to the bottom, while the other reverses direction.

Using the LP-PAN and NaP3, it was easy to identify the callers. I just waited at the bottom of the band and got into the log when he moved from the top of the pile to a clear frequency at the bottom. It has been fascinating looking at the pileups with the LP-PAN.

Steve, K6UM (in OR, not the Bay Area)
