Some folks are proud of their amateur radio accomplishments. Sometimes, you don't appreciate the accomplishment until you have achieved it yourself. 

When I visit someone's shack and I see a #1 Honor Roll plaque, I am very impressed as I know how difficult it is to achieve that. It takes a focused, sustained effort over many years. 

When I attend a DX banquet and they do the last man standing, I am always so impressed to see the accomplishments of W6OAT, W3UR, N6QQ and K6NA. They started early and they have accomplished a great deal.  I respect that. 

I have no problem with the ARRL creating new awards. If you don't want it, you don't need to do anything. 

Paul N6PSE
