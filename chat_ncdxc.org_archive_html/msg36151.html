If you do not have the pre-LoTW contacts in a digital log, they go nowhere (duh!).  If you can download the .adi file from LoTW, it means you must have previously uploaded your log so it could be matched with confirmations sent by other logs and grant confirmations.  You   probably synced your confirmations from LoTW to your log already, or should.

I have my whole log in DXKeeper and it shows the status of each pre-LoTW contact in terms of its being confirmed and/or verified.  DXKeeper will let me export the log or any portion of it I choose for upload to ClubLog.

Doing so tells ClubLog the confirmation status of every contact.  Then, ClubLog can do its magic in terms of knowing all about your activity and create neat reports for you.  Highly recommended.  As is DXKeeper.
