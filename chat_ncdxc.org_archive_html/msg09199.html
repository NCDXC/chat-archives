I worked K1N yesterday on 10 CW, almost certain it was a pirate given their website info and the fact that they were operating 10 SSB at the same time.  But when I checked the OQRS log last night, I found my 10 CW QSO in the log.  
I also noted that they revised their website this morning, and the statement that they would be operating only 10 SSB had been dropped.  The K1N op on 10 M CW is legit.  Good luck & 73!

We'll continue to work on this but please understand the difficulty in getting enough reliable bandwidth to accurately move data off a desert island smoothly.
