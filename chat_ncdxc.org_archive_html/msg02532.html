I have the full K-Line when I need it, but my new KX-3 seems to have stolen the show. I have it hooked up to a decent antenna system – phased 40M verticals, and am using it in the AA contest – 10 watts and watching the HDSDR screen mesmerized.

 

The audio on the KX3 is probably what is blowing me away the most – even the little speaker is amazing – but with the Heil Proset 3 phones – WOW. And the feel of this little tank of a key – the Begali Traveller Light is something else. 5 watts will be as fun as 500 watts now!

 

It makes me just want to send CW – and not care what “entity” I’m chasing – which has been my life for the last 11 years – hi hi.
