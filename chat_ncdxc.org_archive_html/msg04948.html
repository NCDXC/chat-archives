Quick search of the internet, my first entry gave me what is posted below.  Wrapping your coax connectors with good solution is much better idea than to use NoAlox that improves electrical conductivity (between core and shield).

Antioxidant Compound
Noalox is antioxidant compound that penetrates aluminum oxide. It guards against oxidation.  Noalox improves electrical conductivity and produces a cooler connection.  It is excellent for wire connections and aluminum antenna parts.  This is what you should use when assembling an antenna that has parts that will oxidize.  This is great stuff.
