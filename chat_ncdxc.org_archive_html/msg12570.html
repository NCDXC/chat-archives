Remember last year’s “Centennial Challenge”? You got points for working the W1AW/portable stations, for working ARRL officers, etc. Some folks worked it seriously, chasing down Section Managers and other folks.

It turns out that you don’t even have to *apply* to earn an award. Obviously, they have all of the logs of the various W1AW/portable operations, along with those of the participating officers. You don’t have to submit YOUR log; they can just combine all of THEIR logs and add up your points.

Notwithstanding the fact that it took them NINE MONTHS to add up some numbers, it turns out that I won FIRST PLACE for both Anguilla (VP2EAQ) and Antigua (V25M) without even trying (or even thinking about the competition). I simply made a lot of Qs and some of them were ARRL officers and such. I received the certificates yesterday and laughed.
