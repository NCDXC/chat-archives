Some Observations:

Total Qs: 42,922
# of Unique Callsigns = 15,487 or 36%. Excellent. Among the highest of all
DXpeditions this year based on % of Uniques.

https://secure.clublog.org/uniques.php
C21HA = 39.1%

-- 33% of all QSOs were on 20m = the money Band
-- 60% of all QSOs were on CW; 36% on Phone and 3% on RTTY
