Hi NCDXF fellas, I can attest to Kurt’s knowledge and excellent set of skills. He and Glenn-K6NA recently raised my 72’ tower and installed a very heavy SteppIR 3B36 at the top. This was a very difficult installation in a confined space. Kurt and Glenn were prompt, professional and a pleasure to have on this project. We used a big heavy crane on this project. There simply was no other way. I figure that because of Kurt’s knowledge and experience, he saved me money by having the crane here for only one day instead of multiple days if this had been solely an amateur project.
 
Last month, I had Kurt come back to swap out my Yaesu rotor with a larger M2 Orion 2800 rotor. He has special tools to jack up the mast and swap rotors while still on the tower.
 
I’m really glad that we have guys like Kurt and Glenn available to us. Frankly I don’t know how I would have completed the job without them. These guys are Professionals and know how to tackle the really tough challenges and bring unique value to their clients.
 
Thanks guys!
