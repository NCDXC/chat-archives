A few of you have asked about the results of the IDXC 2019 logo contest.  Unfortunately, we only had 2 entries, even after extending the deadline two weeks.  Neither was suitable for creating a high-quality patch that could be successfully embroidered on a polo shirt.

I was able to find some time using familiar graphic design software to create something that the planning team approved.  We’ve received the patches from our vendor and they look very good — should show up well on a green, gray, or blue polo shirt.

http://73dx.com/idxc2019/logo4.html

We’ll be opening up IDXC registration on Monday, December 17th so you can order your very own shirt.  : >)
