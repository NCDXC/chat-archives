Hi All,

Just to see how much it would cost to ship an amp. to Libya I went to USPS.com and did a price quote.  I used the dimensions and weight of an Ameritron 811HD plus some allowances for packaging materials.  Due to the large package size the only service level available was the Global Express Guarantee at $920.  In other words USPS wouldn't allow you to ship a large package via a cheaper and slower service level like First Class or Global Priority Mail.  Then I went to UPS.com and requested a price quote.  After entering all the relevant data it said I needed to contact my local UPS office for a quote.  In other words if I had to ask 'how much' chances are I can't afford it, hi.  As I indicated in an earlier email the cost to ship an antenna analyzer is between $22 and $33 depending on the service level.  It seems unlikely we could collect $2,000 in donations for an amp. or find a donated amp. and still collect $1,000 in donations to cover the shipping cost.  To me the answer is obvious; the antenna analyzer has the most bang-for-the-buck.

One generous NCDXC member donated $100 for the cause.  A RigExpert AA-54 with shipping to Libya would cost about $370.  I'm personally contributing $100 and another generous Colorado ham is also contributing $100.  I still need $70 to complete the gift.  If you are interested in donating please send me a PM.  Once I have $370 in total donations I will send an update to Chat.  I will personally send the analyzer to Abubaker however I will also send him a note listing all the generous donors for the gift.  I suggest Glenn to be the point of contact for all sked requests as he was already working with Abubaker on the best times and bands for the West Coast.  Thanks to all who responded positively to the great cause.
