SPE 1.5K FA with tuner < 1 year old connected to ICOM 7610 with Dan Tassell cables. 2 Antennas 30/17/24 rotating dipole and 10/15/20 Yagi - all SWR 1.5-2.5 range for FT8 freq. expect 30m which is bit high at 2.5-3. SPE when first setup tunes fine across all ranges. 
 
1. Early symptoms (1 +month ago)
 Occasional "High SWR Warning" during TX. After checking SWR with RigExpert and confirming that its is well below 1:3. I would "TUNE"  and it would work fine. Multiple bands. Always on LOW power.  In all cases the SPE would have been working fine (FT8)  on the band/power level for some period of time. 
 
2. Last week
 After High SWR Warning during TX. After checking SWR and re-"TUNE". I noticed that there is NO POWER out. But there is no other error either. 
 
Turning off / on did not help. But letting the system cool and re-tried. POWER returns and it seem to work just fine
 
3 This week 3/9
 After High SWR warning. Again, rechecking the SWR and "TUNE"d. There was NO POWER out on TX. 
 
Tried cool down (10+ min) and turn on again. This time SPE turns on and shuts down right away.  Wait another hour then It turns on just fine, but no power out on TX :) 
 
Thoughts, any other things I can check / debug. Seems like it needs to go repair - bummer.
