QST de K6MKF

DX Bulletin 01

>From Lupton Downs Amateur Radio Club (LDARC) Headquarters

San Jose, CA  - October 18, 2013

To all radio amateurs

 

HAWAII, KH6. Mike, K6MKF is QRV as KH6/W6NAG from 20 OCT 2013 until 30 OCT 2013. Grid square BL10SR and IOTA OC-019.  Activity is holiday style on the HF bands using CW, SSB and Corona.  Increased Corona effects may lead to garbled SSB and QLF CW.  QSL this operation to W6NAG home call.

 

- 73 de Mike, K6MKF, W6NAG, 3D2C, NCDXC, IDXG, RRC #933, K3-P3-KPA500-KAT500 Addict, Maui
