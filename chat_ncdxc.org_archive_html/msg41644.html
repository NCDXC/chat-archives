I regret buying a 6 element KLM 15 M single band long boom Yagi.  Why regrets?  We (N6KT, N6VQ and I) installed it on a 105' crank-up tower that was only guyed to the Saratoga rental home chimney.  The clothesline wire used to attach it to the chimney broke and the tower and beam became sort of useless.  At least the house was undamaged (we think).  I regret spending the money without having a solid plan to use it.
Al  N6TA
