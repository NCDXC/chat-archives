Flex Ltd., the Singaporean–American multinational electronics manufacturer, does not make FlexRadios. FlexRadio Systems is a separate, privately held company based in Austin, Texas, specializing in the design and manufacture of Software Defined Radios (SDRs) marketed as FlexRadios for amateur radio, government, and commercial use.

FlexRadio Systems and Flex Ltd. are independent entities with different products and markets. FlexRadios are produced and sold exclusively by FlexRadio Systems.
