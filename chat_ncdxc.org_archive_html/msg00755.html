 Jim, as Wayne-N7NG has pointed out, I was mistaken about PT0S using the Navy for transportation. They used a fishing vessel which added to their costs.
 
Again, the topic of this discussion is DXpedition costs and how can the challenge of raising enough money to carry out DXpedition to the rarest of entities such as Bouvet or Crozet be met in this current climate?
 
I have not intentionally downplayed the efforts of George and the PT0S team, however I think you will agree that four guys on a Brazilian rock is not of the same magnitude or complexity as a DXpedition to Bouvet or Crozet would be.  George and Harri have certainly established that they specialize in low-band DXpeditions and have done well in that regard.
 
The DX Community expects more out of most DXpeditions and they are often judged on how many total contacts and how many unique operators were satisfied with a QSO.

On 12/18/2012 9:32 AM, paul@n6pse.com wrote:
Jim-K9YC likes the DXpeditions done by George-AA7JV. George just finished activating PT0S with a group of three other guys. Transportation was provided by the Brazilian Navy and I tend to think their costs were minimal. Not much of a chance that our US Navy or the French Navy will take us where we want to go.  

I don't understand the desire to downplay the efforts of this team. George's other trips have been on a small sailing vessel, and, I think, he sails it himself. It's just the two of them, so he doesn't have to pay a crew. George's costs are minimal for several reasons. First, there are only a few operators (for most of his trips, only two). Second, he's quite smart about systems engineering, as noted. Third, he and his expeditioners travel pretty close to the bone..Fourth, they figure out places to go that he can access for minimal cost, and with a small scale trip.

Also, let's look at results. PT0S's objective was 160 and 80M, and they made 3,027 QSOs on 160M, 4473 on 80M, 13,910 uniques. I studied statistics of a bunch of recent trips, and the only trip that made more 160M QSOs was HK0NA, with a cast of thousands; HK0NA,VP8ORK, and 3B7C made more 80M QSOs. PT0S beat 5T0SP, YI9PSE, 7O6T, NH8S, 3D2C, and 3D2R on these bands.

This "bang-for-the'buck" is not at all unusual for AA7JV and HA7RY. They made 3,425 160M QSOs.at TX3A, 2,000 at VK9GMW, 1,232 at VK9WWI  And they did it with TWO GUYS ON A BOAT that they sailed themselves.
