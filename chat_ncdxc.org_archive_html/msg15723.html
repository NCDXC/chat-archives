I got back from SFO at about 1 AM this morning and tuned 75 and 40 for TX5T on phone.  They were on 40 CW but on for 2 minutes and off for a couple minutes.  This went on for half an hour.  I needed the SSB QSO and sent SSB? A few times when it seemed nobody was calling.

At 0846, the op popped up on 7122 kHz.  I called him 5 up and got his first and my first QSO.  He was fumbling around a bit mentioning an intermod problem with another station but finally got going a bit.  He worked a AZ station, a VK and a JA.  The signal was S9 on the meter, very strong.

Who knows about other nights, but the conditions were excellent.
