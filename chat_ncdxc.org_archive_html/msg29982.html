I used the RM series single band resonators way back in the mid 70s in my VW bug.  Worked OK but I was wanting local comms, not DX.  Never did any work above 20 NM.
Nothing you can do about noise you drive by other than use the noise blanker on your rig.  A cheap rig will have a poor noise blanker.  Noise comes with the territory, as they say.  You may encounter noise generated by the tires on the road.  Some tires cause it and others do not.  Dragging a braid under the chassis might help but I never tired it.  I just lived with it.
My rig was a Kenwood TS520, a mostly solid state transceiver with 6146 finals.  I put it into the back seat, usually, and set the knobs and went off from there.  I also used it sitting on the front seat, too.  Never mounted under the dash or trunk, like you can do these days.
Check reviews of antennas on eHam.net or other places and get some ideas.  There is likely a mobile group on Groups.io.  Have a look and see.
GL es 73,
