I don't think I've ever had a bad antenna.  Some were better than others.

I WAS and DXCC with my Cushcraft R7 from a city lot and 100 watts CW and Phone.  I'm sure I could have done both achievements sooner with a beam, but I worked with what I had.

I still like my Buddipole a lot.  I have used that antenna many times doing portable work.  I've used it several times at home setting it up on the roof of my house with guys and a counterpoise wire for 80 meters CW and SSB when contesting.

My most reliable antenna is my Alpha Delta 80,/40m dipole stretching from the Ash tree in the front to the fence in the back up over the house.
