On Dec 13, 2021, at 9:19 AM, Paul N6PSE via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

NCDXC friends, 

This was posted in W3UR's Daily DX (the best source of DX info)

SV2/A - Mount Athos
SV2RSG/A, Monk Iakovos, was on the air November 30 from 1345 to 24Z
December 3, not continuously of course, but that was the period of
time he was sometimes on the radio.  This week he is planning to do
FT8 and CW starting December 14 at 1500Z, open-ended at this point. 
QSL via SV1RP.  The Hellenic Radio Amateur Team says a pirate station
has been on the air too, causing Iakovos to announce his modes and
bands ahead of time and then post the log to Club Log every two hours. 
Operations December 4-9 were the phony station.


Good luck in working the real SV2RSG/A
