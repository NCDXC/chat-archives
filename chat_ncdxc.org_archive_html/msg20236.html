Fir those that are not using digital modes, the DX is less exotic but 20 M has provided EU and AS pretty regularly.  I have been working with 10 W most of the time and getting my ‘fix’.  S50 was o this AM at S9 on my S-meter.  Around 0400, we hear UAs if we are not beaming to VK/ZL or the frequent FK8.

And, if you want to work C9 on 40 SSB, Bill, ZS6CCY is currently stranded in C9 and has a 2nd home and station as C(1CCY, always readable and usually workable.  Look for him about 7145 to 7152 kHz around 7 AM when he makes a near daily appearance with NR6Q or AF6TC.
