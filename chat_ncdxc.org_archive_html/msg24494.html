On Nov 15, 2021, at 9:31 AM, Paul Booth via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

I probably wrote about this before, and pretty sure Paul, N6PSE has too, but something has struck me recently on FT8.  Much of the time, not all, maybe not most, the DX calls within the first 1000Hz.  A number of times just in the last week, I've had my QSO interfered with by someone local, meaning most likely within the USA, call on top of the DX frequency such that I never received his 73 or he got completely lost, especially if weak signal to begin with.  Now I'm sure the USA station did not intentionally QRM the DX here but a lot of this could be avoided if a convention were adopted that we all call the DX above 1000Hz regardless of what we can or can't see under 1000Hz.  Does something like this exist already?  Does this make sense or am I missing something?
