The Palo Alto Amateur Radio Association (PAARA) runs one of the best Field Day events in the State, if not the Country.

http://paara.org/pages/fieldday.html

They operate from beautiful Bedwell Bayfront Park, just off Hwy 101 in Menlo Park.

http://www.menlopark.org/Facilities/Facility/Details/Bedwell-Bayfront-Park-6

As a Class 4A entry again this year, they could use a few more operators.  If you would like to sign up for an hour or two this Saturday or Sunday --- CW or SSB -- please contact Rebar (N6DB) at:

Email:  chezrebar@gmail.com

and he'll provide you with further information.
