> On Wed, Mar 18, 2020 at 3:39 PM Paul N6PSE via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
>>
>> I see a lot of guys calling the 5Z4 on his frequency. He is at 14.074 and at 2202 Hz.
>>
>> If you are also calling him at 2202 Hz or thereabout, you are competing with all the other guys calling in the same place at the same time. You are essentially cancelling each other out.
>>
>> Here is how to go split.
>>
>> When you click on a station calling CQ, WSJT-X will put you on his frequency Simplex. I feel it is okay to make one call simplex, then go Split for subsequent calls. By doing so, you increase your odds of working the DX quickly and easily.
>>
>> To go split: Simply hold down your shift key and with your mouse, move your red cursor into a clear spot on the WSJT-X waterfall. Then let go.
>>
>> As you continue to call, try to keep your red cursor in a clear spot as much as you can.
>>
>> Good luck!
