I'm inclined to agree; 6o3t.


They had a HIGH focus on EU because of their ridiculous noise floor (and our path is over EU to get there, two struggles at once); but I did eke out a few contacts for the last AF entity on my list (ATNO, one of a few last year).  One spotlighting event helped, radio magic; suh-weet!  Sadly limited to NA on more than FT8; their noise floor was just stupid high there for much else (poor planning?).


Other teams were more productive and fun (fw5k, I got every slot that they offered NA, a qualifier, because I could) but closing out a continent ranks pretty high on my list.


xf4b was another ATNO (worked him a few years ago but crickets for QSL); who (being gentle) is near the bottom of my 'like' list for lack of variety (two phone bands, the third being weaker than QRP so unusable, no other options).  He did make the EU folks whine and cry (for amusement value).
