Team

 

I love chasing DX, I love the NC6DXC and I very much appreciate the effort all of you are putting into trying to improve the NCDXC, our policies, our procedures, recognition of the deserving and all of it.

 

We are brothers and sisters sharing in a wonderful hobby and bless you all. Feel the love?

 

I propose we try to be a little more loving and respectful and appreciative of each other…especially when we may disagree…because we are all a bunch of pretty accomplished nice people sharing a common interest and love and we probably enrich each others’ experience in our beloved hobby. I say…more love, more understanding and more enjoyment. Life is too short..especially for some of we old farts. We know who we are.

 

So there…take that!

 

73, Ted, K6XN, cranky old fart
