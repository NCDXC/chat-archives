RIck,

It is really no different than a QSO in any other mode.  

There is an exchange sequence, not unlike a sequence for any other mode.  

CQ w/grid square, grid square, signal report, roger +, signal report, roger roger.     

If the stations do not do the sequence together and do not get to the end the software will not offer to log.  The honest operator wouldn't push the log it anyway button.

And then it all goes into LOTW (or wherever) for cross-checking.
