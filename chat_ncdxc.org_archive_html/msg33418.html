I am trying to collect confirmations . Here are my steps - am I missing anything or is there  a simpler path ? 

1. Upload my log to LOTW , CLUBLOG 
2. Wait a month (not for expeditions) to see what LOTW comes automatically. Sync Clublog with LOTW (manual)
3. Walk thru CLUBLOG and "Request QSL" . Do paypal etc as required
4. If there  is a pointer to another QSL handler then got hru that process (hopeful for LOTW confirmation)
4. Wait a bit more
5. Make a list of QSO with no confirmation, check QRZ confirmations and check QRZ to see which ones are "Bureau" and create QSL cards and mail them off to the right Bureau's, QSL Handler
6. Make a list of ""Direct ONLY"  that have QRZ confirmation and mail them
7. Send email for still missing ones and (maybe ) send direct. 
8. Collect QSLs for missing one to send for verification!
