Well, it took 30+ years to happen, but a real bad forest fire forced us to flee last night at 2230 local.  Very bad smoke, red sky, and orange lights on the ridge top we look out onto.  Loading up cars with valuables is no fun!  We went back this morning and the CHP let us in which meant there was no immediate danger but we left after 3 hours of added sorting and moving stuff from the house to a less dangerous part of the property.  We sure hope to look back and say how lucky we were but that is a story not yet written.  Staying in a nice hotel in Los Gatos.
K9YC has been ordered to evacuate.  Same with K6XX and likely W6GJB, guys to my west and in big danger.  Keep us all in your thoughts as we contemplate how to return to normal.
