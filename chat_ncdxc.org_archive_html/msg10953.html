I almost never ‘cut class’ to work DX but did today.  Got home at 1 PM in time to work the 9X0 and VK9WA on 15 and 10 CW, the slots I feared missing.  Had time before the sun was down to get the dipole down, retuned and back up for 80 M SSB tomorrow morning, if the Gods will cooperate!

This has been a good DXPedition from my somewhat limited perspective.  A lot of stations, on a lot, and listening for appropriate areas where then should have been listening.  I was able to make QSOs when I had time to try.
