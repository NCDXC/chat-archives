For anyone looking for Roly, A35RR, here’s a portion of an e-mail I received from Roly this morning (Aug 9, 2018):

 

“Good copy Ross,

Sorry you had to wait two cycles !!

Had to sign with yet another JA first.

I have resisted going to DX-pedition mode but the hordes are so thick that I may just have to relent ☺

I only have 50 watts at max so going DX-pedition mode will half that effective power if I have 2 QSOs going at once and quarter that power if I up it to 4  QSOs at once.  Even with the 50watts I get a fair number of repeats so effectively lowering the power will only increase the repeat rate unfortunately.

Still we plod along…………..

I will upload to LoTW for you when I get home to some decent internet access !!

Cheers

73

Roly – ZL1BQD (A35RR)”

 

Roly uses both eQSL and LoTW for confirmations.

 

73,

Ross
