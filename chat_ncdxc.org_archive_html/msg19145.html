I too thought that VP8PJ was a superb DXpedition as was their previous VP6D Ducie activation. This team really knows how to get the job done!

I really liked how they just worked the band until it would just give out and then they would go to the low bands. There was very little (if any) preference given to any one area. 
They just worked who they could work when they could work them. I really think that is the best way. 

I do think they should have done some non Fox/Hound for Asia as I suspect the F/H multispream signals were not making it that far. I had hoped to get them on 12/10 meters but the propagation
just wasn't there. Even my 15 meter contact was marginal. 

The lack of an online log didn't bother me. 

I sure had a lot of fun chasing this team and I really look forward to their future operations. 

73, 

Paul N6PSE
