Greetings all--

Was wondering if I could bug you for some advice.  So..  this year, after being mostly QRT after an aggressive march up the dxcc ladder in 2010, I have been somewhat active again.  I have recently crossed the threshold of 1000 challenge confirmations, and completed 17 meter confirmations to make that the 5th technical dxcc band confirm wise, (first warc but 12 is coming soon and 30 underway).

I need to pull 23 more cards, many in hand already to apply for 5BDXCC. All the status's listed under the club for me are LOTW only, as my hard card collection went into deep storage after I went through some family changes.. but I have bunches..

So..  for those who did one big submission, did you

submit for single bands?

For 8BDXCC + Challenge - I had 500 cards for my first QSL check. The QSL manager was not at all happy. So I recommend submitting once for each band. I prefer a local QSL card checker over sending to the ARRL for such a large amount of cards. 

wait for completion of all qsl's?

Wait until you have all for a band. I would not ask a QSL manager to check more than an entire band (100) at a time - just so I remain friends with the checker - hi hi
did you submit for the challenge award?

Yes - for 1000 and 1500 and am at 1900 now - so 2000 maybe next year . . . 

did you order a plaque for 5bdxcc - Dxcc challenge?

Yes, plus the endorsements for 8BDXCC

I never really submitted my credits after the initial dxcc so I have many many to submit..  i

How much did the whole thing cost?  

A lot. $2 per card - just for band slots and 8BDXCC - and ATNO's (towards Honor Roll) I made big donations. Never kept track - just closed my eyes and paid "da man" . . . 


Just looking for some advise about what most folks do.. what seems reasonable..

Use LOTW as much as possible - as you have. Many more use it now than when I started DX-ing in 2001 - hi hi. 

any advise would be appreciated.
