During the Saturday night banquet at IDXC 2013 in Visalia, CA, the following awards were presented.

1. PTØS DXpedition of the Year Award to Fred Carvalho (PY2XB) -- as chosen by NCDXC members (from the DXpedition Survey earlier this year).

2. Member of the Year:  Bob Lanning (W6OPO)

3. Member of the Year:  Phil Verinksky (W6PK)

4. DXer of the Year:  Mike Flowers (K6MKF)

In addition, 22 Major Prizes were awarded during the convention.

See the NCDXC website for more details:    http://ncdxc.org/

Congratulations to Fred Carvalho (PY2XB) and the entire PTØS DXpedition team as well as W6OPO, W6PK, and K6MKF for some very well-deserved awards !
