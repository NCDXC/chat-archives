I worked ARRL DX CW QRP last weekend, and learned some very interesting things about 40M propagation. First, there's a very nice peak around sunrise in EU, which occurs between about 05Z and 0730Z, depending on where in EU. Second, I discovered a wonderful greyline opening around 15Z. Between 1455Z and 1517Z, I worked RU1A, SN2M, YT7A, UR5WA, 9A9R, and A65BP, with a couple of JAs in between. And this is with 5 watts! I almost fell out of my chair when A65BP came back to me.
Also, I've seen a note that A35T will be on 160M tonight, and with an improved RX antenna. I'll be waiting around their sunrise 0615Z, I think). 

73, Jim K9YC
