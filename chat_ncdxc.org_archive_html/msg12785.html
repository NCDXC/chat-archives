Bill,
 
Ur correct in that Vlad will no longer use LoTW.  Been that way for a couple years or maybe longer.
The price for one card for one of his calls is $3 as you figure.  Another card for another call is an added $1.50.  You can do it cheaper if you tell him to use the bureau or you can send 2 IRCs instead of $3.
 
He is pretty good about getting cards out but do not expect a quick turn.  I have had him send me cards a couple of times without any problems.  Sure wish he would reverse course on LoTW.  It would save him or his helpers a lot of time.
 
On the hard card thing, Not really difficult but anything non-electronic is going to be more work.  I do this process 2-3 times a yer depending on how many cards come my way that I need ARRL to verify.  you need to do an on line DXCC application where you fill in the QSOs.  Print it after paying for it and givei t and the cards to a card checker.  It then gets mailed out and you will get your credits in about 6 weeks.
