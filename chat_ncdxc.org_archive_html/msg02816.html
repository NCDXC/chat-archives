7-104 DXpedition Funding

DXpedition to submit proposal for funding to the NCDXC Board of Directors via email to

board@ncdxc.org that addresses the following guide lines:

a. Executive Summary of the proposed DXpedition

July 1, 2013 NCDXC Page 39 of 45

b. At least one (1) of participant(s) needs to be a current NCDXC members, as verified by

the online roster

c. The Board may on occasion choose to fund non-member operations.

d. Make an effort to work Zone 3 stations

e. Favorable consideration will be given to proposal for maximizing Qs, Bands and Modes

to Zone 3

f. The DXpedition Country/Entity needs to be on Top 100 needed list for North Americas

documented by ClubLog.org: http://www.clublog.org/mostwanted.php

g. The DXpedition has to be planned to be QRV a minimum of seven (7) days

h. The NCDXC logo printed on the DXpedition’s QSL card and on the DXpedition’s

website

i. Contribute the DXpedition’s story in the form of an article to be published in the DXer

within six (6) months from conclusion of the DXpedition

j. Presentation about the DXpedition to be presented by a member of the DXpedition at a

NCDXC regular meeting within next six (6) months from conclusion of the DXpedition

k. Logs shall be submitted to LoTW within three (3) months from conclusion of the

DXpedition for NCDXC members and all logs within 6 months. The NCDXC will provide

a list of members. Paper QSLs answered within six (6) months from conclusion of the

DXpedition
