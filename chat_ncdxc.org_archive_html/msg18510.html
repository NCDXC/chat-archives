Bethlehem has always been a challenge to operate from. Some of the more successful E4 activations have taken place from small villages in the West Bank. 
Gaza is too dangerous. 

I remember the E4X team telling me that they had S9 noise anytime the elevator in their hotel operated. Anytime it was moving, they were deaf!
There are a lot of man made noise sources there that make hearing anything a real challenge. 

The ZC4 guys had weak signals but they really had determination and conditions were just perfect on a couple of their nights. 
I know that some W6/W7 got them on 160 but I did not, but thrilled to get them on 80. It was good timing, luck, a good amplifier and good conditions
and a good Operator on the other end. 

73,
