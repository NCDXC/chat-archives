Paul, that’s wise advice. Our usual best practices in CW and SSB pileups need some modifications for digimodes. FT8 requires the same strategic thought that goes into being successful and remaining well liked in any pileup. Listen first, call off frequency, be aware of the tone and timing of the DX’s current Q, and don’t let your PC think for you. And then listen some more! Thanks.
