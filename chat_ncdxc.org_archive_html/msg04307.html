My K-Line is by far the best gear I have ever owned. The way the components all work together is just the best. And the people at Elecraft and their service – even beats Ten Tec – which I have always admired.

 

The only component I have had problems with is the KPA-500. It has failed twice in its first year of warranty. This second time – it was just barely covered by the warranty. The fine folks at Elecraft even called me to explain exactly what caused the fault each time – and in each case – required a small circuit improvement and new firmware. Extra fault tolerance features were added to prevent such things from happening again.

 

I use the amp almost daily, and I did have a “twinge” to return to a tube amp and manual tuner after this second failure. But because the design of the entire system is what it is – fantastic, I will soldier on and ride along with these improvements. I have this gut feeling that nailing down a bullet proof solid state amp takes time – something that tube amps have had decades to do.

 

And since I manage the first ever Beta test program at Splice Machine in San Francisco – I know all too well the cycle it takes to perfect a product.

 

Maybe Elecraft will send me a nice shirt or hat for being such an Elecraft “fan boy”? (Just kidding, but talk about loyalty!).

 

73,

 

Rich

KY6R

 

P.S – I found that the 110 watts from the straight K3 worked almost as much DX as with 500 watts. I just had to make sure my pileup “timing” was better than when I use the amp – where I can be a little more “lax” . . .
