This weekend is ARRL DX SSB

This is NOT a major NCCC club event.  If MLDXCC or other club wants to get into this fray please go for it and have fun. If you don't belong to a club where they want your score, then use the default: NORTHERN CALIFORNIA CONTEST CLUB.

Interesting that the TX5K will just be getting on the air as ARRL DX gets under way. I have a personal interest in this one because I've been involved with this group during the planning, but decided not to go on this one myself. Local KBers WA6O, NP4IW, and N6XG are there. KY6R is in contact and posting his blog about this trip here:
http://ky6r.com/

The really interesting aspect of this trip is the return of "DXA" where you can watch the action in near real time:
http://www.dxa2.org/
Obviously, they are still setting up but I suspect first QSOs will be tomorrow just ahead of the opening bell of ARRL DX.  Please be aware of their split operations.

Contest Exuberantly!
