I'm not sure where to send these since the tres amigos are retiring, so
I'm posting them here.



Some additional comments:

Many people complained to me that they couldn't hear and/or see
the presentations.  The projector was a tiny one that seemed
to be designed to carry in a laptop bag with room left over
for the laptop.  This is completely inadequate for an
auditorium.  The pocket size projector didn't have the wide
angle optics to fill the screen, and was already too dim.
It could have been moved back farther from the screen at
least by moving some chairs.  The audio built into the
room didn't get to the back very well.  The audience
mikes were inadequate and the speakers didn't repeat
the questions.

Too bad, because there were great people presenting.  I
thought this was taking place in a "convention center".
Where was the A/V support?
