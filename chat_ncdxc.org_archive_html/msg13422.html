Thanks Bob.  Wow, it's indeed quite expensive at the regular price.  There are not too many uses for a double male connector however I do have one (non-Amphenol) in my shack.  I have my LP-100A sensing coupler connected to the input of my external tuner via a double-male PL-259.  The couple is small enough to be hung on the back of the tuner.  With this method I avoid having a short jumper between the tuner and the coupler.

Are there any other uses for a double-male PL-259?  I bet the double-female outsells the double-male by 100:1.
