This was a very interesting and in depth interview with the legendary Don Wallace W6AM done in 1984.  Don talks about how he got into radio and his service in WWII. 

He also shows his station including his acres of Rhombic antennas. Towards the end, he shows his 500 watt HF mobile station. 

It was long but worth watching:
