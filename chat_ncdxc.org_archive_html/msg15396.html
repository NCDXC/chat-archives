I have learned that the latest version of WSJT-X (for some reasons) will not permit the Baker Island team to operate FT8 on their announced frequency of 50.316. They have changed the plan to operate on 50.323. This is BOTH the beacon frequency, and the operating frequency for 6 meters FT8. There is an operator monitoring this station 24/7. 

If you can hear the beacon on 50.323, call them on that frequency, using Hound mode above 1000 Hz. 

Good luck!
