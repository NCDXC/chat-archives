2023 marks my 41st year as an amateur operator and my 31st year as a "die hard" DXer. 

I've just got to say that the past few months have been so much fun, chasing and working so much rare DX.  It is incredible to have sunspots and amazing propagation. 

It was a super thrill to work Crozet on CW and FT8. Yesterday, the 3Y0J boys were super loud on 20 CW as they near Bouvet. 

If anyone needs a station to work Bouvet, you are welcome to visit the N6PSE shack. 

73, 

Paul N6PSE
