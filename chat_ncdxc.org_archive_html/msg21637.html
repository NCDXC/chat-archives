Well, DXing has helped fill the pandemic restricted hours very well. 

 

I’ve made 4587 DX QSOs this year to date and 429 of them have been on 80M.  I need 8 more band slots on 80M for DXCC there.

 

With my very modest 80M antenna, my 2020 highlights have been:

 

JT1CO on 3.574 in FT8 at 2020-03-04 14:43:00Z (JT: Mongolia)

XV1X on 3.504 (QSX 3.505) in CW at 2020-01-05 15:17:40Z (3W: Viet Nam)

5T5PA on 3.574 in FT8 at 2020-03-15 06:15:00Z (5T: Mauritania)

ZS1SC on 3.574 in FT8 at 2020-03-28 04:02:00Z (ZS: Republic of South Africa)

JD1BHA on 3.574 in FT8 at 2020-11-07 13:40:00Z (JD1-O: Ogasawara)

 

And being called by 9K2HQ and VU2MB will I was working other DX on 17M also made my day.

 

But honestly, the best DX is the last one I worked.

 

Happy Thanksgiving, everyone – enjoy and stay safe and well!

 

- 73 and good DX de Mike, K6MKF, NCDXC Secretary

2020 is almost over, what was your best DX of 2020?

 

Looking over my log, I was crazy busy on FT8 and filled a lot of band slots. I don't think I had any new countries this year but the highlights are:

 

Worked VP8PJ So Orkney in February from 15m to 160m on SSB/CW, RTTY and FT8. 

 

I also worked at E44CC Palestine in Feb for a few band slots. 

 

TI9A  Cocos Island was also great fun in early February. 

 

Working SU3YM, SU1SK, HZ1ZM on FT8 during the Summer months were also memorable. 


What has been your best DX thus far?
