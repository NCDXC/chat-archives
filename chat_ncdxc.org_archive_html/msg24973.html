Hello everyone!

My name is Sophie and I am 7 years old. I have been a member of Girl Scout troop 60943 for two years.

The Girl Scout Cookie drive is one of my favorite times of the year because it gives me the chance to sell delicious cookies to everyone I know.

I learn how to run my very first business, manage money, talk to people, be responsible and have fun! Hope you buy 100 boxes!

My troop plans to do 3 things with the money we earn selling cookies: save for the future, donate to a good cause, and spend on activities.

Thank you!
