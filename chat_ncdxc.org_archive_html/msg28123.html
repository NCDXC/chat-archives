Steve, your recollection of FT5GA is accurate and that is why I always feel that the best DXpeditions are a blended EU/US/JA effort. 

Mixed teams simply perform better. Additionally, each team needs to have a master strategist. One who decides which bands/when. Someone with the frame of mind of satisfying the greater good and not focusing on his homies back home. You can go home and be a Hero at Friedrichshafen and a Zero at other lesser served regions.

Well, I haven't worked them at all -- still looking for the ATNO -- and I'm currently not hearing them either.  It's very frustrating.

 

And for those of you with 2, 3 or more contacts already, thanks for nothing!  There have been multiple days  where I've heard them just fine on 17M, 15M or 30M, mostly on FT8 but also sometimes on CW.  But I wasn't able to get through the monstrous pileup.  And I'm not exactly running QRP here:  the station is an Elecraft K4D with a SPE 1.3K-FA amp.  I'm limited to an inverted-V on 30m, but have a hex beam at 35' on 17 and 15.  So I should certainly have been able to work them.

 

Yes, I know it's not just people working them multiple times that have kept me from being successful, but it sure as hell didn't help.
