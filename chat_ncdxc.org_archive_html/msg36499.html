Still not busy, S5 here on peaks (noise floor -132 dBm).  With narrow bandwidth and APF on (K4), easy here.  There are some fades, but that doesn't last long.

They DO take their time (seeking EU for the most part); so there are long pauses between calls.

I suggest some timely speed, they move on quickly if not called often.

They also have a nasty habit of bumping the VFO some KHz (or a screen tap), so you'll want to watch the display or listen wider.
