Off the DX topic, but of possible interest....

Some time ago I bought a used FT-817 to aid in noise location. The NiMH pack that was included was near EOL and died. I ordered a pack online from bigupbattery.com for a good price, on 31 January, received order confirmation, and waited. And waited. It is not here yet. In fact, it is not even close.

My inquiry several weeks into February brought the excuse that delay was due to "Chinese New Year"--the first disclosure that the part was coming from Hong Kong. They declared that the CNY glut was over and it would ship immediately. It did not. Now the excuse is that postal regs forbid shipping batteries by air. They gave me no opportunity to cancel but shipped---today--via SURFACE. So two months will have elapsed before receiving the part. No caveats or warnings on the website, only a courteous reply with no apology or discount.

I have told them I am finished with them. Now I have to locate another battery pack. I have not decided what to do with the phantom order.

Take this as a heads-up---the dark side of internet retail---rather than as my just letting off steam.

Garry, NI6T
