Started trying at 630 AM. He was weak but readable here on HF2V and loop but working only a few NA.  Then.by 730 he got louder but was working all EU all the time.   What times have people in SF Bay Area found to be best for working them on 80m?

Worked them at 5:19 AM local easily on a
low (40 ft) inverted vee.  But I'm in
Sac County, not the Bay Area, FWIW.

You want to work them sooner rather than
later because EU is still in full daylight
then.  At 6:30 local, northern and/or eastern
EU at least is nearing sunset.

I find it useful to run a gray line map.  My
DX suite software comes with this map.
