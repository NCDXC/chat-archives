I agree we should not water down DXCC nor change the entities.
What should be changed is that for HR you should contact 340 entities in all 3 modes (CW, SSB, Digital) and all bands (6 to 160m).
If you don't comply, you are out of the HR until you do. Who said it should be easy, right? And it should take all your life and beyond.

Hard to be serious with such a flawed game. You get 3 DXCC entities in Rome , within less than 3 miles: (Italy, The Vatican and The Sovereign Order of Malta) and then the US is the size of EU. Underwater reefs. Uninhabited Islands (there is a reason they are not populated: Bouvet?).  

ARRL should abandon DXCC and create a new one based on ITU zones. That at least proves your station can reach the whole world. For those zones that have no populated masses of land, track ships for /MM (easy to do these days). Create DXPeditions that traverse oceans.  Change is good. DXCC is so 20'th century.

My two cents as a new Ham (2018)
