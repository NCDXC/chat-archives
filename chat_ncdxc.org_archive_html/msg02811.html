Members and non-members of NCDXC –

 

Donations to DXpeditions are made by the board depending upon the financial condition of the club (which is very good) and what is believed to be an expedition that club members (and the DX community) want and need.

 

As treasurer I perform the mechanics of making the donations at the board’s direction.  Since I make the donation I often get a nice Thank You note back which I share with the board. 

 

The board has agreed these thank you messages need to be publicized so you as dues paying members see where some of you club’s money is going.  How it benefits your DX experience.

 

Below is one of those messages. 

 

73,

 

Bob – W6OPO
