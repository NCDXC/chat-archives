Yes. When he does that and the timing makes sense, I send my call three
times.


The wall of RTTY callers is particularly bad.  So bad that you can

I got through on 15M by using the P3 to find a hole.  Of course, that's
only a hole on my end, and may not be on his. :)

When he's mostly working eastern US, whose signals are MUCH stronger
than ours, it makes no sense for us to try to call on the frequency of
the last Q. What I do is lay back a few seconds, look for the nearest
hole to that last Q, and call there.
