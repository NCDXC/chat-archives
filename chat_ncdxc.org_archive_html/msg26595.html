On Fri, Aug 26, 2022 at 7:06 PM Paul J via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
Fellow NCDXC Ops, 

As a heads up, this weekend is the WW Digi contest.  Hope to see you out there.  If you're not familiar, it's a digital mode contest that will likely be operated primarily on FT4 and FT8.

The exchange is gridsquare only - no SNR needed. If you get a station over the weekend that either ignores your TX2-first call - or - doesn't return a signal report on FT mode - its very likely they (we) are working in the contest.  Try sending a grid and see if you get a response.

For more info on what's going on, how to send grid-only during the test, etcetera - please see https://ww-digi.com/ .  I'm a fairly new contester but I believe this will make for a pretty epic weekend on FT8, given the last 48 hours conditions.

Every contact counts, so hope to work some of you between 5a tomorrow local, and 5a Sunday.

-Paul W8GJK
