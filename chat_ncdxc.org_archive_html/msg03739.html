Hi All,

I appreciate your patience last night.  Pooyan has a nasty cold and he overslept.  He wanted to apologize to the group for being late.  0230z is 6am local time in EP so he's doing us a big favor.

Pooyan told me he's not a proficient CW operator.  His comfort level is at around 12-13wpm.  It would help tremendously if you adjust your speed to match his comfort zone.  Also please be respectful to others when he picks up a partial call.  Please don't call if your call doesn't match the partial call 100%.  By helping Pooyan increase his work rate you are also increasing your own chance of working him.  Lastly, last night he had a problem with the Russians trying to work him and they were 20 over or more.  If he has to QSY he'll let me know the new freq.  I will post the new freq. to this mailing list.

The next sked is scheduled on Sunday 0230z which is our Saturday evening.  We will do CW only running split up 2.  Let's hope conditions will be good.  GL to all!

ps Pooyan is interested in doing digital modes.  My experience is that the decoder doesn't work well on signals with polar flutter.  Please correct me if I'm wrong on this.  For the really small stations perhaps a mode like JT65 might just be the ticket?
