Congratulations to NCDXC member and PARA President, Kristen McIntyre K6WX for being inducted into the CQ Magazine Amateur Radio Hall of Fame this past weekend at Hamvention 2017!

From CQ Magazine: Kristen McIntyre, K6WX - Apple software engineer and inventor (her name is on 22 granted or pending patents), promoter of STEM (science, technology, engineering and math) subjects for girls through various talks and YouTube presentations

A well deserved honor!
