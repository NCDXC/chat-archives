Yeah, the King just decided to rename the place and that was that!

For those of you that have not visited to 3DA-land, it was quite an eyeopener for the XYL and me.  It started with having to register my laptop computer at the border.  Who does that?  Once across the border and around a few bends, the landscape revealed that this place has sold out to logging and the lumber industry.  Clearcutting and managed planted forests as far as the eye could see.  Sure, there were some places of natural beauty but too few for my liking.  Interesting experience.
