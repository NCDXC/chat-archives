Oops -

 

A minor correction to the "treasurers" term of office. 

 

The treasure's position is open at each year's election just like officers and directors.  The difference is that the treasurer can be re-elected over and over for a maximum of 5 terms (5-yrs) where other officers the limit is 2-years and directors at large is 3-years.

 

Bylaws (Art IV Sec 4 & 8) pages 4 and 6.
