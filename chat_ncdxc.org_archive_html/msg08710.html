i dont do lotw
Why not? Are you independently wealthy? :) LOTW (and to a lesser extent eQSL) save you and everyone who wants a QSL from you a lot of time and money. 


....will is still be on there??


Do you mean after you've loaded your log?  Sure.
The better computer logging software makes LOTW and eQSL VERY easy to use once you get your account set up. I use DXKeeper (free, well supported). It imports logs from other loggers. Once you've jumped through hoops to get an LOTW account, you plug the password into DXKeeper and tell it to upload your log. A day or so later, you tell it to download your QSLs. Roughly 55% of the 112,000 QSOs I've made since moving here in 2006 are confirmed in LOTW. Since I started over on 9BDXCC when I moved here, that's a LOT of postage I've saved. :) Easily enough to buy a loaded K-line.
