If you guys need Ethiopia, this info comes via W3UR's The Daily DX. 

ET - Ethiopia
At ET3AA, January 14-21, K4ZW, W9XY and EY8MM will be with local EARA
ops, the Ethiopian Amateur Radio Association.  They will spend a week
renewing friendships, recruiting new club members from the students at
the university, and doing some on-air operating guidance, as well as
working on the club station itself.  Of course they will be giving out
DX QSOs.  They have requested permission to operate overnight and are
hoping that will come through.  In previous operations a noise source
comes on at nightfall and is on until sunrise, wiping out the HF
spectrum.  They are hoping for the best this time.  QSL via N2OO.
