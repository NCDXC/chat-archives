On Jul 16, 2019, at 10:24 AM, Richard (Rick) Karlquist via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

This overall looks good, but it has one important flaw:

The casters are light duty plastic ones that are attached
to particle board with wood screws.  If I put something
heavy on this desk, like my Alpha 9500, I will be in great
danger of the casters breaking loose if the floor is not
perfectly smooth.  (Been there, done that).  I may buy
this anyway, but replace the casters with heavy duty
versions that have larger mounting plates, and then I
will mount them using bolts and nuts and washers, rather
than wood screws.  I have owned several desks in the
past where I had to re-engineer the casters or add ones
where they didn't exist from the factory.
