David,

When you enter the site, go to Enter  QSLs and you will see a list of your QSOs with call, date, band and mode.  If you see nothing, there is nothing to edit or delete.  If you see the list, each can be delete or edited.

When you log out, the list you left remains there until you go back and enter more QSOs. This list ultimately is printed out for presentation with the hard QSL card for inspection by a checker or if you send it in to HQ.

However, I have never entered via importation of a file.  I have too few to enter so I do them manually.

 

Hope that helps a tiny bit!
