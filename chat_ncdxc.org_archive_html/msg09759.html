As of this message, all members of NCDXC in good standing has been configured into the N6NKT-L Echolink node.  This node connects our W6TI 2 Meter repeater to the internet via Echolink application.  If you are not in good standing (club dues not paid yet) nor member of the NCDXC, sorry.  NCDXC Echolink node can only be access by members in good standing.

The Echolink program would be required on your computer or portable device to access the N6NKT-L Node.

For PC users:  http://www.echolink.org/
iPhone or Android device: check for echolink in the store.

Feel free to post a question to the chat.  There are also other members who are very familiar with Echolink as well.

Good DXing
