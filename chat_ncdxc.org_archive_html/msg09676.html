The opening on 12M this morning was excellent.  It was the best I'd heard them all trip.  The pileup was um, intense.  There were enough robocallers with no ears (trolls?) that it wasn't possible to track where they were listening (here at least).

Hopefully they'll redo the opening tomorrow and stand by for West Coast.  That is my best last chance at this for ATNO unless they can do the same on Monday our time.

Right now they're barely above the noise floor here -126db on 10 and not worth trying... even with 600 watts.

I'm presuming that besides the pileups, there is significant noise since they are in a hotel, which signifies a town...

Disappointing but that's what it is...
