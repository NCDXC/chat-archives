So I can just keep SuperHound checked in the Advanced settings then? I felt last time I had to switch between the two to get to regular hound. 

 

 

My bad. The operator will need to detect whether F/H or SuperFox is used and manually switch in the Settings pane. It’s easy to determine which is which: Normal F/H is one or more 50 Hz wide streams running in the low part of the band. Be sure to call above 1000 Hz. Superfox spreads over 1500Hz, with a pilot tone at 750 Hz +/-.
