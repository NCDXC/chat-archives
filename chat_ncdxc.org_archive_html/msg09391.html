Their sunrise is around 1200Z, so I set the alarm for 1130Z this morning, and found them on 1845 kHz CW. Good operator, not very busy. Worked up about 1 kHz in about 5 minutes. They were not loud, and with the usual multi-path long QSB. The first time they came back to me, I responded 5NN TU, but didn't hear the RR. So I called them again a few minutes later, and got a solid RR. 

I think they're running K3s and KPA500s.
