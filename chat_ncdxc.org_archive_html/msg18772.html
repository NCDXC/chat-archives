I tried to watch a recent meeting about FT8.  The
slides were projected on a screen and then a
web cam was aimed at the screen.  Guess what:
THAT DOESN'T WORK. If nothing else, the speaker
should give a URL where his slides can be found.

Also, the speaker didn't have his own microphone,
but depended on a mike that picked up the noise
of table utensils better than his voice.

If you are going to put a meeting on line,
please don't show it as only a live web cast,
without a recording.

While we're on the topic, remember to have
the speaker repeat the question before giving
the answer, unless you have a 2nd mike for the
audience.  It's amazing how often I see presentations
where I can only hear the answer and not the question.

Rick N6RK
