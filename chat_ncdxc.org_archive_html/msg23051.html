Folks,

 

Wahhab YI3WHR logs QSOs after he sends his RR73 – which you may not have received.

 

I had this occur last night on 20M. 

 

At 0325Z I received: 210505_032545    14.074 Rx FT8    -16  0.6 1769 K6MKF YI3WHR -05 – but no RR73.

 

Later, I worked him at 0414 and had a clean exchange with an RR73 from him.

 

When I checked his log on Club Log this morning, I found he had logged two QSOs with me on 20M.   I used the ALL.TXT file in WSJT-X to recover the time for the first QSO, and OQRS confirmed that QSO when I ordered my QSL card.

 

If you’ve exchanged signal reports with YI3WHR, it might be worth checking Club Log – you might be in his log!
