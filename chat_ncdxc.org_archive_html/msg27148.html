Thank you to everyone who has chimed in. I really appreciate your kind words and support!

 

Reception has been very positive, and sales surprised me even with the lack of publicity. I guess there really is a demand. This AM when the Daily DX announced it, I saw a more traffic but will not see numbers until later tonight. Up until this AM I sold about 60 copied plus over 2000 page reads on Kindle unlimited with the only publicity being in the DXer and Facebook. I think the shock of me actually posting to Facebook may be bigger news as I post once every 3-4 years at best. Most copies sold in the US, with a few in the UK and India. I just turned-on extended distribution so we’ll see how that goes. Once the holiday rush dies down, I will try and do a little more promoting. Printing is very backlogged from what I have heard due to the season. Still waiting for half my author copies. Direct orders take priority, so I am glad folks are getting them quickly. I know…not the best timing.

 

I would be happy to sign any copies (minus the Kindle version) if you can read my handwriting. Hi hi
