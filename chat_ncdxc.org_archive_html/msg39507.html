Early reports from the island noted sporadic internet ability.  However, as a research facility with lives at stake; one would expect better unless he was denied net access and he brought his own (Starlink for example, though a mini might prove incapable of coverage without specific programming by the Musk team, simple lack of signal over a dead zone).

As such, the (far less than daily) frequency of uploads, is disappointing.  Tick tock repeat.

On the other hand, for the OT ops will say; it used to be FAR worse, not knowing until it was too late to adapt (the team had returned), multiple dupes were the norm, even expected for the new ones.  Having Livestream and near instant satisfaction is a discussion for another time and place (we’re spoiled now).

It started off with me as an ATNO.  Since then, 80 FT8 last night is the 4th band for me (all of the easy ones done now), 20 CW was a treat but phone may easily be a challenge, UNTIL and IF he spreads the coverage more evenly to all zones.  And with EU ops often being so generous, helpful and polite every day; it could be impossible (ditto JA for our long path).  [And just for balance, there is a LOT of NA poor behavior, whining and sheer stupid on the air, every day.  We have little room to gripe, but we will anyway.]

I want phone for the DXCC trifecta; the rest is gravy for the feast.

High bands are possible during our morning hours for short time windows; IF (and only IF) there is cooperation from EU (or he simply uses the filtering in MSHV, which has gagged, choked and hung up several times, as noted here).  And if the sun behaves too.

Sadly the post COVID rush into the field, has slackened; he’s about the only new location activated.  But we take what’s offered.
