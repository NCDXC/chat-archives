Paul, I just finished a Raspberry Pi project to do a world clock similar to the Geochrons but much more.  It's connected to a 4K monitor.  Really simple and cheap to do;  even thought about doing a presentation of such at a club meeting.  I also plan to use one to remote my station (a bit more complicated).  I just use a Raspberry Pi 4 Model B, 2GB which is available just about everywhere.  There are stripped down versions too depending on how much you want it to do.  There is a whole set of ham radio applications available for free.  MFJ also has a remote server based on this.  I know that Lucas has a lot of experience with Raspberry Pi....maybe his next book?
