Ah, 160M … I do miss it.  

 

Back in 1976-1985 I lived in a home with a very long N-S lot, and I had an OCF dipole that worked pretty well on 160M.   I enjoyed working stations up and down the East Coast on CW and SSB, sometimes even AM.   I thought it was a great band because it wasn’t very crowded and the 160M ops seemed to be somewhat more collegial and tolerant than those on other bands.  

 

Now that I’m here on my San Jose postage stamp-sized lot, I doubt if I will ever have an antenna here that is effective on 160M.   I’ve thought about shunt-loading my 40 ft. mast, but I really don’t have any place to put the radials – and the Site Committee (XYL) is not very accommodating in that regard.

 

So I have 160M band envy … ;>)

 

- 73 de Mike, K6MKF, W6NAG, VP NCDXC, Conway Reef 2012, K3-P3 Addict, Dachshund Rescue, Maui
