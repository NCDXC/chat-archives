I will be presenting at the Pacificon 2013 Antenna Forum, and the title is “DXCC Honor Roll in One Solar Cycle: A Little Pistol Adventure”. In the words of one person who reviewed this presentation at a Bay Area Club Meeting:

 

“It's more about the journey and education along the way than it is about the final achievement. An informative and humorous look at reaching DXCC Honor Roll with a "little pistol" station.” (Peter, W6DEI).

 

If you attended my IDXC “DX Academy” presentation, the presentation has now been updated and will include two new antennas that I have put up since then – a 2 element 40M phased vertical array – using the Christman Feed, and my new 160/80M antenna - a Cushcraft MA-160V on top of a 30’ telescopic mast – to get it to 60’. I describe the matching network that I built at the base - to match on both bands without switching . . . .

 

http://www.pacificon.org/2013/
