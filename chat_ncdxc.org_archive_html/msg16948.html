Banquet seating requests are made by email to mailto:cadxainfo@gmail.com

The request should include the names and callsigns of those who wish to sit together. Everyone being assigned a banquet seat must have paid for the banquet (but not necessarily be registered for the convention; it’s perfectly alright to just purchase meal tickets for folks who don’t care about the DX/Technical sessions).

You can purchase banquet tickets in advance ONLY until 5 pm Pacific time THIS AFTERNOON (March 30). Pre-registration closes at that time.

Banquet tickets will be available for purchase at the convention, and the banquet seating coordinator will be there and can assign/move seats on request and as available.

There are currently 479 people who have purchased banquet tickets, and there are 520 total seats available with the current table configuration. That gives some flexibility in moving people around to be with others they wish to dine with. If a LOT of banquet tickets are sold to walk-ins, we have the ability to add more tables and seats.

I look forward to seeing everyone in Visalia! (And don’t forget to register TODAY to get the pre-registration price break, and be part of the special pre-registration raffle drawing.) Raffle tickets at the pre-registration special price ($20 for 24 tickets; it’s $20 for 20 tickets at the convention) can be purchased by calling me at (408) 395-5700, using any major credit/debit card. I will accept raffle ticket purchases for a few days after today, but I need to prepare the registration packets this week, so time’s a-wasting!

Rich KE1B
