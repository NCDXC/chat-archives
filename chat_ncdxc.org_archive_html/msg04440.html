Hi Tony,

Whenever a station from whom I want a QSL card uses OQRS (the Online QSL Request System), I use that method.  It saves money and time compared to using postal mail.  I just charge the amount requested for the DX station to mail me a direct reply to my credit card.

But if there's no OQRS available, I usually send my card direct and enclose a self addressed envelope plus a couple of the newest, cleanest one dollar bills I can find.  I've been pretty successful getting replies this way.

Good luck.

Rusty, W6OAT
