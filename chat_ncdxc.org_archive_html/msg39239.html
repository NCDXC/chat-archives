My Hygain T2X “tail twister” has finally packed it in. It’s been getting stalled from time to time, but would always recover after a spin to the opposite direction. I’ve concluded that the issue is internal to the rotor, such as missing teeth on the ring gear, since the mast/antenna assembly rotates easily by hand.
