Greetings from Boulder Creek to my fellow DXers.  DX has been a little thin recently and few confirmations to QSOs.  However, Santa came today in the form of the mail carrier:

 

IA0MZ (CE9) for 15 M SSB.  First time this slot confirmed

PZ5T for first 80 CW slot  confirmation (plus 8 other QSOs)

V85T for first 80 CW slot confirmed

Z60A.  LOTW but first card for the album (including a QSO I did not make on 40 CW!)

A52BH for a 40 M CW contact.

 

Missing is a UA2 on 40 SSB I have been trying really hard to get confirmed.

I cannot recall a day when I got four envelopes on the same day.  I’d be happy to do it again!

 

All the best DX and health to you all and urs.
