Every year at Visalia, Bob N6TV proposes to eliminate the nonobtanium entities. About 80% of the hands vote NO. Then he asks the audience to raise their hand if you are honor roll, 80% of the hands go up. Then he proposes not to eliminate but to add a new entity for each that is nonobtanium, 80% of the hands vote NO.

DXCC is a long tradition in Ham Radio and as traditions go, don’t adapt to changes in time until they become meaningless.

Many islands were once visited by romantic and wealthy operators. They are now off limits by country laws. 

Somebody is going to get hurt by keeping this idiotic list alive.
