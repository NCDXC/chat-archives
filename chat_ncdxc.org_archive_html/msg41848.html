Subject: 2026 Santa Maria DX & Contesters Convention - Registration Fee

 

Hello all,

 

The 2026 Santa Maria DX and Contesters Convention is now a little more than two months away! We hope that you are getting as excited about it as we are! The organizers, Jim, N6TJ and Dick, N6AA are working hard to make this a fun event. It will be lower key than what you may be used to at the IDXC in Visalia but a lot of fun, nevertheless. Events are being worked on and added to the EVENTS page regularly so keep an eye on that page on the website.

 

The Convention Registration Fee has now been set. Until now, only the Friday Contest Dinner fee had been set as Jim and Dick were working diligently on various events (many are still being finalized, in fact) and with the Santa Maria Inn on the number of meeting rooms needed before settling on the Convention Registration Fee cost.

 

Full Registration is $100 per person and includes Convention Registration, the Saturday Night Happy Hour (partially paid by our sponsors), the Saturday Night Banquet (also partly paid by our sponsors) and the Sunday Breakfast (again, also partly paid by our sponsors). This does not include the Friday Contest Dinner.  You can go to our website Registration page to pay or pay here: https://paypal.me/ne6i/100USD If paying for more than one person, please multiply the number by $100.

 

If you want to forgo meals and Happy Hour, Registration is just ten bucks! You will be on your own for meals elsewhere other than the hotel. To pay for this option, go here: https://paypal.me/ne6i/10USD Again, If paying for more than one person, please multiply by $100.

 

If PayPal is not your thing, please contact me for alternative payment options. You can send a check via my QRZ address if you like. Zelle will work but email me for my Zelle payment address first. Other options may be available.

 

And for those who registered for the Friday Contest Dinner but have not yet sent payment for it, please do send your payment along. Thanks.

 

Payments for Registration and the Contest Dinner are due by April 1, 2026. If you have already submitted it, thanks.

 

As always, I will update the website within 72 hours to reflect receipt of your payment.

 

Need to cancel?  Send Jim, N6TJ or me an email so that we can update our registration lists, notify the venues, and refund any payments you have made.

 

Questions, comments? Hit any of us up via email addresses on our Contact Us page.

 

We look forward to seeing you in Santa Maria!

 

73, Dennis NE6I

Webmaster
