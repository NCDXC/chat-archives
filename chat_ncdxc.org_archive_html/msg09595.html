CC&Rs are nasty documents that eliminate many of the rights we have long associated with the ownership of property. Any ham who has gone house-hunting in the last decade has learned that a VERY large fraction of homes for sale are covered by CC&Rs. If you live in one of these places, your ham antennas are limited to little more than a wet noodle.
