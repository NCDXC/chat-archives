I hope if I left anything in Newington it was a little sanity concerning some of these things. I wrote a QST sidebar about the operation from the "enclosure" at Aves Island --late in 2004, I think.
The whole point of being "land based" was to know where the operation took place, not rules for rules sake; not for power trips. There is no question in my mind that the 1994 BS7H operation should have counted. I didn't push that because not that many people needed it, and it wasn't worth the "political capital."
