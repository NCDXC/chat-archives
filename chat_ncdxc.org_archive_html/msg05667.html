I think everyone is being a little hard on these guys.  At least they were up. None of us went.
1] They were workable here on most bands -with modest effort--except for 10M's.
2]They did work more Eu stations than US/Zone 3 stations but thats normal--Eu is right next door--and much louder--and more French.
Eu has/makes the same complaints when places like Kingman Reef is up. Its just one way the cookie crumbles.
3] It's their ball game. They are paying almost the whole bill and thus get to set the rules.
If we provided major funding, and by that extracted an agreement that they would call W6's,--thats a totally different matter. I do think they made efforts to contact US stations--maybe not as much as we would have liked.
4] That said the French are a little more prone to favoritism. But they are not the Lone Ranger.
That's a part of the hobby--fair or unfair.
5] The belief that we are the most "deserving" of the "deserving"is not universally held.
I'm glad they were up.
