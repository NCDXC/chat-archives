Team

 

In reading an old Dxer from 1997 I was reminded of how much fun it is as a Dxer to actually *do it* and to remember, and appreciate,  old friends and to help new aspiring “DXers” and new amateur radio operators.

 

http://www.ncdxc.org/newsletter/1997/dxer0897.pdf

 

 

I plan to get back to following my own advice given back in 1997 and I for one intend to be nicer to some of you guys moving forward. J. We all have a shared love of a wonderful hobby.  Lets get out there and *do it*...and love it…whatever *doing it* means to you that caused you to become and love being a DXer......

 

73, Ted, K6XN,  doing it……

 

RE: *DOING IT* from the August 1997 Dxer:

 

* Its a great time to be a DXer and/or a contester! Its also

a great time to build rigs, put up antennas, learn new

skills, meet new friends and revisit memories of things

we enjoyed doing with old friends...and even more

important.......*doing it*.....to be is to do......

I had forgotten how much fun all of this DXing really is and

how much fun it can continue to be. There is no substitute

for actually doing it. Talking about it or thinking about it

is just not the same. I relearned this lesson again and

again this summer...that the fun is in the *doing*....”*
