Last nite was a pleasant surprise. Had worked ZD9 on 40-10M except 30. Literally on the way to bed decided to take one more listen on 30M. This was an a great reminder to me that even an apparently dead band is often quite open. Also interesting to note that the band where I have the least antenna (low vertical GP) and lowest power (200W) was by far the easiest QSO. Hmm maybe some luck too!
