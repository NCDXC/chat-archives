Dave,

For shack noise like you have, almost any over the ear units with the cushy ear pads will do the job.
I have a pair of Heil phones and a pair of Yamaha CM500’s.  I use the Yamahas all the time now. The Yamahas are very comfortable, 
have a boom mike that is small and folds back out of the way so you don’t even know its there, also very reasonable cost at $60.00.
