Hi All,

Congrats on the many contacts made tonight.  This is by far the most successful sked and we were really lucky with the great propagation.  Your persistence has paid off!  Pooyan wants to send his best regards to everyone including those who tried but were not successful.

Pooyan had a major equipment failure at the beginning of the sked.  His SDR radio wouldn't power up.  He suspected the power supply had failed.  He quickly went to plan B which is to use the only commercial radio he owns - a Yaesu 817.  He worked quickly to connect the keyer to the 817 and connect the 817 to another home brew linear (photos attached).  The backup linear puts out 200w PEP.  Despite the wide filter in the 817 he did an awesome job working the pile up.

With the move to the VK just a few months away Pooyan will be vacating his current QTH in two weeks and move to his mother's home.  Next weekend is his last weekend stay at his QTH.  He wants to go QRV at his mother's home but it'll take a couple weeks to setup the equipment.  He hopes to put up a spiderbeam at his new QTH.

Pooyan is still keen on giving out ATNOs and he'll be available again at the same time next week (Sunday 0230z).  Let's hope band conditions will remain excellent.

Paul N6PSE will distribute the log from tonight's sked.  Please send me your log corrections.
 
Best Regards,
