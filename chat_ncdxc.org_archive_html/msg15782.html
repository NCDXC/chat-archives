Tony's signal peaked at 1500 UTC (8AM our time)  By 9AM he was much weaker.

I benefited from the fact that Tony was telling his pileup he was listening up 5 when in reality he was simplex. I made 3-4 calls and got in between the mob of Italians that were constantly calling him. 

I told Tony he was not split and he decided to stay simplex. The pileup erupted after my contact when everyone realized they were calling up and he worked me simplex. 

Many of his locals never stopped calling, no matter who he came back to.
