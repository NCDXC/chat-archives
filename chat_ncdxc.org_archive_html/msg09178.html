 Santa Clara Valley Including San Jose Severe Watches & WarningsNOAA Weather Radio

Wind Advisory

Statement as of 1:30 PM PST on February 05, 2015

...Wind Advisory remains in effect from 4 am Friday to 4 am PST Saturday...

* timing: late Thursday night through late Friday night.

* Winds: sustained southerly winds of 25 to 40 mph with gusts up to 50 mph. Wind gusts up to 60 mph are possible at higher elevations.

* Impacts: potential for downed trees and power lines... resulting in isolated power outages. In addition...diving conditions will be difficult at times.

* Locations: the entire San Francisco Bay area including all of the North Bay. Strongest winds will be near the coast and in the hills.

Precautionary/preparedness actions...

A Wind Advisory means that sustained winds of at least 35 mph are expected or gusts of at least 45 mph. Winds this strong can bring down trees and power lines...and make driving difficult.

Take action to secure trash cans...lawn furniture...and other loose or lightweight outdoor objects that may be easily moved by strong winds.
