Rich (KE1B) and Anna (W6NN) will be QRV in Anguilla for the FOURTH time, from November 19th through November 28th, 2018. As usual, this will be a holiday-style DXpedition, not a 24/7 operation.

The station uses an Elecraft K3S, running 100W into a Buddipole vertical antenna located adjacent to the Caribbean Sea.

Operations will be on all bands 40 through 6 meters (conditions permitting), CW/SSB/RTTY and this year, FT8 (standard mode, not DXpedition Fox/Hound). We will also be operating in the CQ WW DX CW contest on November 24-25.

All CW/SSB/RTTY QSOs will be uploaded daily to Clublog. FT8 QSOs will be uploaded every few days. Once we return home, ALL QSOs will be uploaded to Clublog, LoTW, and eQSL. If you believe you should be "in the log" and are not, send an email to mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com and I will see if we busted the call or made some other error.

Give us a call, spot us on the cluster, and work us in the contest for that Anguilla multiplier!


Rich KE1B/VP2EAQ
