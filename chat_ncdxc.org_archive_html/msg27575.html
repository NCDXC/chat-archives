On 1/17/2023 10:27 AM, Paul Booth via Chat wrote:

A couple of thoughts on FT8 F/H.  A caveat is I'm not an expert but in this case he was operating f/h but not all f/h's are equal.  For example I answered with my report on my original xmt frequency and he acknowledged it.  Here's a reference to distinguish between f/h and mshv.

 

https://www.dxmaps.com/FT8_How.html

 

In short, if multiple messages (to more than one station) were sent on the same slot (frequency) then he's operating F/H.  What I do is to watch what frequencies he's answering to, to find the best frequency range.  He may be more receptive at some frequencies.  Sometimes I even take over a slot that he finished a qso with knowing that it's clear since you can't always know what frequencies are clear on his end.  If in doubt I always call above 1000Hz.

 

Please, anyone correct me if I'm mistaken.

 

73, Paul, W6IBU
