I've done some prop studies using VE3NEA's HamCap, which uses the VOACAP engine. They suggest good times for us are: 

30M:  15Z Short, 14-15Z Long
20M:  00-0430Z Short, 03-05Z and 12-13Z Long
17M:  23Z-03Z Short (EU weaker after 02Z); 04-06Z
15M:  22Z-0130Z Short, 04-06Z, 16Z Long
12M:  19Z-2230Z
10M:  19-20Z
VOACAP doesn't show any openings for us on 40 and 80, but a grayline bump seems likely for us in the Bay Area around 0250Z when both ends are in twilight. My rule of thumb is that the opening can last +/- 15-30 minutes on 160M, +/- 1 hour on 80M, +/- 1.5-2 hours on 40M, 2-3 hours on 30M.
