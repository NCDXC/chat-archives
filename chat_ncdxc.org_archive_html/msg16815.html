I will be looking for signal reports with time and band openings from West Coast DXers. I will take these and update the team. I will batch and send at least once a day, but please don't expect "real time". My QRZ info is correct.

The team will want to exploit the best time and band openings to get as many in the log as possible.

Good Luck in the Pileups, but first, watch for their ship tracker and hope for smooth sailing.

From a personal perspective, I regularly have a 40M QSO with Bill, ZS6CCY after our West Coast sunset and almost always have a short path QSO each evening just running 100 watts. ZS is good on the morning LP 40M path, but I would be surprised if the morning LP path will be as reliable as the night time SP.

73 es Gud Luck,
