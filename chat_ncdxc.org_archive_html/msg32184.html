Bruce K3NQ and I have been successful in getting LOTW credit for QSO's with EP2FAR by emailing him at the email listed in his QRZ bio. It was an ATNO for me, and I think for Bruce too. 

Shahin does not routinely use LOTW, but he responded positively to our emailed requests.

The only way to get a physical QSL card is to mail a QSL card and greenstamps, from somewhere outside the US, to his QSL manager in Russia. Snail mail is still not possible from the US to Russia (oddly, we can still receive mail from Russia).

In your email to Shahin, be sure to put in a plug for world peace. Seriously, I think that helped.
