On behalf of the Mellish Reef 2014 DX-pedition team I want that the BoD and the members of NCDXC for the generous donation to our project.

The project planning is coming along very well. Oceania DX Group (ODXG) in Australia offered their assistance with some in-country tasks and our own team members are each working hard to meet their assigned tasks.

While we have a lot of work remaining this experienced team knows that we need to be ready no later then 45 days before departure. An advance team will arrive in Australia at least a week before sailing to consolidate equipment and procure any last minute items.

West Coast stations should have no trouble working us. With a focus on 160 and RTTY (last RTTY operation from Mellish was 2002) there should be a strong demand.

73 and thanks again,
Gene K5GS
