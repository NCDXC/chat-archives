My Greek ham friends tell me that /A means that the licensee is operating from an alternative location to where they are licensed from. It does not denote Mt. Athos. Monk Apollo correctly uses /A as his licensed was issued outside of Mt Athos. 

Since SV1RP is stating that he was not operating from Mt. Athos during WPX, those contacts shouldn't count for Mt. Athos.
