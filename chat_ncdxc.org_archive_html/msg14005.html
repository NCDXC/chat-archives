I just spoke with Doug.  Unfortunately he did lose his home in the Santa Rosa fire.  Doug said he only had 10 minutes to vacate his home at 3:00 AM.  Unfortunately he couldn’t find his cats and had to leave them behind.  He is concerned about them.

Please keep the Benders in your prayers.
