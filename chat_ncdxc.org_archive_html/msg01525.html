Oh yeah: Clipperton.  It had the aura of the one you may never get to work: Uninhabited, middle of the ocean, most likely surrounded by shark infested waters!  At least that is how I perceived it when I started DXing in the early 70s.  Sometimes we need to stop and remember just how unbelievable it is to work some of these places!  Easier to work since the operations are masterpieces but still a far away place that still deserves the aura.

I was hoping that tonight I could get a 40 CW contact. I've had great fun and great success working TX5K on 160-10 meters, however 40 meters CW has been quite elusive.  I usually hear them at 10DB over S9, however they are ""EU only"" from our sunset to midnight when I cannot stay awake any longer. I got up a few times this week at 4:30AM but did not find them on 40 meters.

I read tonight on the blog  www.ky6r.com that they are starting to tear down early starting with the CW shack. I hope there is still some 40 meter CW for NA.
