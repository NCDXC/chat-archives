Its really the DX Stations call.
1]    If they want primarily ATNO's &/or to log only one DX Call/band  --they simply are active only on one open band ---until almost no one calls. They can even put multiple stations on that band, to accelerate the process,  if they want.
2]     If they operate multiple stations on different bands/modes they "invite" anyone/everyone, that wants it, to call on those bands/modes. And that's what happens.
Which I right to do??  Whatever the DX decides--and then what You decide--- IMO. Every DX'er, wanting that DX station, has to decide what's proper for his particular DX vision. Each of us likely has a different vision within the program rules. Hence there are QRP'ers, Mega-Stations, people who only work one band--- etc.
The "deserving" are thus those who figure out how to get their station/call in the log. No one is entitled to a contact.
