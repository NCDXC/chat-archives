By the time I got your email and turn the radio on at 2356Z he was way down in the noise. I managed to be able to copy him about 50% and sent my call at 900 watts. I thought I had made the QSO and went to their web page (via their entry on QRZ.COM) and used the log checking system. I hadn't made it into their log. So I tried again, and my reception was up to about 80%. It took 3 tries after he started asking about AE6 to the the call through and we completed the QSO. Their log showed me just a few minutes later for an ATNO. Yea!
