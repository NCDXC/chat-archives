Just to loop back around on this.. This is absolutely spot on.  I did some experimenting while chasing the RTTY slots.  All 3 K1N RTTY QSO’s were on the edges of the pile.  I simultaneously observed the pile from my flex 6500 to find the pile edges and sure enough when I called on the edges.. bam!  Btw big power wasn’t an issue.. I forgot to turn on my amp and worked them with drive power.

 

Moral of the story.. stay out of the middle of the pile. Someone needs to be there.. just don’t be one of them : -)
