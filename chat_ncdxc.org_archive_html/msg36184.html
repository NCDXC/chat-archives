On 7/18/2024 10:36 AM, John Miller via Chat wrote:

5.  IDXC is NOT the place for a flea market:  Most important reasons:  (1) Competing with Exhibitors time.  In the past, vendors were upset that attendees were spending valuable time in the parking lot instead of being in the Exhibit Hall talking with them; and (2) messes with the program, as attendees are coming and going in the seminar rooms, interrupting presentations — instead of adhering to the published hourly format.

Plus since the I in IDXC is International, it means many will not be a local and travel (or shipping) for the item/s becomes an issue quickly. The bulk of those attending are not new hams; they've built their station and have established some cred.

There are other events that can be used for flea market. Or a side list created for such posts, the exchange being completed at IDXC; which would not take a large block of time or many from attending other activities. It also allows the noobs access to cheap parts they could use.

The RV spaces (dry camping) in the parking lot, probably no longer (safe or) possible, are missed as well, but could attract more attendees who do not wish to spend the money on a place to sleep in a costly hotel. Yes, parking is problematic everywhere and each space is large enough to hold a tandem bicycle and not much more (I often use two spaces for my full sized truck; my acceptance of stupid has limits).
