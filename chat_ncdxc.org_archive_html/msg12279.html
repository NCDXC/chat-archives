Saw this in the Economist this week.  A new system for specifying a location to 3m accuracy anywhere on the globe.  Check it out at what3words.com.

The Mongolian postal service has already adopted this method, as has a London delivery service.  I can appreciate the need just because our address gets you to our mailbox, but gives no clue as to how to find our house.  Now I just have to say that we are at mulls.overgrown.refresher and a guest can easily find us.  

Watch for this to become a new data field on QRZ.com, right below grid and lat/long.
