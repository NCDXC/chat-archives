The CQ WW contest made for a lot of DX opportunities and I enjoyed the time I spent on the air.  I heard a few of you in there calling some of the DX I tried to log.  I wanted to fill in some slots and did the following:
 
110 QSOs
33 QSOs with entities never worked on CW for a given band
67 QSOs already logged but not confirmed for CW for a given band
10 'back up' QSOs, to ensure I get a confirmation with, say, JA on 10 M.
 
In between, I was able to get another DXCC endorsement application in process on Saturday evening with the kind help of Andreas, N6NU.  99 now ready for ARRL to verify.
 
Andreas also opened my eyes quite a bit in terms of remote DX applications and how to more fully utilize the fine tools in DXLabs Suite.  I may have missed the EU openings on 40 M Sat night but watched Andreas casually work 40 M DX from his Mac book and G5RV.  Awesome!
 
Too bad this weekend had to end but more will come soon!
