Greetings DXers..

I just wanted to send a note to let you know I will be presenting on remote DXing using the Flex radio platform.  If you have been curious or wanted to learn more about the Flex 6000 series, its remote capabilities as well as its overall functionality this would be a great meeting to attend.  It is similar to the presentation I gave to the NCCC last month with updated content for DXers.  I will also bring a Maestro for Flex 6000 unit if you would like to take a close look or get some hands on.  Its not overly technical and should be quite informative for you.

For those that are unaware I have been evaluating the flex 6000 series for about a year now, serve on the Flex 6000 Alpha test team and am actively preparing to deploy a flex 6000 series transceiver to the K6LRG hilltop station.

I hope to see lots of NCDXF members there so I can also get a chance to say hello to my DX club friends in person.

73 and hope to see you Thursday!

Chris
