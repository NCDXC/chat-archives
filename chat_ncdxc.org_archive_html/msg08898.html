At 22:20 1/12/2015, Jim Brown via Chat wrote:

On Mon,1/12/2015 8:42 PM, Dave Gomberg via Chat wrote:

What would anyone recommend for use as noise cancelling headphones?
My shack is not the quietest and I figure this might help a lot with intelligibility. 

What headphones have you tried that don't work for you?
Yamaha CM500, often loud environmental noises (leaf blowers, amplifier fan, etc). Frequently I am trying to pull speech out of low S/N signals (I am lucky if S/N is 5 dB).
What sort of noise in the shack? I find the isolation of Sony MDR7506, Yamaha CM500, and Etymotic Research in-ear headphones are all plenty good enough for anything but guys talking around me. I contest exclusively with headphones for CW and SSB, use speakers for digital modes, casual DX chasing and casual operation. 73, Jim K9YC
