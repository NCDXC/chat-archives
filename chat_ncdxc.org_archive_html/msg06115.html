Two major scores are reported in the DX Ladder: DXCC and Challenge.
Challenge, and the band slot columns, report only current entities,
and were added to the ladder last year to help level the playing
field.

One of the goals of the club is to encourage DXCC (including
Challenge) award achievements.  I don't think we should ignore the
most widely recognized award.  Should the DXCC award be different?
Perhaps, but I am reporting what it is, not what I wish it to be.

Should I add an additional column for total current entities?  That is
a possibility, if I reduce the font size.

How do I feel about deleted entities that I will never have?  Even if
deleted entities were eliminated from DXCC, there will always be
others ahead of me.  But I enjoy the challenge of working new
entities, chatting with other DX hounds, etc.  I'm having lots of fun.

73 de Phil, W6PK
