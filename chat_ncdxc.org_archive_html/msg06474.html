On Apr 14, 2014, at 11:33 AM, Paul Ewing <paul@n6pse.com> wrote:

Hi Rich, I enjoy your blog.
 
I think it is still possible to make #1 HR, however not likely within the next ten years. As you write, US F&W is not issuing permits to access Kingman Reef. My group has just “thrown in the towel” on trying to get a permit to access Kingman Reef. I believe that ultimately Kingman will be visited, photographed and once properly documented it is likely to be deleted.
 
The situation in the DPRK remains very complicated and at times volatile. My group has made several visits to the DPRK in the last year. We now understand the situation there very well. Recent purges (and executions) within their Government’s upper echelon further complicate things. The DPRK has many needs but their desire to control every aspect of life in the DPRK prevents anything currently outside of the norm.
 
Some entities such as Bouvet are probably too expensive to be activated any time soon. The NPI is open to the idea of a DXpedition to Bouvet but a vessel and helicopter would be required. With expensive DXpeditions to Tromelin and Heard Island announced and other entities in the planning stages, the financial “well” might just be tapped out at the moment. Time will tell.
 
I believe that at some time the US F&W will grant permission to access KP1 just as they did for KP5. It may take some good old fashioned lobbying, political arm twisting or perhaps a lawsuit to open that door. The best of the best are working on it and their techniques are tried and tested.
 
So I guess the answer to your question is it still possible to make #1 HR depends on how long you live,
