Forwarding from another bay area ham group

 

Morning all,

 

Sending this as a "I would avoid this guy" with his postings.

His name is Dennis and states he is a veteran wanting help getting his ham license.

He has been posting the same ads on Craigslist and Nextdoor since last October.

Over and over the same "help me get my license" ads on both places.

Many, many replys to him from people with advice and he is still posting.

I replied to him with advice and he responded "can I come by your place and see your equipment".

 

A red flag in my head went off right away.

I will let new hams or old hams come over and see radios and play radio at my place after they have their ham license.

 

I sell things on Craigslist but always meet at the Safeway grocery parking lot, not my house.

 

Anywho, I informed Sal last night on the SanJoseSimplex ragchew about this guy.

Sal had told him to come over and "see his equipment"

After me talking with Sal, he told the guy he wasn't available today and Sal said Dennis "threw a tantrum over texts".

 

So, my advice, avoid contact with this guy.

He might be sincere, but it is not worth the chance that he isn't.

 

 

Tom
