On Friday, July 17, 2020, 3:44:23 PM PDT, Mike Flowers via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:


I would venture a guess and say that the DX is trying to attract the attention of stations who may be transmitting ODD, then those transmitting EVEN by alternating the time interval in which he transmits.

I have experienced this myself calling, let's say, calling CQ DX as EVEN for many cycles and receive no reply.  I then start calling CQ DX as ODD and then often get a response soon after.

There are two cycles going on in FT8 - the EVEN and ODD cycles - and the DX is attempting to be decoded in both over a few moments by changing his interval, I think.

My $.02 on the topic ...
