As I have hinted, the DX Keeper is PICKY and has found every flaw in the ADIF of other apps. 
 
Postel's Law, also known as the Robustness Principle, states, "Be conservative in what you send, be liberal in what you accept from others"

This is why Internet protocols (SMTP being one)  work as well as they do.

+ Furthermore, DXKeeper provides "ADIF Style" options that enable successfully importing QSO records from 13 different logging applications that have taken exceptional liberties with ADIF - like exporting frequencies with two decimal points because "some radios do that".
