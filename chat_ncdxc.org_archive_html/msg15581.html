Interesting topic-

I’ve loaded up vertical rain gutter downspouts at two locations. One, at my old San Leandro mid-floor apartment using the balcony railing as a counterpoise, and the second, in Anchorage,AK using the metal flashing (@ground level) around the corner of the townhouse as a counterpoise. 

Both worked decent for cw and jt65a on 6-60.

The Anchorage BP51 qth downspout doubled as my 10m beacon antenna as well👍
