N1DG did a great study of DXpedition funding. The results we all know - US stations are the major funders of the big DXpeditions.

The question I have is with the too much emphasis of ClubLog's LeaderBoard by EU stations. Do the LeaderBoard guys actually contribute to DXpedition costs? The LeaderBoard and CQ magazine DX Marathon competitions do not require QSLs. 

It would seem dumb for a DXpedition to make the LeaderBoard chasers happy when the actual funders are left with nothing.

Steve, K6UM

It seems obvious to me that a top 10 or maybe even top 20 activation would get more donations if they concentrate on giving out the most ATNO’s possible. From my own personal experience, the amount I donate is much higher for an ATNO than a band slot. I donate ahead of the activation, and then add more if I make an ATNO.

 

I think most first get their ATNO, and then donate. Some probably donate through clubs and foundations (which reminds me – I need to donate to the NCDXF as well) instead of direct – I try to do both.

 

What do you think?
