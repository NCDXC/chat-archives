I am now sold on the 1/2 wave antenna appropriately placed 1/2 or so away from the beach..  quiet and super low takeoff, great SNR on even the weaker sigs.  I learned some valuable antenna stuff today.

Setup time-- 10 minutes, 10 meter fiberglass mast on a stake, with a pvc stand for stability.

Breakdown, 5 minutes 

What a great way to put the KX2 to work.  That antenna was so efficient I never ran more than 8 watts and was totally killing it, farthest was Spain and was effortlessly working the east coast.  Batteries held up well

Big thanks go out to Kimo KH7U, Eran WH6R(what a great person.. seriously cool.) for hooking me up with Rob, W7NX/KH6 (actually all these guys are super cool dudes) for demonstrating this antenna and loaning me one for the day!

What a blast ..  maybe life isn't too short...  (on a Oceania beach with a half wave vertical)
