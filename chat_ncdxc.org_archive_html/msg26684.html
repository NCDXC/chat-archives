Just an observation. From your screenshot, it appears that you are using a WAE SSB (Worked All Europe) log within N1MM for “general” logging. There IS a general logging module in N1MM, called “DX”. WAE is especially odd since it is a unique brand of contest, where you score points by exchanging log entries (called QTC) with other stations. Plus, US stations in WAE SSB can ONLY work Europe, so if you try to work anyone/anything else, the callsign will be grayed out in the entry window, and you will have to do an “override” to log the (seemingly in error) QSO.

Try the “DX” log for general logging (or a general logging program; N1MM is really designed for contesting).

Oh, and Russian/Belarussian stations these days are desperate for QSOs, since many hams (including myself) are boycotting them until Russia gets out of Ukraine. (Easy for me to say/do, since I currently have 5,954 QSOs with Russians/Belarussians in the log—really!) But I don’t have anu problem with others having a different opinion. And of course, keep it up! There’s a LOT of QSOs you can make with a modest station. Just be patient and listen, Listen, LISTEN!

(Sidebar: Belarus (Belaya Rossiya) translates as “White Russia” (no racial overtone intended, that’s just what it is). I prefer a Black Russian (vodka and Kahlua), but there is no country “Chernaya Rossiya”.)
