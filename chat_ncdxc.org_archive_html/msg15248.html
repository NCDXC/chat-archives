I was trying ot break through the pileup for Baker Island, KH1/KH7Z when someone started transmitting (in CW) "KR6DD" over and over on the DX's frequency. Well, I know that call. It is Andy Korsak's, a piller of Palo Alto Amateur Radio Association, an Official Observer, and all around good guy.
I thought either he screwed up of someone is pirating his call. Well, I got ready to send him and email, but the I thought I could get through faster if I caught him on the N6NFI repeater. I fired up my VHF rig and the first voice I heard was his. I reported the issue to him in time for him to fire up his HF rig and listen for himself.
He mentioned that some people had been trying to besmirch his reputation, the details of which I didn't follow very well, being anxious to continue to try to contact an ATNO. I left it for him to handle and got back to the pileup. 

If you were annoyed by this DQRM, please don't blame Andy.
