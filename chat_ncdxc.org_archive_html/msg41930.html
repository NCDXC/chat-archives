I can see where having multistream DXPeds on the standard frequencies would be a problem, especially on crowded frequencies.  If they limited to single stream, or maybe two stream that could alleviate some of the problems and it would still allow casual users the chance to make the contact and maybe learn about DXPeditions and get into chasing DX.  Personally, I didn't know about DXPeditions until I made contact with one on the standard FT8 frequencies and now I'm up to 166 DXCCs worked.  Having it on the normal frequencies will give them a higher potential QSO count, but make it harder for those of us working an ATNO.

I can see the pros and cons to both approaches.  I think available time on target and the goal for Qs made would be the most important factors.
