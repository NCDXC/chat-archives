You should be able to take it to your local post office and trade it in for 1 forever international airmail stamp.  That being said, The US post offices no longer sell IRC's, but are supposed to honor them,  They may however not know how to enter it in their computer.  Mine had to do some research to figure out what code to enter.   

But another thing to consider, is just sending it on to some DX station who you want a card from.  it is cheaper than sending  The $2.00 that many request.  I have a couple of friends who have gone on some dxpeditions, and get them in the mail occasionally.  I but the IRC's from them to use for my qsl'ing.
