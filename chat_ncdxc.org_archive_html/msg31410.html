My 160M CW came through!  It wasn't as solid as I prefer, but I had a good feeling about it.
I'm pleased with the work that was put in here, rehabbing the SteppIR.  A rough (pressure filled) time, but weeks like the past couple, with 2 ATNO and maybe (didn't count) another 100 or so band mode slots filled; worth it!  AND before the snow (unlike prior years)!
For the moment, a functioning station (the jury is out on the operator however).
If w8s opts to get back on 40 phone to work NA again (alarm didn't wake me, so I fixed that), that and 80/160 phone are the only three slots left that I need (except 60M and more RTTY perfect for dessert).  Sadly (or luckily with conditions) they won't offer 160 phone, the last slot to complete the entire entity sweep (with one team too).
With some (deserved) grumbling from callers, the w8s team has provided some opportunities (but no crowd control or much zone filtering, it has been a free for all).  I managed to go from ATNO to 24 Clublog confirmed slots.
I HAVE written the pilots about the RTTY frequencies (20M for example, right in the middle of the FT4 default window, chaos was the result, JA's everywhere hi hi).  I suggested an updated band plan posted on their web site, so keep a watchful eye.
Yes, I'll set the DX alarms for another night...  But sleep would not be a bad thing.  If I can remember how... 

GL all, 73,
