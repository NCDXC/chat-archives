QST:

Here’s YOUR opportunity to be a part of the 2017 International DX Convention Team, without forgoing the Convention Experience.

I need two sets of people (you can be in either or both sets):

(A) Around 5-6 people to stuff registration packets. This will happen on Saturday, April 15th, between 9 am and 3 pm. Lunch is included. Location is The Monkey House, (aka K6MMM QTH), 21885 Bear Creek Way, Los Gatos. A beautiful drive up to the Santa Cruz Mountains on what is always a lovely spring day. You can even put a winning raffle ticket in your own envelope!

At this time, I have two volunteers, besides myself. We need a few more, and it’s always a lot of fun!

(B) Around 10-15 people to staff the Registration Desk in Visalia. Registration will be open from 3 pm until 5 pm on Thursday (NOTE: That’s a day earlier than usual), 11 am until 5 pm on Friday, and then from 7:30 am until 11 am on Saturday. If you can offer a couple of hours, please help us out here. The “rush hour” usually starts at the opening bell each day, and to a lesser extent late Friday afternoon. The Registration Staff are the first people that convention attendees see, and we want everyone to have a GREAT START to their weekend, so only folks with happy, smiling faces need apply.


If you are willing to do any (or more than one) of these jobs, please contact me directly:

mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com

Please, PLEASE, PUH-LEEEEZE do not reply to the reflector!

For registration desk work, please let me know which hours/days are best for you. First come, first pick of best times!

NCDXC *always* gives everyone the best DX convention experience. Please help to be a part of that.

Rich KE1B
