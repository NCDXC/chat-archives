NCDXC friends,
 
Tomorrow, K3LP, N6PSE and N6HC will be conducting a special event and VE/Licensing exam session at Dorothy Grant Elementary School, in Ontario California as K6DGE.
 
Approximately 48 students will be supervised on three stations and we hope to work you!
 
The kids get really excited about getting on the air and making contacts both near and far.
 
48 students are slated to take their license exams following the special event.
 
 
You can find more information at these URLs: www.k6dge.com  and www.k3lp.com
 
 
Hope to see you on the bands!
 
 
Paul N6PSE
