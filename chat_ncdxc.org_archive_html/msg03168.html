I’m a big fan of both SteppIR and Elecraft. I’ve spent thousands of dollars on their gear. Both companies are highly innovative and provide superb customer service. I’ve experienced bugs and technical issues with both companies products.
 
As someone who made a career of implementing and upgrading major software systems, I know that no software/firmware/hardware is perfect. I consider all software to be in “Beta” because there are always anomalies, bugs and patches required. Because software is developed by humans, it can never be perfect.
 
Neither SteppIR or Elecraft provide weekend telephone support. I am confident that Steppir will give Garry-NI6T the support he needs on Monday once he calls their support line. I’ve used that line frequently lately during the construction of my DB36 and I was well supported and very impressed.
