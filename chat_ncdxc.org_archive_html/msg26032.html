Good Afternoon NCDXC...
Here is a copy of the announcement I submitted this morning to the DX Bulletins... Check out my new website, k5we.com . Or go direct to the Chatham DXpedition page k5we.com/zl7-k5we . Hope to put many of you in the ZL7/K5WE log... 

--------------
ZL7 - Chatham Island - Jeff, K5WE, will be active as ZL7/K5WE from the settlement of Kaingaroa on the north coast of Chatham Island beginning 9 September through 21 September, 2022. He will operate all bands 160 through 10 meters using the CW, FT8, FT4, SSB, and RTTY modes. Equipment will include Elecraft & Yaesu Xcvrs, 500 watt amps, a Hexbeam and a Crank-IR Vertical. QSL via Clublog OQRS or K5WE. For more info go to https://k5we.com/zl7-k5we .
