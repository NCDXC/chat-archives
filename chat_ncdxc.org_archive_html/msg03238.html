On 9/18/2013 10:26 AM, Chris Tate - N6WM wrote:

-I will only call if I can copy the DX station properly.
W6GJB rang my phone to get me out of bed about 1430Z. I staggered to the shack, turned on the radio, fired up the big amp, and listened on 40 phone. What I heard was a station buried in the noise, getting called by sixes, and working JAs, who were a LOT louder than anything else. I turned off the rig and went back to the kitchen to brew my shot of caffeine.
90 minutes later N6ML had spotted him on 20M, not very loud but solid copy, and mostly working EU. I called for a while, took a break with the rig on, and returned a half hour or so later when he was a lot louder. Eventually I found the right spot in the pileup, and got him in the log. 

73, Jim K9YC
