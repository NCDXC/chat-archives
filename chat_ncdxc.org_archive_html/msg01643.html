Smitty is a hero to many -- a true visionary who created an amazing legacy for himself in healthcare, education and public service.

I remember speaking to him about this 2006 profile, where I learned about his humble beginnings rooted in India.  His parents were missionaries.  

http://www.paloaltoonline.com/weekly/story.php?story_id=1237

We've known him locally as the pediatric dentist who became the driving force behind the Foothill/De Anza Community College system.  But there was much more to this Giant of a man.  He served under Governor Ronald Reagan in the state health department, and on President Lyndon Johnson's Council on Health.

He was also the founder of Medishare International which redistributes extra medical supplies to third-world countries.

http://www.medishare.org/

I'll miss seeing him at our meetings.  He was always the best dressed ham I ever met.

RIP, Smitty.

73,
John, K6MM
