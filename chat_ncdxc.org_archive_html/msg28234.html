If you have a CW or SSB QSO that is not on the log, I'd encourage you to fill out the log check form on the M0OXO OQRS website. Just click "Not in Log?".  I did, and got a response in less than a day. It was not the response I had hoped for, but it was nice to know that someone had checked carefully for a misheard call sign. Please note that this form is the only way to request a check; they will not respond to emails. They have also stated that they will not respond to inquiries regarding FT8 QSOs.
