As we are at the Autumn equinox, I think the best bands and times will be 20/30/40 meters at our Sunrise and again at our sunset. I would be checking both short and long path depending on what kind of antenna you have. 

Solar conditions are less than ideal but a contact on those bands at those times should be possible.
