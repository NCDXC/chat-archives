I’m with you, Jerry.  Had high hopes of working the Z6 today, especially this morning on 40 0or 20 but it was not going to happen.  Conditions dropped big time from just 24 hours earlier.  Barely heard the signals on 20.  But on 40, they came on about 7:30 AM and called for USA.  They could not hear well and only the best signals made QSOs.  Quite frustrating for both sides, I’m sure.

 

I did hear ZC4A calling CQ on 40 CW but my amplifier is not instant on.  By the time I was ready, he gave up and left due to nobody calling!  It was too early.  He left and never came back.

 

I worked 4S7 AB with 50 W as my consolation prize.  Kamal had an outstanding signal.
