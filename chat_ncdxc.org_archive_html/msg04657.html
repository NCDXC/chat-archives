I would very likely donate $100 upfront and then $20 when I submit my QSL request.

One thing I've learned is that hams have a big emotional block if they feel they are being "charged" for something that should in their mind be free.  Case in point, take a look at the Club Log bureau card survey results. 

I feel that we have truly crossed the tipping point and I think large mega DXpeditions to ultra rare islands may very well become much less common than they are now.
