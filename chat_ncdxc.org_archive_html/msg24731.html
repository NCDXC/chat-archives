Congratulations to all who made it in the log of Monk Iakovos. 

Robert, having been to Mount Athos, I can tell you that with all things related to Mount Athos, "it is complicated."
Of the 21 Monasteries on the Holy Mountain, some follow the Greek Orthodox Calendar, while others follow the Julian or Gregorian calendars.
These calendars dictate when the Monks must fast and when they can rest. 

The best source of information I have found for when Mt Athos is likely to be active is Bernie W3UR's Daily DX.  He often prints when activity is about
to take place and more often than not, he is correct. He is well "connected" to the Holy Mountain. 

All the best and Merry Christmas to all,
