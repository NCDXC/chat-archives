Yeah right...

Question in choosing the meeting location should not be where most people live.  The question should be "how far are you willing to drive?". If the answer is 2 miles, to get most people will be some cluster of members somewhere. If it's 100 miles, the best place moves significantly to the center of membership (Bay Area in whole). I think we're now in somewhere in 5-10 mile radius as the answer (from current members) but I think the right answer should be somewhere in 20 mile range ... this would move the place much further north in Penisula or East Bay... e.g. Palo Alto or Fremont area.

Poll: If you put a poll up and ask where should we meet, of course people say close to their work or home. Poll would already be biased to current membership so question should be if we want to serve current membership with the meetings or do we want to attract new membership with it. That would determine who should you poll.

Livermore has been mentioned few times but I would not go that far immediately. I'd rotate meetings in circle of Santa Clara/Sunnyvale/Mountain View-San Mateo/RWC/Foster City-Fremont/Union City to serve membership coming from different directions and attract new members from those areas.

Driving: believe it or not but there is public transportation available, too. Caltrain and BART works very well. Buses run, too, but not that well and you need to know their routes and timetables much better. If meeting location would be a block away from Caltrain station a lot of deep South Bay members would very easily be able to use it.

Outlaying area: we're already meeting in edge of our membership concentration... Just look at the map. http://w6rk.com/ncdxczips.jpg.

NCDXC != NCCC. True, but both claim to be "Northern California". One has took that seriously, another not.

Chicken-egg problem: of course we have cluster of members in Deep South Bay ... because that's where meetings are. Rotation would bring many more members from wider area. Yes we might lose one or two but we would certainly bring more from wider area.

Holder's Inn only place: you must be kidding. It is not only place in the Bay Area with suitable cabinet, OK food, adquate parking, etc we need. I would not be worried on losing it.
