On Mar 5, 2026, at 5:12 PM, Gregory J. Miller via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

In HamSpots there is the ability to see PSKReporter spots FROM the DX station. If you start seeing 3Y0K reporting what it’s heard then it’s probably a SLIM since they don’t have internet connectivity. 


I don’t believe that’s true. They set up a Starlink antenna, and have (perhaps limited) Internet. They don’t deem it reliable enough to enable Clublog Livestream, but they do have connectivity.

When they went there two years ago, we actually got one of the engineers at StarLink (a ham, not surprisingly) to authorize repositioning a satellite (or two) so that they could cover Bouvet. They did this while the DXpedition was just starting, and deployed it within a day or two. Nice to have friends in the right places.
