Thanks to those who responded!

In my googling (scary that my spell checker recognizes this as a verb!) for a solution I came up with BV Version 7 by DF3CB.

http://www.df3cb.com/bv/features.html

This Windows program allows log imports to be batched up and QSL cards OR labels directly printed.  The program has been around for a long time (1989!) and works fine under Windoze 7 – I haven't (and won't) tried Windows 8.

BV has a ton of features including the ability to include up to 18 QSOs on a single card, sort the print order of the cards so they come out in the right order for the BURO as well as a nifty designer for configuring your own labels or QSL card design.

Although I haven't tried QSL cards, the different laser printers to which I have access cheerfully handle card stock that is as thick as QSL cards – I don't know what impact having gloss on one side of the card will create – I'll have to find some cheap postcards for this test I guess unless anyone has left over QSL cards that are now obsolete?

Anyway, figured a few folks would be interested in the update.

73!
