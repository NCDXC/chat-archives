Hi NCDXC fellas,

Things are running at full speed here at XZ1J. We now have five stations
QRV. Moving our SSB shack really solved our noise problem. 

Band conditions are very good, however we are hearing radar from China and
other strange things on the bands. There is a lot of illegal 10 meter usage
in parts of Asia.

The Internet is very slow and unreliable here. We are uploading logs each
night. Our electricity cuts off from time to time but has not been a
problem. 

The team is really excited and is working well together. We are all working
to satisfy the various needs for an XZ contact.

Please let me know what bands and times you are hearing us. I'd like to
compare your experiences to the propagations studies I have and to also
create awareness within the team.

I'll have some funny stories to share with you when I get back home.

I hope that we satisfy your needs,

73 for now,

Paul N6PSE
