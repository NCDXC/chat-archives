Brian,
      All of the QSOs, except the 17m one match what you have uploaded to ClubLog.  Try manually entering the date and time of your QSO in the box.  Even through we work in UTC, that is derived from your localized computer time.  If the QSO was during a transition time for daylight savings, you can check to see if plus or minus 1 hour from your recorded QSO is accepted by OQRS.  Also check in your ALL.txt file to see if you have a partial QSO that they logged, but is not in your log.  With many DXpeditions that run MSHV, they will log when they respond to your signal report and not wait for your RR73 or 73.  I found that to be the case with a few QSOs on one of the Banaba Island DXpeditions, where I had logged one completed QSO with them on my side on one band, but they had four QSOs with me on FT8 on the same band.
