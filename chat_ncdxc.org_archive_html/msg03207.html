Greetings to the club.

Wow that was exciting working XZ1Z this morning.  being someone who has maybe 20-30 minutes a day to chase dx, its quite gratifying to put an All time new one in the log!

so.. that was an unusual post sunrise peak on 40m this morning.  I know we are all excited to chase dx, but I wanted to point out a couple of items from the DX code of conduct:

-I will listen, and listen, and then listen again before calling.

-I will only call if I can copy the DX station properly.

-will call and then listen for a reasonable interval. I will not call continuously.

-I will respect my fellow hams and conduct myself so as to earn their respect

I realize that I have a high level station, and some may want to test their systems by trying to call over me and "beating me".  OK no problem. Ill let you work them and you can beat me, as long as I get a chance to call afterwards. Lets save the beating me for a contest where I am prepared to do competitive battle for a beer.  Keep in mind I am using 4-500 watts as needed and no more....  so if you blast over me with a Full legal linear, we are probably both gonna have trouble working the station.

Lets try and alternate calls and be respectful at least within the club membership.  I will try if you will ;-).

Just a general request and not targeting any particular operator.....  yet :-).

Chris
