When I was 10 or 11,  I used to listen quite often to Radio Moscow.  I loved to hear their sign-on tones.  I also liked to listen to Moscow Mailbag.  Their English was perfect; their facts were clearly exagerated.  But even as a youngster, I appreciated the fact that I was listening to propaganda and nothing else.

Unknown to my parents, I subscribed to Radio Moscow's publication.  When my father noticed that I was receiving Russian magazines, he was quite upset.  

Those were fun days.
