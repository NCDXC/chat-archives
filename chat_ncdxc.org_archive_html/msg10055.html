On Apr 15, 2015, at 6:18 PM, Andreas Junge <andreas@n6nu.org> wrote:

If you get the Steppir “Dipole” then it does not have a Transformer in it. If you repurpose a “Driven” element, then you may want to remove the built in 1:2 Balun. Multi element Steppirs are designed for 22 OHM, hence the 1:2 Balun. 

 

I am a happy Steppir Dipole user as well :-) 

 

73, Andreas, N6NU
