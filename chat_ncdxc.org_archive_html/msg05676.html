As long as they listen for the West Coast – and even call “West Coast Only” – like TO4E did, as well as some 3B9 and 3B7 operations have – then we will do just fine. I have worked everything (except FT/T and FT/G) in that area with relative ease, and as a little pistol.

 

By far – our morning Grey line will be the path where we can do very well – especially on 40, 30 and 20M – and LP.

 

Here is my full analysis:

 

http://ky6r.wordpress.com/2014/02/07/tromelin-2014-the-west-coast/

 

Before I gave my $100 donation – I asked if they would ask for West Coast only (like TO4E did after we asked the US pilot to relay that request), or if it would be more like FT5GA, and they were very aware of this problem and said they would ask for West Coast only.

 

I feel cooperation and friendship is the right approach. Vote early and often! Keep this thought alive in their minds – there is plenty of time to do so.

 

I remember sitting in the shack for 3 days with TO4E on 40M and 30M pounding in here, and NADA. After the pilot got their attention, everyone on the West Coast lined up and got their ATNO. It was one of a handful of times where a pilot really made the difference.
