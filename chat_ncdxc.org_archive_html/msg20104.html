QST

 

Last year I compiled a topics list for our monthly club presentations.  We’ve had excellent ones too including Mike’s from last night.  I’d like to renew that list and send out again for review  Some of you have emailed me ideas in the past.  Please, even you’ve done so already, I would appreciate a note for any topics you’d like to see presented or do a presentation.  Just a couple that came to mind last night from listening to the discussions:

-grounding and bonding, noise abatement, shack entrance demarcations

-tracking down EMI generated noise from outside the shack

-station remodels – like to see some to get good ideas

-stealth antennas

-any homebrew projects you would like to present?

-DX Etiquette: 

-how to get a QSL from 30 years ago

-DXing through amateur radio satellites

-Remoting your stations

-FT4

 

Also I know there are number of you that are also in other clubs.  Anything interesting there that you saw that we could bring here?

 

Thanks in advance.
