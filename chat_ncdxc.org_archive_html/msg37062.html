You should check out HamAlert too (if you haven't already). You can set up specific triggers for stations or dxpeditions you want to catch. It will send you a text alert on your phone when they are spotted on the usual sites. Even better, you can have it pull your missing dxcc's directly from ClubLog and have it alert you about those as well.
https://hamalert.org
 
I use both HamAlert and DXWatch as the latter will occasionally pick up things not covered in my HamAlert triggers.
