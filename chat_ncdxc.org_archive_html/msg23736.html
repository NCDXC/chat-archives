David, I too have a contact and I can only say what I plan to do and have successfully done many times:

1.  For something this rare I would send $10 (yes, green stamps).

2.  Us a secure No. 10 envelope to send

3.  Inside, use a No. 9 secure envelope, self addressed (don't forget to say USA), and in that envelop goes the $$'s, QSL, fold that flap inside so it doesn't self seal.

4.  Put the appropriate postage on it all and send (think it's $1.20 now?)

 

The only issue at this time is whether mail is being limited between Greece and USA because of Covid restriction.
