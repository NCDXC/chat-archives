I'm under the impression that all previous P5 operations lacked written permission.  In Ed's case he was there for an extended period so the ARRL probably figured he had to have some sort of permission to stay active for that long.  For a short operation such as Dom's my guess is that they would require written permission, or at least some photos showing him along with P5 officials next to the radio.

As to the prior comment on LoTW, I'm under the impression that a LoTW certificate won't be issued until the operation has been approved, at least for uncommon entities.  I know of one big dxpedition that requested a LoTW certificate PRIOR to departure and it took some strong-arming for the ARRL to send the LoTW certificate in advance.
