Well after I wrote last night, they did pop up on 160 (enough that I could hear them pretty well) and it wasn't on a self-repeating recording. But EU EU EU and in watching the cluster spots, NO ONE was happy since something is definitely wrong on site (they're not hearing much 40 and below). It wasn't for lack of attempts, I saw a lot of folks trying... Cluster spots posted showed them heard all across NA and most of western EU.
They've done well 30 and above, it's the lower bands that's the issue. I'm guessing local noise is the issue. I guess we'll find out later, they haven't spent any time updating their web page for news.
Tomorrow is their last day. While not extremely rare, I did put in some band slots into the log (never know when it could be handy). Except for 12 phone, which was solid (I thought) all log entries confirmed. Even the ones I thought shaky proved out, impressive. I'm good.
They've moved to 80 at their sunset (about 5 local here), but can't be heard for a couple more hours.
Who's next? Eritrea perhaps. That one will be pretty rough for a dipole. So has Ascension (ZD8D), but I managed a couple slots.
