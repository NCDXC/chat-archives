I was saddened to read in this morning's Daily DX that S21VU had passed away. 
He was one of the more active/enthusiastic S21 amateurs and he used LoTW. 

From the Daily DX:

S21VU, A.B.M "Tanvir" Hassan, passed at Bardem Hospital in Dhaka April
27 after a stroke on April 18.  He was 36 years old.  He was a
longterm sufferer from obesity and high blood pressure.  He got his
ham license in 2017.
