On 10/8/2012 9:16 AM, paul@n6pse.com wrote:
 
How did you do with the 3D2C DXpedition?    Did you get them on top-band?

Pretty well.  I had Conway confirmed on several bands from earlier trips, but needed 160, 80, and 30.   I worked them on topband running full power, on 30M with 200W, and on several other bands running QRP. Missed them on 80M -- didn't get up early on the day(s) that there were on 80M. 

 
 Was it fun?

Mostly, but it was VERY frustrating when some FOOL was plowing away on 12M CW sending jibberish when he KNEW he was sending jibberish.  I would like to have worked him, he was strong enough I could easily have worked him QRP, but I could not possibly have known if he was coming back to me.  Nothing but massive eqo could have caused him to continue under that condition, but he plowed away for hours. 

How do I know that he knew?  I worked them on 10M SSB with the sole purpose of alerting them to the problem, and the operator responded, "yeah, we know about it." 

73, Jim K9YC
