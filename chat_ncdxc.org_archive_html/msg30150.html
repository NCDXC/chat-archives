I purchased a BuddiHEX made by BuddiPole. The antenna itself weighs 9.5 lbs. I purchased it with the Mastwerks 7 meters rotatable mast and tripod, which weighs 10.9 lbs. You can fit both the antenna and mast system into a hard sided golf bag on rollers. I used it during last year's CQP for an expedition to Kings County and was pleasantly surprised at the performance. The price was a bit high, but it works great.

Chris with BuddiPole was having a special at the time I bought it. It came with the golf case.
