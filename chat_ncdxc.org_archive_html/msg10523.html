Thanks for all the phone calls and e-mails.  We are doing fine and have been allowed
to stay in our house from the start as we are just outside the burn area,

Our main issues has been power outage and smoke.  We were on our propane power generator
for sixty hours when the fire started.  The power lines from the distribution grid are destroyed.
PG&E has brought in a fleet of semi trailer mounted generators to feed 5K plus customers
until line repairs can be made.

Mandatory evacuation and road closures restrictions in Amador were lifted yesterday and we were able
to get to the house my father and I started building in 1969 and where my step mother now
resides.  No visible fire damage but major property loss within a mile.

Last night was the first that the fire did not advance and stands at 72K acres.
 
There is still major fire fighting and people out of their homes in Calaveras County.  Keep up 
the thoughts and prayers.

Ken-K6TA
