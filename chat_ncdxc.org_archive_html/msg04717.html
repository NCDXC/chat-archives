On 12/14/2013 10:53 PM, Rob wrote:
I was curious if there was a primary antenna that enabled you to work most of those 125 countries QRP.
I have a very good antenna farm, and I'm still working on it -- dipoles for 80 and 40 at 120 ft at right angles to each other, dipoles for 30M at about 40-50 ft, also at right angles, a 3-el SteppIR at 110 ft for 20-6M, three nearly full size verticals for 160M with lots of radials, and two reversible Beverages. I'm also arraying the verticals to get a few dB of gain in three directions. I have a 2-el wire Yagi for 40M at 110 ft and another for 80M at 120 ft for 80M. Both are aimed at about 70 degrees azimuth. I'm working at making the 80 Yagi reversible from the shack. 

73, Jim K9YC
