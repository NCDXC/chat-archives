On Jul 23, 2019, at 11:30 AM, DTucker423 via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

Hi Al,  Interesting question.  I have always considered myself as primarily a SSB op, but the log does not really reflect that.  Most of my contacts are from contesting, but I have actually been dong better at CW than SSB, especially in the solar conditions we have been having the last couple of years.  There is also less interference, profanity, “police” etc.  However I also have a large number of RTTY and FT8 contacts in the log as well.  RTTY of course from contests again, but FT8 is a new mode, where I can still find something new.  It is like starting all over with a new call. I am working all bands on FT8 with under 100 watts, And can even run it remotely while traveling, or even from the couch while watching a movie.  I can’t do that on SSB/CW or RTTY.  Long answer, but,  No  it is not because I don’t want to do CW, It is something new to do in addition to CW.  And with only a handful of DXCC entities left to work, it keeps the interest up.

I will note however that I do not consider my accomplishment of DXCC with FT-8 anywhere near the feeling of accomplishment I have with the other modes.
