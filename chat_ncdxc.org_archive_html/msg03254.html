On Sep 20, 2013, at 11:17, Garry Shapiro <garry@ni6t.com> wrote:

Hrane may find the Nature Conservancy a tougher nut to crack--they are not hospitable on Palmyra. And there is a DXCC issue.

The status of Kingman itself is questionable, since the Navy gave Kingman back to USFWS, which is part of Dept. of Interior, not Defense. Kingman has existed as a separate entity due to FDR's Executive Order in 1938 placing Kingman under the Navy, which had satisfied the "separate administration" clause in DXCC Rules. No one has tested that since the reef was transferred to Interior after my and N4XP's DXpedition in 2000. That is, without separate administration, Kingman should be merely a satellite of Palmyra, like Jarvis. I am as curious as anyone as to how ARRL will react.

I guess we shall see. And I suppose you will just think I am being negative, but I assure you this has been an issue. Hrane should cover that issue before he hires a boat.

Garry, NI6T
