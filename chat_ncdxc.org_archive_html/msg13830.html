On 8/20/2017 2:05 PM, Bob Lanning via Chat wrote:

They all say FT-1000.  Yaesu never put a “D” designator on the radio.  The only way I know you can tell is to see if the External Bandpass module with its PL-259 and rotary switch is plugged into the rear panel on the right side of the radio.  The Sub receiver is right next to it inside the cabinet.

 

Internally I believe the “D” model came with 7 of the 8 possible selectivity filters.  The only options were for a 2nd 250Hz filter and the more stable TCXO-1.
