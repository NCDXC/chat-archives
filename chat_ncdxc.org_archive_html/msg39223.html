I used to do it for many years, but no longer. Now my Antennas for the IC-905 occupy that space. I sold off my FlightAware stuff at the electronics flea market maybe 2 years ago. I have a writeup on my website with some interesting things I had captured using the Raspberry Pi (v2 at the time I think). I had it mounted outside in a water-tight box as there are a lot of losses at 1090MHz. Given I am 10 minutes from the airport, I could see the planes taxing at SFO even and see close to 150-200mi out to sea from pacifica. I do recommend a filter and a better, aftermarket antenna or build one if you have the time using 75ohm coax. I did both, pretty close performance. The FlightAware dongles outperform the cheaper receivers.

 

It’s fun, let me know if you need help getting going.

 

Lucas W6AER
