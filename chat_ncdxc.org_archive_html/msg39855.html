On Sun, Jun 8, 2025 at 4:40 PM KE1B via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
Anna W6NN passed away earlier today as a result of complications from a myriad of symptoms that had her in the hospital ICU for most of the past week. Some of you may know that she battled (successfully) with both breast and brain cancer a number of years ago. Her current problems were unrelated to the cancer. I was with her at her passing, which was quick, peaceful, and painless.

Unable to communicate verbally because of an inserted breathing tube, she expressed her final feelings and wishes to me by tapping them out in Morse code!

Anna’s list of radio accomplishments are extensive, including winning the very first Rookie Roundups in all three modes, holding the all-time record for NCCC Top YL in the California QSO Party, and literally dozens of contest victories as part of the K6MMM Monkey Team, and numerous Sweepstakes gavels as part of the Pizza Lover’s 259 (PL259) local radio club. She and I also operated Holiday DXpeditions from five continents, and she held 14 different DX callsigns over her 14 year ham career.

She could run a pileup in any mode, and in numerous languages.

During her retirement, Anna spent most of her time as a professional violinist, performing in five different orchestras, and being the founder and Managing Director of her own orchestra, the Nova Solisti Chamber Orchestra. Her last public appearances were on Friday, June 16th and Sunday June 18th, where she performed to a sold out crowds in Mission Santa Clara and Palo Alto. She entered the hospital a week later, after fighting her illness at home for as long as possible.

As per her wishes, there will be no funeral or memorial service. A celebration in her honor will be held at some future date.
