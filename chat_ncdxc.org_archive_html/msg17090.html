Hi Al,

I realize many would consider it sacreligious, but I’ve been happy with Baofeng HTs for 2m repeaters and EMCOM. I use an 8w UV82-HP in my car as mobile will mag mount, and a standard 5w UV82 as HT. The basic radio can be found for under $50. 

They are no-frills radios and lack signal strength indicators (but do support 70cm as well as FRS & GPRS frequencies). Programming manually is painful, but easy with an optional cable and free Chirp software. 

73,
Jim WU6K
