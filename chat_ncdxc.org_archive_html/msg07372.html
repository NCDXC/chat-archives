       Downloading DXCC Credits
Clicking the*Download*button at the bottom of theDXCC Credits Granted <http://www.dxlabsuite.com/dxkeeper/Help/Progress.htm#DXCC%20Credits%20Granted%20window>windowwill download your DXCC Credits from the ARRL's web site. 

 *

   This function requiresyour LotW account to be linked to your DXCC
   account
   <http://www.dxlabsuite.com/dxkeeper/Help/Progress.htm#Linking%20your%20LotW%20Account%20to%20your%20DXCC%20Account>.

 *

   This function requires you to haveSpecified the correct DXCC account
   number
   <http://www.dxlabsuite.com/dxkeeper/Help/Configuration.htm#DXCC%20account%20#>

     o

       If you have only one DXCC account, your DXCC account number is
       most likely 1.

     o

       If you have more than one DXCC account, you can determine the
       correct DXCC Account Number by usingthis procedure
       <http://www.dxlabsuite.com/dxkeeper/Help/Progress.htm#Determining%20your%20DXCC%20Account%20Number>.
Downloading your DXCC Credits can take awhile, depending upon the number of DXCC Credits to be downloaded, the current performance of the ARRL's servers, and your internet connection speed.At the completion of the Download operation, newly downloaded or updated DXCC Credits will accessible via the DXCC Credit Display, and a report will be displayed showing

   New DXCC Credits granted since the last time the Download function
   was invoked

 *

   Changes in previously downloaded DXCC Credits
