Wire is cheap; SteppIR's are expensive. Not everyone has unlimited space or financial resources.

I have never owned a tower or a rotatable 40m antenna, and I am at the bottom of a canyon.I have 336 counters on 40m with two wire antennas---a horizontal dipole and what I call a delta/delta bisquare, which has a few dB of gain over a narrow azimuth. I don't crush pileups and I don't win DX contests, but I do usually get through. Persistence pays. OTOH, if you have the space and the $$, have a blast.
