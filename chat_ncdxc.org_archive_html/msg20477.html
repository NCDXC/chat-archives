I appreciate you both reading my email, as well as your comments. Rick, there is no question I’m overloaded. My setup is identical to that of the owner of JK antennas, who admitted to the overload, but reassured me his Identical mast and identical  double antenna setup had functioned flawlessly in the Connecticut winters for almost ten years. 
Yes, it makes me a bit uncomfortable and if I can’t get the system to rotate in a less dramatic fashion, I may remove the dipole.
