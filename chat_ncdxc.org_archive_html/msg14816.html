Hello NCDXC friends, 

As you can see on the DXpedition website, the 3B7A team is well on their way to Saint Brandon: http://www.saintbrandondx.com/en/track-us-in-real-time/

They have posted some propagation predictions on their website with a special focus for the US West Coast. You can see them here:

http://www.saintbrandondx.com/en/propagation/

Their West Coast Short Path predictions show that contacts might be made between 1300 UTC and 0500 UTC via the Short Path.

They also show possible Long Path predictions between 0100 and 1600 UTC
The predictions show that 17-30 meters may be the most productive bands for a contact. 

The Short Path Beam Heading is: 355 Degrees
The Long Patch Beam Heading is: 155 Degrees.

Good Luck in working the 3B7A Team!

Paul N6PSE
North American Pilot for 3B7A Team
