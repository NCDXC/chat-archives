I found the offender!

 

It’s my Cisco Cable Modem. The K9AY Loops are maybe 15 – 20’ away from where the cable modem is, and my shack is on the other side of the wall from where the cable modem is. As soon as I unplugged it, the noise curve completely flattened out, the difference was amazing. I also took a portable Sony IC-7600 SWL RX tuned it to the low end of 160M and WOW – overload / hum city when I put it near the modem. I’m going to walk around the house and with that RX and see what else I can find. Heck – check the PG&E pole out front too (that has a transformer on it).

 

The 63’ vertical is about 70’ away from the cable modem, and it does not show that noise curve on the panadapter, and so this is why it worked this morning for E51NOU.

 

Luckily, I get up at 3 or 4 AM each morning now for 160M – so unplugging the cable modem for 160M Q’s won’t bother anyone else in the house. And if I really need the cluster – I have an app for that on my Smartphone.
