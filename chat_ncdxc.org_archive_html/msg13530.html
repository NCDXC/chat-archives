This year's convention was another great one, but
unlike previous ones, I didn't pick up on any
rumors of DXpeditions.  The validity of Bouvet
seems to have been confirmed, but nothing was
heard about Baker Howland.  Various groups last
year were staking this one out, but now I'm not
sure.  The CE0X DXpedition doesn't seem to be
happening (it was supposed to be this month).

On a related note, the Bouvet gang was looking
for donations.  Before donating to them, I
would like to ask if it would be better to
donate to NCDXF and let them give the money
to the DXpedition.
