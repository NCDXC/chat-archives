Hi Jim, 

My contacts with Greece on the high bands have always been just after our sunrise or at or around our sunset (grey line)
Most of my contacts are on 17, 20, 30 and 40 meters. A bell weather signal as to whether there is propagation is Mike-SV9CVY who is very active from Crete. His signals are huge!

Right now at this point in the sun spot cycle, you can work Greece on 30/40 meters without too much difficultly. This fall during the CW WW DX Contest, Greece ought to be common and relatively easy with the exception of SV2ASP/A which is never easy. 

Good luck, 

Paul N6PSE
