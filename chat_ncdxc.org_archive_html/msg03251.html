I happened to see 3B8/G0TSM / Mauritius spotted on RTTY on 17m
yesterday and this fellow had just arrived at his hotel.  He had
written that he simply threw a wire out his hotel window to see what
he could do until his antennas arrived and get them put up.  I worked
him and I show up in his Club Log Expedition page this morning.  He
replaced the "wire out the window" explanation with what I pasted
below, but I think I did pretty good from inside the valley of the
Altamont.  I know he only had 100 Watts but still, I think I did a
pretty good job.  "Bertha (my old amp)  doesn't do 17 meters".... All
I could decifer from his RTTY reply was a bunch of strings of digital
garbage, but I did see a "BM" within the strings of reply and I went
for it with my reply and BINGO for another ATNO.  Yesterday was also a
good day showing up with a confirmation of Zorro, XZ1Z on 20m CW for
another ATNO...what a great feeling to have a double header.

73 de Bill, N6BM
