I’m enjoyed CW now for over 60 years. There’s no question that CW demands more of my aging biologic CPU than SSB, or FT8. I can see a future where AI is not used to fully automate DX chasing…although it would be trivial to have AI driving my station in any mode, but that wouldn’t be any fun at all. 
I’d like to see AI based programs using propagation forecasts, beacons, and real-time band population to guide DXpeditions and individual ops in optimum band selection. DXps have limited resources as is, and AI assistance in optimally allocating those resources could make life richer for all of all. 
AI could easily triangulate slims using PSK reporter data. I’ll volunteer for the Vigilance Committee! The possibilities are endless. 
As far as figuring the timing and frequency of my CW call in the pileup, or the choice of FT8 calling tone, I’ll do that myself. The sense of accomplishment is why we DX in the first place. If lazy ops start using Ai to bypass fundamental skills, they’ll be some humorous way to fool their machine. Think traffic cone on the self driving car’s hood! Imagine the fun!
73. Bruce K3NQ
