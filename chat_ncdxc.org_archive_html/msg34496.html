Greetings!

 

If anyone is interested or wants to re-experience it, I have put up a new video with highlights of the CQ WW WPX SSB contest as seen and heard from my shack. This was 5+ hours edited down to 45 minutes.

 

Some have asked about the software. It is SmartSDR 3.7.4 for FlexRadio 6600 and the DX cluster plugin is Stacks3. It was fun, though I only made a fraction of the usual number of contacts due to recording and only causally contesting this time around. Please visit here to watch if interested: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qI8DcJarI1A
