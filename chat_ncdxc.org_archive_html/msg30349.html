Selling my Yaesu FTDX3000  and SPE Expert 1.5K-FA, plus doing some long overdue shack cleaning. I’ve gone full Flex and need to free up space.

SPE Expert 1.5K – FA  $3500
Purchased new in May, 2018 . Final MOSFET replaced and service performed by Bob Hardie in November, 2018. Has seen very light use since, mainly just utilizing the built-in tuner in bypass mode. No issues, smoke free home. Still installed and available for demo. Wired for 120V (just change plug for 240V). Includes all original packaging, travel case, manual, Dan Tassel interface cable for Yaesu FTDX 3000.

Yaesu FTDX3000  $800
With XF-173CN CW Narrow Filter (new in box, not installed), DVS-6 Digital Voice Memory Unit (installed) and FH-2 Remote Control Keypad, Radiosport headset/mic cable, original packaging and manual, schematics, hardcopy of the Yaesu Technical Supplement.

This has been my HF station transceiver since getting back into ham radio in Feb, 2017. I purchased it used, original owner HRO purchase receipt dated Nov., 2016 included. No issues, smoke free home, still installed in station and available for demo.

LDG YT-1200 autotuner $100
Purchased new in March, 2017. Replaced in May 2018 when I added the SPE 1.5K-FA with built in tuner to my station.

Elecraft KX-3, PX-3, accessories $1500 (KX-3 used, everything else new in box)
On an impulse while attending Pacificon in 2019, I became very interested in portable operation (maybe some SOTA or POTA). I found and purchased a lightly used KX-3 with upgrades (I don’t remember which ones, knowledgeable individuals will be able to identify upon inspection) in original packaging, then proceeded over to the Elecraft booth to purchase a PX-3, KXPD3 Paddle Keyer, canvas travel case and a copy of “The Elecraft KX-Line The Complete Station” by Fred Cady – KE7X to go with it. I also picked up a Bioenno 3Ah battery and charger, a QRPworks SideKar Plus and Tecknet wireless keyboard.

My impulse never developed further and I’ve never used any of this equipment.

W2IHY Audio Gear $500

-W2IHY 8 Band EQ and Noise Gate
-EQPlus
-iPlus Audio Switch 
Includes power supplies and cables, original packaging and documentation. I purchased these new from W2IHY with all the cables needed to interface with a Yaesu FTDX3000, ICOM 7610, a Behringer Mixer and computer. My plan was to configure the system to easily switch microphone and headset between the two radios and computer. Of course, plans change. I sold the 7610, and the Behringer Mixer fell short of my expectations.  I verified all units function with the FTDX3000 then put them back in the boxes and placed them in a closet. They are virtually unused.

3 McElroy Streamkeys  $50 ea

Kent Straight Key  $60

Vibroplex Standard Iambic Key  $80
