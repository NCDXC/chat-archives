Paul,

I think that the St Peter and Paul Rocks DXpedition was amazing.
Remember that these guys had one tent (that leaked) on a hunk
of plywood less than a meter out of the surf breaking over the
rocks. They lost a radio and a PA. They kept awake by chewing
instant coffee when their coke and chocolate ran out. The finally
got a little extra wet sleep after they strung up a hammock under
the scientific station. An yet they still were very good ops thruout
the entire DXpedition. Gimme a break. These guys are DX animals.

Even though SPSP was not a new one for me it was a very fun
DXpedition to work. Assuming my 10 meter cw Q is in the final
log I got them on 10 band modes.

john k6yp
