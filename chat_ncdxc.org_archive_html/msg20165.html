Mike,

I've mostly been off the air for the past few years, but before then I climbed the steep CW learning curve as an adult, which I think is a very different experience than most of my CW peers who had been operating for decades. I found the journey fascinating and most people were very encouraging.

I collected a bunch of notes and resources and observations about that process here:

http://www.dimebank.com/cak/k6dbg/k6dbg_cw.html

I'm sure there are some more modern resources and programs that have emerged since I did that.

VY 73 de chris K6DBG

Check out FISTS – they have slow-speed activities:  https://www.fistsna.org/operating.html

 

Google up ‘slow speed nets’ – they are a bunch.

 

7125 is an old favorite frequency of mine.  In fact, I still have my 7125 crystal from my novice days that I used to work my first DX – Scotland!
