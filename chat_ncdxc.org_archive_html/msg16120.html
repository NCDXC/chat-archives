My own policy is to keep separate logs by location. I do most of my operating from Los Gatos, CA with significant operations from Peterborough NH during my visits there. My CA log shows 169 confirmed with 135 CW, 112 digital, and 68 voice. VP6D will add one to each of these.
My NH log shows only 53 confirmed, not enough for DXCC. All my operation from NH is QRP, and most of it is PSK and FT8. However, the better access to EU probably has given me some entities in NH that I haven't managed to contact from CA.
When I upload to Clublog for the DX ladder, I send them all the contacts from both logs. I justify this behavior by noting that the rules for DXCC say anywhere in the DXCC entity. Sometimes the rules help you and sometimes they hurt you.
