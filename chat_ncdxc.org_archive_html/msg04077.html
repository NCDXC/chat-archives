Greeting.
I am pleased to report that yesterday after nine years my SteppIR came down.  Thanks to the efforts of Al N6TA who did the climbing and Larry W6OD who handled the work on my roof it was down by noon yesterday.

A couple of comments on the condition of the beam. All the rubber element connections were broken. Some had pieces missing.  One element had come out approximately one inch. The new brand connectors should do better.  The elements had many areas of white with exposed fiberglass on the upper side.  The silicon tape on the joints was gone in some cases and partly off in others. some were still intact.  The new polyolefin shrink tubing will be a big improvement.  The control cable insulation was hardened and broke easily with  movement.  There were a few areas with insulation breaks where the cable had been bent..  The control cables to the individual units seem to be in better shape since I mounted it under the boom where it had some protection from the sun. There is a problem in the EHU for the driven element which started this operation.  I haven't opened it to find the problem.  It will be replaced by 30-40 meter driven element. If anyone has any specific questions e-mail me.

Dick NATD
