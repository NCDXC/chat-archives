Robert has been a beneficiary of the generosity of DXers before. Back in the 1990's, a cyclone struck Rodrigues and destroyed Robert's modest station--and heavily damaged his home. He was also dealing with a son with serious medical problems and did not have much time for radio. Life was challenging for him in that period.

When a group of us including Jackie 3B8CF activated 3B9R from Rodrigues in 1999, we invited Robert to join us, which he did. I noted him sitting at an operating position with tears of joy streaming down his face. We left him with enough equipment to reactivate 3B9FR after we left, which he did.

The renewal of his station by NCDXF/NCDXC is deserved and a wonderful thing.

Garry, NI6T
