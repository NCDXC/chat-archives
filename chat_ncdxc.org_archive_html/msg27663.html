On 2023-01-26 09:46, Rick Bates, NK7I via Chat wrote:

Hi Bob,

I don't have (or need) a geochron, but I use a (55") 4K TV as a shack computer monitor and that works very well.  It was cheaper than a monitor only with the bonus of being able to use it for other things (haven't, can) by simply switching inputs (Dish Joey).

Costco has sales this time of year to clear old stock, which is often last years model anyway, so check your local outlet/s; not online.  The 55" (easy to see, plenty of real estate) cost me ~$300 and was so cheap I bought another for a bedroom.

You may find that the 26-32" is a dead zone for 4K (unobtainium), so I'd suggest the geochron display be secondary to whatever other need/s you may have.
