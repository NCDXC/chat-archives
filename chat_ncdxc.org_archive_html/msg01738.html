Here is your NCDXC Question of the Week:
 
What could make the NCDXC a better club?
 
 

It's a well run club and organizationally it's pretty good. We have a great mailing list to stay in touch between the meetings. 

 
What would make the club more valuable and interesting to you?
 

I think that clubs need an event or activities to glue the members together. The NCCC is all about contesting and points as well as strategizing for the next contest. For PAARA (Palo Alto Amateur Radio Association) it's the Field Day. The "50MHz and up" group is all about getting microwave rigs up and running and then using them in contests and activity days. 

In short anything that gets me out of the house and makes me meet like minded people in person. 

Oh, organizing Visalia every other year almost counts.
