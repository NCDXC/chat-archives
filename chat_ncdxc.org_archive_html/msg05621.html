Leaderboard is a pox.

Garry
On 2/14/2014 7:06 PM, Steve Lund wrote:
Leaderboards have little effect on the large DXpeditions with more than 100k Qsos in the log, but a very big effect for smaller DXpeditions.

Take a look at 1A0KM - over 70% of the 22k Qs were with EU. More EU stations worked them on 7 or more bands than there were with zone 3 stations - a single Q from zone 3 got one on the Leaderboard. There is no way to make a valid claim that the Leaderboard did NOT have an effect in this case.

My bigger question is whether the Leaderboard gang is carrying their weight in supporting DXpeditions. There have been multiple DXpeditions to FR and FO/A in the past few years. The same folks are on the top of the Leaderboard. Clearly, they don't need the country for any DXCC purpose. It is just an ego boost.

So, why do DXpeditions need to do this? VU7AG eliminated the Leaderboard, but kept the online logs. That keeps one from needing to make insurance Qs.

So, put me down as one who is still waiting to be convinced that there is any value in the Leaderboard.

Steve, K6UM
