Last night was one of those "magic" nights for the low bands. I worked the A82 guys on 40 SSB well before our sunset and they were loud on 160 CW immediately after our sunset. 

Sometimes, I leave my rig set on 1.840 FT8 just to see what is being decoded while I am out of the shack. 

Well this morning, I saw that I could have been working a ton of EU stations that were being decoded.

With Fall season here, it is now low band season!
