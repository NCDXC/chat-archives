I just wanted to send a brief note to that the Flex radio Maestro is officially shipping today with its initial set of features.  There were quite a few contesters including 3 NCCC members/NCDXC members as well as NR4M and K9CT on the Alpha team representing the interests and features needed by contesters and dxers.  Many of our suggestions were implemented and many more to come.
