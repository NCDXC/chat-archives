Hi Paul,

Good question.  I was happy to work TT8TT on 15 & 17 phone and 30m CW.
 Yesterday I tried calling the 3B9SP OPfor over 2 hours and followed
him all through the band as he worked only the loudest stations.   I
wish I had a working amp and I would have gotten him in my log for
sure..

Today LOTW showed I had finally logged 100 CW QSO's (sans cards) so I
applied for the DXCC cw award.  Learning CW over the last two years
was fun and it sure has paid off with picking up new DX.

73,
Bill, N6BM
