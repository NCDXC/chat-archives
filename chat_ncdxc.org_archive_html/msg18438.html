Last night, I left my station on receiving 160 meter FT8 while I was asleep. I like to see what my station heard while I was away. 

Very interestingly at 1344 UTC 9K5HK (Kuwait) is calling CQ. His initial signal is 14 (not -14)

His second call is 10 (not -10)

His signal strength is higher than most of the local W6 stations. A Bazillion W6/W7 stations call him but he does not seem to work anyone. 

QZR has no listing for a 9K5HK.

Must have been a slim.
