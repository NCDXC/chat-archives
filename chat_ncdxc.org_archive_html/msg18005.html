I have a Honda EM5000 (or something like that) and I can tell you it is NOISY. I ran it for about 7 hours last week (daytime only) during the power outage - it is so loud I couldn't run it with good conscience during the night. I'm now looking into a stationary backup generator that will be powered by natural gas. As Jim S. stated, prolonged power outages may be the new norm (and we seem to have more than our fair share at our QTH), I was thinking I'd probably sell the Honda after installing the new backup generator, but I saw a news report last night that has me reconsidering. We may lose our natural gas supply if there's a major earthquake, so the gasoline powered gen may end up being the backup to the backup!
