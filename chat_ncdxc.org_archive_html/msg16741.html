If anyone is interested, I completed the first 100 DX contacts on 160 meters last night.  A station on the online chat saw I was stuck at 99, the band cooperated a little and Greece crossed the finish line for me.

Then this morning, the Philippines made slot 101.  It was a pretty good opening, I heard China as well but wasn't heard.  Hong Kong (worked yesterday) was also respectfully loud.  I saw others working Indonesia and Asiatic Russia as well.

So far, 81 are confirmed in LOTW, a few will require mail or $$.  Some CW, mostly FT8 (the computer hears better than me these days).

It was a fun winter project, I started with 6 entities before I moved here.
