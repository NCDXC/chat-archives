Hi Dave, 

10-12m, try 10-11am ... 
15-17m, try 2-3pm and then again at our SS, they've been showing on 17m at our SS with decent sigs
20m, try 3-4pm
30-40m, try after our SS, in the 6-7-8pm window

Worked them CW on each band at about the times listed.
On 10-15-20m, with at 3L yagi @ 25' and 1000w
On 12-17-30m, with a ground mounted Cushcraft R7000 vertical and 500w. 

Their pileups have faded, so you should be able to punch through. 

Good luck.
