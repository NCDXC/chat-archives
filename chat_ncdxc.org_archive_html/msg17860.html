+ This API will get you your DXCC award credits:

<https://lotw.arrl.org/lotw-help/developer-query-dxcc-credits/>

+ DXLab's DXKeeper logging component has the ability to download your DXCC award credits from the ARRL and update your logged QSOs to reflect those credits. It takes existing credits into account when generating applications for new DXCC awards. I implemented this function years before the above API became available; it logs into your ARRL DXCC account, downloads your "award credit matrix" in HTML, and extracts the credit information from the HTML. Here's the "how to" description:

<https://www.dxlabsuite.com/dxlabwiki/ManageDXCCCredits>

+ There's more information in the reference documentation:

<https://www.dxlabsuite.com/dxkeeper/Help/Progress.htm#Managing%20DXCC%20Credits>
