On Aug 2, 2017 5:21 PM, "Bob Wilson, N6TV" <n6tv@arrl.net> wrote:
Many of you, as ARRL members, received an email letter from ARRL President K5UR urging you to encourage your Senators and Representatives to vote in favor of the Amateur Radio Parity Act (S.1534/H.R. 555).

Based on email from attorney K1VR, copied below with permission, I plan to do the opposite. 

The bill, which I once favored, has since been revised significantly just to satisfy HOA demands, and now it could do far more harm than good, giving HOA's rights to restrict antennas, backed by Federal Law, that they never had before.

I've posted Fred's Dayton PowerPoint slides, in both PowerPoint and PDF formats, here:

http://www.kkn.net/~n6tv/Dayton_17_Legislation_v5.ppt (1.5 MB)
