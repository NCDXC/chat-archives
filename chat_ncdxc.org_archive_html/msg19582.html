All –

 

For future reference, NZ5F/B on 50.0705 mhz is a solar powered, off the grid 6m beacon that runs continuously (unless I am on the air).  We don’t have beautiful mountains like in CA or PNW but we are failry high up in hill country Texas.

 

The Spring Es season fueled by Stay at Home / Shelter in Place orders is fueling 6m activity.  So far in past ten days worked 6 new band entities (both SSB and FT8) getting my number up to 49 worked and 40 confirmed in LOTW.  Several new grids and a few new ones on SSB (preferred mode), and I am chasing other confirmations.  T

 

here has been great activity early in the season in Nor Cal too.  I was jealously watching all of you work Hawaii, and I think one or two of you worked even further into the south Pacific.  I have Hawaii logged from DM12lo but not from EL09pn.  It is a fun band when it is open so please join in the fray on the Magic Band.  I know it is much more challenging in W6 than here so I feel for you all but there are some major big time 6m DXers in W6 that have DXCC with no digital modes so it is possible and with FT8 it opens up new opportunities for hams who are not total 6m diehards.

 

Have fun this season and don’t forget to spot NZ5F/B if you hear it.


Thanks & 73,

 

Keith

NZ5F
