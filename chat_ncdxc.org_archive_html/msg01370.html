Being a 6m operator it fills in the other 12 hours of the day????

Oh, and it continues the quest of listening to more noise.

Much like 6m you spend a lot of time waiting for those few minutes to complete a Qso. 3D2RX was just above the noise all night, but about 30 minutes before sunrise the signal started building until it was 25 dB above the noise. Enough time for a Qso.

And at least so far there are far fewer tuner-uppers and frequency cops. I'm sure that will change just as it has done on RTTY.

Most antennas are homebrew. My next project for the band is a receive antenna(s). With a barefoot K3, I would hear much more than I could work. The recently added KPA500 has improved that.

Steve, K6UM
