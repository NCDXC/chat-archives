After a few years without a single Holiday DXpedition (thank you, COVID!) Rich KE1B abd Anna W6NN will be returning to their favorite spot, Anguilla, operating as VP2EAQ and VP2EAR from May 24th through June 1, 2023. Operation will be on 40 through 10 meters (and 6m, if conditions are good), CW, SSB, and FT8/FT4 (normal mode, standard frequencies, no F/H, no MSHV), Holiday-style (if my CW is ragged, blame the Caribbean rum!). Rig is a K3S (100W), with a Buddipole vertical directly adjacent to salt water on the Caribbean.

It may not be the rarest of DX, but if you need some obscure band/mode slots, I can fill them in. I will have full Internet access, so feel free to post requests on the cluster (when we are on the air) or via email.

I expect good conditions on 17 through 10 meters, and will work some oddballs like 12m FT4. I will operate primarily CW and Digital (and maybe a little SSB); Anna will work primarily SSB (that YL voice is worth about 2 S-units!) 

Look for VP2EAQ in the CQ WW WPX CW contest, May 27-28.

Of course, we're always looking for the West Coast, and will self-spot as needed (particularly on SSB). Skimmers will spot on CW during the contest. We hope to work you from Anguilla!

Rich KE1B
