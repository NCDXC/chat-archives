I find the "Find Call" tab on the LOTW website to be very helpful in
deciding when to send a QSL card.  It shows the date and time of their
last upload.

Some stations that indicate LOTW "ok" at QRZ.com are not listed at all
on LOTW!  Others have not uploaded logs in years.  In that case I
either send a QSL card or find another station to work, depending on
which is likely to occur first.

Some stations upload very infrequently, and I've noticed that some
contest stations upload around the time of the next contest.

73 de Phil, W6PK
