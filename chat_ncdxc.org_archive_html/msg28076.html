Some notes from this latest video for those who shun (with good cause, I get it) FaceBook: 

3 meter waves with snow.  Resupply attempt Sun-Tu improved weather window; worst case QRT in a few days, if possible will extend.

One tent on the island, operating and food cooking (sleeping presumed), sitting on the floor, no heating, have warm clothes, dry the wet clothes outside, wind dry.

Will try 10M if they can expand the camp, have a 10M antenna (today would have been THE day); hoping to bring another generator on the island with the supplies, food is running low.

Beach has ice (800' from camp).

-=-=
Nothing else was discussed, NO mention of uploading/transferring logs.  Not a word.  In watching the winds from here, they are no longer lee but have the full force of the winds (20-30 knot, unpleasant).

I'd suspect they're DREAMING about a hot shower aboard the Marama at this point too.
