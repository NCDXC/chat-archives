As I pointed out to KR, I think it is the system that has broken down – it requires a lot of promising to get funding from many different groups, so it leads to overpromising and underdelivering – and inherently to “miscommunication”).  They probably did do just about what could be done with that island and if they had said “We’re going to make 17000 QSOs including dupes and whatnot” they would not have got the funding but if they had, no one would be complaining.  Perhaps WE should think about how the system might be changed to make the funding easier to get (in some respects) and harder to get (in other respects) and the communication more clear and honest and make it more of a one stop shop rather than an endless series of smoke filled rooms.  It is fantasy to think that any group can go to Bouvet and work 200k Qsos on all bands for three solid weeks.  Ain’t gonna happen.

 

Well – if you used a R-I B it could happen I guess as long as you could keep the batteries charged.
