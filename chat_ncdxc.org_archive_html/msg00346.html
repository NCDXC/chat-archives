My most challenging entity over the last 10 years is probably the Glorioso Islands.   There have only been a handful of small operations there since 2000.

The last "large" operation was FT5GA in 2009 -- and it wasn't very large....just 5 guys in the French military doing double duty as ham radio operators.

How did I work them?  In my pajamas.  Seriously.

I sat down the morning of October 4, 2009 and didn't move until I had them in the log on 15M CW at 1900 UTC.  Also got them the next day on 15M SSB.

They confirmed via LoTW, but their QSL card is one I really value:

http://www.k6mm.com/dxcc/Glorioso%20Is/dxccfrg.html

73,
John K6MM
