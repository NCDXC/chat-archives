On 11/25/20 at 6:04 PM, chat@ncdxc.org (Paul N6PSE via Chat) wrote:


2020 is almost over, what was your best DX of 2020?
DX from New Hampshire in various forms. All have been LotW confirmed. QSOs from October 2019 were a KX3 with wire antennas: 20M 2 element wire beam and a 40M end fed on 15M. The 2020 contact was a K3 and a 40M dipole. I'm dipping back into 2019, because I arrived in New Hampshire in mid-October and it makes some kind of sense.
I have been much less active because my shack is currently about a mile from where I live and unheated to boot. It's really nice when you can walk a few steps from your kitchen and be on the air.
DL1QQ Germany on 10/27/19 on 20M during the CQ WW SSB contest. I had met Sandy at several Visalia DX conferences, and it was nice to find her on the air. I was running 5W in that contest.
VP6R Pitcairn Island 10/28/19 20M FT8 and 10/30/19 15M CW. A long hop from NH. Having an opening on 15M was a significant treat, and doing them with 15W or less is icing on the cake.
OD5ZZ Lebanon 11/7/20 on 40M FT4. I'm finally getting some mideast traction. Being in the northeast helps.
There's a whole lot of Europe I'm not even mentioning,it's almost like shooting fish in a barrel, but I'm filling in some of the more obscure DXCCs there, like Northern Ireland and Wales.
