While not DX, the USS Hornet (Alameda) will be holding a special event commemorating the 45th anniversary of the splashdown/recovery of Apollo 11 and 12. The radio club, HARC, will be on the air between 1100-1600 and they will have a special QSL card for the event.

 

Some may be more interested in actually visiting the ship for this event. They have many displays and presentations planned. Astronaut Buzz Aldrin will be present. You can read more here http://www.hornetsplashdown.com/
