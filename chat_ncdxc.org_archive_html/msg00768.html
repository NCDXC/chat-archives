Those of you within easy range of Petaluma and having an interest in 160M might want to come up for their Jan 9 meeting. I've been asked to repeat the presentation I did at Pacificon, which pulls together a lot of very useful and serious work by others (most notably N6LF and K2AV, but also W8JI and K3LC. I'll be talking about compromise verticals and radial systems, some innovative and low cost ways of using ground screens, and k2AV's innovative "folded counterpoise, which is enabling a lot of guys to have fun on 160M. As usual, there will we just enough discussion of fundamental concepts to help understand how things work, what matters and what doesn't. 

There's a pdf of my Power Point slides from Pacificon on my website.

73, Jim K9YC
