I was ordered out Tuesday night. Still able to sneak in several times to load stuff but it was dicey last night and this morning.   Even scary but only saw flames last night on a ridge 1.4 mile away.  Thankfully, it did not burn down the 300 feet elevation and over to our side of the valley.  Small miracle but this morning, still very active and dense, dense smoke.

My thanks for those that have offered sites for data etc.  But especially those wishing luck and health.  They mean a great deal.
More tomorrow.
