On 8/5/2025 9:18 PM, Dave AA6YQ via Chat wrote:
+ I have no objection to the ARRL creating a new award that is "attainable"; it can be done without eliminating #1 Honor Roll has it
has long been defined, and long been pursued.
I don't recall anyone suggesting that #1 HR be eliminated, maybe I missed it.

Today, there are more than 10 DXCC entities that are not available to ham radio, and it's been this way for quite some time, as in years.
When it was only China and Albania, or P5 and EZ,  HR was still attainable.

I find it interesting that professional sports can continually modify their rules, yet the ARRL's DXCC can't be modernized.
It's a hobby, and one that's on a downward trajectory.

I do like the idea of an award for "attainable entities", which is the opposite of "Inactive" entities.

Over time it will eclipse HR, as HR becomes a distant memory.
