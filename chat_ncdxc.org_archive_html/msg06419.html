On 4/7/2014 8:18 AM, Paul@n6pse.com wrote:

How was your IDXC 2014?
As always, the highlight was hanging out with old and new friends. A close second were the VU7AG presentation, an IOTA presentation by NL8F about a daredevil expedition to a KL7 island over sea ice that almost wasn't ice, and descriptions by K9CT and K4UEE of the FT5ZM operation, especially the 160M operation.
Low spots were the almost total lack of a technical program (N6BV was the sole exception), terrible sound and mediocre projection in the room where DXpedition presentations were done, presentations that often put me to sleep because the presenter lacked even the slightest sense of stage presence, badly overcooked fish and beef at the banquet, and the incredibly dumb attempts at humor from the podium at the banquet that on the basis of past history I'm confident were repeated at the Sunday morning breakfast. The Wardster, N0AX, is the exception. On the basis of prior experience, I headed for Starbucks at 9 am Sunday, and hit the road at 9:30 am. If you ever see me sitting though one of those again, please shoot me. 

73, Jim K9YC
