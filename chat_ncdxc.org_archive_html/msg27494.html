I have been searching why the Echolink Node (N6NKT-L), connected to our club 2M repeater, get screwed up when I am transmitting on my HF Rig.  The problem does not always appear and that is why it took a long time to possible solution.

After placing couple clamp-on cable filters to the Cat 5 network cable.

Our NCDXC Echolink Node is back online.  Last tested by one of our members from Hawaii.

Please allow for the repeater to drop the transmitter every so often to allow club members to connect via Echolink.
If you are unable to connect to Echolink, please drop me an email with your ham call.
