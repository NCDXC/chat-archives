My last ATNO was with Abubaker on 16 June 2016.  My card went right out along with a friendly donation to help his situation.

We kept in email contact and I let him know I was patient but that nothing arrived after a few months.  He said he had sent a card and said he would do so again.

Meanwhile, he arranged a manager and my confirmation was quick and easy.

 

Well, today I get two hand addressed envelopes with some exotic looking stamps from Libya!

One was dated 25 Sept 2019 and the 2nd 1 Feb 2017.

I suppose they got hung up there for quite a while.  Anybody else get a card from Tripoli direct and how long was it in transit?
