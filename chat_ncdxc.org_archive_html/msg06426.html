How was your IDXC 2014?

The Good...
- reconnecting with guys from previous events and updating each other
- meeting guys from recent DXpeditions, hearing about their experiences and challenges.
- new items at the exhibit, especially for my tower.
- Wake DXpedition presentation.  yes, I enjoyed it even after seeing once before.  My blessing to those Forgotten 98 and their family.  http://www.wake2013.org/pages/forgotten98.html
- much better Sunday breakfast layout, no waiting to get to the food and finding a table to sit at.  There were enough of food for everyone to even have seconds.


The Bad...
- almost no techie presentation.  I remember the days where I had to choose between DXpedition presentations and techie presentations of some sort.
- the exhibit hall had few vendors, room for many more and it closed an hour earlier Saturday than scheduled for some unknown reason.
- number of raffle prizes appeared to be greatly reduced from last year.
- unregistered people could purchase raffle tickets and win for the hourly prizes.



and The Ugly...
- "guess my call" is a waste of time.
- the raffle, took long time to verify the call of the ticket and calling the winning ticket with partial call signs
- not able to reserve a room at the Hotel where the event was hosted.

Bottom line, would I attend next year?  Hell yes.
