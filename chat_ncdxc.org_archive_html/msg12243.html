The Northern California DX Club (NCDXC) elected new Officers and Directors for the 2016-2017 term at its June 16, 2016 meeting.  Our 2016-2017 Officers and Directors are:

 

President - Ross Forbes, K6GFJ

Vice President - Tony Dowler, K6BV

Treasurer - Bob Lanning, W6OPO

Secretary - Art Rizzi, KK6WJZ

Director - Jim Sansoterra, K8JRK

Director - Rich Seifert, KE1B

Director - Lucas Ford, W6AER

Director - John Eisenberg, K6YP

Past President - Mike Flowers, K6MKF

 

The Northern California DX Club is in its 70th year of operation.  Addition information about the NCDXC is available at http://www.ncdxc.org/.

 

- 73 and good DX de Mike, K6MKF, Past President - NCDXC
