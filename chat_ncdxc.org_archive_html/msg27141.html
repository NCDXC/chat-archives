Fellow NCDXCers,

 

As a recent returnee to our great hobby, as well as to DXing, it’s been a standout year. First of all, I am deeply appreciative of the NCDXC fellowship. The sense of all being in the hunt together, but separately, is exhilarating. I greatly appreciate the spots, and have worked many new entities and slots thanks to other’s consideration.

Solar activity has ramped up, and the actual SSN and SFI curves seem to be diverging to a point above the predicted… even better news. The return of DXpeditions has my wife wondering why I’m staying up late, and getting up 3 hours before work. Although, as Paul observed, most of the DX is on FT8, conditions have improved enough that many recent DXpeditions have been notably active on CW and SSB. That is welcome.

 

My memorable contacts (and I’m sure I’ve neglected to mention a bunch) include:

3A/PB8DX and 3A/F6EXC for an ATNO

XP3A on 12 and 10 for some welcome new slots

The Russian DX guys at TY0RU, 5V7RU, and Z21RU who ran exemplary and disciplined activities… now if only Putin would leave Ukraine

SV2RSG/A on 20 and 40 for another ATNO, as well as many suspenseful weeks waiting (and yes, praying) for the QSL to arrive

FH/OK1M for another new one

JT1CO on 80 years ago, just as USPS service to Mongolia was suspended… I finally got the QSL thanks to Andy and Lucas and their Berkeley Mongolian connection

EP2CHD and EP2FAR both worked within 1 minute of each other in October. My QSL to the first’s Russian manager was returned as undeliverable, and the other QSL is possibly on its way to Iran, just as Iran is dispatching killer drones to Ukraine

3X1A on 10

VK0MQ on 17, 20, 30, and 40- Thanks, Matt! I’m glad the penguins didn’t get you!

OY/DL2JRM on 10 CW, and E51JD on 10 SSB.

Robert, 9N7AA, who does a great job wherever he is.

I’m sure there are many others, but it’s been a wonderful year.

I’m grateful for everyone’s help and support as I upgrade my analog brain.

 

73s, Merry Christmas, and Happy Hanukkah to all.

 

Bruce, K3NQ

NCDXC friends, 

 

What was the best DXpedition of 2022 and what was your best DX?

 

For me, there were several very good DXpeditions. J28MD was excellent and gave me many band fills. I also enjoyed the Italian DXpedition Team's TL8AA/TL8ZZ operation. They always do a really great job no matter where they go. 

 

Another noteworthy DXpedition is T88WA as I know their back story, one of hardship for sure!  They gave me some much wanted band fills including T88 on 160 meters. TX5N was very well done and reminded me of their previous VP6D operation. I worked them 10-160.

 

Special recognition as well to 3A/PB8DX and 3A/F6EXC who were worked from 15-80 meters via the short path. Never before has Monaco been so easy to work. 

 

TY0RU, Z21RU & 5V7RU were also fun to chase on the bands. 

 

My best DX this year was EP2C, EK1KE, 4L1CR, ZC4GR and 5A1AL all on 17m FT8 and SV2RSG/A (Mt Athos) on 20 and 40m FT8. 

 

Most of my contacts were via FT8 this year, because that is where most of the DX is!

 

Paul N6PSE
