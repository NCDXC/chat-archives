Hi SWL guys, 

 

I am working my way through this frequency list. (link below) 

The early morning broadcasts from North Korea at 11.710 USB are very interesting. Lots of music, news and propaganda.   

Early evening broadcasts from Radio Havana at 6.000 Mhz USB are very similar. 

 

Check this list out, most of these frequencies are active:  https://ccrane.com/shortwave-frequency-list/

 

Also, I highly recommend getting a subscription to The Spectrum Monitor magazine.  Ross K6GFJ recommended it to me and it is golden. 

Lastly, the 805 page World Radio TV Handbook is quite a detailed directory of radio and tv stations around the world. 

 

I'm looking through old CQ Magazines which list some active US Military frequencies.  So far, 11.175 USB seems to be most active and in the clear (not scrambled)   I hear fighter jets making a rendezvous with aerial refueling tankers on those frequencies.  The main traffic controller is "Mainsail"   . I also hear "Nightingale" "Stalker 276" "Titan 3" and others on frequency. 

 

Thats all I have for now, 

 

Paul N6PSE
