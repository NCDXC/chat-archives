CNSP-24 was launched Sunday April 12th and has just checked in and is now flying over an island in the Mediterranean on course for Italy and Greece. Here is the link to follow its progress.

http://aprs.fi/#!call=a%2FK6RPT-11&timerange=604800&tail=604800

I just returned from the International DX Convention in Visalia, CA so I missed making a couple of prediction updates but the outlook has improved and the balloon is doing great at this slightly higher altitude. We will expect a loss of radio contact as it proceeds further East into Russia and China.
