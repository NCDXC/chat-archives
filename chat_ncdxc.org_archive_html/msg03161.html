On 9/14/2013 9:27 AM, Steve Jones wrote:

My only comment is that email is the worst way for anyone to have a worthwhile discussion, especially when there are disagreements.


I don't see it that way -- it allows us to say what we mean to a lot of the membership and get a discussion going.

 Come to our next meeting at Chevy’s in Foster City this coming Thursday, and talk to us in person!  Face to face I’m sure we’ll all learn something.


For the record, I've attended every NCDXC event that wasn't at Holders -- I'm a regular at the picnic since I discovered it four years ago, and at Visalia. And when I posted last night, I'd forgotten other contributions I've made to the club in the form of group purchases of ferrites and connectors.

My objection to Holders has never been the food. While it's far from my favorite sort of cuisine, I can live with it. As to Chevy's in Foster City -- NCCC met there this past Monday for the first time since I've been a member.  I found the food mediocre, and the service careless. It was a buffet, the food ran out by the time everyone had a first (and limited by the courtesy of those in line) shot at it. It was a good 45 minutes until it was restocked, and by then we were well into our meeting.  I'm hoping we'll find something nicer in that area.  No problem with location, a mile or so off 101.

NCCC has had a half dozen or so meetings at the Cattleman's in Livermore (alongside 580, by the airport), and we've been pretty happy with them.  K6SRZ tells me that REDXA is NOT happy with the Lumberman's up their way.  NCCC recently started meeting at Sneha, a massive Indian  buffet just across Central Expressway from HSC. The food is good, spicy, and cheap, they treat us nicely, and we have their meeting room (not fancy, but quite adequate) at no charge. 

73, Jim K9YC
