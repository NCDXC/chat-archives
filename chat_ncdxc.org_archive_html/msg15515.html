Nice work for an ATNO!

 

I was chasing band-modes here.  8 Q’s on FT8, 7 on CW and 5 on SSB.  I worked zero RTTY even though I spent several hours trying to work that one op who was very slow.

 

You’ll do fine with FT8.  Once you make a few Q’s it will be second nature!  And to me, it’s much easier than RTTY ever is.  I expect this DXpedition will have set a trend toward less RTTY and more FT8 for future operations.
