Take the case of WW2DX. He recently worked a JA on 6m from a West Coast remote. That gets him DXCC on 6m.

But, the interesting question is what he will put on his QSL card. The JA won't know he is remote and might get credit for FN31 and NY when it should have been CN85 and OR.

The WAS and VUCC awards have much smaller geographical limits than DXCC. As an awards manager this puts more work on me to determine if the Qs are actually valid.
