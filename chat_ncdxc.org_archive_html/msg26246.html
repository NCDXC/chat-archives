Ken was a fine man. I had the pleasure of visiting him and Kay at their beautiful home in Pine Grove. Ken's home was on a ridge with clear take off angles in most directions. He had a wonderful tower and quite the array of aluminum in the air. He was on the DXAC and took his role very seriously. 

Ken was always one of the first West Coasters to work our DXpeditions and I could count on getting a call from Kay K6KO right after Ken worked us. 

One of few aspects of this hobby that I dislike is that we are all aging and we have to all too frequently say goodbye to our friends. 

RIP Ken,
