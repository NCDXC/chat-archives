Paul,
       Before I was a ham, and before I finished college, I worked for a company in the south bay that designed signal analysis solutions.  The piece of gear that I loved the most was the Ten-Tec RX-340 Receiver. Rugged, heavy and pretty much a piece of test equipment. It is at least 30 years since I played with one, and it was pretty advanced at the time.  Maybe it isn’t full of waterfalls and modern bells and whistles, but I dream about acquiring one for my shack some day.
