And one more thing -

The vendors need to move freight in and out was satisfied by having
excellent access to the two sets of 6-doors each on the south side of
the room.  They had direct access to the parking lot.

Having the vendor area swapped putting it on the other side of the
curtain wall would have given attendees great access, short distance to
walk, having doors from the main lobby and the exhibit hall lobby
available.  That would have been nice but didn't outweigh the kitchen
access for the banquet and the vendors ability to easily get in and get
back out.

We even had a plan rotating the space by 90 degrees.
