All-

 

My 2013 vintage Mustang software caused me big trouble recently.

 

It runs my SteppIR DB-42 on a SDA100 controller.

 

It just decided to up and restore all the SDA100 settings to factory defaults.  This switched off all my selected options, including my Remote Driver.

 

The antenna showed 10:1 SWR from 2 to 30 MHz, probably because the elements retracted.

 

With no Remote Driver I had no antenna!  All indications were fine on the SDA100 in the shack.  It took Jim Thomas at SteppIR to determine what had happened.  He told me that era of Mustang was unstable and he was surprised it had worked for 5 years.

 

If this happens to you, you can re-enable your options to get the antenna working again, but all custom settings will have to be reset.

 

If you have a PC running Windows XP you can download the latest (stable) Mustang software and install it.  But Windows 7 and 10 are problematic and you may want to get re-programming help from SteppIR.

 

Good luck!
