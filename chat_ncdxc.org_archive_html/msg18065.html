I hope NCCC, NCDXC, and MLDXCC members aren't affected for long by the PG&E "Personal Safety Power Shutoffs" (PSPS).  PG&E is currently running radio ads suggesting that you go to http://pge.com/psps, but of course that website was quickly overloaded and rendered useless.

 

Instead, try this one:

 

https://projects.sfchronicle.com/trackers/power-outages/ 

 

Click the "View Legend" button at the bottom left for an explanation of the colors being used on that map.  If anyone has a power outage that is NOT shown on the current map, that would be interesting to know.

 

Meanwhile, this will be a good test of your generators.

 

Good luck to all who might be affected.
