On Dec 3, 2013, at 7:32 PM, Rich Holoch wrote:

The VU7AG team is responding to requests, but they are weighing in the requests of all others. One thing they are doing exceptionally well is doing their best to hand out ATNO’s and also handing out ATNO’s to “Little Pistols”. (Of which, I am one).
 
The West Coast is actually now doing better than the Midwest – from Texas up through the Ohio Valley. East Coast has had their feast – that’s for sure.
 
EU has been “vociferous”, yet – when you look at their stats – they are WAY ahead of everyone. Clublog lays it out very clearly.
 
I have several friends – not on this Chat list and they have their first VU7 – and so – there are a lot of very happy DX-ers on the West Coast in this regard.
 
Sure – I would love 1 crack at low bands and high bands, and they did respond by adding 30M at our sunset. I have asked – when they can squeeze in the other bands – to do their best – at the right times, and as Garry said – he also gave his advice, and krish told me that Garry’s recommendations are in fact being factored into the new schedule.
 
So – I am sure they will try and juggle many different and sometimes conflicting demands. I cannot remember a DX-pedition that has been as “Open Source” as this one – where you can post on their “e-Pilot” (something I conferred with them about), and also email pilots (old fashioned style), and even make requests on eHam in the DX Forum.
 
I think these guys are great – and since they are all younger than me – they give me hope that there will be future DX-peditions and some up and coming great ops to man these rare ones.
 
73 – hope to see you at the Luncheon Saturday!
