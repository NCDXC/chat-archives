Rich et.al.,

Don't like the process?  Instead of complaining, join the organization that
can fix it.  The club needs people to nominate themselves NOW.  Get on the
board and institute change rather than sit on the outside and complain. 

The board has and continues to follow the process as defined in the bylaws.
Yes that document needs fixing.  Time after time there has been volunteers
to do so but then nothing happens.  This time action on changes is happening
and will be presented for the membership's approval or disapproval.  Get on

this bickering can be stopped.  Until then each board is stuck with what it
has.

the board is open for election - or occupant replacement whether they are
terming-out or not.
