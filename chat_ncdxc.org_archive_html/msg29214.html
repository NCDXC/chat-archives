Hello Everyone,

 

Volodymyr Shenderuk, KN6NAZ, got his US ham license two years after emigrating from Ukraine at the age of 75.

He has been working to get an HF station on the air from his small apartment near Sacramento, but he doesn’t know any hams in the area.

 

Can we help connect Volodymyr with Polish or Ukrainian speaking hams anywhere in the US, and maybe a club near North Highlands California?  

 

 

Volodymyr’s spoken English comprehension makes him reluctant to work phone contacts, but he writes well in English and has a good CW fist from over 50 years as a ham in Ukraine. He also works DMR with Ukraine, Poland, and Moldova because he is fluent in Polish.

 

Please share any ideas for casting a wider net to find help for Volodymyr and support a fellow ham in his new home.

 

Many thanks!

 

73 – Jeff AJ6LG
