Here's what I do, in a situation like this. 

1)  If I copy the station transmitting on odds, instead of evens, I find a suitable spot to transmit even, whether f/h or not, it has to be on the opposite slot.

2)  If there is a question as to f/h, I stay on standard ft8.

3)  If he responds to me, I respond back like regular ft8, staying on my original frequency.

4)  If he continues to send my report w/o a 73, then either he no longer hears me or expects to hear me on a different frequency.

5)  I then respond on the opposite slot ON the frequency he is responding to me.

6) If after the two tries I start all over again.

Seems to work for me in these situations.

I’m hoping we can get some clarification of this issue, so we all know what we should be doing going forward, particularly in light of the comments recently from the 3Y0J team regarding callers not using F/H mode.
