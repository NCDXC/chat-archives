We had several of these two element SVDA's at Socotra Island for 7O6T. I don't know who made them. They were on our hotel roof and a half mile from the Ocean.

As compared to my five band Spiderbeam, the SVDA performance was terrible. It would have been much better if we could have had them next to the salt water but that was impossible.

We used Hex Beams at ST0R in conjunction with my five band Spiderbeam. The Hex beams do okay but the Spiderbeam Yagi with three elements instead of two, is better.

I wish the EP6T team was using the Spiderbeam at Kish Island.

Paul N6PSE
