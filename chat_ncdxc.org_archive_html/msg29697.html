Working all callers is part of establishing "good will".  That is not a political statement either, it's part of what we (are supposed to) do.  Yes, I worked the Z6 before it was established as legit (THAT is politics) as I privately celebrated their independence.
Just don't pound on the door (continuous calling) while I'm actively calling the DX that *I* want hi hi.  That's a direct path to being ignored.
But honestly, there are so many UA/UR stations (like JA and a few EU) that are 'persistent' (put in the best polite light) that I work other callers first whenever possible.  I may/not return to answer them later, it often just opens a flood gate and creates a distraction from the targets I wish to chase (part of being in a less active state and a rare county of exactly 2 DXers).
The JA lately (becoming more aggressive the last few years) are a lot like the whitetail deer that are very abundant here in North Idaho; feed just one and 20 more appear from the trees; soon 50 are at the door.  I'm guessing that is part of them being so group (culturally) connected.
For the JA hordes, I may cut power in half every other transmission until the demand fades (and turn the beam).  That's as gracious an escape as I can manage without being rude.  If they still hear me at QRPp levels, they're worthy enough to answer, hi hi. 

Or I shut down or change bands to chase another entity (rude but real).

73,
