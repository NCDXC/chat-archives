ZD9W is running MSHV.  You can call normal FT8 or as a Hound. The advantage of calling him in normal FT8 is that you can call him in open areas of the waterfall
below 1000Hz.   Many of us have success calling in the 200-300Hz or 800-1000 Hz range for MSHV stations. Just don't call in his space on the waterfall. Leave that for the hounds who will give their singal reports there.
