I meant ham assholes, but yeah administrative ones too.
Rats (the rodent kind) for example, are very good at hitching a ride on just about anything. Every ship that goes to these remote islands must follow protocols to prevent introducing invasive plant and animal species of all kinds. We had to present a very elaborate plan on our controls when we went to S. Orkney. Braveheart went through extensive certifications to be able to operate as an expedition ship to qualify for landings. Among many other protocols, for example we scrubbed our boots and dipped them in a disinfectant bath before departing and arriving at the ship. It's a real problem. Humans spread seeds and creatures wherever we go. California is over run with invasive species from hundreds of years of plants and animals being brought here intentionally or accidentally.
Way more moving parts than people realize to pull off a successful expeditions. Which is why the bitching on DXSummit and other forums is so offensive and insulting to anyone that actually puts the work in to do one. 

Props to Yuris. He's done many solo operations to difficult destinations.
