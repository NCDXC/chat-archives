Just to clarify:  Unless I’m misreading the Trident Award application, there is no certificate option….just the Trident plaque for $95.

DXCC Item Order Form July 2025
PDF Document · 243 KB

It feels like this award has been created to partially recognize the fact that achieving Honor Roll (331/340 entities) is not going to be possible for young DXers.  The Trident Award with endorsement plates at 200 or 300 will be looked at as a more attainable high-end DXCC award going forward. For those who need a visual reminder of their DXCC achievements — the three-pronged Trident Award will look good on the wall.  For those who are happy tracking their DXCC achievements in a database — a simple no-cost Google spreadsheet works well.  Either way…stay motivated….chase the DX, be the DX, get on the air.
