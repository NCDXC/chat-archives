No one has posted about what happened the last time ARRL tried attacking HOA's with some bills. The bills were sent thru the sausage making machine. What they morphed into was a bill which only allowed 1 meter size antennas of the size covered by the OTARD rules. No flagpoles, no invisible wires, no nothing. This alchemy was engineered by Senator Bill Nelson, who was in bed with the HOA's, and especially "The Villages" in his home state of Florida.
I don't know who wrote the present bills, but I still am not seeing any explanation of where 43 feet came from. This doesn't make it sound like they were very well thought out. Many other red flags here.
I am also suspicious about how we must act NOW, with no explanation of why we couldn't have done this 6 months ago, or 6 months from now, etc.
