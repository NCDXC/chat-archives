It would take a complete rewrite of eQSL to adapt it for LOTW needs; while ARRL LOTW could be made simpler (without multiple transfer encryptions and complexity while maintaining security AND allow editing to correct errors if only to reduce server storage spaces and simplify/speed the database searches).  That cost of rewrite and the cost of the servers and timely access; pretty steep these days.  Who pays (in advance) for that?

eCards quickly get deleted here since they are not proof in any sense and can be spoofed all too easily (a basic photo editor and some imagination).  None of them have been print worthy for display, not a single eCard; delete!  (And I've gotten 6 from one station, for the same contact, ENOUGH already!)

I (like many) have boxes of cards but zero plans to ever move again (it's my kids problem to resolve eventually).  And it's always pleasant to get cards (or mail) from places I will never see firsthand.

I agree that team uploads to LOTW should be a basic requirement for DXCC credit, but as an income tool for expenses, what is a reasonable time delay period?

I 'donate' to support the teams and as a reward for efforts that I consider worthy.  And with the knowledge that my small amounts, add up.  I don't have the resources to pay a lot to all teams so I spread it out over the year/s.  I know full well that their requested amounts, BARELY MIGHT cover actual expenses as well.  So I keep my expenses, reasonable to share with more.

It is simple grace that any team upload to LOTW nightly while on site, without costs.  And rare.

Reasonable is a term much like 'soon'... it's highly variable and leaves a lot in doubt.  😉
