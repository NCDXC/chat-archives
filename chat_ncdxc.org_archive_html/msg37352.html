Hi Everyone,

Just a quick note to let you know that Eric Swartz WA6HHQ CEO of Elecraft
will speak at the November 21 NCDXC meeting. Eric will be discussing "What
is new at Elecraft" I suspect that he will discuss new products coming later this
year and in 2025 as well as up coming upgrades to K4 firmware. Certainly he
will address remote operation of the K4. This is a terrific opportunity to ask 
questions about your Elecraft equipment. If you are shopping for a new radio,
perhaps for Christmas, here is a great time to learn about Elecraft equipment
and ask Eric for information.

You can attend the meeting in person at the Wagon Wheel BBQ in Mountain
View (Google "Wagon Wheel BBQ Mountain View" for details and an excellent
map) or virtually via the NCDXC Zoom connection (The link will be sent out prior
to the meeting). The Wagon Wheel serves some decent BBQ and many of your 
friends will be there.

See you all there!

John K6YP
