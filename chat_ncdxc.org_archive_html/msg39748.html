He would lose callers because some couldn't decode him that wide; there is a 9-10 dB hit in signal strength in SF mode.  He's 3 streams now at +20 here; I could afford the dB hit but EU (many calling) perhaps not.

IF the station is heard LOUD, SF is no problem; he would lose callers because he could potentially work at a higher rate.

ALSO:
Due to a fluke in multistream, the full call may not be sent IF one stream is only sending to one station (not two).

ONLY when sending data to TWO stations in one stream, is the full call shown (or CQ).

This is probably by design, since no one could otherwise determine who <...> is until there was a CQ (not often sent).

LOTW demands the entry be EXACTLY as the other station sends; in this case kh7al/kh9 or it will not confirm the QSO.  kh7al is fine, if he's home in Montana, not Wake.
