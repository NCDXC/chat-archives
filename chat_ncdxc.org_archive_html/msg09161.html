On Feb 5, 2015, at 4:13 PM, Richard Courtway via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

A thought.  At 30-35 wpm , on CW, how long does it take for a valid contact ?  So - how many ATNO stuns are deprived a contact ?

I don’t think this is the issue…  its overall rate thats the question not the time for a single contact.  Rate is inversely proportional to the volume of stations in the pileup.  The more stations that go after band/mode slots in the name of DX challenge, the greater the pileup and the slower the rate - thus depriving folks of ATNO opportunities.  Moreover, I’ll bet that the majority of folks seeking band/mode slots are well equipped - high power and good antennas - so making it more different for the little pistol.

At one level, I wonder how much difference there is between a DQRMer and the sum of stations looking for band/mode fills?  The driving emotion is different but the end result is the same.

I’m noodling on the different impacts and arguments that result from both DXers and Contesters - interesting food for thought!
