haha 3 people asked privately what antenna so I thought I would share with everyone.

It's the Spark Plug Gear 100W EFHW that is usually for POTA/SOTA.  Not sure its ratio.  Could be 49:1 but I don't know transformer windings yet.

https://www.sparkpluggear.com/shop/p/the-bundle-starter-pack-sdw77

I set it up running East/West (N/S is broadside).  Last night I put it up and tuned it for 40m so it's somewhere around 66'.  I connected it to a tree in the front yard, and it is  running over the roof basically flat, to my air choke coax.  I tied my antenna rope to the air choke to keep the strain off the Spark Plug.

It tunes 40, 20, 10 just great on the IC-7300.  The other bands are either 4+ or 3.5 to 3.8.  Basically not really usable. 

But right now I just care about 40, 20, 10.

Since I know this works well I'm going to look at other EFHW antennas that are meant to be up all the time and not a portable antenna for POTA/SOTA.
