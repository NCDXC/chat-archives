For Sale Icom IC-7800 200 Watt Transceiver 

For sale is my IC-7800 transceiver purchased new from HRO by me. The IC-7800 contains two identical high dynamic range receivers and incorporates Icom’s local oscillator phase noise upgrade and 3 roofing filters. The radio has the latest software load activating the pan scope waterfall display. The IC-7800 is a truly great radio on which I have worked 342 DX entities. The radio’s key specifications are: 

* +40dBm third-order intercept point (IP3) in the HF bands

* 110dB receiver dynamic range
* 15, 6, 3 kHz crystal roofing filters and user selected DSP narrow filters for CW and SSB.
* 160 through 6 meter all mode transceiver operation
* 30 kHz to 60 MHz general coverage receiver
* 1 Hz tuning steps

* Continuously adjustable full-duty RF output
* 200 watts maximum to 5 watts minimum (SSB, CW, RTTY, PSK, and FM modes)

* 50 watts to 5 watts (AM mode)
* 85-265 VAC Input, 800 VA , 55 pounds

Some of the outstanding features of these "Masterpieces of transceivers" include:

* Dual receivers with identical full specs and independent controls
* Digital IF filters (adjustable from 50 Hz to 3.6 kHz)
* High-specification in-band IMD
* Specially designed band pass filters
* High-stability OCXO unit
* Digi-Sel digital pre-selector
* Digital Twin Pass Band Tuning (PBT)
* Dedicated 50MHz band mixer and preamplifiers
* Dual AGC loops DSP controlled for maximum blocking dynamic range
* Variable DSP noise blanker and noise reduction
* Manual and auto notch DSP filters
* Audio and twin peaking filter functions
* DSP RF speech compressor, VOX, audio equalizers, and adjustable transmit bandwidth
* Built-in voice, CW, RTTY, and PSK memory and contesting keyers
* Advanced CW features and functions:
* Built-in RTTY and PSK31 modulator/demodulator
* Transverter BNC I/O connector (offset frequency programmable for actual RX frequency)
* Tuning rate auto step-up function
* Triple band stacking register
* Front panel USB port and CF memory card slot
* CI-V interface and RS-232C connector for PC
* Four antenna connectors
* Video monitor connector for larger function and band scope display
* Built-in, high-speed automatic antenna tuner 

The radio is in very good physical condition and works perfectly.  The radio has a few surface scratches. The only issue is that the front panel stereo ¼” phone jack is flaky. I have a brand new Icom supplied, replacement phone jack. Installing the jack should take under an hour. The radio comes with it’s original manual, data sheet, Icom supplied rack mount ears, a nice dust cover and factory boxes .
