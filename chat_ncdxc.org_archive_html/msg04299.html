Dear NCDXC friends, 

Soon, you will me meeting at Holders for your monthly meeting.

The Board is going to give you their version of transparency and I suspect some rationalization for their decisions.

Here are the hard questions that you should be asking:

The Board is elevating the newest board member with five months of board experience to club President. This member is also relatively new to the club. This club member initially indicated that he did not want to be President. This is being done despite my long involvement in the club and my year and a half as a board member.

Is this about putting the strongest candidate in the Presidents seat?  Or blocking a candidate who wants to make effective changes?    You decide.

The Board has yet to act on my motion to hold a special election in January. Is this transparency?  Is this giving the members a choice?  You decide.

Guys, I'm not about conspiracy theories or space aliens, but human behavior can be an odd thing. This whole matter reeks and we should not accept these recent events as they are unfolding.

Make your board do the right thing. Make them listen to the membership. I will accept a vote of the membership and that will be the end of it.

Respectfully,

Paul N6PSE
