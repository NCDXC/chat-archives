On Jun 10, 2024, at 4:06 PM, Al N6TA <al.n6ta@gmail.com> wrote:

I am surprised that nobody has mentioned the dissatisfaction that the west coast felt with the previous activation, FT5GA.  There were a lot of heated exchanges between some on this chat site and the pilots in France.  I did not pay attention then since I already had a contact and was not thinking about bands and modes. But there were a lot of very unhappy DXers in our midst.
Al  N6TA


I believe the problem(s) with FT5GA were well documented and understood. From our perspective, that group seemed to completely ignore NA, both East and West Coast, and spent their time filling up the logs of their friends back in France. When asked to listen for NA (or other places), their response was (paraphrasing), “We’re already busy working massive pileups. Why do we want to ask MORE people to call us?”

That’s why this team specifically asked for pilots in each major area, understanding that East and West Coast NA are like separate entities (from a propagation perspective). Now, Marek may not follow instructions well (perhaps his first grade teacher did not check the box for “Works and plays well with others” on his report card), but at least there is SOME appreciation of the international community this time around.
