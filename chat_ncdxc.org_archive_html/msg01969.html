I fully agree with all of the comments so far.  There is no requirement for either club to make a profit on the IDXC.  If everyone has a good time and the convention breaks even financially, it is a success.  Profits are good if they are used to promote DX such as contributions to DXpeditions or the NCDXF.  Profits that sit in the bank are a waste.

 

A visit to http://www.ncdxf.org/pages/contributors-major.html will show that the SCDXC did not contribute last year whereas the NCDXC contributed $4,000.  The SCDXC is listed as having contributed “at least $5,000 over the years”.  So if the reason to go back to the H.I next year is to maximize profits to be donated to the NCDXF, well…

 

After last year at the H.I., I was seriously considering not going this year until the venue was changed.  I am extremely glad that I did go (great job John, Kevin and the crew!).  If it goes back to the H.I. it is not likely that I will go next year.  We need to get this message to the SCDXC group.  Do we need a petition?

 

73,

- Craig, AE6RR
