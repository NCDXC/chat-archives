The original WSJT-X and the variants, do not listen for your answer on their tones, but listen within the entire spectrum they are using.

The original intent of the F/H tone change (and limit callers to > 1 KHz) was enforced separation (callers:reports) for increased successful completion.  The variants break this intent, some chaos happens as the result.

Which means if you're not using F/H (and not changing tones), it's fine, as long as they decode you.
