I’ll be doing the (usual) “early bird” 8 AM presentation. The title:

“DXCC Honor Roll #1 Antenna Strategies (or how to work FT4TA and VK0EK)”

It will contain HFTA, EZNec, YW, DX Atlas, Stu, K6TU’s Propagation Service (using the “advanced antenna.13 file for my site location, and Dean, N6BV’s propagation charts). Plus antenna construction photos (100% home-brewed antennas) and hints and tips. I will also cover how I eliminated most of the negative interaction I didn’t know I had until I followed Tom, N6BT’s advice and modeled ALL of my antennas together. And a little bit on noise elimination on the low bands after building one of Jim, K9YC’s chokes. Finally, a comparison of phased vertical arrays vs. high dipoles.

It will also contain information on both my QTH as well as VK0EK and FT4TA locations. 

Hope you can make it.
