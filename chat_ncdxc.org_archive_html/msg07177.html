I worked him at 0125z for a new mode.  He was Q5 copy but with deep QSB.  The QSB wasn't an issue in the sense that the small group of callers were very courteous to let each QSO finish before calling again.  I listened for 45 min. after my contact and his signal had built up to a solid Q5.  In many cases 'Bimbo' got the calls correct the first time but the stateside stations would repeat their calls again.  Obviously he could hear much better.  He gave me a 57 report and his signal wasn't moving my S meter when I made the contact.
