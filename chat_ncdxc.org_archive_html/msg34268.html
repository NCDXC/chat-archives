I cannot vouch for it, but I bought a MeanWell 36 V, 200W switcher to run my SteppIr antenna.  I cannot say that the little unit SteppIr provides is bad, but I wanted a lot of current headroom to ensure any friction in my antenna would not cause an immediate motor stalling.  The SteppIr groups said enough good about MeanWell that i bought one.  I ought to hook it up.
