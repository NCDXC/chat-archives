Also fun for me to page thru.  I thought these rigs were later than ’40.  I got a lot of history to learn!

I have a pair of 813s that I used to run orange in the plates and see now that they are rated at 100 W and were one of the more expensive tubes back then.  Had I known they were so costly, maye I wold not have dirven that amp soo hard!  Anyone want a good deal on a pair of 813s?  RF deck only but great fun to run…

 

I also liked the list of air variable condensers from National and Cardwell.  I did not know they were so old but now I may be able to identify some of my old stuff.

 

Fun any way I look at it!
