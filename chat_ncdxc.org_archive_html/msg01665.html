Here is your NCDXC Question of the Week:

 

What entity has been most elusive for you?   Which one has been active but you just have not been able to get it in your logs, or you have done so with great devotion and patience?

 

 

For me, a number of them come to mind. SU was very elusive until Vlad-SU9VB went there and I was able to make multiple band contacts with him on CW. Mt. Athos was very elusive for a long time. I must have spent ten years trying to work Monk Apollo and had concluded it may never happen. Well, New Years Eve 2012 I found him on 40 SSB about 11PM our time and I now have that elusive QSL card.

 

I've never heard an EP-Iran station-Ever!  I am hoping that I can nail down EP in the coming months or years. I know that there are several that are active but between their timing, propagation and their operating methods it just hasn't happened yet.
