Since you can't convert a K3 to a K3S - you can come close - lets call it a K3(+). My K3 is at Elecraft being upgraded with a new synth and DSP board, and I'm also adding a matching sub RX and new synth for the first time (all filters will match as well).

Years ago, I used an Orion II with diversity receive and loved it. I vaguely remember using some wire horizontal antenna on the main RX and a vertical on the sub RX. Having the different mixes in my stereo headphones was a joy. I may have even used a K9AY for a second receive only antenna . . . just don't remember.

Do you use Diversity Receive? And if you do - what antenna combination have you found to be most effective?

I'm MUCH more of an SWL-er than anything - so this is a new trick my old K3 will do and which will really up the ante as well.
