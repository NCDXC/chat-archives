Strictly speaking, FT8, as well as its predecessors like JT65 and JT9, are also forms of multiple frequency-shift keying, but the ADIF folks gave them their own mode designators before deciding to make FT4 and other new modes submodes of MFSK. It can be a bit confusing (or maybe the word is "annoying").

73, Greg N6PM
