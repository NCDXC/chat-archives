First, I wouldn’t say that this proposed DXpedition is “just after” a previous one. There’s a two year separation here.

Second, despite (or perhaps, because of) Bouvet 2023, Bouvet is still #11 on the Clublog “Most Wanted” list, making it a desirable destination.

Lastly, I think most people think that Bouvet 2023 was a massive clusterf**k, including Ken (LA7GIA). By “doing it right” in 2025, Ken wants to correct any prior errors, and restore his reputation in the DX community. These are laudable goals, and I hope he is successful (and safe).

That said, it may be difficult to raise funds, at least from major foundations, due to the history. NCDXF previously granted Ken $100K (the largest grant in the Foundation’s history) for Bouvet 2023. That resulted in ~18,000 QSOs, less than one-tenth of the original plan. Granted, Ken returned $40K of that investment, but it’s still a lot of money for not a lot of success. (I was also the single largest individual contributor to Bouvet 2023, and did not receive a penny in refunds. I made one QSO with them, and it was sheer luck. I wasn’t even sure I got through until I saw their log.)
