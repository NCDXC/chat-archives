Annie and I will have to consider these for our POTA IC-7300s.  

I wonder if there will be a firmware upgrade offered to current IC-7300 owners to give them some of the new functionality that can be supported in the existing box? That would be nice and even swing some to rip and replace to get the full feature set. 

Looking back on what the IC-7300 and the FT-710 have done to the used equipment market, older VERY nice equipment is going to have trouble being sold for more than $500 or $1000.  Not a bad thing if you're on a limited budget. Also creates more opportunity for new hams to get on HF without as big of an expense. Annie just re-homed her first HF rig IC-756Pro3 for only a little less than what we paid for it.
