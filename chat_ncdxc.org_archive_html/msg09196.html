The second storm in December twisted my rotator inside the tower.  The triangular plate the sits inside the tower section just pushed out the horizontal truss elements and turned 120 degrees, ripping out the connector in the process. Not fun to fix.

 

In anticipation of the coming storm I cranked the tower down, threw a rope over the boom and tied it to a tree. (Heading 100. I still hope to work K1N on 12m even though the antenna is only up 25 ft.)

 

Anyone else taking drastic measures?
