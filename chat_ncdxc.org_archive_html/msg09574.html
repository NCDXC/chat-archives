The bill does not give broad authority to override any and all private contract provisions of any kind. It addresses the specific use of PRB- 1 that the FCC could have invoked unilaterally but has continually deferred to the US Congress for authority to use. That’s all that this bill would do. You could live in a development that had CC&Rs 50 years old that no one even remembers. It would take just one malcontent to dig it up and cause you lots of problems.

I didn’t mean to start a controversy. Contact your Congressional Representative if you feel so inclined either way.

Be careful what you ask for. This bill gives the “guvmint” the authority to override private contracts. In general, I believe this is a bad idea. You may like the result when it overrides an HOA provision against antenna construction, but you might not like the result in many of your other private matters.
