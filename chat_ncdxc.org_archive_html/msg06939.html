> return two days later.  If ferrite beads don't work

Ferrite beads are NOT part of a solution for HF, because their resonance
is in the 150 MHz region. At HF, a single turn through a ferrite core
yields a small value of L with a bit of R. Next to useless. For
effective choking at HF, multiple turns must be wound through a suitable
ferrite core to move the resonance to the range of the TX, which also
multiplies the impedance at resonance.

Study k9yc.com/RFI-Ham.pdf  This is the solution that Steve used.
