NCDXC friends, I’ve had an amazing week here in Syria. The Country is slowly working to recover from over ten years of war. This was not a “Civil War” this was a terrorist onslaught by ISIS and Al Nusra Front and the Free Syrian Army. The Syrian’s did not invite or initiate this war.

The damage done is unprecedented. The level of suffering and human misery is extreme.

Anyway, I’ve made some great connections here and crisscrossed the country seeing and experiencing as much as possible. Amateur Radio Diplomacy is the central purpose for this trip.

Tomorrow, I’ll be in Baghdad and then back to home.

73 for now,

Paul N6PSE
