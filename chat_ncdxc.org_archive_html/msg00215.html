On Mon, Oct 22, 2012 at 8:08 AM, <paul@n6pse.com> wrote:
Good morning, here is your NCDXC Question of the week?
 
 
How did you do with the TT8TT and 3B9SP DXpeditions?

I didn't work TT8TT. I had expected it to be 'easy'. The 5Z4B beacon is back on the air and I hear it daily on SP in the morning and LP in the evening. The TT8TT signals were never strong in Oregon.

I haven't worked 3B9SP, either. Their signals have been good on 20/17m, but they are on those bands at least an hour late for Oregon. The 20m RTTY signals have been loud, but the polar flutter has made clean print difficult. They were really loud tonight on 17m cw, but the one of the less experienced ops was at the key.

Neither was an ATNO, but I did need both on RTTY.

Steve, K6UM
