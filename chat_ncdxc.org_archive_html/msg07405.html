The World Wide Radio Operators Foundation (WWROF) is pleased to present A Long Overdue Review 
of Gray Line Propagation on the Low Bands with Carl Luetzelschwab, K9LA. Carl will trace the 
origin of gray line propagation, and show that there is a problem with the current explanation 
that propagation along the terminator is efficient. He will then provide an alternative 
explanation for gray line that satisfies both observations and ionospheric physics.
