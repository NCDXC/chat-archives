A reminder that on May13, Saturday, the club is providing a grouping of  free "spots" to members (only, please) at the De Anza School swap meet. The swap begins at 6am and  runs till about noon. We will buy  6 or more spaces, each two cars wide, for the members to sell from. You will pay to park in the public lot but that's all.  There is NO room at our site for your car.  Bring a table, a tarp, whatever you wish to use.  They have coffee and donuts for sale so that's covered. The advance team will be in line at 4:30 am should you wish to join us.....

De Anza College is in Cupertino at 21250 Stevens Creek Blvd. 95014. Questions?   JIM K8JRK and Tony K6BV and Lucas W6AER for info
