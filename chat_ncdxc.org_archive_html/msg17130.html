Over the past two days, I have noticed that FW5JG's clock is off by several seconds which makes it possible to decode him but very few are working him unless they manually override the timing of their transmissions to coincide
with his. Many guys were calling him last night but few got through due to the timing issue.

Heard and attempted to work FW5JG this morning on 40m FT8 standard mode.  FW5JG was transmitting on the 40m call frequency at about 2000 Hz.  Their QRZ info says they will work FT8 in the Fox/Hound mode.

In the FT8 operating manual it says Fox/Hound is for DXpeditions like this but should be used on another frequency away from the call frequency to avoid QRM and confusion.

I tried for 30 minutes but never completed the contact.
