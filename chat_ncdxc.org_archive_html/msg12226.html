All club members are welcome to visit my station and try to work the P5. Since I worked him last December, I won't be calling him.

He was weak but workable on my Steppir DB36 antenna, with a K3 and 1400 watts from my Expert 1.3K Amp.

My contact info is in the club roster. I'm located in the East foothills of San Jose at 400ft with a clear shot to Asia.
