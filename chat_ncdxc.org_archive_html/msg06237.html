As noted, Win 8 upgrades to Win 8.1 and I think you will find that Lenovo is actually installing 8.1 on new laptops...
just bought son YP series and that is 8.1 but states 8 on site...

As to 8 being a different UI, my son was able to tame metro and cage it completely. Now he is happy with a more 7 style UI... OTOH, there were a few Metro features he found nice after he got used to them... the biggest PITA is that Win8 likes to think you have a touch screen and is completely configured for that setup ...

Chris is correct on the driver issue, your old EMU will not likely work on 8 (if you never got it to work on 7 then you have NONE chance on 8). MS has decided that the only way to protect the masses is to require "officially sanctioned drivers" which is several hundred dollars each year from the driver author...

Take careful stock of just which drivers you really need on your laptop. Check them out, then decide.
