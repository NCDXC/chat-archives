The NCDXC is one of a few organizations that is an "influence" organization and shapes the hobby as we know it today.  

Just look at the DXpedition activity that has originated from this club over the past four decades. Look at the roles that our members play or have played in other influence organizations such as NCDXF, Yasme and others.  Few organizations can muster the talent and devotion from its members that the NCDXC can.

Look at the success and prestige that has come from the IDXC.  If anyone wants to be intimate with most aspects of this great hobby, the NCDXC is the place.

Paul N6PSE

Jim-
 
Here’s a few-
 
Socialize with a bunch of friendly people who share your enthusiasm for the Magic of DX
 
Enjoy great monthly presentations on DX technologies and techniques
 
Learn about the history of DXing from some of the operators who made it happen.
 
Rub elbows with a number of current well-traveled DXers.
 
Help support DXpeditions around the world
 
Receive spots and other valuable DX information via Chat well before anyone else
 
Draw on a friendly crowd who can help solve your station and/or antenna problems
 
Help put on a great show with the IDXC every other year at Visalia
