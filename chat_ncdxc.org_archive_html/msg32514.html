I think you are conflating the Bouvet and Peter I expedition plans. To my knowledge (I am in contact with Ken, LA7GIA), they are not planning to use helos on Bouvet, only on Peter I (where they are an absolute necessity). The plan for Bouvet is to wait for an opening to land, set up on the island with a minimal team, and operate remotely from the boat. They are not using George Wallner’s “RiB”, but are planning a similar configuration (perhaps without the box), and are getting *some* guidance from George (who has become the world’s expert on remote DXpeditioning).
