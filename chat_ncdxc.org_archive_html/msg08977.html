As a DXCC card checker I have seen the pride hams take in showing their cards and explaining the difficulty in making the Q. And I know that some day when Monk Apollo gives me my last remaining country I will feel like I had just walked on the moon..... No matter who knows or what new award it produces... For me DXCC is a personal contest where I compete against Mother Nature. Perhaps we need to take a page from the DXCC QRP rule book and just submit a list of calls worked and certify we did it QRP or whatever. There is already a lot of cheating in the present system by those who seek some recognition. Just sending in a list would give them the award and perhaps they could sleep at night too
