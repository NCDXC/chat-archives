Some time ago I posted a request to both NCCC and NCDXC for info re the current status and whereabouts of Hugh Clark K6HFA, late of Fresno, who was an activator of many IOTA island groups in the past several decades. My mundane purpose was to obtain a QSL for one of Hugh's Alaskan IOTA groups, which I worked before I was bitten by the IOTA bug.

Until now, every path has been cold. Email to his last published email address brought no replies. His qrz.com page has not been edited in a long time and he does not appear to be in Fresno, or at least seems not to be QRV from there (info from W6DPD directly and K2VCO indirectly). He has not been active in IOTA. I was coming to the conclusion that perhaps he is SK or no longer involved in radio.

But yesterday, perusing dxworld.net for IOTA insights, I moused across a feature called "Caption This" and there was a submitted caption by K6HFA dated October 14--but with no hooks to an email address or anything else. So it seems Hugh is alive and at least keeping up with DX. I tried another email to his last published address but do not expect a reversal of fortune.

Why this email? I want to ask again if anyone knows the current whereabouts of Hugh Clark, K6HFA, late of Fresno.

Yes, IOTA has been a bit slow this past week.

Garry, NI6T
