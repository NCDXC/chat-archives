It all depends on what your goals are. If I cared to have a 70' tower, I would have 2 antennas - a SteppIR that covered 40 and 30, and would have a vertical for 160 and 80. Oh that would then let me best participate in challenge oriented (max band slot) contests and awards.

But since I decided to give up on anything that requires mega-band slots, and because I don't want any tower bigger than my AB-577 military system, I went with a full sized 3 element mono bander on 20M, phased vert array on 40M, and a KY6R Special on 160/80 that fills in for the other bands, like 30 and 17.

But my goals are very different than others, because I go mountain biking even more than play ham radio these days.

But I really love monoband antennas. Is rather have three mono band antennas on 3 fave bands than a big tall and heavy multibander.
