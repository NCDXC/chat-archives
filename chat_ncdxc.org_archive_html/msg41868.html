I did not join in previously about which antennas work well.  I started with a Butternut vertical and worked more than 120 countries with that.  They I put up a tower and I bought a TH11DX when it was originally manufactured by Hygain.  The only modifications I made were I slightly adjusted the lengths of some of the elements and added a vertical support to hold up the boom with phyllistran so the boom was horizontal rather than bending. I completed the entire country list to number #1 several years ago.

Regarding rigs, the only one I regret and eventually gave away was the Yaesu FT1000MP. I replaced it with the FTDX5000 and still use it.  I had to replace the small numeric displays and it is still working well.
