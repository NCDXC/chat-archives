Rusty, Rich,
That is very strange, and I have no idea either how those fields could have become populated, other than a possible program bug.

I went through the frequency calibration procedure once, and it worked pretty well. It steps though all of the WWV and Standards frequencies, from a user entered low frequency, to an upper frequency. In my case, 6 meters. It was amazing to see that there was a few Hz of error, especially as the frequency increased. A linear curve fit was easily achieved.
But, somewhere along the line the curve fit values were lost, possibly when I had upgraded the program.
To me, it's not a very useful feature unless I were interested in participating in the Frequency Measurement tests.
