Friends,

 

I'm still a bit in shock after hearing this news, and some of you may have already heard, but it is with a very heavy heart that I report that our good friend Greg DesBrisay became a silent key from a heart attack on July 13th just one day shy of his 61st birthday.

 

Greg was a good friend to many of us. We knew him as a warm, enthusiastic, kindred spirit in our collective hobby.

 

He was one of our finest CW operators, even being one of the inspirations for me to learn CW a few years ago when I saw him in action at the WVARA Field Day and CQP. He was also a great engineer and elmer, always having a keen perspective on whatever we were trying to do. Beyond operating, Greg made several contributions to the club, including coordinating our Electronics Flea Market for the last several years.

 

Most recently, he operated with me remotely at NW6P for ARRL Field Day, but he was also starting to become a regular contributor to the N6RO team.


There are no concrete plans for any service yet due to our favorite pandemic, but we will let you know as soon as we know anything else.

 

Rest in Peace, N6GD ... -.-

. . 

 

73,

 

Bill/W9KKN
