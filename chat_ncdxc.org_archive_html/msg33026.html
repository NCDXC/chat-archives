I missed out on Somalia last year (only heard here on the QRP band, 30M).  My noise floor was a few dB too high (a neighbor got through).  It’s better now (but improving THAT never ends).

Somalia and Syria are the last two I need from that part of the world for ATNO, then a handful of scattered unlikely islands and a couple probably never gonna happen anytime soon places (DPRK and Turkmenistan).

But, we’ll see what happens.  If we can make a half dozen contacts with Yemen with them using 100 watts and a stick on the beach, Somalia is doable.  Off the charts dangerous for a Yank, but doable.

I’m optimistic this will happen (Syria, not so much).
