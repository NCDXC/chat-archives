My two cents. It's always nice to have a gathering of Hams and find familiar faces and make new friends.
Presentations were mixed. Some were good some were ok (my humble opinion).
Vendors were similar to last years.
My local club (LARK Livermorme Amateur Radio Klub) had a booth outside with lots of free kits to build tape measure 2m yagis. Very successful in engaging all kids, scouts and many grown ups fox hunting around the property. The funniest one was the last one on Sunday when they had a guy with the fox transmitter (they are so small these days) hidden inside his jacket. Fox was walking around the lot disorienting the chasers. Finally somebody figured it out.
