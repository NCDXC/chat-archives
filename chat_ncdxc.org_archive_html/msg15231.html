On June 27, 2018 at 5:04 PM Paul N6PSE via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

Yes, Jim, I worked them on FT8. You have to be in "Hound" mode and you have to call above 1,000 Hz.

You enter KH7Z in your call box and then hit "enable TX"  and it will start calling in the proper sequence.

Or you can wait for them to call CQ which they occasionally do. If you are not in Hound mode or your time is not synchronized, it won't work.
