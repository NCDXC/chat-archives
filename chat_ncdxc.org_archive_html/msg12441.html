Three days in a row I miss a shot at a 10M QSO.  They like to move to 12 CW when I am about half the way home from the rock pile.

80 M in evening is another opportunity for a new band but I have a new neighbor that has something that goes on with sunset, providing a S9 challenge on 80.  Failed two nights in a row.  Heard ‘em but could not copy well enough to know if my call was being sent.  I need another s unit to make a QSO and before they do the EU thing.

 

A few months ago I sent off QSLs to two different ops in Pitcairn In hopes that these guys that used to operate would take sympathy on my need for a confirmation.  One of them was involved in the sex trial and likely has other things on his mind.  In a couple of years, maybe a card will appear providing a ray of sunshine on an otherwise rainy DX day.

 

I still love the hunt; easy to work makes it less satisfying.  Hope you all are having fun.

 

GL to all.
