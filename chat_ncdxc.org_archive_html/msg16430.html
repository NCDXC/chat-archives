Ed,

I recently recabled my US Tower HDX589 crank up.   I contacted US Tower and they emailed me the cabling diagram.  I would ask them to send you a cabling diagram for the MA-550.  Its not that difficult to do yourself especially for a MA-550.

BTW, order the aircraft cable from somewhere else than US Tower.  They want an arm and a leg for their cable kits.
