Paul and Rick,

This is a really great question. I did not listen on 40 last night so don’t have an answer. However I did listen on both 15 and 20 yesterday (maybe 7-8 hrs in total) and had exactly 3 decodes of ZD9! The 3 were consecutive cycles for a total of 1 minute 30 seconds of opportunity lol. This is a huge curiosity to me as I tend to hear pretty well, have a zero to negative horizon in that direction, had little to no noise etc. 

I am not familiar with the terrain on ZD9 but suspect it may not be favorable for the west coast. Additionally operating on FT8 “normal “ frequencies greatly increased my QRM from other stations. Parenthetically, I put the ZD9’s call in PSKR at times to see what and where he was hearing. When I checked I saw he was hearing Europe, east and southeast USA and JA/ Asia. West and PNW was almost nothing!

Sorry for the lengthy response especially one not providing a solution. Glad I don’t need him for an ATNO but need him for several band slots.
