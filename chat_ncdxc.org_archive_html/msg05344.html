As you can see from the posted minutes, after a lengthy discussion by all the board members, I made the motion to discontinue the DXpedition of the Year award.

 

From last year’s challenging conversation(s) with Paul N6PSE about the award it became clear to me there are too many negatives associated with awarding one DXpedition over another. 

 

All these teams put thousands of dollars (7-10k) of their own money into the effort.  Additionally their heart and soul and with some risk of injury even death yet they participate to the fullest – all teams.

 

Personally I award them with sponsor donations much as the NCDXC does.

 

73,

 

Bob – W6OPO
