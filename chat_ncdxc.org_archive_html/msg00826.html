Here is your NCDXC Question of the Week (QoTW)
 
 
We all know that there are some DXCC entities that have not been activated for a number of years. Some entities appear to be impossible to activate for various reasons.

Question: Should the ARRL consider de-listing any entity that has not been activated for a prescribed number of years, perhaps 15 years of no activity?
 
Would this enable some to reach the Honor Roll, or would this be a step backwards and make it too easy to reach HR?
 
What are your thoughts?
