Yesterday, I worked them on 15 meters.  Amazingly, they were armchair copy, unlike the other bands.  Maybe I was just lucky.

Last night I worked them on 80 (already had 160).  They were barely audible with static crashes.  I somehow got past the JA wall.  What seems to be working is call on the frequency of the previous QSO.  They don't seem to tune up high to avoid the congestion.

Also worked them on 30 CW, again through the JA's and again on the coat tails of the previous QSO.

Unless they try 10 or 12, I'm all done.  Looking forward to getting a full nights sleep finally.

Rick N6RK
