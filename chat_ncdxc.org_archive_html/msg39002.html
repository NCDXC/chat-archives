With respect to the questions about when a certain entity (Turkmenistan & Syria) might be activated. I can assure you that teams of DXpeditioners are poking hard at the top 50 most wanted. Many of us are hoping with a change of our administration that things at the US Fish & Wildlife might loosen up.
