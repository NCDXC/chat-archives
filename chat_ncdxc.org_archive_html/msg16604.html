Title: FT8 DXpedition Mode

I just worked T31EU on the 30m band in the FT8 mode.  I have observed that they’ve been working the FT8 mode on the call frequencies rather than going to another frequency to work digital pile-ups. 

The operator’s manual say in the Dxpedition mode the station should be a Dxpedition AND work off the call frequencies of the different bands.

I find it a bit confusing wondering if I should be calling as Hound or just try and work simplex.

Anybody else wonder about this?
