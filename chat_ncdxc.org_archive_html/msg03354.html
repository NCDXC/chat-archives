On 9/27/2013 10:25 AM, k6md@aol.com wrote:
remoting takes away the level playing field

What's level about a playing field where one ham has a townhouse with antennas hidden in the attic and another has 8 acres with multiple antennas at 110 ft?  What's level about a playing field when one ham is in W1, where DXCC in a weekend running QRP is possible, and another is in W6 where DXCC in a weekend with 1.5kW and great antennas is tough?

I could go on, but I'm sure you get the idea. :) 

N6WM, N6ML, and a few others (with advice from W4UAT, I think) have built a very nice station on a low peak over by Livermore, and set it up for remote operation. That's their PRIMARY station, I think, and they built it. The fact that it's on a nice peak probably has the effect of moving them several hundred miles east when working in that direction!
