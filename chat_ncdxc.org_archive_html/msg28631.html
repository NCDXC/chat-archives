I’d score it as odd, SA came in best (by 6 dB) while looking at OC, an offset of 90 deg here in N Idaho.  A normal opening is more direct.

I saw (3) KH6, FK8 in the Pac, saw callers for ZL and Tony 3D2AG (I REALLY want that one, having worked him on all other bands).  And a smattering of SA, most I already have so I didn’t call again (one Brazil who must be stone deaf).

I’m wondering what the Kp7 of last night (still Kp6) has to do with it.

Prop is witchcraft to figure out.  It has rules, except when it doesn’t.

I’m STILL completely closed on ALL HF bands, can’t even see callers to J8NY in the Caribbean.  That’s no small amount of frustration since I want new slots in Cambodia (XU) and ATNO for St Brandon (3B7).

The only cheery news of the day, the large amp has been shipped back, I should be louder by the weekend.
