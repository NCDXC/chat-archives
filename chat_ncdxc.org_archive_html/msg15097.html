Hi Aaron,

Wow, whoever gave you that quote sure doesn’t want the work!  

 

To put in my towers it cost MUCH LESS!  

1. I paid about $100 per hole for a guy with a backhoe to dig it out,  

2. I had the local steel shop cut and bend the rebar for the cages and then I wired them together myself

3.  Slid the cage in the hole onto a couple cement chunks at the bottom

4.  A couple of 2x6 boards and some stakes to form it, & a couple of 2x4’s to hold the base up

5. The biggest cost was @$1000 for 8 yards of cement 

 

Good luck!
