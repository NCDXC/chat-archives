I KNEW there was a reason I've felt so lazy lately about DXing.  This quantifies it but one goes where the DX is, not the other way around... this includes DXpeditions too. 

A few of the digital were RTTY, some FT4 and a chunk of FT8.  If I remember, only a couple are ATNO, VP8PJ was one (then on several band modes).

Sparse offerings is an understatement this year, bloody stupid virus.   I've been selective, only working new slots and answering those who called me, often in the middle of an exchange.

It was a joy to work Morocco on CW today... yes, another band slot but it was just nice to use that mode again!  It had been a while, a little rusty.
