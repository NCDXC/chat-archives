Just an update:

I have received fourteen requests for SF keys. Two have been approved (Pitcairn and Vanuatu). One was approved (Fernando de Noronha), but the team withdrew their request because they refuse to publish their operating frequencies or any details of their operation prior to starting it. They are quite paranoid about announcing their operation in advance, in fear of some other group going to the same place and “stealing their thunder”. (They were like that in their funding request as well.)

The keys for the approved operations have not yet been communicated to the requesters, but that should happen soon. Once done, we should list their callsign and dates of operation somewhere on our website.
