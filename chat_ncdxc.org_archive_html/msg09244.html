As you might already know – the VK0EK Heard Island 2015 DX-pedition is in fund raising mode. We have an attractive matching fund program that you can read about here:

 

http://vk0ek.org/2014/12/21/angel-donor-creates-50000-matching-fund-for-the-heard-island-expedition/

 

If you have not already donated, please help make the VK0EK Heard Island 2015 DX-pedition possible. Here is a list of the sponsors and donors so far (please scroll down):

 

http://vk0ek.org/2014/12/21/angel-donor-creates-50000-matching-fund-for-the-heard-island-expedition/

 

Heard Island has not been activated since VK0IR – back in 1997! With much stricter access – with a new AAD plan in place, and because of the cost to get to this polar entity, it could be that long again until it is activated next. We are in full gear preparing and many exciting things are in the works – and will be posted in the News Section of our web site.

 

Your support is very much appreciated.
