These are all great ideas!  I worked the radio room of the Iowa last year when I was in San Pedro and am planning on operating from the carrier in San Diego in September.

I love the "MOCK DX Expeditions" idea.  Out here in Ventura County one of our members has done a "Mini-DXPedition" to the Catalina Islands for the last two years.  Last year he timed it so that it would be a Lighthouse OTA, POTA, and IOTA activation.  I think this meets what you're looking for, it's a unique location, it required a boat to get there (there are regular boat trips there year-round), and you could come back that night or sleep over.  They had about 3-4 hours to operate before the return boat ride.

I have not yet had a chance to partake in these, I've always been out of town those weekends, but those who have gone have really enjoyed it.

Good morning Paul,

Thanks for your request. Great idea.

Some of my ideas include:

1. Classes and exam for Amateur Extra Class license given by NCDXC team. There are very few
classes for Extra Class given locally.
2. Club visits to interesting sites such as the Hornet, SLAC, KPH etc.
3. NCDXC Field Day
4. Discuss ways to interest new blood into our hobby DXing or ham radio in general
5. Club electronics project Antennas, using a VNA, building QRP 40 meter transceiver etc.
6. Program to improve skill of DX operators
7. Classes to improve proficiency of FT8 operators

I hope this helps.

john k6yp
