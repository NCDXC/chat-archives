If not, why not?

1) Even with 170W, a decent preamp, and a 5-el Yagi  pointed at it, the system is noisy to me, and I can almost never talk to it. I sometimes hear stuff before others do and I try to post spots, but I can't tell if anyone copies them, so I mostly don't bother.
