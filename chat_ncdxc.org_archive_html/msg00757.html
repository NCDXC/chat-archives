On Dec 18, 2012, at 2:40 PM, paul@n6pse.com wrote:

The DX Community expects more out of most DXpeditions and they are often judged on how many total contacts and how many unique operators were satisfied with a QSO.

Paul, 

and that's just it. I think it is OK to disappoint some people. I think that if you mount an expedition then it should matter that it meets YOUR objective.  Did it fit your budget?  Did you have fun? Did you activate the bands you wanted? Look at Nigel, G3TXF, as far as I can tell he is CW only and he goes to a lot of places.  No SSB band/slots? Tough. I missed Annobon Island (3C0). Their setup/equipment was minimal and so was mine. I could have complained and expected a bigger antenna and amp on their side :-) 

Maybe we should phase out DXCC entities if they have not been activated for 15 years or more. Do we really think there will be another BS7 any time soon? Peter I - anybody? If they are too expensive to get to, then let's ditch them. Paul, maybe that's your question for next week?

73,

Andreas, N6NU
