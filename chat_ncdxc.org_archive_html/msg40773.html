In my young ham opinion a "valid QSL" depends on what you're going for.   I am NOT currently chasing the ARRL's printed DXCC.  I have 102 DX contacts.  Some confirmed some not, but I know I worked those stations.

If you want a printed DXCC cert from the ARRL then their rules will have to be followed.

If someone sends me an electronic QSL card I don't reply.  There are too many versions of e-qsl cards.

You do you.  I'll do me.  Have Fun :)
