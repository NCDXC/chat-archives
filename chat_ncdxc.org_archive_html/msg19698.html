Note: SV2RSG has a 100 watt radio and Bernie-W3UR will ask him to listen for W6 around 1500 UTC. 

SV/A – Mount Athos
SV1GRM, Teo, updates us on SV2RSG, Monk Iakovos, activity from Mount Athos starting late next week. He will be running a Yaesu FT-450 and 20 meter wire dipole. SV2RSG/P activity is planned for 20 meters only on SSB and CW. Teo says “Please be careful because the operator is a little bit "rookie"!!!”. QSLs should go direct to:

Monk Iakovos Kutlumusian
Holy Monastery Koutloumousiou
Karyes 63086
Greece
