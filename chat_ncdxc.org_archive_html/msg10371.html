I'm forwarding the following sad news from Warren, NR0V (he is not subscribed to this list):

I received some very sad news mid-day Wednesday that Pete, W6XX, passed away Wednesday morning.  This is a great loss to the amateur radio community.  He was a fine gentleman and an excellent operator and engineer.  Pete was always willing to share his technical expertise with the amateur community.  I worked with him on a few things over the years.

The question has been raised by some of Pete's friends as to whether there would be some local radio club that would be interested in picking up his call so that it could "live on".

73,
Warren  NR0V
