When I was a "young" ham in 2022 I really wanted an EFHW antenna. It would work well in my small yard so I made one.  Then I really knew I enjoyed HF and contesting so I got a Hexbeam and stuck it on a pole that I mounted on the side of my house. I quickly made my way through a Yeasu FT-850 (fantastic first radio), a Kenwood TS-450 (I loaded it with good filters too - great radio), and then an ICOM IC-7600. I operated barefoot during this period and I mostly sold each rig to afford the next one, except the Kenwood, I kept that one until recently.  After I sold the Kenwood I bought a used K3/P3 combo and a few months later a used K4D fell into my lap at a very good price.  I consider myself mostly content now, especially since I am borrowing a KPA500/KAT500 from a very good friend too.

But, the best piece of gear that just might be attainable, or maybe just a pipe dream, is a new QTH with land so I can put up a tower or two with better antennas. That would really make me happy.
