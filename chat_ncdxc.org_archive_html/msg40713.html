I assume we mean the USB cable for the 7300 vs. the old style CI-V cable. I had good luck with this one from Amazon:
Tripp Lite USB 2.0 Hi-Speed A/B Cable with Ferrite Chokes (M/M) 6-ft. (U023-006), Black
amazon.com
It was one of the few such cables I could find that was well shielded and had ferrites on both ends. It solved some weirdness with stray RF occasionally killing my serial ports to the radio. You can probably find alternative suppliers for Trip Lite.

Just FYI, I wanted something similar for my IC703 for field use and the best I found was with L-Com (the 703 is notorious for havintg RF/USB issues without a good cable). 
USB 2.0 cables A-MicroB male w/ferrites 2M
l-com.com

I’ve been happy with both cables for their intended use case.
