On 9/22/2023 9:23 AM, Bruce Bern via Chat wrote:
Glad you’re back from the hospital in good shape! In addition to the excellent suggestions already given, check “sound settings” and be sure that codec is indicated as both speaker and mic. 
I’ve also had just dumb lapses in xcvr settings too. Usually USB instead of USB-data, wrong selectivity selections, or NB or NR selected. 
After a Win update, the codec sound settings noted above are frequently lost, as well as frequently reset to default “enhancement”. Be sure that all sound enhancements are OFF in advanced sound settings. 
GL and let us know what you find.
