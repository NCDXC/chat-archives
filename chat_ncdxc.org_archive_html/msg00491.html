On 11/22/2012 12:53 PM, KE1B wrote:

Or does this exist already?
Yes and no. The "K3 Utility," written for Windoze, Mac, and Linux, allows access to SOME setup functions, but not all of them. It can also SAVE and RECALL a complete configuration. It also makes it VERY easy to download firmware upgrades and upload them to the radio. There's also a macro language that is supported by the K3 Utility and most logging software. I suspect that it's pretty well documented, but since I haven't had the need for it, I'm only guessing.
Elecraft manuals gets updated every year or two as features are added, mostly via firmware upgrades, and are published on the website as pdf files. Until that revision is published, doc for the firmware updates takes the form of "What's New" notes in the doc that accompanies the firmware download.
Every so often I print the sections of the manual that describe Menu settings and stick them in the file with my K3 Manual.
For years, I have urged Elecraft to edit their manuals and generate a new pdf whenever changes are made to the radios by firmware or hardware on an ongoing basis. I also think the manuals need a far better index to call attention to features like this. With modern publishing, it's trivially easy to do this, and it would make things far easier for the user. Last I looked, they have not taken my advice. I also suspect that some of the "features" Ed has described with respect to turn off parameter save and power failure recovery are not as well documented as they might be.
