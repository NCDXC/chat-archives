Paul

 

This is the first time I have accessed your Blog. I found it very helpful and interesting and I appreciate what you are doing and why you are doing it.  Thank you.

 

With reference to the definition of a “Bully Lid” IMHO everyone makes a mistake every now and then…but after being nicely and privately having this brought to one’s attention…and a consistent pattern emerges and continues over time that indicates it is not a series of accidents or “senior moments”  but rather an intentionally deployed   “operating style” then these actions you have identified appear to me fit a reasonable definition of a “Bully Lid”.

 

Pity this is happening as a result of the actions and operating style of a fellow accomplished  Dxer……

 

Thanks again for what you are doing for Ham Radio and reminding  us from time to time of what we should actively continue to aspire to and where some of us may  need to consider improving. Not everyone will agree and sadly people will often forgive people who tell a lie  but OTOH ...... most will NEVER forgive someone for telling the truth about things they would rather not acknowledge and deal with.  J  

 

Hopefully some other fellow DXers  may privately and lovingly and caringly persuade the operator in question that he  diminishes himself, his DX achievements  and the hobby we all enjoy together and that he  is really better than this.

 

73, Ted, K6XN
