Jerry,

why don’t you downgrade your 3-Element StepIR to a 2 Element with 30/40?

You will reduce you boom from 16 feet to 57 Inches and the weight will go from 51 pounds to 30 pounds

You are only losing roughly 1.5DB in gain while you get to keep 30/40 and all the WARC bands. 

It can be done with parts you already have. The shorter boom also has a much smaller visual impact.

73, Andreas, N6NU
