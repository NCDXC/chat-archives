Al,

I’ve noticed a trend recently in FT8 protocol where stations will call out of the blue skipping the TX1 and just hit immediately you with a TX2 signal report. I suppose this is an attempt to shorten the exchange even more for faster QSO rates. Maybe this is what happened to you? I’ve seen it mostly with Japanese stations, but not exclusively.

At least I think that’s what’s happening.
