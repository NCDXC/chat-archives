Current status - online in listen mode only for now

Updates:

- I have extended idle voice traffic timeout to 30 minutes
  In a few days, I will try to extend it out much longer.

- Next test to see if any of my normal modes on 40M produce RFI causing the 2 meter echolink radio to go offline.
  This will take place during the next few day/nights

- I also noticed on my iPhone, with latest OS, if I had the Echolink app running in the background, then the iPhone idles down.
  Sometimes, the proxy data gets funny and will not allow you to connect to the Echolink node.
  My current solution is to remove the Echolink task from the phone's background then reconnect. Or, modify your sleep mode on your phone. 
  Keep an eye on phone battery level while running the Echolink app.

- If you have problems/questions connecting to N6NKT-L node, drop me a message directly.

73 de N6NKT. George w.
