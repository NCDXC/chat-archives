This is very sad news. Many of us had feared this outcome. 

The SCDXC has a long and storied legacy. We had hoped that it would live on. I have long enjoyed reading their monthly newsletter (online) and I have come to know many of the members as very fine people. 

The NCDXC board is discussing how to do outreach and embrace those in SCDXC that want to join the NCDXC. Art N6MFT is leading those discussions which are already underway. 

If you have ideas or suggestions, please send them to any board member as we are meeting on Tuesday night to discuss all of the possibilities.
