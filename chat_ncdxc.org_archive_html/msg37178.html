This comes from Bernie W3UR's Daily DX:


TI9 - Cocos Island
TI2JJP, Jose, is currently on Cocos Island. Previously he was a co-owner of the Oceania vessel, which is usually used to take tourists and expeditions to TI9. A last minute offer was made to Jose to go to Cocos Island, which he took. Jose took a rig and wire and obtained permission from Costa Rica’s National Park Service to stay at the main house on the island. So far activity has been on 10 meters FT8. However while trying to set up the FT8 program Jose has apparently set up the callsign as TI9/I2JJP and some how forgot the T. TI2CC, Carlos, apparently called Jose on a satellite phone informing him of the error. Unfortunately Jose has not been able to correct the problem. This is clearly a case of work it first and worry later (WFWL).
