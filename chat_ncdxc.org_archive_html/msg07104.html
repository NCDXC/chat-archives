I very successfully modified the MA160V so I could use two in my 40M phased array (4 dB end fire, 3 dB broadside and 18 dB F/B), and – still be useable as a “regular” MA160V on 160M.

 

Surprisingly – and due to a parasitic phasing effect – on 30M it exhibits 10 dB F/B – not sure if there is any gain or not.

 

It loads up on 80M – but need to test to see how well it does there – I expect that I gave something up on 80M, and I’m fine with that.

 

I’ll write this up in full on my blog in the next week or so . .
