NCDXC,
I just got off the phone with HRD support.

They say HRD DM780 6.3.0.417 has a known bug in it that will cause it to lock up. If you have this issue they say the programers are working on the issue.
This showed up for me when using DM780 CW with Winkey, US Navigator.

On another note, Also look at your Windows add and remove program list, if you have more than one HRD version showing. this could cause corrupted files. If you have this issue do not just remove the other versions. You are advised to contact Tech support at HRD for help.

Good DX & 73
