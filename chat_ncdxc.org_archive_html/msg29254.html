Breaking News!

 

Dear Fellow DXer:

 

The 2023 IDXC is almost here!

 

We have some exciting developments to share with you:

 

The great folks at DX Engineering will sponsor drinks for both the Friday and Saturday "Attitude Adjustment Hours" These take place from 5:00-6:30 PM in the Eucalyptus Courtyard at the end of the program each day. Each attendee will be provided with four free drink tickets. It is almost time to step up to the Bar! We thank DX Engineering for their generous sponsorship of this popular event.

 

We are delighted to report that the Northern California DX Foundation will have their Radio in a Box (RiB) on display. You can learn more about this emerging technology at IDXC 2023.

 

DX University and Contest Academy are almost sold out! If you wish to attend, register for those programs soon.

 

Pre-Registration for the Convention closes on Monday, April 10th. You may do a "walk in" registration during the Convention, however certain banquets and programs may be sold out.

 

We have pulled out all of the stops on Raffle Prizes! This is a great opportunity to take home an Elecraft K4D, an ICOM IC-7610 or a Flex Radio 6400. You must be registered for the Convention to participate in the Raffle.

 

You can now register for the Convention with many exciting options.
