No, I think entities should be de-listed only when they become “deleted countries” under DXCC rules.  Honor Roll should be an honor, not something easily reached in a “reasonable” time period.  This idea reminds me a bit of giving the losing Little League teams trophies too, to maintain the players’ self-esteem!  I know K6YP reached HR in a little over 10 years of focused attention.  I reached mine in about 40 years of more casual DXing.  And I cried in my beer for months after missing Glorioso in 2009, but that’s part of the game.  Now #1 HR with the most deleted countries does take a full lifetime of operating, but when we honor the OT’s every spring at Visalia, we are really just acknowledging that these guys have been DXing since 1945 (or before) and are lucky to still be alive.   

 

I say don’t mess with the entities list.

Here is your NCDXC Question of the Week (QoTW)

Would this enable some to reach the Honor Roll, or would this be a step backwards and make it too easy to reach HR?

 

What are your thoughts?
