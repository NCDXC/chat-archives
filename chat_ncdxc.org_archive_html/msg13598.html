HI all...the electronics flea market at De Anza college is this Saturday. The show starts at 5am and is over at noon.  Those of us getting there early will do the paper work as " Occasional Sellers"...WHICH WE ALL ARE..so no sellers permit is required from you. If you are handed a form fill it in and check "My sales are exempt occasional sales"...but you likely won't see a form if you come directly to our site. The repeater is 145.23 with 100hz. pl for those so inclined.

The selling area is in the corner of the rather large parking  lot. The corner is bounded by Stelling Road and Stevens Creek Blvd. That is the North East  corner for those of you who navigate that way.  Entry from Stevens Creek is best. 

Note that you have to pay to park...the charge is $3 and the machine takes dollar bills or quarters..odd..anyhow, get the ticket and put it on your dash so the cops, who do patrol, can see it.

If you get on the De Anza College  web site to see more detail be aware THIS IS THE ELECTRONIC FLEA MARKET, not the normal people flea market which occurs on a different Saturday  It may be worth your time to print off the site map...and note the rows are lettered, the spaces numbered..
