Good idea, Tony.  And in my case, unfortunately I already know that I will not be at NCDXC's November meeting so I won't be able to check cards this time.

I am an authorized card checker for all the CQ Magazine awards which, among others, include WAZ, WPX, USA-CA and DX Field awards. These awards all have slightly different checking rules because they're set by the individual awards managers rather than by CQ Magazine itself. So it is really helpful to me to know in advance that someone might want cards checked at an NCDXC meeting. That gives me time to review the checking rules for that particular award to ensure we get it right. 

Another point, and I suspect Russ will share my view on this, is that we come to club meetings to enjoy the program and spend time chatting with old friends and making new friends. So if you have QSLs you want checked, please contact us prior to the meeting to ensure we've allocated enough time to get the job done. I'm happy to arrive early or stay late to check cards, but trying to check them during the actual meeting usually does not work out well.
