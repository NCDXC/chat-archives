Hi and welcome Dan WB4RFQ.

 

As one of the about 200 members of the Northern California DX Club I want to welcome you to the SF Bay Area.  There is a strong DX community here.

 

I have been a ham and essentially only interested in DX since 1961 but I only joined NCDXC in 2006.  You know the story, school, family, job etc. got in the way.  I had retired and decided it is time to “really” learn how the DX portion of the hobby works.  What better way than to mingle with those how are more experienced at it than I was.  The club has a lot of experienced DXer’s and they want to share what they have learned.

 

Locally to where you will be living in San Francisco I know of a member Dave NE5EE.  Then Tony K6BV in Pacifica on a hill-top.  They are listed on QRZ.com.  There may be others I don’t know.

 

When you get moved in, join us at our monthly meetings.  I know there is carpooling going on so check it out.  The meetings always have a program covering all aspects of chasing DX.

 

The club has recently started having the meetings using the internet.  Those who live out of the area can join the meeting using WebEx.  Audio and video is provided.

 

Most of the members are more centrally located toward the South Bay.  That doesn’t mean we don’t have members outside the greater bay area.  Even some international members too.

 

See www.ncdxc.org for details. 

 

Also see www.dxconvention.com for the IDXC convention in just a week or two.  A really big event.  This year (odd years) NCDXC produces the event.  Even years SCDXC does it.  I hope to see you there.

 

Again welcome to the area.

 

73,

Bob – W6OPO

San Jose
