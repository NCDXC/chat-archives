Folks,

The 70th meeting of IDXC will be held from April 12-14, 2019 at the beautiful Convention Center in downtown Visalia, CA. If you are going, NCDXC needs your help staffing the registration desk. Please consider volunteering some of your time to welcome and providing registration packets to our guess.

 

Please sign up via the registration staffing sign up sheet. Please provide your name and call sign. We need people to sign up for all the green open slots, so we have enough staff to allow for a smooth orderly checking progress.  Even if all the slots are fill up for the time you want to volunteer, go ahead and sign up by just adding your name and call to an existing filled out entry.

 

You can sign up online at: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1LirRUZ_VkgfzCboezMr5zhZ8rpkuaiGZ8lw3KYVDTnw/edit#gid=0

 

Registration time are:

Thursday         15:00 to 17:00

Friday              11:00 to 17:00

Saturday         08:00 to 12:00

 

Any question or issues emails me or call/text me at 408 823 3784

 

We really need your help. Please consider volunteering some of your time and help NCDXC make IDXC 2019 the best DX Convention.

 

 

73
