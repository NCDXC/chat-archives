I became aware of him at 1540 via a spot at 1533.  I found him and could copy well enough to call.  So, I called for an hour but could not work him.  He came back to a ‘N6’ once but the other callers dominated and he worked somebody else.

He was Q4 to Q5 for that hour and he QRT at 1640.

I heard one 7 and two 6’s get through.  He worked EU mostly but in the latter part of that hour, he took more NA.  Never asked for NA or west coast.

He signed his call only once in the hour I listened but it was clear.  When he came back to a station, it was that callsign followed by the signal report twice followed by BK.  If the QSO was good, ‘73’ and on to the next.

 

This was the only time in 48 years I had a chance to work him.  Second time I heard him.  Of course, there were many chance but I was working while he was QRV.  Maybe I will have another chance before long.  Only one left I need to work…
