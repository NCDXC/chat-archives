Hi Bob:

For 20, 17, 15, 12, and 10M, a Hex Beam on a push-up mast or small tower with modest rotor is the way to go.  This video might help.  https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iSJRDzW11Bk

The KIO Hex Beam is very good, but the store is temporarily closed.
https://www.k4kio.com/store/

The NA4RR Hex Beam is another good option: https://k4hex.com/

If $$ aren’t a problem, then the 2-element SteppIR is a winner….and adds both 40M and 6M.
https://consumer.steppir.com/shop/horizontal-antennas/2-element-antenna-products/2-element-yagi-antenna-package/

73
John, K6MM
