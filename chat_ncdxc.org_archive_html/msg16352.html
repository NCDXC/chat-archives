I got a flyer with a QSL card listing the Code of Conduct and decided to read it again.  I have always felt like I respect it and follow it.  Oops…

One item says this:

‘I will not trust the DX cluster and will be sure of the DX station’s call BEFORE calling.’

 

I definitely violate that ‘rule’ since I often make a QSO before the DX finally signs his call.  My personal rule is I need to get solid copy of the callsign or I will not log the QSO.  I hate to sit there after the QSO as the band is going out waiting to copy that call but that is my rule.

So, if you want to claim to follow the code, make sure you know what it is!  I didn’t know it!
