I have a bunch of nice, vintage cameras for sale. I should have sold them LONG ago, but here we are. :) All are Canon. 

1 - F1 Body  Well used, I bought it from a working pro.
2 - A1 Bodies One was my second body when I was shooting film (used with my F1), it's got minor scratches on the metal parts. My XYL's A1 is virtually new, not a scratch, barely used. 

1 - 24mm FD f:2.8
1 - 35mm FD f1:2
1 - 50mm FD f:1.4
1 - 85mm FD f:1.8
1 - 35-105mm FD f:3.5 zoom, macro
1 - 28mm FL f:3.5
1 - 135mm FL f:2.5
All of these lenses are in good shape, all have lived with UV filters on them from the day I bought them, and the filters go with them. The FL lenses date from my time in the civil rights movement (1968-70). They are great lenses, but they have no automation.
These lenses can be used on some of the new digital cameras with inexpensive adapters.
I've never had any trouble with any of this gear, and although it's been carefully stashed away for at least ten years, I believe everything is in good shape.
I also have a Canon Digital Rebel XT to sell. It's their inexpensive, but very nice half-frame version, the current version of which is in the under $1K range. This one is from 2005, and comes with the inexpensive 18-55mm 3.5-5.6 zoom. It's Rachelle's, and she likes her little Panasonic better. It's virtually new in the box -- I don't think she's ever used it. The body is much nicer than the lens.
If you have any interest, or know someone who might, please contact me directly, off the reflector. Whatever doesn't go to friends will go to EBay.
