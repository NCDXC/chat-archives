This message is intended for my NCDXC friends. It is not intended for publication outside of the NCDXC.
 
DX Magazine lists Mount Athos as the #2 most wanted entity on the West Coast.
 
As you may know, Monk Apollo’s three element SteppIR Yagi blew down in a storm this winter.
 
Several groups have approached Monk Apollo about replacing or repairing his antenna. A group of Greek amateurs
from Athens is now taking Monk Apollo’s antenna back to Athens for repairs. They plan to return to Athos in
April near Easter where they will re-install the antenna and tower.
 
There may be some activity from Athos once the antenna is reinstalled.
 
We had been hoping that Monk Apollo would lend his support for a DXpedition from Athos that would raise money
and replace his antenna and antenna support but that has not happened.
 
Good luck in catching this ultra rare one from the West Coast,
 
Paul N6PSE
