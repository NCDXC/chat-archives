On 2/24/2013 2:02 PM, paul@n6pse.com wrote:
 
For those of you that enjoy operating on 160 meters, what is the appeal of "Top Band" 

It's all about the challenges, both technical in the form of antennas, and operational in the form of studying and reacting to propagation. 

I did a "how to" presentation at Pacificon and for the Redwood Empire club on antennas for 160M aimed at mere mortals -- that is, those of us with limited space. My space is not limited, but most club members are.  It's on my website. http://audiosystemsgroup.com/publish.htm

I've been running QRP )5W) during recent 160M contests, and am up to 44 states worked. I'm missing VT, WV, SC, and a few others in the east. Best QRP DX so far is PJ2T, 6Y, KP2, VY2, .and one of the Oceania DX trips last year.

73, Jim K9YC
