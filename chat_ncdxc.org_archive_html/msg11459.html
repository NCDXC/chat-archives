The K6M “Year of the Monkey” Special Event went quite well. I was surprised that such an “invented” event could raise so much activity, including a number of folks who were chasing the coveted “5 Band/Mode slot” certificate!

In total, we made just over 2100 QSOs across 37 countries and all fifty states. There were some nice pileups at times, and some of our “guest monkeys” had their first opportunity to work in that mode. I think a few of them are now MUCH better operators, and ready to take on contest conditions.

We had a lot of fun, decorating the house with a Chinese New Year motif, and placing (stuffed) monkeys in every room.

Special thanks to all of the guest monkeys:

John AC6SL, Tuck AF6GQ, JV K6HJU, Robert KB6QXM, Glen KG0T, Fred KJ6OOV, John KJ6ZL, Vladimir KK6YGB, Rick N6DQ, John N6FBA, and David W6DTW.

Seven stations qualified for the “5 Band/Mode Slot” certificate, and those have gone out, along with dozens of special QSL cards.

FYI: You can get a special event callsign for just about any reason. We chose this one because of the monkey connection, but you can host your own party, get on the air, and attract a lot of activity with just a little effort. 

Rich KE1B
