On Mon,1/12/2015 8:42 PM, Dave Gomberg via Chat wrote:
What would anyone recommend for use as noise cancelling headphones? My shack is not the quietest and I figure this might help a lot with intelligibility.
What headphones have you tried that don't work for you? What sort of noise in the shack? I find the isolation of Sony MDR7506, Yamaha CM500, and Etymotic Research in-ear headphones are all plenty good enough for anything but guys talking around me. I contest exclusively with headphones for CW and SSB, use speakers for digital modes, casual DX chasing and casual operation.
