On 11/24/2013 8:09 PM, SPWoo wrote:
Perhaps the conditions we saw tonight is just an anomaly.
I think it is. Although I'm not a student of propagation, I've learned from contesting that 20M is often open to Northern EU for an hour or two in the mid- to late-evening, and we've also had that great success with Pooyan a bit earlier. Things were quite different during CQWWCW Saturday night -- when I returned from a family Bar Mitzvah and fired up the rig, 20M was dead but 40M was jumping to EU. Indeed, 40M hasn't been that good to EU in a long time.
I worked CQWWCW QRP, looking to increase my QRP DXCC count. Beginning Saturday night, I added 13 countries, bringing the total worked to about 125. The new ones included 3DA0, D2, and a bunch in the Caribbean. Worked D4C on all bands but 160M, mostly getting him with only a few calls. 20M was the only tough one, taking about 5 minutes. That station clearly has great ears and great operators!
