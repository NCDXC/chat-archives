Team,

 

FT4TA QSLs arrived today plus a  donation “thank you card” signed by the ops.  The envelope had three different  beautiful “Tromelin” color commemorative postage stamps affixed. I am an old stamp collector…and these stamps are the first “Tromelin” commemorative stamps I have ever seen. Wow!  Many years ago I was also delighted to receive 1A0KM Knights of Malta QSLs in an envelope with beautiful color Sovereign Order of Malta  commemorative stamps.

 

I love this hobby!  73, Ted, K6XN
