Just curious to see who on this Chat List has designed their own yagi(s) – using EZnec, and figured out their taper schedule based on the aluminum they had on hand, and then used a hack saw and drill press and pop riveter to build their antenna.

 

The biggest project I ever have done from scratch was a two over two 17M stack. I have also built a great 3 element 17M yagi.

 

These days I just use nested Moxons (Cabellas Crappie Poles and wire) – and I have gotten lazy, but have a bunch of extra aluminum in the “back 40” that  I might be willing to give to those who like building their own antennas.
