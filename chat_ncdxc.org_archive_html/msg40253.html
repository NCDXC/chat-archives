My first antenna LOL! (I got a Section win in SSB Sweepstakes with that antenna operating from a cabin in Clear Lake State Park.) I still use it on my Jeep.

 

Bandwidth is super-narrow on 80m but for FT8 that’s not a problem once you get it just right. It doesn’t radiate all that well on the low bands, but it works and it’s great when you’re surrounded by salt water like KH9. I swapped out the short whip for a 17’ telescoping whip and was able to use the coils to get down to 160m.
