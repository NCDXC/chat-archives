On Mon, Oct 17, 2022 at 4:37 PM Rick NK7I via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

Team TY0RU, with a couple stumbles at the start (before the bulk of the team arrived) is now rapidly filling a lot of slots in three basic modes (phone, CW and digital).  They're off to a great start (I have 14 slots at the moment, 12 of those are new).

Paul, N6PSE asks every year for the team that has done the best, why I mention this.

So far, TX5N (Austral) provided the most enjoyment (and band slots) for me;  but an honorable mention to TY0RU and D60AE (near antipodal, 20 slots for me, 16 new) should be made too.  (AND they both use Clublog AND leaderboard for rivalry/bragging rights, more fun all around). 

D60AE provided more mode opportunities than any other recent team (including 10M FM for EU, which they also favored by far over any place, boo!).

So keep your vote in mind while things are still fresh, I'm reasonably certain Paul will ask, now that we have teams venturing out again. 

I also suggest that your consideration is not all about total slot opportunities but about providing everything possible, to all places evenly (leveling the playing field based on available propagation).  THAT can be a challenge for any team, instead of simply looking for (easier) high numbers; but demonstrating better ham spirit.  (Simply calling OC, OC or SA SA etc is basic filtering, IF hams bother to listen.)

73 es gud dx,
Rick nk7i
