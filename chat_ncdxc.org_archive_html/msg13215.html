While an important tool, I've often noted that the impossible happens too. According to VOA Cap, I wasn't supposed to be heard in several places over the last couple years, but the confirmations prove it happened. Low mangled antenna (an example of 'put up wire, see what happens') into SoPac on the low bands, often. Simple, no; time consuming, absolutely.
In other words, it's a tool, not a seer. If I can hear them above a certain level, I try. Sometimes the magic happens. More times than one would expect. Just open the door, see what passes through it.
