WILLIAM M. PASTERNAK WA6ITF (SK) 1942-2015

I'm sharing this now, courtesy of our friend, Don Wilbanks AE5DW. I got acquainted with Bill when I asked if he would publicize the Johnny Cash Birthday Special Event Amateur Radio Station that was being put on by the NorthEast Arkansas Radio Club in 2012. He told me, "You're a broadcaster, why don't you 'voice' the story for Newsline?" I did, and shortly thereafter, Bill asked me to be one of the rotating roster of anchors/reporters for the Amateur Radio Newsline. It has been my honor to be associated with Bill and ARN since then. Bill considered all of us as "family." We are all saddened at his passing, but his impact on the hobby and service of amateur radio is an amazing legacy. Over the past few months, even with his health in decline, he was still feisty, always concerned about Newsline, and we developed a long-distance friendship that I will treasure for the rest of my life. God bless his family and his many, MANY friends around the world. Until we meet again, rest in peace and ~73~, my friend.--N5ASH
