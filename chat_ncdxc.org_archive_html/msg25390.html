I suggest you join the SteppIR google group and search the archives
for "paint".  A lot of info should come up, since every steppir
antenna has this problem.  There is no single type of paint that
is "optimum".  There are tradeoffs with cost, difficulty of application,
toxicity, etc.

It is not entirely clear if you can repair exposed fiberglass with
paint, as opposed to resin.

Discussion groups dealing with fiberglass boats may also be a source
of info.
