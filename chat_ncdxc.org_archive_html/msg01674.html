QST:

Once again, I have been snookered into managing the registration process for the upcoming Visalia International DX Convention. Many of you have already registered and are hoping to win one of the big raffle prizes being offered. (As the old joke goes, "Meet me halfway--buy a ticket!)

At this time I am asking for helpers to work on two tasks:

(1) STUFFING Registration Packets for the 600+ folks who have pre-registered for the convention.

I need 4-5 people to work for a few hours on SATURDAY APRIL 13th. Tentative time is 9:30 am - 1:30 pm, at my QTH in the beautiful Santa Cruz Mountains, just 30 minutes from downtown San Jose and most of Silicon Valley.

Lunch is included, along with the opportunity to enjoy the fresh mountain air, and to be the person who puts the winning raffle ticket in someone's envelope (maybe your own!). You do not need to be going to the convention to help out on this task. Hey, if you can work 200+ QSOs/hour as a Single Op, we should be able to stuff a measly 150 packets/hour working as a Multi/Multi!

(2) STAFFING the Registration desk at the Convention.

I need around a dozen people to put in some time registering visitors at the reception desk in Visalia. You can sign up for an hour or more, depending on your schedule and desire to help out. The work is easy (handing out the stuffed packets and shirts, and/or registering walk-ins), and you get to meet EVERYONE who is attending, since they all have to stop at the reception desk!

Registration runs from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm on Friday April 19th, and from 7:30 am until around 11:00 am on Saturday April 20th. Peak hours are expected to be Friday and Saturday mornings, and Friday around noon. Please let me know which hours you can or cannot work, and I'll put together a schedule. One big bonus: *YOU* won't have to wait in the queue to register; you can pick up your own packet and be all set to enjoy the conference!
