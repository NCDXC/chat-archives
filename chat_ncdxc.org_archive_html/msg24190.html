This is one you should work just on general principles!  RA0LQ/mm presently is passing through Grid Field DJ which is an all-water Field out in the middle of the Pacific Ocean.  Nobody lives there, so the only way to get this field is by working a maritime mobile passing through.  Also, because WSJT treats his maritime mobile callsign as a special callsign, it doesn't display his grid, so most folks have no idea how rare this one is. There is no pileup on him and he's easy to work. His ship is moving fast, so don't delay in going after him.  He'll probably be into another field by the time the sun comes up tomorrow.  And he QSLs via LoTW usually within 72 hours of making the QSO.
