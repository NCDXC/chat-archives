Oh, my favorite piece of ham gear is my Collins 75A-4.  I never use it now, it’s just an antique.  But whenever I dig it out to play with, I always marvel at the tracking IF strip, with all the coil slugs moving up and down in tandem, and the rotating BFO box to provide IF offset.  All done with fine mechanics long before any digital tuning was available!
