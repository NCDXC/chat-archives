Subject: Update on FCC Amateur License Renewals Post Government Shutdown

﻿On November 17th the FCC published announcement DA 25-943 to address various issues related to the most recent government shutdown.

Paragraph 1 (ULS) states that amateur Radio licenses that need to be renewed between 10/01/25 and 3/5/26 can now be submitted not later than 3/5/26.

In addition the W5YI VEC website notes that the FCC will process renewal applications based on which licenses will expire earliest and not necessarily based on when the renewal application was received.
