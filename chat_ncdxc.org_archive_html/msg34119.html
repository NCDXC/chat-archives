Will,
There will be so much DX on the air at any time of the day, that you can find nearly anything.  EU will be best before noon, especially on 15 and 10 M, assuming propagation is good.  You will find Latin America mid day onwards.
Mornings on 80, 40 and 30, EU long path until about 1700z, but you can work them without a beam, too.  I am sure you will make a lot of new DXCCs along the way.
have fun.  i will be doing some 5 W calling to get a bit closer to DXCC.  i got H40WA on 10 M today for QRP.
Al  N6TA

Will,

On 20 meters, EU should be good long path in the morning, South America short path in the afternoon, along with long path to Asia at that time.  Late afternoon/early evening try Asia short path.  If the band stays open EU should come in short path in the evening.  These are all big generalities, but worth a try!
