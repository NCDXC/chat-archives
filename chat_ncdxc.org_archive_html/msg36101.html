Here are several observations:

You can't decode a Super Fox unless you select Hound (Super Hound) in WSJT-X  2.7.0-rc5  (this is a good improvement over F/H) 
You can decode the callers that are not calling as a Fox. 

When you select Hound (Super Hound) you can decode the Fox and the other Hounds.  The transmissions appear more like spread spectrum as there are no defined signals, no "clear spots" 

Every few minutes, the "K8R verified" appears on the screen. 

You can see below,
