I went through all of the latest online info this morning on FT8WW. Although a ClubLog update was scheduled for early this morning GMT, it has not yet occurred. Clublog is updated through 0400Z 12/25. In addition, Thierry has responded to the FT8 cycle confusion by changing his transmit cycle to evens going forward. This way, callers who are mistakenly in F/H mode will not QRM him, and will be able to contact him. He also requested that initial transmissions should not include the grid square, but that callers double-click on TX1 to skip to the signal report as the first transmission. This saves one transmission cycle, and speeds up the process.

 

Both my contacts with him have been long path. He is located almost at the antipodal point for the SF Bay region. The difference in distances between SP and LP for us is only about 1300 miles. In fact, the nearest “populated “ area to our antipode is Kerguelen!

 

I’m off work this week, and with the rainy weather am not running or hiking… without much else to do, looking up trivia has become fun. Happy new Year to all!
