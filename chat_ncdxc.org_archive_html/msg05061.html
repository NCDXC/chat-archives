Rich, it's very early in the DXpedition. Any good DXpeditioner is probably making notes on bands and propagation by time of day.

 

Remember, when they were maritime mobile, we were hearing them well via the long path on the high bands during our early evening. The morning paths were SP just as it was today.

 

Now on the island, they have three element Yagis on the high bands and verticals on the lower bands. They have 500 watts on the high bands and 1500 watts on the bands 40-160.

 

From what I see, they are doing extremely well within the first 24 hours of their operation. I  would expect them to refine and dial in even more over the next few days. I'm very impressed with what I've heard so far from FT5ZM.   This is going to be a fantastic Dxpedition by the way they are starting off,
