What I recommend is that you get a quality screwdriver antenna and put a
102 inch CB whip on top of it and mount that on your vehicle.  You
can get a tuning controller so you change frequency with the touch
of a pushbutton, sitting in the driver's seat.  Depending on your
HOA situation, you might be able to run coax from the car to your
house; otherwise, get a 12V rig and operate in the car (parked).
The HOA can do nothing about it.

A while back, a California station completed DXCC mobile ...
on 160 meters ... using such a setup (he did have an amplifier).

This will be much more effective than solutions in the Buddipole
class.

Rick N6RK
