I thought I would add when I made my QSOs today since the spots were a couple hours before I was in the shack.  Conditions change so it you want to compare notes, here are my QSO times:

40 SSB: 1320Z

40 CW: 1347Z

20 SSB: 0124Z

17 SSB: 0128Z

20 CW: 0135Z

 

17 M was most difficult due to a relatively weak signal with QSB and some local QRN.  Q2 to Q4 from 0120Z when I heard them till 0128 when worked.

20 M should be a good band for those that need this one as they are strong here.  In a few days, the competition will likely thin out as usual.

 

GL to all and happy hunting!
