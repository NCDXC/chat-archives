On Nov 13, 2013, at 8:59 AM, Dave Gomberg wrote:

> At 08:14 11/12/2013, KE1B wrote:
>> This is exactly the sort of scenario that convinced me to put up my Optibeam.
> 
> Rich, what prompted you to choose the Optibeam over the Hexbeam from K4KIO?
> 

First, I don't think this antenna was available when I put up the Optibeam in 2008. 

Second, the Hexbeam is really a compromise antenna for small footprint and light tower loading. It's fine for what it is, but I don't think the performance would be the same as a full-size, full footprint Yagi. My Optibeam is also 3 elements on 20/17/15/12, and 5 elements on 10m. (And other OBs are even bigger than that.)

I compared antennas from SteppIR, Bencher, Hygain, Force 12 and Moseley, but didn't look at the "wire beam" options seriously.
