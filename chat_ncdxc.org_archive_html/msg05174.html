Thanks - that's what I have experienced. Worked them on 40, 30 and 20M and
they had S9 ++ signals each time. I'm working full time in SF - and so just

have been where their signals were just booming in.

and the solar numbers are better this time. 

Heck its only been day 4 today - and wow - they sure are blazing a trail!

NCDXC fellas, here are my notes on FT5ZM so far

10 meters-have not heard them.
12 meters, very weak on CW before our sunset
15 meters, very weak between 5:30-6:30 PM via short path. (Watch for skewed
paths)
17 meters, very weak on CW before our sunset, short path.
20 meters, very loud, S9 short path after sunrise and long path around
sunset.
30/40 meters, quite strong at our sunrise and sunset, usually short path is
better. These have been the better bands for me,
80/160 meters, I have not heard them

I hope this info helps any club members trying to get their first contacts.

Paul N6PSE
