Last year, world traveler, expeditioner, mountain climber and film-maker Jason Rodi chartered a small ice breaker ship called the Hanse Explorer, for a 33 day voyage from South America to South Georgia, Crozet, Bouvet, and Marion Island. He called this his "Expedition for the Future"
 
He has produced several short films that depict various aspects of his adventure.
 
 
This first film is 4.5 minutes long and shows scenes from So Georgia and Bouvet.
 
http://vimeo.com/41228203
 
 
 
This second film is 11 minutes long and depicts scenes from So Georgia, Crozet and Marion Island.
 
http://vimeo.com/album/1788616/video/61537556
 
 
I think you will find them interesting,
