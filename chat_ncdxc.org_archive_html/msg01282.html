I found there are a few interesting tools on the web for up-to date DX spots.

With the introduction of Skimmers (bots) almost all CQ calling CW and RTTY stations are automatically spotted immediately - sometimes within 2 or 3 seconds of me hearing the CW call. 

DXSUMMIT

One browser window that I usually keep open all the time is the DX Spots screen of DXSUMMIT. The information is refreshed every minute automatically.

   http://www.dxsummit.fi/DxSpots.aspx

When i am interested in a specific band I use the band filter screen:

  http://www.dxsummit.fi/CustomFilter.aspx

If trying to work a specific station I often use the Dx Summit search feature to find out where the DX is at right now:

  http://www.dxsummit.fi/Search.aspx

Plug in the call sign in questions.  I used 9U4U in my case. This shows me who is spotting the DX and where they are at. This is a sample result:

CLUBLOG

I use club log.org quite a bit. There is a feature that tailors DX spots to what I need based on my uploaded log. Only All Time New Ones are listed. If you have an account there, click on the "DXCLuster" link in the "Club Log Tools" section

  https://secure.clublog.org/personal_spots.php
