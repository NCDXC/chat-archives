Both the Comfort Suites and Marriott at Convention Center were sold out by 10:35 am.  This was confirmed by telephone.

 

Some of you may have accidentally made a reservation at the Comfort Suites since it was the only hotel listed at 10 am.  At that time, I called and they said "Give us a few minutes and the rest of the hotels will be listed," which did indeed happen.  I got one reservation for myself at the Marriott, then tried to make a second for N6XI, but by then they were all gone.

 

I again talked to a nice lady at the Convention and Visitors Bureau.  She assured me that it was not just one guy booking 20-50 rooms at once that locked everyone out.

 

All reservations will transfer from the "passkey" system to the individual hotel reservation systems no sooner than March 16th, 2016.  At that time you'll be able to transfer reservations to someone else if needed.  So at that time usually some rooms will open up as people cancel.


73,

Bob, N6TV
