VU3FWG was on 10M FT8 with a good signal all day today — both long path and short path.

 

I tried calling him both ways off and on for several hours, in between weekend chores.  Almost gave up but finally worked him at 0023 UTC.  I saw both WB6JJJ and N6IE calling him all day….hope they got through.
