Al, et.al.,

Reminds me of the "Great List Debate"at the "Joint Meeting" in Fresno (Hilton Hotel) in 1979.

Around that time, we were visiting my parents in the North California, and my mom said that I should visit one of their neighbors "who is a DXer." I did, and I (delicately) found that he was an aficionado of Lists and Nets. 

Interestingly, however, as he told me how he really liked the comradery of the group, I decided to keep quiet - very quiet. I have since realized that it was the social network of the day, and he really enjoyed it!
 
I wisely kept my comments to myself and left our visit with a slightly different perspective.
