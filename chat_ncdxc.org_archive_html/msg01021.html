The Gizmo/Gadget that wins my vote is MicroHAM’s microKEYER II.

 

This gadget is impressive.  I found it to be so well thought out in its features, construction and flexibility.  The quality of the parts in particular the special cable for the connection to all the jacks on the radio.  First class plugs and professionally installed.

 

Those Slovaks thought of everything.  It takes care of all audio and signaling needs as an interface between a computer and the radio plus user inputs (footswitch etc.) and the radio.   As those who have one know, it can be a bit of a challenge learning how to do everything but the US support guy, Joe Subich W4TV is very helpful.  Amazing gizmo.

 

See www.microham.com/index1.html and click on About to learn more about the company.
