I think the term "self adsorbed" is too kind for much of the behavior on display in the pileups. These were schoolyard bullies all grown up and armed with radios.

What we can address are the DX Kops who are transmitting on top of the DX. Some are trying to be helpful and some are just adding to the Lids doing the same thing.

I clearly recognized the voices of three of our club members among those DX Kops transmitting on top of FT5ZM. One of those club members even picked up the wrong mic and started barking on W6TI.

It is never okay to transmit on top of the DX intentionally. We all make mistakes with split on/off but intentionally transmitting over the DX is never appropriate.
