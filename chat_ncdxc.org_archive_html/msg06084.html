Wow!  In the span of just a few days, I managed to work some major ATNOs in regions that I thought would elude me for a very long time - and I literally just received my 101'st confirmed QSO on LoTW!  I've requested my certificate, so I guess Im almost in the club.  As for DX in the past couple of days, I managed to work 4Z4DX, VU2GSM, TA3X, 4S7FRG, and 9J2T on three bands. 

 

These, along with AD8J/YA, were all on my long-shot list.  I've never been able to copy any ops from these areas in the past, and didn't think I would be able to any time soon. Seems like they began to fall for me just as the solar flux dropped below 150.  Go figure.  All-in-all, not bad for a sunspot cycle that the experts were saying would be anemic at best.

 

Now comes the pile of about 90 or so QSL's that I have to get out.
