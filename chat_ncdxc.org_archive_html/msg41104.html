For those that are using Ham Radio Deluxe for your logging program. 

I highly encourage you to hold off on updating to version 6.9.0

While the upgrade goes well and updates the log database, 

It completely breaks the ability to export your log in plain ADIF format. 

This means that you cannot upload to Clublog or M0URX. 

Ham Radio Deluxe says they will have a bug fix out soon that should remedy the issue.
