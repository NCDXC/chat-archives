On Sat, Jun 20, 2020 at 10:06 AM Steve Jones N6SJ via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

I saw in the Silent Keys of the new July QST that George Allan, W6YD has passed away.

 

I first met George when I first attended the IDXC in Visalia around 1996 or 97.  He was running the convention and rushing around like a whirling dervish.  He did it ALL!

 

Around 10 years later John Eisenberg K6YP and I took over the convention from George for the 2007 and 2009 IDXC’s.  I remember John and I sitting with George at a restaurant (in Los Banos?) trying to do a “download” of all his convention tasks and procedures. George was very generous with all his details, but it became clear that he ran the convention by “feel”.  We had to invent our own process!  But George was always very enthusiastic and complimentary of our “new-fangled” way of doing the convention.  It’s hard to believe he put that huge effort in for 10 years or more!  I know he had great help from Dick Letrich, W6KM, who carried on as our Treasurer for a long time.

 

I always enjoyed seeing George, either at the IDXC, or occasionally at our monthly NCDXC meetings in Sunnyvale.  It was a long drive for him, so he didn’t show up that often.  George always had this great exuberant and mischievous grin on his face!

 

“Yankee Doodle” RIP, I’ll miss you sorely.

 

73,

Steve

N6SJ
