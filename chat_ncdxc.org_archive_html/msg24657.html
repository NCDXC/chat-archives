Roberto and Paul,

 

Wake Island was my best DX of the year too. Randy was a real gentleman and very patient when our first sked didn’t work out, he gave ma another and we just made the SSB QSO (he was using 10 or 20 watts on the beach). 

 

And the protracted haggling with ARRL over whether Randy’s QSOs would count was also memorable - all turned out well in the end. 

 

Other highlights of 2021 included ATNOs including Bangladesh, Jordan, Iraq, Mauritius and Réunion using just a multiband vertical with an incomplete radial field, due to a small lot and neighbors who care too much about their view!
