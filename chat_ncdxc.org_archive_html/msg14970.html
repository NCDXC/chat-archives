I worked 3B7A on Sunday, same time, same frequency, same mode, also using 100W (waiting for the linear to warm up), but I'm in the flatlands.

How well do you hear them on 18130 from the hilltop?  Marginal down here.

At an antipode, you get antipodal focusing, which makes the path less lossy.  The real difficulty with 3B7 is that it is straight over the pole.  Compare to tran-equatorial ZS, which is duck soup, but the same distance as 3B7.  I once observed one end of a QSO from
W6 to ZS with both ends being mobile stations.
