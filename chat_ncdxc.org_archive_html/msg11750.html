I could barely hear them on 1818.5 with an Inverted-U antenna that I installed last year.  He listened down at about 1817.1 to 1817.5.

Thought I heard him come back to me (I was calling with 800 watts), but never saw it logged.  He did work several West Coast stations but I didn’t hear anyone in the Bay Area come through.  Only Washington and Oregon.  Did hear W6SZN and KA7T calling but no confirmation.

The good news is that the VK0EK team is listening to advice from the Pilots.  They put a station on 160M this morning at the right time.

I suspect they’ll be on TopBand every day between 1300-1400 UTC looking for West Coast.

They should be moving to the more favorable Spit Bay location by the end of this week.  That has to be done via the Braveheart — not over ground.

Not sure what 160M antenna will be activated from there.  Stay tuned.
