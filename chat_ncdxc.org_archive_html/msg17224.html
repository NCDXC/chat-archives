Or, where is he now?

I am missing the E31 action and everything else as I wander around EU with the XYL.  Had to go to YO to try to make  progress on selling the house but our main dude ended up in a hospital in Frankfurt.  So, as we wait for him to recover from whatever put him there, we headed out in the rental car.  The Romanian/Serbian  border guard actually asked for our phone number and wants to see our place!  This is the same border I crossed in May 1983 and boy, was that a different experience.

YT was first with a night in Belgrade.  I saw an old tribander along the way but the XYL would not let me stop and snoop!  Then on to 4O which is a beautiful country.  Lots of mountains that would make a good QTH but only in some directions.  Then, up to 9A which has lots of beautiful coastline and historical places.  Having been here in 1983, things have changed in major ways.  They have discovered tourism big time and some of the charm is gone forever.

We drove back to 4O and on a whim, decided on a right turn to ZA.  Different to be sure an worth a day and overnight to look things over in a very active city.  My driving skills were put to the test with cars, pedestrians, bicycles, dogs and double parked cars blocking traffic lanes.  Some close calls but out this afternoon OK..

Today, Serbia put its troops on highest alert for new Kosovo problems.  The XYL tells me that we are NOT going to try to go to Z66.  Darn!.  Going to have to save that side trip for another time.

Back to YT tomorrow and on to Romania to see if we can do what we came to this region to do.

Good DX to all in the meantime.
