Just in case it was missed, Clublog OQRS is now active for KH1/KH7Z.

So I went on Clublog to get the card (LOTW already confirms, I like the cards too)...  It turns out that they show 17 contacts for me, taking 14 band slots; 3 dupes.  160M CW was on purpose, he was so loud (an honest S9) and easy, it just didn't seem real so since he wasn't busy, I tried again (2nd call, both times). Both panned out, it WAS that easy.

Two other dupes are in FT8.  One contact logged twice within a few minutes (counts as the same anyway) because an ACK packet wasn't received (20M) by me, so the program continued trudging along, THEN got the RR73 and logged at both ends.  I knew this, saw it in the online log but know it was from ONE conversation in FT8.

The other FT8 (160M), they got the report from me and logged it, I didn't get the RR73 from them so it didn't log on my end (I got them later, so I didn't lose out).  This isn't the first time I've noted it but it was the first time in DXpedition mode.

My take is that FT8 is viable but that DXpedition mode isn't quite there yet because there is no confirmation method after the final RR73, it's presumed, but one side can still be unlogged, negating the entire conversation in the confirmation process.  It works but is still a tad fragile (well so is any other mode).

All in all, I had a blast.  Well done guys, well done.
