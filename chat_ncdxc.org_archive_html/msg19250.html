I have been in touch with SCDXC to suggest they hold a virtual DX convention event this April.  

I thought it would be fun for them to create a little excitement by conducting a free DX teleconference via WebEx or ZOOM.

Everyone would like to hear a first-hand report from VP8PJ on their trip to South Orkney Island as well as other DX-related topics from speakers who had already committed to be in Visalia.  

A schedule could be published in advance on their website, with login details so folks can call in from their homes around the country to participate.  Several one-hour seminars could be held during “DX Day”.  If necessary, the same topic could be repeated on different days, etc.  

It would be an opportunity to preserve the 71st IDXC meeting this year in a unique way.

They’re considering it.  Fingers crossed.
