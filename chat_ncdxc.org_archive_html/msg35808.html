Marek (FT4GL) has just communicated the two days he wishes to dedicate to ATNO... It is active for people who HAVE NEVER CONTACTED FT4GL on Friday June 14 and Sunday June 16 from 11 a.m. UTC... Thanks to the stations who already made the QSO to play the game and give the LITTLE PISTOLs a chance. If this turns into a nightmare, blame the troublemakers and not Marek who wants to give everyone a chance. Team FT4GL


If you have worked Marek on ANY BAND OR MODE, please sit back and let everyone else have a shot at it. Glorioso may not be activated again for a decade or more. He’s not that hard to work if all the “new slot chasers” just shut up for a couple of days. From the Left Coast, look for him on 20/17/15/12m. He will try to focus on SSB, but may also be operating FT8 at the same time. On FT8, he is F/H, so call above 1000 Hz.


Please make sure you can HEAR HIM before you CALL HIM (on any mode).
