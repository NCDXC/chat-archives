Don’t know about “Worked all UN”, but I once received a solicitation to apply for the "Ladies of Siofok" Award, which is given for making some specified number of contacts with members of the Siofok YL Club (Siofok is a county in Hungary). No big deal, we see these kinds of mini-awards all the time, but they sent me a sample of the award and I was intrigued by the language they used.

The award looks like a diploma, and the border contains pictures of all of the YLs in the club, with their callsigns. In the middle is the proclamation, which reads:

"Officially confirmed that [callsign] has made bilateral intercourse with the radio amateur champion young ladies of Siofok," and is signed by Eva, HA3FRE.

It's hard enough to make 2-way contacts with these fine Hungarian YLs, but I can't imagine having that much bilateral intercourse, especially given the distance. I think Anna needs to fix the translation for them (unless they are really celebrating bilateral intercourse, which might justify its own award).
