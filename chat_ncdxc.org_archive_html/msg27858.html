Jerry, a couple of tips I can offer:
1) only call if you can hear him (odd or even slot)
2) only call on the opposite slot (after you hear him and know which slot he's on)
3) find an open frequency to call
4) He's F/H if the DX transmits on even, then you're on odd.  F/H also if he transmits to more than one station on the same freq/slot. We transmit above 1KHz.
5) If he's transmitting odd, in this case tonight, assume FT8 regular, or MSHV and you can transmit anywhere (but not his freq), even.
Well, that's what I've doing at least.  Let me know if I missed something.

Good luck.
