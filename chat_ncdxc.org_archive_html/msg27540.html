It is scary how much money can go into this "hobby".  There are quite a few privately built contest stations here in the US that focus on antennas - for sure (e.g. Craig, K9CT).  Those fortunate to have a successful business (especially if it's a related business like KC4NX).  I'm not sure how many there are out west vs the East, due to property values I suppose.  Hmm - why I'm in Arizona now.   Tom, N6BT's antenna farm is quite impressive just 10 miles north of me.
