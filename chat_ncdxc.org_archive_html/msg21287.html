Why not try out the Zombie Shuffle “contest” on Friday night? (Why a “shuffle”? Because Zombies can’t Sprint.)

http://www.zianet.com/qrp/ZOMBIE/pg.htm

It’s the craziest set of rules, designed to maximize fun. Runs from 4 pm to midnight, local time, on 80/40/20 meters, CW only, QRP ONLY!

There is even an N1MM module for it. Folks are encouraged to pick “scary” names, and scoring is so exponential that you can make a handful of QSOs and still score in the millions! Part of the exchange is your Zombie number, but if you don’t have one, use your area code, or zip code, or address number; it doesn’t matter, and there is no log checking.

This year, yours truly K6MMM will be one of the Special Bonus Stations, sending Zombie Number 2020, and my “name” will be “Covid”. (Ain’t nothing scarier than Covid 2020!)

I will be on 20m for the first hour or so, then go to 40 and 80 after dinner for a few more hours.

If you want to run, call “CQ BOO”.

Come and be a Zombie for an evening. (Although some of you may be zombies MOST of the time, anyway.)

Rich KE1B/K6MMM
