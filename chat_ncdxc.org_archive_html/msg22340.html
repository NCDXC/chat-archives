I found this useful and my results generally reflect my RX EQ settings in my K3.  I may tweak them a bit more with this data.

 

https://hearingtest.online/

 

I took the test once with my PC speakers, and once on my iPhone with a set of wired earbuds.  The results were very similar by audio frequency range and, as I expected, up a level or two with the earbuds as they blocked most ambient noise.

 

At the end of the test, you are able to print out the audiogram from your test, which is useful
