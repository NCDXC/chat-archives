I use Windy, too.  Be aware that the data can be pulled rom two sources.  I use the ECMWF source based on a long time worldwide sailor with experience, K7ZSD.  Also know that this data is one every 12 hours.  It is not real time so it changes infrequently.  You can zoom in and click on a place and it will pull up the forecast for that location and give you several parameters by hour for a few days. My example:
https://www.windy.com/42.410/-124.420?rain,42.465,-124.412,9

Windfinder is another to look over.  Lots of configuration. 
https://www.windfinder.com/#5/43.3412/-111.0498/2023-01-04T18:00Z/report

 And if you want clouds and rain, try Wetter.com, a Deutsch site but lots can be done there if you are patient and figure it out.

GL with the storm.  I am forecasted for 80 kt gusts tonight.  Tower heading to the lowest setting!
