I don't think you can infer the location of a station by its signal strength. There are Germans operating with legal limit to gain antennas and others operating 20W and a wire.
Also, DL is 30 degrees from the Bay Area and 5A1 is 37. Unless you have an extremely high gain antenna it would be difficult to asses the real direction with such a small difference.
I'm not saying it's not legit. 
However, what gives me a bit of suspicion is the fact that he never stays on a band more than a few minutes. Stations who don't want to be located by triangulation somehow operate that way. 
Correlation is not causality. Hope he's legit. I have him on 2 bands.

My two cents,
Roberto

I tend to think that 5A1AL is really in Libya for these reasons:

1) His signals are in the right direction of 37 degrees for me. 
2) His signals are very weak, relying on propagation for the heavy lifting. German signals are always significantly stronger. 
3) A few years ago, Jonathan W6GX led a fund raising effort to help 5A1AL improve his station. 
4) Given 5A1AL's activity level, if he was truly operating in Northern Germany, I think he would have been found by now.
