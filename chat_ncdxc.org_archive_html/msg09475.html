I have just received word from NCDXC President Mike, K6MKF, that the NCDXC intends to make a matching fund donation of $1000 - which will double to $2000 due to our "angel" matching fund participant.

I want to thank Mike, the Board and the entire NCDXC for this vote of confidence, and also share something I have been doing that is very much special for West Coast hams:

http://vk0ek.org/propagation/

Over a year ago, I asked Dean, N6BV if he could run some HFTA plots of Heard Island, because back in 1997, VK0IR had some difficulties working the West Coast. So much so, they resorted to having a "West Coast Day". The problem? Like my QTH here in Orinda - there are blocking mountains. In fact - on Heard Island its much worse - the mountain that blocks the West Coast is 8000 feet! Its called "Big Ben" or Mawson Peak.

With modern technologies, including Dean's HFTA and Stu, K6TU's propagation analysis tool, we have been able to hone in on the best way to enable better access to Heard Island for the West Coast. Furthermore, we have just received some additional excellent news. On Heard Island, there are scientific shelters that were not there in 1997, and they are called "Apples". We have been granted access to these shelters both at Atlas Cove and Spit Bay! And to top that off - we have also been granted use of amphibious vehicles by our charter - Heritage Adventures. There is even more great news ahead - but I better wait until Dr. Bob gets back from his ZL, VK, HS and XW world tour. 

I have been working with Dave, K3EL, Vadym, UT6UD and our antenna guru and mentor, Tom, N6BT, (who I have worked with for years now). His experience with both Team Vertical as well as the entire Force-12 line of antennas will serve us very well. I have been so honored to work with such Northern California "mentors" Dean, Tom and Stu, and value them as members of our team. Its been a ton of fun!

I'm offering a few extra little tidbits here because my heart and soul has been in this project - and very much with the West Coast in mind, and this donation and vote of unanimous support means everything to me and the entire VK0EK Team. 

Thanks so much - and we aim to produce amazing signals to the West Coast!

73,

Rich
