Interesting conditions this morning on 80M FT8

From 1445 to 1530, I had strong decodes from 4Z5AV, YO7CFD, LA9GX, YL2SM, UN/OH7O and others. 
They were -10 to -12 at times but all were completely deaf. 

They called CQ a lot and primarily worked EU stations. 


Nice to see good conditions on 80M despite no QSOs made.
