FWIW, I have seven confirmed contacts so far with PT0S using my amplifier
and zero contacts running barefoot. I only run 100W on 12M and I have
*really* tried to work them barefoot on 12M apparently without success. I
thought that I had worked them twice on 12M CW but I am not yet in their log
for a 12M contact. My 100W on 12M just isn't cutting it...yet.

I intend to keep trying on 12M with just 100 W but I am not optimistic I
will be successful with the current propagation and level of "competition"
on 12M for PT0S.

With the short time left to work these guys (2 days...maybe???) I recommend
borrowing an amplifier asap and go QRO. Life is too short IMHO with this DX
operation to run strictly "QRP". The wonderful guys on the "Rocks" are tired
and they are short on food, sleep and they are having equipment and antenna
problems. They deserve QRO signals from us if we can do it!!! :-)

If I had a spare linear amplifier I would loan you one... but sadly my
functional extra linear amplifiers are up at are "cabin" in the Sierras
right now.

GL and congratulations on working the other DX (Maldives and Uganda)!!

73 and GL, Ted, K6XN

PS See you in the pileups on 12M with the other low power guys!  GL to us!
