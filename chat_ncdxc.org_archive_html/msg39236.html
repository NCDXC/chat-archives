My Hygain T2X “tail twister” has finally packed it in. It’s been getting stalled from time to time, but would always recover after a spin to the opposite direction. I’ve concluded that the issue is internal to the rotor, such as missing teeth on the ring gear, since the mast/antenna assembly rotates easily by hand. This afternoon I had to uncouple the rotor from the mast and swing the beam manually to 3B9 to get the my needed 10m slot.

Norm’s rotor repair is out-of-business, and I’m not even sure about parts availability. Does anyone know of any surviving rotor repair businesses?

Dave, WI6R has a solid used one waiting for me at Visalia, but I’d like to get my current one up and running as a spare for the future.
