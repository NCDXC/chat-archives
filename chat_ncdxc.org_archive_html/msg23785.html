After similar weirdness that only affected me on 15, I purchased a big, fat, shielded, and filtered USB A>C cable. That cured my issues completely. As always, be sure your station is well bonded, and if you need more RF filtration, consider some large mix 31 ferrite rings that allow a gazillion turns of cable. DigiKey and Palomar have them all with rapid shipping. 
My 2 cents… now if I can only get rid of that warbling tone I hear in my filled molar during transmit cycles.
