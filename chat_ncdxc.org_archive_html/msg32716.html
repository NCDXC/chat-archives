My wife and I really enjoyed this year’s Holiday Launcheon. The first since the pandemic. 

The buffet was good. Plenty of selections and large plates to hold it all. 🍽️🍮☕️. 

It was good to see so many of you !  Eventually we had to stop ✋ taking so Art could say a few words then head us to the buffet. 

I really enjoyed reconnecting with so many friends. 

THEN John Miller K6MM put on his presentation. As always John was entertaining and certainly fun. He had us laughing. Thanks John-we sure enjoy your presentations. 

BTW: Michaels A/V equipment and installation worked flawlessly!  

It was nearly 3pm, I was full but had just enough energy to get into the car. Sure was good getting out of the house and seeing everyone. Thanks for coming.
