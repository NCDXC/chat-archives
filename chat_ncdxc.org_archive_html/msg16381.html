The TT-1 was Heathkits only mutual conductance tube tester.   I believe it was their last tube tester. The previous models tested just emissions.  I have never used one but hear from the antique radio guys that it is a very good unit.  That price seems kinda high but maybe that is the going price for one in good condx.

Could you get by with an emissions tube tester ?  Could get on for a lot less.
