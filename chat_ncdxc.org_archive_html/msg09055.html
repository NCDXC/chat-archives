The change in the operating options for this once 'rare' entity is amazing!  I wonder how much the tsunami changed the government attitudes.
 
VU4, ANDAMANS ISLANDS. Charly, HS0ZCW/K4VUD/VU3CHE, and a team are plan-
ning to activate VU4 between between March 6-18th. This will be simple
antennas and 100 watts operation. Their activity can be improved with
loaned or donated gear if anyone wishes to help them. They are only
looking for antennas and gear only, no cash for the operators. If you
are interested to help, contact Charly at:    hs0zcw@gmail.com

ADDED NOTE: The National Institute of Amateur Radio (NIAR), invites all
radio amateurs to Port Blair (VU4) for the "Hamtech 2015", between March
6-7th. Special licenses are available for foreign visitors.
 
All information is available at:    http://niar.org/downloads/niar-info.pdf
