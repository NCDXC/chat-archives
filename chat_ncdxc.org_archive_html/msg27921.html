Screenshots of FT8 QSO's are a great idea, anytime it's a new entity, mode or band slot. Often even if the QSO was not logged by the other station, they can find you in their ALL.TXT log which records all FT8 activity. For example, if signal reports were exchanged but the RR73 or 73 was not heard by the other party, emailing them the screenshot may provide the proof that you need to convince your QSO partner to amend their log.

OTOH there is some divergence of thought as to what constitutes a valid FT8 QSO (exchange of signal reports? RR73 or RRR sent? RR73 RRR received?  73 sent?  73 received?) and there are some hams who will never amend their log. But screenshots have saved my bacon many times.

73 and good DX,
Andy K6ZP
