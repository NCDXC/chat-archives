I've earned all my Q's (243 countries and counting) *AT MY* station in my spare time and never moved further than 120 miles from my first (meager) station.  I've been offered elegant stations to use but my moral code won't allow me to cheapen my efforts.  I will earn those Q's on my own, with my own gear at my station; period.  Anything less is cheating.

A return to the 150 mile from 'home' rule (all contacts originate within 150 mile radius of the starting point) for any award is the 'high road'; to tighten or challenge hams to do better instead of pandering or wimping out.  That rule would allow reasonable accommodation for the HOA inflicted or similar for entities that span multiple time zones.

Shame on you ARRL, shame, shame, shame!  You've opened the door wider to cheapen the certificate.   Shame!

73,
