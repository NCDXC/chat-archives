Hi Jerry;

They are NOT using Fox/Hound, even though they made a big deal about saying they would.  Don't know why.

So turn off Fox/Hound mode, and call during the Even period, listen Odd.  The bottom line is if you can hear them when not transmitting but you stop hearing them when you start to transmit, then you're probably calling on top of them during the wrong period.
