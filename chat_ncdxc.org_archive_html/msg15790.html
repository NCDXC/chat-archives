Hi all,
 
The California QSO Party is coming up next month and lots of stations will be looking to work as many CA counties as possible.  Many of you might already know this, but only recently did I learn that I can automatically add my CA County to the info I upload to LoTW.  If you’re interested in doing this, start by opening your TQSL program.  Select "Station Locations" from the drop down menu.  Click on your callsign (in case you happen to have multiple LoTW certificates for multiple stations).  That will open an "Edit Station Location" window which permits you to enter your grid square, ITU Zone and CQ Zone.  The trick now is to click on the "Next" box in the lower portion of that window.  That will open yet another window which allows you to enter your state and county.  Once you do that, close all the windows and you are done.  From now on, your county will automatically be included in your LoTW confirmation data.
 
I hope this is a useful hint for folks like me who didn’t know how to do this.
