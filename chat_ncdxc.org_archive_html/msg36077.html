I have Firefox delete everything (cookies, local storage, etc.) every time I close it. The tradeoff of minor inconvenience is worth the increase in security and privacy I feel.
Deleting all cookies should not "break" any web site - although they would like you to think it might. What would they do if they couldn't track and catalog your every move on the internet?

Firefox + uBlock Origin blocks pretty much all the nasty trackers with very few side effects. Highly recommended.
