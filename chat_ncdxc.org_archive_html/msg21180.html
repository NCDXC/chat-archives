JX2US operation, which involved an op with
a work assignment on the island.  At the

from roughly 6 to 8 PM local, he is on, then
goes QRT.  The time between dinner and bedtime.

not open to stateside at that time.  I see
no spots from this side of the Atlantic.

the real JX DXpedition planned for next year.
