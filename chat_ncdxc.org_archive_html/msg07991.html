I spent the whole weekend working W1AW/7 remotely from home via RemoteHamRadio.com to help out W7RN in Nevada.

Made 1,780 WARC Band QSOs from all over the world.  50% CW, 50% SSB.  What a blast.

Stopped for 30 minutes to try to work Monk Apollo today on 18.150 SSB.  I could clearly hear him but couldn't get through the EU wall.
He graced us with his presence for about 60 minutes and then was gone.  As someone posted today, " His duty is not to this world".  : >)

Yes...great weekend for DX.
