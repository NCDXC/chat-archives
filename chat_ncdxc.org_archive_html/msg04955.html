Rich,

I started DXing in Oct 1978 with a converted CB radio. Worked around 150 countries on 10m QRP. I made Honor Roll in March 2001 all on QRP, along with the 1500 level of the DXCC Challenge. So 22 years to Honor Roll with QRP.

I'm still 3 short. FR/G - worked 3 times on QRP, but none counted due to French protests; VU4 - stupidly tried to work them on QRP; and T5 - hear 6O0LA often, but can never break the EU pileups.

#1 Honor Roll is a lifetime achievement and is not easy. 

Steve, K6UM
