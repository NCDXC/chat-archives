Fellow DXers,

 

Today, Bob Allphin, K4UEE, who is an Honorary NCDXC Member, announced that his KP1-5 Project team had been granted permission to operate a Dxpedition from Navassa Island (KP1) in January, 2015.  Their complete Press Release is available here:  http://kp1-5.com/new/news.htm

 

According to Club Log, Navassa is the #1 Most Wanted Entity, based on their Global Log.

 

Upon reading this news, the NCDXC Board of Directors voted to donate $1000 to the KP1-5 Project team’s Navassa Island Dxpedition, and that donation has been completed by PayPal.

 

Bob Lanning, W6OPO, led the effort of writing the NCDXC letter of recommendation for Bob, K4UEE, and his KP1-5 Project team.   The NCDXC letter must have contributed in some measure to their success, and we hope that the Club’s donation will help them have an outstanding Dxpedition.

 

- 73 and good DX de Mike, K6MKF, President - NCDXC, IDXC 2015, W6NAG, IDXG, ADXG, RRC #933, K3-P3-KPA500-KAT500 Addict, Maui
