Only one program can be listening to wsjt-x at a time. So when you click "listen" in jtsync, other things like jtalert or (my favorite) Gridtracker won't hear it. You only need to listen for one or two cycles, hit "calculate" if you want to sync to the barrage of everyone else, adjust the clock, then unclick "listen" and the other programs will go back to hearing wsjt-x. 

If you're using it to sync to a single station instead of going for the average, you don't even need to click listen. Just enter the dt value from the other station manually and adjust.
