I want to add my support/experience for VK0EK’s Contact Us process.
I just noticed Rich’s KY6R message about getting requests via his personal email.  He goes on to say – “…PLEASE use the “Contact Us” so that the 10 very dedicated and hard working DXer / Support agents can help you resolve your issue or answer your questions.
73, Rich KY6R”

The Contact Us invokes a Help Desk process that works – I have first hand experience with it.  If there is an issue you need a response for use Contact Us.  You will get an immediate acknowledgement of your request and a response in a few hours or a day – I did.  The Help Desk sometimes called Trouble Desk, works!
