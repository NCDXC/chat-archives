Some more info for those wanting ET3AA from Bob, W9XY:

 

The last 3 days have been a holiday called Epiphany.  Security was tight so they couldn't get in there.

 

I asked about 20 meters since much of the activity as been on 10, 12, 15 and 17 meters, and his answer was:

 

"No problem, glad to help! About 20, we’ll probably continue on the higher bands for a bit. Right now they are pleasantly surprised to be able to work so many US stations on 17 after the bands being bad for so long. Still, if propagation isn’t so good, we can move up to 20. The daily noise at sunset is a huge issue too, and I know it’s really a stretch for you guys on the west coast because it’s early out there. I’ll continue to work with them when possible. My schedule is four days on, two off, so you never know when we will be active.  😉

 

73 again!  Bob"

 

I'd be looking for them, and will, this Monday and Tuesday mornings.

 

73, Paul, W6IBU
