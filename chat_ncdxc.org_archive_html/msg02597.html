Hello Ross Forbes --

 

Could you let the members of your amateur radio organization know about two large yagi antennas that will be for sale on Ebay? The antennas are located in Carmichael, a suburb of Sacramento. The owner is deceased; a school bought the property and will be building on it.

 

The largest five-element yagi has an estimated boom length of 46 feet and the driven element is approximately 35 feet long. The tower sold with the antenna is approximately 75 feet high. There is also a discone-type antenna above the yagi.

 

The smaller five-element yagi has an estimated boom length of 24 feet and the center element is approximately 22 feet long. Because of coils on three of the elements, this yagi must be a multi-bander.

 

The Ebay auctions on these antennas will begin on Saturday, June 29 and end 10 days later on July 9. For more details on the antennas, details of the sale, and excellent photos of them, go to auction number 271229704510 for the larger yagi and auction number 271229716564 for the smaller yagi.

 

I am handling the auction on behalf of the school. Hams should contact me if they would like to visit the school to view the antennas.
