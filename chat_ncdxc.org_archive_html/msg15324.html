It was clearly stated on the team info and blog pages that version 1.9.1 was needed.  I've seen it mentioned on some of the DX pages as well.  The word was passed.

It appears that if someone is using 1.9.0 and completes the contact (the popup displays) that the fox (running 1.9.1) may not have the same entry, so the contact is 'lost' (logged only at the hound end).  The log upload seems to confirm this as well.  (I've been using 1.9.1 and my FT8 were confirmed in the upload, one I couldn't even hear.)

While frustrating, even heart breaking, it's a growing pain of FT8 since the DXpedition mode is mere months old.  Make an insurance dupe if you were using 1.9.0 is the safest answer, UNLESS it was confirmed in the upload.

Use ONLY the latest in FT8, even if it goes into beta again.

RIck nhc
