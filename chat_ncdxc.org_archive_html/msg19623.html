JT1CO is at 332 degs SP from my station in CM97.  I consistently the strongest decodes on that heading, ranging from -11 to -19.   No decodes more that 15 degrees off this heading, and no decodes LP.  SteppIR 3L at 38’.

 

Propagation predictions forecast an S5- S6 signal at 0400Z.

 

My conclusion is that this signal is emanating from approximately 332 degrees, which agrees with Mongolia.  I also note that JT1CO has been calling CQ for over 14 minutes without a break.   So, the operator is either manually defeating the ‘TX watchdog’ feature or this is some FT8 software without TX time limits. 

 

At this point, it’s just an FT8 beacon from grid OO20 …
