My biggest regret was ignoring the laws of physics, and putting up 80 and 160 horizontal antennas that I did not have adequate support to fix at effective heights. 
For suburban lots with only a 40’ mast and 40’ trees, only verticals work on 80 and 160. 
Low horizontal antennas are great for reception, but verticals with lots of radials seem to be the only solution. Now if we could only amend the laws of physics!
Oh… that, and every antenna I ever bought with traps.
