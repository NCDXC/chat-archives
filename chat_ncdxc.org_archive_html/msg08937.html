I'm puzzle by why we reprint pieces like this without comment. For example, statements like "it was easy to work over 200 entities on 10 meters during 2014." True only if you happen to live near the Atlantic basin. My favorite example of "east coast blinders" is a statement by KX9X in QST that QRP DXCC in a weekend contest was "easy." Hell -- my FIRST weekend DXCC with 1.5kW and a damned nice antenna farm was six years after I moved here, and I tried REAL hard. Several members with great stations and who are fine operators have noted how wonderful conditions are when they can work 110 countries on a single band in a weekend. 

73, Jim K9YC
