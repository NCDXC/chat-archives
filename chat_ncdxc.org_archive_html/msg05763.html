I also was disappointed--but not surprised--nothing has changed in Newington.  To call it a committee report while also reflecting a personal view was not a surprise....who says this whole complicated mess isn't the  personal fiefdom of a few?  The report is wandering, call it unfocused, and generally doesn't say much beyond "we  are studying matters"..which is also true to form.  It was also disappointing to read that "input...has been generally slow with a few exceptions".  I'm not sure what they expected for so little effort to gather input much less encourage it.  Further,it remains very strange to me that they asked for a survey of the subject from a British club--but not, to my knowledge, from any stateside groups.
