On Tue, Mar 12, 2019 at 9:54 AM Kevin Rowett via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
I use INSTEON devices in my house for automated and remote control functions.

Recently, I added a brand new, 2477S device - it’s a  wall mount on/off switch.  While “Linking” it, I pressed the off paddle. I heard a buzz - about 200Hz.  Listening closer, I copied “K”.  The timing isn’t weighted very well, but, it clearly is morse code.  I pressed the ON paddle, out comes more morse code. Can’t copy it yet, as the low tones made it hard to get all the symbol, and the volume is very low.  The sound of the paddle and the relay click cover up the first few elements.
