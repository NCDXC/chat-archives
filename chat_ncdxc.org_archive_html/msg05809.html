While dreaming about a scientist / ham / DX-er showing up at Norway's new Bouvet base and activating it for say, a year (heh heh), I started wondering if Bouvet's propagation is more like South Africa - or something more along the lines of Tristan de Cunha / Gough or Ascension. 

I did hear Petrus when he was on Bouvet, and while he had a vertical and 100 watts, by the time I could call him - he said he was having a terrible wind storm and had to shut down. It seemed to me at the time that propagation was a bit "easier" from the West Coast beaming on the Short Path. 

Of course the big issue would be along the lines of what happened with PT0S - NA, SA, AF and EU could all hear them and work them equally strong, and so it didn't seem like the West Coast had it too easy. Luckily I already had St. Peter and Paul Rocks on several bands - and chose not to even bother with PT0S. 

Bouvet would have similar "howling" and nasty pileups.

Your thoughts?
