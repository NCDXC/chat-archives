Greetings,
I have had solar electric for eleven years and think it is clean. Although recently a big noise problem has developed for me and I haven’t figured out what or where is the source of the problem. My inverter is a German unit SMA Sonny Boy.  There have been two articles that I know of in QST about solar electric  systems. A recent one which may have been in 2018 discusses how to clean up a solar system.. It also mentions that Solar Edge components are noisy both inverter and I think it was an equalizer at the individual  panels which is  a big problem.  As I recall the article said the ARRL was working with them to clean them up.  You might check with the ARRL and see what they know about different solar electric components when you find out what kind of components are going to be installed near you..,

Dick N6ATD
