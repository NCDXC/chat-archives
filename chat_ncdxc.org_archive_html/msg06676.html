NCDXC Friends,

I'm having a problem with the motor control box on my recently acquired (used) US Tower MA-550MDP, and would like to see if anyone has experience that could be of help in diagnosing the problem.  

Since the unit went operational recently on 12/31/13, I have had to manually trigger the relays inside the control box to raise and lower the tower, this was the method used by the prior owner for several years so was a known issue.  I took the control box to Visalia last month and delivered it to US Tower who diagnosed the problem to be with the up/down limit switch(s) on the tower, not the control box, and two new ones were purchased but have not yet been installed.  I re-installed the control box and the initial attempt to raise the tower a few feet (~5') was successful.  Later during that day however when I went to raise it to its full height, the motor would not engage after many attempts using the same manual method.  After many attempts it did engage but after rising another ~5' I noticed smoke coming out of the "up" relay.  I immediately removed all power and have not made any further attempts to raise or lower the tower. I have reexamined the cabling and have verified that the reconnections were made correctly.  US Tower will not provide a schematic for this unit.  

Does anyone have experience that could help diagnose the problem?

Thanks & 73,
Dennis AG6HE
