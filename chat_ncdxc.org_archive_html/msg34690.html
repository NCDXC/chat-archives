Paul and all,

I agree with Paul’s point. I believe that this particularly important on 6M. Folks relatively new to the band might not know how rare some of these HF common entities are. 

Some might recall my recent presentation to the club on 6M DX’ing and propagation modes, I commented that probably 20-25% of the new ones I’ve worked on 6 were in for a minute or two to maybe 10 minutes. Some not to be heard by me again for months or years. This is very unlike HF where more numerous and predictable openings occur. Most recently I decoded FO5QS for exactly 2 minutes. This would have been number 99 for me on 6M.  However I couldn’t break the pile of 4 / 5 land stations several of which already had FO5 confirmed. 20+ years on 6M and it was the first time I copied FO here - tough path through the mountains for me!

All this to say if it’s rare or rarish on 6M and you have it worked sit back and help others to work it. 

My two cents!

Chuck NA6XX
