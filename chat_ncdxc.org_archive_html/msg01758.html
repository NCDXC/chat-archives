Folks,

I received a call from PG&E today saying they will be installing a SmartMeter in my home.  I asked about RFI problems (as in FROM the meter to me) and was given a number to call.

The CSR I spoke with said that interference is rare but possible so I asked to opt-out… only to be told I have to pay for the privilege!  They wanted a one time fee of $75 plus $10/month…

Needless to say, I'm not paying for their inconvenience…  

So…  does anyone have any experience of these meters and whether they cause interference to your station?
