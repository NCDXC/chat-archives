During my QRV as W6RK (9 years.. before that as OH6EE in early 80s), there has been perhaps handful of HV activations. They're always quick, few days max, mostly unannounced and working EU or propagation is not suitable.

This time it looked promising... and luckily they extended to Monday night QRV when after my last meeting at work I left the office so quick... just to get to the Stanford (where I operate these hard to get countries, home wires not sufficient) just to suddenly hear their signal to drop to noise. Luckily they turned their amp back on (or beamed to USA again?) and it was few calls and they were in the log.

Instant satisfaction, that's what this is sometimes all about! :-)
