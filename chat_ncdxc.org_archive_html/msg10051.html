Are you unable to access the NCDXC club's repeater, W6TI, because you live outside of the San Francisco Bay Area?

Are you on a trip out of the area or at work and wish to listen to the club's repeater?

If you are a member of the club in good standing, there is a solution for you - Echolink.

NCDXC maintains an Echolink link to the W6TI repeater.  From Echolink, you can listen and chat with others using many portable devices and/or your computer without you using any radio equipment.

N6NKT-L Echolink node is able to connect you to the repeater.  If there are questions about Echolink, check out http://www.echolink.org/ .
 
One of the most common problem that has prevent individuals from connecting to the N6NKT-L and other Echolink nodes as been configuration issues of the enduser's internet router.  Please ensure that TCP and UDP ports has been correctly configured into your internet router/fireware.  The ports in question are 5198 - 5200.

If you have questions or technical issues with your router, feel free to email n6nkt@yahoo.com.

Looking forward hearing from members outside the San Francisco Bay Area.
