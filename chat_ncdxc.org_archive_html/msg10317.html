I'm seriously considering purchasing a Flex 6300.

My needs are simple, I am chasing the last 5 I need to get to DXCC Honor Roll #1 and also chase DXCC on 160M.

I've had my K3 for years and use a Ten Tec Eagle twice a year when we stay in Bend, Oregon.

The other thing I am very interested in is propagation monitoring. I've always been much more of an SWL than anything, but besides some RBN testing, I also still look for odd openings. Like this fantastic 20m opening that lasts for 30 minutes starting at 3:30 AM that I used to work FT5ZM with zero competition. That path opens nearly every day and I expect it could be open for VK0EK. I found it by accident while watching the grass grow on 160m.

So, my guess is that the 6300 would be more than enough for my needs.

I'm not interested in setting it up as a skimmer. I'd like to do diversity rx, but someone told me only the 6700 can do that. 

If you gave a Flex 6000 series, I'd appreciate your thoughts.
