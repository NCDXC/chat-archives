Jim, there are a lot of positive things happening in the NCDXC, perhaps you don't see them because you don't come to the meetings.   Speaking of meetings, our next meeting is in Foster City and we are trying to find an East Bay venue for a following meeting.  

Ross and the Board have been listening to the membership. Issues are being addressed.

Yesterday, the Board discussed your issue of access to the club repeater. Solutions and coverage is actively being discussed.

New DXers are actively being mentored. W6OAT and I have opened our shacks for new DXers such as Jake-K6MP and others.  

Sure, there are a few cranky old timers in the club, but there are also many fine, positive people who are eager to share their positive views of this great hobby.

73 and gud DX!
