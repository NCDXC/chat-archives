Alan,

The Potter & Brumfield (TE Connectivity now) KA series Relays have been replaced by the KU series Relays.
The KUP (like the older KAP) is a covered relay designed for Plug-In use, but this particular one could be adapted because it has the same solderable terminals and the mounting can be adapter by moving the relay slightly or placing a new Mounting hole where the Relay Tab holds it to the base.  To do this means removing the snap on clear plastic cover and filing clearance for a screw to mount the relay where there is currently a Flathead 6-32 holding it to the base.  Put a longer Pan head screw there to mount the relay.  



 Search for  KUP-14d15-12    It's only about $16 on Amazon.  

Here are both of the datasheets for the old and new relays to compare.
