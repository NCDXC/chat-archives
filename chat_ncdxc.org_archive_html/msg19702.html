What can you do when you have to stay home?  Build antennas, of course.
I added a wire for 60 meters under my 80 meter dipole.  Very worthwhile band now with FT8, and, of course, they're all "new ones".  
Also been stringing wire, 1,000 feet of it, through the trees for a 630 meter transmit antenna. Only running 36 W drive for an ERP of about 1 W, but getting good WSPR reports.  I'll be able to run more power by this weekend and try some JT9.  (5 W ERP is the legal limit.) 
There's a very active, and growing, group of MFers out there.  See the njdtechnologies.net website if you're interested.
73 and stay safe,
Glen W6GJB
