Keith, Mt. Hamilton might seem like a good place but it is not. The Observatory folks are very RF sensitive up there and the University of California Police will ask you to leave as soon as they spot your antennas. Save your gas and time and go there for the view but not radio.
 
I think the best spots are:
 
1) The large paved lot on Skyline Rd above Palo Alto (Great for North and East)
2) Sierra Rd above East San Jose (Great to the West)
3) Altino Blvd @ Donnici St-Vieira Park on top of Communications Hill in central San Jose.
4) There are a few good spots on Montebello Rd above Cupertino but neighbors are wary of visitors and parked cars.
5) Some guys do very well next to the salt water at the Palo Alto Marina, Shoreline Park in Mtn View etc.
 
 
Good luck!
 
Paul N6PSE
