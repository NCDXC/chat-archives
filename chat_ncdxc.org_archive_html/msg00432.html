Rich and Harry

It wasn’t bad over an hour ago when I originally announced the PT0S on 21295 KHz. I am listening again at 21295 KHz now and his calling frequency is relatively clear here in Saratoga ....still an occasional LID....but I am currently hearing PT0S making five or six unmolested contacts (or more) in a row with minimal to no interference at all.

I am glad I missed the lidfest by working them earlier but if I needed PT0S on 15M SSB I would be in their calling him QSX UP right now.

Call me radical but IMHO most DXers are pretty good and not Lids but it only takes a few to create some serious problems.

If I needed PT0S on 15M SSB I would recommend getting on 21295 KHz and rotate your beam to about 88 degrees right now!

GL es 73, Ted, K6XN

What a madhouse! The V84SMD station was on 21290, listening 5 up. PT0S was on 21295, listening 5-30 up. So lots of folks calling the V84 on top of PT0S, and of course the frequency-cops are telling them that they are all lids, but of course the interferers can't hear the cops because they are listening to V84, 5 down. The V84 realized what was going on, and switched to listening 5 down, i.e., 21285, but not everyone got the message.

BTW, there is never a good reason to request spreads of 25 kHz (and sometimes they are working station waaaay beyond that). On 12 and 17m it can take out the entire band. Even on 15m, it's not necessary.

Worked them about 12 kHz up, on 21307.
