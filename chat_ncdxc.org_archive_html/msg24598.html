I've heard 5V7ST on FT8 over the past couple of days (no QSO yet), but the QRZ page for this callsign lists the callsign as "No Active". The parent callsign OK1FCJ lists a number of DXpeditions on his QRZ page, but the last Togo DXpedition listed was in 2017.

Some folks have labeled this station as a pirate on DXsummit.fi, and I have not seen any mention in Bernie's Daily DX newsletters. But the geographic distribution of stations receiving this station has seemed  consistent with a West African location.

Does anyone know if this is a pirate? It seems somewhat likely.
