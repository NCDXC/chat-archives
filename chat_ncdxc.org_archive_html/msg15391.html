    I was thinking that noon in KH6 would be the optimum time for KH1 to
    be QRV
    on 12 meters, since KH6 is midway between KH1 and W6.
    Noon in KH6 is 3PM local here so I listened for the KH6RS beacon on
    24.93
    and 21.15 at
    3 PM local (2200Z).  The 12m beacon had quite a bit of QSB, but would be
    strong enough to work.  The 15m beacon was booming in and was
    comparable to
    the KH1 on 21.023.
    I am thinking that the DXpedition running a kW vs the 100W beacon
    compensates for twice the distance.  Thus the theory is that KH1
    would be
    workable at 3 PM on 12m.
    I checked back at 5 PM, and there was zero signal on 12m from KH6RS, but
    still solid copy on 15.
    Another interesting thing was that I could reverse the beam and copy the
    4U1UN beacon easily.

    I wish the DXpedition would listen for
    the W6WX beacon on 12 meters so they know when to get on for the
    west coast.
    With 8 stations now on, you would think they could devote a few
    hours to 12
    meters.

    I'm sure they're doing the best they can under bad condx.
