My solar system was installed by Clean Solar, and it works great.  No RFI whatsoever. In fact, my Clean Solar salesperson did everything he could to help me feel good about the system before it was installed. He put me in touch with their engineering department so I could discuss options from a technical standpoint, and also arranged for me to take my KX3 to the house of one of their customers so I could do some rudimentary RFI checks. I’m very happy with the system, and with the company. 

FYI, the microinverters on my system are the EnPhase IQ6+.  I’ve found no RFI on any band from 75m through 70cm. 

I think the fact that Enphase is a German company, and that RFI laws are much stricter for manufacturers in Europe than in the US, may contribute to the fact that the system is very RF quiet. 

I’m happy to give you the name of the people I worked with at Clean Solar if you are interested.
