What is it about Scarborough that makes it so “desirable” to both the Chinese and Filipinos? (And, I think Vietnam also claims some sort of sovereignty as well.)

There are no natural “resources” there; in fact I suspect there is no “there” there at high tide. (Apologies to Gertrude Stein.) It can’t really serve as a strategic “base of operations” since it is almost impossible to build anything on the shoal.

As for proximity, it’s clearly much closer to the Phillipines than China, but of course, China is much more aggressive and expansionist than the Phillipines. So maybe it’s just China flexing its muscles to see how much it can push before someone pushes back.
