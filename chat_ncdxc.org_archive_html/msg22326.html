Truman,

There are non-vertical wire antennas that some have used with reasonable performance.  A tuner is needed to get it to resonate on the bands of interest and some may benefit from open wire feedlines for much of the run to the shack.

 

Also, if you go vertical, I recommend consideration of a magnetic receiving loop antenna for the 80 M band to enable more signals to get to your ears.  Mine has enabled many contacts that I could not work because I could not hear them.

 

GL

Al
