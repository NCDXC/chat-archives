There is a good reason why no one else has spoken up; it is the Board's reaction to a single contrary voice that quells all debate.

Who would speak up in that environment? I will wait to see what the next Board does before entering the fray; this one isn't interested in the discussion.

And to those who would say that I should run for a Board position to institute change from within, I respond that I *volunteered* to take one of the recently vacated Board seats, and the Board instead chose to *recruit* an alternative who better fit their style of operation, i.e., unilateral decisions and personal attacks.
