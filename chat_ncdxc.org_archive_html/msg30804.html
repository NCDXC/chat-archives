Since the odds are against most of us at this time, a neighboring DXer (the only other in my county) and I played some.  We have discovered an anomaly; that the best RX path to ZD9W is not direct (108 from N Idaho) but is at 40 degrees.  By SEVERAL dB difference.
I'm still being spotted in SA skewed, so IF (huge if) the path is reciprocal, Yuris is loud enough to give us two loud mouths a shot.
It may be polar effect, warped long path, no ideas... file that under WSHIR (Weird Stuff Happens In Radio).  You might want to check also. 

Good luck all, still trying for the ever elusive ATNO.
