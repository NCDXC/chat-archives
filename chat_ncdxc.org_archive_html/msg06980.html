Ditch the reflector and put it into your garage. Same with the 16 foot boom. You need it if/when you are going to sell the antenna as a 3 element.

Let’s make a new boom out of 5 feet of 6160 1.75inch Aluminum tube. You can find this locally at Alan Steel in Redwood City. It will probably run between $80 and $100.

Drill 6 holes in the boom and mount the driven 30/40 trombone and the director according to the drawing in the manual (from the web site).

Then you flash the 2Element firmware into the SDA100 controller - done. I do have the files.

This is probably less than a day of work. Getting the antenna off the tower and then back on is probably the hardest part,

I’ll be willing to help you with the project.

73, Andreas, N6NU

Jerry,

why don’t you downgrade your 3-Element StepIR to a 2 Element with 30/40?

You will reduce you boom from 16 feet to 57 Inches and the weight will go from 51 pounds to 30 pounds

You are only losing roughly 1.5DB in gain while you get to keep 30/40 and all the WARC bands. 

It can be done with parts you already have. The shorter boom also has a much smaller visual impact.

73, Andreas, N6NU
