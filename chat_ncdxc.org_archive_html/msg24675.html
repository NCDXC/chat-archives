2021 was an interesting year. What was your best DX?

 

I worked a ton of new entities on Digital (FT8) for the first time, but I think my best

DX was SV2RSG/A Mount Athos on 20 FT8 and 40 FT8.

 

During this year, I also worked EP2LSH, SU1SK, YI3WHR, JY4IB and ST2NH on FT8 for new digital entities. Oh and XW0LP on 80 meters for a minor miracle. I think he was QRP.
