I finally managed to break through the bedlam this morning and work FT5ZM on 20m phone. Their signal is rock-solid this morning, and I got through after only a few calls.

The op that answered asked for my prefix a couple times and commented that I wasn't very strong, but we managed to complete the QSO.  It was only after the QSO was done that I noticed that the tribander  was pointed roughly 300 degrees instead of 270!  Oops...

As they say, sometimes it's better to be lucky than good!
