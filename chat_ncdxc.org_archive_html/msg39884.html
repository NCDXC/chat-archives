Hello NCDXC friends, 

I want to share a great experience I had with a local company to install mobile antennas on my new truck.

I just bought a new Ford F-150 and I wanted several NMO mounts installed on the roof of the cab. A fellow amateur suggested RFC Wireless in Fremont. I called them and they said "Bring her in"

I had three NMO mounts installed, two for antennas and one for a GPS antenna. 

They charged me for 1.5 hrs of labor which was very reasonable. And they assumed the risk if they messed up the job.  I am glad that I found some Pro's locally to install my antenna mounts.
