Announcing is a nice added change; he was up as much as 100 (unannounced) with the EU hordes earlier today.  That was crazy to attempt any tracking of his receiver (but EU was louder to my station).

I got lucky to spot the station he worked, and was loud enough to get through the wall (on the back of the team antenna, no subtlety at all).

Good luck all around, tonight is the FINAL night; tear down and exit is their plan for tomorrow.  There are still a couple low band slots that need filling for me; that’s been the Achilles heel (wimpy signals with peaks set for EU; hard to hear in the PINW).  Lightning nearby two nights ago, made listening nearly pointless.

Last night, easy 160 cw copy but he was deaf as a post with many callers.  Then went to FT8, which (third try over 4 nights) was able to get.  60M CW simplex… just wow, but confirmed (ran an insurance dupe, one of them came through).

But on the other hand, the ATNO for me, is fairly assured now.  It’s been fun; not so many of those left to bag and tag (@ 323).
