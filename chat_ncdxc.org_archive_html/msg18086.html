På tirsdag 29. oktober 2019, 16:32:15 CET skrev Paul N6PSE via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> følgende:


Last night, VP6R was active on 160 meters FT8 using Fox/Hound. 

He was running several channels and for about two of those hours he was working EU and alternatively working US stations at the same time. 

His signal reports to the EU stations were as strong or stronger than the signal reports given to the US stations. 

I've really had fun using FT8 to gauge propagation. Many nights I go to bed leaving it on at 1.840 FT8 normal just to see what my station "hears" during the night. 

Anyway, this VP6R operation has been superb and a lot of fun to work!
