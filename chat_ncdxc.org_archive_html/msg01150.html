NCDXC Members and Friends,

W6TI/R is again experiencing harmful interference from
N6VQL/R a repeater system located in Meadow Lakes,
CA above Fresno. The interference is occurring in the Bay
area but is more severe in outlying areas such as the
Sierra Foothills. Russ Benston and I will attend a NARCC
hearing next weekend. The purpose of the hearing is to
determine if N6VQL/R should be coordinated on the same
frequency pair as W6TI/R. This hearing is our best chance
of preventing N6VQL from gaining a permanent coordination
on the pair used by W6TI for more than 30 years. N6VQL/R
is part of a large network of 2 meter, 440 and 902 MHz
repeaters owned by the Central Valley Radio Club (CVRC)
of Fresno. Their machines operate from several locations
with stated altitudes above 4000 feet ASL and are causing
interference with W6TI/R and W6FM/R in San Louis Obispo.
CVRC has pending requests for coordination for addition two
meter and 440 MHz repeaters. They are building an empire.

Now is the time to act to prevent coordination of N6VQL on
147.360 MHz. If you are experiencing interference or have
experienced interference from N6VQL when using W6TI/R,
NCDXC needs your help.

Please sent me a concise email stating the call sign of the
repeater interfering with W6TI/R and if possible the call signs
of the stations using the offending repeater. If possible provide
dates and times of the interference and/or describe how often
the interference occurs. Please also describe why the interference
is harmful. (Interference from N6XXX prevented me from hearing
a DX spot for 7O6T in Yemen and as a result I did not work this
very rare DX station.) Please state your name and call in the
message.

Please, we need your input if you are experiencing interference to
W6TI/R. I need your input by Monday so that Russ and I can put
together a package of material to present to the NARCC board to
prevent coordination of N6VQL/R on 147.36 unless it can be shown
that this machine is not causing harmful interference with W6TI/R.

If there are no reports of interference from NCDXC members it will
be very difficult to prevent N6VQL/R from being coordinated. If you
are experiencing such interference now is the time to act. Please
contact me at the email address below.

Many Thanks and 73,

John Eisenberg K6YP
