I love chasing DX but I am not much of an award chaser. I have applied for only basic DXCC with 105 entities but have worked over 340 entities.  I've not applied for WAS/WAZ or any other awards. Chasing awards just isn't my thing. 

I consider myself a "communicator". I still enjoy the "magic" of making contacts and gauging propagation. I love CW although I am not that good at it. I've had a blast with RTTY and now I am having a load of fun on FT8. 

For me, there are still many "thrills" left in amateur radio. Volunterring as a VE, mentoring and working to license young people is very enjoyable. I like to make my station available to those that don't have a station so that they can enjoy the thrill of radio as well. 

73, 

Paul N6PSE
