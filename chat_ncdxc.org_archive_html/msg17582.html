I just recently noticed this undated announcement from the old Halted Specialties Company (HSC) online here:

http://halted.com/  

According to the Wayback Machine (web archive) this was first posted on April 23rd, so I guess all of their old inventory may have moved to Excess Solutions by now.  Does anyone know?

I found no mention of this on the Excess Solutions web site (such as it is):

http://www.excesssolutions.com
