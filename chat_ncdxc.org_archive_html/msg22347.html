Yep, the writings been on the wall for some time now.  I used to go over a lunch a lot to Fry's where they started in Sunnyvale, and there was a bookstore in that area too and St. John's burgers.....hmmmmm.  There's a couple of places in Santa Clara you can still get electronic parts.  
Alltronics:  https://anatekinstruments.com/
Anchor:  https://anchor-electronics.com/
Other than that I have to wait for mail now to get parts from Mouser or Digikey.  I did read that Radio Shack was coming back but it was not clear whether they would again support electronic components.
