I really enjoyed Mike Flower’s K6MKF program last night at the NCDXC club meeting.  Not only was it extremely informative but was fun too.  I’m still hung up on three things:

 

1 – Who was the Third Man?  Orson Welles’ character?  Tuska?

2 – Earle Ham and his license – first one.

3 – Mr. Ball and his mother’s pie making that capitalized Ball and his friend Tuska’s little business leading to Hiram Piercy’s interest in radio?

 

Mike – you mesmerized me with your fact finding, sorting it all out and quality presentation.  Too bad you missed an opportunity to meet with Ball and Tuska descendants, the Third Man(?), many decades ago when living in New England.

 

Thanks for an entertaining evening. Thanks.

 

BTW:  The video projection and sound system was flawless.  Even the thumb drive plug in for one or two audio recording playbacks went with out any trouble.  Clean and clear.

 

73,

Bob – W6OPO
