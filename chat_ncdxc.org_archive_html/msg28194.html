Hi Al, 

Monk Lakovos SV2RSG has not been very active lately.  The best resource to know when you might anticipate him being active is to subscribe to W3UR's The Daily DX. He often reports when Monk Lakovos will be active. These activities often fall during special times in the Greek Orthodox Calendar which is much different than our own. 

My most recent contacts with SV2RSG:

2/21/2022  0156 UTC  40m FT8
2/20/2022 1731 UTC 20M FT8

Good luck with this most difficult catch.
