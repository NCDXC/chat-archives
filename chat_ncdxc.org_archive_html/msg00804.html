"DXA is a dynamic website that allows DXers to view the current status and activities of the DXpedition in near-real-time. 

 

Within a minute or so after making a contact with the DXpedition, the DXer is able to see confirmation of that contact entered in the expedition log. Information such as the callsigns logged in the last minute and in the last hour, and the bandmodes currently being worked by the DXpedition, are displayed in a simple, automatically-updated interface. 

 

The entire application runs within a standard browser--no software needs to be downloaded or installed."

 

http://www.cordell.org/DXA/
