On Jan 13, 2023, at 8:53 PM, Al N6TA via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

I stumbled on this again when trying to figure out how to confirm a VP2V I just worked.  I seem to have received some confirmations and then found that there are standings for those that participate.  A somewhat humbling experience to plug in my call to see where I stand: Number 163,906 on the leaderboard for band countries.  I guess I need to get to work again!
Does anyone use this and if so, why?


I hae seen a FEW (typically small-fry DX) folks use this, but it isn’t recognized by ANYONE (except perhaps the people who use it). I think it started as QRZ trying to move into the space of eQSL.cc and Clublog (and to a lesser extent, LoTW). It’s totally a vanity system. Sometimes someone will send me an email asking to confirm a QSO on QRZ, and I will do it (for them), but I don’t follow it myself. More interesting to me is the number of “hits” to my various callsigns on QRZ. I think I have over 124,000 lookups on my personal call.

I do upload my logs to eQSL.cc, for the benefit of those people who do use that system (I believe it is recognized by CQ for WAZ awards), but I don’t track my own records on that site.
