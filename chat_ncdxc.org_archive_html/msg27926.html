﻿Hi guys, I'm pleased to report we were able to clear all our equipment through Customs with no further issues.  I think the main issue with them was understanding how radio can be a hobby. They understand rugby and fishing are hobbies but radio?  They kept associating us with a commercial or business enterprise.

Bob and I are currently assembling antennas on or near the beach and should be active today.  Likely CW and FT8.  SSB may be an issue since we have two tables right next to each other.

Look for YJ0A on the spotting networks.
