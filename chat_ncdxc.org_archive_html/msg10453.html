Tony-

 

I’ll leave the roof work to the younger set, but I do have a comment on the antenna mount:  It doesn’t appear your non-penetrating base plate is the problem.  Rather you need good solid guy anchors in the right locations at the eaves of the roof.  If you can do that, you probably don’t need to penetrate the roof anywhere, although there may be notches required where the guys bend around the edge of the shingles.  A stronger base bolted through the shingles won’t prevent the wind blowing over your GAP antenna.  It will simply tear out the base from its mounting holes instead of dragging it sideways.  Good guys and hell-for-stout guy anchor points should solve your problem.  And I agree with Jim K9YC that you need insulators at the ends of the radials before they transition to the poly guy rope.
