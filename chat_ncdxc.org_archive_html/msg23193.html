Its fun to sync my log with LoTW via the DXKeeper tools.  After the sync, I look to see what was a new confirmation but usually nothing that I ‘need’.  Fun because I get confirmations from way back.  Today, it was TY8G from 1996 on 40 CW.  Another oldie was confirmed in February for ZA1AJ from 1994.  20 CW.  And in December last year, a YO from 1991.

 

For those not using LoTW, it is quite a nice feature and I encourage you to ‘suck it up’ and enter a paper log into a logging application so you can get to re-live some of those od QSOs!
