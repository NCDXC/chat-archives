Steve,

I worked Monk Apollo once on 9Apr2002 at 0426Z on 20m SSB. I have no notes or memories of anything special about the QSO except having it in the first place. I "merely" broke the pileup. I have only heard Apollo a very few times in all these years. Diminished condx to EU lately do not help.

My other two Q's with Athos were with Baldur on CW during his infamous car trip in 1991, Athos is one of about 12 still needed on RTTY.

This is a tough one--one QSO for me out of 68000 in my log. A little bit of Irish/Welsh good luck would help you a lot.

Garry
