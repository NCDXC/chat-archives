Wow, what a fun weekend for DX!  How did you do?

 

I primarily focused on two bands this weekend. Ten meters was open world wide during daylight and I dabbled on 40 meters during darkness.

 

I must have worked 200 entities on ten meters!   My best contacts on 10 meters SSB was C37N, XX9O, V84O, ZD8X, 5R8M, and CN2AA. I worked most European countries with ease.

 

Forty meters wasn’t as productive, however I did work Martti Laine as CR2X during daylight!

 

I heard a lot of really good operating and some really bad audio. Some guys need to see Bob Heil!
