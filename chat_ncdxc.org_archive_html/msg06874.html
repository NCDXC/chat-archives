K6TU and I went up to Canada College in Redwood City to activate 10GHz. K6ML went out rove in th central valley in the Los Banos area.

The best QSO was over a 355 km path from CM87UK to CN80QO (Redwood City to Redding). Both reported S8 signal, so SSB was easy.

As usual, Norm, N6JV, in Sacramento was worked. He is like a beacon on 10GHz :-)

We also attempted Redwood City to Frazier peak ( DM04MS top of the Grapevine/almost LA). While both sides heard the carriers at times it was not enough for even a CW QSO. RWC to Frazier is roughly 420KM!

It was a fun day though. And yes, this is DX on 10GHZ (10368MHz) :-)

73, Andreas, N6NU
