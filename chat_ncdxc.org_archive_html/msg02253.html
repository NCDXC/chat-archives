I think that Bill,N6BM and several others summed it up well.  Here is how I feel about it. #1 One of the members that complains the most and says he will drive all over hell to go to a meeting....never shows up for the meetings at Holders.  # 2 This is the NCDXC and not the NCCC and I am tired of everyone trying to compare our club to the CC. BS as far as I am concerned.
 I think Russ, Ross and I have a long history of meetings from the time they were held all over the place, including Sacramento. they didn't work out for several reasons. The traffic has increased to nearly double of the old days so a long distance trip to help some on the outlaying fringe area members will cause most to simply not bother to attend. There is no simple answer except that our current location has been just fine. I hope that common sense prevails. I am not opposed to a one or two times a year to attend an outlaying meeting, but lets be reasonable, most of the Bay Area can get to Holders from two major highways.
