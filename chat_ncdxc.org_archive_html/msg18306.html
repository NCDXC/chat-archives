Rick, like you, I am enjoying activity on 160 meters. As our days get shorter, there is more and better DX on the low bands. 

EU is loud on 80 and Western EU is coming in now and then on 160. 

FT8 has opened up a whole new dynamic with the ability to work signals that you cannot hear with your ears. 

And as you point out, a receiving antenna is very beneficial for 160 meters as the transmit antenna is often a poor receiving antenna. 

See you on Top Band!

Paul N6PSE
