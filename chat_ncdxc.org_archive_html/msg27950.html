Yes, you can have a pirate and the real station on the same band in opposite slots.  That would create the havoc that we've seen lately.  Which one is real?  Some sites are spotting both (according to an email I got).

IF this is the same clown as McQuarie a few months back, (same azimuth from here) he chose a tone exactly one stream step (60 cycles) above the real station highest and kept in sync.

I was chatting via online message with the real station at the time, saw the issue (significant difference in signal strength) then told him, so he stopped for a couple cycles while the fake continued; then the real one changed bands (which only worked for a short time, until spotted).

Check your bearings, if the signal is louder from Australia (SoPac) instead of short path (90 degree variance here); it's almost certainly, a pirate.

I'd LOVE to be mistaken, I have such an entry in my log too.
