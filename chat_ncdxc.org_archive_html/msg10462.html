NCDXC Chat Subscribers,

 

At the August 16, 2015 Board of Directors meeting, the Board voted to update the NCDXC Chat - Terms of Use which are shown below:

 

NCDXC Chat - Terms of Use

 

NCDXC Chat is an open email reflector sponsored and maintained by the Northern California DX Club for the purpose of discussing DX and DX-related topics.

 

Subscribing to and using the NCDXC Chat email reflector is an explicit acceptance of these Terms of Use.

 

Personal attacks of any kind, political postings and off-topic postings are a violation of the Terms of Use and may result in removal from the list as determined by the NCDXC Chat Postmaster and the NCDXC Board of Directors.

 

-- NCDXC Board of Directors, August 16, 2015
