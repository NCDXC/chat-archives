Gary,

There are now 3 separate DXCC submission processes with 3 different costs. The operate completely independently.

The simplest and the lowest cost is the LotW submission. That process runs directly from the LotW website. The credits shows up in your LotW account in a few days.

The second process is the Online DXCC application. This requires physical cards that must be verified by a DXCC Field Checker. The application and instructions can be found at https://p1k.arrl.org/onlinedxcc/   I've used this process several times. I like it because the QSLs do not need to be sorted and can be entered when received. The credits show up in the LotW account within a week of the application being received by ARRL.

The third and original process is fully paper. Your paper QSLs must be sorted in band/mode order and you fill out a paper application. You then take the application to a DXCC Field Checker. The application is then sent to ARRL, where all the data must be manually input into the DXCC record.

So, it is possible to have one Lotw, one Online and as many paper DXCC applications in the system at one time.
