NOW you understand my 'aggressive' approach to a DXpedition, even more so on one that I focus on (like TX5S).
Get in EARLY is one of my constants... I got through on 10 SSB on the 21st (their first use) and that wasn't a challenge; just being quick to respond before the hordes descended.  It was actually MUCH LOUDER after I worked them, I caught the band opening.
