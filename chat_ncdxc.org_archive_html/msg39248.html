Agreed.  Just for winter fun, a few of us are scrambling to build the annual marathon scores.    My rotor died the second week of Jan.  When it died, I had around 50 countries and all 40 zones.

A bucket truck was called in to turn it north until the weather allowed the tower to tilt (it must be north or the tilt plate doesn’t work).  That doesn’t allow looks anywhere else (Andaman and others were closer to 270).

Two months later, between snow storms, it was fixed, but while pointing north and mistreating the 160 L, using the 80 Ground plane (and a little SteppIR 180) my count was in the 170’s.

So it CAN be done, you just have to draw on old school and skills, some imagination and creativity.

Right now, I’m stuck at 211 (plus another that CQ counts); but it’s only a quarter to April.  Considering the intensity of the solar storms and how the polar cap slams the door shut; dat ain’t bad.
