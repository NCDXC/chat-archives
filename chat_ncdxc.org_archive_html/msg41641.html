Certainly the antenna I put up getting back on the air in 1980 at our new house, in Mountain View.
I picked up a HyGain 18AVQ?  80-10M Trap Vertical. I buried 4 radials for each band in the ground (before I knew they should be shorter when buried), and even with my new FT-101ZD and a Clipperton-L it took just over 6 months to work my first 100 countries.  Yes, with the radials too long my feed point was underground.  Then the traps...  Oh my...
However, with this same poor setup, I ran a regular Phone Patch for a fellow in Finland to his daughter in Marin County every week at Midnight on 10M.  When was the last time 10M was that open at midnight?
Fast forward to a temporary location in 2014, the ARRL CW DX Contest in just 6 hours worked 105 countries.  Antenna was a ladder line feed full size 80M Dipole at about 34 feet. Used on all Bands 80-10M. 

We often learn the most from our mistakes.

Dave WI6R
