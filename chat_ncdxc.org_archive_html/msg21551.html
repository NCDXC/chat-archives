I remain skeptical regarding a safe and inclusive Hamvention in 2021. The logistics of vaccinating the nation are daunting, even ignoring the potential issues of anti-vaxxers  and pandemic deniers. Until the vast majority of the population is vaccinated, masks and social distancing will continue to be necessary because even with a 90+ effective agent, there is still a possibility that the vaccine was individually ineffective. This is especially applicable to older patients.

It’s going to require a national effort akin to WWII or the Space Race. I suspect that Covid will remain with us, in greatly reduced numbers, and possibly become a low prevalence,  seasonal, but potentially lethal bug.

In the meanwhile, DXing while sheltering remains completely safe.
