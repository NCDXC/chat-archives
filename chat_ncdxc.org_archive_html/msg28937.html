Hi Folks!

I‘m delighted to announce my long-promised book is now available on Amazon!

The CW Way of Life: Learning, Living, and Loving Morse Code (in a Digital World): Rutkowski, Chris: 9798377407164: Amazon.com: Books

 

It was – and is – the questions and conversations on this and other groups that provide(d) the motivation not just to help, voicing my own opinions, but to collect, investigate, analyze, and codify, and get to the bottom of “how things work.” How WE work – doing code.


This is not just a work of documentation – it is a research report – I was on a DXpedition into the roots of who we are and where we came from and how we got here and where we’re going. And good news: I found some REALLY interesting stuff along the way.

 

Quoting myself, from the back cover of the book:

“CWOL’s unique curriculum combines techniques dating back to the time of Morse with modern training principles to create a learning system like no other. The most important component is you! No computer needed!

More than just a “how-to,” CWOL takes you on a deep dive into the history, theory, and practice of Morse. It reveals:

 

     - How your brain turns sensations into thoughts.

     - Morse’s true rhythmic nature - harmonics - not ratios!

     - How to ride the "dit train" to fluid keying.

     - The lost technologies of code talking and speed-keying.

     - The true cause and prevention of Glass Arm.

     - How to read and write Morse like music  - on paper.

Enjoy! 😊

And my undying thanks to the members and officers of WVDXC. I hope I make you proud!

73 Chris NW6V
