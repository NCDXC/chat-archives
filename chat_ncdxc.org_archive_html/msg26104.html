7P8AB is a familiar call to me.  I worked this call in March of 1972, my first 7P.  15 M SSB.  For those that were around in those days, the Africanners group was on 21355 kHz daily and a lot of AF checked in to work the needy.  Cal, W2PPG, Herbie,WA6BJS, etc. hosted and it was quite civil compared to later ‘DX nets’.

Almost certainly a re-issued callsign.

CU down memory lane!
