On 11/20/2013 10:48 AM, Bob Lanning wrote:
Board meetings are announced.  They are always posted on the club’s web site as well as membership meetings.

Excuse me, but this email reflector is the far more appropriate place to post notices that the board wants someone to see. 

It's one thing if an election is not contested and it's hard to find volunteers to take club offices, and we know that has been true for a while. But this election is clearly contested, and should be put to a vote of the entire membership, only a fraction of which are able to attend meetings. Further, the slate of those running for office should be posted to this email reflector well in advance of the vote.

I have a prior commitment (to a jazz concert) and cannot make the meeting to cast a vote.

None of this diminishes our appreciation of the work the board does, for Ross's service, nor for Jim stepping into the breach. I think we should be thankful that there is interest in helping run the club.

73, Jim K9YC
