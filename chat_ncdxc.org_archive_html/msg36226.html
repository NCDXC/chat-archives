My M2 Orion has been on the tower since I relocated to Boston from Los Altos back in 1997.  It rotates a Force 12 EF-420/240, a Force 12 4BA, and an M2 6M7:

http://www.ambersoft.com/Amateur_Radio/Beauty%20and%20Beast%20D%202016-11-08.jpg

Lately, it's started occasionally grinding to a halt when rotating clockwise through one portion of its range; Murphy's Law of DXing predicts that it will become totally inoperative just before P5 becomes QRV on CW while pointing 90 degrees to one side or the other.

Thus I'm seeking recommendations for a replacement.  I've never been a tower climber and I'm not going to start at age 72, so I'd love a rotator that will last another 27 years with minimal attention. Perhaps that's another M2 Orion, but I'd love to hear about multi-year experiences with alternatives.

Is anyone using DX Engineering's new rotator?
