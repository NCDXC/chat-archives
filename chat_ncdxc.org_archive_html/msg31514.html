From Tom, NW6P:

I and Matt, WX5S just got back from visiting Ross Forbes, K6GFJ at stroke rehab unit at Valley Medical Center.  Ross was a Stanford for a routine heart valve replacement and suffered a stroke during the procedure.  He was treated right away with TPA to break up the clot.  He stayed at Stanford for a few days and then was moved to the Sobrato Rehabilitation Pavilion at Valley Medical Center. 

On Sunday Oct 22, Kevin Rowett, K6TD and I visited Ross.  He is able to walk to the bathroom with assistance and do physical therapy.  
On Tuesday, Ross is much better and was able to do 555 steps in 9 minutes during PT.  
It is very boring sitting in the room. Ross would welcome visitors.  He is expected to be there at least until Thursday week.  
He is in Room A4111 in the Sobrato Pavillion, adjacent to the Womens center.  The Sobrato Pavillion is on the left as you enter via Renova Drive from S. Bascom Avenue.  Use valet parking and walk through Womens center to get to the Rehab Building.

73, Tom NW6P
