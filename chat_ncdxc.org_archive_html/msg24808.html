Al,

I missed IH9YMC, but worked IH9/OK1M a couple of months ago on 15m - I had not realized this was "African Italy", i.e. the island of Pantelleria. I think I'll send him a QSL!

By the way, Pantelleria gained notoriety in Roman times as a place of exile for those out of favor with the Emperor. In the middle ages, it was intermittently controlled by pirates. In 1943 the Allies used Pantelleria as an airbase for the invasion of Sicily. A lot of history for such a small island.
