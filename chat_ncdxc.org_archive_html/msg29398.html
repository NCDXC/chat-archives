I too enjoyed seeing everyone again although a number of hams I know were not there and were missed but everything was back to normal.  I appreciated the solemn SK list.  It reminds us of what we will someday leave behind and how we might impact that.  I liked the new young ham winning the 7300.  I enjoyed talking to the folks that I know better now at the Elecraft booth.  I enjoyed meeting new people at the tables for the breakfast and DX Banquet.  And, I enjoyed that my wife was able to share the experience and while not a ham, she enjoyed the presentations.  Although I think she originally came to keep me from buying anything......hi hi
