Vijay,

I wonder if the devices in the SPE 1.5K PA are screwed down or soldered down.
It sounds like your output FET's are over heating a symptom of high thermal
resistance from the FET to its heat sink. This could be caused by loose screws
or a poor solder joint on the copper heat spreader under the FET(s). Operating
FT8 with its 15 minute key down period will certainly exasperate the problem.
You should either fix the amplifier or get it fixed before you blow the finals.

If you have questions please ask. Good luck.

73,

John Eisenberg K6YP
