As I posted last week, I have finally completed WAZ (Mixed) and I am ready to get my cards checked.  I contacted Floyd, N5FG and asked about Checkpoints (card checkers) and other means of submission.  LoTW printouts and DXCC records are NOT acceptable.  Only paper cards are accepted for WAZ (other award managers such as the Field Award have different criteria).
 
The list of Checkpoints in California is listed below.  Notice that there in only one in N CA and that one is in Davis (about 100 miles from me).  Most people in other states do not realize the size of CA, many of the others are 300 to 400 miles away.  I do not trust the USPS with QSL cards that may be impossible to replace (I have had too many letters and packages delivered to the wrong address and I have received mail address to others frequently), so I am reluctant to trust my cards to the Post Office, I will probably use FedEx even though it is more expensive.
 
I asked Floyd if he could appoint a Checkpoint card checker in our area.  He said that in 10 years I was the only one who had asked so apparently there is no demand.  I believe that one of the requirements to be a Checkpoint is that the checker already has the WAZ award.  How many members have WAZ awards?
 
If you think that is would be good to have a checkpoint in the Bay Area (it would be nice if it was someone who regularly attends NCDXC meetings), please contact Floyd at n5fg@i-55.com.  If he gets enough of a response, he may decide to appoint someone.  If you have WAZ, please let him know.
