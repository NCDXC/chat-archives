I worked JW0X  (already had TX5N on 30 FT8) but they clearly have a noise issue on 30M.  The reports they give are much lower than expected when I worked them easily, no amp, on 20M  90 minutes earlier.  Now I'm not decoding them, their audio peak doesn't look right (pointy, not flat top).

The larger issue is that in Fox/Hound mode, the caller has NO means to control what the ack tones will be and mine was seriously close to the TX5N team tones when I ack'ed Svalbard.  It had potential to cause a hit on the copy of someone calling TX5N.  Sharing, not a good plan, even half a world away (and TX5N is 2 streams of +9 on the back of the beam, JW0X +7 one, on the front).

[NOW is an excellent time to call TX5N on 30 FT8, they're silly loud now.]

On the other hand, where else can one fine JA, Arctic, EU, SoPac and SA stations, at the same time this time of night?

Only 30M apparently.

GL Paul and thanks for the spot!
