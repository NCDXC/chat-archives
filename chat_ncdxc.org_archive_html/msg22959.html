Hi,If anyone needs KH9 Wake Island on SSB, or as a band fill:Randy WW6RG/KH9 is available for skeds from KH9 Wake Island. He is an airline pilot, and he is available on certain days, and certain times.You can arrange a sked with Randy by emailing him at ww6rg@yahoo.com
