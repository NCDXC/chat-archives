When working a pileup, I occasionally will ask for QRP and mobile stations, as I often operate this way myself, and know how hard it can be to crack the pile. Of course, many "big guns" just keep calling (it's pretty obvious they're not QRP when they are 10-over-9 from 2000 miles away). I work anyone who *says* they are QRP, but I log the call with the /QRP suffix (which can cause LoTW to NOT confirm, unless they upload with the /QRP and have a certificate for that callsign).

Once I got a call from a big-signal UA1 (European Russia). He said, "I'm running 'Russian QRP' -- only one kilowatt!" I think he was only half-joking.
