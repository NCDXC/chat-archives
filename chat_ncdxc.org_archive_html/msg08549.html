Good morning NCDXC fellas,
 
This morning on the www.tromelin2014.com website, they have posted many photos of the island and their antenna/station setup.
 
Several interesting things to note:
 
1) They did have a two element SteppIR Yagi. That might explain the better than expected signals.
 
2) They had numerous vertical antennas including at least one four square. Most of the verticals are further from the salt
water than I would have expected. It is interesting to see how such a simple set up could be so effective.
 
 
Happy Thanksgiving!
 
Paul N6PSE
