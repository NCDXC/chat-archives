…the 2017 International DX Convention in Visalia, that is. April 21-23rd, 2017.

For those of you who have not yet registered, this would be a good time to get in on the LOWER PRE-REGISTRATION PRICES. Walk-ins and lollygaggers have to pay more, but that’s not YOU.

Also note that if you want one of the REALLY NICE Convention Polo Shirts, available this year in THREE DIFFERENT COLORS (Wow!), and THREE DIFFERENT STYLES (Mens with or without pocket, and Ladies), the deadline for shirt orders is THIS Friday, March 17th, at 5 pm Pacific time. Check it out on the website. Lots of folks are ordering two or three shirts, because we got away from the (boooooring!) Navy Blue, and instead offer Black, White and Blazing Red. Available in Mens or Ladies cut, sizes Small through 5XL (that’s right, 5XL). Just order through the Registration system at:

http://dxconvention.com


Also, you can still purchase RAFFLE TICKETS in advance. Advance purchasers get 20% more tickets for the same price. That’s right, TWELVE for the price of ten ($10). You can mail a check to:

Rich KE1B
21885 Bear Creek Way
Los Gatos, CA 95033

or call (408) 395-5700 to charge your tickets using any major credit card.

Due to California law, we cannot take raffle ticket orders online.

Finally, we need some volunteers to help out with operations before and during the event. Specifically we need:

(A) Around 5-6 people to stuff registration packets. This will happen on Saturday, April 15th, between 9 am and 3 pm. Lunch is included. Location is The Monkey House, (aka K6MMM QTH), 21885 Bear Creek Way, Los Gatos. A beautiful drive up to the Santa Cruz Mountains on what is always a lovely spring day. You can even put a winning raffle ticket in your own envelope!

(B) Around 10-15 people to staff the Registration Desk in Visalia. Registration will be open from 3 pm until 5 pm on Thursday (NOTE: That’s a day earlier than usual), 11 am until 5 pm on Friday, and then from 7:30 am until 11 am on Saturday. If you can offer a couple of hours, please help us out here. The “rush hour” usually starts at the opening bell each day, and to a lesser extent late Friday afternoon. The Registration Staff are the first people that convention attendees see, and we want everyone to have a GREAT START to their weekend, so only folks with happy, smiling faces need apply.

(C) Around 10-15 people (perhaps more) to staff the Raffle Ticket Sales Booth. All you need to do here is take people’s money (always lots of fun!) and give them almost-worthless pieces of cardboard in return. People with smartphones or iPads who are willing to load (and learn) a credit-card swipe application are especially helpful.


If you are willing to do any (or more than one) of these jobs, please contact me directly:

mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com

NCDXC *always* gives everyone the best DX convention experience. Please help to be a part of that.

Rich KE1B
