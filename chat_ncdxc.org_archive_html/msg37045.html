I disagree the ARRL has no redeeming value.  We tend to forget the elements the ARRL has done successfully, when they do crazy things like politics.  


The ARRL is a voice in Washington DC, and the the WRCs.  For good or bad, they are the organization of Hams.  They do get pubs out.  We always think LoTW could be better, and it can.  They had the vision to start it, and get it working.  The world would look much different, and probably worse off without the ARRL.
