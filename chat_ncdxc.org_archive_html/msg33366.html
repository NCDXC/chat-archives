I do remember well, since she was my first VU4, Andaman-Nicobar contact.  My log note says this:

"split and on 1st call!  Barefoot, again!  (note, 5/2015: Two days later, the 9.0 quake turns this into a disaster operation)"

She got permission to go there to demonstrate ham radio and show that it was not harmful and that it was good for disaster comms.  Quite the timing, and I believe it may have opened up VU7 and VU4 as a result.
