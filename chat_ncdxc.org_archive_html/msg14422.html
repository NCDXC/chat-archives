Hi Ross, Team, 


Having been down in this part of the world, the Antarctic Convergence current typically flows from West to East around 45 degrees South Latitude at anywhere from 3-4 kts. So it comes at no surprise, running on one engine, the ship captain would want to head east with the current, rather than trying to buck it, the winds and the seas ... 


And Cape Town will be an awesome place for the team to resurface.  

Fingers crossed, all goes well for them.
