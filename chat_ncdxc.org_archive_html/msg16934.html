The USS Hornet volunteer Radio Division, which includes the Hornet Amateur Radio Club (HARC), is raising money to restore four of the ship’s original HF antennas. The starboard upper 3-wire fan, port 3-wire fan, and the discage antennas are in need of element repair, mounting repair, and corrosion control. The project will increase safety, correct improper mounting, and restore functionality for HARC’s use. Please consider helping us reach our goal.

 

https://www.gofundme.com/uss-hornet-antenna-restoration
