The following awards were given out at the 2017 International DX Convention in Visalia from the Northern California DX Club:

 

Bob Schmieder, KK6EK, received the NCDXC DX’er of the Year in recognition of his efforts to organize the VK0EK DX-pedition to Heard Island.

 

Paul Ewing, N6PSE, received the NCDXC DX’er of the Year in recognition for his efforts as co-leader of the VP8STI, South Sandwich Island,  and VP8SGI South Georgia DX-pedition.

 

Kenneth Opskar, LA7GIA received the Dr. William Howard Myers, W6OL Award for excellence as a CW operator on a DX-pedition.

 

Mike Flowers, K6MKF, received the NCDXC Member of the Year award for his activities to help promote the NCDXC as well as his NCDXC history programs given at monthly NCDXC meetings.
