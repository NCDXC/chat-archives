Hello NCDXC friends from Tehran.

I had hoped to be able to update my Blog and Facebook pages while here to tell you about our Intrepid adventure, however those sites are blocked from within Iran.

It's 3AM here in Tehran and this is when their internet is most useable.

Yesterday, we roamed all over Tehran on foot and via the subways. Tehran is a very big city. We visited various government buildings and later the National Museum and the Crowne Jewels.

I always enjoy visiting the open air Bazaars in this part of the world. Everyone has been very nice on this trip however we did have an encounter with the secret police which I will write about later.

We ended the day visiting the former US Embassy in Tehran. It has a large wall around it that has colorful murals in various ant-American themes.   

Later this week, we plan to have a dinner with some EP hams in Tehran to conclude our Intrepid-DX Group's 2014 friendship tour.

I'll update my blog with many great photos when I can.

73 for now,

Paul N6PSE
