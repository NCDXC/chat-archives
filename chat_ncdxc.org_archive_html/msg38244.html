On Dec 22, 2024, at 9:54 AM, Chortek, Robert L. via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

I’ve been using Version 7.2.1-Dev for less than 24 hours and love the following improvements (in no particular order):

1. The waterfall cursers now have two lines running vertically from the top of the waterfall to the bottom under the cursor, which show you exactly where you are in frequency and even tell you the exact frequency.

2. There are band buttons under the RX frequency window.  Clicking on a button will take you to a preset band frequency. It is no longer necessary to click twice to change bands.

3. You can set the band activity window and the receive frequency window so that when you change bands, all history is erased. I really like this feature because I am no longer distracted by outdated information from the previous band.

4.  The filters are greatly improved. There are even audio alerts, which now tell you, for example, new dxcc on band, new dxcc, new CQ zone, and any of a very wide variety of alerts, all of which can be customized. It’s absolutely fantastic.

There are many other features which I have yet to use, but so far I find this an absolutely fantastic improvement and highly recommend it.

I’d like to hear what other folks think of this version and what they like most about it.
