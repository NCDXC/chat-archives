I have the same problem, Jim. If someone is REALLY asking for, and working only W6/W7, I sign KE1B/6 (especially on CW/Digital). I have a LoTW certificate for the /6 call, and it is linked to the base call, so DXCC credits merge.

If they are calling for “NA West Coast”, I sometimes sign “KE1B, in California” (on SSB). And sometimes stretching out my call with the “in California” leaves my “tail” in the clear for the DX, and they will come back with “The California station, go ahead”.

Whatever works.
