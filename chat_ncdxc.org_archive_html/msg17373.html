I have heard that the NCDXC declined to provide any financial support for the upcoming VP8-O South Orkney expedition.  This one hits near and dear to me, and gives me concern.  First, most of my fellow Dxpeditioneer colleagues from VP6D, a wonderful and skilled international group of top amateurs are making this adventure. Second, I actually need VP8-O as an ATNO to move up the DX ladder.  (PS how many of you have VP8-O in FT8?)  As you can imagine, this is extremely disappointing to me as a member of the NCDXC and also a team member with this groups last and quite successful expedition to Ducie Island.  I think it was quite evident the level of professionalism involved.   So I would like to understand the BoD’s rationale for not supporting this expedition, and encourage the the club leadership to vote to contribute in support of the South Orkney expedition.

 

Whats the deal here?  Can someone loop me into how we as a premier DX club can move forward and support this expedition team?  Much smaller DX clubs seem to be pulling it off.. I am sure we can do it.  What say we use our club dollars smart?
