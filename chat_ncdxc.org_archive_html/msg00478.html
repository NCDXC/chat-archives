Lots of folks (including me) have been complaining about the HUGE spreads that PT0S has been using; as much as 15 kHz on CW, 30-40 kHz on SSB, and at one point they were working up 65 kHz on RTTY.

So yesterday, for some strange reason, they were running on 17m CW, *SIMPLEX*! What a madhouse! Hundreds of folks calling within a few tens of Hz of each other, and loud domestic stations drowning out the PT0. (At least there was no need for the frequency cops! He was NOT listening "Up! Up! Up!".)

And I got through with just one call. (It showed up in his uploaded log today.) Easy, if you are on a mountaintop with a Yagi and legal limit, and you know where he is listening. (That last part is the most important.)
