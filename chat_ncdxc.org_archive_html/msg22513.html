I'm fascinated by the fact that you could buy a (presumably) fully functional TV receiver in 1940! Though I don't think there were any commercial broadcast TV stations in the US at that time.

I love looking through the old catalogs and ham magazines. I've attached a link to a copy of the Nov 1975 issue of QST, which contains a fascinating story (starting on p. 117) of an American ham visiting Nazi Germany before the war. After some innocent QSO's with hams in the Soviet Union, he had to flee Germany just ahead of the SS! He also had some pungent first-hand comments about der
