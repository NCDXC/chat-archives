I was not able to work them from my incredibly low investment shack. Lol. I tried for four to five hours over the the past week to try them. I heard them as loud as 57 on SSB on 17m and 559 on 20m CW. 

I am sure if I was able to take the entire week off from work I would have been able to work them from this shack. Several kind folks from the club with more robust shacks offered me the opportunity to work them from their shacks but I decided against it. I think part of the thrill is to work them from your own QTH and set of working conditions. To those folks I thank you. 

I am up early this morning listening for them on 20m longpath. Regardless of working them or not I enjoyed the chase. The QRM was probably the worse I have seen since last years VU7 DXpedition, and the forever splits were insane. How wide was the widest??
