Hello Folks,

 

Everyone is welcome to join in on the NCDXC W6TI repeater, 147.36+ MHz, PL 110.9, for an evening at the DXers’ Lounge this Wednesday, May 23rd at 0300Z (8PM PDST).

 

No program, schedule, roster or agenda, just a bunch of DXers getting together on W6TI/R to chat about DX, DXing and all things Amateur Radio … so just jump in and give a shout!

 

For those NCDXC members who are too far from the W6TI repeater to reach it directly, you can use EchoLink to connect via the N6NKT-L node.

 

I hope you can join the discussion on the W6TI repeater!

 

- 73 and good DX de Mike, K6MKF, Past President - NCDXC
