On 3/4/2013 8:02 AM, paul@n6pse.com wrote:
The XT2TT DXpedition is in their final days. How's your experience been working them?

I have XT2C confirmed on 80 and 40 in LOTW from 2007.

I'm in the XT2TT log for seven QSOs, once each on 40-12, all CW, except for 20M where I worked them only on SSB and RTTY.   I've listened for their spots in 160 and 80, but never heard them well enough to call, and I've not seen them spotted on 10M (my spots are filtered by VE7CC for west of the Rockies). They've always sounded like first rate operators.
