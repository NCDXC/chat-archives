https://www.dropbox.com/s/ydfesveonxx7gnq/2019%20slvarc%20auction.pdf?dl=0

Let me know if there are any problems with the link or download.

As always,  If you want to bid on an item without coming to the meeting, contact me directly at mailto:ke1b@richseifert.com to arrange a pre-specified, sealed (private) maximum bid. Your bid will win the item if the real-time bids do not exceed your pre-bid (a la eBay). You will get the item at a lower price than your pre-bid if the real-time bids do not go that high. The auctioneer will bid on your behalf, up to your specified maximum.

If anyone wants a higher-resolution photo of any item (for closer inspection), please contact me and I will send it along. (I kept the images fairly small in the catalog to avoid huge downloads.)

I’ll update the catalog regularly as new items come in.

Bid early and often! Feel free to pass this email to anyone who might be interested.
