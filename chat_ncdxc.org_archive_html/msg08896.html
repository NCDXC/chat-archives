I use bose Quiet comfort NC when I am working cw at N6RO and K6LRG.  Both shacks have enough fan.. AC.. chatting.. activity during operation that I find them quite useful.  I have a couple CM-500's and the legacy Heil headsets from ages past and all work great, like Jim said in all but the noisiest of environments.  If you go with a NC set think about the big picture.. and quality of engineering is very important.  Heil for a short spell had a knockoff of the Bose NC set, that was cool because it had a boom mic.. but the engineering piece didn't go so well.. forcing him to recall ALL of them for defective NC(feedback or balance?) issues.   Not sure you would get all the bang you want for the (Many) bucks of the Bose NC set.  

I also have a set of Radio Sport that work good, but in order to reject ambient noise they are tightly fit around the ears.. and if you are on the air.. like for 24-36 hours you will need to switch out for something else mid op.    I would be happy to bring a wide variety of my Ham headset impulse buy collection to a meeting if you would be interested in trying them on.
