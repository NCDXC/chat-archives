Here is your NCDXC Question of the week:

 

 

What do you think DXing will be like in 5-10 years:

 

 

 

 

 

My response: I have to admit that I am worried about the future of DXing in the future. A lot of us are not getting any younger and the young guys are just not diving into amateur radio. I also worry about the limitations/impact placed upon future DXers by Home Owner's associations, deed restrictions, CC&R's and the trend towards apartment living for our younger folks.

 

It is not all doom and gloom however. Radio, antenna and accessory technology is better than it has ever been. I am very intrigued by remote access technologies via the Internet. Some radio clubs are setting up Internet remote HF transceivers for their club members to use and I definitely think that is the way of the future.

 

I think the DXCC program will evolve and we will see new countries on the horizon. I predict that entities such as P5, EZ and YK will see major DXpeditions within the next five years. I hope I am right!

 

73,

 

Paul N6PSE
