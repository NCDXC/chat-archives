The all-time new ones (ATNO) are a statistic calculated by clublog, I believe.   

If you look up the expedition on Clublog there are quite a number of tables listing a number of different statistics.

 One of them is “New DXCC”.

 I assume Clublog calculates this by comparing the entries by the Dxpedition log against the various clublog user’s logs, as listed in clublog.
