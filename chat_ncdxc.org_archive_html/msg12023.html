My 2016 Visalia International DX Convention photos are now online at http://bit.ly/Visalia2016 .

Permission is hereby granted for publication of the link and any of these photos in the NCCC Jug, NCDXC DXer, SCDXC Bulletin, SDDXC Bulletin, The NCDXF Newsletter, The FARS Relay, The Daily DX, MLDXCC Nugget, REDXA Sunspots, and the ARRL Contest Update.

Suggested photo credit: "Photo by N6TV."

For photo use in other publications, please contact me directly.

Please publish only the short link (http://bit.ly/Visalia2016), not the site it forwards to (PicasaWeb).  Posting the link to club reflectors would be appreciated (except for NCCC and MLDXCC, which I've already copied).

To search for your own photos, press Ctrl-F (Find) in your web browser, then enter your callsign (ignore the Picasa "Search" box at the top; it does not work).

For best viewing, click the Slideshow button, top left.

To save a high resolution copy of any photo, first select the photo, then at the top, Select Download -> Download photo.

Bob, N6TV
