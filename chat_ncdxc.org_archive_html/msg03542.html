I am not sure what kind of calculator has been used in this but I have always wondered this claim. If location moves ~2000 miles, so does antipode. Midwest antipode is not in Western Pacific but just ~2000 miles east of our antipode... somewhere more toward Australia but still way out in Indian Ocean.

For example Kiribati is not 11,717 miles from Midwest. It's less than half of that... 5333 miles per http://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/distanceresult.html?p1=77&p2=274.

Nauru 7263 miles: http://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/distanceresult.html?p1=77&p2=276

Western Pacific antipode is somewhere between South America and Africa.

http://www.antipodemap.com/
