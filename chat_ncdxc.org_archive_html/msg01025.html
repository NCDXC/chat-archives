I would nominate the RemoteRig RRC-1258MkIIs which allows you to control a radio over the Internet.  It does a fantastic job with great audio and CW keying.  I believe that the CW keying algorithm is the key (no pun intended) to the products success.  It does a perfect job of maintaining good CW keying over the Internet.  With CC&R’s and high local noise, I would not be on the air without RemoteRig.

http://www.remoterig.com/wp/

 

The microHAM microKEYER II is my second favorite, I use one with my RemoteRig boxes to generate perfect CW keying from the computer.

http://microham-usa.com/store/product-info.php?pid1.html

 

However, neither of these products are new in the last year if that is a consideration.
