Hi Jim (and group),


Ah, you heard me; with a better antenna than I had in CA for 160M.  More importantly the CA 160M noise floor of -60 to -80 dBm is a consistent -117 dB, here in N ID.  I haven't even gotten around to making things in the house quiet until the full station gets built, but in time I expect to improve things.  I can HEAR!


Tonight was a safety Q with YN4 (got them a couple nights ago as well, piece of cake).  I also used FT8, power out was about 550 watts in all cases.  They are consistently one of the loudest signals on the band, louder than some locals.  I likely didn't need the amp but ...

If my ailing ears didn't lie, I was also able to work CT9/DJ2YA on Madiera on 160M CW tonight too, we'll see if that proves out, it was near ESP (do I hear a "DUH!"?).  AF has always been a challenge for the west coast (or at least me) but I've managed (Tanzania on 20 phone a few days ago, also on the 160M inverted L).  For sheer distance on 160, New Zealand wins but the Madiera is just plain FUN (when I could hear him, he was calling a long CQ, by the time he listened, the QSB shifted against me, I was surfing it).  Crossing my fingers on this one (as with many before and I will again).


My goal is to make DXCC on 160M this winter (add to the set).  Tonight CT9/ was slot #64 worked (48 confirmed, a couple more are DXpeditions that won't post until 'later').  I figure one more month of shortening daylight, then another month to be where it is  now... so I have two months to get it done.

Come Spring, I'm going to hogtie a contractor and make him listen to polka until the tower foundation gets built; then it gets fun.  I have the parts standing by (tower, rotor, antenna), just need the hole and concrete, to spec.  It's a small job and everyone wants the big jobs ($400K+ homes being built, for here that's expensive), not a weekend job.


If I read the reports correctly, this weekend should be excellent for the Top Band up into perhaps 40M but lousy for anything else.  So if you can load anything on 160M, now is the time.

I've been hitting 160M almost exclusively this month and it's been a complete blast (adding ~30 countries since Sep).


73 from the north lands,

Rick wa6nhc

"I DX, therefore I ham!"
