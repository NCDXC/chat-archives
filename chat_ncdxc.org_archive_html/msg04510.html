I hope the dipole is better.  Much easier for me than making a good radial system.
Another factor in favor of a high dipole here in most of NorCal is the effect of soil conductivity. Our very poor soil has VERY little effect on the dipole, but a MAJOR effect on a vertical. To see this, study the Power Point on Mounting Height for Vertical Antennas that I did at Pacificon this year. http://k9yc.com/publish.htm
