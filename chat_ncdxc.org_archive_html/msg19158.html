Yes, they did an amazing job.  

Even when they were barely above ESP they heard my wimpy 500W to a dipole.
Considering work kept me away (or asleep) with no time to really work them I still got them on 40 SSB and 20 CW.  
They just didn't quit until you were in the log.  I didn't do any insurance contacts but I'm confident I'll be in the next log upload for the CW contact on the 29th.

Accolades due at Visalia!  

After that contact on the 29th my (modern) backup radio failed (28 years young) due to a failed trimmer in the PLL, and my Flex returned on UPS from the mother ship right after they went QRT.
Such is Murphy's law.  Grrrr  
My S-Line doesn't have crystals installed for the WARC bands, but I should have gone to storage and gotten my crystal pack and put them in.  You lucky retired folks!

Dave WI6R
