Hi Garry,

From what I was told by another ham, it cost him over $7 to send a QSL card out. If that is the case, IMHO $10 is reasonable. Even in more advanced countries a QSL card to send back to the US is easily over $2 and sometimes over $3 or more. IMHO we are spoiled here in the US when I look at postal rates. Of course, he could do LOTW, QSL manager, etc but maybe for him there is excitement in doing QSL cards.
