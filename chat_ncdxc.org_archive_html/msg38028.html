My math was ok, but the channel frequency was not.

DX is 5351.545 up 1-3 (posted on their site).

Our next window up, at channel 3 is 5358.5 center, minus 1.4 KHz is 5357.1 channel edge (a little higher for TX bandwidth).

5357.1 - 5351.545 = up 5.555 (or well beyond expectations).

The US loses out on this slot.  9L5A is out of band for the US on 60M.  Disappointing (and loud now) but nothing new for an EU crew.
