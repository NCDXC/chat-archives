She hears well (had me at +5 to her -18) but there is a serious EU wall, so you want power and tones accordingly.  I got through on a lower (~500) tone; legal limit into 4 elements at 60'.

The band is up/down by 10 dB (median 5 dB) and she takes short breaks often.  (She was -12 t0 -23 here this morning).

Since her tone is shifting by a few cycles, I expect temps are part of the reason for the breaks.

Patience.  Be thankful she doesn't go to multistream.

She was on 15M around 7 AM this morning (Pac time), along with VK0MQ but no path to the west coast (or PINW), so I suggest watching then also.
