On Tue, Mar 17, 2026 at 10:22 AM Rick nk7i via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

For fudging time, use 15 minute increments (0, 15, 30, 45), per hour.  That comes from the ARRL 'window' standard.  When the OQRS pops up with what it deems correct, you can adjust your log if needed.

Yes, I've had to tidy what HRD logbook messed up more than once; it tends to randomly put in the wrong times, sometimes more.  In this event, it didn't bother to log 30 FT8 but that was in the all.txt file (a rerun, but that's not the point).

The site also doesn't like HRD ADIF (in any format); it always leaves gaps requiring manual entries (tedious with so many slots attained).

ALSO, if you must change the date on Charles site, DO NOT USE the calendar but TYPE the date in, his page won't update a calendar correction.

I much prefer OQRS via the Clublog site, it's just simpler and works.  But on both sites a button for 'select all' for the various entries (THEN LOTW/LOTW+card/Bureau) would be a nice addition for slot hounds.

BTW j51a bounty was paid yesterday, LOTW confirmed today.  I REALLY like how that team plays in all aspects!  FIRST class effort and a bar for others to hope to attain.
