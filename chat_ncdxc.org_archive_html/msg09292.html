Good Luck!!! For me it was very important to listen carefully to what the K1N operator was saying because he would announce every few minutes that he was changing the band of frequencies where he was listening….and whom he was listening for  e.g.   JA only…EU only….Outside  NA…..VK, ZL…..Asia…..OC……Everyone….etc… but most of the pileup would keep calling where he was no longer listening….it was tough to hear him some times due to QRM  but those who heard his instructions and called where he wanted them to call…when they were wanted…. were successful…..last night he kept pretty much to 3725 KHz as his transmit frequency and he usually listened outside NA on 3795 KHz +/- and listened for NA (and others) on either 3770-3780 or on 3730-3750.
