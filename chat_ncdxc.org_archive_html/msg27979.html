Will,

When the DX answers your call, you have only 3 attempts at responding.  If not successful after that, they quit and move on.

At least that is the norm, I've also watch the robot mode answer for 40-50 times waiting for the answer (tying up a stream for MANY minutes).

SO, if you're going to WSJT-X and the first report doesn't get through (they answer again), tap the UP arrow on the tones, so your tone matches theirs, before your next transmission.  In that way, you do what is 'expected' for the F/H sequence in a generally lesser signal space.
