Many folks space it 4-8' above and inline with the boom, so it's out of phase with the other elements and doesn't bugger up their tuning.  You have to remember that offset when using 30M.

Consider the winds in your area and the side loading from that... steel not cheap TV mast (or moly), total rotor load and so on, all the way down.

You can shorten the 30M elements with a coil (at the tips is best) if you know someone who can bend tubing into a pig's tail...

If you have more than a 30M dipole; "Uh Houston..."
