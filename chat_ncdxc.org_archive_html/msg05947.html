Sometimes we get in a huff when we think someone is scamming us or pulling a fast one such as selling or profiting with QSL cards.

I have a friend who is an active ham in a rare Middle Eastern country. He regularly has to pay "Backsheesh" or payments to his government minders so that he can stay on the air and make contacts.  Would it be improper for him to request a certain amount for his QSL card so that he can remain active?   Would you rather not be able to work this rare country because their ways inflame your sensibilities?

Face it fellas, this is how it is in many parts of the world. There are few governments that are like ours, warts and all.
