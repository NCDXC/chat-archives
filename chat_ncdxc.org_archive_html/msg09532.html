*This is on the DX World.net website.

*NEWS UPDATE from N6PSE for DX-World*— We are now QRV with three stations. Unfortunately, two of our antennas and one logging laptop was damaged during shipment to Eritrea. Our plans to use WARC bands during the contest had to be cancelled. We have implemented backup antennas and we will try to obtain another laptop to allow for a fourth station.
Last night, we had a terrific run on 20 SSB, working the most difficult areas including W6/W7. Tonight we will be on 20 CW again looking for the most difficult areas to reach.
Internet is a big problem in Eritrea. Please do not send the team members anything other than text messages. Recordings, sound files and propagation documents cannot be opened. Remember what 26KBS dial up was like? 

Our website is live atwww.eritreaproject.net <http://eritreaproject.net/>

We hope to work you and get you in our logs!

73, E30FB
