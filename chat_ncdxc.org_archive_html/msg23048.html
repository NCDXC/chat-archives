﻿It has been an unusual early start for 6m season probably due to folks on FT8 modes which have really changed the landscape of 6m operation.  Since the third week of April we have had consistent openings to South America nearly daily here in Texas (EL09PN) and folks northeast of me which robust 6m stations are starting to work southern Italy which is extremely uncommon this early in May. Generally we see this activity in late May or more likely early June. Seeing SSSTP propagation from Japan this early is also extremely unusual. I see both Han (JE1BMJ) and Tac (JA7QVI) being spotted already, generally haven’t seen signs of them until mid June. Hope this is shaping into a fun 6m season because I am ready this year although super super busy with work. I am back traveling again and will be in Bay Area next week for a short visit seeing clients after over a year being lockdown.
