I was saddened to learn of Roger's passing. I am not sure that we ever met, but he was a regular on the W6TI repeater
and often chatted with us during our pursuit of DX. He loved the chase just like all of us. We rejoiced together when he got ATNOs. 

I knew that Roger was very ill and that his time was short. He told us so a few weeks ago on the repeater. He said he could not have
visitors due to Covid concerns. I know he was hoping to make it to work Bouvet. I regret that I did not get to meet and know him better. 

I've said it before and I will say it again, but one aspect of this hobby that I dislike is that due to our vintage etc, we often have
to say goodbye and mourn our friends. Be sure to appreciate all that you have in life. 

Rest in Peace Roger, 

Paul N6PSE
