I got a late start on chasing VK0EK this morning.

At 1430z DXA showed that they were on both cw and rtty on 40m. I could hear the JAs calling on 7038, but no trace of them on 7039.

I then listened on 7016, but there was only digital garbage on the frequency. The LP-PAN showed a big signal at 7014. Lots of JA calls there - took me about 5 minutes to realize that the S9+++ signal on 7014 was actually VK0EK! I finally found the JA they were working on 7012.3 and one call later I was in the log.

How did they do this? 20 kHz separation is too little for filters to work. Did they position the new delta loop to have a null with the 40m 4 square? Inquiring minds would like to know.

I hope the switch modes tomorrow. I need VK0H on rtty.

Steve, K6UM
