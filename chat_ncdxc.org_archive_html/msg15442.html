The point of the instructions to wait until you hear them call CQ is to make sure you can respond if the fox calls you.  (It only causes pointless QRM if you call when you can't hear the fox.)    So, hearing any transmission by KH7Z is sufficient to accomplish the same thing.  Go for it!   

It's best to call between 3000 and 4000 Hz where there are fewer competing stations.

And one more thing, a correction to George's note:  Call KH7Z (do not call CQ).


Greg N6GD
