Hello everyone,

I have a KF7P bracket on my old TriEx W51 tower with DB18 and a few
smaller antennas. 

KF7P is fantastic to work with and the bracket was made and shipped in a
timely fashion. He'll ask for some pictures and measurements. The
 bracket fit perfectly. I bought the engine hoist from Harbor Freight, bolts
right on the bracket. It was very easy to install.

It only takes 10 to 15 seconds to go full height. I cover the hoist with a U-
shaped sheet metal enclosure. (A big mailbox will work also)

I've had it for 6 or 7 years and no problems.

I can send pictures and videos if anyone is interested.

73,
