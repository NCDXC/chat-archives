Put me down as one who hopes they don't attempt to operate from Spit Pt.

I doubt that it will improve signals that much via SP as the flux is so low. They need to give W6/7 some exclusive time during the daily LP openings.

And more importantly they need to have someone operating 160/80/40 during the 12-15z time period. This morning they only had 1 station on any band and it was not any of those. Why were the other 4 stations not manned?

At 1430z they did start up on 40m ssb. Outstanding signal on 7070 and DU7ET asked them to listen for NA on 7170. But, the op continued to work JA/EU even though he was aware that he was over S9 in NA and stations were patiently waiting.

One thing I know is you can't work them if they are not on the air.
