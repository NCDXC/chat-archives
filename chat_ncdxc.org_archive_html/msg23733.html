Any words of advice about this. . . ?

The manager for SV2RSG/A is SV1RP.   I emailed him and he confirmed that I have a good QSO.   But he says he's not on LOTW and said I should send a "SASE via QRZ," by which I assume he means his address in QRZ.   I asked him if I could send him some $$ by PayPal (and thus avoid sending my card), but he didn't address that question in his email response.  I also asked him how much to send and he said "You can send a million dollars (you asked). You can send as much as you want and a donation to help to the monk Iakovos if you can."  I responded by saying "Oh, I was going to send 2 million, so I guess I got off easy."  

But seriously folks, how do you send postage in this day and age if the person doesn't take PayPal?   "Green stamps"?   They're not very secure and may not be easily converted to local currency.  

Any words of wisdom, secret intelligence, or other advice on sending SASEs in general in this day and age, and QSLing this rare one in particular?
