If Bruce's mounting plate is like that also, shear loads on the bolts should be minimal.

Leaving the bolts loose on what is supposed to be a bolted tension joint will apply shear loads on the bolts that may have not been anticipated in the design and initial fastener selection.   You will want to check the shear strength of the bolts being used.
