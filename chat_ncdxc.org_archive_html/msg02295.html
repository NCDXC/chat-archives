I had an FT-2000D for a while, and thought the ergonomics of the rig were among the best ever. However, the filtering and ability to work stations with close in string signals seemed seriously lacking. In fact, several features in this area (VRF, etc) just seemed to be very weak – even after that “PEP Upgrade”.

 

I’m betting that the AC0C might be the thing that brings the radio up to the spec that the radio advertised it was. I was disappointed because it seemed not even as good as my old FT-1000D and even the MK II with all of the Inrad / Collins filters.

 

Anyone have any experience with this? I wouldn’t mind one as a backup – and general rag chewing radio – since it was pretty darned nice on SSB – and just ergonomically speaking.
