Ditto - 5 watts to a Diamond 85' inverted vee with apex up 50' (sound
familiar?). I think they are only 4500 miles away, but I had tried to call
them the last couple of days on 80M CW with no luck - easy peasy today - one
call.

They have also been easy using the UrbanBeam up 55' on many bands 40M and
above. I'm enjoying sticking to 5W with a KX-3 and my goal is to chase DXCC
and IOTA entities (combined) and work 300 entities from ether list - all
QRP, CW and using the KX-3. I call it "The Free Range QRP DX 300 Challenge".

What makes it so much fun is that one QSO I can be working K7TRI off the
Oregon Coast for a "counter" (from the IOTA List) and the next day, someone
from the DXCC list. 

No formal awards, no stinkin' badges. Just fun. If I want an award I have to
make one myself.
