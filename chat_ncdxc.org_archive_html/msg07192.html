To All NCDXC Members:

The 2015 International DX Convention Logo Design Contest has been open for three weeks now --  but logo submissions have bit a bit slow.

So we're extending the deadline one week.  Please put on your creative caps and submit your candidate logo before 5:00 PM Friday, August 15, 2014.

The winner will receive a complimentary 2015 IDXC Convention Shirt.  More importantly you will get to see your fantastic creation displayed everywhere:  The Convention Guide, Convention Shirt, Convention Patch, Convention Website, The DXer Newsletter, and all over the internet.

Full details available on the NCDXC website:

http://www.ncdxc.org/pages/logocontest3.html

Go for it!

73,
John Miller, K6MM
Kevin Rowett, K6TD
IDXC 2015 Co-Chairs
