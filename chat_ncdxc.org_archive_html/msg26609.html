Hi, Bob,
IMO, definitely a hex beam. Full size elements, no traps or loading, no controllers, handles 2 KW, light weight (25 lbs), and very broadband flat SWR. 11 ft turning radius. Wind load is about 5 sq ft. Most hex beams also include a 6m element as standard. 40m elements can be added on, but increase the weight and wind load.
Myself and Rich WB6EWM are using the KIO hex beam with great success.
They are great antennas.
I recommend the KIO hex beam.
https://www.k4kio.com/

73,
Roger
