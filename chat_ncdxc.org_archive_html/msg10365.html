I have several vintage computer monitors that are surplus to my needs. Two are 1024 x 768 Dells, two are much higher resolution Dells. All work really well with a P3, and the high res monitors are also great for extending the desktop. All are in good working condition, and as far as I know, have no RF issues.
There's also a Samsung that runs on 12VDC that DOES have RFI issues -- thanks to its touchscreen interface, it turns flips in the presence of strong RF, and puts out noise as well.
The 1024x768 Dells and the Samsung are free -- pick up at my QTH or at a club meeting. I'd like to get $50 each for the high res Dells -- I paid good money for them, and they are excellent monitors.
