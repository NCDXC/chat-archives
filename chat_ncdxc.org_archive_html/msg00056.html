Hi Paul,

 

I did very well.  I needed Conway for an all-time new one and worked them on at least one mode on all bands from 80 to 10 (I don’t have a 160 antenna).  I have 11 Qs in the log, 6 on CW and 5 on SSB.  30m was the toughest one.  I used my remote station in Truckee with a G5RV and about 1200 W.

 

Thanks to the team for a great expedition and a new one!
