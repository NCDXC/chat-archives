Somehow, I got in their log with 100w and a wire (running out a second-story window to a city tree on the street). I was a -23, but actually made 2 QSOs (30 minutes apart) because I didn’t show up in the real-time online log on the first one. I went for the insurance QSO, and got the RR73 the second time, and successfully requested their QSL card. This was nothing short of a miracle for me. I don’t often see that I’m being heard outside NA on 40m.
