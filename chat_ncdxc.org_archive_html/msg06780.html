Read ur blog---enjoyable...tnx for taking the time to share your thoughts....73/DX.........
as an aside, the P5 issue-----there is a building exactly on the DPRK & ROK border, at Panmunjon, managed by the UN/military authority.....I tried to use it once to activate P5---let me tell u why....
The building straddles the exact border, and 1/2 of it is in the DPRK, and the other half is in the ROK. When both sides meet,the table in the middle is split right down into DPRK & ROK halves,with each side sitting on their side for discussions&negotiations. Now,when one side wants to use the building alone,without the other side present, they go in first and lock the DPRK or the ROK side,so those sides cannot enter during their discussions.......it was my idea to go in, and use the ROK side and undergo 'military discussions', but to bring along a rig,& an antenna for inside use.....and put P5 on the air from this building. The antenna&rig would be on the DPRK side of the building.......the problem of course would be to navigate the burocracy of the DPRK to get their permission to demonstrate communication from that site, as well as the antiquated ARRL burocracy for recognition....lastly, to get permission from the UN command to do this,as a demonstration project.....well,I struck out at the beginning of this permission ladder----the UN Command would not hear of it,and so it didn't go anywhere......now,if there is someone out there better connected than I, go for it.....hihi.....some time ago, I secured a 'rock' from P5---at great risk for the guy that did this for me,unasked....he just heard I wanted one for the W6ISQ Collection...and did it....damn near caused an international incident, and got waxed by the snipers....oy veh.....and, I did write a short paper for the NCDXF newsletter about this event several years ago.......beats the hell out of getting shot out of the water by the Chinese trying to a Dxpedition from 'Spratly' or whoever claims that rock for the moment..........and so it goes......carry on.......Gerald (Jerry) Griffin,K6MD.
