On Tue, Oct 28, 2025 at 10:44 AM Rick nk7i via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

The 10M opening was (gone now here, NA/EU only now) really interesting.  I would usually suggest the team finally found the rotor control, but the path is over EU so it seems that conditions just chose to favor us.  I was decoding 4 streams at the peak (and I'm 800++ miles N from most of you; Rusty is about 400 west).

Now the lower bands (160-60 left here) and other modes, will be a big challenge.  I heard them last week on 30 CW (long path) clearly but could not bust the EU wall (SP).  Hearing them (even on 80) hasn't been easy with lower gain antennas and their noise... wait and see.

Although I wouldn't mind a 6M spotlight either...  I've logged AF on 6 from up here.

I'm NOT whining or complaining; from ATNO to 7 bands, all from my station (no rent a radio), nothing to whine about.

GL all,
Rick nk7i
