Craig-

 

I am very happy with my RigExpert AA-54.  With a USB cable to the PC, it plots Smith charts, return loss, SWR, etc etc.  I swept my 5-band cubical quad with it from 13 to 30 MHz and got a nice graph of the whole antenna.  I’musing it right now to check my new SteppIR while it’s still on the ground.  Doesn’t seem to eat batteries.  If I were buying it today I probably would get the AA-170 to cover 2M as well.
