I have a very rough looking 80-20 M RF deck built with a pair of 813 tubes.  I used his many hundreds of hours in the 70s but it has sat since about 1979.  There is no HV supply and it is not fussy unless you're a purest that wants a perfect  pi output match to the tube.  This works but it is not elegant!
If you want a project to get a KW on the 80-20 M bands, this would serve you.  Clean it up, gin up a simple HV supply, one voltage at maybe 1.5kV and you are there.
In an open 19" rack panel with no cover.  YOu can enjoy watching the tubes get red to orange depending on how long you talk!
Let me know if you are interested in a pick up.
