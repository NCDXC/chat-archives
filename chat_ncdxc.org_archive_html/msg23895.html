I am sure many of you in the NCDXC remember my dad, very long time member, Smitty, AKA W6CS, AKA W6JZU.

My sister and I are looking now to sell the home, built on a location precisely for its ham possibilities. It was frequently used for countless contests over the decades by various NCCC members, earned dad 5BDXCC, used with much of dad’s charity work, got me OSCAR WAS #14, host to visiting VUs, ZAs, UL’s and so on.

So dad’s tower and antennas are now available. Not sure how either have held up over the years of disuse however. And, if anyone wanted a dream location in the hills part of Los Altos Hills, we’re preparing to accept offers. .
