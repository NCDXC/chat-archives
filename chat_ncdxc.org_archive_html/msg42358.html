Yesterday in our Board meeting, Club VP Lucas W6AER gave an update that he had talked with Peter W6OOL our W6TI club repeater administrator. As we have internet at our radio site, a DMR hot spot will be purchased and configured to access our repeater and internet.  This will enable club members within the W6TI talk group to access the NCDXC repeater from anywhere with internet connectivity. 

Lucas will update us further when the project is ready to implement.
