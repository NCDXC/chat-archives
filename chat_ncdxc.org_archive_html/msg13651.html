I thought I would try something new for this contest: turn down the power to minimum and see what I could work.  My Bird 43 and 100W slug showed 10 W so that is what I used for most of my contacts.  I stopped after 50 QSOs and did not try very hard at any time (except for the 90 Min chase of the Mt Athos at legal power).  I earned 35 unique entities :

 

AS 13

EU 16

SA 5

OC 10

AF 2

NA 4

 

Bands:

40 M in morning 7

20 M evening 35

15 M afternoon/evening 8

 

I had to give a few serial number repeats but most that I tried, I worked.  Those that could not hear me may have had local QRN or QRM 9that I could not hear).

 

Anyway, fun to see what I could.  Give it a try.  Quite satisfying and surprising to me.
