You can filter by mode in DX Watch.  Under "DX Spots", select "create your filter."  The last filter column is "mode." You can filter on ssb, cw, psk31, beacon, sstv, rtty, and ft8. I tried ssb and it eliminated all the ft8 spots on 15.
