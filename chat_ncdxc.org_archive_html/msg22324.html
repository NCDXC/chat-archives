Truman,
K9YC did a study on the question of rooftop vs ground mounting of vertical antennas and found a clear advantage to rooftop mounting.
I have an HF9V on a second-story metal roof and it works ok.
I would also point you to an article by K6MM in QST some years back on his 160m vertical, using a full 1/4 wave wire spiraled onto a 2 inch pvc pipe, and using a wire along his back fence as counterpoise.  You could use the same approach for an 80 meter vertical.  That would surely beat any bottom-loaded vertical.
73
Glen W6GJB
