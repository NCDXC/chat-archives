Last night, I watched some W6's get through to TI9A on 30, 80 and this morning on 40 meters FT8. 

I inquired with those W6's about their power output and indeed, High Power gave them success. They were all surprised at the weak signal reports received from TI9A. 

TI9A may have a high noise issue that lets them hear the stronger signals on FT8.

Just a theory.
