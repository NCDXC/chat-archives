I have been timing the rise and fall of the signals of the TX3X guys and find them remarkably uniform.  For example...on 17 cw they would drop out severely for 10 to12 seconds and then be sold copy for 3 to 5. When I sent my call 8 or 9 seconds into total silence I was pleasantly surprised to hear them come back loud and clear....and drop out totally right after....I doubt this is an original insight but the predictability was as high as I recall 
At 30 feet..20, 17,15, 12 and 10 all doing a bit of stop watch clicking.  This is what comes of sitting in front of a hissing radio...Jim
