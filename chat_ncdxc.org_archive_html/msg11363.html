>From direct knowledge…

1) better reporting of DXp costs
2) cost of getting somewhere with a boat has risen dramatically - the fees the boat charges
3) costs of getting permits, landing fees, dock fees, etc have rally escalated - mostly related to gov’t tapping the cruise lines asa new source of revenue.
4) larger DXps - more ops, more antenna, etc - mostly because a DXp can’t get permission for another ten years (K1N, K5P).
