For those of you who weren't able to make the meeting, the important news is Tromelin in late October, early November.  Many  of the details are at their site..tromelin2014.com...and as of late yesterday, please note our logo under "  How you can help".   Tromelin is #3 on the Most Wanted  list for the West Coast.   This #3 is the highest of any region in the world and as such your Board wanted them to tell us how they were going to address that---especially after a less than efficient Glorioso expedition. They were very responsive  (and noted they had nothing to do with Glorioso) and in a long memo itemized antenna choices, grey line focus, awareness of the very brief windows and solicited our specific input so that the operators would have a strategy we took part in.   To that end I asked Stu, K6TU, to work with them which he has already begun.  They note the upcoming Reunion operation (app.500 miles from Tromelin) and strongly suggest we note openings, strengths etc. and provide feedback to the Tromelin team.   We gave them $1000, one of the largest grants we've made, based on widespread need and a clear effort on their part to focus on us as efficiently as they can.
