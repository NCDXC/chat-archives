Checking the Entries that will Appear in the Ladder:

The DX Ladder uses entries from Clublog's NCDXC League page or from the webform at http://ncdxc.org/pages/dxladder.html, depending on the member's preference.

I obtain the ClubLog entries used in the DX Ladder from: http://www.clublog.org/league.php
Club: NCDXC
Settings:
All Bands, Current Entities, Confirmed are used for all DX Ladder columns except "DXCC".
All Bands, Current & Deleted, Confirmed  are used for the "DXCC" column in the DX Ladder.

ClubLog rebuilds the League tables once per day.  As a result, the NCDXC League table may be delayed by a day or more after they you upload your data.

The 160m through 6m, but not 60m, bands are used for calculating the Challenge score in the Ladder.
