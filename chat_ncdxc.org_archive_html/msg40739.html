The league also won't recognize printed post cards that are 100% electronic (ie no handwriting at all).  They want some form of human interaction.
This makes complete sense and I fully agree with that policy.  When I used to print my own QSL cards, I always signed them in ink.  Thus they were accepted by card checkers.
