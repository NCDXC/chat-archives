No, I don't need a map--I know where it isn't, which is QRV.

I am perched on 3523 and there has not been a posting for FT4TA in quite a while--about an hour-- on any band. We were assured takedown would not begin until Sunday our time; maybe they meant Sunday Zulu.

I reconfigured my 2-wire NE/SW Beverage. First,I removed DC power from the switching arrangement and disconnected the Beverage wires from the feed box; then I tied the 2 wires together and drove  it against ground, using one of those 75/300 ohm TV transformers to configure a single-wire Beverage, presumably unterminated. I am hoping for an improvement in noise floor toward Tromelin and await the sunset bump.

But there is no sunset bump, because Tromelin is not QRV.

I am running out of tricks. Am I waiting in vain?
