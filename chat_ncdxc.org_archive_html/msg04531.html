I did on the air tests of 80m dipoles at 60 feet
and 130 feet and a ground mounted 60 foot
vertical.  I am on flat, high conductivity
ground, with a horizon of a few degrees.

My results were that the vertical was
interchangeable with the high dipole for
DX.  Both beat the low dipole by typically
10 dB.  For signals within a few hundred miles,
the low dipole won by typically 10 dB.

The vertical had a very large ground screen.
