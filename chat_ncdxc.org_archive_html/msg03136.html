I don't believe you really know what many of us are doing to support new
DXers. Some of us just don't talk a lot about it as others may do.

I believe the NCDXC is a good place for activist grouchy old farts (we all
know who we are) who sometimes know what they are talking about and
sometimes don't...but are still lovable in their own ways and who
nevertheless contribute and their hearts are in the right place.......and
for selfish grouchy old farts who may just want to get together and enjoy
each other's grouchiness and talk DX....or for an excuse to get out of the
house and have an adult beverage. Call me radical.  :-)

73 and good DX.

Jim, there are a lot of positive things happening in the NCDXC, perhaps you
don't see them because you don't come to the meetings.   Speaking of
meetings, our next meeting is in Foster City and we are trying to find an
East Bay venue for a following meeting.  

Ross and the Board have been listening to the membership. Issues are being
addressed.

Yesterday, the Board discussed your issue of access to the club repeater.
Solutions and coverage is actively being discussed.

New DXers are actively being mentored. W6OAT and I have opened our shacks
for new DXers such as Jake-K6MP and others.  

Sure, there are a few cranky old timers in the club, but there are also many
fine, positive people who are eager to share their positive views of this
great hobby.

73 and gud DX!

Paul N6PSE
