Hi all,

I ran and activated Tulare County for the California QSO Party this weekend.  I ended up doing mostly CW and only a little SSB.  76% CW vs. 24% SSB.  That surprised me.  What surprised me most was CW was more fun for me than SSB. :)  Thanks for all the encouragement over the last year or so.

          Soapbox :
  I specifically told my family I am working this contest.  I
  had most of the day on Saturday to myself and the contest.  
  But Lunch, Dinner and a few errands had to be done.  I worked
  9 contacts on 40m Saturday night to get some mults, but I
  was exhausted after working until 10:30 PM Friday night.  On
   Sunday I started late because of family obiligations.  After
  just a few SSB contacts and zero returns on calling CQ I
  moved back to CW.  The CW portion FAR exceeded my expectations
  On Saturday I ran at 12 wpm and sent QRS when needed when
  I was calling CQ CQP.  It was fun.  Several small 3-4 call
   pileups.  On Sunday I had an hour of calls.  39 in a row for
  an hour and that was the most I've ever done in a row.  I
  did use a decoder on Saturday, but as the time went on I used
  it less and less and just sent AGN? or K? W?  V? N?  hihi.
  I think this showed me that I can work and run in a CW contest.
