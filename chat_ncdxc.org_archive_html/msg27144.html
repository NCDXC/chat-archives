Congratulations to our very own Lucas Ford on the publication of his exhaustive guide to DXing. I was one of many members privileged to read this cover-to-cover in the last month. Even though Lucas considers this a guide for DX beginners, I learned quite a bit! The book is available at Amazon at

 

https://www.amazon.com/Ham-Radio-DX-Complete-Rockstar/dp/B0BNH93TS5

 

Lucas has been working on this for 3 years. Please take a moment to look it up on Amazon, follow Lucas, and read his book. It’s important to leave a review on Amazon, which moves the book up the rank order listings and will help make Lucas world famous.
