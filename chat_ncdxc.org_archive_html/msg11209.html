Sunrise for VP8STI is around 0530Z, so the evening is not over yet. I heard a small flurry of callers from the Pac NW around 0400. K7ZV got through and I think W7KW, VE5RA and KA7T. Others north of  us appear to have a path tonight also, like VA5DX and I heard VY1JA calling, and also KA6BIM. Number of callers appears to be increasing around 0445Z.

 Not one dit here from VP8, but my Beverages are broken, I am not well enough to to climb the hill and fix them and they are acting like BOGs. I am using my TX antenna.
