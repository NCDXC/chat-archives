I hope you are having success with the Baker Island DXpedition KH1/KH7Z.  Conditions are sometimes excellent and other time simply band noise.  But it is the chase isn’t it.

 

Reminder – NCDXC Picnic July 14, RSVP by July 5.  Well over 60 are signed up so far.  The picnic is NCDXC sponsored – free for a member and one guest.  I need a count for the cater so please RSVP.

 

If you are coming to the picnic be sure to RSVP by the end of the week.  I give the caterer the count over the weekend.

 

NCDXC club members and a guest are invited to the 2018 NCDXC Summer Picnic on Saturday, July 14 2018.  Great time to visit with other club members and their guests.  The park is beautiful.  There is shade but also lots of sun so hats are a good idea.

 

Again this year it will be held at:

 

Shoup Park (Patriots Corner)

400 University Avenue

Los Altos, CA 94022

 

The picnic begins at 10:00AM and is over at 2PM.  Lunch is served at 12:00PM noon.  Following lunch the club’s donation to NCDXF will be made.

 

For the enjoyment of all our members, this year’s picnic will, once again, be catered by Andy's BBQ.  It is important that we have an accurate headcount for our caterer before the picnic. Please RSVP to Bob Lanning, W6OPO, at treasurer@ncdxc.org, and tell him if you’ll be attending and if you’ll be bringing a guest.  RSVPs cannot be accepted after Thursday, July 5, 2018.

 

 

Menu selections:

Spareribs

Beef Brisket

BBQ Chicken

Smoked Sausage Links

BBQ Beans

Potato Salad

Cole Slaw

Corn Cobbettes

Assorted Cookies

Assorted Sodas/Bottled Water

 

Plates, flat wear and napkins are included.  You are welcome to bring a desert if you’d like.  No Alcohol!

 

Russ Bentson, K6KLY, will be checking QSL cards for DXCC applications.  Be sure to bring your cards, completed application and a #10 SASE addressed to the ARRL. 

 

From past experience we know it can get hot at Shoup Park in July. There are picnic tables and shade but consider bring folding chairs and portable shade or move into shady areas as needed and perhaps a small folding table as well.

 

Looking forward to seeing you on Saturday July 14.
