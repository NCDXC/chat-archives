Wind blowing??

 

Many antennas will exhibit swings in SWR with wind do to the elements spacing changing with the wind.  I have that with my Force-12 C3S especially on 10 & 15 meters.

 

Loose connections should exhibit fine SWR or infinity – nothing in between.  Had that here too with a broken lead in the Window Wire (450) feeding the dipole.
