Greetings from Colorado!
 
This years DX Convention in Visalia was awesome!  The new venue was perfect.
 
The Marriott Hotel is 1000% better then the old Holiday Inn and the Convention Center facilities were outstanding!
 
I understand a few issues like sore feet (no carpet or pads) in the exhibit hall, and some electrical issues were a few of the minor problems I heard about from the exhibitors. Both issues can be addressed in the future.
 
As an attendee, I would like to see future years at the new venue.
 
The nice comfort of the Marriott rooms were better. Also, the hotel's food was extremely superior to the Holiday Inn. The ability to walk a few blocks and have a nice variety of dining choices was pleasing. Downtown is a much better location.
 
I arrived Thursday evening and found out about the local farmers market a block away. I was able to walk around and sample the local fare.
 
I understand they do the farmers market every Thursday evening so maybe next year we can have the opportunity to sample the local fare again.
 
One rumor around the convention was the one about moving back to the old rusty Holiday Inn???
 
I hope this was just a rumor. To go back to that awful Holiday Inn property would be like shooting yourselves in the foot.
 
Please consider the Marriott for next year.
 
Even if it costs a little more per night, it would be well worth it.
 
Just my 2 cents worth.
 
Hope to see everyone again next year and I will see you all in the pile-ups!
