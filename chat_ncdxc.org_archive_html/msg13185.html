On Sun, Mar 12, 2017 at 09:28 Rick WA6NHC via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

Their propagation tool on the website and ham cap both seem to think there will be openings 15M and below but their timing hasn't been useful to the West at all.  They've stayed on bands that are conducive to EU/AS and don't seem to be trying (a guess, not a slam) for much more.

As far as them building the stations, that was giving them a benefit of the doubt.  I know they've shut down a few times for assembly (raising antennas), it seems logical that the team can either build it THEN get on the air or build WHILE being on the air with what is assembled...  That's a hard decision to make.

As far as waiting, I'm never that patient.  Even though I have a cap gun station, I jump right in at the gate because life (solar events, storms, local noise) happens, it's best to try all times that are open to us.  The 20th is only a week away, best to jump in at any chance before the fickle sun throws a CME at us.
