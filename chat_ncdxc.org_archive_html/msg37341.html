Hello NCDXC friends, 

The CQ World Wide SSB Contest starts later today at 00:00 UTC. This is a great opportunity to grab some rare DX entities whether you are competing or not. 

The DX stations will be simplex (not split) , many will be quite loud. 

To work them, call with your call, if they reply with a signal report and their CQ Zone, reply with "Five Nine Zone 3"

They don't want your state or any other information. 

Things will be slower and easier on Sunday if you don't get the DX station today or Saturday. 

If you want to read the contest rules, they are available here:
https://cqww.com/rules.htm
