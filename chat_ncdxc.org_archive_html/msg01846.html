Here is your NCDXC Question of the Week:

 

What goodies are you hoping to check out at Visalia?   A new rig?  New antenna?

 

 

 

My answer:

 

I want to check out the new CrankIR antennas by Steppir. These could be just what we need for the low bands on DXpeditions. They look like they would be light and would travel well. Some of my team likes the fact that there is no motor, control cable or power required. These antennas have the potential to allow us to travel with a lot less gear since they can cover various bands with the turn of the crank.
