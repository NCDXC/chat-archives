It happen to me yesterday. A friend with a super station was checking me out while I was working a station in Argentina. I did not receive a RR so I kept sending my exchange. He texted me: He gave you RRR. 
This is the problem with ft8. The computer works as long as the sequence completes as expected. If not is up to the operators to guess what happen. Still brain involved but with no input to judge. 
In contests this has been the biggest problem. High error rates (nil), etc. Ed Muns has been studying it for improvement. 
Last WWDigi I tried to work a CA station 100 times. Never responded. 
I contacted them after the contest. They apologized their computer logged me earlier in the contest,  I never received confirmation,  then every time I answered CQ ignored me because I was a dupe. 
Cw or SSB involves human brain that can communicate and solve these issues. 
Not everything is rosy in the land of FT...🤪
