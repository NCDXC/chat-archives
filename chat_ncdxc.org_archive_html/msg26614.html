FWIW, if you plan to put up a hex on a mast with the rotor at the bottom — which is a great way to do it — then I would avoid the NA4RR hex. The center support for that antenna is sized to fit irrigation pipe, not antenna mast sections… and yes, they’re different. If you try to install the NA4RR hex at the top of a standard telescoping mast, you’ll find that you’ll need to shim the antenna-to-mast connection or the antenna will not fit securely. It’s a tremendous pain in the butt. Take my word for it.
