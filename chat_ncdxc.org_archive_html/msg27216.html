FT8 is 10 -to- 12 dB better than CW (As much as 2 S-units better)

 

Minimum detectable CW SNR is 1 dB*

If that is in 250 Hz, then CW  C/No = 25 dB-Hz

FT8 Decoding SNR thresholds are -20.8 to -18.6 in 2500 Hz

FT8 C/No = 15.4 -to- 13.0 dB-Hz

 

* Wikipedia number for trained listener
