I suggested we put those entities is a "suspended" state at the Visalia DX Forum a couple years ago when I was on the panel.
If you go down the top 50 most wanted it becomes quickly obvious a significant percentage of them have not or will not be on the air for 20+ years whether due to government policy, landing permits, rising seas, skyrocketing cost, etc.
Any entity that was subsequently activated would be immediately back on the active list. All that moves then is the number needed to make Honor Roll/Top of Honor Roll. The number of "entities" remains the same.
I have zero hope a policy like this would ever be done by the current ARRL governance.
