Many folks have asked about sending flowers for Carl, AI6V’s Celebration of Life event this coming Sunday.  I talked last night with Sue, AI6YL and she asked that in lieu of sending flowers that you consider making a donation in Carl’s memory to Hospice.  If you wish to do that, you can do it online by clicking on this link:
 
https://hospicenet.org/html/donate.html
 
If you don’t want to make an online credit card donation, click on the link and it also will tell you how you can mail a donation via check.
 
Hospice’s donation form provides an area where you can ask that an acknowledgement of your donation be sent to someone.  If you wish to let Sue know of your donation, the info you will need for the form is Sue A. Cook, 7559 West Agate Avenue, Las Vegas, NV 89113.

Please pass this info along to anyone else you think would like to know.
 
73 de Rusty, W6OAT
