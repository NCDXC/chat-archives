I had a StepIR BigIR with the 80M coil installed in Los Gotos. It was attached to the edge of my garage roof, about 8-10 feet off the ground. I used 2 tuned radials for each band, stretched out over the roof and extending into the backyard. (The lot had enough room for a full size 160M dipole. I had a common-mode choke built following K8YC's tables.
The antenna worked very well at full power with my Expert 1.3K amp. While there was no extra work in the shack, I had to convince my grandson that the noises the antenna made while tuning were normal. :-)
I'm looking forward to installing it here in New Hampshire, after the snow goes away. I have two possible locations:
A kind of swampy area with plenty of room for radials. The swamp may get fairly dry during dry weather. 

  The top of an outbuilding at about 8-9 feet.

Any suggestions about which location would work better?
