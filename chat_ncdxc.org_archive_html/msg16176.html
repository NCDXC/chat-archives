Paul -
I use RUMlogNG on the Macintosh. It is a DX logger and appears to have all the features you want. (There are some I am unsure of as I don't use them.) It is available from the Apple App Store.
It also has a contest logger which is pretty good. The big upside of the contest logger is that is uses the CAT interface with the K3/KX3 to send CW and RTTY.
