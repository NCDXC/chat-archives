2014 IDXC - Visalia

Sponsoring Hotel Bookings and Special raffle

The 65th International DX Convention will be held at the Visalia Convention Center
Thursday April 3 through Sunday April 6.

The SCDXC has listened to your phone calls and read your e-mails.
The SCDXC will be making the changes and
improvements that you have requested!!!

Hotel Reservation should be made directly with the Sponsoring Hotels beginning on the
date and time at the telephone numbers shown below.

When you book your Hotel reservations be sure to tell them you are attending the
2014 IDX Convention at the Visalia Convention Center.

Comfort Inn - Downtown 800-424-6423 May 13 @10:00 AM
Hampton Inn 559-732-3900 May 13 @10:00 AM
Lamp Liter Inn 559-732-4511 May 13 @10:00 AM
Holiday Inn 559-651-5000 May 13 @10:00 AM
Comfort Inn - Airport 559-651-3700 May 13 @10:00 AM
Marriott 559-636-1111 May 27 @10:00 AM

NO FAX RESERVATIONS

To be eligible for a very special drawing you must book at one of the 6 Sponsoring hotels
listed above. You will receive one special raffle ticket in your registration packet for each
night booked for the Convention (a maximum of 4 nights, Thursday - Sunday).
* Only hotel reservations made after the dates shown above are eligible, note the
Marriott can't begin taking reservations until Monday, May 27
* Only one hotel reservation per Convention registration will be counted toward the raffle
prize.

* You must occupy the room.
* For persons sharing a room, raffle tickets will only be available to the person listed on
the reservation. Sharing the prize will be the responsibility of the winner.
* Reservations made after Friday March 7, 2014 will not be eligible for the drawing.
Reserve Your Room Early - Only 1200 Tickets will be
Printed!

Check back from time to time for the details of the Special Raffle Prize to be
awarded to a 2014 Convention attendee who is staying at one of the Sponsoring
Hotels.
