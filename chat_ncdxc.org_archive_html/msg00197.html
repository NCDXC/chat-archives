Good morning, here is your NCDXC Question of the week?

 

 

How did you do with the TT8TT and 3B9SP DXpeditions?

 

 

 

My response: I was pleasantly surprised by the TT8TT operation. I had not fared well with any of their 60 operations and my expectations were low for TT, even though I needed it for a new one!   I was very pleased to hear the TT8TT Ops asking for West Coast starting about mid-way through their operation. Indeed, they made a good effort to work the West Coast over a period of days. Good band conditions prevailed and I was able to get them on 10, 15, and 20 meters!

 

The 3B9SP operation has been more challenging due to the further distance (further than Chad) and the fluctuating band conditions. The NCDXC donated to support this DXpedition and I am glad that we did as the DXpedition Leader has been in communication with me and has made a good effort to log as many NCDXC members as they could. They will QRT tomorrow. T

 

Thus far, I have them on 20 CW, 20 SSB and 17 CW.  I thought I had them on 40 CW but it appears that was a slim.

 

How did you do with TT8TT and 3B9SP?

 

Paul N6PSE
