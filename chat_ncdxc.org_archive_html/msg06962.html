Jerry,

 

Only one thought on the matter –

KT34A is a trap tribander.  If you are interested in the WARC bands there maybe power limitations on the KT34A on those bands (12 & 17) due to the traps.  If there is a WARC band option for the KT34A then that should work.

 

Sure a lot of KT34As out there.

 

I’m using the trapless C3S from Force-12 so no power issues.  Also weighs only 26 pounds and low visual profile.  Just need a reasonable tuner for 12 & 17.  Thirty meters – no way.  The dipole works better here.
