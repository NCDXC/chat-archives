Yes.  I have experienced the same thing.  I'm having a hard time hearing stations that normally would be easy for me to work.  I think I have two problems.  One is some local RFI that I didn't have before.  And the other is just really bad constant QRN....frying pan noise at S7.  I would say conditions were much better a year ago.  I hope the QRN will get better as we get into the full swing of winter.

On the downslope of a solar cycle we see a lot more solar disturbances (i.e. high solar wind) that don't really help the higher bands but hurt the entire HF spectrum.  On the upslope of a solar cycle we had very good SFI but not as many solar disturbances.  The high SFI didn't seem to affect the lowbands as much as the high solar winds.

One other problem I have on 40m and 30m is voice chatter from Asia.  The problem seems a lot worse than before.  The phone transmissions are broadband and they cover up the weak signals from Asia.
