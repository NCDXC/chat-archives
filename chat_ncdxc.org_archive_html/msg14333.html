This is what Wayne said on the DX University webpage:
- - - - - 
News from the ARRL Board

20. January, 2018:
Effective at the close of the ARRL Board meeting today -- 20. January, 2018 -- an additional DXCC criterion will be added to the ARRL DXCC List Critera. Specifically: Section II, Political Entities.
The new rule adds entities having and IARU Member Society AND being listed on the United States Department of State List of Independent State in the World. Note that BOTH of these criteria must exist.
Additional information, including QSO start dates, etc. should be forthcoming in the next few days.
