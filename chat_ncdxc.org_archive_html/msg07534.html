I worked ST0RK at another ham's house in 1979 during a SCCC meeting.  It seemed weird in some way but WFWL.
The feeling that it was tainted has never left.  Never did it again.  I will not use any remote QSO outside my call area count for anything but some future remote class of award.
 
As time has move on, my ability to compare my  accomplishments to others is more fuzzy/foggy than ever..  And as Ted said, I take pride in working my 337 with no more than 4 elements never higher than 60' and a legal KW.  Regardless, I love the fun of finding DX and working it on my own and continue to marvel at the wonder of this wonderful hobby based on the magical ether.
