In case you haven't noticed, current A, K and SS numbers that most software
shows, are not current.

NOAA has revamped their web site, which must of our world links into.
The new site went 'live' yesterday.  ALL the links everyone uses, are
broken.  This includes some of the text files within their own products
linking back to the old site.  Doh!

Here is a link for current data: ftp://ftp.swpc.noaa.gov/pub/latest/wwv.txt

This link will NOT work in VOACAP (it doesn't do FTP).  I presume HamCAP
will fail also (don't have it).  For folks using HRD, an update will be
forthcoming (it's hard coded in the program).  Others might be able to
manually enter the data.

It means that around the world, everyone that links to wwv.txt will have to
update THEIR links.  That will take weeks.  It's going to take a few days
for the search engines to catch up.
