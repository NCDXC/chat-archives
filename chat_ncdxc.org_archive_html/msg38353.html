Paul,

I think that both modes have their place.

Super Fox worked well and from what I have read in the RC8 release notes may be even better now. The signal strength hit isn’t that terrible and the possible throughput is very high.

Fox/Hound is OK until the DX station uses way too many streams and their signal is so weak that most cannot decode them.

 

The problem with Super Fox is that some people will need to install the newest RC8 program since General Availability (GA) release 2.6.1 does not have this mode.
