Paul, IMHO the Bureau will be around for years to come.  Amateurs around the world still like to receive
the hard copy QSL for a contact.  LoTW is great because it allows fast confirmation and no fuss.  The
cost is almost nothing compared to sending and receiving a hard copy QSL card.  I think being able
to use the OQRS system is another means to receive your cards without sending one.  However,
since almost everything is rather costly these days, the Bureau is a very inexpensive way to send and
receive cards although the time for return can be months to years.  Our CA bureau has about 26
(give or take) volunteers and cards are mailed every 3-4 months.  We are all managed by Robert
Devine, KC6AWX.  He is also a member of the NCDXC so perhaps he may have a word or two on
the subject.  Hope that helps Paul.
 
73 Alan
