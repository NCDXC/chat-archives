FWIW although I don’t have an opinion on the “best”… (ANY Dxpedition has my admiration)…  But I really take my had off to those willing to seek input on what it takes to work different parts of the world where their demand is likely highest.
 
As an example, Krish contacted me ahead of the expedition requesting propagation help – I provided VU7AG a prediction widget as well as running a full set of predictions for Krish based on their specific set up.  It was clear from those that working the Left Coast was going to be a long path opportunity.  He was super responsive and open to input.
 
It was great to have similar requests from TX5D, C82DX, S21ZBC, VK9MT, VU7AG, T33A, K9W and currently FT5ZM. 
 
Anyone Dxpedition which digs deep into their own pockets has my gratitude and through donations to NCDXF, my financial support.
 
Wish there was some way to get our compatriots in EU to do the same and not complain when Dxpeditions spend so much time calling for NA/SA only.
 
Stu K6TU
