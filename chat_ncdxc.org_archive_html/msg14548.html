I have seen 4B4B spotted on a few bands/modes, and just had to chuckle. A few years back, there was a Revillagegido DXpedition under 4A4A.

This is the ONLY DXpedition that I worked (and confirmed) in all 26 possible band/mode combinations, from 160 to 10 meters, CW/SSB/RTTY. I remember getting the last one: I was leaving town the next day, and I heard them on 160 SSB in the late evening. I called and asked if he could QSY to 160 RTTY (the only one I was missing, and a pretty obscure band/mode in any case). He said to come back in an hour or so. And he was there, I made the Q, and that was the only time I was ever at the top of the leaderboard with 26 QSOs. 

I don’t need 4B4B on any band/mode.

Rich KE1B
