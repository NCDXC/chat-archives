On Tue,1/13/2015 9:32 AM, Dave Gomberg via Chat wrote:

What headphones have you tried that don't work for you?
Yamaha CM500, often loud environmental noises (leaf blowers, amplifier fan, etc). Frequently I am trying to pull speech out of low S/N signals (I am lucky if S/N is 5 dB).
What sort of noise in the shack?
These are the sorts of noise that noise-cancelling headsets can attack. Many years ago, I had a Sennheiser headset for evaluation that worked quite well on an airplane. I've heard that some of the PVRC guys like the Bose noise cancelling headsets. Both companies are well established audio companies.
