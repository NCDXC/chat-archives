I had a great time at IDXC!

Sure there were a few areas that could have been improved but
they did not detract from the main event. I enjoyed seeing old
friends and making new ones. I thoroughly enjoyed both K4UEE's
excellent Amsterdam Island presentation and K6MM's superb talk
on his recent Wake Island adventure. It was a kick to spend time with
and thank Krish for the VU7 and VU4 QSOs. He is a dynamo and
fascinating to speak with. It was great to see the latest and greatest
stuff from the vendors. I wonder if the Pixel loop would solve my QRN??
Everything I came for was there and I came away with ideas a few things
to do better next year year when it is our turn. Can't ask for more.

I had a great time at IDXC!
