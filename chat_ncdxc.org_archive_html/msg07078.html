The subject line says it all -- lots of double-hop sporadic-E to areas east of the MS river. I picked up two new grids in VE2, heard, but did not work, several stations in New England. Three new grids in the US, two of them double hops. In the early evening, 6M CW sounded like 40M.
These openings have been best on CW and JT65 -- double-hop openings tend to be short, spotlite-like, and often not very strong.
