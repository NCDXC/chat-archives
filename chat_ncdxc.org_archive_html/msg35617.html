I started to browse the document by ZL2IFB.  Great intro and lots of other details.

I learned how to toggle from a RRR to RR73 and what they are intended to mean.

If you send RR73, you are telling your QSO partner you are done and he need not send the 73 back.  if you send RRR, it seems to suggest you are expecting the 73 in reply to fully complete if you are one that likes a 'full QSO'.  Of course, when auto sequence is active, which is usually is, your software will go ahead and try to send a 73 even when you get a RR73 from the DX.

If i got this wrong, it would be nice to hear from someone that knows.
