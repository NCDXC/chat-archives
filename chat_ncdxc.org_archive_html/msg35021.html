We have a Kp of 8 now which hasn't been seen in 20 years... forecast says 9 is possible.  Aurora best viewing times 11PM-2AM Pac time TONIGHT.  (Hey a cheap date night!)

For once, no cloud forecast here (N ID) to match northern lights appearing.  Cell phone cameras MAY be sufficient, long exposures (use night mode).  For real cameras, 20 second, higher ISO on a tripod.  There IS more than the eye can see (it's the way our eyes work in low light).

While it severely tanks most HF bands, pay attention to 6M, there seems to be a correlation of high Kp and stellar 6M openings.  Not always, but often enough to pay attention.  That you can do now...
