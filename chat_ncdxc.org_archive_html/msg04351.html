Hi All,

I just want to provide a quick update on Pooyan.  Pooyan has a new setup at his mother's QTH.  The radio is a hacked Icom IC-M700TY marine radio.  The antenna is a non-resonant dipole 16 meters long, 25 meters high, fed with homebrew ladder line, and tuned with a remote tuner Icom AT-130 at the feed point.  With a SS linear the total power output is 200w.  This morning I walked into the shack and I saw a spot for EP3PK working split, hihi.  I worked him on 20m at 1433z.  Later we tried 17m and 15m however there was no propagation.  It seems 20m is the money band.  He wants to work NA West Coast so I've scheduled a sked with him this Sunday 0300z (our Sat. evening).  Due to the CW contest this weekend we will be in the SSB portion of 20m.  We will start with CW but we could change mode if condition permits.  I will use email for communication with you.  GL and see you tomorrow.
