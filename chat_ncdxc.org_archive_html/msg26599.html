I am saddened to learn that Kurt K7NV passed away in his sleep last night. 

 

Kurt is well known for building many high performance stations on the West Coast, including my own. He also refurbished WWII Prop Pitch motors into very fine antenna rotators. 

 

He was a Conservative and a Patriot and a good, honest man. 

 

I have no other information at this time.
