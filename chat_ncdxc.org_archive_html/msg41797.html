Paul,

You mention looking at their photos so I’m assuming you must be using QRZ. If so, maybe it’s an artifact of folks with vanity call signs. When I got mine it took a while before it went to my page vs. the previous owner and I had to specifically associate the new call with my previous account. And if someone isn’t even using QRZ, maybe they keep the expired page around vs. just trashing it. I suspect it might be something along those lines vs. a large number of pirates.

It might be interesting to spot check a few instances with the FCC database to see if there isn’t a valid new license associated with the suspicious call.
