The KH1/KH7Z team certainly worked hard towards their goal of handing out ATNOs. They kept 20M, the money band, going all the time, and ran FT8 for the weak of antenna/transmit/receive.
They were an ATNO for me, and I finally managed to get in their log 5 times, with contacts on 30M, 20M, and 17M, with 2 CW, 2 FT8, and 1 SSB. Towards the end of the DXpedition, I decided to only try for new bands, and not for new modes on any bands where I had a contact. I netted nothing, although I had high hopes when they were booming in on 40M CW, but then the completely disappeared. I consider myself lucky for the contacts I did make. 

I'm looking forward to hearing first hand about the trip.
