A couple of thoughts on the current FT4GL operation.  No judgements just observations which may be perceived but maybe helps those for an ATNO?.  First I think he's doing a great job.  I know the rules concerning robot operations and since he's moved away from MSHV here's what I observe:

-much less operation now during his nights, lower QSO count, especially during our daytime

-SSB operation seems to occur from his mid-afternoon to early evening

-SSB operation does reach west coast but likely for short openings

-SSB operation has been occurring 1100z-1300z so likely long path

-he usually operates around an hour to hour and a half on SSB

 

Could be that sleep has caught up him with too and the move to other shared quarters limits his operation.  There's also a hour he has to shut down (heard him say that on the air).  GL everyone, looking for more SSB operation myself.  Going to have to keep getting up earlier than I was.
