Orion 2800 w/ DC motor.  Rated as good for 30 square feet of wind load.

 

I just put one on my new crank-up tower.  I control it with a Green Heron RT-21.  $1200 for the rotor, $500 for the RT-21.  When I was calibrating it, a steering diode at one of the end stops opened.  I was driving out into the valley on a family trip so I carried it over to Fresno in the trunk of the station wagon.  I stopped in, and Jason had it fixed in about 20 minutes, whilst I chatted with Mike Staal, the owner.  Doubt you’ll get anywhere near that kind of service with any of the other vendors, especially those manufactured overseas.  Now how long it will hold up in our windy mountaintop environment…that’s an update I’ll provide after this coming winter!
