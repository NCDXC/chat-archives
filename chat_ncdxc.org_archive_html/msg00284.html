I don’t use the published predictions and I haven’t looked at them much so I can’t comment on those.  However I do use Ham Cap with IonoProbe and I find it fairly accurate.  Sometimes I can hear stations that are not “supposed to be hearable” according to Ham Cap but I find it to be a good guideline as to when to listen for a station and the signals usually peak at about the predicted time.
