Greetings NCDXC peeps!
A little catch up.. I have been quite busy the last 2 or so years so have not been active in the NCDXC during that time but now that some of my amateur radio obligations are thinning out(a little bit) again, I am swinging a little more emphasis on my DXing pursuits and NCDXC.. 

the giving back part..
As most of you know, last year I joined K5GS, W1SRD and the PDXG luminaries as part of the TX5S Clipperton team, my second adventure with this amazing group. It is always gratifying to give back to the DX community and this team has my highest level of respect. Id bet my life on it.. (and have!) What a great adventure it was and fun to work many of you and others around the world with such a great team. 

on the hunt...  things have been pretty positive on this front
Its been interesting watching the thread on ZS8. I am super grateful to Yuris, I think he hit it out of the park as far as a single guy dx operation and provided me.. and probably many of you an ATNO. We really need to offer him great respect.. As far as I am concerned, he was easy to work with regard to Northern CA vs ZS8 historically.. When he was first qrv he was +00 long path on 20 meters and had him in the log as part of his first 1000 qsos. one has to point that yagi the right direction ;-). Its really hard to be critical about the operation... my OQRS is in.. now.. about that LOTW upload along with the card.......hmmmm
We as dxers have a lot to be thankful recently.. if someone told me a few years ago that over the course of a couple years I would pick up 

Bouvet
Crozet
Glorioso
Mt Athos
Prince edward and marion

I would have said no way.. your crazy!  but yet.. here we are.
Several of these were limited or single op opportunities.. and resulted in many many ATNO. remember its very easy to be critical of DXpedition teams or individuals, but until you have provided the other side of this coin, I suggest you join me in being thankful to operations like Yuris provided. because quite honestly, its getting too damn expensive and increasingly complicated and restrictive to mount boat based expeditions..(as much as I love them) so we need to be respectful and supporting of these small group or single op expeditions because the way things are going right now, they may be your only path to honor roll! You folks sitting in the three hundred and twenties DXCC know what I'm talking about!
Congrats to Yuris.. and to all who managed an ATNO at ZS8 in addition to the others above! And if you didn't get him.. the hunt continues!
Look forward to more conversation and being more active here and with you all 

73
CHris
