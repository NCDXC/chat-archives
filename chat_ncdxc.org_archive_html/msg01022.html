My vote is for the Telepost LP-100 digital vector wattmeter.
It is an autoranging in line digital wattmeter but in addition to giving you
power and swr readings it can give impedance in magnitude and phase plus 
resistance and reactance.  You not only have a wattmeter but a vector 
network analyzer.  Smithchart is available with the software.
