There is a large Russian naval base in Latakia. I stayed in a hotel right next to it. We heard a lot of full auto machine gun shooting from the base in the evening.  The hotel staff explained that this was a common activity. 

The biggest challenge currently is that the Syrian Army controls all movement in the country, with manned checkpoints stopping and searching each vehicle. There seems to be no communication/coordination between the various checkpoints.  I don't think it is likely that any group (even Russians) could move their team and equipment to a place outside of Damascus. 

Past DXpeditions have operated from the MoC building which is shared with the Syrian Telecom Company. Its located in the Embassy district. Unfortunately there is a big mountain to the North of the MoC building.
