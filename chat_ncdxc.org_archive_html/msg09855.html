N0AX published this link in latest Contest Update.

http://w0is.com/oldcallsigns/oldcalls.html

I checked out W6SX.

1920 through 1930 no W6SX in call books.

1931 GPO callbook:

W6SX, Hamilton M. Jeffers, Lick Observatory, Mt. Hamilton, Calif.

1938 callbook, same.

1972 callbook:

W6SX, General, Hamilton M. Jeffers, PO Box 5305, Carmel, CA 93921

I got W6SX in late 1976.

Lick Observatory is still in business.

Jeffers held W6SX for at least forty-one years. Longer than I have (so far).

Google search for Hamilton M. Jeffers yeilds

https://www.google.com/search?q=hamilton+m+jeffers&ie=utf-8&oe=utf-8

An interesting and accomplished OM. Even has a star named after him.

My question to NCCC and NCDXC, does anyone have any recollection of the original W6SX or perhaps a QSL card?

Hank, (the new) W6SX
