On Aug 22, 2014, at 10:31 AM, Bob Lanning via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:

V or not V….

 

I use DXLabs suite of applications.  Here is how it works.  I have tested this backwards and forwards.

 

When I log a QSO, DXKeeper (the logger application) immediately uploads that QSO to Club Log.  I don’t see it happen but checking Club Log I see a W in the entity row and band column.

 

When I get a QSL card in the mail I manually change the record in DXKeeper to show a Y in the Rcvd field for QSL cards.  I right click and select “Upload to Club Log” and Club Log gets the update.  Checking Club Log I see the W is now a C.

 

When I get an LoTW update via a DXKeeper query I make (button push) the LoTW information is received by DXKeeper which puts a Y in the Rcvd field for LoTW for that QSO.  I then right click and select Upload to Club Log.  Checking Club Log I see the W is now a C just like if a card was received.



Interesting. I use MacloggerDX, and the behavior is slightly different. Like you, if I upload QSOs to Clublog that have nothing in the QSL_RCVD field, they show on Clublog as “W” (worked, not confirmed or verified). If I receive a paper card, I manually change the QSL_RCVD field in Maclogger to a code of: “B {date}” (meaning, received unsolicited via Bureau on {date}), “BR {date}” (received in response to my card via Bureau on {date}. “D {date}” (received unsolicited, direct, on {date}) or “DR {date}” (received in response to my card, direct, on {date}). The codes are my own, and I have some others as well (e.g., online QSL request received, etc.).

If I now upload my log to Clublog, any QSO with any of my codes in the QSL_RCVD field now shows as “C” (confirmed) instead of simply “W” (worked).

Now, if I go to LotW and download a QSL report (essentially, an ADIF file of the QSLs received since the last time I did this), and tell Maclogger to “Confirm QSOs with ADIF file”, Maclogger will add an entry to the appropriate QSOs with “LotW {date}”, meaning QSL received via LotW on {date}. This code is *in addition* to any codes that I may have manually entered.

If I now upload my log to Clublog, any QSOs with a “LotW” code in QSL_RCVD will show as “V” (verified), instead of simply “C” (confirmed). This is totally independent of any credits applied to my DXCC account. It is simply a LotW confirmation. There is not a single QSO in my entire log that has a “V” anywhere in the QSL_RCVD field, yet Clublog thinks that all of my LotW confirmations are “verified."
