On 11/19/2013 10:35 AM, James Sansoterra wrote:
> You might want to consider some paper confirms for WAZ...that one is a real monster when you get to 80 and 40 and only paper is accepted. Figures, right, they can't do a simple complete magazine mailing....

Last I heard, eQSL has always been acceptable for CQ awards. Am I wrong?  Also, if you're going for any CQ awards and have a lot to submit, it would be worth asking if LOTW is acceptable.  I recently submitted for the CQ Field award, and the K0KG, CQ awards manager, agreed that a screen print of an LOTW confirmation that clearly showed the location within a Field was acceptable.  As a result, I was able to submit for 175 zones with a combination of paper cards already in hand, printouts of eQSL and LOTW, and a half dozen new cards that I had to solicit. Saved me a LOT of time and money.
