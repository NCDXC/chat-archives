Early on I ran whatever I could build in the form of wire dipoles. BIG regret, cheap 1 to 1 chokes / baluns.
No manufacturers mentioned, but the "white pvc type" did not survive long. I suspect functionally they were very marginal. 
Later on I came across "Balun Design". What a difference in materials and craftsmanship!

As we all sit around waiting for KP5/NP3VI to fill the bands, 

Here is a question: What antenna do you regret buying? 

For me, it was a Cushcraft R7 vertical.  It was a very poor vertical antenna, very noisy and just very ineffective. It was one of my first HF antennas but was quickly replaced by a G5RV dipole that was much quieter and much better. 

What is your antenna regret?
