I use a use Yamaha 1000 that I bought on eBay a few years ago.  Great for ham radio camping,  yard work,  home power source.  

I ran SEVERAL LED floor lights,  cable box,  tv,  large LG refrigerator for 21 hours last week. No problem. 

Consumer Reports say the Harbor Frieght 2k watt Predator is an excellent choice.  Cheaper than a Honda,  quieter than a Honda,  and locally available.  

Don't go over board on a gen set. More wattage doesn't make a generator better.  1,000 to 2,000 watts is sufficient for most people.
