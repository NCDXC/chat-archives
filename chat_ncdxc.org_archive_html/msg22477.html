For those that missed out last evening, the 30M propagation from Botswana is slowly edging across the US; now including some inland southern states (according to the calls I see, some with actual R-xx reports).
So, hang in there a little longer, prop is coming to the West Coast.  The rate of the prop seems to indicate after 00 UTC (5 PM DST PAC) just as it was yesterday.
They are currently spotted ONLY on FT8 on 17 (a slight hint of a path now), 30, 40M (no hope up here) at the moment.  Our mutual darkness doesn't line up; low bands seem unlikely with an extreme long shot at grayline if they think of it.
I will make a presumption that they've only been seen on on FT8 because of the high noise floor (#ItsAfrica!) from the power company.  The notes from the power company (on FB) hint that their daylight hours will be powerless; we'll have to see. 

17M  (I'm not seeing them or any callers)

30M 10.131 F/H

40M 7.056 F/H

They have not uploaded the logs daily (yet) and they ask for no dupes.
I'm certainly no expert, but that's my take from watching the last couple days.  A couple more sunspots wouldn't hurt. 

Good hunting!
