Save the dates! IDXC 2025


Our IDXC 2025 Planning Team is working hard to make IDXC 2025 the best ever!

We are super excited about this event as it comes together. We want to alert you that the International DX Convention will be held at the Visalia Convention Center on April 11-13th, 2025.

You can visit our new website at http://www.dxconvention.com

In the coming weeks and months, we will be announcing:


1) Participating Hotels & Registration Dates.

2) Convention Registration.

3) Speakers and Programs.

4) Major Prizes.

5) Hourly Prizes.

6) Optional programs such as Contest Academy and DX Academy.
﻿
7) Friday Dinners.

8) Exhibitors displaying their latest products.

This is just a partial list of the features & benefits of attending IDXC 2025.
