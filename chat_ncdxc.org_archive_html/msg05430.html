Andreas, I just read this in the BoD meeting minutes. I think most people reading this would come to the conclusion that Jim did,
The W6TI repeater was again discussed in light of the proposed additional equipment needed and potential reduction of signal strength for existing users. Russ, K6KLY, reported an estimated $923 needed for additional repeater coverage. A number of 
Board members reported on the very low usage of the W6TI repeater. The Board
decided to continue to explore ways to increase repeater coverage but decided to drop the splitter idea because of the high noise floor and issues around the loss of capture. Russ, K6KLY, made a motion to drop consideration of upgrades to the W6TI repeater at this time. Tony, K6BV, seconded the motion and the Board passed the motion with a 
unanimous vote.
