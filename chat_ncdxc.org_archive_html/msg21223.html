A word of caution.  I've bought from them before and that auction company provides very good service and communications.  they usually have some unique things especially in the way of old radios.  They are in the neighborhood of where I grew up too.  But in considering cost, consider the shipping which can be substantial.  They use UPS shipping services which charge a lot for the shipping, handling and packing services.  For that receiver to California you would be talking at least $65 or more.  That is what I found out.
