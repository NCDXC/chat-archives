A couple of years ago, Rita, my XYL, and I decided that we needed to move our home from our Boulder Creek QTH since 1989.  After a lot of looking around, we finally found a new destination and will be moving into a new built home in November sometime.  We will be in Southern Oregon, my first home out of W6-land.  Part of the process of moving is to deal with what the real estate agents say will hurt the sale of the home and ham radio antennas are on the list.  No surprise but going off the air is hard to do!

Today, the 4 element SteppIr was disassembled while on the tower by an expert in such manipulations!  With only a couple of ‘close calls’, the Yagi is on the ground with only a minor issue.  Next week we will take down the Rohn 45 sections, one by one and not by cutting guy wires!

 

I have a new tower on order and a new DB36/80 in the box which will occupy me once I move to the new QTH.  With luck, I will be on before the end of the year to terrorize the bands again!

 

Until then, I need to part with the SteppIr and 60’ of Rohn 45.  If anyone has interest or knows of some that might be, please contact me directly for details or questions.  Al.N6TA@gmail.com

 

I wish you all the best DX until I see you on the air!

 

Vy 73,

Al
