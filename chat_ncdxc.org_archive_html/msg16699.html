A friend of mine works with one of the radio groups there. You can listen to a number of stations through various streaming apps. I listen to Radio Maohi using the SOSRadioMaohi app.

 

Years ago, FO5CX used to work at Radio Tahiti when they broadcast on AM, FM, TV and Shortwave. Frequently Victor and some of the other guys would send me messages during their “coconut wireless” programs when residents could send messages to literally anyone who was listening. This was before the Internet and inexpensive long-distance phone calls, so locals would send messages from one island to another. I had to use that one time to alert some people on one of the small atolls in the Tuamotus’s that I was arriving by plane. I had no way of knowing if they actually received the message until I arrived the next day and found someone waiting for me.
