Some observations:

Sometime early this morning, the LoTW queue actually emptied; there were no logs to be processed. As folks this morning started uploading NAQP logs, the backlog quickly jumped up to an hour or more. That says that they cannot process logs at the submission rate following a contest. It will be interesting to see how far behind they get, and how long it takes after this contest for the queue to be emptied.

NAQP is a relatively small contest, being almost exclusively NA. I will watch to see the "bump" after the ARRL Int'l DX Contest and CQ WW, where there are many more logs submitted.

While the current situation is definitely an improvement, I still believe it is a "band-aid", and not a systemic fix. By comparison, ClubLog and eQSL processed Anna's NAQP log within minutes, while LoTW is already hours behind, even with brand-new hardware.
