Guys: The VHF contest starts at 11:00 AM Saturday and ends at 8:00 PM Sunday. Please get on and work me over the weekend on any of the following frequencies & modes. You can rework a station over again only by changing bands not modes. Please see the frequencies below. 

6 meters        50.125 to 50.175 SSB          50.090 to 50.100  CW
2 meters       144.200   +/-  20khz     SSB,             146.550 FM
1-1/4 meters   222.100   SSB, 223.500   FM
3/4 meter        432.100    SSB, 446.000    FM
33 cm             927.500    FM          PL 100hz
23cm             1294.500   FM

The exchange is 59 ,  or 599 on CW,   and your grid square.  (exm: CM97)

All contacts are simplex.  no repeaters allowed.
This is the biggest VHF contest of the year. If 6 meters is open all the action will be there not on FM. 

See you in the contest.      Russ K6KLY
