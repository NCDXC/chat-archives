On Fri, Feb 28, 2020 at 10:39 AM Tox <scott.small@gmail.com> wrote:
(closing loop for those on reflector)
For dxcc award credit, transmitter and receiver need to be within 500m
of each other. A remote site (within same dxcc entity), where you are
doing both xmit and receive from same site, appears to qualify.
Receiving at StationA, transmitting from StationB does not qualify. So
for my initial question of "can I receive using a webSDR a mile away,
and transmit from home?", the answer is No, because it breaks the 500m
site size limit.

(The argument over whether farming contacts from widely distributed
remote stations is in the *spirit* of the game is a completely
different religious debate)
