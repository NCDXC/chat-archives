While eQSL has added some contacts in my log (missed or incomplete log entries, none resulting for DXCC credit gains), I have stopped using it because it's too much bother for no return of consequence (except payment for 'awards' in the ARRL style).

There are a LOT of bluff contact attempts (to be polite); folks attempting to confirm slots when I wasn't even on the air or that band or mode.  Plus the load of SWL confirmation requests means I have to take even more time; for no return (yes, some places may need SWL to get a license which seems... questionable).

The time and cost:value ratio makes it not worthy to me.

Clublog would be simpler to convert for award issuance but they don't want the burden (and I get that too; all donation, volunteer and time limited).
