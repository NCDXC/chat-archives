I just noticed on orders placed to both HRO and DX Engineering the sales tax was incorrect for my location and I was overcharged in both cases.
It's worth it to check your on-line orders since the new law went into place this past April. Many vendors are getting it wrong.
This CA.gov website shows the correct rates for your address. https://gis.cdtfa.ca.gov/public/maps/TaxRates/
