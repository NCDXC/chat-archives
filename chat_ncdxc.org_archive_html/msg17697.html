Now that I have my UrbanBeam up high enough, I am going to switch from my (much beloved) DX Engineering DV-40P phasing system. I will sell it plus 40M phasing cables (centered on 7150 and where it does fine on the CW and SSB DX sub-bands), and I will throw in 2 verticals that are home brewed but tuned and where they all worked like a charm for the last couple of years.

 

First $280 gets all – you will have to pick up. Please note – you will need ground radials. I had 44 interconnected radials that just went between each vertical.

 

This system was only ever beaten by the N6BT DXU-32 that I had up 55’, but that antenna had a 28’ boom and loaded “shorty 40” elements with a “wingspan” of 45’. It weighed 90 pounds – and was so big I couldn’t safely go higher. The DV-40P is really quite an amazing system for DX-ing from a small lot.
