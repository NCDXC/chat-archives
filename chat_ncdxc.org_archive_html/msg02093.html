I’d like to see the NCDXC, possibly in partnership with another club or association sponsor a teacher/ham and ultimately a school based station, such as what is being done in Southern California with the Dorothy Grant Elementary School Club Station (K6DGE) http://k3lp.com/dges_field_day_2.htm
This group has gotten a teacher licensed and now has a group of young kids on the air with donated equipment and antennas.   How cool is that!
 
I’d like to see us have some joint meetings, perhaps with PAARA or NCCC.  There is a lot of passion and excitement in both of those clubs that could add to our member’s excitement.  
I’d like to see our club develop and sponsor several remote HF stations for our club members to use and enjoy. The technology is rather simple and the cost is not a lot. This would be a nice benefit, particularly for our HOA impacted members.
I’d like to see our club be more involved in supporting DXpeditions in and above the support that we provide to the NCDXF. We have a very sound treasury; we just need to budget accordingly. The ultra-rare DXpeditions like Heard and Amsterdam cannot happen unless we all pitch in.
I think we should work closer with the Pacificon organizers. Last year’s event was fantastic and I think there is potential to add more DX related topics.
 
Paul N6PSE
