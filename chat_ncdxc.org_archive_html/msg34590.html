Hi John, thank you for your nice presentation as part of DX Academy. I received a lot of positive feedback and certainly would welcome your further involvement. If you would like to help with Outreach to JARL, EU DX organizations, I would welcome your help with that. 

We advertise IDXC far and wide. I think the cost of travel these days is a significant factor in why we don't have more DX Visitors. Flights from EU and Japan are Very expensive these days. 

We have always held a few rooms at the Visalia Marriott for DX Visitors if they contact us early enough. 

Visitors should be responsible for finding the best deals on flights and lodging. Hopefully our hotel rate will remain competitive. My single room at the Comfort Suites was $600 for the three night weekend. That is more than I have ever paid. 

We will form our IDXC 2025 committee very soon and begin the process of making IDXC 2025 the best event possible. 

Paul N6PSE
