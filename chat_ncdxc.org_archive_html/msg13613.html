I had a 30 year old MA-550 to sell in 2011.  I got
a bunch of low ball offers like what you are talking
about.  Then I got an offer for $1500.00 which was
considerably less than the cost of a new one with
shipping at the time.  I took that offer.

Since then, the new price has gone way up.  Like double or triple.

If a haul it away free unit comes up, the usual suspects
will be trolling for it.  You can't outbid them if it
is free.  You just have to be first.

Figure out ahead of time how you are going to remove it
and transport it away.  The buyer of mine had 10 strong people
available to pick it up by hand and carry it away,
as soon as I needed it gone.

Some free towers require a crane to remove.

My experience with aluminum towers (like the LPT) is that
they have extremely strong resale value.  You could always
get lucky, but don't expect to get them cheap.  I bought
an aluminum tower a number of years ago and the seller
later expressed seller's remorse that he didn't get more
money for it.  I paid his full asking price.  He wouldn't
come down.

Aluminum towers are much more common on the east coast,
as most of the vendors are there.  They are much more rare on
the west coast.

Good luck with your hunt.

Rick N6RK
