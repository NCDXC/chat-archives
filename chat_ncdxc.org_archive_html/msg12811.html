Thanks Kevin.  So – I guess I misunderstood too.

 

Not knowing the FCC process I interpreted the ARRL article to say the removal of the limiting rule was denied.  What I think I see now is that the “waiver” – a temporary condition was denied.  That being because it is in line for a rule-change process.

 

ARRL’s article said “…Most commenters supported Expert’s waiver request, but a couple of commenters — including FlexRadio — demurred.”  Looking up “demurred” in the online dictionary “to make objection, especially on the grounds of scruples; take exception; object”.

 

I take it the waiver is denied but a rule change proceeding is about to happen.  I wonder in which sun spot cycle that will happen in.
