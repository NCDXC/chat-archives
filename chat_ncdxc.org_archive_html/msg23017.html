This is reported in today's edition of the Daily DX, it will be a good chance
for newer DXers to work Monaco:

3A - Monaco
IW1RBI, Ennio, says his group's next 3A operation is coming up soon,
sometime between the end of May and mid-June.  Travel restrictions are
making it impossible for the team to meet in person.  They expect to
do digital modes this time with one station dedicated to FT8 F/H, as
much as 24 hours a day.  They realize not everyone, including
themselves, are really savvy at FT8 yet.  They plan to put out good
instructions.  A web page is in preparation.  They also plan to upload
the log to LoTW.

https://www.dailydx.com/
