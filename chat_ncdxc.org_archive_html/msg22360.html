Jim.  On the antenna lead entrance my recommendation would be:
1)  Bring all external antenna leads to a weatherproof box near the ground, with lightning protection inside.
2)  ground rod near that box, in the ground to ground the box
3)  Then run from there coax to wherever your station is.
4)  If you can run additional ground rods between this box and your electrical ground, connect those together to reduce ground potential difference.
This is all for safety, not for RF reasons.

73, Paul, W6IBU

I feel the AC power upgrade is now under control, so want to focus on antenna connections. I'm picturing a surface-mounted box on the exterior wall near the peak of the roof.
