NCDXC friends, here is an update on QSLing with 4J3DJ.   I followed his process and promptly received several nice QSL cards
in the mail from Germany. A few days later, I received LoTW confirmation. 

The German QSL manager also handles 4K6FO and I received LoTW for those contacts as well. 

So, if you have worked 4J3DJ or 4K6FO, use the QSL process outlined below. 

Thanks,
Paul N6PSE
