If I could split it into hardware and software divisions; it would be a tie in software. 

Both Ham Radio Deluxe and Team Viewer allow me to remotely operate my station from anywhere on the Internet, be it at home or on the road.  This includes as little/much of it as I choose, rig(s), amp and more. 

And as an added bonus for many hams, they're free!  Just add in Skype (also free) and an older windows box (which many folks have) and you're golden. 

You can run HRD without Team Viewer too if your local computer has Internet, but I've been using the iPhone on 4G with the above and it's been great on the road (Bluetooth to the iPhone; VOX via HRD).
