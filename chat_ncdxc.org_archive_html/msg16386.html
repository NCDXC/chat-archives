The last tube tester produced was the B&K 747B in the late 70's.  It is all
solid state and sells on eBay in the $500-600 price range. (The newer the
tester, the newer the charts) 

Be aware that any tube tester produced in the 70's will need to be recapped
and aligned.  B&K included alignment instructions in their manual. Others
may have also.
