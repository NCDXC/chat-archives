I worked CO2AN on CW during the ARRL DX contest on 2/15/2020.  Letters with green stamps got nowhere and no LoTW uploads.  Since I need Cuba on 160 M, I had to look harder about a path to a confirmation.  I found that he had a prior call and dug into that for a while and finally got a prior manager, EA5KB, to help me out.  Pepe told me that CO2AN was transmitting from another CO station for that contest.  He did have the ability to contact those involved and got CO2AN to upload that log.  Not sure if it was the TX site or CO2AN himself but I got my confirmation.  SO, if anybody needed CO2AN, you might have it now from my effort or you know a path to try.

 

I have now confirmed every 160 entity I have worked.  100% but that is little solace when I am a very long way from 100.  32 for 32.
