The analogy that comes to mind is the Titanic, with the
band playing music to a deserted ship until it "suddenly"
sank.  The Campbell store closed some time ago.  I
was in the Roseville store a few weeks ago and saw only
one other customer there.  I instinctively purchased
a spare copy of the item I needed in case they went out
of business.  I remember them way back to when they
had a tiny store on the East side of Lawrence Expy about
the size of a 7-11.  It's been quite a run, but they have
been a dead man walking for many years.

I wonder if this affects the electronic flea market
venue?
