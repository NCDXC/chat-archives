Just a heads-up: Marek will be flying from Mayotte to Glorioso later today (Thursday, UTC), and plans to quickly put up one antenna to go QRV on FT8 around 1200Z Friday May 24. Not sure yet what band; it depends which antenna is easiest to do. Glorioso time is UTC +3.

He will operate digital mode for a while, and if conditions are good, will go to SSB later that day. Then he will take time to put up the rest of the antennas, and will be active (work and sleep permitting) for about 3 weeks. There will be no CW operations.

Check https://ft4gl.blogspot.com for latest information. Be sure to note the Band Plan for frequencies. All FT8 operation will be Fox/Hound; do not call below 1000 Hz. 

We will try to indicate currently active band/mode on the website, but *I* do not have the ability to edit/update the site.

Marek is very aware that EU signals will be much stronger than NA. If we (the pilots) see a good opening to either the East or West Coasts, Marek is prepared to put EU on standby to work us. (We’ll see how cooperative EU is!) Don’t worry if you don’t get through in the first few days. The initial pileups will be massive, but Marek will be there for quite a while.
