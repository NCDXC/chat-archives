Subject: 2026 (formerly Visalia) now Santa Maria Contesting and DX Convention - Register Now

The 2026 Santa Maria Contesting and DX Convention, sponsored by the Southern California Contest Club and San Diego DX Club, takes place on April 10, 11, and 12, at The Historic Santa Maria Inn. Santa Maria is along the coast of California, between northern and southern California hams. American Airlines has direct airline service to Santa Maria Airport, SMX, from Phoenix. Santa Barbara Airport, SBA, to the south, and San Luis Obispo Airport, to the north, are both served by several airlines. 

Convention information is at https://www.socalcontestclub.org/SantaMaria2026/ .  

The full-event cost is $100, which includes the presentations and forums, the Saturday evening Happy Hour, Saturday evening buffet (tri-tip and chicken) dinner, and Sunday buffet breakfast. If you intend to attend the meals, I suggest that you pay for them asap, as the hotel's banquet room does not accommodate as many people as have attended in recent years.

No-meals registration is $10.

Friday's events include a full day presentation on high-frequency operation for beginners, or those otherwise unfamiliar with HF. There are planned sessions dedicated to related popular activities such as SOTA, POTA, IOTA, and digital. Also there are techical takls on subjects such as antennas.

Saturday's DX, Contest, and ARRL Forums are all intended to be two-way exchanges with the audiences on issues currently of interest in those areas. At the start of each Forum, there will be a presentation on a current speciality topic or several current topics.

If you have anything that you would be willing to present, or other suggestions, please communicate with N6AA.

A few exhibitors are expected.

The Friday night contest dinner is at the Far Western Tavern, about 6 miles to the south of the hotel, and costs $62.

Register and pay at https://www.socalcontestclub.org/SantaMaria2026/register.php

Hotels

The Santa Maria 2026 Convention will be held at The Historic Santa Maria Inn at 801 South Broadway, Santa Maria, CA 93454. To make room reservations, call the hotel directly at 805-928-7777 and mention the Santa Maria 2026 (Southern California Contest Club) to get a special room rate of just $99 USD per night plus taxes for Friday and Saturday nights. This pricing includes all rooms except the Cabanas. Parking is free.

• Cut-off Period: The room block has a 21-day cut-off period. Guests must make their reservations before March 19th, 2026 to ensure they receive the discounted rate.


Rate After Cut-off: After the cut-off date, any unused rooms will be released. Guests can still register, but they will be subject to the best available rate.

We have also secured a block of rooms at the Best Western Plus - Big America Hotel at 1725 N. Broadway, Santa Maria, CA 93454. Room rate is also $99.00 USD/night plus taxes and also with free parking. To make a reservation there, call 805-922-5200 and ask for the "Santa Maria 2026 SC Contest Club" rate.

You can see who has registered at https://www.socalcontestclub.org/SantaMaria2026/attendees.php .

Please forward this message to anyone possibly interested 

.Convention Chairnan Jim Neiger, N6TJ, and I, as well as all the expected attendees, hope to see you in Santa Maria.
