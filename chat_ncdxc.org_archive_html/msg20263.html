I think the Bouvet guys have softened their approach as N0UN seems to no longer be their spokesman.

I really want to see this Bouvet thing happen.  I have donated $50 and will donate more upon activation. 

Heck, with 24,000 regular DXers if everyone donated $10 there would be plenty of money for major DXpeditions like this. 

With this many regular DXers, money for an ultra rare entity should not be the limiting factor. 

Problem is, a few give a lot and many give little or nothing. 

Most don't know this but almost all EU Foundations and Clubs pledge a donation up front but only fund the pledged donation after
the DXpedition is successfully completed and the QSL cards are in the mail. 

The current model of fundraising is broken and puts too much financial risk on the DXpedition organizers. 

Paul N6PSE
