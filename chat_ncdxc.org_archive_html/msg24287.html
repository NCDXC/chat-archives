Fellow NCDXC members,

 

Despite our over-the-top fabulous, but virtual, NCDXC IDXC last Spring, I've been eagerly anticipating a real, live IDXC in April, 2022. While speaking to a old friend, Mark, K6FG, I learned that the IDXC may not happen if more hotel reservations are not received. Apparently, the Wyndham requires a minimum number of room reservations or imposes a financial penalty on convention sponsors. Like many of us, I'm wary of the unpredictability of the Covid situation. This concern seems to be keeping DXers from making plans in advance.

 

I just spoke with the Wyndham, and reservations are fully cancellable and refundable up to 24 hours prior to arrival. I, for one, am looking forward to the camaraderie of the DX convention... even with my SCDXC friends who always nail Africa before I do. I'd like to encourage everyone who has considered going to IDXC 2022 to go ahead and make a hotel reservation. Together, we can ensure that the event actually happens.  Won't it be great to donate to future DX-peditions in person, rather than later on OQRS?

 

2022 IDXC details are available at:  http://www.dxconvention.org/

 

Hotel reservations at the Wyndham are available through:   http://www.dxconvention.org/hotel.html

 

73 de Bruce, K3NQ
