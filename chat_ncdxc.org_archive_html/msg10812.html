I just took a look at it.  I find these issues after a short review.

1)  It has no place to enter a starting point to the destinations he has listed.  Trying to say that conditions on the ENTIRE continent is the same, well, just isn't intelligent.  The east coast is not the west coast, ever.  Europe is easy for them, the Pacific Rim is child play for us.

2)  It currently shows a 5 (highly unlikely) on 15 meters for the UK, yet I'm easily hearing them now, on a dipole.  I suspect this is because I'm not where the page expects me to be since there is no place to enter the starting point.  It's not a place to be generic.

Score: D+, not a useful tool at this point; a work in progress.
