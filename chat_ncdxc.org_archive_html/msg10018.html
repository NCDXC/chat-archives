Friday, April 17.  

Dateline northwest Fresno.  PG&E gas line blows up, creating 200 ft. fireball and sending 12 to the hospital.  Route 99 is closed in both directions.  Kung Fu traffic jam.
Your intrepid (sic) reporter has now been an eyewitness to both the San Bruno fireball and the Fresno fireball.

Contest dinner:  three-point swish by K1DG.

Saturday, April 18.  

ARRL Forum:  to remote or not to remote; that is the question.
DX Forum:  let's go to Palmyra again.  Let's hope there will be no bad guys there this time; otherwise, Vincent Bugliosi might be tempted to write another self-laudatory book.
Contest Forum:  Dread Pirate Ward reminds us that the youth of today require instant gratification 24/7.

Saturday PM.

Slideshows on daring expeditions to destitute Islamic republics.
Famous and controversial DXpeditioner, having narrowly escaped death, gives riveting talk.  K6 RIMsky-Korsakow delivers the ultimate compliment.
Mellish was swellish.

Saturday night.

Italian guy, having just robbed a Gilbert and Sullivan costume shop, pretends he's a Gondolier.
Talk on sailing trip takes almost as long as the actual sailing trip.
N6 Hotel California impersonates Willie Nelson.
The Voice of Ascension returns, gives another Maxwell award to Dos Equis.
Several fourth graders, having scraped together their nickels and dimes, make payback contribution to the Intrepid DX Group; everyone in the audience tears up.
9 year old girl wins prize that is bigger than she is.

Sunday morning.

It hasta be Navassa.
TV Bob wins Emmy for his photos.
As we exeunt the facility, the smell of cow dung permeates the air.
Gentlemen, start your engines.

Thinking of going to Visalia 2016?
Joe Bob says check it out.

de AJ6V
