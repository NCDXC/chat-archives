Alan,

For a cost effective system I would recommend LMR400 or better LMR600
for the run to and up the tower. You will need a flexible coax to make the rotor
loop. For this I suggest RG213. The connection  between the LMR400 and the 
RG213 should be made with a well sealed type N connector pair.

I just replaced my rotor and SteppIR control cabling last week and refurbished
the SteppIR EHU boxes on the antenna. I just got the antenna back in the air
last Friday. My coax run is done the way I described  to you except I was lucky
enough to have the necessary 160 feet of 1/2 inch heliax to and up the tower.
It was in perfect shape after 8 years so I left it alone. The real key to success is
well sealed cox fittings.

Good luck with the rotor replacement. Very smart to replace the coax as well.
Be sure to check your rotor cable run as well.
