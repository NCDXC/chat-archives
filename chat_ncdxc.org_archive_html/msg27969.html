I think 3Y0J is (for certain set ups) actually transmitting out of phase (you can do that with JTDX if you click the wrong button); they have said they mean to enforce the F/H only deal – so what do you do?

CHEAT (or load yourself a copy of JTDX, figure out how to use it, and configure it backwards)

Tell your computer it is 15 seconds from now (after turning off all of your auto correct NTP/GPSDO stuff) and voila! You are odd and he is even and you are both F/H on WSJT/X

I tried it – it appears to work but the pile up was pretty humongous and they were going at a very slow rate (like 40-45/hour) and unable to sustain multistream.  Remember – these guys are high speed CW contest operators – they aren’t FT8 operators.  And most of them have probably never seen JTDX before they got on the island.

(Caveat – it can be a chore to reconfigure your computer back to normal time and you’ll need to do that before you work anyone else)

Good luck!
