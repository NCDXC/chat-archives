The real value in the 2nd receiver for a K3 is diversity reception…that’s why they did it years ago.

The same rule applies for the 2nd antenna…you gotta hear them to work them.  I sometimes use a Pixel loop, and sometimes a vertical.

Diversity reception can be mind blowing.  You have to experience it to appreciate it.

BTW, this is one area the FLEX 6700 excels at.
