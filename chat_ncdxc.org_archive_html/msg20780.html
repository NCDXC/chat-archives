Quick update to close out this thread.

I received the SDA100 controller box from John W1JCW and after a little bit of wiring confusion on my part, and through John's tremendous patience I was able to get the steppir 2 ele working again with no issue.

Need to be more careful with the cabling and powering of the controller going forward.  I rerouted the cables to clean up the shack and I am good to go.

In terms of my future antenna farm I plan to put up a 72' tower retractable tiltover tower next spring and will put up a Mosley S33 and my seven element 6m LFA2 on that tower and keep this two element Steppir on the rohn 25 at 47'.  That should wrap things up for me in regards to horizontally polarized antennas.  Down the road would consider moving away from Steppir antennas for non electro mechanical antennas but will keep well enough alone as long as things keep working.
