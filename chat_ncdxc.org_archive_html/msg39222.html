Paul,

I have an ADSB receiving setup running feeding both Flightaware and Flightradar 24. It is interesting to hear a plane or helicopter overhead and be able to see who they are/where they are going. With both services you get free premium accounts since you are supplying data to them. I never got around to geting setup on ADSB Exchange.

I use the Flightaware Pro Stick Plus receiver dongle and their 5.5db antenna with a RaspberryPi.

https://flightaware.store/products/pro-stick-plus
https://flightaware.store/products/antenna-1090mhz

I'm happy to answer any questions you have.
