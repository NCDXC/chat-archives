Jim and everyone using DXKeeper,

 

I learned of the application to download from LoTW to DXK changing those qualified from Y to V.  Iain N6ML (who has a wealth of information and readily shares it) told me about it back in 2011.  It works but obviously can be.  It pretends to be a browser and looks for matches to the criteria.

As you can imagine it is fragile.  Any change to the webpage will cause the application to fail.  So far - so good.

 

I have used it 2-3 times.  But I only use it if things look a bit out of control otherwise I perform the task of getting Club Log from C to V as I described.  Of course it makes my logbook accurate too.

 

Here is a clip from Iain's email with the link:

 

"Unfortunately DXKeeper currently has no way to extract the results of a DXCC submission back out of LotW to update your log (changing the status to 'V'), but VE3NEA developed a hack to do this, called DXCC Credit Importer, which you can find here:

 

http://www.dxatlas.com/Misc/DxCred.zip "

 

There is hope Dave AA6YQ can embed such a function in DXLabs. 

 

73,

Bob - W6OPO
