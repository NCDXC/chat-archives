Hello NCDXC fellas,
 
This is a reminder that we are having a sked with EP3PK tonight at 7:30 PM (0230 UTC)
 
We will advise via chat and the club repeater of the frequency and mode as Pooyan is ready to go.
 
This will be a shared sked with the Mile High DX Association in the Denver area.
 
 
Remember the rules:
 
1) Only call EP3PK if you can hear him.
 
2) This sked is for those seeking an all time new country. No band fills please!
 
3) If on CW, there is no need to send his call.
 
4) Please do not post him on any DX Clusters. He is doing this sked to give us West Coast guys a chance before he relocates to Australia very soon.
 
Good Luck!
