Hi Christian,

Thank you for being part of the DXpedition to Christmas Island!

For the 3 Northern California clubs that I belong to, the many good
conversations and spots are the norm, but rather unfriendly behavior
occasionally occurs.  There is something about using a keyboard that
sometimes brings out the worst in some people.  I wish people would
save their messages for awhile, then review, before launching.  Many
club members have unsubscribed from Chat after some of these posts.

The unfriendly behavior that you saw on the Chat reflector is not
representative of the behavior of the club.  I have always enjoyed the
club meetings and found the members to be very friendly at them.  If
you are in the bay area during one of our meetings, I hope you will
pay us a visit!

73 de Phil, W6PK
