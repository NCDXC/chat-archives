Since the WebEx session didn't work this afternoon (me)/evening (you), I'll give everyone on chat the update.

1. Check WWW.TX3X.COM for the current position of the boat (the Evohe)
2. The boat left Auckland, headed to Noumea, about 1 PM today (Sept 18 ZL time)
3. All radios, antennas, etc., shipped from California arrived without any delay in good condition.
4. All radios were loaded on the boat last Saturday
5. All antennas and other equipment shipped here was stowed on board Sunday.
6. K5GS, HA5AO and myself spent Monday and Tuesday purchases supplies (tables, chairs, lots of drinking water, etc.), then hauling it to the dock where the crew loaded everything on the boat.
7. The crew purchased food supplies on Wednesday
8. We had a dinner for the crew at the Lone Star Cafe, on Tuesday evening. TX3X t-shirts were given to each crew member
9. Pista, HA5AO, is on board the Evohe sailing to Noumea
10. All of the team is communicating last minute details on our team reflector
11. K5GS and K6GFJ are in Auckland for the next week, then we fly to Noumea on Sept 25th.
12. All 12 team members will arrive in Noumea on the 25th or 26th.
13. Last minute supplies (ie: snack food) will be purchased in Noumea
14. The TX3X team will sail for Chesterfield in the 27th or 28th.
15. We plan to operate on all bands, 6 through 160 meters
16. We will have stations on 15 and 20, or 20 and 30 meters around the clock on both modes.
17. Other stations will be on the other open bands.
18. According to ClubLog, there is a high demand for SSB, so look for lots of SSB operation plus CW and RTTY
19. We have a doctor on the team and a nurse on board
20. Radios are Elecraft K3's, KPA500's, and P3's
21. Antennas are N6BT vertical yagi's, 2 element Steppir yagi's, hex beams, vertical antennas for 40 and 80, and a Battle Creak Special for 160.
22. QSL information is available at WWW.TX3X.COM
23. We may be on the air /MM, but NOT using TX3X.
24. K5GS is sending update information to the DX bulletins

I'll send more updates when available from K5GS.

73,
Ross
