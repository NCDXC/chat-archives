I thought I would throw in my 2 cents worth.  For the past several years I have been using a Force 12 XR5.
It has no traps and other than once when a lead shorted out it has had zero problems.
The main culprit has been the wiring to the antenna and the rotators.  The animals
have done a job on the wiring and I have had to replace the Ham IV rotator a couple of times.

I enjoy the Force 12 as it has been pretty reliable.  If I was younger and had more
antenna space, I would probably go with one of the Steppir antennas.  The Hams 
near me in the South Bay area hear the DX about 15-20 minutes before I do and
sometimes I don't hear the DX at all.  

I have been a Ham for 73 years.  In the early days I tried two or three different beams
beams but don't recall the brands. Most had traps.

73 Alan K6IPM
