I seldom write but here is my take.  I joined the NCDXC club primarily for two reasons.  One was for the expertise on so many issues that most of you have.  The other was to learn much more about DXing.  I have been a Dxer for over 60 years but there is always much more to gain by listening to others and to see how they have achieved their goals and objectives.  I am amazed and thrilled when you report an ATNO or some rare and exotic place

The part I dislike the most is what appears to be the negative comments and shots taken by the membership to other members of the club.  I understand that we all have views on the vast assortment of subjects that come before us from time to time.  Not usually will a decision rendered be acceptable to everyone as we all have a point of view one way or the other.  That is part of life.

Having said this, I would venture to say that we should respect one another and try to avoid any type of character assassination.  This is probably difficult as we tend to be very passionate about our hobby.

Now you all have a chance to voice your opinion on my points of view.  Thank you for allowing me to be a part of this great organization.

73 de Alan, K6IPM
