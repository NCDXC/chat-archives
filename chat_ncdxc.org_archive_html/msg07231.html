My philosophy has been, if you’re going to do it, do it.  In other words going to spend money on an amplifier go to the limit.  Otherwise my experience has been – “…I wonder if I had 1500 watt…?”

 

Regarding tubes I’m confused.  The 4CX800A/GU74B were cheap and plentiful.  Then a rumor start that Svetlana stopped making the tube and prices started going up.  They were still available but for how long?  Was this the truth?  I don’t know but it bothered me.  Then Alpha offered a trade in program.

 

After many years on the 91b (two 4CX800As) I traded it in for the 8410 (two 4CX1000s) with lots of head room.  I already have and use a Collins 30S-1 on the S-Line so I had good experience with 4CX1000As (it uses one).  Then ACOM comes out with the 2000 using two 4CX800s!  So when considering an amplifier and its tube compliment – what is the truth?

 

73,

Thinking about these amps. Hard decision – years ago I tried running full power, and had one neighbor complain. He’s not easy to deal with at all (would believe I’m interfering with him even when I’m off the air). Very hyper – runs around a lot waving his hands – yelling at his gardener. Uses a squeegee on his flat roof when it rains. I do believe I turned the power back to 1000 watts and then everything was OK.

 

Since going to 500 watts – not a peep – or a problem with anyone, and it’s been that way for 5 years. Amazing what a PITA it is when you get in that situation. I’ve really enjoyed the quiet all these years.

 

So maybe I could get away with 1000 watts, and so – maybe going from 500 watts to 1000 is a good compromise and worth the bother (?). This is only to help bust through the big rare ATNO pileups a couple times a year. I use 110W from the K3 for everything else.

 

I ran several propagation charts at K6TU.NET, and it does seem like the next 500 watts up (500 to 1000) is more of a step up than 1000 to 1500W. I was looking at FT/T and VK0/H – the next two that I need that are announced. I could easily convince the XYL to go for the ACOM 1000 – (even new), the ACOM 1500 would require some serious “selling”. I’ve heard rumors that some of these tubes are very difficult and expensive though – which makes me think just stick with the KPA-500/KAT-500.

 

Tough decision. I have increased my gain antenna wise significantly this year on several bands – and have mono banders on 160, 40, 20 and 15M (significant gain increase on 40 and 20M) – so always love to push it to the limit.

 

Thoughts?
