All,

 

I want to thank the Northern California DX Club Awards Committee and membership for the most unexpected honor of being named the NCDXC 2013 DXer Of The Year at the IDXC Saturday evening banquet.   To say I was surprised would be a massive understatement!

 

I am very pleased and honored to receive this prestigious award, and I will always endeavor to maintain the high DXing standards that it represents.

 

Thank you all very much.
