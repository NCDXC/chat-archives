2015 Holiday Luncheon – 1st announcement. 

 

The club’s Holiday Luncheon will be held at Michaels at Shoreline.  The same place we had such good fun at last year.

 

When:  December 12, 2015

 

Time:  11:00AM to 3:00PM

 

Where:

Michaels at Shoreline

2960 North Shoreline Blvd.

Mountain View, CA 94043

 

Who:  The event is for current NCDXC club members and one guest.

 

In later announcements the menu selections will be available for you to choose from.  As with last year the club’s website will guide you through your meal selection and how to pay.

 

Come and enjoy the company of NCDXC members and guests and the Holiday Season. 

 

Please mark your calendars reserving Saturday December 12th 11AM-3PM.

 

73,

NCDXC Board
