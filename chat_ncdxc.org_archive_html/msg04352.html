On Fri, Nov 22, 2013 at 12:04 AM, Paul@n6pse.com <paul@n6pse.com> wrote:
Dear NCDXC friends,

By now, I've received several reports of what transpired at the club meeting. In the intervening hours there has been some fallout to my call for action. I'm told that the board said this is all happening due to flaws in the by-laws. Well that is hogwash!  The board could have installed any board member as President. The by-laws don't list any order of succession. Is this about putting the best person in the role, or blocking a passionate and dynamic leader who wants to make effective changes that just scares the board to death.

After all, Dxpeditioners are not passive people. We make things happen.

Since I have brought this to your attention:

1) I've received email from the board telling me what a bad person I am and how I've damaged and embarrassed the club for my own personal gain.

2) I've received email from your new Club President threatening to send his lawyer after me. Has he ever heard of Freedom of Speech or the concept of a contested election?

3) As I'm a member of the board, I'm seeing email messages about possibly pushing out a Special Election until May.  What is their motivation to deny you a choice in your club leaders?

4) The board is discussing not doing a minute of club business at the December meeting, acknowledging that will delay any action that is not already taken. Why is the board refusing a Special Election?

Is this about putting the best person in the role?  Giving the members a choice? Or is this about
Personal petty payback?

Some on the board seem to still carry a grudge after my comments about our club's choice of DXpedition of the Year.

Since July, they have delayed talking about process improvements that I have been proposing. Several board members want to cancel the Dxpedition of the Year award entirely. Why are they trying to deny you another choice?   Let me go on record, I have no desire to receive this award and will refuse it if given to me. So much for personal gain.

I want to be clear, there are some very fine men on the board, but as a whole, there is a lack of leadership, a lack of interest in making changes and some dysfunction.

Real leadership is needed. You deserve choices.

What are they afraid of?  Why are they denying you a choice in you club leader?
