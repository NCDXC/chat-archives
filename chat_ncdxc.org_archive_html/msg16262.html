On Wed, Dec 5, 2018 at 9:02 AM Steve Lund via Chat <chat@ncdxc.org> wrote:
You are seeing two problems.

1) version 2.0-rc5 can't decode version 1.9.1. As greater than 75% of the users are using 1.9.1 or earlier, that is a lot of signals that won't decode.

2) version 2.0-rc5 has a very serious bug. It can't decode signals with negative DT values. In theory this would be 50% of the signals, but a positive bias makes it closer to 35%.

I keep both -rc5 and 1.9.1 on my computer and use both. Except for the negative DT issue, -rc5 is superior.

Steve, K6UM
