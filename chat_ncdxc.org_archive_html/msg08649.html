For a classic yagi, taking the hit on the side of the boom presents the least wind load (surface area).  It's not just the antenna taking the hit, but the entire structure.  It's just worse at the top. 

Winds from the south, aim east or west.  Since the wind direction changes at ground level, it won't be perfect.   Maybe next pass on the tower add a streamer to watch air movement up high?

Lowering the tower if possible is also a great idea.  On the other hand, you'll test your anchors.  :-/
