I am assisting Ken K6HNZ and his son, Jeff Ruddock to dispose of Ken's radio equipment and antenna/towers. Ken is now 93 years young and is hanging up his mic and key.

Ken has a US Tower Model MA850MDP that was purchased in 2015 and installed on his property south of Pescadero right on the coast. (Not at the QRZ address)

The tower has some corrosion on the pulleys and the motor is in bad shape but we believe there is a spare set of pulleys, motor etc and the mast can be brought into service.

The tower is available free to a good home. You will have to dismantle and arrange for transport.

There are also a number of home made HF antennas covering various bands.

The antennas are also available to interested parties.

Pictures and manuals are available for anyone who is interested. If so, let me know, I will provide the link to the information and put you in contact with Jeff!
