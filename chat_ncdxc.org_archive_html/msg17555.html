Rick,

No, but I believe FT8 BW is 50Hz, and a typical CW filter is 500Hz, so there's your first 10dB.  Then there's the modulation coding gain, slowly integrated over a long time period, along with some kind of error correction.  Not an insignificant advantage!  But the big disadvantage for me is the impersonal experience of staring at a screen rather than talking directly to someone in another language (Morse).

-Steve
