Is it just me, or are the W6 folks having a ton of problems hearing H40WA on 17 and 20M? Reception on 10 and 12M has been decent enough, but I have had a very hard time copying the DXpedition on either FT8 or SSB modes for 17 and 20M bands. My 12M and 20M FT8 QSOs don't show up on CL either. 

10M digital - Fairly easy to copy
12M digital and SSB - Easy to copy. SSB QSO on CL, but FT8 QSO from last night still doesn't show up on Clublog
17M SSB - No copy here. 
17 digital - No copy earlier, but finally faint copy a few hours ago and the QSO showed up on Clublog.
20M SSB - No copy at all
20M digital - Very faint copy after many hours, got an RR73, but clublog doesn't show the QSO after 2 hours. 


I don't know if this is just my bad luck or if others are having problems too, chasing them down.
