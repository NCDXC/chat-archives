For those wondering why the Bouvet folks aren't livestreaming, this website does a great job at showing where all the Starlink satellites are at any given moment: https://starlink.sx/. Not surprisingly, Bouvet doesn't show up on the map, but South Georgia, which is at almost the same latitude, does. As you can see, the Starlink coverage for South Georgia, and hence also for Bouvet, is nearly nonexistent. We know occasional connections are possible, since the logs and some videos have been uploaded, but a consistent connection for livestreaming would be problematic at best. I would imagine that if they were livestreaming, and there were a lot of drop-outs leading to many QSOs not being upload live, that the number of folks trying to work them repeatedly might increase, not decrease.
