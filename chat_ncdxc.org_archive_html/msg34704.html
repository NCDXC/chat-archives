I've noted in myself that Clint Eastwood "GET OFF MY LAWN"* type of attitude is better understood, (even endorsed) more each day hi hi.  I'm slightly less tolerant of aberrant behavior in many areas, annually.  I refuse to accept that it's aging, but evidence supports that.

If we do not call out poor behavior, no matter the duration of the op being licensed; we condone it.  It is not a place for passivity or silence.

We have an abundance of new hams (including Extra class) that only know how to pass the test, but are otherwise completely clueless (as shown on the air, EVERY hour).  We have longer duration hams dabbling in new tech or modes, without understanding or asking for help.  (And older hams that aging confusion, has not been gentle.)

It SHOULD be stated, not to create a P&M (gripe) list, but as behavior correction.  Otherwise, they have no opportunity to learn.  ALL species correct each other and their young within the pack/herd.  We need to do the same.  Mentor, not attack; increase cooperation.

At first, privately to save face.  If that fails (and more so when they are a member); here on the list.  And a relative few, it appears, prefer to be uh, bungholes.  That alas, is a unique but common feature to humanity alone.  😉

Back on topic, calling a DX station while having a confirmed log entry (except Marahon points, this year) needs to be called out as rude for any correction to be possible.  Or it is condoned and allowed to spread.
