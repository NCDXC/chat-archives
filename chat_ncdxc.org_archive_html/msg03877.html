Here is a web site that is just what I need (besides the ON4UN Lowband Book):

 

http://www.w0btu.com/Beverage_antennas.html#Beverage_Antenna_Transformers

 

Lucky I have all the 9:1 binocular wound XFMR’s and all of the resistors I need. I even have the ground rods and wire and other “doo dads”.

 

And sure enough – when I unplug the cable modem wall wart this morning, the noise drops to what would be “normal” 160M noise – and it makes the difference between hearing DX and not.

 

Oddly enough, the S meter doesn’t change much – but wow – I can sure see the difference in the Elecraft P3 Panadapter, and of course, turning off the preamp and playing with the K3’s NR settings completes the “magic”.
