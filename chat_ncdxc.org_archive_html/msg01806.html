Rich,

if you are going for two wire antennas/Inverted V's  with the the apex up on your tower I would suggest you check out the ZS6BKW version of the G5RV.

http://www.nonstopsystems.com/radio/ZS6BKW.pdf

It works without tuner on 40, 20, 17, 12, 10. It also does 80M, but it's 6:1 at the bottom end. No problem - even for the built in tuner on my PW-1.

However, it's not very good on 15M.

My length came out to 92 ft total (41 ft each leg) and the 450 Ohm ladder line is 41 feet. My apex is at 55ft.

I have the NEC model for it in an Inverted V config and it has really nice lobes on the higher bands. I use it on 80, 17, 12 with excellent results. Let me know if you want me to email it to you.

73,

Andreas, N6NU
