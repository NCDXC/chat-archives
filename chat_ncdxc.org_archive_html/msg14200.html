Hi Lee,

I am exceptionally happy with the Gap Titan DX vertical.  It’s essentially a
bunch of vertical dipoles for all of the bands 10-80.  No radials, no coils, no traps, and no transformers.  It is well built and has survived very high winds during big storms this spring and summer.  Granted, I am in a great location up in the hills, but i have had very little trouble breaking through pileups with it.  I currently have it mounted on a 4-ft pipe on the ground with rope guys, and I plan to put it up on a 30-ft mast to get it above my roof, but i’ve been getting such great results i’m not certain it’ll be worth the effort (except to free up the yard for other uses)!

The only drawback I’ve seen is that I had to do a good bit of searching for tuning instructions, and help from the factory was slow.  Once i figure that out, it was straightforward to tune it up.  I would be happy to share what i learned if you decide on this antenna.    

Greg, N6GD
