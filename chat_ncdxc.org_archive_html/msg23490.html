I've been watching XT2AW work the world (literally) on 17M (logged him before the crush began); he has done a good job of rotating zones, trying to be fair.  It has been, well tedious, slow, limited productivity.
But it makes me curious about when it's reasonable to expect a station to move out of the common area (FT8).
I saw MANY callers (and I'm only watching in one direction); he clearly sees a LOT more.
At which point should a station creating a huge pileup, move out of the common area (18.100) and into another portion of the band (18.095), perhaps switching to F/H mode?
The argument being that it's more fair for other stations to move the pileup away; that running even one stream allows 2 callers at once with no signal degradation; higher Q rate.
I'm not about to tell anyone how to manage their station, but what are your thoughts?  (Yes, FT8 is not the preferred mode here, it's where the DX migrates lately and DOES show instant prop conditions.)
