Another great night on 160M, almost over now with the sunrise shortly.

EU was open much of the early evening, AS early morning.
New entities heard here; Kazakhstan, Liechtenstein, Switzerland and Estonia (I didn't have enough signal to make the contact) and I added Hungary (two stations, both CW) and China (FT8) so now my Top Band count is 119 worked, 95 confirmed.
It looks like another banner season for the low bands; something to do until the sunspot reappear.  (I added Ukraine on 80 CW also.)
