For those using a Macintosh, backups happen automatically (in fact, hourly) with Time Machine. (I just checked, and my last backup was at 8:07 am today, less than an hour from when I am writing this.) Time Machine keeps hourly backups, and then archives EVERYTHING, although older backups become daily, weekly, and then monthly to save space. The user doesn’t have to do ANYTHING.

For extra safety, I clone my Time Machine drive to an external HD every six months or so, and store it in the locker in my tennis club. (Offsite backup, without needing lots of Internet bandwidth.) Of course, I can’t get into my tennis club right now (!), but they are re-opening this week, just for tennis, with heavy restrictions.
