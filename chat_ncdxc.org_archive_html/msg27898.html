On Thu, Feb 9, 2023 at 2:41 PM Christopher Hoover <ch@murgatroid.com> wrote:
isn't FT8 the same as SSB?  


ITU emission designators: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Types_of_radio_emissions

 "SSB" as in phone is J3E emission type.  2K80J3E if you include bandwidth.

FT9 is 8-tone FSK.  It is treated as 2K80J2D although perhaps that's arguable.
