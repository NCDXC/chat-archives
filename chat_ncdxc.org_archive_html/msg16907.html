The 3Y0I team is reporting that they are only 160 miles from Bouvet. That means that they will probably land on Thursday and set up on Friday and Saturday. 

Our friend Stu-K6TU has provided a free Propagation Prediction widget that you can use to get a prediction of the times and bands that might be best to work Bouvet. 

You can do that here: https://www.k6tu.net/?q=node/add/dx-prediction-3y0i

My Prediction indicates that 20/30/40 meters will be best with some chances also on 17 and 15 meters. The strongest opening appears to be on 30 meters between 0230 and 0900 UTC. 

Good luck to everyone who needs Bouvet!
