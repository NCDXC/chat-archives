Good morning, Bob
It appears that ET3AA is running up to 6 streams on MSHV, which dramatically reduces the power in each stream. I worked him last night around 8 PST on 20 while he was single streaming on 14074. With 2el at 30’ (for the storms) I got a -10 and gave him a -8 running 1500w out with my KPA1500. I was worried he was a slim, but got happy tidings from ClubLog this morning.
I’ve had the amp since winter 2019 and have used up to 1500w out on my Telepost with FT8 for many prolonged pileups. No issues with the final devices to date.
The efficiency of the devices improves as the power rises. I keep the efficiency screen up and have the fans set quite conservatively. I have noticed a more rapid increase in heat sink temp at lower powers, where the efficiency drops from 50-55% to as low as 30%. It’s important to keep it tuned, watch the temp, and don’t inadvertently run more than 1.5 Kw under any circumstances. Be sure your wattmeter is calibrated and GL!
