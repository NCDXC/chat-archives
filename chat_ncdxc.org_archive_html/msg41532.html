Everyone seems to favor those operations that met their personal needs.  I believe the operations that meet the needs of the most DXers worldwide need to be considered.  It is not just about ATNOs because many are looking for Challenge points.
9L8MD was a very well run operation that gave out many contacts on many modes and was available to just about anyone that bothered to try and a lot did.  I think they did a great job.
I personally benefited from the SV/A that came out of nowhere (thanks again, Paul, for the text!) as it gave me my last ATNO.  But, they did not announce their operation for some reasons, likely good.  The Somalia team also did not announce their operation and I would like to know the reasons for these surprise operations.  I missed Somalia because I did not know about it so I have some sour grapes!
In the end, Yuris going to ZS8 was the culmination of years of effort and he made a lot of DXers happy.  I'd like to have seen more CW but he had a lot of constraints and was out to make as many as possible happy.  Due to his dedication and personal sacrifice to activate this one, he gets my vote.
Al  N6TA
