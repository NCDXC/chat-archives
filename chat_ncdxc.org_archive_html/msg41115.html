I can't argue with moving to Linux. Good plan.
But to clarify the facts: MS does NOT have your Bitlocker key unless you let them have it. It's trivial to set up Bitlocker and not share the key.
Nor do they have access to your data, again, unless you share it through back up or OneDrive.
If you do not use your MS account to log into Windows 11, they actually have little access to anything.
So, use local accounts, uninstall OneDrive, never login with your MS account, don't use the MS store, etc. There are a lot of guides on the internet to keep MS out of your business. Adjust depending on your personal risk tolerance balanced against the hassle.
And NEVER EVER use Copilot.
Who knows what will happen with Windows 12, but the trend is clear. Everyone wants your data to feed the AI beast and monetize you in some way.
I have no love for MS and plan to move to Linux or Proxmox as the core operating system running on the hardware. I'll still run Windows in an isolated VM to keep all my radio software intact.
