I’m sure I’ve said it before on this forum, but I absolutely LOVE my Optibeam OB11-5. It’s been up for 12 years, has NEVER needed adjustment or repair (I haven’t dropped the tower in all that time). It assembles perfectly, and it’s a real piece of “fine German engineering.”

I might go for one of the larger ones, if you can swing it (literally), like the OB16-5.

Not cheap, but you won’t regret it.
