If anyone is looking to participate in Field Day, all are welcome to join the San Lorenzo Valley ARC for a traditional, big-group, outdoor Field Day extravaganza. This year, we are also hosting members of the Saratoga ARA. Operation is from the Calfire training site on 13575 Empire Grade, Santa Cruz. This is a superb location, in a large flat field at 3000' with commercial power available (for refrigerated food, lighting, etc.), working toilets, lots of room for campers or tents, etc. 

We will be operating 3A, with HF stations on 80-10m, CW/SSB/Digital. In addition, there is a VHF/UHF station, a very well-equipped satellite station (we make 50-75 satellite QSOs each year), and a Get-On-The-Air station with full-time coaches for non-hams and HF newbies.

All operation is from RVs/trailers (no tents in the summer heat), and each HF station will be equipped with Yagi antennas on the high bands, and a number of 50-75' tower trailers, so making QSOs will not be a problem.

Set-up starts Friday at 9 am; all are welcome to come up and help with antennas and station setup. There will be a pizza dinner around 6-7 pm on Friday.
We will be on-air for the full 24 hours of 11 am Saturday through 11 am Sunday. There will be casual lunch available on Saturday, a big BBQ dinner on Saturday evening, and coffee/breakfast on Sunday (all at no charge).

Come on up, operate your favorite mode, bring the kids, bring non-ham friends, and enjoy the kind of Field Day you remember when you were a teenager (only without dupe sheets). Overnight camping is welcome, both on Friday and Saturday night.

Rich KE1B
President, San Lorenzo Valley ARC
