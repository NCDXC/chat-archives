Hello fellow members,

Here is how to get to the restaurant where our March 16 meeting will be held.

Access to the Wagon Wheel BBQ restaurant is relatively easy from either 
Northbound or Southbound 101. You get off at Moffett Bvld. heading away 
from Moffett Field, and if Northbound cross over Highway 101. There is a 
light at where both freeway on/off ramps intersect with Moffett Bvld. If coming 
from the South you will go through both lights. If coming from the North just one light. 
Continue a short distance to the next light. This light is for Leong Drive on the
left and a large Google facility on the right. Turn left into this exit and 
immediately turn left onto Leong Drive at the Tee. The Wagon Wheel BBQ 
is on your at right at 861 Leong Dr, Mountain View, CA kissed almost up against 
the freeway. Pull in and park. If you drive too far on Moffett Bvld. you will cross under 
Highway 85. If this happens you need to make a U turn on Moffett Bvld and return.
Moffett Bvld may also be accessed from Central Expressway or Middlefield.
I suggest checking out the attached map or Googling the address and looking at 
the details of the Leong Drive exit from Moffett Bvld.

C U at tghe March 16 meeting,
