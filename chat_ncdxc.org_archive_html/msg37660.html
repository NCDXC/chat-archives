Hey Al,

Anecdotally, I can tell you that many of the call signs in a DXpedition log don't read:
- DX Bulletins (paid or free)
- Press Releases
- Facebook
- X
- or even the DXpedition's website

I came to this conclusion after 10 years of doing DXpeditions and managing the incoming emails.  Many of the callers are casual DXers, they don't follow the DX news to the same extent as the more active DXers, i.e. the Eat-Sleep-DX types.    While this may not explain why Honor Roll people are calling, there is another angle:

I wasn't calling because I'm working on a house project, otherwise I would have been part of the chastised group.  I do remember reading that a group made that request, but don't remember which one (I read a lot of bulletins and reflectors). Maybe some of those Honor Roll callers were looking for a Marathon point and didn't remember the request?    Possibly some knew of the request and WFWL.

Cheers,
