Dear NCDXC friends;
 
 
You may recall, back in October, We collected some money to help my son's friend Julian Di Mari get his first rig. We collected $148 plus, Don Ferguson donated an ICOM HT that he won at Pacificon.
 
All of the items (see attached photo) were sent to Julian as a donation.
 
This morning, Julian took and passed his Technician's License at a VE session in Granite Bay. Julian is now among us in the amateur radio fraternity. Julian lives in Auburn and plans to continue his studies and take the General Class exam later this summer. He will have his new Technicians license in approximately two weeks. Many thanks to those who donated towards Julian's first rig including:
 
 
Kip Edwards, Jake Whiting, Dennis Lyden, Aaron McClure, Bob Harmon, Mike Flowers, Dick Letrich, John Eisenberg, Harry Makler, Don Ferguson, James Sansoterra and myself.
 
TU and 73,
 
Paul N6PSE
