Rick,

I do have the NN4ZZ tilt plate and Karlock for my DB18E / Wilson MT-61 and I could not be happier. I would never put up an antenna on a foldover tower without one again - ever.

Without the Karlock a heavy wind would be able make the tilt plate swing up and lift the antenna. 

Having the antenna horizontal and parallel to the ground when the tower is lowered and folded over makes it easy to reach everything. 

So, this is a double thumbs up. 

I can probably send you some pictures if you are interested.

73, Andreas, N6NU
