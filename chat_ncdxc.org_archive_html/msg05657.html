On 2/18/2014 9:58 AM, Paul Ewing wrote:

Here are their Stats by Continent:
Thankfully, I already had Reunion confirmed on 20 and 40 CW from previous operations. TO7CC gave me CW and SSB on 17 and CW on 80, already confirmed on LOTW. Considering the relatively lackluster treatment by this operation of hams outside EU, I think I did as well as could be expected.
Some interesting observations. Strangely, the leaderboard shows NO CW operation on 20 or 40, which should have been money bands for NA. Their webpage said that they planned a low band focus, yet they had only 550 Qs on 160M, 486 of which were to EU. Only 37 to NA, 11 to AS. 80M was slightly less lopsided -- 1057 to EU, 181 to NA, 114 to AS. So this expedition with an alleged low band focus made only 4% of their contacts on 80 and 160, and 79% of those to EU. Indeed, their highest QSO totals were on 15, 12, and 10.
