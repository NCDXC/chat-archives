*US Coast Guard Auxiliary*

*National Response Directorate *

*Telecommunications Division*


  Calling All amateur radio operators !

  Announcing an amateur radio special event commemorating


          The U.S.  Coast Guard Auxiliary’s  76^th anniversary


  U.S. Coast Guard Auxiliary members, who are amateur radio operators,
  will be operating as “special event radio stations” on Saturday
  October 17, 2015.


  Over 50 stations from Coast  to Coast will operate on _amateur H.F.
  frequencies_ to commemorate the 76^th ^anniversary of the U.S. Coast Guard Auxiliary.


   USCG Auxiliary H.F. stations with 1 X 1 calls  will be operating
across the H.F. bands from Coast Guard stations, Lighthouses , Vessels, Battleships, etc. during this event.


    /All amateur operators are encouraged to contact USCG AUX special
    event radio stations on high frequencies during this event.  Voice and digital modes will be operational during the event.

J2BB – The Battleship New Jersey will also be operational as a U.S.
Coast Guard Auxiliary Special event station !


   When :  Saturday  October 17, 2015

Where:   50 stations will be operational on most H.F. Bands  -


   /Don’t miss your chance to get a Free Commemorative  USCG Auxiliary QSL with an SASE sent to a special event station./


  *When you join the Coast Guard Auxiliary, you could find yourself saving lives, protecting the environment from polluters, guarding our shores from terrorists and much more**.*

We aid or save countless boaters every year. Every day the auxiliary
saves hundreds of thousands in property and saves countless life's.**

**

Learn more about the U.S. Coast Guard Auxiliary : http://www.cgaux.org/

All amateurs are invited to contact on H.F.  many of the  USCG AUX Event  –  1 X 1 stations

Help us Commemorate the USCG Auxiliary 76^th Anniversary.
