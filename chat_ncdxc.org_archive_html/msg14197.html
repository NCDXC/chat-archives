Welcome.  I don't know if they are still made anymore but when I go camping

I take an Outbacker antenna.  I have several.  Some are big and can handle

500 watts, others break down into smaller pieces.  None require radials.

They use taps to change bands however but much simpler than finding taps on

a loading coil.  And with 100 watts I work all over the world;  anything I

can hear that is.
