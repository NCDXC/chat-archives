Hi Bruce,

As an ARRL VE, I am happy to report that many people are still working to get licenses and upgrades.  I work with a small group where typically 8 VEs show up for a test session, and we've had more than 20 candidates per session this calendar year.  The majority of first-time applicants pass the initial exam, and a few even pass the next level on the spot.  I really enjoy getting to tell a pre-teenager that they just earned their first Ham license, and that happens in many sessions.  And many other candidates earn upgrades. 

Whether these new Hams (or Hams with new privileges) become involved enough to want to chase DX is another story...
