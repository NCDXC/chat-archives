I had to pipe in on this conversation as a ham who probably had a power surge.  The house I'm in now is relatively new and rebuilt since the fires in 2017.  In one short week/day I had to figure out why my dishwasher, microwave, K4D, Palstar Auto tuner, gas oven and a power supply all went down the drain.  Some guys told me it was a lightning strike or wild RF from my amp.  I didn't buy that, but I will tell you it was unbelievably expensive, and it took me awhile to recognize the correlation.  Will this happen from the solar flare?  Not sure but if you think it can then you might start the unplugging process and stop the worry.  

Sit back and watch the No. Lights the next two nights.  See ya on 20 ssb!
