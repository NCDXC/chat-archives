Right now, the ONLY reason why I maintain my ARRL membership is that it allows me to use their outgoing QSL bureau, mostly for all the cards I get from my holiday DXpeditions. If this ByLaw passes, I will simply drop my membership, and note on my QRZ and Clublog OQRS that I no longer accept bureau cards. They are already an anachronism, what with LoTW and Clublog (and to a lesser extent, eQSL) providing all the confirmation one needs.

Did you know that ARRL Sweepstakes “Gavel Winners” (top clubs) now have to PAY for the gavel? And they made this change RETROACTIVE so that gavels won in previous years (but not yet sent out, due to the incredible inefficiency of ARRL’s Contest Department) must be paid for, even though there was no such rule in place when the gavel was won. (Pizza Lover’s 259 has won the “Small Club” Sweepstakes gavel many times, but I, a founder of the club, have never actually seen one.)
