Hi Wayne,

A few years ago, the NCDXC put together a series of "courses" as part of an Elmering project.  Many of these presentations were recorded and are available here:

https://ncdxc.org/elmer-courses/

Elmer Courses – Northern California DX Club
The NCDXC had offered several Elmer courses during late 2017, early 2018. We have decided to leave these up for future reference as many topics will be relevant for years to come. During the 2018 IDXC in Visalia, CA the team was honored with Member of the Year award for all of their efforts.
ncdxc.org

I presented the first two, 'Introduction to HF Operating" and "What to expect with HF Operations", which are here:

https://www.ncdxc.org/elmer/Introduction%20to%20HF%20Operation%20-%20K6MKF-20170924%200158-1.mp4


https://www.ncdxc.org/elmer/HF%20Radio,%20What%20To%20Expect%20-%20K6MKF-20171008%200158-1.mp4

There are a couple of minutes of 'prep' and Webex 'joining tones' at the beginning of my two presentations and likely most of the others.

The NCDXC intends these presentations and materials to be used to encourage more folks to become Amateur Radio operators, and if these materials can be of assistance to you, please use them!
