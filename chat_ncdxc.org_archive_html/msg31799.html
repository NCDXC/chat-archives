Not a direct answer to your question, but some additional data points:

For many years I had a 160 meter vertical with a relay to switch it to
80 meters.  I tried various vacuum relays and they would work for a while (maybe a year or so) and then invariably die.  I figured out a way to modify a conventional heavy duty contactor so that it has very wide contact spacing, which I described in a QST Hints and Kinks article around 15 years ago.  Anyway, said contactor was installed on the vertical and worked perfectly for many years until the vertical blew down in a storm and smashed the relay when it hit the ground.
