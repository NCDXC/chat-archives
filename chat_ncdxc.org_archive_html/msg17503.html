It was very illuminating to read on page 9 that FT8 is only 6 dB
better than CW.  Furthermore, on page 7 the reader is warned
not to drive the transmitter too hard, so it may be that after
backing off by say 3 dB, as recommended on page 9, you are
left with only a 3 dB advantage vs CW.  Thus the argument I
constantly hear that small stations need FT8 as an "equalizer"
isn't a very convincing one in terms of abandoning CW.
