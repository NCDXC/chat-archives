Paul, 

Thanks for asking.  I've been a good boy (all relative of course) this year.   After several years of coal, my current FlexRadio is 12 years old in January, still chugging along though with my 2023 RF-KIT RF2K-S!  
...and I promised the XYL to seriously downsize my collection of vintage radios...  Collins Gold Dust Twins, Joe Walsh's Signal One CX7-A, and many more.  I have a long list with pictures!

So I took Santa's Sleigh (my 'little used since retiring' 34 Foot Gooseneck Race Car Trailer) to California and returned with an MA-550MDP with MARB Rotor Base, raising fixture, another spare used rotor (spares are important these days), and a WARC Antenna Mount.   This is to replace the MARB-550 I foolishly gave away in 2013 after sitting beside the house for 12 Years - No antenna towers in our Arroyo Grande, CA neighborhood due to HOA (but I did with a hidden antenna).

Dang, another, my third 3'x3'x6' dig.  The first (1989), my 8 year old Gary, handed the last few buckets of dirt up to me from down under. The second in 2021 - Oh too painful!
Now, looking for a strong young man for the last one.  I've heard some Movers will do darn near anything, per hour.
