Check the audio on the wsjt settings to make sure the right sound card is selected.

If it is, you'll need to look at the sound settings in the operating system.  If you're running windows, having the speaker as the default device and the ic7300 as the default communications device is helpful.  Also rename your USB audio device to something more identifiable, like IC7300-RX for the input and TX for the output.  If you rename them, you'll need to reselect them in the wsjt settings.

Also the output level of the IC7300-RX USB audio device is important, as well as disabling spatial effects and other sounds.
