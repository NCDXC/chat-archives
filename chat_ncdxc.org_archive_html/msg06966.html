Rich..I have the Force 12 C-3SS at 35 feet and the Force WARC 2/2  6 or 7 feet under it. Combined weight is 43 pounds, turning radius max is 14.7 feet.  Very small lot, very limited for sites to place, power lines on two sides.  For 80 and 40 the MFJ (!) 1792 vertical run with 2 counterpoises only and an inverted L barely 20 feet up at its center (35 feet at the tower, 10 feet at the anchor point) complete the farm. Most impressed with the Force pair, so-so with L although it got Amsterdam on 30 and the vertical works surprisingly well on the grey line. Been chasing zones on 12 and 17...very pleased with the results. Power to date is 800 or so watts.  JIM
