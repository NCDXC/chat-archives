Fellow travelers,

I do not yet have a proper Visalia room and the convention looms.

I missed the reservation scramble months ago because I had a day full of medical appointments to attend. Many of you know that I have been dealing with multiple medical problems starting four years ago which have essentially put on hold--perhaps permanently--my participation in meetings, DXpeditions and contesting. I have essentially been QRT for two years--and I miss it. My broken antennas from 2016 are still broken.

I have been on the lookout for an extra or share situation at the Marriott or close by. Further out does not compute as I have essentially  stopped driving due to my medical issues and won't have my own car. I can get a ride; I need a room.

This strategy has not worked yet and I have narrowly missed several "extra" rooms that have changed hands.

So this is an open appeal to both clubs for some consideration/help to get me a bed close to or at the convention center. If not, I will miss a convention that I have attended almost continuously for over 27 years. So far I have had one gracious offer--but there is only one bed and sleep deprivation has been at the core of my issues.

I have shared my room many times over the years and I am hoping that someone will return the favor.

Please reply off-line.
