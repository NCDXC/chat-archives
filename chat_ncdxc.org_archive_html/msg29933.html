They have a constant S9 (or higher) noise floor, worse as you drop to the lower bands.

There is nothing they can do about it; so any attempts to reach them, have to compensated from our side.

He was +1 (one stream) when I called, gave me -08 with 1450 watts into 4 elements at 60'... that's a fair amount of ERP.

They have a log periodic, not a lot of gain but good f/b.

They went to 5 streams while I was still watching, in the -7 range to me.  Then I moved off looking for the next target hi hi.

Congrats for those that made it, they're not easy (or loud most of the time).
