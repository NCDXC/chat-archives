Keith, I have worked some of my best DX during solar minimums. There is still propagation, just not nearly as good as at the peaks of the sunspot cycle where you can work the World on 10 meters with ten watts. 

 

Right now, the SFI is 144 which would be great, however the A-Index is 18 and the K-index is 5. 

 

Spaceweather.com says a G1 Solar Storm with a CME towards earth is in progress. 

 

That said, the low bands are rocking hot!   W6 guys are working ZS6NJ on 80 meters right now which is really exciting!

South African stations are loud on 40 meters right now. 

 

So during solar minimums, we just adapt and enjoy the low bands.
