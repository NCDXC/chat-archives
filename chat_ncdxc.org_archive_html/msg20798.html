I have just completed a huge project as I have been working from home most days. 

I have never been much for chasing awards, until now, I had the time and I got the bug. 

I have been spending several hours each day entering contacts from old paper logs into my computer logging program and uploading to LoTW frequently. 

It has been like a walk down memory lane looking at each of these contacts, many during the best years of the sunspot activity. 

When I started, I only had 105 DXCC entities confirmed and was not chasing. 

Now, I have 312 confirmed and DXCC on 10-160 meters. My biggest regret in Ham Radio is not starting PC logging earlier in the hobby. 

I will get with Russ soon to check about twenty cards that are not confirmed via LoTW, which should put me on Honor Roll. I still need Bouvet, Syria and Turkmenistan.

Having fun with amateur radio is what it is all about!

73, 

Paul N6PSE
