The Official Results of the BoD Meeting have been posted here:

http://www.arrl.org/member-bulletin?issue=2024-01-21 

Thanks to all your emails and other feedback, the good news is that the proposal to Revise Bylaw 46 was WITHDRAWN.   The bad news is that it could soon return in the future, perhaps in only slightly modified form, so be prepared to go through this exercise all over again.
