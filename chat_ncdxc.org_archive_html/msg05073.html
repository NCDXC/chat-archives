Some time ago I purchased a used FT-817, mostly for noise-hunting. It has a NiMH battery pack, took a charge when I got it, and has been trickle-charged since, charged from the supplied wall wart. It appeared to work on battery power---the displays seemed OK--but when I went noise-hunting, it was dead. Expecting a dead pack, I opened the radio and removed the battery pack, but it shows almost 11V. It   also shows a suspiciously unconnected black wire. This may or may not be the problem, but I have no schematic.

If anyone reading this has relevant experience with an FT-817, I would like to ask a few simple questions about this. Please email me if you are that person.
