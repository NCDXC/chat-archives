It wouldn't be the first case F/H was asked for, but in using (the all time EU fave) MSHV it's taking advantage of another flaw in MSHV, it doesn't CARE where the tones are, in F/H or not.  WSJT-X ignores tones below 1 KHz, except in hearing closure reports to the reports sent.

It's pretty easy to see it's MSHV with differences of up to 7 dB between streams in the same transmission.  And explains in the low valleys of QSB, only x/6 streams are decoded.  WSJT-X takes more care with the audio (and F/H mode), rarely a difference more than 2 dB.

I was NOT in F/H mode when I worked them (because WSJT-X would have randomly put my tone above 1KHz).  After 90 min plus of calling, dropping out of F/H and going to tone 230, logged in 5 minutes.

Log upload should begin in 3-5 hours so we'll know.
