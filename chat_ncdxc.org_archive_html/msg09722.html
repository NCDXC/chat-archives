> Flex radio has released a major update to the Flex signature series(6000) system greatly improving stability, adding additional capabilities, adding native lan based remote control of the rig along with many many more improvements.

Have they slowed the keying rise time? These radios don't look very good
for occupied bandwidth in recently published ARRL Lab tests. I think the
rise time was set at 1.5 msec, which would certainly contribute to
making the keying nasty. Cleaner radios like the TS590S can be set to 8
msec, and they look pretty good (second to the K3). Even better, they
could follow Elecraft's lead and use a raised cosine function to shape
the keying waveform.
